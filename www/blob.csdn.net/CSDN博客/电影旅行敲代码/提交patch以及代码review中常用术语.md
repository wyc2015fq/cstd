# 英文交流及代码review常用术语
- LGTM  —  looks good to me
- ACK  —  acknowledgement, i.e. agreed/accepted change
- NACK/NAK — negative acknowledgement, i.e. disagree with change and/or concept
- RFC  —  request for comments, i.e. I think this is a good idea, lets discuss
- WIP  —  work in progress, do not merge yet
- AFAIK/AFAICT  —  as far as I know / can tell
- IIRC  —  if I recall correctly
- IANAL  — “ I am not a lawyer ”, but I smell licensing issues
- NFC  — no functional change
- TL;DR — Too Long; Didn’t Read 主要作用就是告诉读者，这篇内容篇幅比较长，如果不想深入探讨或时间有限，可以看总结 
- FTR — for the record
- IIUC — if I understand correctly
- ISTM — It Seems To Me
- FYI — for your information供你参考
- Welcome to the club — It means you’re in the same situation as they are.
- As a rule of thumb — 根据经验
