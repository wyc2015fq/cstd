下面我们开始研究数学上什么是“微分”，怎么把“微分”加起来完成“积分”。
**1 积分**
比如要求![f(x)=x^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x)%3Dx%5E2) ，在![[0,a],a > 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2Ca%5D%2Ca%20%3E%200) 下的面积：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113848176?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
把![[0,a]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2Ca%5D) 平均分成![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 份，每份长为![\Delta x=\frac{a-0}{n}=\frac{a}{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba-0%7D%7Bn%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bn%7D) ：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113848184?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
这些点的坐标是这么一个数列（把![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) 点去掉）：
![\{a_i\}=\left\{\frac{a}{n}, \frac{2a}{n},\cdots, \frac{(n-1)a}{n}, \frac{na}{n}\right\},i=1,2,\cdots,n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_i%5C%7D%3D%5Cleft%5C%7B%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bn%7D%2C%20%5Cfrac%7B2a%7D%7Bn%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%20%5Cfrac%7B(n-1)a%7D%7Bn%7D%2C%20%5Cfrac%7Bna%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%5C%7D%2Ci%3D1%2C2%2C%5Ccdots%2Cn)
点之间的间隔为![\Delta x=\frac{a}{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bn%7D) ，所以上述数列可以简写为：
![\{a_i\}=\left\{\Delta x, 2\Delta x, \cdots, (n-1)\Delta x, n\Delta x\right\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_i%5C%7D%3D%5Cleft%5C%7B%5CDelta%20x%2C%202%5CDelta%20x%2C%20%5Ccdots%2C%20(n-1)%5CDelta%20x%2C%20n%5CDelta%20x%5Cright%5C%7D)
以这些坐标为终点，宽为![\Delta x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x) ，高为![f(a_i)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(a_i)) 作矩形：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113848180?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
每个矩形面积为![f(a_i)\Delta x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(a_i)%5CDelta%20x) ，它们的面积和为：
![\begin{aligned}    S_n        &=\sum_{i=1}^n f(a_i)\Delta x\\        \\        &=(\Delta x)^2\Delta x+(2\Delta x)^2\Delta x+\cdots+[(n-1)\Delta x]^2\Delta x+(n\Delta x)^2\Delta x\\        \\        &=[1+2^2+\cdots+(n-1)^2+n^2](\Delta x)^3\end{aligned}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%0A%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D%0A%20%20%20%20S_n%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%26%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%20f(a_i)%5CDelta%20x%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%26%3D(%5CDelta%20x)%5E2%5CDelta%20x%2B(2%5CDelta%20x)%5E2%5CDelta%20x%2B%5Ccdots%2B%5B(n-1)%5CDelta%20x%5D%5E2%5CDelta%20x%2B(n%5CDelta%20x)%5E2%5CDelta%20x%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%26%3D%5B1%2B2%5E2%2B%5Ccdots%2B(n-1)%5E2%2Bn%5E2%5D(%5CDelta%20x)%5E3%0A%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%0A)
已知其中的级数：
![1+2^2+\cdots+(n-1)^2+n^2=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2B2%5E2%2B%5Ccdots%2B(n-1)%5E2%2Bn%5E2%3D%5Cfrac%7Bn(n%2B1)(2n%2B1)%7D%7B6%7D)
所以上面的式子继续算下去：
![\begin{aligned}    S_n        &=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}(\Delta x)^3\\        \\        &=\left(\frac{2n^3+3n^2+n}{6}\right)(\Delta x)^3\end{aligned}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%0A%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D%0A%20%20%20%20S_n%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%26%3D%5Cfrac%7Bn(n%2B1)(2n%2B1)%7D%7B6%7D(%5CDelta%20x)%5E3%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%26%3D%5Cleft(%5Cfrac%7B2n%5E3%2B3n%5E2%2Bn%7D%7B6%7D%5Cright)(%5CDelta%20x)%5E3%0A%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%0A)
因为![\Delta x=\frac{a}{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bn%7D) ，代入上式可得：
![S_n=\sum_{i=1}^n f(a_i)\Delta x=a^3\left(\frac{1}{3}+\frac{1}{2n}+\frac{1}{6n^2}\right)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_n%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%20f(a_i)%5CDelta%20x%3Da%5E3%5Cleft(%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2n%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B6n%5E2%7D%5Cright))
当![n\to\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cto%5Cinfty) 的时候，矩形面积和就是曲面下的面积：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113848864?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
从数学上就是，曲面下面积![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) 为：
![\begin{aligned}    S=\lim_{n\to\infty}S_n        &=\lim_{n\to\infty}\left[a^3\left(\frac{1}{3}+\frac{1}{2n}+\frac{1}{6n^2}\right)\right]\\        \\        &=\frac{1}{3}a^3\end{aligned}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%0A%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D%0A%20%20%20%20S%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7DS_n%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%26%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cleft%5Ba%5E3%5Cleft(%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2n%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B6n%5E2%7D%5Cright)%5Cright%5D%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%26%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7Da%5E3%0A%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%0A)
在极限的帮助下，算出了曲面下的面积。
**2 新的开始**
既然得到了曲面下的面积了，是不是微积分课程完了？并没有，实际上刚刚开始。
**2.1 计算复杂**
上述计算方法早在微积分这门学科正式成立之前就有了。
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113848838?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
博纳文图拉·弗兰切斯科·卡瓦列里（1598 －1647），意大利几何学家。在他的著作中就提到了上面这个求解方法，那时候还没有极限，他是直觉地认为当![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 足够大时：
![a^3\left(\frac{1}{3}-\underbrace{\frac{1}{2n}+\frac{1}{6n^2}}_{可以忽略}\right)=\frac{1}{3}a^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5E3%5Cleft(%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D-%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2n%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B6n%5E2%7D%7D_%7B%E5%8F%AF%E4%BB%A5%E5%BF%BD%E7%95%A5%7D%5Cright)%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7Da%5E3)
把矩形面积加起来就需要计算级数（级数就是数列的和），但级数的计算往往很复杂。
这种计算方法简单粗暴，比如，想求正弦函数曲线下![[0,\pi]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2C%5Cpi%5D) 之间的面积，也可以通过矩形和来计算，但是计算起来并不简单（详细计算步骤可以查看[这里](https://www.matongxue.com/madocs/847.html)）：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926114604146?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
欧拉就是一个级数计算大师，当时很多数学家求“积分”时，算不出级数就向欧拉求救，所以有句话是这么说的：“欧拉，他是所有人的老师”（出自另外一位数学家拉普拉斯之口）。

“圆内小三角形的和”与“曲线下的矩形和”看起来异曲同工，但毕竟三角形和矩形还是不一样，能否把这两者统一起来？
还有，能不能用微积分来计算一段曲线的长度（马上就可以看到如何去做）：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113848915?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
例子可能举得还不够好、不够多，但可以看出目前所学的微积分还不够抽象，不足以解决更多问题。
**2.3 小结**
不管是为了计算更加简便，还是为了扩大微积分的应用范围，我们都需要开始新的学习。
**3 微分**
不管为了计算的便利性，还是覆盖更多的应用场景，都需要把“微分”这个概念抽象出来。
**3.1 矩形微分**
比如，想求![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 区间内![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x)) 曲线下的面积![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) ：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113848930?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
把![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 平均分成![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 份，每份长为![\Delta x=\frac{a-b}{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba-b%7D%7Bn%7D) ，每个![\Delta x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x) 对应一个![\Delta S_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20S_i) ：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113849565?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
很显然：
![S=\sum_{i=1}^n \Delta S_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%20%5CDelta%20S_i)
从里面随便选一份，为了表示一般性，其面积记为![\Delta S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20S) ：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113849484?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
在同样的位置，以![\Delta x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x) 为底、![f(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x_0)) 为高作矩形，其面积记为![\textrm{d}S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextrm%7Bd%7DS) ：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113849497?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
很显然，随着![\Delta x\to 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x%5Cto%200) ，![\Delta S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20S) 与![\textrm{d}S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextrm%7Bd%7DS) 会无限接近：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113849906?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
实际上两者都是无穷小：
![\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\Delta S=0=\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\textrm{d}S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%0A%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta%20x%5Cto%200%7D%5CDelta%20S%3D0%3D%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta%20x%5Cto%200%7D%5Ctextrm%7Bd%7DS%0A)
![\Delta S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+S) 与![\textrm{d}S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextrm%7Bd%7DS) 有各自的名字（命名的来由可以查看[这里](https://www.matongxue.com/madocs/847.html)）： 
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/7ibFSuc91QWvmNQicaCXHcdpOTRrlzFyicGHOJ8QL6fLMkjpMMU6hhJSEymiamtClKTUXZibfsV1QJ4bnDcHXky4XEQ/640?wx_fmt=png&tp=webp&wxfrom=5&wx_lazy=1&wx_co=1)
“微分”是对“差分”的近似，以“直”代“曲”（“微分”就是“直”，“差分”就是“曲”），也叫做线性近似：
![微分\approx 差分](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%E5%BE%AE%E5%88%86%5Capprox%20%E5%B7%AE%E5%88%86)
在极限下有（因为![\Delta x=\frac{a-b}{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba-b%7D%7Bn%7D) ，所以![\Delta x\to 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x%5Cto%200) 相当于![n\to\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cto%5Cinfty) ）：
![S=\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\sum_{i=1}^n \textrm{d}S_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%3D%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta%20x%5Cto%200%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%20%5Ctextrm%7Bd%7DS_i)
其中，![\textrm{d}S_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextrm%7Bd%7DS_i) 对应之前的![\Delta S_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20S_i) 。
稍微总结下：
![\begin{array}{c|c}    \hline    \quad\quad&\quad\color{blue}{差分}\quad&\quad\color{orange}{微分}\quad \\    \hline \\    \quad符号 \quad&\quad \Delta S \quad&\quad \textrm{d}S\quad\\     \quad求和 \quad&\quad \displaystyle S=\sum_{i=1}^n \Delta S_i \quad&\quad \displaystyle  S=\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\sum_{i=1}^n \textrm{d}S_i\quad\\    \\\hline\end{array}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%0A%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7Cc%7D%0A%20%20%20%20%5Chline%0A%20%20%20%20%5Cquad%5Cquad%26%5Cquad%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%E5%B7%AE%E5%88%86%7D%5Cquad%26%5Cquad%5Ccolor%7Borange%7D%7B%E5%BE%AE%E5%88%86%7D%5Cquad%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%5Chline%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%5Cquad%E7%AC%A6%E5%8F%B7%20%5Cquad%26%5Cquad%20%5CDelta%20S%20%5Cquad%26%5Cquad%20%5Ctextrm%7Bd%7DS%5Cquad%5C%5C%20%0A%20%20%20%20%5Cquad%E6%B1%82%E5%92%8C%20%5Cquad%26%5Cquad%20%5Cdisplaystyle%20S%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%20%5CDelta%20S_i%20%5Cquad%26%5Cquad%20%5Cdisplaystyle%20%20S%3D%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta%20x%5Cto%200%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%20%5Ctextrm%7Bd%7DS_i%5Cquad%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%5Chline%0A%5Cend%7Barray%7D%0A)
**3.2 三角形微分**
要求圆的面积![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) ：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180926113849984?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
同样的可以把它均分为![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个扇形：

**3.4 小结**
“微分”是“差分”的线性近似，是对“以直代曲”思想的数学表达。
有了“微分”之后，上述几种“积分”（包括面积、曲线长度等）就可以统一表示为：
![积分=\lim\sum 微分](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%0A%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%3D%5Clim%5Csum%20%E5%BE%AE%E5%88%86%0A)
上述几种“微分”的严格定义不尽相同，这里暂不给出，后面不断完善。
