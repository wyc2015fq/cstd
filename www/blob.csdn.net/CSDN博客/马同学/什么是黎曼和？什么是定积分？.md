因此，以左侧的函数值为高的矩形和可以如下计算：

也见识过它的古怪性质。这里也要把它拉出来作一个反面典型。![D(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D(x)) 的图像是没有办法画的，非要画也就是这样的：

**3 黎曼和**
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413195.jpeg?x-oss-process=image/watermark,type_ZmFuZ3poZW5naGVpdGk,shadow_10,text_aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==,size_16,color_FFFFFF,t_70)
格奥尔格·弗雷德里希·波恩哈德·黎曼（1826－1866）是德国数学家，黎曼几何学创始人，复变函数论创始人之一。在数学界搞风搞雨的黎曼猜想也是他的杰作。
基于对刚才两种情况：
- 
抛物线下的曲边梯形
- 
狄利克雷函数下的曲边梯形
的思考，看到不同划分带来的效果，黎曼先发明了黎曼和，进而定义了曲边梯形的面积，也就是定积分。
**3.1 任意划分**
![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 不一定需要均分为![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 份，可以任意分割：
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413378.gif)
很显然用于分割区间的点符合：
![a < x_1 < x_2 < \cdots < x_{n-1} < b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%20%3C%20x_1%20%3C%20x_2%20%3C%20%5Ccdots%20%3C%20x_%7Bn-1%7D%20%3C%20b)
令![x_0=a,x_n=b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%3Da%2Cx_n%3Db) ，那么集合：
![P=\{x_0, x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_{n}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%3D%5C%7Bx_0%2C%20x_1%2C%20x_2%2C%20%5Ccdots%2C%20x_%7Bn%7D%5C%7D)
称为![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的一个![\color{Salmon}{划分}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E5%88%92%E5%88%86%7D) 。划分![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 定义了![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个子区间：
![[x_0, x_1], [x_1, x_2], \cdots,[x_{k-1}, x_k],\cdots, [x_{n-1}, x_n]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_0%2C%20x_1%5D%2C%20%5Bx_1%2C%20x_2%5D%2C%20%5Ccdots%2C%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2C%20x_k%5D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%20%5Bx_%7Bn-1%7D%2C%20x_n%5D)
![[x_0, x_1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_0%2C%20x_1%5D) 称为第![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 个子区间，更一般的![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D) 被称为第![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个子区间：
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413184.png)
第![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个子区间的长度为![\Delta x_k=x_k-x_{k-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x_k%3Dx_k-x_%7Bk-1%7D) ：
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413197.png)
**3.2 任意高度**
对于某一个划分![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) ，在其第![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个子区间内随便选一个数![\xi_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi_k) ：
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413336.gif)
以![f(\xi_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(%5Cxi_k)) 作为矩形的高：
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413372.png?x-oss-process=image/watermark,type_ZmFuZ3poZW5naGVpdGk,shadow_10,text_aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==,size_16,color_FFFFFF,t_70)
那么矩形的高度也可以是任意的：
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413542.gif)
**3.3 黎曼和**
根据刚才的讲解，可以得到如下定义：
> **设函数![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x)) 在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上有定义，在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上任意插入若干个分点：**
![a = x_0 < x_1 < x_2\cdots < x_n = b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%20%3D%20x_0%20%3C%20x_1%20%3C%20x_2%5Ccdots%20%3C%20x_n%20%3D%20b)
**这些分点的集合：**
![P=\{x_0, x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_{n}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%3D%5C%7Bx_0%2C%20x_1%2C%20x_2%2C%20%5Ccdots%2C%20x_%7Bn%7D%5C%7D)
**称为![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的一个![\color{Salmon}{划分}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E5%88%92%E5%88%86%7D) 。划分![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 定义了![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个子区间：**
![[x_0, x_1], [x_1, x_2], \cdots, [x_{n-1}, x_n]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_0%2C%20x_1%5D%2C%20%5Bx_1%2C%20x_2%5D%2C%20%5Ccdots%2C%20%5Bx_%7Bn-1%7D%2C%20x_n%5D)
**它们的长度依次为：**
![\Delta x_1=x_1-x_0,\Delta x_2=x_2-x_1,\cdots,\Delta x_n=x_n-x_{n-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x_1%3Dx_1-x_0%2C%5CDelta%20x_2%3Dx_2-x_1%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5CDelta%20x_n%3Dx_n-x_%7Bn-1%7D)
**在每个子区间![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D) 上任取选取一个数![\xi_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi_k) ，以![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D) 为底，![f(\xi_i)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(%5Cxi_i)) 为高构造矩形，这些矩形的和：**
![A_n=\sum_{k=1}^{n}f(\xi_k)\Delta x_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_n%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df(%5Cxi_k)%5CDelta%20x_k)
**称为![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上的![\color{Salmon}{黎曼和}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E9%BB%8E%E6%9B%BC%E5%92%8C%7D) 。**
之前计算的![A_L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_L) 、![A_R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_R) 是黎曼和：
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413435.jpeg?x-oss-process=image/watermark,type_ZmFuZ3poZW5naGVpdGk,shadow_10,text_aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==,size_16,color_FFFFFF,t_70)
狄利克雷函数中划分出来的矩形和![A_{有理}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%9C%89%E7%90%86%7D) 、![A_{无理}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%97%A0%E7%90%86%7D) 也是黎曼和。
**4 定积分**
随着![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的划分不断变细，所有子区间的长度趋于0时，黎曼和不断地逼近曲边梯形的面积：
![](https://img-blog.csdnimg.cn/20181113185413613.gif)
这个过程的严格化如下：
> **设函数![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x)) 在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上有定义，对于![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上的任意划分![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) ，![\xi_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi_k) 为子区间![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D) 上任意选取的数，子区间![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D) 的长度为![\Delta x_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x_k) ，记：**
![\lambda=max\{\Delta x_1,\Delta x_2,...,\Delta x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%3Dmax%5C%7B%5CDelta%20x_1%2C%5CDelta%20x_2%2C...%2C%5CDelta%20x_n%5C%7D)
**如果下述极限存在：**
![I=\lim_{\lambda\to0}\sum_{k=1}^{n}f(\xi_k)\Delta x_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%5Clim_%7B%5Clambda%5Cto0%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df(%5Cxi_k)%5CDelta%20x_k)
**则称![\color{Salmon}{被积函数}f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E8%A2%AB%E7%A7%AF%E5%87%BD%E6%95%B0%7Df(x)) 在![\color{Salmon}{积分区间}[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E5%8C%BA%E9%97%B4%7D%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上![\color{Salmon}{可积}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E5%8F%AF%E7%A7%AF%7D) ，![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 为![\color{Salmon}{积分下限}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E4%B8%8B%E9%99%90%7D) ，![b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b) 为![\color{Salmon}{积分上限}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E4%B8%8A%E9%99%90%7D) ，![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 为![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x)) 在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上的![\color{Salmon}{定积分}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E5%AE%9A%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%7D) ，![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 为![\color{Salmon}{积分变量}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E5%8F%98%E9%87%8F%7D) ，可以标记如下：**
![I=\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%5Cint_%7Ba%7D%5E%7Bb%7Df(x)dx)
回到之前讨论的问题：
- 
抛物线下的曲边梯形：![A_{Ln}=A_{Rn}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7BLn%7D%3DA_%7BRn%7D) ，以及各种划分都相等，所以![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 存在，可积
- 
狄利克雷函数下的曲边梯形：![A_{有理}\ne A_{无理}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%9C%89%E7%90%86%7D%5Cne%20A_%7B%E6%97%A0%E7%90%86%7D) ，所以![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 不存在，不可积
这里新引入的积分符号是莱布尼兹创造的：
![S\quad\xrightarrow{\quad拉长\quad}\quad \int](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5Cquad%5Cxrightarrow%7B%5Cquad%E6%8B%89%E9%95%BF%5Cquad%7D%5Cquad%20%5Cint)
其中，![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) 代表英文中的求和（“sum”），拉长的![\int](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint) 则表明积分是和的极限（“limits of sums”）。这个符号相当精练，可以表达非常丰富的信息：
