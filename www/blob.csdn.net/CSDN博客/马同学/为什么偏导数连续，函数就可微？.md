# 为什么偏导数连续，函数就可微？ - 马同学 - CSDN博客
2018年10月23日 17:50:27[马同学高等数学](https://me.csdn.net/ccnt_2012)阅读数：7147
多变量微积分里面有这么一个结论：
> **如果函数![z=f(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z%3Df(x%2Cy)) 的偏导数![\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20z%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D) 、![\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20z%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D) 在点![(x_0,y_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cy_0)) 连续，那么函数在该点可微。**
下面来解释这个结论，并且减弱这个结论的条件。
先简单阐述下“连续”、“偏导数”、“可微”的意义，后面要用到。如果非常熟悉了，可以直接跳到最后一节“偏导数连续推出可微”。
**1 连续的含义**
通俗来说，用笔作画，不提笔画出来的曲线就是连续的：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173946329?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
**1.1 没有缝隙**
我们对连续的函数曲线的直观感受是没有缝隙：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173946447?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
如果把曲线看作一条道路的话，那么不管是蚂蚁、人还是自行车，都有能力从左边走到右边：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173946606?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
而不连续的曲线会有断裂：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173946495?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
蚂蚁通过能力太差，就没有办法跨过裂缝：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173946637?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
**1.2 另一层含义**
从代数上我们可以看到另外一层含义。假设![f(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x_0)) 附近某点为![f(x_0+\Delta x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x)) ，根据连续的性质有：
![\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}f(x_0+\Delta x)=f(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta%20x%5Cto%200%7Df(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x)%3Df(x_0))
利用极限的性质可以得到：
![\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}f(x_0+\Delta x)=f(x_0)\implies f(x_0+\Delta x)=f(x_0)+\underbrace{o(\Delta x)}_{代表非常小的值}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta%20x%5Cto%200%7Df(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x)%3Df(x_0)%5Cimplies%20f(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x)%3Df(x_0)%2B%5Cunderbrace%7Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%7D_%7B%E4%BB%A3%E8%A1%A8%E9%9D%9E%E5%B8%B8%E5%B0%8F%E7%9A%84%E5%80%BC%7D)
因此上式表明，![f(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x_0)) 与附近![f(x_0+\Delta x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x)) 的值相差非常小，这层含义也是没有“缝隙”的另外一种阐述。
**2 可微的含义**
**2.1 单变量函数的微分**
一元的情况下，在![(x_0,f(x_0))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cf(x_0))) 点可微指的是，在![(x_0,f(x_0))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cf(x_0))) 点附近可以用直线来近似曲线，这根直线就是切线：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173946514?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
距离![(x_0,f(x_0))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cf(x_0))) 越近，这种近似越好，体现为切线和曲线之间的相差越来越小：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173946869?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
令![\Delta x=x-x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%20x%3Dx-x_0) ，那么![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 附近曲线与直线的近似可以表示为：
![\underbrace{f(x_0+\Delta x)}_{曲线}\quad=\quad\underbrace{f(x_0)+f'(x_0)\Delta x}_{切线}\quad+\quad\underbrace{o(\Delta x)}_{代表非常小的值}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x)%7D_%7B%E6%9B%B2%E7%BA%BF%7D%5Cquad%3D%5Cquad%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0)%2Bf%27(x_0)%5CDelta%20x%7D_%7B%E5%88%87%E7%BA%BF%7D%5Cquad%2B%5Cquad%5Cunderbrace%7Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%7D_%7B%E4%BB%A3%E8%A1%A8%E9%9D%9E%E5%B8%B8%E5%B0%8F%E7%9A%84%E5%80%BC%7D)

**2.2.1 偏导数**
首先要对偏导数有所了解。多变量的函数![f(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x%2Cy)) 可以是三维空间中的曲面
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/2018102317394736?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
平面![y=t,t\in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dt%2Ct%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是一系列平面，它们与曲面交于一条条曲线：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173947154?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
很显然，点在这些曲线上运动，![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) 是不会变化的，只有![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 会变化：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173950891?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
偏导数![\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D) 所求的也就是在这些曲线上运动的点的速度（变化率），对于![(x_0,y_0,f(x_0,y_0))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cy_0%2Cf(x_0%2Cy_0))) 点，知道它的偏导数就可以得到这条曲线在此点的线性近似，也就是这条曲线的切线，或者称为偏微分：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173950672?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
这种近似关系可以表示为：
![\underbrace{f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0)}_{曲线}\quad=\quad\underbrace{f(x_0,y_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x}_{切线}\quad+\quad\underbrace{o(\Delta x)}_{代表非常小的值}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x%2Cy_0)%7D_%7B%E6%9B%B2%E7%BA%BF%7D%5Cquad%3D%5Cquad%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2Cy_0)%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5CDelta%20x%7D_%7B%E5%88%87%E7%BA%BF%7D%5Cquad%2B%5Cquad%5Cunderbrace%7Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%7D_%7B%E4%BB%A3%E8%A1%A8%E9%9D%9E%E5%B8%B8%E5%B0%8F%E7%9A%84%E5%80%BC%7D)
同样的道理，偏导数![\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D) 描述的是只有![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) 值变化的曲线上的点的速度，假设这样的曲线为![f_y(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_y(x%2Cy)) ，其切线与之的近似关系可以表示为：：
![\underbrace{f(x_0,y_0+\Delta y)}_{曲线}\quad=\quad\underbrace{f(x_0,y_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\Delta y}_{切线}\quad+\quad\underbrace{o(\Delta y)}_{代表非常小的值}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2Cy_0%2B%5CDelta%20y)%7D_%7B%E6%9B%B2%E7%BA%BF%7D%5Cquad%3D%5Cquad%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2Cy_0)%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D%5CDelta%20y%7D_%7B%E5%88%87%E7%BA%BF%7D%5Cquad%2B%5Cquad%5Cunderbrace%7Bo(%5CDelta%20y)%7D_%7B%E4%BB%A3%E8%A1%A8%E9%9D%9E%E5%B8%B8%E5%B0%8F%E7%9A%84%E5%80%BC%7D)
**2.2.2 微分**
多变量的函数![f(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x%2Cy)) 在![(x_0,y_0,f(x_0,y_0))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cy_0%2Cf(x_0%2Cy_0))) 点的微分，指的是在![(x_0,y_0,f(x_0,y_0))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cy_0%2Cf(x_0%2Cy_0))) 点找到一个平面来近似曲面，这就是切平面：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173950236?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
切平面与曲面的近似可以表示为：
![\underbrace{f(x_0+\Delta x, y_0+\Delta y)}_{曲面}\quad=\quad\underbrace{f(x_0,y_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\Delta y}_{平面}\quad+\quad\underbrace{o(\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2})}_{代表非常小的值}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x%2C%20y_0%2B%5CDelta%20y)%7D_%7B%E6%9B%B2%E9%9D%A2%7D%5Cquad%3D%5Cquad%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2Cy_0)%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5CDelta%20x%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D%5CDelta%20y%7D_%7B%E5%B9%B3%E9%9D%A2%7D%5Cquad%2B%5Cquad%5Cunderbrace%7Bo(%5Csqrt%7B(%5CDelta%20x)%5E2%2B(%5CDelta%20y)%5E2%7D)%7D_%7B%E4%BB%A3%E8%A1%A8%E9%9D%9E%E5%B8%B8%E5%B0%8F%E7%9A%84%E5%80%BC%7D)
上面出现了![o(\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=o(%5Csqrt%7B(%5CDelta%20x)%5E2%2B(%5CDelta%20y)%5E2%7D)) ，这是因为此点的邻域是一个平面（下面用圆来表示这个平面，实际上这个圆可以任意大小）：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/2018102317394955?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
此圆的半径可以表示为：
![r=\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%3D%5Csqrt%7B(%5CDelta%20x)%5E2%2B(%5CDelta%20y)%5E2%7D)
**2.3 微分与偏微分的关系**
很显然，过![(x_0,y_0,f(x_0,y_0))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cy_0%2Cf(x_0%2Cy_0))) 点，并不是只有![x,y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%2Cy) 方向的曲线（两个方向的曲线的切线就是偏微分）：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173950979?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
还有无数别的方向的曲线（随便画了两条）：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173950227?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
这些曲线的切线（假如有的话）要在同一个平面，这个平面就是切平面，才叫做可微（详情参考之前给出的参考文章）。
而偏微分只是无数切线中的两条，所以：
![偏导数存在\mathrel{\rlap{\hskip .5em/}}\Longrightarrow 可微](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%E5%81%8F%E5%AF%BC%E6%95%B0%E5%AD%98%E5%9C%A8%5Cmathrel%7B%5Crlap%7B%5Chskip%20.5em%2F%7D%7D%5CLongrightarrow%20%E5%8F%AF%E5%BE%AE)
比如![f(x,y)= \frac{x y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x%2Cy)%3D%20%5Cfrac%7Bx%20y%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bx%5E2%2By%5E2%7D%7D) 就是偏导数存在，但是不可微。它的图像是：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173950780?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
在![(0,0,0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(0%2C0%2C0)) 点，![f(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x%2Cy)) 与![x=0,y=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0%2Cy%3D0) 的交线是下面红色的直线，分别与![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 轴和![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) 轴重叠：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173951450?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
因此，在![(0,0,0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(0%2C0%2C0)) 点的偏微分就是![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 轴和![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) 轴。但是![f(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x%2Cy)) 与![y=x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dx) 的交线是：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173952334?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
在![(0,0,0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(0%2C0%2C0)) 点形成了一个尖点：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/2018102317395217?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
很显然此曲线的切线不存在（此曲线的左右切线由方向导数决定）。因此![f(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f(x%2Cy)) 在![(0,0,0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(0%2C0%2C0)) 点不可微（具体细节也请参看参考文章）。
**3 偏导数连续推出可微**
前面说了很多，就是为了得到下面这个表格：

下面讲解涉及的三维图像太复杂，不容易看清，所以把三维图像画到二维中，应该不会影响理解。
先给出![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 、![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 、![C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C) 、![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 四个点，把它们的三维坐标也标出来：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173951432?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 点的偏导数连续，分别为：
![\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\quad \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5Cquad%20%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D)
从![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 出发，运动到![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) ，很显然只有![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 方向有变化：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173951660?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
因此![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 点的值为：
![\underbrace{f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0)}_{B点}=\underbrace{f(x_0,y_0)}_{A点}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x+o(\Delta x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x%2Cy_0)%7D_%7BB%E7%82%B9%7D%3D%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2Cy_0)%7D_%7BA%E7%82%B9%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5CDelta%20x%2Bo(%5CDelta%20x))
继续往上走到![C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C) 点：
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20181023173951958?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L2NjbnRfMjAxMg==/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)
因为偏导数连续，所以附近的偏导数也是存在的，假设![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 的偏导数为![\frac{\partial f}{\partial y_b}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y_b%7D) ，那么可得：
![\begin{aligned}\underbrace{f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0+\Delta y)}_{C点}    &=\underbrace{f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0)}_{B点}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y_b}\Delta y+o(\Delta y)\\    \\    &=\underbrace{f(x_0,y_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x+o(\Delta x)}_{B点}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y_b}\Delta y+o(\Delta y)    \\    &=f(x_0,y_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y_b}\Delta y+o(\Delta x)+o(\Delta y)\end{aligned}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%0A%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D%0A%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x%2Cy_0%2B%5CDelta%20y)%7D_%7BC%E7%82%B9%7D%0A%20%20%20%20%26%3D%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x%2Cy_0)%7D_%7BB%E7%82%B9%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y_b%7D%5CDelta%20y%2Bo(%5CDelta%20y)%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%26%3D%5Cunderbrace%7Bf(x_0%2Cy_0)%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5CDelta%20x%2Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%7D_%7BB%E7%82%B9%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y_b%7D%5CDelta%20y%2Bo(%5CDelta%20y)%0A%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%26%3Df(x_0%2Cy_0)%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5CDelta%20x%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y_b%7D%5CDelta%20y%2Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%2Bo(%5CDelta%20y)%0A%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%0A)
这里就是关键了，因为偏导数连续，所以![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 、![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 偏导数差不多，有：
![\underbrace{\frac{\partial f}{\partial y_b}}_{B点偏导}=\underbrace{\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}}_{A点偏导}+o(\Delta x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y_b%7D%7D_%7BB%E7%82%B9%E5%81%8F%E5%AF%BC%7D%3D%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D%7D_%7BA%E7%82%B9%E5%81%8F%E5%AF%BC%7D%2Bo(%5CDelta%20x))
因此上式可以改写为：
![\begin{aligned}f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0+\Delta y)    &=f(x_0,y_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x+\underbrace{\frac{\partial f}{\partial y_1}}_{\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}+o(\Delta x)}\Delta y+o(\Delta x)+o(\Delta y)\\    \\    &=f(x_0,y_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\Delta y+\underbrace{o(\Delta x)\Delta y+o(\Delta x)+o(\Delta y)}_{等价于o(\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2})}\\    \\    &=f(x_0,y_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\Delta x+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\Delta y+o(\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2})\\\end{aligned}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%0A%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D%0Af(x_0%2B%5CDelta%20x%2Cy_0%2B%5CDelta%20y)%0A%20%20%20%20%26%3Df(x_0%2Cy_0)%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5CDelta%20x%2B%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y_1%7D%7D_%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D%2Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%7D%5CDelta%20y%2Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%2Bo(%5CDelta%20y)%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%26%3Df(x_0%2Cy_0)%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5CDelta%20x%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D%5CDelta%20y%2B%5Cunderbrace%7Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%5CDelta%20y%2Bo(%5CDelta%20x)%2Bo(%5CDelta%20y)%7D_%7B%E7%AD%89%E4%BB%B7%E4%BA%8Eo(%5Csqrt%7B(%5CDelta%20x)%5E2%2B(%5CDelta%20y)%5E2%7D)%7D%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%5C%5C%0A%20%20%20%20%26%3Df(x_0%2Cy_0)%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D%5CDelta%20x%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D%5CDelta%20y%2Bo(%5Csqrt%7B(%5CDelta%20x)%5E2%2B(%5CDelta%20y)%5E2%7D)%5C%5C%0A%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%0A)
至此，得到了![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 点可微的结论（上面的等价性没有证明，在一些《数学分析》书籍中，可微采用的是类似的定义）。
如果仔细看上面的证明，会发现只用到了![\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20f%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D) 连续，因此条件可以减弱一些：
> **如果函数![z=f(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z%3Df(x%2Cy)) 的偏导数![\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20z%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20x%7D) 、![\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%20z%7D%7B%5Cpartial%20y%7D) 在点![(x_0,y_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=(x_0%2Cy_0)) 及其邻域存在，偏导数其中之一在邻域内连续，那么函数在该点可微。**
