什么是共轭转置矩阵？
矩阵有实数矩阵和复数矩阵。[转置矩阵](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E8%BD%AC%E7%BD%AE%E7%9F%A9%E9%98%B5&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1Y1ryDknWb4PHmkmWb3nHfd0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHD4njc3nWmv)仅仅是将矩阵的行与列对换，而共轭[转置矩阵](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E8%BD%AC%E7%BD%AE%E7%9F%A9%E9%98%B5&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1Y1ryDknWb4PHmkmWb3nHfd0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHD4njc3nWmv)在将行与列对换后还要讲每个元素共轭一下。共轭你应该知道，就是将形如a+bi的数变成a-bi，实数的共轭是它本身。所以，实数矩阵的共轭[转置矩阵](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E8%BD%AC%E7%BD%AE%E7%9F%A9%E9%98%B5&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1Y1ryDknWb4PHmkmWb3nHfd0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHD4njc3nWmv)就是转置矩阵，复数矩阵的共轭转置矩阵就是上面所说的行列互换后每个元素取共轭。
