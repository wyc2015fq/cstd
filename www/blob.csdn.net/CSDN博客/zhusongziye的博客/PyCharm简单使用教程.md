二、python版本管理切换

　　File>settings>Prgject:python>project interpreter　

![](https://note.youdao.com/yws/public/resource/d3736a71e42ff2a9c338830a1ba0e3fd/xmlnote/1C6F7613644F4B519EAD8DD92D7B35C3/13258)
