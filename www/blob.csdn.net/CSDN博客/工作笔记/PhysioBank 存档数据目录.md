This page lists all currently available databases in the PhysioBank archives, organized according to the types of signals and annotations contained in each database:

此页列出了目前所有PhysioBank中可用的数据库，根据每个数据库所含信号的类型和注释情况进行分门别类 


- [Multi-Parameter Databases](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#multi). Available signals vary, but may include ECG, continuous invasive blood pressure, respiration, oxygen saturation, and EEG, among others.





          多参数数据库：不同数据库所含信号会有差异，但有可能包括心电，连续有创血压，呼吸，血氧饱和度，脑电等


- [ECG Databases](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#ecg). Also see[Multi-Parameter Databases](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#multi), most of which include ECG signals.

          心电数据库：参见多参数数据库，其中大部分都包含心电信号


- [Interbeat (RR) Interval Databases](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#rr). These contain beat annotations obtained from ECG recordings, but the ECG signals are not available. Also see[ECG
 Databases](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#ecg), most of which include beat annotations in addition to the original ECG signals.

         RR间期数据库：这些数据库包含从ECG记录中获得的心搏注释，但是ECG信号是不能使用的。参见心电数据库，除原始心电信号外大部分数据库都包含心搏注释。


- [Gait and Balance Databases](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#gait)

          步态与平衡数据库


- [Neuroelectric and Myoelectric Databases](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#neuro)

          神经电与肌电数据库


- [Image Databases](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#image)

         图像数据库


- [Synthetic Data](http://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/#synthetic)

          合成数据
