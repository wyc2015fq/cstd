# 5G Serves New Verticals（5G服务于新的垂直市场）
> - Since 3GPP was founded (1998), member companies have sought to offer new applications and services via data services of mobile telecommunications.
- 5G expands offerings by specializing radio and network capabilities, exposing them.
- – Enhanced Mobile Broadband
- – Massive Internet of Things
- – Ultra-Reliable & Low Latency
- 自3gpp成立（1998年）以来，会员公司一直寻求通过移动电信的数据服务提供新的应用和服务。
- 5G通过专门的无线电和网络功能扩展产品，并将其公开。
- –增强的移动宽带
- –海量物联网
- –超可靠和低延迟
# Stage 1 Requirements Frozen（第一阶段要求冻结）
> 
• 5G Requirements approved March 2016 include
– Specialization via Network Slicing, Diverse Mobility Management, expanded QoS and Policy control, Exposure of capabilities
– Efficiency via a common core for multiple accesses (fixed, mobile/terrestrial [LTE, NG NR and more], satellite), optimized signalling, traffic steering, content delivery features, energy use optimization,
– Radio related improvements via Self Backhauling, Flexible Multicast/Broadcast service, Long Range in Rural Areas, and support for service improvements
– Service improvements via Higher Data Rates, Supporting Higher Speeds, Low Latency, High Reliability, High Accuracy Positioning, Security Features
• Specific targeted services
– Vehicle to Everything, Industrial Automation, Tactile Internet, UHD Video, …
## 2016年3月批准的5G要求包括
- –通过网络切片实现专业化、多样化的移动管理、扩展的QoS和策略控制、功能的公开
- –通过多个接入点（固定、移动/地面[LTE、NG NR和更多]、卫星）的通用核心提高效率，优化信号、交通转向、内容交付功能、能源使用优化，
- –通过**自回程、灵活的多播/广播服务、农村地区的远程服务以及对服务改进的支持，实现与无线电相关的改进**
- –通过**更高的数据速率、支持更高的速度、低延迟、高可靠性、高精度定位和安全功能来改进服务**
## 特定的目标服务
- –车辆到一切，工业自动化，触觉互联网，超高清视频，…
## Supported Scenarios（支持的方案）

- – Support 5G CN; or
- – Support NG NR; or
- – Support both
- • Migration
- – Devices operate in all scenarios to avoid incompatibility / market fragmentation.
- • Early freeze of Rel-15 NSA architecture (Mar 2019)
- •5G部署
- –支持5g cn；或
- –支撑Ng Nr；或
- –同时支撑
- 迁移
- –设备在所有情况下运行，以避免不兼容/市场分割。
- •REL-15 NSA架构的早期冻结（2019年3月）

# 5G Phases
## Phase 1（第一阶段）
> - (Rel-15, 06.18 stage 3 freeze)
- • Base features (with forward compatibility)
- – Roaming, Charging, Management, QoS &Policy Control, Service Continuity, Network Sharing…
- • Enhanced Mobile Broadband
- – NSA and SA scenarios
- – LTE, NR and Untrusted Non-3GPP Access
- – Positioning, Public Warning System, Access Class Barring
- • SMS, IMS, Emergency Services & Priority Services
- (重新部署 15, 06.18 第3阶段冻结)
- ·基本功能 (具有正向兼容性)
- –漫游、充电、管理、QoS 和策略控制、服务连续性、网络共享..。
- ·增强的移动宽带
- –国家安全局和 SA 方案
- –lte、NR 和不受信任的非3gpp 访问
- –定位, 公共警告系统, 接入类禁止
- ·短信、IMS、紧急服务和优先服务

- eDRX & MTC features
- – Device to Device
- – V2X
- – Satellite Support
- – Trusted Non-3GPP Access
- (重新增加 16, 12.19 阶段第3阶段冻结)

# Other 5G Aspects (for Phase 2)

• 5G Media
– Ongoing work on streaming enhancements, Studies already for Virtual Reality, ’5G Media.’

– Studies prepare for reliability and low latency specific features for the CN and RAN.
• 5G CIoT aspects
– Rel-16 will see CN and RAN IoT features.

– Satellite access network support (high latency, etc.) CN and RAN.
