Stationary subspace analysis~

Stationary Subspace Analysis (SSA)is
 a [blind
 source separation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blind_source_separation)[algorithm](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Algorithm) which
 factorizes a [multivariate](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multivariate)[time
 series](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_series) into [stationary](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stationary_process) and [non-stationary](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Non-stationary) components.
