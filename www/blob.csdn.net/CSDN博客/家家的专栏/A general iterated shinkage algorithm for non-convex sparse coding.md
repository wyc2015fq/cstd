A Generalized iterated shrinkage algorithm for non-convex sparse coding

From zhanglei




Sparse coding with non-convex Lp-norm minimization (0<p<1)

**better than ** Sparse coding with convex L1 norm miminization.




non-convex Lp-norm sparse coding算法有：


1、iteratively reweighted least squares

2、Iteratively thresholding method

3、look-up table




通过扩展soft-thresholding operator, 提出A generalized iterated shrinkage algorithm,解决非凸的稀疏编码问题。
