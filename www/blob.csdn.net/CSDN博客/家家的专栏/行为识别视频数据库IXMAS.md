数据格式：

        该数据库包含了11个人做13个动作，重复3次，这13个动作包括1 - check watch, 2 - cross arms, 3 - scratch head, 4 - sit down, 5 - get up, 6 - turn around, 7 - walk, 8 - wave, 9 - punch, 10 - kick, 11 - point, 12 - pick up, 13 - throw (over head), 14 - throw (from bottom up). 这儿给了matlab的源程序，也有对动作的标记。
