# 这个电路中的LM393工作原理是怎样的。其中R-Light是光敏二极管.Light是LED灯VCC是6V.
![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20160416114445950?watermark/2/text/aHR0cDovL2Jsb2cuY3Nkbi5uZXQv/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70/gravity/Center)

[LM393](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=LM393&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1dBn1nkmHR4uWbLP1u-uj610ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnH6zrHmYP1b3njRdPWb1n1Dsn0)是电压比较器，将接在R-Light端的[光敏二极管](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E5%85%89%E6%95%8F%E4%BA%8C%E6%9E%81%E7%AE%A1&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1dBn1nkmHR4uWbLP1u-uj610ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnH6zrHmYP1b3njRdPWb1n1Dsn0)接收光照时产生的电阻值变化变成电压信号传递给电压比较器的同相输入端INB+，这个变化的电压信号与电压比较器的反相输入端INA-端的[基准电压](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E5%9F%BA%E5%87%86%E7%94%B5%E5%8E%8B&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1dBn1nkmHR4uWbLP1u-uj610ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnH6zrHmYP1b3njRdPWb1n1Dsn0)相比较，当同相端INB+电压大于反相端INA-端电压时，电压比较器的输出端OUT输出高电平电压，当同相端INB+电压小于反相端INA-端电压时，电压比较器的输出端OUT输出低电平电压，此时Light [LED灯](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=LED%E7%81%AF&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1dBn1nkmHR4uWbLP1u-uj610ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnH6zrHmYP1b3njRdPWb1n1Dsn0)亮。
在没有光照时，[光敏二极管](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E5%85%89%E6%95%8F%E4%BA%8C%E6%9E%81%E7%AE%A1&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1dBn1nkmHR4uWbLP1u-uj610ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnH6zrHmYP1b3njRdPWb1n1Dsn0)的电阻值很大，电阻R23与该[光敏二极管](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E5%85%89%E6%95%8F%E4%BA%8C%E6%9E%81%E7%AE%A1&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1dBn1nkmHR4uWbLP1u-uj610ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnH6zrHmYP1b3njRdPWb1n1Dsn0)组成的分压点电压升高，使同相端INB+电压大于反相端INA-端电压，电压比较器的输出端OUT输出高电平电压，此时Light [LED灯](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=LED%E7%81%AF&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1dBn1nkmHR4uWbLP1u-uj610ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnH6zrHmYP1b3njRdPWb1n1Dsn0)不亮。在有光照时，光敏二极管的电阻值很小，电阻R23与该光敏二极管组成的分压点电压下降，使同相端INB+电压小于反相端INB-端电压，电压比较器的输出端OUT输出低电平电压，此时Light [LED灯](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=LED%E7%81%AF&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1dBn1nkmHR4uWbLP1u-uj610ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnH6zrHmYP1b3njRdPWb1n1Dsn0)亮。
接在反相端INA-端的电位器VR2用于调节该端的电位电压，这个电压也就是电压比较器输入的阀值翻转电压，用于光照灵敏度调节。
