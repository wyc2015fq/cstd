### 方法一、官网下载安装包

安装Scala和安装Java差不多，如果你有eclipse和JDK那么直接点下一步就好。就是安装包有点大，整个240多MB。

进入官方下载页面如下：

![](https://img-blog.csdn.net/20180524193039538?watermark/2/text/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNzZG4ubmV0L3FxXzM5NTIxNTU0/font/5a6L5L2T/fontsize/400/fill/I0JBQkFCMA==/dissolve/70)






另外推荐两个sbt辅助插件：[https://github.com/sbt/sbt-assembly](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//github.com/sbt/sbt-assembly)

package project to a fat jar 将所有的依赖package到一个jar中[https://github.com/jrudolph/sbt-dependency-graph](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//github.com/jrudolph/sbt-dependency-graph)
