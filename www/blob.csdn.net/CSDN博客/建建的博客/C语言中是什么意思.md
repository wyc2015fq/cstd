在文本处理中, [CR](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=CR&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k), LF, [CR](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=CR&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)/LF是不同[操作系统](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E6%93%8D%E4%BD%9C%E7%B3%BB%E7%BB%9F&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)上使用的[换行符](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E6%8D%A2%E8%A1%8C%E7%AC%A6&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k).
Dos和windows采用回车+换行[CR](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=CR&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)/LF表示下一行, 
而[UNIX](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=UNIX&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)/Linux采用[换行符](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E6%8D%A2%E8%A1%8C%E7%AC%A6&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)LF表示下一行，
[苹果机](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E8%8B%B9%E6%9E%9C%E6%9C%BA&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)(MAC OS系统)则采用回车符CR表示下一行.
CR用符号'\r'表示, 十进制[ASCII代码](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=ASCII%E4%BB%A3%E7%A0%81&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)是13, [十六进制](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E5%8D%81%E5%85%AD%E8%BF%9B%E5%88%B6&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)代码为0x0D; 
LF使用'\n'符号表示, [ASCII代码](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=ASCII%E4%BB%A3%E7%A0%81&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)是10, 十六制为0x0A.
所以Windows平台上换行在文本文件中是使用 0d 0a 两个字节表示, 而[UNIX](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=UNIX&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)和苹果平台上换行则是使用0a或0d一个字节表示.
一般[操作系统](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E6%93%8D%E4%BD%9C%E7%B3%BB%E7%BB%9F&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)上的运行库会自动决定文本文件的换行格式. 如一个程序在windows上运行就生成CR/LF换行格式的文本文件，而在Linux上运行就生成LF格式换行的文本文件.
在一个平台上使用另一种[换行符](https://www.baidu.com/s?wd=%E6%8D%A2%E8%A1%8C%E7%AC%A6&tn=44039180_cpr&fenlei=mv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YvPHDLP16snHRLuW6zmHPh0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EPHfknjfYnj0k)的文件文件可能会带来意想不到的问题, 特别是在编辑程序代码时. 有时候代码在编辑器中显示正常, 但在编辑时却会因为换行符问题而出错.
很多文本/代码编辑器带有换行符转换功能, 使用这个功能可以将文本文件中的换行符在不同格式单互换.
