我不是妈妈，没有怀抱黑孩子的体验。看样子，这个黑孩子很乖。“By the end of November, 622
 health care workers had been infected with the Ebola virus and 346 of them died, according to the World HealthOrganization.”至今，抗击埃博拉的医护人员死了一半，数字惊人。
美国”时代周刊”说：“For tireless acts of courage and mercy, for buying
 the world time to boost its defenses, for risking, for persisting, for sacrificing and saving, the Ebola fighters are TIME's 2014 Person of the Year,"这5个“for”使得埃博拉战士成为今年“时代周刊”的年度人物。自然，我国的抗击埃博拉战士也是“年度人物”。
愿这个黑孩子早日康复出院！
