EXAMPLE 1
A woman wishes to rent a house. If she lives
*x* miles from her work, her transportation cost will be *cx* dollars per year,while her rent will be 25*c*/(*x*+1) dollars per year. How far should she live from work to minimize her rent and transportation expenses?

Then* x*=4 or *x*=-6. We reject *x*= -6 because 0 ≤*x*.
The only interior critical point is
*x*= 4.
Step 3 we use the Direct Test.
At* x*= 0, *y*=
*c*·0 + 25
*c*/(0+ 1) = 25*c*.
At* x*= 4,
*y*= 4*c*+ 25
*c*/ (4+ 1) = 9*c*.
At* x*= 9,
*y*= 9*c*+ 25
*c*/ (9+ 1) = 11.5*c*.
CONCLUSION* y* has its minimum at
*x* = 4 miles.
So the woman should live four miles from work. (See figure 3.6.1.)
