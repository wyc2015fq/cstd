**我与小娜（****16****）：引力波的作用有多大？**
**      小娜知道，****2****月****11****日，****LIGO****官方网站发表文章“****LIGO DetectedGravitational Waves from Black holes****”，文中提及引力波的实际效果有多大（多么微弱），给我们国内引力波鼓噪者一点儿颜色看看。**
**      小娜知道，“****According to Einstein****’****s theory, the distance between the mirrors****（激光反射镜）****will change by aninfinitesimal amount when a gravitational wave passes by the detector. A changein the lengths of the arms****（****4****公里长的激光探测臂）**** smaller thanone-ten-thousandth the diameter of a proton(****质子，大约有****10-19 meter) can be detected.****“**
