如何评价某个interface的可用性，<[HumanComputer Interaction—Concepts, Methodologies, Tools, and Applications](http://www.amazon.com/Human-Computer-Interaction-Methodologies-Applications/dp/1605660523/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1328000480&sr=8-1)>中给出了三个指标。





|Effectiveness有效性|The extent to which the intended goals of use of the overall system are achieved.|Interface所属的system使用的实际效果如何？|
|----|----|----|
|Efficiency高效性|The resources that have to be expended to achieve the intended goals.|使interface所属的system达到预定效果，需要投入多少资源？|
|Satisfaction满意度|The extent to which the user finds the overall system acceptable.|用户对interface所属的system可在多大程度上接受？|


这儿提到了effectiveness和efficiency。其实我们更常用的是它们的形容词：effective和efficient。这两个词出现在各个领域中，长的很像，很容易弄混。其实从上面表格中的解释来看，effective表达的是使用的实际效果好，而efficient表达的是投入产出比高，完全是两个层面的衡量。](https://so.csdn.net/so/search/s.do?q=tools&t=blog)](https://so.csdn.net/so/search/s.do?q=resources&t=blog)](https://so.csdn.net/so/search/s.do?q=system&t=blog)](https://so.csdn.net/so/search/s.do?q=interface&t=blog)
