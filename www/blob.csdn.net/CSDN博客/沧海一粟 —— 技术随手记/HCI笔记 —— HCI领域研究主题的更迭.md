接触了一段时间HCI这个领域，感觉也真够乱的。

这本书<[Research Methods in Human-Computer Interaction](http://book.douban.com/subject/4489312/)>号称介绍了很多HCI的现状和研究方法，看看到底讲的怎么样。




先看看将近30年的HCI研究都在研究什么。




HCI领域研究主题的更迭
|**Year**|**Topic**|
|----|----|
|1980s|About how people interacted with simple (or not so simple) office automation programs, such as word-processing, database, and statistical software.The basics of interfaces, such as widgets, dialog boxes, and error messages, were the focus of much research.|
|Late 1980s and early 1990s|Area of usability engineering methods.|
|During the early to mid 1990s|New types of interfaces and communication, such as web pages, e-mail, instant messaging, and groupware.Communication.|
|Around 2004-2005|User-generated content that was shared, such as photos, videos, blogs, and wikis.Focus on collaboration, connections, emotion, and communication, and on whether people like an interface and want to use it.|
