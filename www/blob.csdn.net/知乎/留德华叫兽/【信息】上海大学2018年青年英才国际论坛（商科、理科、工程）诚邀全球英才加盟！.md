## **论坛简介**

## ***Introduction***

“上海大学青年英才国际论坛”定于2018年4月-5月举办，旨在为海内外青年英才搭建高水平的学术交流平台。诚邀海内外优秀青年学者来校进行学术交流和参观考察，助力青年学者的发展成长。同时加强对上海大学发展战略、师资队伍建设、科学研究等方面的深入了解，吸引海内外青年学者共同助力上海大学的高水平大学建设。

本届论坛设置四个分论坛，分别为：商科论坛、理科论坛、智能运载科学与工程论坛和文史与社会论坛。
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-3cce003cad912809880852c4d847a351_b.jpg)
*In order to provide a high calibre, international academic exchange platform for top scholars at home and abroad, SHU is launching the “International Forum for Young Scholars” initiative, to be hosted between April and May 2018.SHU sincerely invites exceptional young scholars from all over the world to join us for this opportunity to further their career and professional development, and knowledge of SHU.*

*Through this forum, we believe scholars will gain a deeper understanding of SHU’s developmental strategy, teaching environment, scientific research, and other aspects of life at SHU.*

*The forum includes four sub-forums, focusing on different fields: Business Studies, Natural Science, Intelligent Vehicle Science & Engineering, Humanities and Social Sciences. *

## **学校简介 **

## ***Profile of Shanghai University***

上海大学是上海市属、国家“211工程”重点建设的综合性大学，是国家教育部、国家国防科技工业局与上海市人民政府共建高校，国家“双一流”世界一流学科建设高校。

上海大学学科门类齐全，涵盖哲学、经济学、法学、文学、历史学、理学、工学、管理学、艺术学等学科门类。现设有25个学院、1个学部（筹）和2个校管系；设有71个本科专业，40个一级学科硕士学位授权点、171个二级学科硕士学位授权点、13种硕士专业学位类别（其中工程硕士含13个工程领域），20个一级学科博士学位授权点、79个二级学科博士学位授权点、18个自主增设二级学科博士学位授权点（含7个交叉学科博士点），19个博士后科研流动站。拥有4个国家重点学科、11个上海市一流学科，8个学科进入ESI国际学科排名全球前1%。在2017年QS全球建校50年以下大学排名中列世界前60名、中国大陆第1名，2017年USNews世界大学排名中列中国大陆第31名，2018年QS世界大学排名中列中国大陆第15名。

上海大学积极贯彻落实选才国际化要求，通畅人才集聚通道，紧紧围绕“一流基础学科建设计划”和“前沿重点学科培育计划”，充分利用中央和地方两个人才计划平台，面向海外公开招聘战略科学家和中青年杰出人才，共同推进高水平大学和一流学科建设。
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-aaa3e0f11ae271bee45fd8fbf6a86842_b.jpg)
*As a public university, selected for the state key construction “Project 211”, Shanghai University is jointly sponsored by the Ministry of Education, Shanghai Municipal People’s Government and the State Administration of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense. Shanghai University is listed among those statewide universities transforming their own preeminent disciplines into first-class programmes under China’s “Double-First Class” initiative.*

*Shanghai University has multiple disciplines, covering Philosophy, Economics, Law, Literature, History, Natural Science, Engineering, Management and Arts. Has over 25 colleges; 1 Academic Administrative Department; and two university-administrated faculties; 71 undergraduate programmes; 171 master degree programmes; 13 majors for master degree programmes; 79 doctoral degree programmes; 18 independently increased doctoral degree programmes in second-class disciplines (including seven interdisciplinary doctoral degree programmes) and 19 postdoctoral workstations. Moreover, SHU has 4 national-level key disciplines; 11 first-level disciplines in Shanghai, and 8 disciplines ranked in the top 1% in ESI. In 2017, it ranked among the world’s top 60 universities with a history of less than 50 years, topping the list of such universities in China’s mainland; was ranked 31st in 2017’s ‘US News Global University Rankings’ (within Mainland China), and 15th in 2018’s ‘QS World University Rankings’. (In Mainland China)*

*Shanghai University is open to the world’s academics and researchers, attracting first-rate scholars to join SHU through the “Talents Plan” platform, set up by China’s central government and Shanghai’s local government, focusing on the “First-Class Basic Discipline Construction Plan” and “Frontier and Key Discipline Development Plan”.*

## **邀请对象与学科领域  **

## ***Applicants and Disciplines***

**邀请对象**

***Requirements ***

1.年龄不超过45周岁，在海内外高水平大学或研究机构取得博士学位，并具有连续2年以上海外科研工作经历；

2.在所从事学科前沿领域取得突破性成果或具有良好的发展潜力；

3.有志于回国发展，立足上海，从事学术研究工作。

*1. Applicants should be aged below 45, have a PhD qualification from top universities or institutions;  plus more than two years research experience abroad.*

*2. Possess recognized, outstanding academic achievement and excellent research potential in the related field.*

*3. Aspire to return to China, be based in the Shanghai area, and engaged in academic research work.*


**学科领域**

***Disciplines***

**商科论坛：**管理科学与工程、应用经济学、工商管理、会计、金融、理论经济学、工业与系统工程、运筹学、统计学、心理学等学科或相关领域。

***Sub-Forum for Business Studies: **Management Science and Engineering, Applied Economics, Business Administration, Accounting, Finance, Theoretical Economics, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Operations Research, Statistics, Psychology and related disciplines.*

**理科论坛：**数学、物理、化学、力学、生命科学等。

***Sub-Forum for Natural Science: **Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Mechanics, Life Science, among others.*

**智能运载科学与工程论坛**：智能制造、运载与机电装备、人工智能、智能传感与感知、网络化系统及控制、海洋科技、先进高端制造关键材料、前沿新材料、功能材料与器件等。

***Sub-Forum for Intelligent Vehicle Science & Engineering: ** Intelligent manufacturing, Launch Vehicle and electromechanical equipment, artificial intelligence, intelligent sensing and perception, networked system and control, marine science and technology, advanced high-end manufacturing key materials, cutting-edge new materials, functional materials and devices,among others.*
