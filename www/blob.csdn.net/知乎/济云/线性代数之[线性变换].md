# 线性代数之[线性变换] - 知乎
# 

这篇继续线性代数部分的内容, 主要是关于线性变换的主要的定理与结论的总结.

这一部分是线性代数学中最精彩也相当重要的一部分, 很多重要的思想概念,以及代数方法在这一部分的知识里都有体现. 

## **线性变换的定义**

> **定义:**
线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 称为**线性变换**(一般我们使用花体拉丁字母 ![\mathscr{A},\mathscr{B},\cdots](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%2C%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%2C%5Ccdots) 代表V的变换, ![\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol{\alpha})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29) 代表元素 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 在变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 下的象)，如果对于V中任意元素 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha,\beta} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta%7D+) 和数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 中任意数 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) ，都有
![\begin{equation} \mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol{\alpha+\beta})=\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol{\alpha})+\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol{\beta}),~~\mathscr{A}(k\boldsymbol{\alpha})=k\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol{\alpha}) \end{equation} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bequation%7D+%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%7D%29%3D%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%2B%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D%29%2C~~%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28k%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%3Dk%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29+%5Cend%7Bequation%7D+)

以上定义与等式所表示的性质也说成**{线性变换保持向量的加法与数量乘法}**。(线性空间V中的{**恒等变换**}或称{**单位变换**} ![\mathscr{E}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BE%7D) ，即 ![\mathscr{E}(\boldsymbol{\alpha})=\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BE%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) ) , 以及{**零变换**} ![\mathscr{O}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BO%7D) ，即 ![\mathscr{O}\boldsymbol{\alpha=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BO%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D0%7D) 都是线性变换。线性变换具有一些简单的性质，参考北大《高等代数》Page275。)

## **线性变换的运算**

设 ![\mathscr{A},\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%2C%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) 是线性空间V的两个变换，它们的运算定义如下：

> **{乘积} **![\mathscr{AB}(\boldsymbol{\alpha})=\mathscr{A}(\mathscr{B}(\boldsymbol{\alpha}))~~~~\boldsymbol{\alpha}\in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BAB%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%3D%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%29~~~~%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%5Cin+V) 线性变换的乘法满足结合律，但一般不满足交换律。
**{加法} **![\mathscr{A+B}(\boldsymbol{\alpha})=\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol{\alpha})+\mathscr{B}(\boldsymbol{\alpha})~~~~\boldsymbol{\alpha}\in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%2BB%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%3D%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%2B%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29~~~~%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%5Cin+V) 线线性变换加法适合结合律与交换律。
**{数量乘法} **数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 中每个数都决定了一个数乘变换 ![\mathscr{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BK%7D) ，数量乘法定义为 ![k\mathscr{A}=\mathscr{KA}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%3D%5Cmathscr%7BKA%7D) ，其适用与结合律、交换律、分配律。

**线性空间**![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)**上的全体线性变换，对于如上定义的加法与数乘，也构成了数域**![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P)**上的一个线性空间。**

V的变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 称为{可逆的}，如果有 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的变换 ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) 存在，使 ![\mathscr{AB=BA=E}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BAB%3DBA%3DE%7D) 这时变换 ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) 称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的**逆变换.**

设 ![f(x)=a_{m}x^{m}+a_{m-1}x^{m-1}+\cdots+a_{0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Da_%7Bm%7Dx%5E%7Bm%7D%2Ba_%7Bm-1%7Dx%5E%7Bm-1%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2Ba_%7B0%7D) 是 ![P[x]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5Bx%5D) 中一多项式,  ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是V的一线性变换，我们定义 ![f(x)=a_{m}\mathscr{A}^{m}+a_{m-1}\mathscr{A}^{m-1}+\cdots+a_{0}\mathscr{E}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Da_%7Bm%7D%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5E%7Bm%7D%2Ba_%7Bm-1%7D%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5E%7Bm-1%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2Ba_%7B0%7D%5Cmathscr%7BE%7D) 显然 ![f(\mathscr{A})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%29) 是一线性变换，称为**线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的多项式**。同一个线性变换的多项式的乘法是可交换的。

## **线性变换的矩阵**

设 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D) 是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一组基， ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bn%7D%7D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中任意n个向量。存在唯一的线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 使 ![\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{i}})=\boldsymbol{\alpha_{i}},~~i=1,2,\cdots,n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bi%7D%7D%29%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Bi%7D%7D%2C~~i%3D1%2C2%2C%5Ccdots%2Cn)

> **定义:**
设 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一组基, ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中的一个线性变换。基向量的像可以被基线性表出：
![\begin{cases} \mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1}} ={ a }_{ 11 }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ 1 }+{ a }_{ 21 }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ 2 }+\cdots +{ a }_{ n1 }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ n } \\ \mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{2}} ={ a }_{ 12 }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ 1 }+{ a }_{ 21 }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ 2 }+\cdots +{ a }_{ n2 }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ n } \\ \cdots \cdots \cdots \\ \mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{n}} ={ a }_{ 1n }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ 1 }+{ a }_{ 21 }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ 2 }+\cdots +{ a }_{ nn }{ \boldsymbol\varepsilon }_{ n } \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%7D+%3D%7B+a+%7D_%7B+11+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+1+%7D%2B%7B+a+%7D_%7B+21+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+2+%7D%2B%5Ccdots+%2B%7B+a+%7D_%7B+n1+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+n+%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%7D+%3D%7B+a+%7D_%7B+12+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+1+%7D%2B%7B+a+%7D_%7B+21+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+2+%7D%2B%5Ccdots+%2B%7B+a+%7D_%7B+n2+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+n+%7D+%5C%5C+%5Ccdots+%5Ccdots+%5Ccdots+%5C%5C+%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D+%3D%7B+a+%7D_%7B+1n+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+1+%7D%2B%7B+a+%7D_%7B+21+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+2+%7D%2B%5Ccdots+%2B%7B+a+%7D_%7B+nn+%7D%7B+%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon+%7D_%7B+n+%7D+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D)
用矩阵形式表示，即
![\begin{equation} \mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol\varepsilon_{1},\boldsymbol\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\boldsymbol\varepsilon_{n})=(\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol\varepsilon_{1}),\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol\varepsilon_{2}),\mathscr{A}(\boldsymbol\varepsilon_{n}))=\boldsymbol{(\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n})A} \end{equation}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bequation%7D+%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%29%3D%28%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%29%2C%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%29%2C%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%29%29%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%28%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%29A%7D+%5Cend%7Bequation%7D)
其中 ![\boldsymbol{A}=\begin{pmatrix} { a }_{ 11 } & { a }_{ 12 } & \cdots & { a }_{ 1n } \\ { a }_{ 21 } & { a }_{ 22 } & \cdots & { a }_{ 2n } \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ { a }_{ n1 } & { a }_{ n2 } & \cdots & { a }_{ nn } \end{pmatrix}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+11+%7D+%26+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+12+%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+1n+%7D+%5C%5C+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+21+%7D+%26+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+22+%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+2n+%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots+%26+%26+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+n1+%7D+%26+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+n2+%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%7B+a+%7D_%7B+nn+%7D+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D) 矩阵 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D)**在基**![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D+)**下的矩阵**。

设 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D+) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一组基，在这组基下，每个线性变换按以上公式对应一个 ![n\times n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Ctimes+n) 矩阵。这个对应具有以下性质：
- 线性变换的和对应于矩阵的和；
- 线性变换的乘积对应于矩阵的乘积；
- 线性变换的数量乘积对应于矩阵的数量乘积；
- 可逆的线性变换于可逆矩阵对应，且逆变换对应于逆矩阵。

设线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 在基 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D+) 下的矩阵是 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) ，向量 ![\boldsymbol{\xi} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cxi%7D+) 在基 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D+) 下的坐标是 ![(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_%7B1%7D%2Cx_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2Cx_%7Bn%7D%29) ，则 ![\mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\xi}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cxi%7D) 在基 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D+) 下的坐标 ![(y_{1},y_{2},\cdots,y_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28y_%7B1%7D%2Cy_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2Cy_%7Bn%7D%29) 可以按以下公式计算

![\begin{pmatrix} { y }_{ 1 } \\ { y }_{ 2 } \\ \vdots \\ { y }_{ n } \end{pmatrix}=\boldsymbol{A}\begin{pmatrix} { x }_{ 1 } \\ { x }_{ 2 } \\ \vdots \\ { x }_{ n } \end{pmatrix}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+%7B+y+%7D_%7B+1+%7D+%5C%5C+%7B+y+%7D_%7B+2+%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+%7B+y+%7D_%7B+n+%7D+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+%7B+x+%7D_%7B+1+%7D+%5C%5C+%7B+x+%7D_%7B+2+%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+%7B+x+%7D_%7B+n+%7D+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D) .

> **定理:**
设线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 在两组基
![\begin{equation}\label{xxbhj1} \boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}}~~~~(1) \end{equation}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bequation%7D%5Clabel%7Bxxbhj1%7D+%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D~~~~%281%29+%5Cend%7Bequation%7D)
![\begin{equation}\label{xxbhj2} \boldsymbol{\eta_{1},\eta_{2},\cdots,\eta_{n}}~~~~(2) \end{equation}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bequation%7D%5Clabel%7Bxxbhj2%7D+%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Ceta_%7B1%7D%2C%5Ceta_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Ceta_%7Bn%7D%7D~~~~%282%29+%5Cend%7Bequation%7D)
下的矩阵分别为 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 和 ![\boldsymbol{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D) ，从基(1)到(2)的过渡矩阵是 ![\boldsymbol{X}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BX%7D) ，于是 ![\boldsymbol{B}=\boldsymbol{X^{-1}AX}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7BX%5E%7B-1%7DAX%7D) .

设 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 和 ![\boldsymbol{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上两个 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 级矩阵，如果可以找到数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上的可逆矩阵 ![\boldsymbol{X}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BX%7D) ，使得 ![\boldsymbol{B}=\boldsymbol{X^{-1}AX}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7BX%5E%7B-1%7DAX%7D) ，就说**![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 相似与 ![\boldsymbol{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D)**，记作 ![\boldsymbol{A}\sim\boldsymbol{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5Csim%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D) .

相似是矩阵之间的一种关系，**具有反身性，对称性，与传递性. **同时, 相似矩阵具有相同的秩与行列式，两个相似矩阵的各自的逆，k次方，以及它们的多项式也相似。

> **定理:**
线性变换在不同基下对应的矩阵是相似的；反之，相似矩阵可视为同一线性变换在不同基下所对应的矩阵。

## **特征值与特征向量**

> 设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个线性变换，如果对于数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 中一数 ![\lambda_{0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_%7B0%7D) ，存在一个非零向量 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon%7D) ，使得 ![\begin{equation}\label{tzz1} \mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon}=\lambda_{0}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon} \end{equation}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bequation%7D%5Clabel%7Btzz1%7D+%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon%7D%3D%5Clambda_%7B0%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon%7D+%5Cend%7Bequation%7D) , 那么 ![\lambda_{0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_%7B0%7D) 称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的一个{**特征值**}，而 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon%7D) 称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的一个{**特征向量**}。
设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上的 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 阶矩阵， ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是一个文字，矩阵行列式 ![det(\lambda\boldsymbol{E-A})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=det%28%5Clambda%5Cboldsymbol%7BE-A%7D%29)
![\begin{equation}\label{tzz2} \left|\lambda\boldsymbol{E-A}\right|=\begin{vmatrix} \lambda -{ a }_{ 11 } & -{ a }_{ 12 } & \cdots & -{ a }_{ 1n } \\ -{ a }_{ 21 } & -{ a }_{ 22 } & \cdots & -{ a }_{ 2n } \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ -{ a }_{ n1 } & -{ a }_{ n2 } & \cdots & \lambda -{ a }_{ nn } \end{vmatrix} \end{equation}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bequation%7D%5Clabel%7Btzz2%7D+%5Cleft%7C%5Clambda%5Cboldsymbol%7BE-A%7D%5Cright%7C%3D%5Cbegin%7Bvmatrix%7D+%5Clambda+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+11+%7D+%26+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+12+%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+1n+%7D+%5C%5C+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+21+%7D+%26+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+22+%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+2n+%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots+%26+%26+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+n1+%7D+%26+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+n2+%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%5Clambda+-%7B+a+%7D_%7B+nn+%7D+%5Cend%7Bvmatrix%7D+%5Cend%7Bequation%7D)
称为 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的{**特征多项式**}，这是数域上的一个 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 次多项式。

**特征子空间  **对于线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的一个特征值 ![\lambda_{0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_%7B0%7D) , 全部适合条件 ![\mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\alpha}=\lambda_{0}\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%3D%5Clambda_%7B0%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 的向量 ![\boldsymbol\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha) 所成的集合，再添上零向量，是V的一个子空间。称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的一个特征子空间，记为 ![V_{\lambda_{0}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B%5Clambda_%7B0%7D%7D) ，其维数恰是从属于 ![\lambda_{0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_%7B0%7D) 的线性无关的特征向量的最大个数。

若 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 为方阵 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的特征值，则：
- ![k\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Clambda) 为 ![k\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的特征值；
- ![\lambda^{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%7Bm%7D) 为 ![\boldsymbol{A}^{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5E%7Bm%7D) 的特征值；
- ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 与 ![\boldsymbol{A}^{T}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5E%7BT%7D) 的特征值相同；
- ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 可逆时， ![\lambda^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%7B-1%7D) 是 ![\boldsymbol{A}^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5E%7B-1%7D) 的特征值； ![\lambda^{-1}\left|\boldsymbol{A}\right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%7B-1%7D%5Cleft%7C%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5Cright%7C) 是 ![\boldsymbol{A}^{\ast}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5E%7B%5Cast%7D) 的特征值；
- ![f(\lambda)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Clambda%29) 为 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的多项式的特征值。

相似矩阵有相同的特征多项式，特征方程，特征值。

> **Hamilton-Caylay 定理**
设 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上一个 ![n\times n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Ctimes+n) 矩阵， ![f(\lambda)=\left|\lambda\boldsymbol{E-A}\right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Clambda%29%3D%5Cleft%7C%5Clambda%5Cboldsymbol%7BE-A%7D%5Cright%7C) 是 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的特征多项式，则![f(\boldsymbol{A})=\boldsymbol{A}^{n}-(a_{11}+a_{22}+\cdots+a_{nn})\boldsymbol{A}^{n-1}+\cdots+(-1)^{n}\left|\boldsymbol{A}\right|\boldsymbol{E}=\boldsymbol{O}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5E%7Bn%7D-%28a_%7B11%7D%2Ba_%7B22%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2Ba_%7Bnn%7D%29%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2B%28-1%29%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cleft%7C%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5Cright%7C%5Cboldsymbol%7BE%7D%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7BO%7D)

**推论: **设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是有限维空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的线性变换， ![f(\lambda)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Clambda%29) 是 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的特征多项式，那么 ![f(\mathscr{A})=\mathscr{O}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%29%3D%5Cmathscr%7BO%7D) .

**矩阵的迹  **方阵 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的主对角线元素的和称为 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的迹，记为 ![tr(\boldsymbol{A})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=tr%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29) .

设 ![\boldsymbol{A,B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%2CB%7D) 都是 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 阶方阵， ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 是一个数，则

1) ![\textrm{tr}(\boldsymbol{A}+\boldsymbol{B})=\textrm{tr}(\boldsymbol{A})+\textrm{tr}(\boldsymbol{B})；](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextrm%7Btr%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%2B%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D%29%3D%5Ctextrm%7Btr%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29%2B%5Ctextrm%7Btr%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D%29%EF%BC%9B)

2) ![\textrm{tr}(k\boldsymbol{A})=k\textrm{tr}(\boldsymbol{A})；](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextrm%7Btr%7D%28k%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29%3Dk%5Ctextrm%7Btr%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29%EF%BC%9B)

3) ![\textrm{tr}(\boldsymbol{A}\boldsymbol{B})=\textrm{tr}(\boldsymbol{B}\boldsymbol{A})；](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextrm%7Btr%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D%29%3D%5Ctextrm%7Btr%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BB%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29%EF%BC%9B)

4)相似矩阵具有相同的迹；

5)若 ![\lambda_{1},\lambda_{2},\cdots,\lambda_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_%7B1%7D%2C%5Clambda_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Clambda_%7Bn%7D) 是 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的全部特征值，则 ![\textrm{tr}(\boldsymbol{A})=\lambda_{1}+\lambda_{2}+\cdots+\lambda_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextrm%7Btr%7D%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29%3D%5Clambda_%7B1%7D%2B%5Clambda_%7B2%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2B%5Clambda_%7Bn%7D) .

## **线性变换的对角矩阵**

设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个线性变换， ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的矩阵可以在某组基下为对角矩阵的**充分必要条件**是 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 有 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个线性无关的特征向量。

> 属于不同特征值的特征向量是线性无关的。

如果 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间V中，线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的特征多项式在数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 中有 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个不同的根，即 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 有 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个不同的特征值，那么 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 在某组基下的矩阵是对角行。

在复数域上的线性空间中，如果线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的特征多项式没有重根，那么 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 在某组基下的矩阵是对角形。

> 如果 ![\lambda_{1}\cdots,\lambda_{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_%7B1%7D%5Ccdots%2C%5Clambda_%7Bk%7D) 是线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的不同的特征值，而 ![\alpha_{i1},\cdots,\alpha_{ir_{i}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha_%7Bi1%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bir_%7Bi%7D%7D) 是属于特征值 ![\lambda_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_%7Bi%7D) 的线性无关的特征向量, ![i=1,\cdots,k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i%3D1%2C%5Ccdots%2Ck) ，那么向量组 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{11},\cdots,\alpha_{1r_{1}},\cdots,\alpha_{k1},\cdots,\alpha_{kr_{k}}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B11%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7B1r_%7B1%7D%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bk1%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bkr_%7Bk%7D%7D%7D) 也线性无关。

## **线性变换的值与核**

设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是线性空间V的一个线性变换, ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的全体像组成的集合称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的**值域**，用 ![\mathscr{A}V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7DV) 表示. 所有被 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 变成零向量的向量组成的集合称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的**核**，用 ![\mathscr{A}^{-1}(\mathbf{0})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Cmathbf%7B0%7D%29) . 线性变换的值域与核都是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的子空间. ![\mathscr{A}V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7DV) 的维数称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的秩， ![\mathscr{A}^{\mathbf{0}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5E%7B%5Cmathbf%7B0%7D%7D) 的维数称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的**零度.**

设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的线性变换， ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D) 是V的一组基，在这组基下， ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的矩阵是 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) ，则
- ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的值域 ![\mathscr{A}V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7DV) 是由基像组生成的子空间即 ![\mathscr{A}V=L(\mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1}},\mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{2}},\cdots,\mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{n}})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7DV%3DL%28%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%7D%2C%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D%29)
- ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的秩等于 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的秩。

> 设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是n维线性空间V的线性变换。则 ![\mathscr{A}V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7DV) 的一组基的原像及 ![\mathscr{A}^{-1}(\mathbf{0})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Cmathbf%7B0%7D%29) 的一组基合起来就是V的一组基，由此还有 ![dim(\mathscr{A}V)+dim(\mathscr{A}^{-1}(\mathbf{0}))=n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dim%28%5Cmathscr%7BA%7DV%29%2Bdim%28%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Cmathbf%7B0%7D%29%29%3Dn) .

{推论} 对于有限维空间的线性变换，它是单射的充分必要条件为它是满射。

应该指出，虽然 ![dim(\mathscr{A}V)+dim(\mathscr{A}^{-1}(\mathbf{0})=n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dim%28%5Cmathscr%7BA%7DV%29%2Bdim%28%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Cmathbf%7B0%7D%29%3Dn) ，但是 ![\mathscr{A}V+\mathscr{A}^{-1}(\mathbf{0})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7DV%2B%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Cmathbf%7B0%7D%29) 并不一定是整个空间。

## **不变子空间**

设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个线性变换， ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 是V的子空间。如果 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 中的向量在 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 下的像仍在 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 中，即 ![\forall\mathbf{\xi}\in W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall%5Cmathbf%7B%5Cxi%7D%5Cin+W) ，有 ![\mathscr{A}\mathbf{\xi}\in W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cmathbf%7B%5Cxi%7D%5Cin+W) ，我们就称 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 是 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的不变子空间，简称 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) —子空间。 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 在不变子空间W上引起的变换记为 ![\mathscr{A}\mid W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cmid+W) ，称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 在不变子空间W上的限制。

我们有以下几个结论：
- 单个特征向量生成一维 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) —子空间；一维 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) —子空间必由某个特征向量生成；
- 线性变换 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的属于特征值 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 的特征子空间 ![V_{\lambda}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B%5Clambda%7D) 是 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) —子空间；
- ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) —子空间的和与交还是 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) —子空间；
- 若线性空间V的子空间W是由向量组 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha{2},\cdots,\alpha_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bn%7D%7D) 生成的，即 ![W=L(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha{2},\cdots,\alpha_{n}})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W%3DL%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bn%7D%7D%29) 则 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 是 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) —子空间 ![\Longleftrightarrow \mathscr{A}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{i}}\in W~~(i=1,2,\cdots,n)；](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLongleftrightarrow+%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Bi%7D%7D%5Cin+W~~%28i%3D1%2C2%2C%5Ccdots%2Cn%29%EF%BC%9B)

不变子空间与线性变换矩阵化简之间的关系：

## **Jordan标准形介绍**

定义:

形式为 ![\boldsymbol{J}(\lambda ,t)={ \begin{pmatrix} \lambda & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & \lambda & \cdots & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\ 0 & 0 & \cdots & 1 & \lambda & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 1 & \lambda \end{pmatrix} }_{ t\times t }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BJ%7D%28%5Clambda+%2Ct%29%3D%7B+%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+%5Clambda+%26+0+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+0+%26+0+%26+0+%5C%5C+1+%26+%5Clambda+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+0+%26+0+%26+0+%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots+%26+%26+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+0+%26+0+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+1+%26+%5Clambda+%26+0+%5C%5C+0+%26+0+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+0+%26+1+%26+%5Clambda+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D+%7D_%7B+t%5Ctimes+t+%7D) 的矩阵称为{若尔当(Jordan)块}，其中 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是复数，由若干个若尔当块组成的准对角矩阵称为{若尔当形矩阵}，其一般形状如

![\begin{equation}\label{zdjjz} \begin{pmatrix} \boldsymbol{A}_{1} & & & \\ & \boldsymbol{A}_{2} & & \\ & & \ddots & \\ & & & \boldsymbol{A}_{s} \end{pmatrix} \end{equation}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bequation%7D%5Clabel%7Bzdjjz%7D+%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D_%7B1%7D+%26+%26+%26+%5C%5C+%26+%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D_%7B2%7D+%26+%26+%5C%5C+%26+%26+%5Cddots+%26+%5C%5C+%26+%26+%26+%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D_%7Bs%7D+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D+%5Cend%7Bequation%7D)

其中 ![\boldsymbol{A}_{i}={ \begin{pmatrix} \lambda & & & & \\ 1 & \lambda & & & \\ & \ddots & \ddots & & \\ & & 1 & \lambda & \\ & & & 1 & \lambda \end{pmatrix} }_{ { k }_{ 1 }\times { k }_{ 1 } }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D_%7Bi%7D%3D%7B+%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+%5Clambda+%26+%26+%26+%26+%5C%5C+1+%26+%5Clambda+%26+%26+%26+%5C%5C+%26+%5Cddots+%26+%5Cddots+%26+%26+%5C%5C+%26+%26+1+%26+%5Clambda+%26+%5C%5C+%26+%26+%26+1+%26+%5Clambda+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D+%7D_%7B+%7B+k+%7D_%7B+1+%7D%5Ctimes+%7B+k+%7D_%7B+1+%7D+%7D)

并且 ![\lambda_{1},\lambda_{2},\cdots,\lambda_{s}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_%7B1%7D%2C%5Clambda_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Clambda_%7Bs%7D) 中有一些可以相等。

> **引理**
![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上线性变换 ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) 满足 ![\mathscr{B}^{k}=\mathscr{O}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5E%7Bk%7D%3D%5Cmathscr%7BO%7D) ，k是某正整数，就称 ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) 为V上的幂零变换。对幂零线性变换 ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) ， ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中必有下列形式的一组元素作为基
![\begin{equation}\label{mlj} \begin{matrix} \boldsymbol\alpha_{1} & \boldsymbol\alpha_{2} & \cdots & \boldsymbol\alpha_{s} \\ \mathscr{B}\boldsymbol\alpha_{1} & \mathscr{B}\boldsymbol\alpha_{2} & \cdots & \mathscr{B}\boldsymbol\alpha_{s} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ \mathscr{B}^{k_{1}-1}\boldsymbol\alpha_{1} & \mathscr{B}^{k_{2}-1}\boldsymbol\alpha_{2} & \cdots & \mathscr{B}^{k_{s}-1}\boldsymbol\alpha_{s} \\ \mathscr{B}^{k_{1}}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}=0} & \mathscr{B}^{k_{2}}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{2}=0} & \cdots & \mathscr{B}^{k_{s}}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{s}=0} \end{matrix} \end{equation}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bequation%7D%5Clabel%7Bmlj%7D+%5Cbegin%7Bmatrix%7D+%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7B1%7D+%26+%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7B2%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7Bs%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7B1%7D+%26+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7B2%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7Bs%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots+%26+%26+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5E%7Bk_%7B1%7D-1%7D%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7B1%7D+%26+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5E%7Bk_%7B2%7D-1%7D%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7B2%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5E%7Bk_%7Bs%7D-1%7D%5Cboldsymbol%5Calpha_%7Bs%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5E%7Bk_%7B1%7D%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%3D0%7D+%26+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5E%7Bk_%7B2%7D%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%3D0%7D+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5E%7Bk_%7Bs%7D%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Bs%7D%3D0%7D+%5Cend%7Bmatrix%7D+%5Cend%7Bequation%7D)
于是 ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) 在这组基下的矩阵为一准对角形.

设 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 是复数域上线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个线性变换，则在 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中必定存在一组基，使 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 在这组基下的矩阵是若尔当形矩阵，称为 ![\mathscr{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BA%7D) 的若尔当标准形。

每个 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 级复矩阵 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 都与一个若尔当形矩阵相似。

## **最小多项式**

根据 Hamilton-Caylay 定理，任给数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上一个 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 级矩阵 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) ，总可以找到数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上的一个多项式 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) ，使 ![f(\boldsymbol{A})=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29%3D0) ，如果多项式 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 使 ![f(\boldsymbol{A})=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D%29%3D0) ，则称 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 以 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 为根。而以 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 为根的多项式中，次数最低的首项系数为 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 的多项式称为 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的{**最小多项式**}。

最小多项式的一些基本性质：
- **引理1**  矩阵 ![\boldsymbol{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7BA%7D) 的最小多项式是唯一的；
