## **导数定义与性质**

在此先给出定义在 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 的子集上的可微函数的定义, 并给出计算导数的基本法则.

> **[导数]**
	令 ![D\subset \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%5Csubset+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) , 称函数 ![f:D\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AD%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 在点 ![x_0\in D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%5Cin+D) 可微, 若满足:
![f'(x_0):= \lim_{\mbox{$\begin{array}{c} x \rightarrow x_0 \\ x \in D\setminus \{x_0\}\end{array}$}} \frac{f(x)-f(x_0)}{x-x_0}= \lim_{\mbox{$\begin{array}{c} h \rightarrow 0\\ h \neq 0\\ x_0 +h\in D\end{array}$}} \frac{f(x_0 +h)-f(x_0)}{h}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x_0%29%3A%3D+%5Clim_%7B%5Cmbox%7B%24%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7D+x+%5Crightarrow+x_0+%5C%5C+x+%5Cin+D%5Csetminus+%5C%7Bx_0%5C%7D%5Cend%7Barray%7D%24%7D%7D+%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%29-f%28x_0%29%7D%7Bx-x_0%7D%3D+%5Clim_%7B%5Cmbox%7B%24%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7D+h+%5Crightarrow+0%5C%5C+h+%5Cneq+0%5C%5C+x_0+%2Bh%5Cin+D%5Cend%7Barray%7D%24%7D%7D+%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x_0+%2Bh%29-f%28x_0%29%7D%7Bh%7D)
	存在. 有时也用符号 ![\frac{df}{dx}(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdf%7D%7Bdx%7D%28x_0%29) 替代 ![f'(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x_0%29) , 称为函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在点 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处的**导数(微商).**
	若 ![\forall x \in D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x+%5Cin+D) ,  ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 皆可微, 则称函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 上是**可微的**.

上面的定义中可以考虑单侧极限, 进而得到单侧导数的定义.函数在一点未必有定义但可以在这一点处有导数.

导数的几何意义是明显的.考虑函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 附近的图像, 经过图像上的两点 ![(x_0,f(x_0)),(x,f(x))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_0%2Cf%28x_0%29%29%2C%28x%2Cf%28x%29%29) , 可以做一条割线.  ![x\rightarrow x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Crightarrow+x_0) 时, 若 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在点 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处可导 , 则这条割线的极限位置, 就是 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处的切线,斜率为 ![f'(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x_0%29) .
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-fdb63617edab41d0de3cbe7d60821eaf_b.jpg)
其实切线可以视为函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处的**线性近似**,从这里,我们引入微分学的主要工具之一, **有限增量公式**, 也就是**微分**的概念.

> **[微分]**
	设 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 附近有定义的函数,如果存在常数 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 使得
![f(x)=f(x_0)+A(x-x_0)+o(x-x_0),\quad x\rightarrow x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Df%28x_0%29%2BA%28x-x_0%29%2Bo%28x-x_0%29%2C%5Cquad+x%5Crightarrow+x_0)
其中 ![o(x-x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=o%28x-x_0%29) 为无穷小量.则称 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处可微,  ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处的线性映射 ![x\longmapsto Ax](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Clongmapsto+Ax) 称为 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处的**微分**, 记为 ![df(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df%28x_0%29) .

导数和微分的关系,表现为有限增量公式,即

> **[有限增量公式]**
	设 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 附近有定义, ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处可导,当且仅当 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处可微. 并且有 ![A=f'(x_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3Df%27%28x_0%29) , 即
![\Delta y= f(x)-f(x_0)= f'(x_0)\Delta x +o(\Delta),\quad x\rightarrow x_0.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+y%3D+f%28x%29-f%28x_0%29%3D+f%27%28x_0%29%5CDelta+x+%2Bo%28%5CDelta%29%2C%5Cquad+x%5Crightarrow+x_0.)
上式也可以写成另一个形式
![\Delta y = f'(x_0)\Delta x+\omega(x)\Delta x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+y+%3D+f%27%28x_0%29%5CDelta+x%2B%5Comega%28x%29%5CDelta+x)
其中函数 ![\omega(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%28x%29) 满足 ![\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow x_0}\omega(x)=\omega(x_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim%5Climits_%7Bx%5Crightarrow+x_0%7D%5Comega%28x%29%3D%5Comega%28x_0%29%3D0) .

由此可立得

> 	若函数 ![f:D\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AD%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 在 ![x_0\in D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%5Cin+D) 上可微,则函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 点连续.

以上命题得逆命题并不成立. 在整个定义域上处处连续单数处处不可微的一个例子是 Weiestrass 给出的函数.

由极限的运算和导数的定义, 容易得到导数的运算性质:

设函数 ![f, g: D\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2C+g%3A+D%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) , 均在点 ![x\in D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+D) 可微, 则 ![f+g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Bg) , ![fg](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=fg) , ![f/g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Fg) 均在 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 点可微, 且
- ![(f+g)'(x)=f'(x)+g'(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2Bg%29%27%28x%29%3Df%27%28x%29%2Bg%27%28x%29)
- ![(fg)'(x)=f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28fg%29%27%28x%29%3Df%27%28x%29g%28x%29%2Bf%28x%29g%27%28x%29)
- 若 ![g(x)\neq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29%5Cneq+0) , ![\left(\frac{f}{g}\right)' (x) = \frac{g(x)f'(x)-g'(x)f(x)}{g^2(x)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7Bf%7D%7Bg%7D%5Cright%29%27+%28x%29+%3D+%5Cfrac%7Bg%28x%29f%27%28x%29-g%27%28x%29f%28x%29%7D%7Bg%5E2%28x%29%7D)

下面是重要的**链式法则**. 用导数的定义容易证明.

> **[链式法则]**
	设 ![f:D_1 \rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AD_1+%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 在点 ![x\in D_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+D_1) 可微, 同时 ![f(D_1)\subset D_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28D_1%29%5Csubset+D_2) . 再设 ![g:D_2 \rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%3AD_2+%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ,在点 ![y:=f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3A%3Df%28x%29) 可微. 那么两个函数的复合函数 ![g\circ f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%5Ccirc+f) 在 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 可微.且
![(g\circ f)'=g'(f(x))f'(x).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28g%5Ccirc+f%29%27%3Dg%27%28f%28x%29%29f%27%28x%29.)

接下来是另一个重要的结果, 使得我们在知道一个函数的导数的情况下, 可以计算这个函数的反函数的导数. 当然, 反函数的存在是有条件的, 为此先给出函数单调的定义.

> **[单调]**
	我们称函数 ![f:I\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AI%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ( ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 为区间)**单调增加(减少)**, 当 ![\forall x_1, x_2 \in I, x_1 < x_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x_1%2C+x_2+%5Cin+I%2C+x_1+%3C+x_2) 时, 有 ![f(x_1)\leqslant(\geqslant)f(x_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x_1%29%5Cleqslant%28%5Cgeqslant%29f%28x_2%29) . 称函数 ![f:I\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AI%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ( ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 为区间)**严格单调增加(减少)**, 当 ![\forall x_1, x_2 \in I, x_1 < x_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x_1%2C+x_2+%5Cin+I%2C+x_1+%3C+x_2) 时, 有 ![f(x_1)< (>) f(x_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x_1%29%3C+%28%3E%29+f%28x_2%29) .

于是关于反函数存在及求导规则, 有以下结果:

> **[反函数求导法则]**
	令 ![I\subset \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%5Csubset+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 为一开区间, 而函数 ![f:I\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AI%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是严格单调递增(递减)的, 那么, 存在连续函数 ![\varphi:f(I)\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%3Af%28I%29%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) , 其满足 ![\forall x \in I, \varphi\circ f(x)=x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x+%5Cin+I%2C+%5Cvarphi%5Ccirc+f%28x%29%3Dx) , ![\forall y \in f(I), f\circ \varphi(y)=y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+y+%5Cin+f%28I%29%2C+f%5Ccirc+%5Cvarphi%28y%29%3Dy) . ![\varphi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi) 称之为**函数$f$在区间$I$上的反函数**, 用符号 ![f^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D) 表示. 若函数 ![f:I\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AI%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)  在 ![x \in I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x+%5Cin+I) 上可微, 并且 ![f'(x)\neq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x%29%5Cneq+0) , 那么 ![\varphi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi) 在点 ![y:=f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3A%3Df%28x%29) 可微, 并且
![\varphi'(y)=\frac{1}{f'(x)}=\frac{1}{f'(\varphi(y))}.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%27%28y%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bf%27%28x%29%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bf%27%28%5Cvarphi%28y%29%29%7D.)

接下来给出高阶导数的定义.

> 	如果函数 ![f:D\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AD%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是可微的, 而其导函数 ![f'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27) 在 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 上又是可微的, 则记 ![f'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27) 的导函数数为 ![f''](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%27) , 称为 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的二阶导数. 如此继续定义, 可以得到 ![f,f'',f^{(3)},\cdots,f^{(n)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cf%27%27%2Cf%5E%7B%283%29%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2Cf%5E%7B%28n%29%7D)
	这一系列函数, 每一个都是前一个的导函数. ![f^{(n)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B%28n%29%7D) 称为函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 阶导函数. 也可以用符号 ![\frac{d^{n}f}{dx^{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E%7Bn%7Df%7D%7Bdx%5E%7Bn%7D%7D) 来表示.

另外我们用符号 ![C^k(D,\mathbb{R})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5Ek%28D%2C%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%29) 表示由 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 上的 k-重连续可微的实函数构成的向量空间. 定义 ![C^\infty(D,\mathbb{R})=C^\infty(D):= \bigcap_{k\in\mathbb{N}}C^k(D)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28D%2C%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%29%3DC%5E%5Cinfty%28D%29%3A%3D+%5Cbigcap_%7Bk%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%7DC%5Ek%28D%29) 为 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 上无限阶连续可微的实函数构成的向量空间.

对于高阶导数的计算, 一个主要的工具就是**莱布尼茨(Leibniz)公式**:

![\begin{alignat*}{2} (uv)^{(n)} & = & C_{n}^{0}u^{0}v^{n}+C_{n}^{1}u'v^{(n-1)}+C_{n}^{2}u''v^{(n-2)}+\cdots+C_{n}^{n}u^{(n)}v^{(0)}\\ \quad & = & \sum_{k=0}^{n}C_{n}^{k}u^{(k)}v^{(n-k)}.\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad \end{alignat*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balignat%2A%7D%7B2%7D+%28uv%29%5E%7B%28n%29%7D+%26+%3D+%26+C_%7Bn%7D%5E%7B0%7Du%5E%7B0%7Dv%5E%7Bn%7D%2BC_%7Bn%7D%5E%7B1%7Du%27v%5E%7B%28n-1%29%7D%2BC_%7Bn%7D%5E%7B2%7Du%27%27v%5E%7B%28n-2%29%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2BC_%7Bn%7D%5E%7Bn%7Du%5E%7B%28n%29%7Dv%5E%7B%280%29%7D%5C%5C+%5Cquad+%26+%3D+%26+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7DC_%7Bn%7D%5E%7Bk%7Du%5E%7B%28k%29%7Dv%5E%7B%28n-k%29%7D.%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad+%5Cend%7Balignat%2A%7D)

这里举一个数学分析中的重要而且有趣的例子. 我们知道函数 ![e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex) 是 ![C^\infty(\mathbb{R})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%29) 的, 可以借此构造一个不那么平凡的 ![C^\infty(\mathbb{R})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%29) 函数的例子,其中的辅助函数,在解决一些问题时有用.

定义函数 ![f(x):=\begin{cases} e^{-\frac{1}{x^2}},\quad x\neq 0, \\ 0,\quad x=0, \end{cases}.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3A%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+e%5E%7B-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%5E2%7D%7D%2C%5Cquad+x%5Cneq+0%2C+%5C%5C+0%2C%5Cquad+x%3D0%2C+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D.)

	显然这是一个 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的非常值函数.而事实上利用链式法则和数学归纳法,可以证明这个函数在 ![x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0) 处的任意阶导数都等于 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) .

## **可微函数性质**

关于可微函数典型的一些性质, 我们从微分中值定理开始介绍, 并给出一些应用. 还有重要的泰勒公式. 这些内容构成了微分学的核心部分.

**中值定理**

先引入局部极大值(极小值)的定义

> 	设 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是定义在度量空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的实值函数, 称 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在点 ![p\in X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+X) 取得**局部极大值(极小值)**, 若 ![\exists \delta >0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+%5Cdelta+%3E0) , 当 ![d(p,q)<\delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28p%2Cq%29%3C%5Cdelta) 而且 ![q\in X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=q%5Cin+X) 时, 有 ![f(q)\leqslant (\geqslant)f(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28q%29%5Cleqslant+%28%5Cgeqslant%29f%28p%29) .

> **[Taylor]**
	设函数 ![f:(a,b)\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3A%28a%2Cb%29%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 在 ![x_0\in (a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%5Cin+%28a%2Cb%29) 取得局部极大(小)值, 而 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处可微, 则 ![f'(x_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x_0%29%3D0) .

我们常见的中值定理有三个, Rolle, Lagrange, Cauchy,这三个中值定理一个比一个更一般. 

> **[Cauchy 中值定理]**

若在上面的定理中取 ![g(x)=x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29%3Dx) , 则得到:

> **[Lagrange 中值定理]**
	连续实函数 ![f:[a,b]\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3A%5Ba%2Cb%5D%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) , 其在 ![(a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29) 中可微(端点不要求), 则 ![\exists \xi \in (a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+%5Cxi+%5Cin+%28a%2Cb%29) , 使得 ![\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}=f'(\xi).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bf%28b%29-f%28a%29%7D%7Bb-a%7D%3Df%27%28%5Cxi%29.)

> **[Rolle 中值定理]**
	连续实函数 ![f:[a,b]\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3A%5Ba%2Cb%5D%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) , 它们在 ![(a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29) 中可微(端点不要求), 同时 ![f(a)=f(b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28a%29%3Df%28b%29) , 则 ![\exists \xi \in (a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+%5Cxi+%5Cin+%28a%2Cb%29) , 使得 ![f'(\xi)=0.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28%5Cxi%29%3D0.)

一个函数可以有处处存在(但可能在某些点间断的)导函数, 但**并非每个函数都是某一个函数的导函数**.作为中值定理的一个应用, 我们可以得到关于导函数的两个重要的基本定理.

> **[达布(Darboux)定理]**
	设 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在区间 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 上可微, 则 ![f'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27) 具有介值性.

以及

> **[导函数极限定理]**
	设函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在点 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 的某邻域 ![U(a)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%28a%29) 连续, 在 ![U(a)\setminus\{a\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%28a%29%5Csetminus%5C%7Ba%5C%7D) 内可导. 若导函数 ![f'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27) 在 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 存在极限,则函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 点可导, 且 ![f'(a)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28a%29) 在 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 连续.

由以上定理推论得知: 若函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在区间 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 上可微, 那么**其导函数**![f'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27)**不存在第一类间断点**.

**泰勒公式**

前面谈到研究函数性态的工具, 函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在定义域上一点 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处可微, 那么在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 的近旁可以用一个线性函数去近似. 若一个函数高阶可微, 在局部我们可以考虑用多项式来逼近, 以方便研究函数在局部的性态.这就是可以称为**微分学顶峰**的**Taylor 展开公式(定理)**.

> **[(带Peano余项的)Taylor 公式]**
	若函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 阶可微,则有
![f(x)= \sum_{k=0}^{n}\frac{1}{k!}f^{(k)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^k +o((x-x_0)^n), \quad (x\rightarrow x_0).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%21%7Df%5E%7B%28k%29%7D%28x_0%29%28x-x_0%29%5Ek+%2Bo%28%28x-x_0%29%5En%29%2C+%5Cquad+%28x%5Crightarrow+x_0%29.)
其中
![R_n(x):= f(x)-\sum_{k=0}^{n}f^{(k)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^k = o((x-x_0)^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R_n%28x%29%3A%3D+f%28x%29-%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df%5E%7B%28k%29%7D%28x_0%29%28x-x_0%29%5Ek+%3D+o%28%28x-x_0%29%5En%29)
称为**Peano余项.**

上述定理中, 余项 ![R_n(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R_n%28x%29) 是一个无穷小量. 实际应用中,我们往往需要对逼近的误差进行更精确的估计, 这要求函数拥有更强一点的条件. 以下结果又称为**Taylor 中值定理**.

> **[Taylor 中值定理]**
	设函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![(a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29) 上有直到 ![n+1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B1) 阶的导数, 对于 ![x_0, x\in(a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%2C+x%5Cin%28a%2Cb%29) , 存在点 ![\xi,\zeta\in(x_0,x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi%2C%5Czeta%5Cin%28x_0%2Cx%29) (或者 ![\xi,\zeta\in(x_0,x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi%2C%5Czeta%5Cin%28x_0%2Cx%29) , 这取决于 ![x_0, x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%2C+x) 的大小关系), 有
![f(x)= \sum_{k=0}^{n}\frac{1}{k!}f^{(k)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^k + \frac{1}{(n+1)!}f^{(n+1)}(\xi)(x-x_0)^{n+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%21%7Df%5E%7B%28k%29%7D%28x_0%29%28x-x_0%29%5Ek+%2B+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%28n%2B1%29%21%7Df%5E%7B%28n%2B1%29%7D%28%5Cxi%29%28x-x_0%29%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D)
以及
![f(x)= \sum_{k=0}^{n}\frac{1}{k!}f^{(k)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^k + \frac{1}{n!}f^{(n+1)}(\zeta)(x-\zeta)^n(x-x_0).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%21%7Df%5E%7B%28k%29%7D%28x_0%29%28x-x_0%29%5Ek+%2B+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%21%7Df%5E%7B%28n%2B1%29%7D%28%5Czeta%29%28x-%5Czeta%29%5En%28x-x_0%29.)
这两个展开式的余项分别称为 **Lagrange 余项**与 **Cauchy 余项**.

## **应用:关于微分方程的一些结论**

这一节的主题是微分学, 作为这一主题的延伸应用, 最后我们谈论一点微分方程. 

**微分方程**是数学方程的一种,用来描述某一类函数与其导数之间的关系. **微分方程的解是一个符合方程的函数**.(在初等数学的代数方程里, 其解是常数值.) 微分方程的应用十分广泛, 可以解决许多与导数有关的问题, 比如: 物理中许多涉及变力的运动学、动力学问题;如空气的阻力为速度函数的落体运动等问题. 此外, 微分方程在化学、工程学、经济学和人口统计等领域都有应用.

下面给出几个关于微分方程的结论.

我们用 ![a\leqslant x\leqslant b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cleqslant+x%5Cleqslant+b) , ![\alpha\leqslant y \leqslant\beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Cleqslant+y+%5Cleqslant%5Cbeta) 表示平面上的矩形区域 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) ,设 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 定义在 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 上的实函数. 所谓**初值问题**

![y'=\phi(x,y),\quad y(a)=c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%27%3D%5Cphi%28x%2Cy%29%2C%5Cquad+y%28a%29%3Dc)

的解,是 ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上的一个函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ,其满足 ![f(a)=c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28a%29%3Dc)  , ![\alpha\leqslant f(x)\leqslant \beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Cleqslant+f%28x%29%5Cleqslant+%5Cbeta) ,并且 , 设 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 为定义在 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 上的实函数. 所谓

![f'(x)=\phi(x, f(x)),\quad (a\leqslant x\leqslant b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x%29%3D%5Cphi%28x%2C+f%28x%29%29%2C%5Cquad+%28a%5Cleqslant+x%5Cleqslant+b%29) .

> 	设函数 ![\phi:\mathbb{R}\rightarrow\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%3A%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Crightarrow%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是 Lipschitz连续的, 那么初值问题最多只有一个解.

**证明: **为证明这个命题,我们先证明一个引理:

**       引理: **可微函数 ![F:[a,b]\rightarrow \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%3A%5Ba%2Cb%5D%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 满足![\forall x\in[a,b],|F'(x)|\leqslant A|F(x)|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x%5Cin%5Ba%2Cb%5D%2C%7CF%27%28x%29%7C%5Cleqslant+A%7CF%28x%29%7C), $A>0$为一个常数. 若 ![\exists x_0\in [a,b], F(x_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+x_0%5Cin+%5Ba%2Cb%5D%2C+F%28x_0%29%3D0) , 则在 ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上 ![F\equiv 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Cequiv+0) .

	定义 ![\delta:=\frac{1}{2A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3A%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2A%7D) . 在 ![I:=[a,b]\cap[x_0-\delta,x_0+\delta]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3A%3D%5Ba%2Cb%5D%5Ccap%5Bx_0-%5Cdelta%2Cx_0%2B%5Cdelta%5D) 中取点 ![x_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_1) ,满足 ![|F(x_1)|=\sup_{x\in I}|F(x)|.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7CF%28x_1%29%7C%3D%5Csup_%7Bx%5Cin+I%7D%7CF%28x%29%7C.)

	由 ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) 的连续性, 这样的 ![x_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_1) 是取得到的.由推论(1),有

![\begin{multline*} |F(x_1)|=|F(x_1)-F(x_0)|\leqslant |x_1-x_0|\sup_{\xi\in I}|F'(x)|\\ \leqslant A\sup_{\xi\in I}|F(x)||x_1-x_0|\leqslant A\delta|F(x_1)|=\frac{1}{2}|F(x_1)|. \end{multline*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bmultline%2A%7D+%7CF%28x_1%29%7C%3D%7CF%28x_1%29-F%28x_0%29%7C%5Cleqslant+%7Cx_1-x_0%7C%5Csup_%7B%5Cxi%5Cin+I%7D%7CF%27%28x%29%7C%5C%5C+%5Cleqslant+A%5Csup_%7B%5Cxi%5Cin+I%7D%7CF%28x%29%7C%7Cx_1-x_0%7C%5Cleqslant+A%5Cdelta%7CF%28x_1%29%7C%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%7CF%28x_1%29%7C.+%5Cend%7Bmultline%2A%7D)

	这说明 ![F(x_1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%28x_1%29%3D0) , 进而有 ![F(x)=0~(\forall x\in I)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%28x%29%3D0~%28%5Cforall+x%5Cin+I%29)

	于是我们便证明了这样一件事: 存在 ![\delta>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3E0) , 当 ![F(x_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%28x_0%29%3D0) , 则 ![\forall x\in [a,b]\cap[x_0-\delta,x_0+\delta], F(x)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x%5Cin+%5Ba%2Cb%5D%5Ccap%5Bx_0-%5Cdelta%2Cx_0%2B%5Cdelta%5D%2C+F%28x%29%3D0) .

	如果函数 ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) 在 ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 上不完全等于 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) ,那么总是存在或最小的 ![\xi_1\in(a,b],F(\xi_1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi_1%5Cin%28a%2Cb%5D%2CF%28%5Cxi_1%29%3D0) , 或是最大的 ![\xi_2\in[a,b), F(\xi_2)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi_2%5Cin%5Ba%2Cb%29%2C+F%28%5Cxi_2%29%3D0) . 而这与我们前面证明的事情是矛盾的.于是引理便得到了证实.

	现在假设 ![f_1,f_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_1%2Cf_2) 均为初值问题的解, 满足 ![f_1(a)=f_2(a)=c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_1%28a%29%3Df_2%28a%29%3Dc) . 那么函数 ![F=f_1-f_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%3Df_1-f_2) 满足 ![F(a)=0.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%28a%29%3D0.)

	由 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 为 Lipschitz 连续, 故存在一个常数 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) ,满足

![|F'(x)|=|\phi(x,f_1(x))-\phi(x,f_2(x))|\leqslant A|f_1(x)-f_2(x)|.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7CF%27%28x%29%7C%3D%7C%5Cphi%28x%2Cf_1%28x%29%29-%5Cphi%28x%2Cf_2%28x%29%29%7C%5Cleqslant+A%7Cf_1%28x%29-f_2%28x%29%7C.)

	由以上引理, 显然 ![F\equiv 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Cequiv+0) , 也就是说 ![f_1=f_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_1%3Df_2) . 命题得证. ![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)

现在,为了对常微分方程的解的增长"形态"有一个定性的了解. 考察以下微分不等式:

![f'(x)\leqslant \gamma f(x)^\beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x%29%5Cleqslant+%5Cgamma+f%28x%29%5E%5Cbeta)

其中 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 为一个正函数, ![\gamma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cgamma)为某一常数,而 ![0\leqslant\beta<1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleqslant%5Cbeta%3C1) .

设对于 ![x\geqslant a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cgeqslant+a) , 函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 满足以上不等式. 令 ![g:=f^{1-\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%3A%3Df%5E%7B1-%5Cbeta%7D) , 则有

![g'(x)=(1-\beta) f^{-\beta}(x)f'(x)\leqslant \gamma(1-\beta).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%27%28x%29%3D%281-%5Cbeta%29+f%5E%7B-%5Cbeta%7D%28x%29f%27%28x%29%5Cleqslant+%5Cgamma%281-%5Cbeta%29.)

设 ![g(a)=c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28a%29%3Dc) , 那么结合前面的推论(3), 容易验证,对于 ![x\geqslant a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cgeqslant+a) , 有

![g(x)\leqslant\gamma(1-\beta)(x-a)+c.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29%5Cleqslant%5Cgamma%281-%5Cbeta%29%28x-a%29%2Bc.)

那么对于函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ,就有

![f(x)\leqslant(\gamma(1-\beta)(x-a)+c)^\frac{1}{1-\beta}.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%5Cleqslant%28%5Cgamma%281-%5Cbeta%29%28x-a%29%2Bc%29%5E%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1-%5Cbeta%7D.)

这里可以看到, ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 渐进增长的趋势类似于幂函数 ![x^{\frac{1}{1-\beta}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1-%5Cbeta%7D%7D) .

具体的可以总结为以下几类:
- ![f'=\gamma f.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%3D%5Cgamma+f.)  这种情况, ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 正比于函数 ![e^{\gamma x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7B%5Cgamma+x%7D) , 也就是说这个解函数成**指数级增长(exponential growth)**;
- ![f'=\gamma f^{\alpha}\quad(\alpha>1).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%3D%5Cgamma+f%5E%7B%5Calpha%7D%5Cquad%28%5Calpha%3E1%29.)  这种情况下, 正的解函数呈现爆炸式的增长, 也就是说在某些有限的区间上, 函数值可以向无穷大增长. 有时也称其**双曲增长(Hyperbolic growth)**. 
- ![f'=\gamma f^{\beta}\quad(\beta<1).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%3D%5Cgamma+f%5E%7B%5Cbeta%7D%5Cquad%28%5Cbeta%3C1%29.)  这种情况下, 解函数最多呈**多项式增长(Polynomial growth)**.
- 当然, 最简单的情况就是 ![f'=\gamma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%3D%5Cgamma) . 此时, 解函数呈**线性增长(Linear growth)**.
