## **序列与极限**

这里我们快速的过一遍关于数列与级数的这一些基础的内容.这些内容在复数域 ![\mathbb{C}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D) 上都是成立的.

> 度量空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 中的序列 ![\{ x_n \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B+x_n+%5C%7D) 称**收敛的**, 当 ![\exists x \in X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+x+%5Cin+X) , ![\forall \varepsilon >0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon+%3E0) , ![\exists N\in \mathbb{N}_+ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D_%2B+),  ![d(x_n, x)<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28x_n%2C+x%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon). 我们说序列 ![\{ x_n \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B+x_n+%5C%7D) 收敛于 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) . 记为
![\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}x_{n}=x.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7Dx_%7Bn%7D%3Dx.)
如果序列不收敛, 我们称其为发散的.

收敛序列具有以下一些性质:
- 序列 ![\{ x_n \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B+x_n+%5C%7D) 收敛于 ![x\in X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+X) , 则在 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 的任意邻域中均包含序列中的无限项.
- 若序列收敛, 则极限唯一.
- 若序列收敛, 则序列有界.
- 若 ![E\subset X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset+X) , ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 是 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的聚点, 则 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 中存在收敛于 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 的序列.

由定义容易验证, 序列的四则运算:

> 对序列 ![\{s_{n}\}, \{t_{n}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bs_%7Bn%7D%5C%7D%2C+%5C%7Bt_%7Bn%7D%5C%7D) , 有 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}s_{n}=s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Ds_%7Bn%7D%3Ds) , ![\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}t_{n}=t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Dt_%7Bn%7D%3Dt) . 那么
-  ![\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}(s_{n}+t_{n})=s+t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D%28s_%7Bn%7D%2Bt_%7Bn%7D%29%3Ds%2Bt) ;
- 对于任意的数 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) , 有 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}cs_{n}=cs](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Dcs_%7Bn%7D%3Dcs) , ![\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}(c+s_{n})=c+s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D%28c%2Bs_%7Bn%7D%29%3Dc%2Bs) ;
- ![\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}s_{n}t_{n}=st](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Ds_%7Bn%7Dt_%7Bn%7D%3Dst) ;
- 若 ![\forall n, s_n \neq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%2C+s_n+%5Cneq+0) , 且 ![s\neq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5Cneq+0) , 则 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}1/s_{n}=1/s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D1%2Fs_%7Bn%7D%3D1%2Fs) .

Note: 对于 ![\mathbb{R}^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7Bn%7D) (或 ![\mathbb{C}^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D%5E%7Bn%7D) )上的序列, 以上定义和结论均适用.

回顾一下柯西(Cauchy)序列的概念.

**[Cauchy 序列]**

> 度量空间中的序列 ![\{ x_{n} \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B+x_%7Bn%7D+%5C%7D) 称为 Cauchy 序列, 当其满足 ![\forall \varepsilon >0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon+%3E0) , ![\exists N\in \mathbb{N}_+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D_%2B) , ![\forall n>N, m>N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%3EN%2C+m%3EN) , 有 ![d(x_n, x_m)<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28x_n%2C+x_m%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon) .

如果度量空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X)中的每个 Cauchy 序列在在度量空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 中收敛, 就称它是**完备的**. 在此我们指出关于 ![\mathbb{R}^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Ek) 的完备性的两个个等价的描述:
- ![\mathbb{R}^{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7Bk%7D) 中的每一个柯西列均有极限点.
- **[Bolzano-Weierstrass]**![\mathbb{R}^{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7Bk%7D) 每个有界序列必含有收敛的子序列.

我们已经知道**度量空间中的收敛序列是有界的, 但是有界不一定收敛**. 然而, 存在收敛性等于有界性的一种,  ![\mathbb{R}^{1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7B1%7D) 上的单调序列就是如此.

## **数项级数**

关于序列极限的类似的结构和结果可以应用于(以 ![\mathbb{R}^{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7Bk%7D) 中的元素为项的)级数.

对于有如下形式的序列 ![\{S_{n}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BS_%7Bn%7D%5C%7D) :

![S_{n}=\sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{n},](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_%7Bn%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Da_%7Bn%7D%2C)

符号 ![\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}a_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Da_%7Bn%7D) 称为无穷级数, 若 ![n\rightarrow \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty) 时, ![S_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_%7Bn%7D) 收敛于某一 ![S\in \mathbb{R}^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Ek) , 则说无穷级数收敛.写成 ![S=\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}S_{n}=\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}a_{n}.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7DS_%7Bn%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Da_%7Bn%7D.)

我们说以上级数绝对收敛, 当级数 ![\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}|a_{n}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C) 收敛.

Cauchy 收敛准则重新叙述如下:

> **[级数的 Cauchy 收敛准则]** 级数 ![\sum a_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_%7Bn%7D) 收敛, 当且仅当
![\forall \varepsilon>0 , \exists N\in \mathbb{N}_+ , \forall n \geqslant m \geqslant N : \left| \sum_{i=n}^{m}a_i \right|\leqslant\varepsilon.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon%3E0+%2C+%5Cexists+N%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D_%2B+%2C+%5Cforall+n+%5Cgeqslant+m+%5Cgeqslant+N+%3A+%5Cleft%7C+%5Csum_%7Bi%3Dn%7D%5E%7Bm%7Da_i+%5Cright%7C%5Cleqslant%5Cvarepsilon.)

由此可以直接得到结论: 若级数级数 ![\sum a_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_%7Bn%7D) 收敛, 则 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}a_n =0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Da_n+%3D0) (这是级数收敛的**必要但不充分条件**.)

对应于单调序列收敛性与有界性等价, 关于级数有结论: 各项非负的级数收敛, 当且仅当部分和序列 ![\{s_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bs_n%5C%7D) 有界.

判断**通项非负的级数**收敛的判定, 我们有非常有用的"比较检验法".定理叙述如下:

> - 若 ![\exists N_0 \in \mathbb{N}_+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N_0+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D_%2B) , ![\forall n>N_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%3EN_0) , ![|a_n|\leqslant c_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Ca_n%7C%5Cleqslant+c_n) , 而级数 ![\sum c_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+c_n) 收敛, 则级数 ![\sum a_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_%7Bn%7D) 收敛.
- 若 ![\exists N_0 \in \mathbb{N}_+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N_0+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D_%2B) ,  ![\forall n>N_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%3EN_0) , ![a_n \geqslant d_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n+%5Cgeqslant+d_n) , 而级数 ![\sum d_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+d_n) 发散, 则级数 ![\sum a_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_%7Bn%7D) 发散.

所以当使用不同的比较级数的时候, 就进一步衍生出了不同的判别法. 往往比较级数收敛的"越慢", 判别法就越"精密", 但是也相应越复杂.这里仅例举最常用的两种判别法: 
- 几何级数:若 ![0<|q|<1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3C%7Cq%7C%3C1) , 那么 ![\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}q^n =\frac{q}{1-q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Dq%5En+%3D%5Cfrac%7Bq%7D%7B1-q%7D) .以几何级数为比较级数可以得到**比率验敛法**.

> **[比率验敛法]** 对于级数 ![\sum{a_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum%7Ba_%7Bn%7D%7D) ,
- 若 ![\limsup_{n\rightarrow \infty}\left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right|<1,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Climsup_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7Ba_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%7Ba_n%7D%5Cright%7C%3C1%2C) 则级数收敛; 
- 若 ![\exists n_0 \in \mathbb{N}_+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+n_0+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D_%2B) , 当 ![n\geqslant n_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cgeqslant+n_0) , ![\left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right|\geqslant 1,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7Ba_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%7Ba_n%7D%5Cright%7C%5Cgeqslant+1%2C) 则级数发散.
- p 级数:若 ![p>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%3E1) , ![\sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%5Ep%7D) 收敛,否则发散.以 p 级数为比较级数可以得到**根值验敛法**.

> **[根值验敛法]** 对于级数 ![\sum a_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_n) , 令 ![\alpha = \limsup_{n\rightarrow \infty}\sqrt[n]{|a_n |}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%3D+%5Climsup_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D%5Csqrt%5Bn%5D%7B%7Ca_n+%7C%7D) ,
- 若 ![\alpha<1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3C1) 时, 级数收敛; 
- ![\alpha>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3E1) 时, 级数发散; 
- ![\alpha =1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%3D1) 时本判别法无法判定.

## **Abel 方法--关于一般项级数的收敛**

**阿贝尔(Abel)方法**是一种很有用的古典分析技巧, 在级数的收敛性理论还有有关积分计算中常常用到.阿贝尔方法的基础就是以下称为**分部求和公式**(又叫和差变换公式)的恒等式, 相当于积分中的分部积分法. 由这个式子出发, 可以得到**{阿贝尔引理}**, 进而导出**一般级数**的**阿贝尔判别法**与**狄利克雷判别法**, 以及之后给出的**级数乘法定理**. 

> **[分部求和公式]**
设有两个序列 ![a_n, b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n%2C+b_n) , 当 ![n\geqslant0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cgeqslant0) 时, 令 ![A_{n}=\sum_{k=0}^{n}a_{k},](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7Bn%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7Da_%7Bk%7D%2C) 令 ![A_{-1}=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B-1%7D%3D0) , 那么, 当 ![0\leqslant p\leqslant q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleqslant+p%5Cleqslant+q) 时, 有
![\sum_{n=p}^{q}{a_n b_n}=\sum_{n=p}^{q-1}{A_{n}(b_n - b_{n+1})}+A_q b_p - A_{p-1} b_p.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%3Dp%7D%5E%7Bq%7D%7Ba_n+b_n%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3Dp%7D%5E%7Bq-1%7D%7BA_%7Bn%7D%28b_n+-+b_%7Bn%2B1%7D%29%7D%2BA_q+b_p+-+A_%7Bp-1%7D+b_p.)

***Abel 收敛判别法***

> 若序列 ![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) 单调有界, 级数 ![\sum b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+b_n) 收敛, 则 ![\sum_{n}^{\infty}a_n b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Da_n+b_n) 收敛.

***Dirichlet 收敛判别法***

> 若级数 ![\sum a_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_%7Bn%7D) 的部分和 ![A_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_n) 构成有界序列, ![b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_n) 单调递减趋于 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) ,则 ![\sum_{n}^{\infty}a_n b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Da_n+b_n) 收敛.

最后给出莱布尼茨(Leibnitz)关于交错级数收敛的判别法.

> 设 ![a_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n) 单调递减趋于 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) , 则交错级数 ![\sum_{n}^{\infty}a_n b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Da_n+b_n) 收敛.

## **级数的加法与乘法**

***级数加法***

> 如果 ![\sum a_n =A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_n+%3DA) , ![\sum b_n = B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+b_n+%3D+B) , 那么 ![\sum(a_n +b_n) = A+B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum%28a_n+%2Bb_n%29+%3D+A%2BB) , 而且对于任意常数 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) , ![c\sum a_n =cA](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%5Csum+a_n+%3DcA) .

关于级数的乘法相对复杂, 这里仅讨论"Cauchy 乘积".

> **[Cauchy 乘积]**
设有 ![\sum a_n =A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_n+%3DA) , ![\sum b_n = B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+b_n+%3D+B) .令 ![c_n = \sum_{k=0}^{n}{a_k b_{n-k}}\quad(n=0,1,2,\cdots)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_n+%3D+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%7Ba_k+b_%7Bn-k%7D%7D%5Cquad%28n%3D0%2C1%2C2%2C%5Ccdots%29)
称级数 ![\sum c_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+c_n) 为所给的两个级数的积.

级数的积和级数之间的以来关系是很复杂的, 级数 ![\sum a_n =A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_n+%3DA) , ![\sum b_n = B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+b_n+%3D+B) 收敛并不保证它们的乘积级数是收敛的. 关于级数乘积有定理

> **[Mertens]**
若级数 ![\sum a_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_n) 绝对收敛,  ![\sum a_n =A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+a_n+%3DA) , ![\sum b_n = B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+b_n+%3D+B) , ![c_n = \sum_{k=0}^{n}{a_k b_{n-k}}~(n=0,1,2,\cdots)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_n+%3D+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%7Ba_k+b_%7Bn-k%7D%7D~%28n%3D0%2C1%2C2%2C%5Ccdots%29) ,则 ![\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}c_n =AB.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Dc_n+%3DAB.)

反过来, 若级数 ![\sum c_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+c_n) 收敛, 那么该级数的和是否就是 ![AB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AB) 呢? 问题的答案是肯定的.应用阿贝尔的方法可以得到如下定理:

> **[级数乘法定理]**
若级数 ![\sum c_{n}, \sum a_n, \sum b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+c_%7Bn%7D%2C+%5Csum+a_n%2C+%5Csum+b_n) , 分别收敛于 ![A, B, C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2C+B%2C+C) , 并且 ![c_n = \sum_{k=0}^{n}{a_k b_{n-k}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_n+%3D+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%7Ba_k+b_%7Bn-k%7D%7D) , 那么 ![C=AB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%3DAB) .
