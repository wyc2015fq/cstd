## **线性空间的定义与简单性质**

以下是线性空间的定义.

> 设 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 是一个非空集合， ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 是一个数域. 在集合 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的元素之间定义了一种代数运算，叫做**加法**；这就是说给出了一个法则，对于 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中任意两个元素 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 与 ![\boldsymbol{\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D) ，在 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中都有唯一的一个元素 ![\boldsymbol{\gamma}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cgamma%7D) 与它们对应，称为 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 与 ![\boldsymbol{\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D) 的**和**，记为 ![\boldsymbol{\gamma=\alpha+\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cgamma%3D%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%7D) . 在数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 与集合 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的元素之间还定义了一种运算，叫做**数量乘法**；就是说对于数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 中任意数 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 与 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中任一元素 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 在 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中都有唯一的一个元素 ![\boldsymbol{\delta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cdelta%7D) 与它们对应，称为 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 与 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 的**数量乘积**，记为 ![\boldsymbol{\delta}=k\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cdelta%7D%3Dk%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) . 如果加法与数量乘法满足下述规则，那么 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 称为数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上的**线性空间**.

加法满足下面四条规则：
- 1) ![\boldsymbol{\alpha+\beta=\beta+\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%3D%5Cbeta%2B%5Calpha%7D) ;
- 2) ![\boldsymbol{(\alpha+\beta)+\gamma=\alpha+(\beta+\gamma)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%28%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%29%2B%5Cgamma%3D%5Calpha%2B%28%5Cbeta%2B%5Cgamma%29%7D) ;
- 3)在 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中有一个元素 ![\mathbf{0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7B0%7D) ，对于 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中任意元素 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 都有 ![\boldsymbol{0+\alpha=\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B0%2B%5Calpha%3D%5Calpha%7D)
- 4)对于 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中的每一个元素 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) ，都有 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中的元素 ![\boldsymbol{\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D) ，使得 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha+\beta=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%3D0%7D) ( ![\boldsymbol{\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D) 称为 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 的**负元素**)。
数量乘法满足下列两条规则：
- 5) ![1\boldsymbol{\alpha=\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D%5Calpha%7D) ;
- 6) ![k(l\boldsymbol{\alpha}=(kl)\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%28l%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%3D%28kl%29%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) .
数量乘法与加法满足下列两条规则：
- 7) ![(k+l)\boldsymbol{\alpha}=k\boldsymbol{\alpha}+l\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28k%2Bl%29%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%3Dk%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%2Bl%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) ;
- 8) ![k(\boldsymbol{\alpha+\beta})=k\boldsymbol{\alpha}+k\boldsymbol{\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%7D%29%3Dk%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%2Bk%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D) .

以下是直接从定义可推导的线性空间的一些简单性质：
- 零元素是唯一的；
- 负元素是唯一的；
- ![0\boldsymbol{\alpha=0};~k\boldsymbol{0=0};~(-1)\boldsymbol{\alpha=-\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D0%7D%3B~k%5Cboldsymbol%7B0%3D0%7D%3B~%28-1%29%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D-%5Calpha%7D) ；
- 如果 ![k\boldsymbol{\alpha=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D0%7D) ，那么 ![k=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D0) ，或者 ![k\boldsymbol{\alpha=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D0%7D) .

## **线性空间的维数, 基与坐标**

> 设 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上的一个线性空间， ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}},(r\geq1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D%2C%28r%5Cgeq1%29) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中的一组向量， ![k_{1},k_{2},\cdots,k_{r}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7B1%7D%2Ck_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2Ck_%7Br%7D) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 中的数，那么向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}=k_{1}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}}+k_{2}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{2}}+\cdots+k_{r}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%3Dk_%7B1%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%7D%2Bk_%7B2%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2Bk_%7Br%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D)
称为向量组 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 的一个**线性组合**, 或称 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 可以由 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D)**线性表出.**

设

![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}~~~~~~~~(1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D~~~~~~~~%281%29)

![\boldsymbol{\beta_{1},\beta_{2},\cdots,\beta_{r}}~~~~~~~~(2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cbeta_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cbeta_%7Br%7D%7D~~~~~~~~%282%29)

是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中两个向量组。如果(1)中的每一个向量都可以用向量组(2)线性表出，那么称向量组(1)可以用向量组(2)线性表出；如果(1)与(2)可以互相线性表出，那么向量组(1)与(2)称为**等价**的。

线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}},(r\geq1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D%2C%28r%5Cgeq1%29) 称为**线性相关**，如果在数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 中有 ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) 个不全为零的数 ![k_{1},k_{2},\cdots,k_{r}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7B1%7D%2Ck_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2Ck_%7Br%7D) ，使

![k_{1}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}}+k_{2}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{2}}+\cdots+k_{r}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{r}=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7B1%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%7D%2Bk_%7B2%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2Bk_%7Br%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%3D0%7D)

如果向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}},(r\geq1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D%2C%28r%5Cgeq1%29) 不线性相关，就称为**线性无关.**

关于 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 元数组的性质依然适用，这里仅列出几个常用结论：
- 单个向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 是线性相关的充分条件是 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D0%7D) 。两个以上的向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 线性相关的充分条件是其中有一个向量是其他向量的线性组合。
- 如果向量组 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 线性无关，而且可以被 ![\boldsymbol{\beta_{1},\beta_{2},\cdots,\beta_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cbeta_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cbeta_%7Br%7D%7D) 线性表出，那么 ![r\leq s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Cleq+s) .
- 如果向量组 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 线性无关，但向量组 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r},\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%2C%5Cbeta%7D) 线性相关，那么 ![\boldsymbol{\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D) 可以被 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 线性表出,而且表法是唯一的。

如果线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中有 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个线性无关的向量，但是没有更多数目的线性无关的向量，那么 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 就称为 **n 维的**；如果在 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中可以找到任意多个线性无关的向量，那么 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 就称为**无限维**的。

在 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中， ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个线性无关的向量 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D) 称为 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一组{**基**}。设 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中任一向量，于是 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n},\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%2C%5Calpha%7D) 线性相关，因此 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 可以被线性表出： ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}=a_{1}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1}}+a_{2}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{2}}+\cdots+a_{n}\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%3Da_%7B1%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%7D%2Ba_%7B2%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2Ba_%7Bn%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D) 其中系数 ![a_{1},a_{2},\cdots,a_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7B1%7D%2Ca_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2Ca_%7Bn%7D) 被向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 和基 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D) 唯一确定，这组数就称为 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 在基 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D) 下的**坐标**，记为 ![(a_{1},a_{2},\cdots,a_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_%7B1%7D%2Ca_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2Ca_%7Bn%7D%29) .

> 如果在维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中有 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个线性无关的向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bn%7D%7D) ，且 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中任一向量都可以用它们线性表出，那么 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 是 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维的，而 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bn%7D%7D) 就是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一组基。

## **基变换与坐标变换**

设 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1},\varepsilon_{2},\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%7D) 与 ![\boldsymbol{\varepsilon_{1}',\varepsilon_{2}',\cdots,\varepsilon_{n}'}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cvarepsilon_%7B1%7D%27%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7B2%7D%27%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cvarepsilon_%7Bn%7D%27%7D) 是 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中两组基，其关系是

设向量 ![\boldsymbol{\xi}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cxi%7D) 在这两组基下的坐标分别为 ![(x_{1},x_{2},\cdots,x_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_%7B1%7D%2Cx_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2Cx_%7Bn%7D%29) 与 ![(x_{1}',x_{2}',\cdots,x_{n}')](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_%7B1%7D%27%2Cx_%7B2%7D%27%2C%5Ccdots%2Cx_%7Bn%7D%27%29) 。将上式以矩阵形式表示，为

或

## **线性子空间**

数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上的一个线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个非空子集合 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 称为 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个**线性子空间**（或简称子空间），如果 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 对于 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的两种运算也构成数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上的线性空间。

> 设 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的非空子集。则 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的子空间的**充分必要条件**是关于 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的两种运算封闭，即 ![\forall\boldsymbol{\alpha,\beta}\in W;k, l\in P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta%7D%5Cin+W%3Bk%2C+l%5Cin+P) ，均有 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha+\beta},k\boldsymbol{\alpha}\in W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%7D%2Ck%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%5Cin+W) .

单个的零向量所组成的子集合是一个子空间，称**零子空间. **线性空间中，零子空间和线性空间本身这两个子空间叫做**平凡子空间, **其他子空间称为**非平凡子空间**. 齐次线性方程组的全部解向量构成一个子空间，称为其**解空间.**

设 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中的一组向量这组向量所有可能的线性组合所成的集合是非空的，其构成 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个子空间，称为**由**![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D)**生成的子空间**，记为 ![L(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D%29) .

> 1)两个向量组生成相同子空间的充分必要条件是这两个向量组等价。
2) ![L(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D%29) 的维数等于向量组 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 的秩。

我们还有以下定理.

> **[基扩定理]**
设 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 是数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 维子空间， ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 是 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 的一组基，那么这组向量必可以扩充为整个空间的基，也就是说，在 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中必定可以找到 ![n-m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n-m) 个向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{m+1},\alpha_{m+2},\cdots,\alpha_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Bm%2B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7Bm%2B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bn%7D%7D) ，使得 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Bn%7D%7D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一组基。

## **子空间的交与和**

如果 ![V_{1},V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D) 是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的两个子空间，那么它们的交 ![V_{1}\cap V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%5Ccap+V_%7B2%7D) 也是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的子空间。

> **[定义: 子空间的和]**
设 ![V_{1},V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D) 是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的两个子空间，所谓 ![V_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_1) 与 ![V_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_2) 的和，是指所有能表示成 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%7D) ，而 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}}\in V_{1},~\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}}\in V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%7D%5Cin+V_%7B1%7D%2C~%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%7D%5Cin+V_%7B2%7D) 的向量组成的子集合，记做 ![V_{1}+V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2BV_%7B2%7D) .

如果 ![V_{1},V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D) 是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的两个子空间，那么它们的和 ![V_{1}+V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2BV_%7B2%7D) 也是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的子空间。

> **[维数公式]**
如果 ![V_{1},V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D) 是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的两个子空间，那么 ![dim(V_{1})+dim(V_{2})=dim(V_{1}+V_{2})+dim(V_{1}\cap V_{2})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dim%28V_%7B1%7D%29%2Bdim%28V_%7B2%7D%29%3Ddim%28V_%7B1%7D%2BV_%7B2%7D%29%2Bdim%28V_%7B1%7D%5Ccap+V_%7B2%7D%29)

**[推论] **如果 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中两个子空间 ![V_{1},V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D) 的维数之和大于 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) ，那么 ![V_{1},V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D) 必含有非零的公共向量。

## **子空间的直和**

设 ![V_{1},V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D) 是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的子空间，如果 ![V_{1}+V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2BV_%7B2%7D) 中每个向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 的分解式

![\boldsymbol{\alpha=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}},~~\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}}\in V_{1},\boldsymbol{\alpha_{2}}\in V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%7D%2C~~%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%7D%5Cin+V_%7B1%7D%2C%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%7D%5Cin+V_%7B2%7D)

是唯一的，这个和就称为**直和**，记为 ![V_{1}\oplus V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%5Coplus+V_%7B2%7D) .

> 和 ![V_{1}+V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2BV_%7B2%7D) 是直和的**充分必要条件**是等式 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%3D0%7D) , ![\boldsymbol{\alpha=\alpha_{i}}\in V_{i}~~(i=1,2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D%5Calpha_%7Bi%7D%7D%5Cin+V_%7Bi%7D~~%28i%3D1%2C2%29) 只有在 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{i}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Bi%7D%7D) 全为零向量时才成立。

**[推论]** 和 ![V_{1}+V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2BV_%7B2%7D) 是直和的**充分必要条件**是 ![V_{1}\cap V_{2}=0.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%5Ccap+V_%7B2%7D%3D0.)

> 设 ![V_{1},V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的子空间，令 ![W=V_{1}+V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W%3DV_%7B1%7D%2BV_%7B2%7D) ，则 ![W=V_{1}\oplus V_{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W%3DV_%7B1%7D%5Coplus+V_%7B2%7D) 的**充分必要条件**为 ![dim(W)=dim(V_{1})+dim(V_{2}).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dim%28W%29%3Ddim%28V_%7B1%7D%29%2Bdim%28V_%7B2%7D%29.)

设 ![V_{1},V_{2},\cdots,V_{s}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2CV_%7Bs%7D) 都是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的子空间，如果和 ![V_{1}+V_{2}+\cdots+V_{s}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2BV_%7B2%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2BV_%7Bs%7D) 中每一个向量 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D) 的分解式 ![\[\boldsymbol{\alpha=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}+\cdots+\alpha_{s}},~~\boldsymbol{\alpha_{i}}\in V_{i}(i=1,2,\dots,s)\]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%3D%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2B%5Calpha_%7Bs%7D%7D%2C~~%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Bi%7D%7D%5Cin+V_%7Bi%7D%28i%3D1%2C2%2C%5Cdots%2Cs%29%5C%5D) 是唯一的，这个和就称为直和。记为 ![V_{1}\oplus V_{2}\oplus \cdots \oplus V_{s}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%5Coplus+V_%7B2%7D%5Coplus+%5Ccdots+%5Coplus+V_%7Bs%7D) .

> 设 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 是线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个子空间，那么一定存在一个子空间 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 使 ![V=U\oplus W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%3DU%5Coplus+W) .

![V_{1},V_{2},\cdots,V_{s}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2CV_%7Bs%7D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一些子空间，下面这些条件是等价的：

1)] ![W=\sum V_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W%3D%5Csum+V_%7Bi%7D) 是直和；

2)] 零向量的表法唯一；

3)] ![V_{i}\cap \sum_{j\neq i}V_{j}=\{\mathbf 0 \}~~~~(i=1,2,\cdots,s)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7Bi%7D%5Ccap+%5Csum_%7Bj%5Cneq+i%7DV_%7Bj%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Cmathbf+0+%5C%7D~~~~%28i%3D1%2C2%2C%5Ccdots%2Cs%29) ；

4)] ![dim(W)=\sum{dim(V_{i})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dim%28W%29%3D%5Csum%7Bdim%28V_%7Bi%7D%29%7D) .

\footnote{补充：若欧几里得空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的子空间 ![V_{1},V_{2},\cdots,V_{s}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D%2CV_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2CV_%7Bs%7D) 两两正交，那么 ![\sum V_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+V_%7Bi%7D) 是直和。}

## **线性空间的同构**

数域 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 上两个线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 与 ![V'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%27) 称为{**同构**}的，如果由 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 到 ![V'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%27) 有一个双射 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 具有以下性质：

1) ![\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha+\beta})=\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha})+\sigma(\boldsymbol{\beta})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%7D%29%3D%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%2B%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D%29) ;

2) ![\sigma(k\sigma)=k\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28k%5Csigma%29%3Dk%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29) ,

其中 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha},\boldsymbol{\beta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%2C%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Cbeta%7D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中任意向量， ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 是 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 中任意数。这样的映射称为{**同构映射**}。

同构映射具有以下基本性质：
- ![\sigma(\mathbf{0})=0,~\sigma(\boldsymbol{-\alpha})=-\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28%5Cmathbf%7B0%7D%29%3D0%2C~%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B-%5Calpha%7D%29%3D-%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29) ；
- ![\sigma(k_{1}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}}+k_{2}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{2}}+\cdots+k_{r}\boldsymbol{\alpha_{r}})= \\ k_{1}\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}})+k_{2}\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{2}})+\cdots+k_{r}\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{r}})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28k_%7B1%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%7D%2Bk_%7B2%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2Bk_%7Br%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D%29%3D+%5C%5C+k_%7B1%7D%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%7D%29%2Bk_%7B2%7D%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%7D%29%2B%5Ccdots%2Bk_%7Br%7D%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D%29)
- ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 中向量组 ![\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\cdots,\alpha_{r}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D) 线性相关的充分必要条件是，它们的象 ![\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{1}}),\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{2}}),\cdots,\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha_{r}})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B1%7D%7D%29%2C%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7B2%7D%7D%29%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha_%7Br%7D%7D%29) 线性相关；
- 如果 ![V_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_1) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的一个线性子空间，那么 ![V_{1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D) 在 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 下的象集合 ![\sigma(V_{1})=\{\sigma(\boldsymbol{\alpha})\mid\alpha\in V_{1}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28V_%7B1%7D%29%3D%5C%7B%5Csigma%28%5Cboldsymbol%7B%5Calpha%7D%29%5Cmid%5Calpha%5Cin+V_%7B1%7D%5C%7D) 是 ![\sigma(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28V%29) 的子空间，并且 ![V_{1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D) 与 ![\sigma(V_{1})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28V_%7B1%7D%29) 维数相同；
- 同构映射的逆映射以及两个同构映射的乘积还是同构映射。

> 数域$P$上有两个有限维线性空间同构的**充分必要条件**是它们**有相同的维数**。
