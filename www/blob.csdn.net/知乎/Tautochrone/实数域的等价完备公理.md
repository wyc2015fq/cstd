# 实数域的等价完备公理 - 知乎
# 

实数域的构造有各种各样的方法. 传统数学分析教材喜欢用公理化定理：实数域是具有**Dedekind完备性**的**序域. **具体来说实数域 ![(\mathbb{R};+,×,\leq)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%3B%2B%2C%C3%97%2C%5Cleq%29) 是满足如下性质的集合：
- P1 (加法交换律)
- P2 (加法结合律)
- P3 (加法单位元)
- P4 (加法逆元)
- P5 (乘法交换律)
- P6 (乘法结合律)
- P7 (乘法单位元)
- P8 (乘法逆元)
- P9 (乘法结合律)
- P10 (序的自反性)
- P11 (序的反对称性)
- P12 (序的传递性)
- P13 (序的完全性)
- P14 (加法保持序)
- P15 (乘法保持序)
- **P16 (Dedekind完备性/确界存在原理) **上有界非空子集都有上确界

![\forall E\subset \mathbb{R} (E\ne\emptyset\ \wedge\exists a\in\mathbb{R}\ \forall x\in E(x\leq a)\Rightarrow \exists s\in\mathbb{R}(s=\mathrm{sup}\ E) )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+E%5Csubset+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D+%28E%5Cne%5Cemptyset%5C+%5Cwedge%5Cexists+a%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5C+%5Cforall+x%5Cin+E%28x%5Cleq+a%29%5CRightarrow+%5Cexists+s%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%28s%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bsup%7D%5C+E%29+%29)

**可以证明所有Dedekind完备序域都同构**，也即实数域在同构意义上是唯一的，dhchen在他的live讲义中给出了一个方法.

然而这样的集合是否存在？一种构造性的方法是从有理数域 ![\mathbb{Q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D) 构筑实数域 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，即所谓Dedekind分割，这种方法用来证明确界存在原理是非常直接的。具体方法不详述了.

本文的目的是从确界存在原理推出其他与实数完备性有关的定理，并且证明它们的等价性。具体来说是：

**设**![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)**是序域（即满足以上性质P1~P15），则以下命题等价：**
- **(Dedekind完备性)**上有界非空子集都有上确界;
- **(Cantor闭区间套)**设 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的闭区间序列 ![I_n=[a_n,b_n]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_n%3D%5Ba_n%2Cb_n%5D) 满足 ![I_1\supset I_2 \supset......](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_1%5Csupset+I_2+%5Csupset......) ，则 ![\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}I_n\ne \emptyset](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DI_n%5Cne+%5Cemptyset) . 特别地，若 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty} (b_n-a_n)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7D+%28b_n-a_n%29%3D0) ，则 ![\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}I_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DI_n) 有且只有一个点.
- **(Heine-Borel有限覆盖)**![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的有界闭集是**紧致集**.
- **(Bolzano-Weierstrass聚点存在)**![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的有界无限集有聚点.
- **(Bolzano-Weierstrass收敛子列) **![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的有界闭集是**列紧集**.
- **(Cauchy完备性)** 所有Cauchy序列都收敛+ **(Archimedes性)**![\mathbb{N}\subset\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 无上界

> **名词解释**
**有界，收敛，上确界：**这还用解释吗哼~
**聚点：**![p\in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是 ![E\subset \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 的聚点，若 ![\forall \varepsilon>0\  \exists q\in E(0<|p-q|<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%5C++%5Cexists+q%5Cin+E%280%3C%7Cp-q%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29)
**闭集**：![E\subset\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是闭集，若 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的所有聚点都在 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 中
**紧致集**： ![E\subset\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是紧致集，若对于任意的开集族 ![\{U_i\subset\mathbb{R}|i\in I\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BU_i%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7Ci%5Cin+I%5C%7D) 满足 ![E\subset \bigcup_{i\in I} U_i ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset+%5Cbigcup_%7Bi%5Cin+I%7D+U_i+) （称为开覆盖），都存在有限子集 ![J\subset I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=J%5Csubset+I) 使得 ![E\subset \bigcup_{j\in J} U_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset+%5Cbigcup_%7Bj%5Cin+J%7D+U_j) （称为有限子覆盖）.
**列紧集**：![E\subset\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是列紧集，若对于任意序列 ![\{a_n\}\subset E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D%5Csubset+E) ，都存在收敛子序列 ![a_{n_i}\rightarrow p\in E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bn_i%7D%5Crightarrow+p%5Cin+E) .
在任意度量空间中，紧致性与列紧性是完全等价的，这个性质不限于 ![\mathbb{R^n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%5En%7D) .
**Cauchy序列**： ![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) 是Cauchy序列，若 ![\forall \varepsilon>0\ \exists N\in\mathbb{N}\ \forall m,n>N(|a_m-a_n|<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%5C+%5Cexists+N%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5C+%5Cforall+m%2Cn%3EN%28%7Ca_m-a_n%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29)

我们注意到上面出现了两种完备性的表述：**Dedekind完备性**与**Cauchy完备性**. 这两个表述不是等价的，Cauchy完备性弱于Dedekind完备性. 存在满足Cauchy完备性但不满足Archimedes性(从而不满足Dedekind完备性)的序域，构造详见这个回答：

我们的证明思路是循环证明 *![(1)\Rightarrow(2)\Rightarrow(3)\Rightarrow(4)\Rightarrow(5)\Rightarrow(6)\Rightarrow(1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%29%5CRightarrow%282%29%5CRightarrow%283%29%5CRightarrow%284%29%5CRightarrow%285%29%5CRightarrow%286%29%5CRightarrow%281%29) .*

### 确界存在 ![\Rightarrow ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow+) 闭区间套

考虑序列 ![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) ： ![b_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1) 是 ![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) 的一个上界. 假设不然，则存在 ![a_m>b_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_m%3Eb_1) ，于是 ![I_m\cap I_1=\emptyset](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_m%5Ccap+I_1%3D%5Cemptyset) ，与闭区间套假设矛盾.

![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) 有上界，由确界存在原理，它有上确界，设 ![a=\sup_{n\in\mathbb{N_+}}a_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3D%5Csup_%7Bn%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN_%2B%7D%7Da_n) ；

同理 ![\{b_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bb_n%5C%7D) 有下确界，设 ![b=\inf_{n\in\mathbb{N_+}}b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%3D%5Cinf_%7Bn%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN_%2B%7D%7Db_n) .

假设 ![a>b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Eb) ，则 ![\exists p,q\in\mathbb{N}(a_p>b\wedge b_q<a)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+p%2Cq%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%28a_p%3Eb%5Cwedge+b_q%3Ca%29) ，即 ![a_p>b_q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_p%3Eb_q) ，从而 ![I_p\cup I_q=\emptyset ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_p%5Ccup+I_q%3D%5Cemptyset+) ，与闭区间套假设矛盾.

于是 ![a\leq b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cleq+b) ，而 ![\forall n\in\mathbb{N_+}(I_n\supset[a,b])](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN_%2B%7D%28I_n%5Csupset%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29) ，于是 ![\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}I_n\supset[a,b]\ne \emptyset](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DI_n%5Csupset%5Ba%2Cb%5D%5Cne+%5Cemptyset) .

若 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty} (b_n-a_n)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7D+%28b_n-a_n%29%3D0) ，则 ![a=b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Db) . 若不然，假设 ![\varepsilon=b-a>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3Db-a%3E0) ，则 ![\forall n\in\mathbb{N_+}(b_n-a_n>b-a=\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN_%2B%7D%28b_n-a_n%3Eb-a%3D%5Cvarepsilon%29) 与极限矛盾.

于是 ![\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}I_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DI_n) 是单点集.

### 闭区间套 ![\Rightarrow ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow+) 有限覆盖

首先紧致集的闭子集也是紧致集，这是在任意度量空间中都具有的性质，证明从略. 而对于任意有界闭集，总能找到一个闭区间包含该集合，要证明有界闭集是紧致集，只要证明任意闭区间都是紧致集即可.

其次，紧致集的有限并是紧致集. 考虑 ![E_1, E_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_1%2C+E_2) 紧致，存在一个开覆盖 ![\bigcup_{i\in I}U_i\supset (E_1\cup E_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcup_%7Bi%5Cin+I%7DU_i%5Csupset+%28E_1%5Ccup+E_2%29) ，which is also ![E_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_1) 和 ![E_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_2) 的开覆盖. 由紧致性，则分别存在有限子覆盖 ![\bigcup_{i\in J_1}U_i\supset E_1,\bigcup_{i\in J_2}U_i\supset E_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcup_%7Bi%5Cin+J_1%7DU_i%5Csupset+E_1%2C%5Cbigcup_%7Bi%5Cin+J_2%7DU_i%5Csupset+E_2) ，于是 ![\bigcup_{i\in J_1\cup J_2}U_i\supset (E_1\cup E_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcup_%7Bi%5Cin+J_1%5Ccup+J_2%7DU_i%5Csupset+%28E_1%5Ccup+E_2%29) 是一个有限子覆盖，于是 ![E_1\cup E_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_1%5Ccup+E_2) 是紧致集. 可以归纳得，紧致集的有限并是紧致集.

现在考虑闭区间 ![I_0=[a_0,b_0]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_0%3D%5Ba_0%2Cb_0%5D) ，假设它不是紧致的. 而 ![[a_0,b_0]=[a_0,\frac{a_0+b_0}{2}]\cup[\frac{a_0+b_0}{2},b_0]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba_0%2Cb_0%5D%3D%5Ba_0%2C%5Cfrac%7Ba_0%2Bb_0%7D%7B2%7D%5D%5Ccup%5B%5Cfrac%7Ba_0%2Bb_0%7D%7B2%7D%2Cb_0%5D) ，则 ![[a_0,\frac{a_0+b_0}{2}]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba_0%2C%5Cfrac%7Ba_0%2Bb_0%7D%7B2%7D%5D) 或 ![[\frac{a_0+b_0}{2},b_0]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B%5Cfrac%7Ba_0%2Bb_0%7D%7B2%7D%2Cb_0%5D) 不是紧致的（如果她们同时紧致，就与“紧致集的有限并是紧致集”矛盾）. 记其中一个非紧致集为 ![I_1=[a_1,b_1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_1%3D%5Ba_1%2Cb_1%5D) .

重复这个二分过程，就得到一个闭区间套 ![I_0\supset I_1\supset ......](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_0%5Csupset+I_1%5Csupset+......) 且其中每个区间都是不紧致的.且注意到 ![b_n-a_n=\frac{b_0-a_0}{2^n}\rightarrow 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_n-a_n%3D%5Cfrac%7Bb_0-a_0%7D%7B2%5En%7D%5Crightarrow+0) 由闭区间套原理，存在唯一的 ![x\in\bigcup_{n=0}^\infty I_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+I_n) .

考虑 ![I_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_0) 的一个开覆盖 ![\{U_i|i\in I\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BU_i%7Ci%5Cin+I%5C%7D) ，存在 ![(c,d)\in\{U_i|i\in I\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28c%2Cd%29%5Cin%5C%7BU_i%7Ci%5Cin+I%5C%7D) 使得 ![x\in(c,d)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin%28c%2Cd%29) . 而 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}(b_n-a_n)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7D%28b_n-a_n%29%3D0) 蕴含 ![\exists k\in\mathbb{N}(I_k=[a_k,b_k]\subset(c,d))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+k%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%28I_k%3D%5Ba_k%2Cb_k%5D%5Csubset%28c%2Cd%29%29) ，也即 ![I_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_k) 有有限子覆盖， ![I_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_k) 是紧致的，这与闭区间套中每个区间都不紧致的假设矛盾.

综上，所有闭区间都是紧致集，而有界闭集能被闭区间包含，因而都是紧致集.

### 有限覆盖 ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) 聚点存在

对于有界无限集 ![E\subset\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，假设它没有聚点.

存在闭区间 ![I\supset E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%5Csupset+E) ，由于 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 没有聚点，因此对任意 ![p\in I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+I) ，存在 ![\varepsilon_p>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon_p%3E0) 使得 ![(p-\varepsilon_p,p+\varepsilon_p)\cap E=\{p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28p-%5Cvarepsilon_p%2Cp%2B%5Cvarepsilon_p%29%5Ccap+E%3D%5C%7Bp%5C%7D) . 我们发现 ![\{(p-\varepsilon_p,p+\varepsilon_p)|p\in I\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28p-%5Cvarepsilon_p%2Cp%2B%5Cvarepsilon_p%29%7Cp%5Cin+I%5C%7D) 是 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 的一个开覆盖，然而它的任意有限子集都不能覆盖 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ，因为 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是无限集而 ![\{(p-\varepsilon_p,p+\varepsilon_p)\cap E|p\in I\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28p-%5Cvarepsilon_p%2Cp%2B%5Cvarepsilon_p%29%5Ccap+E%7Cp%5Cin+I%5C%7D)  的元素是单点集. 因此存在 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 的开覆盖没有有限子覆盖，这与有限覆盖原理矛盾.

于是有界无限集都有聚点.

### 聚点存在 ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) 收敛子列

这个推论就非常直接了，因为实数序列（的值域）是至多可数集. 分情况讨论：

设 ![\{a_n\}\in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是一个序列，

(1)若值域 ![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) 是有限集，则必定存在 ![p\in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 和 ![N\in\mathbb{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D) 使得 ![\forall n>N(a_n=p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%3EN%28a_n%3Dp%29) . 于是子列 ![b_n=a_{n+N}=p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_n%3Da_%7Bn%2BN%7D%3Dp) 是常数列，收敛.

(2)若值域 ![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) 是可数集，由聚点存在原理，存在聚点 ![p\in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) . 由聚点定义 ![\forall k\in \mathbb{N_+}\ \exists n_i\in\mathbb{N}(0<|a_{n_i}-p|<\frac1k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+k%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BN_%2B%7D%5C+%5Cexists+n_i%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%280%3C%7Ca_%7Bn_i%7D-p%7C%3C%5Cfrac1k%29) ，于是子列 ![\{a_{n_i}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_%7Bn_i%7D%5C%7D) 收敛到 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) . 

### 收敛子列 ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) Cauchy完备+Archimedean

**Part 1: Cauchy序列收敛**

首先，Cauchy序列有界. 设 ![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) 是Cauchy序列，取 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ，存在 ![N\in\mathbb{N_+}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN_%2B%7D) ，取 ![m\in\mathbb{N_+}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN_%2B%7D) ，有 ![\forall n>N(|a_n-a_m|<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%3EN%28%7Ca_n-a_m%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29) . 于是 ![|a_n|=|a_n-a_m+a_m|\leq |a_n-a_m|+|a_m|<\varepsilon+|a_m|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Ca_n%7C%3D%7Ca_n-a_m%2Ba_m%7C%5Cleq+%7Ca_n-a_m%7C%2B%7Ca_m%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%2B%7Ca_m%7C)

取 ![M=\max\{|a_1|,...,|a_n|,\varepsilon+|a_m|\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%3D%5Cmax%5C%7B%7Ca_1%7C%2C...%2C%7Ca_n%7C%2C%5Cvarepsilon%2B%7Ca_m%7C%5C%7D) ，于是 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 是 ![\{a_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_n%5C%7D) 有界.

由于Cauchy序列有界，由Bolzano-Weierstrass定理，它存在收敛子列 ![a_{n_i}\rightarrow \alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bn_i%7D%5Crightarrow+%5Calpha) . 

取 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ，Cauchy序列蕴含 ![\exists N_1\in\mathbb{N}\ \forall m,n>N_1(|a_m-a_n|<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N_1%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5C+%5Cforall+m%2Cn%3EN_1%28%7Ca_m-a_n%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29) ，自然有 ![\exists N_1\in\mathbb{N}\ \forall i,n>N_1(|a_{n_i}-a_n|<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N_1%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5C+%5Cforall+i%2Cn%3EN_1%28%7Ca_%7Bn_i%7D-a_n%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29) ；收敛子列蕴含 ![\exists N_2\in\mathbb{N}\ \forall i>N_2(|a_{n_i}-\alpha|<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N_2%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5C+%5Cforall+i%3EN_2%28%7Ca_%7Bn_i%7D-%5Calpha%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29) .

取 ![N=\max\{N_1,N_2\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%3D%5Cmax%5C%7BN_1%2CN_2%5C%7D) . 于是 ![\forall n,i>N(|a_n-\alpha|\leq|a_n-a_{n_i}|+|a_{n_i}-\alpha|<\varepsilon+\varepsilon=2\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%2Ci%3EN%28%7Ca_n-%5Calpha%7C%5Cleq%7Ca_n-a_%7Bn_i%7D%7C%2B%7Ca_%7Bn_i%7D-%5Calpha%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%2B%5Cvarepsilon%3D2%5Cvarepsilon%29) ，于是 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}a_n=\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7Da_n%3D%5Calpha) . Cauchy序列收敛.

**Part 2: Archimedean**

首先，收敛数列是Cauchy列. 因为对 ![a_n\rightarrow\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n%5Crightarrow%5Calpha) ，![\forall \varepsilon>0\ \exists N\in\mathbb{N}\ \forall m,n>N(|a_m-a_n|\leq|a_n-\alpha|+|a_m-\alpha|<2\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%5C+%5Cexists+N%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5C+%5Cforall+m%2Cn%3EN%28%7Ca_m-a_n%7C%5Cleq%7Ca_n-%5Calpha%7C%2B%7Ca_m-%5Calpha%7C%3C2%5Cvarepsilon%29) .

假设Archimede性不成立，即 ![\mathbb{N}\subset\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 有上界 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) ，那么序列 ![a_n=n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n%3Dn) 有界. 由Bolzano-Weierstrass定理，它有收敛子列 ![a_{n_i}=n_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bn_i%7D%3Dn_i) .

然而 ![\forall i\ne j(|n_i-n_j|\geq 1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+i%5Cne+j%28%7Cn_i-n_j%7C%5Cgeq+1%29) ，于是 ![a_{n_i}=n_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bn_i%7D%3Dn_i) 不是Cauchy序列，这与它是收敛序列矛盾.

### Cauchy完备+Archimedean ![\Rightarrow ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow+) 确界存在

设 ![E\subset\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubset%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)  有上界，我们需要构造Cauchy序列来“逼近”它的上确界：

设 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的全体上界的集合为 ![\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) ，取 ![x_0\in E, \ y_0\in\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%5Cin+E%2C+%5C+y_0%5Cin%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) s. t.  ![y_0>x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y_0%3Ex_0) . 

给定 ![x_n\in E,\ y_n\in\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n%5Cin+E%2C%5C+y_n%5Cin%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) . 若 ![\frac12(x_n+y_n)\in\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac12%28x_n%2By_n%29%5Cin%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) ，则令 ![x_{n+1}=x_n,\ y_{n+1}=\frac12(x_0+y_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_%7Bn%2B1%7D%3Dx_n%2C%5C+y_%7Bn%2B1%7D%3D%5Cfrac12%28x_0%2By_0%29) ；若 ![\frac12(x_n+y_n)\notin\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac12%28x_n%2By_n%29%5Cnotin%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) ，则 ![\exists p\in E(p>\frac12(x_0+y_0))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+p%5Cin+E%28p%3E%5Cfrac12%28x_0%2By_0%29%29) ，令 ![x_{n+1}=p,\ y_{n+1}=y_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_%7Bn%2B1%7D%3Dp%2C%5C+y_%7Bn%2B1%7D%3Dy_n) .

于是我们归纳地构造了递增序列 ![\{x_n\}\in E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D%5Cin+E) 和递减序列 ![\{y_n\}\in\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7By_n%5C%7D%5Cin%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) ，且满足 ![y_{n+1}-x_{n+1} \leq\frac12(y_n-x_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y_%7Bn%2B1%7D-x_%7Bn%2B1%7D+%5Cleq%5Cfrac12%28y_n-x_n%29) . 于是![0\leq\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}(y_n-x_n)\leq\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac1{2^n}(y_0-x_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleq%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7D%28y_n-x_n%29%5Cleq%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac1%7B2%5En%7D%28y_0-x_0%29%3D0) . 序列 ![\{y_n-x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7By_n-x_n%5C%7D) 收敛. 

> 注意 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac1{2^n}(y_0-x_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac1%7B2%5En%7D%28y_0-x_0%29%3D0) 用到了Archimedes性：
对 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ，由Archimedes性，存在整数 ![N>\log_2 \frac{y_0-x_0}{\varepsilon}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%3E%5Clog_2+%5Cfrac%7By_0-x_0%7D%7B%5Cvarepsilon%7D) ，于是 ![\frac{y_0-x_0}{2^N}<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7By_0-x_0%7D%7B2%5EN%7D%3C%5Cvarepsilon) ， ![\forall n>N(\frac{y_0-x_0}{2^n}<\frac{y_0-x_0}{2^N}<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%3EN%28%5Cfrac%7By_0-x_0%7D%7B2%5En%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7By_0-x_0%7D%7B2%5EN%7D%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29) ，于是 ![\frac{y_0-x_0}{2^n}\rightarrow 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7By_0-x_0%7D%7B2%5En%7D%5Crightarrow+0) .

由于 ![\forall m,n\in\mathbb{N}(x_m\leq y_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+m%2Cn%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%28x_m%5Cleq+y_n%29) ，我们有 ![\forall\varepsilon>0\ \exists N\in\mathbb{N}\ \forall m>n>N(x_m-x_n<y_n-x_n<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%5C+%5Cexists+N%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5C+%5Cforall+m%3En%3EN%28x_m-x_n%3Cy_n-x_n%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29) ，即 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 是Cauchy序列. 同理 ![\{y_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7By_n%5C%7D) 也是Cauchy序列.

由于Cauchy序列收敛，我们设 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}x_n=L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7Dx_n%3DL) ，于是 ![\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}y_n=\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}(y_n-x_n)+\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}x_n=0+L=L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7Dy_n%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7D%28y_n-x_n%29%2B%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty%7Dx_n%3D0%2BL%3DL) . 我们要证明 ![L=\sup E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%3D%5Csup+E) ：

假设 ![L\notin \mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Cnotin+%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) ，则 ![\exists q\in E(q>L)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+q%5Cin+E%28q%3EL%29) . 令 ![\varepsilon=q-L>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3Dq-L%3E0) ，于是 ![\exists k\in\mathbb{N}(|y_k-L|=y_k-L<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+k%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%28%7Cy_k-L%7C%3Dy_k-L%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29) ，即 ![y_k<q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y_k%3Cq) ，与 ![y_k\in\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y_k%5Cin%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) 矛盾.

假设 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 不是 ![\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) 的最小元素，即 ![\exists L'\in\mathscr{U}(E)(L'<L)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+L%27%5Cin%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29%28L%27%3CL%29) . 令 ![\varepsilon=L’-L>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3DL%E2%80%99-L%3E0) ，于是 ![\exists s\in\mathbb{N}(|x_s-L|=L-x_s<\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+s%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%28%7Cx_s-L%7C%3DL-x_s%3C%5Cvarepsilon%29) ，即 ![x_s>L’](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_s%3EL%E2%80%99) ，与 ![L’\in\mathscr{U}(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%E2%80%99%5Cin%5Cmathscr%7BU%7D%28E%29) 矛盾.

综上，对每个上有界子集 ![E\in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，都存在 ![L=\sup E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%3D%5Csup+E) .
