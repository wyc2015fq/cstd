## **映射**
- **映射(mapping)**是两个集合之间的对应关系
- ![f:A\rightarrow B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AA%5Crightarrow+B) 表示非空集合 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 到非空集合 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 的一个映射.
- ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 称为**定义域(domain)**，![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 称为目标域(codomain/target)， ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 称为对应法则(relation)
- ![\forall x \in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x+%5Cin+A) ，有唯一的 ![f(x)\in B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%5Cin+B) 与之相对应. ![x\mapsto f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cmapsto+f%28x%29)
- **存在性、唯一性、封闭性**（画Venn图说明）
- 集合 ![\{f(x)|x\in A\}\subset B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf%28x%29%7Cx%5Cin+A%5C%7D%5Csubset+B) 称为**值域(range)**

> {中。美，英}到{北京，华盛顿，莫斯科，伦敦}按“国家对应的首都”的映射
{-3,-2,-1,1,2,3}到{1,4,9}按“取平方”的映射
- **单射(injection)：**![f:A\rightarrow B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AA%5Crightarrow+B) 称为单射，若 ![\forall x,y\in A,x\ne y\Rightarrow f(x)\ne f(y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x%2Cy%5Cin+A%2Cx%5Cne+y%5CRightarrow+f%28x%29%5Cne+f%28y%29)
- **满射(surjection)：**![f:A\rightarrow B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AA%5Crightarrow+B) 称为满射，若值域等于目标域( ![\forall y\in B,\exists x \in A, s.t.f(x)=y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+y%5Cin+B%2C%5Cexists+x+%5Cin+A%2C+s.t.f%28x%29%3Dy) )
- **双射(bijection)**：既是单射又是满射（一一对应）
- 画图说明单射、满射、双射
- 双射 ![f:A\rightarrow B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AA%5Crightarrow+B) ： ![\forall y \in B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+y+%5Cin+B) ，有唯一的 ![x\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+A) 使得 ![y=f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Df%28x%29) 与之相对应. 这样得到的映射 ![f^{-1}:B\rightarrow A, y\mapsto f^{-1}(y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%3AB%5Crightarrow+A%2C+y%5Cmapsto+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28y%29) 称为 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的**逆映射(inverse mapping)**
- **两个映射相等，若它们的定义域和对应关系相同**

## **函数的基本性质**
- **单调性**
- **增函数(increasing~)：**若区间 ![M\subset D_f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%5Csubset+D_f) 满足 ![\forall x_1,x_2\in M,x_1>x_2\Rightarrow f(x_1)>f(x_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x_1%2Cx_2%5Cin+M%2Cx_1%3Ex_2%5CRightarrow+f%28x_1%29%3Ef%28x_2%29) ，则称 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 在区间 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 上**单调递增**， ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 是 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 上的**增函数**，![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 是 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 的**(单调)增区间**
- 函数随自变量增大而增大 ![y=|x|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3D%7Cx%7C)
- **减函数(decreasing~)：**若区间 ![M\subset D_f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%5Csubset+D_f) 满足 ![\forall x_1,x_2\in M,x_1>x_2\Rightarrow f(x_1)<f(x_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x_1%2Cx_2%5Cin+M%2Cx_1%3Ex_2%5CRightarrow+f%28x_1%29%3Cf%28x_2%29) ，则称 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 在区间 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 上**单调递减**， ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 是 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 上的**减函数**，![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 是 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 的**(单调)减区间**
- 函数随自变量增大而减小
- 求单调区间：定义法、图像法

> 写出一次函数、反比例函数、二次函数、对勾函数的单调区间
- 复合函数的单调性：内外同增、内外同减为增；内增外减、内减外增为减（**同增异减**）
- **单调区间书写时只能写“和”不能写“并”**
- **奇偶性**
- **奇函数(odd~)：**若 ![\forall x\in D_f,f(-x)=-f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x%5Cin+D_f%2Cf%28-x%29%3D-f%28x%29) ，则称![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 是奇函数.
- 奇函数的图像关于原点对称
- 若奇函数 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 在 ![x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0) 处有定义，则 ![f(0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%280%29%3D0)
- **偶函数(even~)：**若 ![\forall x\in D_f,f(-x)=f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x%5Cin+D_f%2Cf%28-x%29%3Df%28x%29) ，则称![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 是偶函数.
- 偶函数的图像关于 ![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) 轴对称
- 证明奇偶性：定义法、图像法

> 奇函数：正比例函数、反比例函数、对勾函数
偶函数：常函数，绝对值函数 ![f(x)=|x|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D%7Cx%7C) ，抛物线 ![f(x)=x^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Dx%5E2)
既奇又偶： ![f(x)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D0)
非奇非偶：绝大部分函数
- 定义域相同的两个函数：
- 奇+奇=奇；偶+偶=偶；奇×奇=偶；偶×偶=偶；奇×偶=偶
- 偶函数在关于原点对称的区间上单调性相同
- 奇函数在关于原点对称的区间上单调性相反
- **周期性：**若 ![\exists T\ne 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+T%5Cne+0) 使得 ![\forall x\in D_f,f(x+T)=f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x%5Cin+D_f%2Cf%28x%2BT%29%3Df%28x%29) ，则称 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 是**周期函数(periodic function)**， ![T](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T) 称为 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 的**周期(period)**
- 若![T](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T) 是 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 的周期，则按定义， ![2T,3T,4T...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2T%2C3T%2C4T...) 也是周期
- 若 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 的周期里一个最小的正数，则该数称为 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 的**最小正周期(fundamental period)**
- 最小正周期不一定存在！
