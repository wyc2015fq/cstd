# 一个基数运算的命题证明，以及关于实数域和复数域等势的讨论 - 知乎
# 

> **Proposition:** 设 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是无限集， ![Y\subset A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Y%5Csubset+A) 是可数集且 ![A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+Y) 也是无限集，求证： ![\mathrm{card}\ A=\mathrm{card}\ A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A%5Cbackslash+Y)

今早被大佬翻牌子问到这个问题，正好最近处理一些基数运算的问题，就去查了一下.

(本文假定大一数学分析中关于基数的知识，所涉定理不再重复证明)

就该命题本身言，证明是不难的. 我们知道有限个可数集之并也是可数集. 既然 ![A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+Y) 是无限集，则它必有可数子集 ![B\subset A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%5Csubset+A%5Cbackslash+Y) . 而 ![B\cup Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%5Ccup+Y) 到 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 的双射容易构造：设 ![B=\{b_1,b_2,...\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%3D%5C%7Bb_1%2Cb_2%2C...%5C%7D) ， ![Y=\{y_1,y_2,...\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Y%3D%5C%7By_1%2Cy_2%2C...%5C%7D) ，双射 ![f:B\cup Y\rightarrow B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AB%5Ccup+Y%5Crightarrow+B)

![f(x)= \begin{cases} b_{2i} & x=b_i\\ b_{2i-1} & x=y_i \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+b_%7B2i%7D+%26+x%3Db_i%5C%5C+b_%7B2i-1%7D+%26+x%3Dy_i+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D)

而注意到 ![A=(A\backslash(B\cup Y))\cup B\cup Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%28A%5Cbackslash%28B%5Ccup+Y%29%29%5Ccup+B%5Ccup+Y) 且 ![A\backslash Y=(A\backslash(B \cup Y))\cup B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+Y%3D%28A%5Cbackslash%28B+%5Ccup+Y%29%29%5Ccup+B) ，于是有双射 ![g:A\rightarrow A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%3AA%5Crightarrow+A%5Cbackslash+Y)

![g(x)= \begin{cases} f(x) & x\in B\cup Y \\ x & x\in A\backslash(B\cup Y) \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+f%28x%29+%26+x%5Cin+B%5Ccup+Y+%5C%5C+x+%26+x%5Cin+A%5Cbackslash%28B%5Ccup+Y%29+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D)

从而有 ![\mathrm{card}\ A=\mathrm{card}\ A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A%5Cbackslash+Y) . QED.

## What if Y is not countable?

一般地我们可以得到如下定理：

> 设基数 ![\alpha \geq \beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%5Cgeq+%5Cbeta) 且 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 无限，则有 ![\alpha+\beta=\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%3D%5Calpha)  (Hungerford, Theorem 1.8.10)

证明主要依靠Zorn引理，用到的技巧被Hungerford称为"ordered by extension"，这个技巧最初在证明任意集合基数可比较 (Hungerford, Theorem 1.8.7) 时用到.

我们知道可数集是最小的无限集，也即 ![\mathrm{card}\ Y \leq \mathrm{card}\ A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+Y+%5Cleq+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A%5Cbackslash+Y) ，而 ![\mathrm{card}\ A=\mathrm{card}\ ((A\backslash Y) \cup Y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28%28A%5Cbackslash+Y%29+%5Ccup+Y%29) ，由上述定理就可以立刻得到 ![\mathrm{card}\ A=\mathrm{card}\ A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A%5Cbackslash+Y) 。现在让我们来证明这个定理.

设 ![X:=A\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3A%3DA%5Cbackslash+Y) ，把集合 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 复制两份，即定义 ![X_i=\{(x,i)|x\in X\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_i%3D%5C%7B%28x%2Ci%29%7Cx%5Cin+X%5C%7D) ，我们如果能证明 ![\mathrm{card}\ (X_1\cup X_2)=\mathrm{card}\ X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X_1%5Ccup+X_2%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X) ，则有 ![\mathrm{card}\ X \leq \mathrm{card}\ (X\cup Y)  \leq \mathrm{card}\ (X_1\cup X_2) = \mathrm{card}\ X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X+%5Cleq+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X%5Ccup+Y%29++%5Cleq+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X_1%5Ccup+X_2%29+%3D+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X) ，再应用Schroder-Bernstein定理就能得到 ![\mathrm{card}\ (X\cup Y)=\mathrm{card}\ X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X%5Ccup+Y%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X) 了.

> Schroder-Bernstein定理：若存在 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 到 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 的单射和 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 到 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的单射，则存在 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 与 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 之间的双射。
用基数表达就是：
![\mathrm{card}\ A \leq \mathrm{card}\ B\ \wedge\mathrm{card}\ B \leq \mathrm{card}\ A \Rightarrow \mathrm{card}\ A = \mathrm{card}\ B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A+%5Cleq+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+B%5C+%5Cwedge%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+B+%5Cleq+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A+%5CRightarrow+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+A+%3D+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+B)

我们把 ![X_1\cup X_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%5Ccup+X_2) 写作 ![X\times \{1,2\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Ctimes+%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D) ，为了找到双射 ![X\times \{1,2\}\rightarrow X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Ctimes+%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%5Crightarrow+X) ，我们需要借助Zorn引理. 具体说就是在一个双射构成的集合中，构造一个偏序关系，证明每个全序子集(chain)都有一个上界（which is still 双射），于是这个集合有一个最大元，即一个“最大的双射”.

> Zorn引理：若非空偏序集 ![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) 上每个全序子集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 都有上界，则 ![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) 有最大元.
上界：若 ![\alpha \in S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%5Cin+S) 满足 ![\forall \beta \in U\subset S\ (\alpha \geq \beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cbeta+%5Cin+U%5Csubset+S%5C+%28%5Calpha+%5Cgeq+%5Cbeta%29) ，则 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 称为 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 的一个上界.
最大元：若 ![\alpha \in S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%5Cin+S) 满足 ![\forall \beta \in S\ (\alpha \geq \beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cbeta+%5Cin+S%5C+%28%5Calpha+%5Cgeq+%5Cbeta%29) ，则 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 称为 ![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) 的最大元。注意 ![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) 未必是全序集，最大元 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 只需要大于所有可以与 ![\alpha ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+) 比较的元素即可.
可以证明Zorn引理与选择公理、良序原理相互等价，是独立于ZF集合论系统的.

设 ![\Omega :=\{(f,Z)|f:Z\times\{1,2\}\rightarrow Z\ \ \text{bijective},Z\subset X\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega+%3A%3D%5C%7B%28f%2CZ%29%7Cf%3AZ%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%5Crightarrow+Z%5C+%5C+%5Ctext%7Bbijective%7D%2CZ%5Csubset+X%5C%7D) ，其中的元素是双射与 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的子集构成的有序对 ![(f,Z)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2CZ%29) .

在应用Zorn引理前，我们必须保证 ![\Omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega) 不是一个空集。也就是我们必须找到一个 ![Z\subset X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Z%5Csubset+X) 和双射 ![Z\times\{1,2\}\rightarrow Z](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Z%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%5Crightarrow+Z) . 一个显然的例子是 ![Z=\mathbb{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Z%3D%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D) ，双射 ![f_\mathbb{N}:\mathbb{N}\times\{1,2\}\rightarrow \mathbb{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%3A%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%5Crightarrow+%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D) 可以用奇偶数给出：

![f_\mathbb{N} (n,i)= \begin{cases} 2n & i=1\\ 2n+1 & i=2 \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D+%28n%2Ci%29%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+2n+%26+i%3D1%5C%5C+2n%2B1+%26+i%3D2+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D)

 于是在 ![\Omega ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega+) 上可以定义如下的偏序关系：

![(f_1,Z_1)\leq(f_2,Z_2)\Leftrightarrow Z_1 \subset Z_2\ \mathrm{and}\ f_2|_{Z_1\times\{1,2\}}=f_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f_1%2CZ_1%29%5Cleq%28f_2%2CZ_2%29%5CLeftrightarrow+Z_1+%5Csubset+Z_2%5C+%5Cmathrm%7Band%7D%5C+f_2%7C_%7BZ_1%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%7D%3Df_1)

即 ![f_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_1) 是 ![f_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_2) 在一个更小子集上的限制.

考虑一个全序子集 ![\{(f_i,Z_i)|i\in I\} \subset \Omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28f_i%2CZ_i%29%7Ci%5Cin+I%5C%7D+%5Csubset+%5COmega) ，容易看出它的一个上界应该是 ![(f_m,Z_m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f_m%2CZ_m%29)，其中 ![Z_m = \bigcup_{i\in I}Z_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Z_m+%3D+%5Cbigcup_%7Bi%5Cin+I%7DZ_i) 且 ![f_m(x)=f_i(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_m%28x%29%3Df_i%28x%29) 对每个 ![i\in I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i%5Cin+I) . 不难验证 ![f_m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_m) 是一个双射，而且 ![(f_m,Z_m)\geq(f_i,Z_i)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f_m%2CZ_m%29%5Cgeq%28f_i%2CZ_i%29) 对每个 ![i\in I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i%5Cin+I)，也即 ![(f_m,Z_m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f_m%2CZ_m%29) 确实是一个上界. 由Zorn引理， ![\Omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega) 有最大元 ![(\bar{f},\bar{Z})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cbar%7Bf%7D%2C%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%29) . 也即存在一个双射 ![\bar{Z}\times\{1,2\}\rightarrow \bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%5Crightarrow+%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) ，或 ![\mathrm{card}\ (\bar{Z}\times\{1,2\})=\mathrm{card}\ \bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) .

可是我们还不能断言 ![\bar{Z}=X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%3DX) ，这个证明还要绕一个小弯：我们研究 ![X\backslash\bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Cbackslash%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) ，通过反证法说明 ![X\backslash\bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Cbackslash%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) 是有限集。而从基数上说，无限集 ![\bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) 并上有限集 ![X\backslash\bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Cbackslash%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) 是没有区别的（这个证明是构造性的，无需使用Zorn引理），这样就能说明 ![\mathrm{card}\ \bar{Z}=\mathrm{card}\ X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X) ，从而容易说明有 ![\mathrm{card}\ (X\times\{1,2\})=\mathrm{card}\ X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X) .

假设 ![X\backslash\bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Cbackslash%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) 是无限集，则它有可数子集 ![W \subset X\backslash\bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W+%5Csubset+X%5Cbackslash%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) ，上面的论证已经说明了，对可数集 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) ，存在双射 ![h: W\times\{1,2\}\rightarrow W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3A+W%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%5Crightarrow+W) ，那么我们就构造出了比 ![(\bar{f},\bar{Z})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cbar%7Bf%7D%2C%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%29) 更大的元素 ![(f_h,\bar{Z}\cup W)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f_h%2C%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%5Ccup+W%29) ：

![f_h(n,i)= \begin{cases} \bar{f}(n,i) & n\in\bar{Z} \\ h(n,i) & n\in W \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_h%28n%2Ci%29%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%5Cbar%7Bf%7D%28n%2Ci%29+%26+n%5Cin%5Cbar%7BZ%7D+%5C%5C+h%28n%2Ci%29+%26+n%5Cin+W+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D)

这与Zorn引理的假设矛盾，于是 ![X\backslash\bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Cbackslash%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) 是有限集。

> ![\mathrm{card}\ \bar{Z}=\mathrm{card}\ (\bar{Z}\cup (X\backslash\bar{Z}))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%5Ccup+%28X%5Cbackslash%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%29%29) 的证明： (Hungerford, Lemma 1.8.9)
假设 ![X\backslash\bar{Z}=\{k_1,...,k_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Cbackslash%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%3D%5C%7Bk_1%2C...%2Ck_n%5C%7D) ，设 ![\bar{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7BZ%7D) 有可数子集 ![T=\{x_1,...\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T%3D%5C%7Bx_1%2C...%5C%7D)
容易验证如下映射是双射：
![f(x)= \begin{cases} x &x\in \bar{Z}\backslash T \\ k_i & x=x_i,i=1,...,n \\ x_{i-k} & x=x_i,i>n \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+x+%26x%5Cin+%5Cbar%7BZ%7D%5Cbackslash+T+%5C%5C+k_i+%26+x%3Dx_i%2Ci%3D1%2C...%2Cn+%5C%5C+x_%7Bi-k%7D+%26+x%3Dx_i%2Ci%3En+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D)

## 更强的结论

从 ![\mathrm{card}\ (X\times\{1,2\})=\mathrm{card}\ X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X%5Ctimes%5C%7B1%2C2%5C%7D%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X) 出发，我们可以证明更强的结论： ![\mathrm{card}\ (X\times X)=\mathrm{card}\ X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X%5Ctimes+X%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X) ，定理一般可以表述为：

> 设基数 ![\alpha \geq \beta \neq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%5Cgeq+%5Cbeta+%5Cneq+0) 且 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 无限，则有 ![\alpha\beta=\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Cbeta%3D%5Calpha)  (Hungerford, Theorem 1.8.11)

证明技巧与上面几乎相同，即构造

![\Omega :=\{(f,Z)|f:Z\times Z\rightarrow Z\ \ \text{bijective},Z\subset X\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega+%3A%3D%5C%7B%28f%2CZ%29%7Cf%3AZ%5Ctimes+Z%5Crightarrow+Z%5C+%5C+%5Ctext%7Bbijective%7D%2CZ%5Csubset+X%5C%7D)

由Zorn引理，它有最大元 ![(g,Y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28g%2CY%29) . 我们观察到 ![\mathrm{card}\ (X\backslash Y)<\mathrm{card}\  Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X%5Cbackslash+Y%29%3C%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C++Y)，否则存在 ![X\backslash Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%5Cbackslash+Y) 的子集 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 使得 ![\mathrm{card}\ W = \mathrm{card}\ Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+W+%3D+%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+Y)，即同时存在 ![Y\times Y\rightarrow Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Y%5Ctimes+Y%5Crightarrow+Y) 和 ![W \times W \rightarrow W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W+%5Ctimes+W+%5Crightarrow+W) 上的双射，于是存在双射 ![h:(Y\cup W)\times (Y\cup W)\rightarrow Y\cup W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3A%28Y%5Ccup+W%29%5Ctimes+%28Y%5Ccup+W%29%5Crightarrow+Y%5Ccup+W) ，从而与Zorn引理矛盾.

于是我们有 ![\mathrm{card}\ (Y\times Y)=\mathrm{card}\ Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28Y%5Ctimes+Y%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+Y) 和 ![\mathrm{card}\ X=\mathrm{card}\ Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+Y) (由上个定理可得)，自然可以推出 ![\mathrm{card}\ (X\times X)=\mathrm{card}\ X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+%28X%5Ctimes+X%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bcard%7D%5C+X) .

这个定理有个有力的推论：
