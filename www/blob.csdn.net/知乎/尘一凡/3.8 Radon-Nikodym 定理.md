**3.8 Radon-Nikodym 定理**

这一节我们都在测度空间 ![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29) 中考虑，其中 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是[带号测度](http://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36652587)(signed measure)。

**Section 1 绝对连续（absolutely continuous）**

**定义1（绝对连续）**

设![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29)是测度空间（![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是[带号测度](http://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36652587)）， ![\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu) 是定义在 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 上的带号测度。我们称 ![\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu) 是相对于 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 绝对连续的（记作 ![\nu \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu+%5Cll+%5Cmu) ）,如果对任意可测集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ，当 ![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 时，有 ![\nu(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%29%3D0) 。

**注1**：符号 ![\ll](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cll) 可以理解为"远小于"的意思，照着这个理解，若 ![|\mu|=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%3D0) ,因为 ![\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu) "远小于" ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) ,因此也有 ![\nu=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%3D0) ，这与上面定义是符合的。

**注2**：回忆[3.5节定义1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37337326)，我们也定义过绝对连续：测度空间![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29)中（ ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是**正**测度）， ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是有限的带号测度。 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 相对于 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是**绝对连续的**（absolutely continuous），如果对任意![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0),存在![\delta>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3E0),使得对任意可测子集![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E)，当![\mu(E)<\delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cdelta)时，有![|\lambda(E)|<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E%29%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon)。

可以证明，满足3.5节定义的绝对连续，也是本节定义的绝对连续。另外，下面定理1还要证明：如果对任意可测集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ,若 ![|\mu|(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty) 可以推出 ![|\nu|(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty) ,那么两种定义是等价的。

**引理1**

设 ![\mu,\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%2C%5Cnu) 是测度空间 ![(X,\mathfrak{a})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%29) 上的两个**带号测度**，下面的三个命题是等价的：

(a) ![\nu \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu+%5Cll+%5Cmu) ；

(b) ![\nu^+ \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B+%5Cll+%5Cmu) 且有 ![\nu^- \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E-+%5Cll+%5Cmu) ；

(c) ![|\nu| \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C+%5Cll+%5Cmu) ；

> 设 ![X=A\cup B \ (A\geq0,B\leq0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA%5Ccup+B+%5C+%28A%5Cgeq0%2CB%5Cleq0%29) 是空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是Hahn 分解。

(i)"(a) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (b)"：（明确一下我们要证明什么？根据绝对连续的定义，我们要证的是： ![\forall E\in\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+E%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ,若 ![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) ，有 ![\nu^+(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29%3D0) 和 ![\nu^-(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29%3D0) 。）

任取可测集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 满足 ![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 。由 ![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 可推出![|\mu|(E\cap A)=|\mu|(E\cap B)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%5Ccap+A%29%3D%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%5Ccap+B%29%3D0) （见[1.6节习题2](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36652587)，下面附有直观理解）。
那么根据(a) ![\nu \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu+%5Cll+%5Cmu) ,就有 ![\nu(E\cap A)=0,\nu(E\cap B)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%5Ccap+A%29%3D0%2C%5Cnu%28E%5Ccap+B%29%3D0) 。
而由 ![\nu^+,\nu^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B%2C%5Cnu%5E-)的定义有：![\nu^+(E)=\nu(E\cap A),\nu^-(E)=\nu(E\cap B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29%3D%5Cnu%28E%5Ccap+A%29%2C%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29%3D%5Cnu%28E%5Ccap+B%29) 。
因此我们证明了 ![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 可推出![\nu^+(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29%3D0) 和 ![\nu^-(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29%3D0)，即是(b) ![\nu^+ \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B+%5Cll+%5Cmu) 且有 ![\nu^- \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E-+%5Cll+%5Cmu)

(ii)"(b) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (c)"：
由(b)有：![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 可推出![\nu^+(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29%3D0) 和 ![\nu^-(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29%3D0)。又因为 ![|\nu|(E)=\nu^+(E)+\nu^-(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3D%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29%2B%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29) ，故 ![|\nu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 。即是(c) ![|\nu| \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C+%5Cll+%5Cmu) 。

(iii)"(c) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (a)":
由(c) ![|\nu| \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C+%5Cll+%5Cmu) 有： ![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 推出 ![|\nu|(E)=\nu^+(E)+\nu^-(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3D%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29%2B%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29%3D0) ，而 ![\nu^+(E)\geq 0,\nu^-(E)\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29%5Cgeq+0%2C%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29%5Cgeq+0) ，故 ![\nu^+(E)=\nu^-(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29%3D%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29%3D0) 。那么就有 ![\nu(E)=\nu^+(E)-\nu^-(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%29%3D%5Cnu%5E%2B%28E%29-%5Cnu%5E-%28E%29%3D0) 。即是 ![\nu \ll \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu+%5Cll+%5Cmu) 。

**定理1**（在限制条件下，本节定义1和[3.5节定义1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37337326)是等价的）

设 ![\mu,\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%2C%5Cnu) 是测度空间 ![(X,\mathfrak{a})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%29) 上的两个**带号测度。**

如果有： ![|\mu|(F)<\infty, \forall F\in\mathfrak{a}\Rightarrow|\nu|(F)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28F%29%3C%5Cinfty%2C+%5Cforall+F%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%5CRightarrow%7C%5Cnu%7C%28F%29%3C%5Cinfty) ,

那么， ![|\nu|\ll |\mu|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%5Cll+%7C%5Cmu%7C)![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow) 对任意![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0),存在![\delta>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3E0),使得 ![\forall E\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，当![|\mu|(E)<\delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3C%5Cdelta)时，有![|\nu|(E)<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon)。

> *注：这个定理我理解不透彻，想不出为什么这么证明，只能照抄望讲义。望对这个定理证明思路理解深刻的朋友不吝告知。*
(i)" ![\Leftarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftarrow) ":
![\forall E\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，设![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0)。那么对任意小的 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ，都有 ![|\nu|(E)<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon) 。说明 ![|\nu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 。因此 ![|\nu|\ll |\mu|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%5Cll+%7C%5Cmu%7C) 。

(ii)" ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) ":
假设不然。那么就存在 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ,对任意 ![\delta>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3E0) ，存在 ![E\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ,当 ![|\mu|(E)< \delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3C+%5Cdelta) 时， ![|\nu|(E)\geq\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon) 。那么我们取 ![\delta=\frac{1}{2^n},n\geq1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5En%7D%2Cn%5Cgeq1) ,就得到集合序列 ![\{E_n\}\subseteq \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BE_n%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，满足当 ![|\mu|(E_n)< \frac1{2^n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E_n%29%3C+%5Cfrac1%7B2%5En%7D) 时， ![|\nu|(E_n)\geq\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E_n%29%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon)。
然后令 ![E=\overline{\lim_{n\to\infty}}E_n=\bigcap_{n\to\infty}^{\infty}\bigcup_{k=n}^{\infty}E_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3D%5Coverline%7B%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%7DE_n%3D%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbigcup_%7Bk%3Dn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_k) ，我们验证：(a) ![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) ;（b） ![|\nu|(E)\geq\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon) ；(如果有(a)(b)那么就与 ![|\nu|\ll |\mu|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%5Cll+%7C%5Cmu%7C) 矛盾，从而命题得证)
(a)对任意 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 都有：![|\mu|(E)\leq |\mu|(\bigcup_{k=n}^\infty E_k)\leq\sum_{k=n}^{\infty}|\mu|(E_k)\leq\sum_{k=n}^{\infty}\frac1{2^k}=\frac1{2^{n-1}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%5Cleq+%7C%5Cmu%7C%28%5Cbigcup_%7Bk%3Dn%7D%5E%5Cinfty+E_k%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bk%3Dn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E_k%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bk%3Dn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac1%7B2%5Ek%7D%3D%5Cfrac1%7B2%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D) 。
说明 ![|\mu|(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E%29%3D0) 。
(b)由[1.2节测度性质(8)](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/35850693)有： ![|\nu|(E)=|\nu|(\overline{\lim_{n\to\infty}}E_n)\geq\overline{\lim_{n\to\infty}}|\nu|(E_n)\geq\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3D%7C%5Cnu%7C%28%5Coverline%7B%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%7DE_n%29%5Cgeq%5Coverline%7B%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%7D%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E_n%29%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon)
(因为 ![|\mu|(\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n)\leq \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}|\mu|(E_n)\le \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac1{2^n}=1<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmu%7C%28%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%29%5Cleq+%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7C%5Cmu%7C%28E_n%29%5Cle+%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac1%7B2%5En%7D%3D1%3C%5Cinfty) ,由假设 ![|\nu|(\bigcup_{n=1}^\infty E_n)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%5Cinfty+E_n%29%3C%5Cinfty) ,因此满足1.2节测度性质(8)的条件)

**定理2（Rodon-Nikodym定理）**

测度空间为 ![(X,\mathfrak{a})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%29) , ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是 ![\color{blue}{\sigma\text{-finite measure}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%5Csigma%5Ctext%7B-finite+measure%7D%7D) , ![\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu) 是![\color{green}{\sigma\text{-finite signed measure}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B%5Csigma%5Ctext%7B-finite+signed+measure%7D%7D) ,且 ![\nu \ll\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu+%5Cll%5Cmu) 。

那么有：

(1) 存在 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的可测函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ,使得 ![\nu(E)=\int_Ef \ d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%29%3D%5Cint_Ef+%5C+d%5Cmu) ,其中 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是可测集且满足 ![|\nu|(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty) ; 

(2) ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是唯一的 ![[\mu]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B%5Cmu%5D) （在 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 意义下的）

**注1**： ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 没要求是可积的（可积是意思是在整个空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上可积）。如果 ![\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu) 是有限的带号测度，则 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 关于 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是可积的（若 ![|\nu|(X)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cnu%7C%28X%29%3C%5Cinfty) ,则 ![\nu(E)=\int f \ d \mu <\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%29%3D%5Cint+f+%5C+d+%5Cmu+%3C%5Cinfty) ,故![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 关于 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu)可积）。

**注2**：这里![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是唯一的 ![[\mu]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B%5Cmu%5D)是指：若也有可测函数 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 满足![\nu(E)=\int_Eg\ d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%29%3D%5Cint_Eg%5C+d%5Cmu) ，那么 ![f=g \text{ a. e }[\mu]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3Dg+%5Ctext%7B+a.+e+%7D%5B%5Cmu%5D) (在 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 意义下的)。

**定义3 (Rodon-Nikodym导数)**

定理2结论的函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 称为 ![\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu) 对 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 的Rodon-Nikodym导数，记做 ![\frac{d \nu}{d\mu}=f \ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bd+%5Cnu%7D%7Bd%5Cmu%7D%3Df+%5C+) （ ![\text{unique a. e. } [\mu]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bunique+a.+e.+%7D+%5B%5Cmu%5D) ）或者 ![{d \nu}=f{d\mu} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Bd+%5Cnu%7D%3Df%7Bd%5Cmu%7D+) ( ![\text{unique a. e. } [\mu]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bunique+a.+e.+%7D+%5B%5Cmu%5D) )。

> （证明Rodon-Nikodym定理）
证明思路（先在 ![\mu,\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%2C%5Cnu) 是有限测度下证明存在唯一函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 满足结论，然后推广到 ![\mu,\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%2C%5Cnu) 都是 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) -finit signed measure，方法是将空间分别按照 ![\mu,\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%2C%5Cnu) 分成可数的有限部分，对**每一**块相交的部分同时满足![\mu,\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%2C%5Cnu) 是有限测度，故存在函数 ![f_{ij}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_%7Bij%7D) ,最后将所有的 ![f_{ij}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_%7Bij%7D) 拼起来）

(Part1) 先假设![\mu,\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%2C%5Cnu) 是有限的(正）测度且 ![\nu\ll\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%5Cll%5Cmu)
（1）证明存在性：
(step1:构造出这个函数，记做 ![f_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_0) )
令集合 ![D=\{\hat{f}:\hat{f}\geq0,\text{measurable}, \& \int_E\hat{f }\leq\nu(E),\forall E\in \mathfrak{a} \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3D%5C%7B%5Chat%7Bf%7D%3A%5Chat%7Bf%7D%5Cgeq0%2C%5Ctext%7Bmeasurable%7D%2C+%5C%26+%5Cint_E%5Chat%7Bf+%7D%5Cleq%5Cnu%28E%29%2C%5Cforall+E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%5C%7D) (即 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 是某些函数 ![\hat{f}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Chat%7Bf%7D) 构成的集合，这些函数要求：非负、可测、且对任意可测集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 有 ![\int_E \hat{f}\leq\nu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_E+%5Chat%7Bf%7D%5Cleq%5Cnu%28E%29) )。那么 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 是非空的( 比如![\hat{f}=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Chat%7Bf%7D%3D0) 就属于 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) )。令 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 是 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D)中的函数在整个空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 积分的最大值，即 ![\alpha=\sup_{\hat{f}\in D}\{\int_X \hat{f}\ d\mu\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3D%5Csup_%7B%5Chat%7Bf%7D%5Cin+D%7D%5C%7B%5Cint_X+%5Chat%7Bf%7D%5C+d%5Cmu%5C%7D) 。因为 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 是最大值，那么就存在 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 中的函数序列 ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) ，它们的积分逼近这个最大值，即 ![\{f_n\}\subseteq D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+D) , ![\ \lim_{n\to\infty}\int f_n\ d\mu=\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C+%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+f_n%5C+d%5Cmu%3D%5Calpha) 。
令 ![g_n=\max\{f_1,\cdots,f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n%3D%5Cmax%5C%7Bf_1%2C%5Ccdots%2Cf_n%5C%7D) （pointwise）和 ![f_0=\sup_{n\geq1}\ g_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_0%3D%5Csup_%7Bn%5Cgeq1%7D%5C+g_n) (pointwise)。

(a) 那么 ![0\leq g_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleq+g_n) , ![g_n\leq g_{n+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n%5Cleq+g_%7Bn%2B1%7D) 且 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}g_n=f_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7Dg_n%3Df_0) ，且 ![g_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n) 是可积的（原因见[3.6节习题4](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37364245)）那么由[MCT](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37364245)，得 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int g_n \ d\mu=\int f_0d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+g_n+%5C+d%5Cmu%3D%5Cint+f_0d%5Cmu) 。又因为 ![g_n\geq f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n%5Cgeq+f_n) ,故 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int g_n\ d\mu\geq\lim_{n\to\infty}\int f_n\ d\mu=\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+g_n%5C+d%5Cmu%5Cgeq%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+f_n%5C+d%5Cmu%3D%5Calpha) 。因此 ![\int f_0d\mu\geq\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_0d%5Cmu%5Cgeq%5Calpha) 。

(b)另一方面， 有![\int f_0d\mu\leq\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_0d%5Cmu%5Cleq%5Calpha) ，原因：
显然有 ![\int_E g_n \ d\mu\leq \nu(E),\forall E\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_E+g_n+%5C+d%5Cmu%5Cleq+%5Cnu%28E%29%2C%5Cforall+E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) (仿照[3.6节习题4](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37364245)的方法，得到 ![E=\cup_{j=1}^nE_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3D%5Ccup_%7Bj%3D1%7D%5EnE_j) 。那么 ![\int_E g_n \ d\mu=\sum_{j=1}^n\int_{E_j} f_ j \ d\mu\leq\sum_{j=1}^n\mu(E_j)=\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_E+g_n+%5C+d%5Cmu%3D%5Csum_%7Bj%3D1%7D%5En%5Cint_%7BE_j%7D+f_+j+%5C+d%5Cmu%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bj%3D1%7D%5En%5Cmu%28E_j%29%3D%5Cmu%28E%29) )。
对 ![\chi_Eg_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cchi_Eg_n) 应用(a)的步骤，可证 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int \chi_Eg_n \ d\mu=\int \chi_Ef_0d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+%5Cchi_Eg_n+%5C+d%5Cmu%3D%5Cint+%5Cchi_Ef_0d%5Cmu) ，即是 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int_E g_n \ d\mu=\int_E f_0d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint_E+g_n+%5C+d%5Cmu%3D%5Cint_E+f_0d%5Cmu) 。而对任意 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 已证 ![\int_E g_n \ d\mu\leq \nu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_E+g_n+%5C+d%5Cmu%5Cleq+%5Cnu%28E%29) ，故 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int_E g_n \ d\mu\leq \nu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint_E+g_n+%5C+d%5Cmu%5Cleq+%5Cnu%28E%29) 。因此 ![\int_E f_0d\mu\leq \nu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_E+f_0d%5Cmu%5Cleq+%5Cnu%28E%29) ，即是 ![f_0\in D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_0%5Cin+D) 。因此 ![\int f_0d\mu\leq\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_0d%5Cmu%5Cleq%5Calpha) （根据上面![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha)的定义， ![\alpha=\sup_{\hat{f}\in D}\{\int_X \hat{f}\ d\mu\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3D%5Csup_%7B%5Chat%7Bf%7D%5Cin+D%7D%5C%7B%5Cint_X+%5Chat%7Bf%7D%5C+d%5Cmu%5C%7D) ）。
那么综合起来就有 ![\int f_0d\mu=\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_0d%5Cmu%3D%5Calpha) 。

接下来验证  ![\nu(E)=\int_E f_0d\mu,\forall E \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%29%3D%5Cint_E+f_0d%5Cmu%2C%5Cforall+E+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)  。我们令 ![\lambda(E)=\nu(E)-\int_E f_0d\mu,\forall E\in\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28E%29%3D%5Cnu%28E%29-%5Cint_E+f_0d%5Cmu%2C%5Cforall+E%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ,只需验证 ![\lambda\equiv0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5Cequiv0) （恒等于0）

(i)首先注意到 ![\lambda\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5Cgeq0) （因为 ![f_0 \in D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_0+%5Cin+D) ）,即 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是（正）测度。

(ii)我们考虑 ![\lambda-\frac1m\mu,\ m=\mathbb{Z}^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda-%5Cfrac1m%5Cmu%2C%5C+m%3D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B) ,则 ![\lambda-\frac1m\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda-%5Cfrac1m%5Cmu) 是一个signed measure。
令 ![X=A_m\cup B_m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA_m%5Ccup+B_m) 是 ![\lambda-\frac1m\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda-%5Cfrac1m%5Cmu) 的Hahn分解（ ![A_m \geq0,B_m\leq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_m+%5Cgeq0%2CB_m%5Cleq0) ）。
令 ![A_0=\cup_{m=1}^{\infty} A_m,B_0=\cap_{m=1}^{\infty} B_m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_0%3D%5Ccup_%7Bm%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+A_m%2CB_0%3D%5Ccap_%7Bm%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+B_m) 。
那么 ![X=A_0\cup B_0 \ \&\ A_0\cap B_0=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA_0%5Ccup+B_0+%5C+%5C%26%5C+A_0%5Ccap+B_0%3D%5Cvarnothing) 。
因为 ![B_0\subseteq B_m\ \&\ B_m\leq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_0%5Csubseteq+B_m%5C+%5C%26%5C+B_m%5Cleq0) ,所以 ![0\leq\lambda(B_0)\leq \frac1m\mu(B_0)\to0,\text{ as }m\to\infty ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleq%5Clambda%28B_0%29%5Cleq+%5Cfrac1m%5Cmu%28B_0%29%5Cto0%2C%5Ctext%7B+as+%7Dm%5Cto%5Cinfty+) 。
故 ![\lambda(B_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28B_0%29%3D0) 。

(iii)接下来证明在 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的另一半 ![A_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_0) ，也有 ![\lambda(A_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28A_0%29%3D0) 。
假设不然，即 ![\lambda(A_0)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28A_0%29%3E0) 。因为 ![\lambda \ll\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda+%5Cll%5Cmu) ,故 ![\mu(A_0)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28A_0%29%3E0) ( 思考为什么 ![\lambda \ll\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda+%5Cll%5Cmu) )。
那么存在某个 ![A_m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_m) 使得 ![\mu(A_m)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28A_m%29%3E0) （原因 ![0<\mu(A_0)\leq\sum_{m=1}^{\infty}\mu(A_m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3C%5Cmu%28A_0%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bm%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28A_m%29) ）。
令函数 ![h=f_0+\frac1m\chi_{A_m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3Df_0%2B%5Cfrac1m%5Cchi_%7BA_m%7D) ,则 ![h\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5Cgeq0) 。下面验证 ![h\in D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5Cin+D) 。
![\begin{align*} \int_Eh \ d \mu&=\int_Ef_0\ d\mu+{\frac1m\mu(E\cap A_m)}\\ \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Cint_Eh+%5C+d+%5Cmu%26%3D%5Cint_Ef_0%5C+d%5Cmu%2B%7B%5Cfrac1m%5Cmu%28E%5Ccap+A_m%29%7D%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D) 。因为 ![(\lambda-\frac1m\mu)(E\cap A_m)\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Clambda-%5Cfrac1m%5Cmu%29%28E%5Ccap+A_m%29%5Cgeq0) ,故 ![{\frac1m\mu(E\cap A_m)}\leq\lambda(E\cap A_m)=\nu(E\cap A_m)-\int_{E\cap A_m}f_0 d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7B%5Cfrac1m%5Cmu%28E%5Ccap+A_m%29%7D%5Cleq%5Clambda%28E%5Ccap+A_m%29%3D%5Cnu%28E%5Ccap+A_m%29-%5Cint_%7BE%5Ccap+A_m%7Df_0+d%5Cmu) 。
因此 ![\begin{align*} \int_Eh \ d \mu&=\int_Ef_0\ d\mu+{\frac1m\mu(E\cap A_m)}\\ &\leq\nu(E\cap A_m)+\int_{E\backslash A_m }f_0\ d\mu\\ &\leq\nu(E\cap A_m)+\nu(E\backslash A_m)(因为f_0\in D)\\ &=\nu(E) \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Cint_Eh+%5C+d+%5Cmu%26%3D%5Cint_Ef_0%5C+d%5Cmu%2B%7B%5Cfrac1m%5Cmu%28E%5Ccap+A_m%29%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Cleq%5Cnu%28E%5Ccap+A_m%29%2B%5Cint_%7BE%5Cbackslash+A_m+%7Df_0%5C+d%5Cmu%5C%5C+%26%5Cleq%5Cnu%28E%5Ccap+A_m%29%2B%5Cnu%28E%5Cbackslash+A_m%29%28%E5%9B%A0%E4%B8%BAf_0%5Cin+D%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Cnu%28E%29+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D) ,即![h\in D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5Cin+D)。
那么 ![\int h \ d\mu\leq\alpha=\int f_0d \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+h+%5C+d%5Cmu%5Cleq%5Calpha%3D%5Cint+f_0d+%5Cmu) (根据 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 的定义和(b)已证 ![\int f_0d\mu=\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_0d%5Cmu%3D%5Calpha) )。
然而另一方面， ![h=f_0+\frac1m\chi_{A_m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3Df_0%2B%5Cfrac1m%5Cchi_%7BA_m%7D) ，故 ![\int h\ d \mu=\int f_0\ d\mu+\frac1m+\mu(A_m)>\int f_0\ d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+h%5C+d+%5Cmu%3D%5Cint+f_0%5C+d%5Cmu%2B%5Cfrac1m%2B%5Cmu%28A_m%29%3E%5Cint+f_0%5C+d%5Cmu) 。从而得到矛盾。
因此 ![\lambda(A_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28A_0%29%3D0) 。结合(ii)就得到 ![\lambda(X)=\lambda(A_0)+\lambda(B_0)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28X%29%3D%5Clambda%28A_0%29%2B%5Clambda%28B_0%29%3D0) ,因此 ![\lambda\equiv0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5Cequiv0) 。

即是 ![\nu(E)=\int_E f_0d\mu,\forall E \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%29%3D%5Cint_E+f_0d%5Cmu%2C%5Cforall+E+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)
