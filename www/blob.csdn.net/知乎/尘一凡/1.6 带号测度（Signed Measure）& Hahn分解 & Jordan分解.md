**1.6 带号测度（Signed Measure)**


**Section 1 带号测度（signed measure）的定义**

**定义1 （带号测度）**

设 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X)为全空间， ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) -代数。集合函数 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 成为带号测度（signed measure），如果满足以下条件：

![\begin{align*} &(1) \ s :\mathfrak{a} \to (-\infty,\infty] \text{ or }[-\infty,\infty)\\ &(2)\ s(\varnothing) =0\\ &(3)\ s \text{ is contably additive}\\ \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%281%29+%5C+s+%3A%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%5Cto+%28-%5Cinfty%2C%5Cinfty%5D+%5Ctext%7B+or+%7D%5B-%5Cinfty%2C%5Cinfty%29%5C%5C+%26%282%29%5C+s%28%5Cvarnothing%29+%3D0%5C%5C+%26%283%29%5C+s+%5Ctext%7B+is+contably+additive%7D%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

> 注1：从定义知， ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 的定义域是![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) -代数，值域不能同时取到 ![- \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-+%5Cinfty) 和 ![+\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2B%5Cinfty) 。
注2：与测度不同，带号测度 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 可能没有单调性。

**例子 **设 ![\mu_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_1) 和 ![\mu_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_2) 是 ![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D) 上的测度，且其中一个是有限测度。令 ![s(E)=\mu_1(E)-\mu_2(E),\forall E\in \frak{a} \ (*式)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%3D%5Cmu_1%28E%29-%5Cmu_2%28E%29%2C%5Cforall+E%5Cin+%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D+%5C+%28%2A%E5%BC%8F%29) ，则可以验证 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是一个带号测度。

一般来说，定义域相同的测度的线性组合（组合系数为实数）也是带号测度，只要某个测度是有限的。接下来证明，任何一个带号测度 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 都可以表示成上面例子(*式)的形式。

**定义2（正集、负集和零集）**

设 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是测度空间 ![(X,\frak{a})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D%29) 上的带号测度。

如果对任意的 ![A\subseteq E,A\in\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+E%2CA%5Cin%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D) 有 ![s(A)\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29%5Cgeq+0) 成立，则称 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是关于 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 的 ![正集(positive \ set)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%E6%AD%A3%E9%9B%86%28positive+%5C+set%29) ，记为 ![E\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cgeq+0) 。

如果对任意的 ![A\subseteq E,A\in\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+E%2CA%5Cin%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D) 有 ![s(A)\leq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29%5Cleq+0) 成立，则称 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是关于 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s)的![负集(negative \ set)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%E8%B4%9F%E9%9B%86%28negative+%5C+set%29) ，记为 ![E\leq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cleq+0) 。

如果 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 既是正集又是负集，则称 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是零集（null set ），记作 ![E=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3D0) 。此时**等价于 **![\forall A ,A\subseteq E \ ,A\in\mathfrak{a},s(A)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+A+%2CA%5Csubseteq+E+%5C+%2CA%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2Cs%28A%29%3D0) 。

> .注1：正集就是其所有可测子集的带号测度都是大于等于0。负集就是其所有可测子集的带号测度都是小于等于0
注2：注意区分好 ![E\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cgeq0) 和 ![s(E)\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%5Cgeq+0) 的含义。

**引理1**

设 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是 ![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D) 上的带号测度。有以下结论成立：

![\begin{align*} &(1)\ E\geq0,F \in \mathfrak{a},F\subseteq E\Rightarrow F\geq0\\ &(2)\ E_n \geq0 \ \forall n\Rightarrow \cup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n \geq0\\ &(3)\ E\geq0,F \in \mathfrak{a},F\subseteq E\Rightarrow s(F)\leq s(E) \\ &(4) \ P\geq0,N\leq0\Rightarrow P\cap N=0 \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%281%29%5C+E%5Cgeq0%2CF+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2CF%5Csubseteq+E%5CRightarrow+F%5Cgeq0%5C%5C+%26%282%29%5C+E_n+%5Cgeq0+%5C+%5Cforall+n%5CRightarrow+%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n+%5Cgeq0%5C%5C+%26%283%29%5C+E%5Cgeq0%2CF+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2CF%5Csubseteq+E%5CRightarrow+s%28F%29%5Cleq+s%28E%29+%5C%5C+%26%284%29+%5C+P%5Cgeq0%2CN%5Cleq0%5CRightarrow+P%5Ccap+N%3D0+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

> (1)要证 ![F\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Cgeq0) ，就是证 ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) 的任意可测子集 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) ，有 ![s(A)\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29%5Cgeq0) 。
 可测子集![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 包含于 ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) ， ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F)又包含于 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ，故 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 包含于 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 。由假设 ![E\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cgeq0) ，由正集定义，可知 ![s(A)\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29%5Cgeq+0) 。至此，我们证明了可测子集![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A)![\subseteq](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csubseteq)![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F)，且![s(A)\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29%5Cgeq+0)。故 ![F\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Cgeq0)

(2)(证明思路可从 ![n=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D2) 画图推出)。证明 ![\cup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n \geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n+%5Cgeq0) ，就是证明任意可测子集 ![A\subseteq \cup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n) ，有![s(A)\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29%5Cgeq0)。
![\begin{align*} A\subseteq \cup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n\Rightarrow A &=(\cup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n)\cap A\\ &=\cup_{n=1}^{\infty}(E_n\cap A)\\ &=\cup_{n=1}^{\infty}((E_n\cap A)\backslash\cup_{i=1}^{n-1}(E_i\cap A)) \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+A%5Csubseteq+%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%5CRightarrow+A+%26%3D%28%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%29%5Ccap+A%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%28E_n%5Ccap+A%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%28%28E_n%5Ccap+A%29%5Cbackslash%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%28E_i%5Ccap+A%29%29+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)
对于不同的 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) ，![(E_n\cap A)\backslash\cup_{i=1}^{n-1}(E_i\cap A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28E_n%5Ccap+A%29%5Cbackslash%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%28E_i%5Ccap+A%29) 都是两两不相交的，且 ![s((E_n\cap A)\backslash\cup_{i=1}^{n-1}(E_i\cap A))\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28%28E_n%5Ccap+A%29%5Cbackslash%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%28E_i%5Ccap+A%29%29%5Cgeq+0) (因为包含 ![E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n) 且可测)。
故 ![s(A)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}s((E_n\cap A)\backslash\cup_{i=1}^{n-1}(E_i\cap A))\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Ds%28%28E_n%5Ccap+A%29%5Cbackslash%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%28E_i%5Ccap+A%29%29%5Cgeq+0) ，故![\cup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n \geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n+%5Cgeq0)。

(3) ![F\subseteq E\Rightarrow E=(E\backslash F)\cup F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+E%5CRightarrow+E%3D%28E%5Cbackslash+F%29%5Ccup+F) ，故 ![s(E)=s(E\backslash F)+s(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%3Ds%28E%5Cbackslash+F%29%2Bs%28F%29) 。而 ![E\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cgeq+0) ， ![E\backslash F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash+F) 是可测且包含于 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ，故 ![s(E\backslash F)\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%5Cbackslash+F%29%5Cgeq+0) ，则可知 ![s(E)\geq s(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%5Cgeq+s%28F%29) 。
(4)证明 ![P\cap N=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5Ccap+N%3D0) ，即是证明 ![P\cap N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5Ccap+N) 即是正集又是负集。任取可测子集 ![F\subseteq P\cap N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+P%5Ccap+N) ，则 ![F\subseteq P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+P) 且 ![F\subseteq N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+N) 。 ![F\subseteq P\Rightarrow s(F)\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+P%5CRightarrow+s%28F%29%5Cgeq0) ， ![F\subseteq N\Rightarrow s(F)\leq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+N%5CRightarrow+s%28F%29%5Cleq0) ，因此 ![s(F)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29%3D0) 。故 ![P\cap N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5Ccap+N) 是零集。

**Section 2 Hahn分解定理**

**定理1 （全空间****![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的Hahn 分解定理）**

设 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的带号测度，则可以将全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 分拆（partition）为不相交的可测的两个部分，其中一个部分关于 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是正的，另一部分关于 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是负的。写成数学语言如下： 

![\begin{align*} &s \text{ is signed measure on } X,\\ &\text{Then } \exists A,B\in \mathfrak{a} \ni A\geq0,B\leq0,A\cup B=X \ \& \ A \cap B=\varnothing \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26s+%5Ctext%7B+is+signed+measure+on+%7D+X%2C%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7BThen+%7D+%5Cexists+A%2CB%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%5Cni+A%5Cgeq0%2CB%5Cleq0%2CA%5Ccup+B%3DX+%5C+%5C%26+%5C+A+%5Ccap+B%3D%5Cvarnothing+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

> 注1：此时就说" ![A,B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 关于 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 的一个Hahn分解"，一般来说[分解不是唯一](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//math.stackexchange.com/questions/2400224/on-the-example-of-the-the-hahn-decomposition)的。
注2：定理的证明需要以下两个引理。

**引理2**

设 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是 ![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D) 上的带号测度。则有：

![F\subseteq E,E,F \in \mathfrak{a},|s(E)|<\infty\Rightarrow |s(F)|<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+E%2CE%2CF+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%7Cs%28E%29%7C%3C%5Cinfty%5CRightarrow+%7Cs%28F%29%7C%3C%5Cinfty)

> ![F\subseteq E\Rightarrow E=(E\backslash F)\cup F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+E%5CRightarrow+E%3D%28E%5Cbackslash+F%29%5Ccup+F) 。反证法，设 ![|s(F)|=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cs%28F%29%7C%3D%5Cinfty) ，那么 ![s(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29) 要么为 ![+\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2B%5Cinfty) ，要么为 ![-\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-%5Cinfty) 。
(i)如果 ![s(F)=+\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29%3D%2B%5Cinfty) ，则 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 的值域为 ![(-\infty,\infty]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28-%5Cinfty%2C%5Cinfty%5D) (由 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 定义 ![\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cinfty) 和 ![-\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-%5Cinfty) 不能同时取到)，则 ![E=(E\backslash F)\cup F\Rightarrow s(E)=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3D%28E%5Cbackslash+F%29%5Ccup+F%5CRightarrow+s%28E%29%3D%5Cinfty) 。与假设 ![|s(E)|<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cs%28E%29%7C%3C%5Cinfty) 矛盾，故只能 ![|s(F)|<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cs%28F%29%7C%3C%5Cinfty) 。
(ii)如果 ![s(F)=-\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29%3D-%5Cinfty)，证明类似。

**引理3 **

设 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是 ![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D) 上的带号测度。

如果 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 具有有限非负测度，则 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 包含一个正集 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 。写成数学语言：

![\text{ if } E\in \mathfrak{a},0 \leq s(E)<\infty\Rightarrow\exists A\geq0 \ \&\ \ A\subseteq E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7B+if+%7D+E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C0+%5Cleq+s%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty%5CRightarrow%5Cexists+A%5Cgeq0+%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5C+A%5Csubseteq+E) 。

> (a) 若 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 本身是正集，则证明完成。
(b) 若 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 不是正集，说明 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 存在某个可测子集 ![E_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_1) 有着负测度（i.e. ![s(E_1)<0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E_1%29%3C0) ）。我们取正整数里取最小的 ![n_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n_1) ，使得 ![s(E_1)<-\ \frac1{n_1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E_1%29%3C-%5C+%5Cfrac1%7Bn_1%7D) 成立。考虑 ![E\backslash E_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash+E_1) ，因为 ![s(E\backslash E_1)=s(E)-s(E_1)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%5Cbackslash+E_1%29%3Ds%28E%29-s%28E_1%29%3E0) 。如果![E\backslash E_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash+E_1) 是正集，则证明完成。
(c) 如果![E\backslash E_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash+E_1) 不是正集，则回归到(b)的情况。![E\backslash E_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash+E_1)存在某个可测子集 ![E_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_2) 有着负测度，且 ![s(E_2)<-\ \frac1{n_2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E_2%29%3C-%5C+%5Cfrac1%7Bn_2%7D) （ ![n_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n_2) 同样取使不等式成立的最小正整数）。
(d)依次做下去到第 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 步，如果 ![E\backslash \cup_{i=1}^{k-1} E_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash+%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bk-1%7D+E_i) 仍不是正集，则![E\backslash \cup_{i=1}^{k-1} E_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash+%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bk-1%7D+E_i)存在某个可测子集 ![E_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_k) 有着负测度，且 ![s(E_k)<-\ \frac1{n_k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E_k%29%3C-%5C+%5Cfrac1%7Bn_k%7D) （ ![n_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n_k) 同样取使不等式成立的最小正整数）。
(e)如果上述步骤没法终止，则令 ![A=E\backslash \cup_{k=1}^{\infty} E_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3DE%5Cbackslash+%5Ccup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+E_k) ，也即 ![E=A\cup( \cup_{k=1}^{\infty} E_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3DA%5Ccup%28+%5Ccup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+E_k%29) ，由我们的构造过程知道 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 和 ![{E_k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7BE_k%7D) 是两两不相交的，故 ![s(E)=s(A)+\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}s(E_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%3Ds%28A%29%2B%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Ds%28E_k%29) 。但由**假设 ![s(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty) 是一个有限值**，故 ![\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}s(E_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Ds%28E_k%29) 必需[绝对值收敛](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E7%25BB%259D%25E5%25AF%25B9%25E6%2594%25B6%25E6%2595%259B)（absolutely convergent ），推出 ![k\to \infty,\frac1{n_k} <|s(E_k)|\rightarrow0\Rightarrow n_k\to\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cto+%5Cinfty%2C%5Cfrac1%7Bn_k%7D+%3C%7Cs%28E_k%29%7C%5Crightarrow0%5CRightarrow+n_k%5Cto%5Cinfty) 。
(f) 接下来证明 ![A\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cgeq0) 。任取一可测子集 ![F\subseteq A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+A) ，由 ![A=E\backslash \cup_{k=1}^{\infty} E_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3DE%5Cbackslash+%5Ccup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+E_k) 得![F\subseteq E\backslash(\cup_{i=1}^{k}E_i), \forall k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+E%5Cbackslash%28%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bk%7DE_i%29%2C+%5Cforall+k) 。注意到 ![s(F)<-\frac1{n_k-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29%3C-%5Cfrac1%7Bn_k-1%7D) 不成立，这是因为若成立，则有 ![s(F)<-\frac1{n_k-1}<-\frac1{n_k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29%3C-%5Cfrac1%7Bn_k-1%7D%3C-%5Cfrac1%7Bn_k%7D) ，这和(d)的构造中 ![n_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n_k) 是最小的矛盾（因为有更小的 ![n_k-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n_k-1) ）。故![s(F)\geq-\frac1{n_k-1} ,\forall k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29%5Cgeq-%5Cfrac1%7Bn_k-1%7D+%2C%5Cforall+k) ，由（e）知， ![k\to \infty, n_k\to\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cto+%5Cinfty%2C+n_k%5Cto%5Cinfty) 。而从得到 ![s(F)\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29%5Cgeq0) ，至此证明了 ![A\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cgeq0) 。

[接下来证明Hahn分解定理](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.math.uwaterloo.ca/~beforres/PMath451/Course_Notes/Chapter4.pdf)**。**

> 假设 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 的值域为 ![[-\infty,\infty)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B-%5Cinfty%2C%5Cinfty%29) ，不取 ![+\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2B%5Cinfty) （若 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 值域![(-\infty,\infty]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28-%5Cinfty%2C%5Cinfty%5D) ，讨论 ![-s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-s) 即可）。

对于全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的所有正集 ![A_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_0) ，取![\alpha=\mathop {\sup }\limits_{A_0\geq0}s(A_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3D%5Cmathop+%7B%5Csup+%7D%5Climits_%7BA_0%5Cgeq0%7Ds%28A_0%29) 。因为 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 是上确界且有限，所以存在序列 ![A_j\geq0 ,\forall j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_j%5Cgeq0+%2C%5Cforall+j) ，使得 ![s(A_j)\to \alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A_j%29%5Cto+%5Calpha) 。令 ![A=\bigcup_{j=1}^{\infty}A_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bj%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DA_j) ，则 ![A\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cgeq0) （引理1的(2)），则 ![ s(A)\leq\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+s%28A%29%5Cleq%5Calpha) （ ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 是正集测度的上确界）。另一方面， ![A=\bigcup_{j=1}^{\infty}A_j\geq A_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bj%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DA_j%5Cgeq+A_j) ，故 ![s(A)\geq s(A_j)\rightarrow \alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29%5Cgeq+s%28A_j%29%5Crightarrow+%5Calpha) (引理1的(3))，故 ![s(A) \geq\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28A%29+%5Cgeq%5Calpha) 。因此 ![0 \leq s(A)=\alpha <\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0+%5Cleq+s%28A%29%3D%5Calpha+%3C%5Cinfty) 。

令 ![B=X\backslash A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%3DX%5Cbackslash+A) ，我们断定 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 是负集。若![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 不是负集，则存在可测子集 ![E\subseteq B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubseteq+B) 且 ![s(E)\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%5Cgeq+0) 。而由引理3，存在一个正集 ![A'\subseteq E,s(A')\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%27%5Csubseteq+E%2Cs%28A%27%29%5Cgeq0) 。 ![A'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%27) 和 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 不相交， ![A'\cup A\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%27%5Ccup+A%5Cgeq0) ，那么 ![\alpha \geq s(A\cup A')=s(A)+s(A')=\alpha+s(A')>\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%5Cgeq+s%28A%5Ccup+A%27%29%3Ds%28A%29%2Bs%28A%27%29%3D%5Calpha%2Bs%28A%27%29%3E%5Calpha) ，这就得到 ![\alpha>\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3E%5Calpha) 的矛盾。因此![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 是负集。 

**命题1 (Hahn分解定理不唯一性)**

若全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 存在零集 ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow)![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的Hahn分解不唯一。

（等价命题）若全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 不存在零集 ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow)![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的Hahn分解唯一。

> " ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) "：（证明是构造性的，思路来自画图）若全空间存在零集 ![N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N) ，且全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的Hahn分解为 ![X=A\cup B \ \&\ A\cap B=\varnothing,A\geq0,B\leq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA%5Ccup+B+%5C+%5C%26%5C+A%5Ccap+B%3D%5Cvarnothing%2CA%5Cgeq0%2CB%5Cleq0) 。则 ![N=(N\cap A)\cup(N\cap B) \mathop = \limits^{记做} N_A\cup N_B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%3D%28N%5Ccap+A%29%5Ccup%28N%5Ccap+B%29+%5Cmathop+%3D+%5Climits%5E%7B%E8%AE%B0%E5%81%9A%7D+N_A%5Ccup+N_B) ，则 ![N_A,N_B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_A%2CN_B) 不相交。
任取子集 ![N'\subseteq N_A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%27%5Csubseteq+N_A) ，令 ![A'=A\backslash N',B'=B\cup N'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%27%3DA%5Cbackslash+N%27%2CB%27%3DB%5Ccup+N%27) 。我们断定 ![A'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%27) 和 ![B'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%27) 也是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的一个Hahn分解。容易验证 ![A'\cup B'=X ,A'\cap B'=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%27%5Ccup+B%27%3DX+%2CA%27%5Ccap+B%27%3D%5Cvarnothing) ，只剩验证 ![A’\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%E2%80%99%5Cgeq0) 和 ![B'\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%27%5Cgeq0) 。任取可测子集 ![E\subseteq A'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubseteq+A%27) ，即是 ![E\subseteq A\backslash N' \subseteq A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubseteq+A%5Cbackslash+N%27+%5Csubseteq+A) ,而 ![A\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cgeq+0) ，故 ![s(E)\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%5Cgeq0) ，从而 ![A'\geq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%27%5Cgeq+0) 。任取可测子集 ![F\subseteq B'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+B%27) ，即是 ![F\subseteq B\cup N'\Rightarrow F=(F\cap B)\cup(F \cap N')](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Csubseteq+B%5Ccup+N%27%5CRightarrow+F%3D%28F%5Ccap+B%29%5Ccup%28F+%5Ccap+N%27%29) ,故 ![s(F)=s(F\cap B)+s(F\cap N')\leq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28F%29%3Ds%28F%5Ccap+B%29%2Bs%28F%5Ccap+N%27%29%5Cleq0) (这一步跳了很多步，思考为什么)因此 ![B’\leq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%E2%80%99%5Cleq+0) 。

" ![\Leftarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftarrow) "：若 ![X=A\cup B \ \&\ A\cap B=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA%5Ccup+B+%5C+%5C%26%5C+A%5Ccap+B%3D%5Cvarnothing) 和 ![X=A’\cup B' \ \&\ A'\cap B'=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA%E2%80%99%5Ccup+B%27+%5C+%5C%26%5C+A%27%5Ccap+B%27%3D%5Cvarnothing) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的两个Hahn分解。则存在零集，比如 ![A\backslash A'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+A%27) 。任取可测子集 ![N\subseteq A\backslash A'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%5Csubseteq+A%5Cbackslash+A%27) ，因为 ![A\backslash A'=A\cap A'^c=A\cap B'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+A%27%3DA%5Ccap+A%27%5Ec%3DA%5Ccap+B%27) 。故 ![N \subseteq A \ \&\ N\subseteq B ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N+%5Csubseteq+A+%5C+%5C%26%5C+N%5Csubseteq+B+) ，得到 ![s(N)\geq0 \ \&\ s(N)\leq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28N%29%5Cgeq0+%5C+%5C%26%5C+s%28N%29%5Cleq+0) ，即 ![A\backslash A'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+A%27) 同时是正集和负集，故是零集。

**Section 3 Jordan分解定理**

![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的带号测度， ![X=A\cup B \ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA%5Ccup+B+%5C+)是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的Hahn分解，其中 ![A\geq0,B \leq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cgeq0%2CB+%5Cleq0) 。

![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 中任一可测子集，定义

![\ \ \ \ \ \ \ s^+(E)=s(E\cap A),\ \ \ \ \ \ \ s^-(E)=-s(E\cap B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+s%5E%2B%28E%29%3Ds%28E%5Ccap+A%29%2C%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+s%5E-%28E%29%3D-s%28E%5Ccap+B%29)

则 ![s^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B) 和 ![s^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E-) 就是**测度，**且其中一个测度是有限的。另外还有 ![s(E)=s^+(E)-s^-(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%29%3Ds%5E%2B%28E%29-s%5E-%28E%29) ，这个结果就叫带号测度的Jordan分解定理，下面证明这个定理。

> 注：注意带号测度和测度的区别。

**定理2 （带号测度的Jordan分解定理）**

设 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的带号测度，则存在测度 ![\mu_1,\mu_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_1%2C%5Cmu_2) ，其中一个是有限测度，使得 ![s=\mu_1-\mu_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%3D%5Cmu_1-%5Cmu_2) 。

注：一般来说， ![\mu_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_1) 和 ![\mu_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_2) 不唯一，比如 ![s=\mu_1-\mu_2=(\mu_1+\mu_0)-(\mu_2+\mu_0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%3D%5Cmu_1-%5Cmu_2%3D%28%5Cmu_1%2B%5Cmu_0%29-%28%5Cmu_2%2B%5Cmu_0%29) 。

> 对任意 ![E\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，取 ![\mu_1(E)=s^+(E)=s(E\cap A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_1%28E%29%3Ds%5E%2B%28E%29%3Ds%28E%5Ccap+A%29) 和 ![\mu_2(E)=s^-(E)=-s(E\cap B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_2%28E%29%3Ds%5E-%28E%29%3D-s%28E%5Ccap+B%29) 。
(i) 证明 ![s^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B) 和 ![s^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E-) 是测度。
(a) ![s^+,s^-:\mathfrak{a} \to [0,\infty]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B%2Cs%5E-%3A%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%5Cto+%5B0%2C%5Cinfty%5D)
(b) ![s^+(\varnothing)=s(\varnothing)=0; s^-(\varnothing)=s(\varnothing)=0;](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3Ds%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%3B+s%5E-%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3Ds%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%3B)
(c)对于两两互斥的 ![E_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_i) 有：![s^+(\cup_{i=1}^{\infty}E_i) =s((\cup_{i=1}^{\infty}E_i)\cap A) =s(\cup_{i=1}^{\infty}(E_i\cap A)) =\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}s(E_i\cap A) =\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}s^+(E_i)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B%28%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_i%29+%3Ds%28%28%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_i%29%5Ccap+A%29+%3Ds%28%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%28E_i%5Ccap+A%29%29+%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Ds%28E_i%5Ccap+A%29+%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Ds%5E%2B%28E_i%29)
![s^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E-) 同理可证。
综合(a)(b)(c)可知 ![s^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B) 和 ![s^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E-) 是测度。

(ii) ![s^+(X)=s(X\cap A)=s(A),s^-(X)=s(X\cap B)=s(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B%28X%29%3Ds%28X%5Ccap+A%29%3Ds%28A%29%2Cs%5E-%28X%29%3Ds%28X%5Ccap+B%29%3Ds%28B%29) ，由上面Hahn分解定理的证明“![0 \leq s(A)=\alpha <\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0+%5Cleq+s%28A%29%3D%5Calpha+%3C%5Cinfty)”可知 ![s^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B) 或 ![s^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E-) 其中一个是有限测度。

(iii) ![s^+(E)-s^-(E)=s(E\cap A)+s(E\cap B)=s((E\cap A)\cup(E\cap B))=s(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B%28E%29-s%5E-%28E%29%3Ds%28E%5Ccap+A%29%2Bs%28E%5Ccap+B%29%3Ds%28%28E%5Ccap+A%29%5Ccup%28E%5Ccap+B%29%29%3Ds%28E%29)
所以 ![s=s^+-s^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%3Ds%5E%2B-s%5E-) 。

由上面证明可知，带号测度的Jordan分解是建立在全空间的Hahn分解之上的，而Hahn分解是不唯一的，现在问题是，不同的Hahn分解是否有不同的Jordan分解？答案是否定的，即是**Jordan分解不依赖于特定的Hahn分解**，见下面命题。

**命题2（Jordan分解不依赖于特定的Hahn分解）**

设 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的带号测度， ![X=A_1\cup B_1 \ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA_1%5Ccup+B_1+%5C+) 和 ![X=A_2\cup B_2 \ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA_2%5Ccup+B_2+%5C+) 是两个Hahn分解，证明对任意的可测子集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ，有 ![s(E\cap A_1)=s(E\cap A_2),s(E\cap B_1)=s(E\cap B_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%5Ccap+A_1%29%3Ds%28E%5Ccap+A_2%29%2Cs%28E%5Ccap+B_1%29%3Ds%28E%5Ccap+B_2%29) ，也即 ![s^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E%2B) 和 ![s^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5E-) 不依赖特定的Hahn分解。

> (i) ![E\cap A_1=((E\cap A_1)\backslash A_2)\cup(E\cap A_1\cap A_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Ccap+A_1%3D%28%28E%5Ccap+A_1%29%5Cbackslash+A_2%29%5Ccup%28E%5Ccap+A_1%5Ccap+A_2%29) ，则 ![s(E\cap A_1)=s((E\cap A_1)\backslash A_2)+s(E\cap A_1\cap A_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%5Ccap+A_1%29%3Ds%28%28E%5Ccap+A_1%29%5Cbackslash+A_2%29%2Bs%28E%5Ccap+A_1%5Ccap+A_2%29) 。同理 ![s(E\cap A_2)=s((E\cap A_2)\backslash A_1)+s(E\cap A_1\cap A_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%5Ccap+A_2%29%3Ds%28%28E%5Ccap+A_2%29%5Cbackslash+A_1%29%2Bs%28E%5Ccap+A_1%5Ccap+A_2%29) 。容易证明 ![(E\cap A_1)\backslash A_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28E%5Ccap+A_1%29%5Cbackslash+A_2)
和 ![(E\cap A_2)\backslash A_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28E%5Ccap+A_2%29%5Cbackslash+A_1) 都是零集（仿照命题1的做法），故 ![s((E\cap A_1)\backslash A_2)=s((E\cap A_2)\backslash A_1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28%28E%5Ccap+A_1%29%5Cbackslash+A_2%29%3Ds%28%28E%5Ccap+A_2%29%5Cbackslash+A_1%29%3D0) 。即得 ![s(E\cap A_1)=s(E\cap A_1\cap A_2)=s(E\cap A_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%5Ccap+A_1%29%3Ds%28E%5Ccap+A_1%5Ccap+A_2%29%3Ds%28E%5Ccap+A_2%29) 。
(ii) 同(i)的思路可证明 ![s(E\cap B_1)=s(E\cap B_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%28E%5Ccap+B_1%29%3Ds%28E%5Ccap+B_2%29)

**定义5**

设 ![\mu,\nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%2C%5Cnu) 是 ![(X,\mathfrak{a})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%29) 上的两个测度，称它们是相互垂直的(mutually singular)，如果对 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的分拆 ![A,B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB) （即 ![X=A\cup B \ \&\ A\cap B=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DA%5Ccup+B+%5C+%5C%26%5C+A%5Ccap+B%3D%5Cvarnothing) ）， ![\color{red}{同时}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B%E5%90%8C%E6%97%B6%7D) 有 ![\mu(A)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28A%29%3D0) 和 ![\nu(B)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28B%29%3D0) 。 此时，符号记作 ![\color{red}{\ \ \mu\bot\nu}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B%5C+%5C+%5Cmu%5Cbot%5Cnu%7D) 。
