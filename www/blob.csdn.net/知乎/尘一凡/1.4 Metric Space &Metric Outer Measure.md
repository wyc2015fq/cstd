**Section 1 度量空间（Mettic Space）**

**定义1 （度量 metric）**

设有空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) ，实值函数 ![\rho：X \times X \to R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%EF%BC%9AX+%5Ctimes+X+%5Cto+R) 成为一个度量（metric），如果满足：

![\begin{align*} &(1)\ \rho(x,y)\geq0\\ &(2)\ \rho(x,y)=0\Leftrightarrow x=y\\ &(3)\ \rho(x,y)=\rho(y,x)\ (\text{symmetry})\ \\ &(4)\ \rho(x,z)\leq\rho(x,y)+\rho(y,z) \ (\text{triangle inequality}) \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%281%29%5C+%5Crho%28x%2Cy%29%5Cgeq0%5C%5C+%26%282%29%5C+%5Crho%28x%2Cy%29%3D0%5CLeftrightarrow+x%3Dy%5C%5C+%26%283%29%5C+%5Crho%28x%2Cy%29%3D%5Crho%28y%2Cx%29%5C+%28%5Ctext%7Bsymmetry%7D%29%5C+%5C%5C+%26%284%29%5C+%5Crho%28x%2Cz%29%5Cleq%5Crho%28x%2Cy%29%2B%5Crho%28y%2Cz%29+%5C+%28%5Ctext%7Btriangle+inequality%7D%29+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

> 注1：函数 ![\rho](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho) 被称为度量函数、度量或者距离函数。
注2：给空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 定义度量，也就是说空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上任意两个元素有了“距离”。

**定义2 （度量空间 metric space）**

设有空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X)，在其上配备了度量 ![\rho](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho) 。那么 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 和 ![\rho](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho) 配合成的pair ![(X,\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Crho%29) 就叫做度量空间（metric space）。

> 注：也就是说度量空间的二个要素是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 和 ![\rho](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho) ，一个要素不同，得到的度量空间也就不同。

**例子   **![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维欧几里得空间 ![R^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%5En) ，函数![\rho(x,y)=\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n(x_i-y_i)^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28x%2Cy%29%3D%5Csqrt%7B%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%28x_i-y_i%29%5E2%7D) ，可以验证满足度量定义的条件，故 ![\rho](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho) 是一个度量函数，这个度量函数有个特别的名字，叫“欧几里得度量”。

定义完空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 任意两点的距离，接下来定义与整个集合相关的距离。

**定义3 （子集**![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A)**和**![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B)**的距离）**

设 ![A,B\subseteq X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB%5Csubseteq+X) ，则 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 和 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 的距离定义为： ![\rho (A,B) = \mathop {\inf}\limits_{x \in A,y \in B} \rho (x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho+%28A%2CB%29+%3D+%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cinf%7D%5Climits_%7Bx+%5Cin+A%2Cy+%5Cin+B%7D+%5Crho+%28x%2Cy%29)

**定义4   (点 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 到子集 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的距离)**

设 ![A\subseteq X,x\in X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+X%2Cx%5Cin+X) ，则 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 和  ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A)的距离定义为： ![\rho (x,A) = \mathop {\inf}\limits_{y \in A} \rho (x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho+%28x%2CA%29+%3D+%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cinf%7D%5Climits_%7By+%5Cin+A%7D+%5Crho+%28x%2Cy%29)

**定义4 （子集 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的直径diameter）**

设 ![A\subseteq X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+X) ，定义 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的直径（diameter）为： ![d(A) = \mathop {\sup}\limits_{x,y \in A} \rho (x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28A%29+%3D+%5Cmathop+%7B%5Csup%7D%5Climits_%7Bx%2Cy+%5Cin+A%7D+%5Crho+%28x%2Cy%29)

**定义5 （领域、极限点(聚点)&孤立点&内点，闭集&开集&闭包&、有界集）**

**(a)邻域：**点 ![p\in X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+X) 的 ![\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon) -邻域( ![\varepsilon-\text{neighborhood}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon-%5Ctext%7Bneighborhood%7D) )是指集合 ![B_\varepsilon(p)=\{x\in X: \rho(x,p)<\varepsilon,\varepsilon>0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+X%3A+%5Crho%28x%2Cp%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon%2C%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%5C%7D)

> 注1: ![B_\varepsilon(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29) 就是以 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 为圆心，以 ![\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon) 为半径，在度量 ![\rho](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho) 意义下的“球”，所以 ![B_\varepsilon(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29) 也叫 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的 ![\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon) -开球（open ball）。
注2： 对![B_\varepsilon(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29) 可定义半径，为其直径的一半，即半径 ![r(B_\varepsilon(p))=d(B_\varepsilon(p))/2=\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%28B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29%29%3Dd%28B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29%29%2F2%3D%5Cvarepsilon)

**(b)极限点(聚点)：**点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 叫做集合 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的极限点（limit point / accumulation point），如果 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的每一个邻域 ![B_\varepsilon(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29) 都包含 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的一点 ![q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=q) ，且 ![q\ne p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=q%5Cne+p) 。

> 注：强调 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的一点 ![q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=q)**异于**![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的。

**(c)孤立点：**![p \in E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p+%5Cin+E) 且 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 不是 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的极限点，就称 ![p ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p+) 是![ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+)![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的孤立点（isolated point）

**(d)内点：**![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 是集合 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的内点（interior point），如果存在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的邻域 ![B_\varepsilon(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29) ，使得 ![B_\varepsilon(p)\subseteq E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29%5Csubseteq+E) 。

**(e)闭集**：集合![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 叫做闭的(closed)，如果 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的每个极限点都是 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的点。

**(f)开集：**集合![ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+)![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 叫做开的（open），如果 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的每个点都是 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的内点。

**(g)闭包：**![E\subseteq X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubseteq+X) ，令 ![E'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%27) 表示 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 在 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 中所有极限点组成的集合，则把 ![\bar{E}=E\cup E'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7BE%7D%3DE%5Ccup+E%27) 叫做 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的闭包（closure）。

**(h)内部：**集合 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 所有内点组成的集合叫做 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的内部（interior），记作 ![\text{int } E ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bint+%7D+E+) （或者 ![\mathop E\limits^ \circ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathop+E%5Climits%5E+%5Ccirc+) ）

**(i)有界集：**集合 ![E\subseteq X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubseteq+X) 叫做有界的（bounded），如果它的直径是有限的，即 ![d(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty) 。


**定义6 (收敛序列）**

度量空间中的序列 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 称为**收敛序列**（convergent sequence），如果存在某点 ![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) 使得 ![\rho(x_n,y) \to 0 ,n\to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28x_n%2Cy%29+%5Cto+0+%2Cn%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 。此时就说 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 收敛于 ![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) (或者说 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 的极限是 ![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) )，记作 ![\[\mathop {{\rm{lim}}}\limits_{n \to \infty } x_n = y\]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cmathop+%7B%7B%5Crm%7Blim%7D%7D%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+x_n+%3D+y%5C%5D) 或者 ![x_n \to y,n\to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n+%5Cto+y%2Cn%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 。

> 注1：序列 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 中每个 ![x_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n) 都是度量空间的点。
注2： ![\rho(x_n,y) \to 0 ,n\to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28x_n%2Cy%29+%5Cto+0+%2Cn%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 的定义为 ![\exists N>0,\forall \varepsilon>0, \text{when } n\geq N,\rho(x_n,y) < \varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N%3E0%2C%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%2C+%5Ctext%7Bwhen+%7D+n%5Cgeq+N%2C%5Crho%28x_n%2Cy%29+%3C+%5Cvarepsilon) 。用 ![\rho(x_n,y) \to 0 ,n\to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28x_n%2Cy%29+%5Cto+0+%2Cn%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 这种说法对于分析问题很方便。

![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta)**命题1（极限点的等价定义）**

点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 是集合 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的极限点 ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow) 存在 ![E\backslash\{p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash%5C%7Bp%5C%7D) 中的一个序列 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 收敛于点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p)

> (i)“ ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) ”: 若点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 是集合 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的极限点，那么由定义 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的每一个邻域 ![B_\varepsilon(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29) 都包含 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的一点 ![q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=q) ，且 ![q\ne p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=q%5Cne+p) 。用这个定义就可以构造一个序列。一种构造是这样的：我们先取 ![\varepsilon_1=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon_1%3D1) 得到邻域 ![B_1(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_1%28p%29) ，那么就有一个异于 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点 ![x_1\in E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_1%5Cin+E) 。我们再取 ![\varepsilon_2=\frac{1}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon_2%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D) ，得到邻域 ![B_{1/2}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%7B1%2F2%7D%28p%29) ，那么就有一个异于 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点 ![x_2\in E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_2%5Cin+E)。如此做下去，到第 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 次，可取 ![\varepsilon_n=\frac{1}{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon_n%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D) ，得到邻域 ![B_{1/n}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%7B1%2Fn%7D%28p%29) ，那么就有一个异于 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点 ![x_n\in E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n%5Cin+E)。当 ![n \to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n+%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 时，我们就得到一个序列 ![\{x_1,x_2,...x_n,...\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_1%2Cx_2%2C...x_n%2C...%5C%7D) 每个 ![x_n\ne p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n%5Cne+p) 且 ![\rho(x_n,p)\leq d(B_{1/n}(p))\rightarrow 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28x_n%2Cp%29%5Cleq+d%28B_%7B1%2Fn%7D%28p%29%29%5Crightarrow+0) 。
也就是说我们找到了 ![E\backslash\{p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash%5C%7Bp%5C%7D) 中的一个序列 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 收敛于点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p)。
(ii)" ![\Leftarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftarrow) "：存在 ![E\backslash\{p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cbackslash%5C%7Bp%5C%7D) 中的一个序列 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 收敛于点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p)，即是 ![\exists N>0,\forall \varepsilon>0, \text{when } n\geq N,\rho(x_n,p) < \varepsilon ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+N%3E0%2C%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%2C+%5Ctext%7Bwhen+%7D+n%5Cgeq+N%2C%5Crho%28x_n%2Cp%29+%3C+%5Cvarepsilon+) ，而 ![\rho(x_n,p) < \varepsilon ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28x_n%2Cp%29+%3C+%5Cvarepsilon+) 的含义就是 ![x_n \in B_\varepsilon(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n+%5Cin+B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29) 。也就是说 ![p ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p+) 的任意一个邻域 ![B_\varepsilon(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%5Cvarepsilon%28p%29) 总包含 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的点 ![x_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n) 且 ![x_n\ne p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n%5Cne+p) ,因此 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 是 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的极限点。

注：这正是为什么叫 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) “极限点”的原因

**![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta) 命题2（闭集的等价定义）**

度量空间中， ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是闭集 ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow) 对于 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 中的序列 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) ,若![{x_n} \to p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Bx_n%7D+%5Cto+p) ,则 ![p\in E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+E)

（也就是说，闭集中的收敛序列的收敛点仍在该闭集内）

> 结合闭集的定义和上面命题1中极限点的等价定义就可以证明。

注1：这个等价定义说明为什么叫闭集，因为闭集收敛的序列封闭
注2：这个等价定义很有用，要记忆和理解，以后证明会经常用到，特别是在泛函分析

**定义7（柯西序列）**

度量空间 ![(X,\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Crho%29) 中，序列 ![\{x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx_n%5C%7D) 叫做柯西序列（Cauchy sequence），如果对任意 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ，存在正整数 ![N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N) ，使得 ![n,m\geq N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2Cm%5Cgeq+N) 时有 ![\rho(x_n,x_m)<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28x_n%2Cx_m%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon) （即 ![\rho(x_n,x_m)\to0\ \text{ as }\ n,m \to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28x_n%2Cx_m%29%5Cto0%5C+%5Ctext%7B+as+%7D%5C+n%2Cm+%5Cto+%5Cinfty) ）

> 注：利用度量 ![\rho](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho) 的三角不等式，容易验证，每一个收敛的序列都是柯西序列

**定义8（度量空间完备性）**

**度量空间(**![(X，\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%EF%BC%8C%5Crho%29) 是完备的（complete），如果其中每一个柯西序列都收敛。

> 注：这个定义很常用，要背熟。

**定义9 （傅雷尔集Borel sets）**

度量空间 ![ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+)中一切开集构成的开集族所生成的 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) -代数称为傅雷尔 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) -代数（Borel  ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) -algebra），记作 ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) ， ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) 中的元素叫做傅雷尔集(Borel sets)，即 ![ \mathscr{B}=\{\text{Borel sets}\}=\{\sigma \text{-algebra generated by open sets of }X\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Ctext%7BBorel+sets%7D%5C%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Csigma+%5Ctext%7B-algebra+generated+by+open+sets+of+%7DX%5C%7D)

> 注1：傅雷尔 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) -代数可以这么理解，首先给定 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 中所有的开集。第一步，在这些开集之间执行交、并、补、可数并运算生成新的集合，把新生成的集合添加到原来的开集得到新集合里。第二步，新集合里的元素（集合）再进行交、并、补、可数并运算，重复第一步的过程，指导不产生新集合为止。（当然，傅雷尔集个数很多没法列出）
注2：闭集、开集都是Borel sets；Borel sets的补集是Borel sets；Borel集列的交、并、上下极限都是Borel sets
注2: ![\text{Borel }\sigma\text{-algebra}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BBorel+%7D%5Csigma%5Ctext%7B-algebra%7D) 是包含所有开集的最小 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数。
注3： ![\begin{align*} \text{Borel }\sigma\text{-algebra}\ \mathscr{B}&=\{\sigma \text{-algebra generated by open sets of }X\}\\ &=\{\sigma \text{-ring generated by open sets of }X\}\\ &=\{\sigma \text{-ring generated by closed sets of }X\} \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Ctext%7BBorel+%7D%5Csigma%5Ctext%7B-algebra%7D%5C+%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%26%3D%5C%7B%5Csigma+%5Ctext%7B-algebra+generated+by+open+sets+of+%7DX%5C%7D%5C%5C+%26%3D%5C%7B%5Csigma+%5Ctext%7B-ring+generated+by+open+sets+of+%7DX%5C%7D%5C%5C+%26%3D%5C%7B%5Csigma+%5Ctext%7B-ring+generated+by+closed+sets+of+%7DX%5C%7D+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

**Section 2 Metric outer measure**

接下来介绍与metric配合的outer measure，称为metric touter measure

**定义10 （metric outer measure）**

设有度量空间 ![(X,\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Crho%29)，外测度 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 称为metric outer measure，如果满足以下性质： ![A,B\subseteq X, \text{if }\rho(A,B)>0\Rightarrow \mu^*(A\cup B)= \mu^*(A)+\mu^*(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB%5Csubseteq+X%2C+%5Ctext%7Bif+%7D%5Crho%28A%2CB%29%3E0%5CRightarrow+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Ccup+B%29%3D+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29)

> 注1：![\rho(A,B)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28A%2CB%29%3E0)![\[\frac{ \Rightarrow }{{\not \Leftarrow }}\]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cfrac%7B+%5CRightarrow+%7D%7B%7B%5Cnot+%5CLeftarrow+%7D%7D%5C%5D)![A\cap B=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Ccap+B%3D%5Cvarnothing) 。
注2：metric outer measure首先是外测度，满足外测度定义的三个性质，另外还满足上述与度量配合的性质。

**引理1**

设 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是metric outer measure，设有集合 ![A,B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB) 满足 ![A\subseteq B ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+B+) 并且 ![B ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B+) 是开集，令集合列 ![A_n=\{x \in A:\rho(x,B^c)\geq \frac{1}{n}, n\in \mathbb{Z}^+\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_n%3D%5C%7Bx+%5Cin+A%3A%5Crho%28x%2CB%5Ec%29%5Cgeq+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%2C+n%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B%5C%7D) ，则 ![\mathop {\lim }\limits_{n \to \infty } \mu^*(A_n)=\mu^*(A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A_n%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29) .

> 引理证明不重要，主要是用于证明下面定理1。

**定理1 （开/闭集是可测集）**

设 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是度量空间 ![(X,\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Crho%29) 上的metric outer measure ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow) 闭集（和开集）是可测集。
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-0f350d29d7f94edd9bfdf6d74220adf9_b.jpg)
> 只证明 ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) 方向， ![\Leftarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftarrow) 方向作为习题。
设 ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) 是任一闭集，取任一集合 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 。只需验证 ![\mu^*(A)\geq\mu^*(A\backslash F)+\mu^*(A\cap F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cgeq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+F%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Ccap+F%29) 。因为 ![\mu^*(A)\leq\mu^*(A\backslash F)+\mu^*(A\cap F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+F%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Ccap+F%29) 对于任意外测度恒成立（次可加性）。

(i) ![\mu^*(A)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3C%5Cinfty) 。则 ![A\backslash F\subseteq F^c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+F%5Csubseteq+F%5Ec) 而 ![F^c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%5Ec) 是开集。由上面引理1，对于集合列 ![E_n=\{x \in A\backslash F:\rho(x,F)\geq \frac{1}{n}, n\in \mathbb{Z}^+\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n%3D%5C%7Bx+%5Cin+A%5Cbackslash+F%3A%5Crho%28x%2CF%29%5Cgeq+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%2C+n%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B%5C%7D) 有 ![\mathop {\lim }\limits_{n \to \infty } \mu^*(E_n)=\mu^*(A\backslash F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E_n%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+F%29) 。
那么因为 ![A\supseteq E_n\cup (A\cap F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csupseteq+E_n%5Ccup+%28A%5Ccap+F%29) ，由外测度的单调性有： ![\mu^*(A)\geq \mu^*(E_n\cup (A\cap F))=\mu^*(E_n)+\mu^*(A\cap F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cgeq+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E_n%5Ccup+%28A%5Ccap+F%29%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E_n%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Ccap+F%29) (等号成立是因为 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是metric outer measure)。上面等式对于任意 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 恒成立，令 ![n \to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n+%5Cto+%5Cinfty) ， ![ \mu^*(E_n)\to\mu^*(A\backslash F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E_n%29%5Cto%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+F%29) 。即得 ![\mu^*(A)\geq\mu^*(A\backslash F)+\mu^*(A\cap F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cgeq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+F%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Ccap+F%29) 。

(ii)若 ![\mu^*(A)=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cinfty) ,不等式 ![\mu^*(A)\geq\mu^*(A\backslash F)+\mu^*(A\cap F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cgeq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+F%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Ccap+F%29) 是显然的。

**定理2 (Borel sets都是可测的)**

如果 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是metric outer space，则任一Borel set 都是可测的。

> 令 ![\mathfrak{a}=\{\mu^* \text{-measurable sets}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Cmu%5E%2A+%5Ctext%7B-measurable+sets%7D%5C%7D) , ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 是一个 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数(？)。每一个开集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 都可以表示为全集 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 和某一闭集 ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) 的差（？），即 ![E=X\backslash F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3DX%5Cbackslash+F) ，而 ![X \in \mathfrak{a} \ \& \ F\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%5C+%5C%26+%5C+F%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) （？），故 ![E \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) （？），即**![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 包含所有的开集**。而![\text{Borel }\sigma\text{-algebra}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BBorel+%7D%5Csigma%5Ctext%7B-algebra%7D)![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) 是由所有开集**生成**的 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数，（由生成的定义），也就是说Borel sets ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D) }是包含所有开集的**最小**![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数。因此 ![\mathscr{B}\subseteq\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D%5Csubseteq%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ( ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)**包含所有的开集且是**![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数)，而 ![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D) 又是所有可测集构成的集族，故 ![\mathscr{B}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BB%7D)中的元素Borel sets都是可测集。

**练习1 **证明定理1的 ![\Leftarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftarrow) 方向，即：如果 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是外测度且每个闭集都可测，则 ![\mu^* ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A+) 是metric outer measure。

> 若闭集 ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) 都是可测，则可证明开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 都是可测（只需令 ![U=X\backslash F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%3DX%5Cbackslash+F) ,再考虑到可测集构成一个 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数即可证）。
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-42626e71b31f50064d7b2e6ddd445ae1_b.jpg)

![\mu^*(A\cup B)= \mu^*((A\cup B)\cap U)+\mu^*((A\cup B)\cap U^c) \ (*) =\mu^*(A)+\mu^*(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Ccup+B%29%3D+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28%28A%5Ccup+B%29%5Ccap+U%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28%28A%5Ccup+B%29%5Ccap+U%5Ec%29+%5C+%28%2A%29+%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29)

接下来证明这样的 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 是存在的。先固定一点 ![x\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+A) ，作 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 的邻域 ![B_x=\{p\in X: \rho(p,x)<\frac{1}{2}\rho(A,B)\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_x%3D%5C%7Bp%5Cin+X%3A+%5Crho%28p%2Cx%29%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%5Crho%28A%2CB%29%5C%7D) , ![B_x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_x) 是开集。令 ![U=\bigcup_{x\in A} B_x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bx%5Cin+A%7D+B_x) ，则 ![ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+)![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 是开集且 ![A\subseteq U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+U) 。接下来证明 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 和 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 不相交，任取点 ![y\in B,z \in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5Cin+B%2Cz+%5Cin+U) ，则 ![\exists x\in A\ \ni \ z\in B_x \ \&\ \rho(x,z)< \frac{1}{2}\rho(A,B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+x%5Cin+A%5C+%5Cni+%5C+z%5Cin+B_x+%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5Crho%28x%2Cz%29%3C+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%5Crho%28A%2CB%29),而 ![\rho(y,z)\geq\rho(x,y)-\rho(x,z)>\rho(A,B)- \frac{1}{2}\rho(A,B)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%28y%2Cz%29%5Cgeq%5Crho%28x%2Cy%29-%5Crho%28x%2Cz%29%3E%5Crho%28A%2CB%29-+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%5Crho%28A%2CB%29%3E0) ，即 ![y\notin B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5Cnotin+B) ,故 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 和 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 不相交。证毕。

**Section 3 Construction of metric outer measure**

![(X,\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Crho%29) 是度量空间， ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 为sequential covering class。

令 ![K_n=\{ A \in K:d(A)\leq \frac1n\}\cup\{\varnothing\},n\geq 1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_n%3D%5C%7B+A+%5Cin+K%3Ad%28A%29%5Cleq+%5Cfrac1n%5C%7D%5Ccup%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%5C%7D%2Cn%5Cgeq+1) ，（这里我们定义 ![d(\varnothing)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0) ）并假设 ![K_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_n) 也是sequential covering class。

> 一般来说， ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 是sequential covering class， ![K_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_n) 不一定是 sequential covering class。例如 ![K=\{[n,n+1]:n \in \mathbb{Z}\} \cup \{\varnothing\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%3D%5C%7B%5Bn%2Cn%2B1%5D%3An+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5C%7D+%5Ccup+%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%5C%7D) 是 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 上的sequential covering class，但 ![K_2=\{\varnothing\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_2%3D%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%5C%7D) 就不是了。

设集合函数 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 满足: ![\lambda:K \to [0,\infty]\ \&\ \lambda(\varnothing)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%3AK+%5Cto+%5B0%2C%5Cinfty%5D%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5Clambda%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0) 。定义 ![\lambda_n=\lambda|_{K_n}:K_n\to[0,\infty]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_n%3D%5Clambda%7C_%7BK_n%7D%3AK_n%5Cto%5B0%2C%5Cinfty%5D) 。设 ![\mu^*_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_n) 是关于 ![K_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_n) 和 ![\lambda_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_n) 的外测度，即是 ![\mu^*_n(A)=\inf \{\sum_k\lambda(E_k):A\subseteq \bigcup_{k=1}^{\infty}E_k\ \&\ \ \forall k ,d(E_k) \leq \frac1n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_n%28A%29%3D%5Cinf+%5C%7B%5Csum_k%5Clambda%28E_k%29%3AA%5Csubseteq+%5Cbigcup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_k%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5C+%5Cforall+k+%2Cd%28E_k%29+%5Cleq+%5Cfrac1n%5C%7D) 。

那么 ![K_{n+1}\subseteq K_{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_%7Bn%2B1%7D%5Csubseteq+K_%7Bn%7D) 且 ![\mu^*_n(A)\leq\mu^*_{n+1}(A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_n%28A%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A_%7Bn%2B1%7D%28A%29) (用 ![K_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_n) 来覆盖 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的方式比用 ![K_{n+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_%7Bn%2B1%7D) 多，故 ![\mu^*_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_n) 能取到更小的下界)

定义 ![\mu^*_0(A)=\lim_{n\to \infty} \mu^*_n(A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_0%28A%29%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D+%5Cmu%5E%2A_n%28A%29) ，那么有以下定理：

**定理3  **按照上述方式定义的 ![\mu^*_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_0) 是metric outer measure。

> 待证，其实不重要。

至此，总结一下： ![(X,\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Crho%29) 是度量空间，由 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 和 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 决定外测度 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) ；由 ![K_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_n) 和 ![\lambda_n=\lambda|_{K_n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda_n%3D%5Clambda%7C_%7BK_n%7D) 决定外测度 ![\mu_n^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_n%5E%2A) ；并且 ![\mu_n^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_n%5E%2A) 单调递增至 ![\mu_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_0) ，即 ![\mu^*_0=\lim_{n\to \infty} \mu^*_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_0%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D+%5Cmu%5E%2A_n) 。现在问题是，是否有 ![\mu^*=\mu^*_0 ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A_0+) ？答案是有可能成立！以下定理说明满足另外条件时，由 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 和 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 决定的外测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是metric outer measure，且等于 ![\mu^*_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_0) 。

**定理4 （外测度成为metric outer measure需要满足的条件）**

设 ![K,K_n,\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%2CK_n%2C%5Clambda) 如前面所述。如果满足另外的条件： ![\begin{align*} &\forall A \in K,\forall \varepsilon>0,\forall n\geq1,\\ &\exists\{E_k\}\in K_n\ni A\subseteq\bigcup_{k=1}^{\infty}E_k \ \& \ \sum_{k=1}^{\infty}\lambda(E_k)\leq \lambda(A)+\varepsilon \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Cforall+A+%5Cin+K%2C%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%2C%5Cforall+n%5Cgeq1%2C%5C%5C+%26%5Cexists%5C%7BE_k%5C%7D%5Cin+K_n%5Cni+A%5Csubseteq%5Cbigcup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_k+%5C+%5C%26+%5C+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%28E_k%29%5Cleq+%5Clambda%28A%29%2B%5Cvarepsilon+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D) ，则 ![\mu^*=\mu^*_0 ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A_0+) 。

> (i)验证“ ![\leq](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleq) ”：对任意集合 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) ，因为 ![K_n\subseteq K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_n%5Csubseteq+K) ，故有 ![\mu^*(B)\leq\mu^*_n(B),\forall n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A_n%28B%29%2C%5Cforall+n)。
(ii)验证“ ![\geq](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cgeq) ”：由 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 的定义知，对于任意集合 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 和 ![\varepsilon >0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon+%3E0) ，存在 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 里面的序列 ![\{A_j\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BA_j%5C%7D) ，使得 ![B\subseteq \bigcup_{j=1}^{\infty}A_j ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%5Csubseteq+%5Cbigcup_%7Bj%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DA_j+) 和 ![\sum_{j=1}^{\infty}\lambda(A_j)\leq \mu^*(B)+\frac{\varepsilon}{2} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bj%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%28A_j%29%5Cleq+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cvarepsilon%7D%7B2%7D+) 成立。
由另外的条件，对于每个 ![A_j \in K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_j+%5Cin+K) ，存在 ![K_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_n) 中的序列 ![\{E_{jk}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BE_%7Bjk%7D%5C%7D) ，使得有 ![ A_j\subseteq\bigcup_{k=1}^{\infty}E_{jk} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+A_j%5Csubseteq%5Cbigcup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_%7Bjk%7D+) 和 ![ \ \sum_{k=1}^{\infty}\lambda(E_{jk})\leq \lambda(A_j)+\frac{\varepsilon}{2^{j+1}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5C+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%28E_%7Bjk%7D%29%5Cleq+%5Clambda%28A_j%29%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cvarepsilon%7D%7B2%5E%7Bj%2B1%7D%7D) 。
因此， ![\bigcup_{j,k}^{}E_{jk} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcup_%7Bj%2Ck%7D%5E%7B%7DE_%7Bjk%7D+) 就覆盖了 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 。我们有 ![\mu^*_n (B)\leq \sum_{j,k=1}^{\infty}\lambda(E_{jk})\leq \sum_{j=1}^{\infty}\lambda(A_{j})+\frac{\varepsilon}{2} \leq \mu^*(B)+\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_n+%28B%29%5Cleq+%5Csum_%7Bj%2Ck%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%28E_%7Bjk%7D%29%5Cleq+%5Csum_%7Bj%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%28A_%7Bj%7D%29%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cvarepsilon%7D%7B2%7D+%5Cleq+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29%2B%5Cvarepsilon) ，让 ![\varepsilon \to 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon+%5Cto+0) ,就得到 ![\mu^*_n (B)\leq \mu^*(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_n+%28B%29%5Cleq+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29) 。
(iii) 综合(i)和(ii)，就得到 ![\mu^*_n (B)= \mu^*(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_n+%28B%29%3D+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29) 。令 ![n \to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n+%5Cto+%5Cinfty) ，就得到 ![\mu^*_0 (B)= \mu^*(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_0+%28B%29%3D+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29)

**总结**

至此，我们总结一下，如何构造度量空间上的测度。

设有度量空间 ![(X,\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Crho%29) 。

Step 1: 根据[1.3节section 1外测度的抽象构造](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36161036)，我们可以构造很多不同的外测度。

Step 2: 但这些外测度不一定能够与度量 ![\rho](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho) 配合，于是根据[1.4节section的定理4](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36438199)筛选出能够成为metric outer measure的外测度 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 。

Step 3: 利用[1.2节section 2的Carathéodory判别条件](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/35850693)，筛选出空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 里的![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A)可测集 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 。

step 4：根据[1.2节section2的定理1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/35850693)，将外测度 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) （ ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 也是metric outer measure）限制在![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A)可测集 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 上，就得到测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) ，即 ![\mu=\mu^*|_\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%7C_%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 。

**例子 （**![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的Lebesgue测度**）**

取 ![X=\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，度量 ![\rho=|x-y|,x,y\in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%3D%7Cx-y%7C%2Cx%2Cy%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 。

那么，Lebesgue外测度是外测度的一种构造，且满足1.4节section定理4，因此也是一种metric outer measure。根据[Carathéodory判别条件](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/35850693)，筛选出 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的可测集，构成Lebesgue可测集。Lebesgue外测度限制在Lebesgue可测集上，就叫Lebesgue测度。

> 根据下一节，Lebesgue可测集包括Borel set。
