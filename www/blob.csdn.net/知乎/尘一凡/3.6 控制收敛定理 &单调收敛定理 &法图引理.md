**3.6 控制收敛定理 &单调收敛定理 &法图引理**

**Section 1 控制收敛定理（Dominated convergence theorem）**

现在我们先抛出两个问题：

(1)若 ![f_n \to f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+%5Cto+f) , ![f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n) 可积，那么 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 不一定可积。（见例1）

(2)甚至，若 ![f_n \to f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+%5Cto+f) , ![f_n,f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%2Cf) 可积，也不能推出 ![\int f_n\to\int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_n%5Cto%5Cint+f) （见例2）

现在问题来了，若 ![f_n \to f \text{ a.e}( \text{ or in measure})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D%28+%5Ctext%7B+or+in+measure%7D%29) ，需要再加什么条件，才能保证 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可积且 ![\int f_n\to\int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_n%5Cto%5Cint+f) 呢？或何时极限和积分可交换次序，即 ![\lim_{n\to \infty}\int f_n=\int\lim_{n\to \infty}f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+f_n%3D%5Cint%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7Df_n) ？

我们给出答案，只需再加以下其中一个条件：

(a) ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) 是Cauchy in mean；

(b)（控制收敛定理）：对任意 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) ,![|f_n|\leq g\text{ a .e }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf_n%7C%5Cleq+g%5Ctext%7B+a+.e+%7D) 且 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 可积；

(c)（单调收敛定理）： ![f_n\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cgeq0) 且 ![f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n) 单调递增，几乎处处收敛于 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 。

注：条件(a)实际是就是3.3节所证明的，但比较不实用；比较实用的是(b)和(c)

**例1**

**(**![f_n\to f,f_n \text{ integrable} \not\Rightarrow f \text{ integrable}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f%2Cf_n+%5Ctext%7B+integrable%7D+%5Cnot%5CRightarrow+f+%5Ctext%7B+integrable%7D)**)**

设在 ![[0,1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2C1%5D) 上的函数序列 ![f_n(x)=\begin{cases} \frac1x,\quad \text{if }\frac1n \leq x\leq 1 \\ 0 ,\quad \text{otherwise}\end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%28x%29%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%5Cfrac1x%2C%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bif+%7D%5Cfrac1n+%5Cleq+x%5Cleq+1+%5C%5C+0+%2C%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Botherwise%7D%5Cend%7Bcases%7D) ， 函数![f(x)= \begin{cases}\frac1x,\quad \text{if }0<x\leq1\ \\0,\quad \text{if }x=0 \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D%5Cfrac1x%2C%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bif+%7D0%3Cx%5Cleq1%5C+%5C%5C0%2C%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bif+%7Dx%3D0+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D) 。

则 ![f_n\to f \text{ a.e.} \ \&\ \text{in measure}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e.%7D+%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5Ctext%7Bin+measure%7D) 且 ![f_n ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+) 可积，但是 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 不可积（[见3.3节习题3](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37233441)）。

**例2**

![(f_n\to f,f_n,f \text{ integrable} \not\Rightarrow \int f_n \to \int f )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f_n%5Cto+f%2Cf_n%2Cf+%5Ctext%7B+integrable%7D+%5Cnot%5CRightarrow+%5Cint+f_n+%5Cto+%5Cint+f+%29)

设在 ![[0,1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2C1%5D) 上的函数序列 ![f_n=n\chi_{[1-\frac1n,1]}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%3Dn%5Cchi_%7B%5B1-%5Cfrac1n%2C1%5D%7D) ,函数 ![f=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3D0) 。则![f_n\to f \text{ a.e.} \ \&\ \text{in measure}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e.%7D+%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5Ctext%7Bin+measure%7D) 且 ![f_n ,f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+%2Cf) 可积。但是 ![\int f_n=1 \not\to \int f =\int0=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_n%3D1+%5Cnot%5Cto+%5Cint+f+%3D%5Cint0%3D0)

**定理1（控制收敛定理(Dominated Convergence Theorem),简写为DCT）**

测度空间 ![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29) ,设可积函数序列 ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) 和可测函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 。如果同时满足以下两个条件

(1) ![f_n\to f \text{ in measure}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+in+measure%7D) (或者(1') ![f_n\to f \text{ a.e.} \ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e.%7D+%5C+) );

(2) 存在可积函数 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) ，使得对任意 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) , ![|f_n|\leq g\text{ a .e }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf_n%7C%5Cleq+g%5Ctext%7B+a+.e+%7D) ；

那么， ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可积且 ![f_n\to f \text{ in mean}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+in+mean%7D) （自然有 ![\int f_n \to \int f ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_n+%5Cto+%5Cint+f+) ）。

> (i)先假设满足(1)和(2),然后验证 ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) Cauchy in the mean,再由[3.4节S1定理1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37274703),就可推出![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可积且 ![f_n\to f \text{ in mean}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+in+mean%7D)。

（第一步：整个空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 函数值不为0的地方可以分成**可数个测度有限部分**的并 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ，然后将 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 一分为二，递增的部分是 ![E_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_k) ，递减的部分是 ![F_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F_k) ）
令 ![E=\bigcup_{k=1}^{\infty}\{x\in X: f_k(x)\not=0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+X%3A+f_k%28x%29%5Cnot%3D0%5C%7D) ,由[3.2节习题2](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37171224)知， ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是 ![\sigma\text{-finite}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%5Ctext%7B-finite%7D) 。再由[3.2节习题3](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37171224)将其表示为： ![E=\cup_{k=1}^\infty E_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3D%5Ccup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%5Cinfty+E_k) , ![E_k\subseteq E_{k+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_k%5Csubseteq+E_%7Bk%2B1%7D) 且 ![\mu(E_k)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E_k%29%3C%5Cinfty) 。再令 ![F_k=E \backslash E_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F_k%3DE+%5Cbackslash+E_k) ，那么 ![F_k\supseteq F_{k+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F_k%5Csupseteq+F_%7Bk%2B1%7D) ，且 ![\lim_{k\to\infty}F_k=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bk%5Cto%5Cinfty%7DF_k%3D%5Cvarnothing) 。

（第二步，估计 ![\int|f_n-f_m|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint%7Cf_n-f_m%7C) 的大小，估计在空间分解后的各个部分进行）
![\begin{align*} \int_X|f_n-f_m|&=\underbrace{\int_{X\backslash E} |f_n-f_m|}_{函数值为0，故积分为0}+\int_E|f_n-f_m|\\ &=\underbrace{\int_{F_k}|f_n-f_m|}_{第1项}+\underbrace{\int_{E_k}|f_n-f_m|}_{第2项} \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Cint_X%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%26%3D%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cint_%7BX%5Cbackslash+E%7D+%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%7D_%7B%E5%87%BD%E6%95%B0%E5%80%BC%E4%B8%BA0%EF%BC%8C%E6%95%85%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E4%B8%BA0%7D%2B%5Cint_E%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cint_%7BF_k%7D%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%7D_%7B%E7%AC%AC1%E9%A1%B9%7D%2B%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cint_%7BE_k%7D%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%7D_%7B%E7%AC%AC2%E9%A1%B9%7D+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)
第1项可以任意小且与 ![n,m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2Cm) 无关： 
因为 ![\lim_{k\to\infty}F_k=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bk%5Cto%5Cinfty%7DF_k%3D%5Cvarnothing) ，由[3.5节S1推论2](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37337326)，有 ![\int_{F_k}g\rightarrow0, \text{ as } k\to\infty ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7BF_k%7Dg%5Crightarrow0%2C+%5Ctext%7B+as+%7D+k%5Cto%5Cinfty+) 。故对任意 ![n,m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2Cm) ，存在 ![k_0 \in \mathbb{Z}^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_0+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B) ,使得所有 ![k\geq k_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cgeq+k_0) ，都有
![\int_{F_k}|f_n-f_m|\leq\int_{F_k}|f_n|+\int_{F_k}|f_m|=2\int_{F_k}g<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7BF_k%7D%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%5Cleq%5Cint_%7BF_k%7D%7Cf_n%7C%2B%5Cint_%7BF_k%7D%7Cf_m%7C%3D2%5Cint_%7BF_k%7Dg%3C%5Cvarepsilon)

第2项也可以任意小：(把 ![E_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_k) 再分成两部分)
令 ![G_{mn}=\{x:|f_n-f_m|\geq\varepsilon_1\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G_%7Bmn%7D%3D%5C%7Bx%3A%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon_1%5C%7D) 。因为 ![f_n\to f \text{ in measure}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+in+measure%7D) ，故![\mu(G_{nm})\rightarrow0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28G_%7Bnm%7D%29%5Crightarrow0) ,因此 ![\int_{ G_{nm}}g\rightarrow0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B+G_%7Bnm%7D%7Dg%5Crightarrow0) （[3.5节S1推论2](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37337326)）。因此存在 ![n_0 \in \mathbb{Z}^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n_0+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B) ,当
![\begin{align*} \int_{E_k}|f_n-f_m|&=\int_{E_k\backslash G_{nm}}|f_n-f_m|+\int_{ E_k\backslash G_{nm}}|f_n-f_m|\\ &\leq \varepsilon_1\mu(E_k)+2\int_{ G_{nm}}g< \varepsilon_1 \mu(E_k)+\varepsilon_2 \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Cint_%7BE_k%7D%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%26%3D%5Cint_%7BE_k%5Cbackslash+G_%7Bnm%7D%7D%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%2B%5Cint_%7B+E_k%5Cbackslash+G_%7Bnm%7D%7D%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%5C%5C+%26%5Cleq+%5Cvarepsilon_1%5Cmu%28E_k%29%2B2%5Cint_%7B+G_%7Bnm%7D%7Dg%3C+%5Cvarepsilon_1+%5Cmu%28E_k%29%2B%5Cvarepsilon_2+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)
综上：存在 ![k_0,n_0 \in \mathbb{Z}^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_0%2Cn_0+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B) ,使得当 ![n,m\geq n_0,k\geq k_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2Cm%5Cgeq+n_0%2Ck%5Cgeq+k_0) 时，有
![\int_X|f_n-f_m|<\varepsilon+ \varepsilon_1 \mu(E_k)+\varepsilon_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_X%7Cf_n-f_m%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon%2B+%5Cvarepsilon_1+%5Cmu%28E_k%29%2B%5Cvarepsilon_2) 。其中 ![\varepsilon,\varepsilon_1,\varepsilon_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%2C%5Cvarepsilon_1%2C%5Cvarepsilon_2) 都可以任意小，而 ![\mu(E_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E_k%29) 是一个固定的数，因此 ![\int_X|f_n-f_m|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_X%7Cf_n-f_m%7C) 可以任意小，故![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) Cauchy in the mean。

(ii)因为![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) Cauchy in the mean，故由[3.4节S1定理1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37274703)，就有 ![f_n\to f \text{ in mean}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+in+mean%7D) (且![\lim_{n\to \infty}\int f_n=\int \lim_{n\to\infty}f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+f_n%3D%5Cint+%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7Df) )。

(iii) 若  ![f_n\to f \text{ a.e.} \ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e.%7D+%5C+) n\to f \text{ a.e.} \  ,我们验证有 ![f_n\to f \text{ in measure}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+in+measure%7D) 。
（我们目标是证：对任意 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) 当 ![n\to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 时， ![\mu(\{x:|f_n-f|\geq\varepsilon\})\to0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5C%7Bx%3A%7Cf_n-f%7C%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon%5C%7D%29%5Cto0) ）
因为 ![\{x:|f_n-f|\geq\varepsilon\}\subseteq\cup_{j=n}^{\infty}\{x:|f_j-f|\geq\varepsilon\}=E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx%3A%7Cf_n-f%7C%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon%5C%7D%5Csubseteq%5Ccup_%7Bj%3Dn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5C%7Bx%3A%7Cf_j-f%7C%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon%5C%7D%3DE_n)
所以 ![\mu(\{x:|f_n-f|\geq\varepsilon\})\leq \mu(E_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5C%7Bx%3A%7Cf_n-f%7C%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon%5C%7D%29%5Cleq+%5Cmu%28E_n%29) 。

因此，我们只需验证，当 ![n\to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 时 ![\mu(E_n)\to0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E_n%29%5Cto0)
注意到， ![E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n) 是递减的，故极限存在,且为： ![\lim_{n\to\infty} E_n=\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n=\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}\bigcup_{j=n}^{\infty}\{x:|f_j-f|\geq\varepsilon\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D+E_n%3D%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%3D%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbigcup_%7Bj%3Dn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5C%7Bx%3A%7Cf_j-f%7C%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon%5C%7D) 。而又有 ![f_n\to f \text{ a.e.} \ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e.%7D+%5C+) 故 ![\mu(\{x:f_n(x)\not \to f(x)\})=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5C%7Bx%3Af_n%28x%29%5Cnot+%5Cto+f%28x%29%5C%7D%29%3D0) 。又因为 ![\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n=\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}\bigcup_{j=n}^{\infty}\{x:|f_j-f|\geq\varepsilon\}\subseteq \{x:f_n(x)\not \to f(x)\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%3D%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbigcup_%7Bj%3Dn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5C%7Bx%3A%7Cf_j-f%7C%5Cgeq%5Cvarepsilon%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+%5C%7Bx%3Af_n%28x%29%5Cnot+%5Cto+f%28x%29%5C%7D) 。因此 ![\mu(\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%29%3D0) 。
若有 ![\mu(E_n)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E_n%29%3C%5Cinfty) ,则由[1.2节S1测度(Continuity from above)性质](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/35850693)，![\[\mathop {\lim }\limits_{n \to \infty } \mu ({E_n}) = \mu (\mathop {\lim }\limits_{n \to \infty } {E_n})=\mu(\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n)=0\]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cmu+%28%7BE_n%7D%29+%3D+%5Cmu+%28%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%7BE_n%7D%29%3D%5Cmu%28%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%29%3D0%5C%5D),接下来验证 ![\mu(E_n)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E_n%29%3C%5Cinfty) 。
设 ![x\in E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+E_n) ,则存在某些 ![j\geq n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j%5Cgeq+n) ,使得 ![\varepsilon\leq|f_j-f|\leq|f_j|+|f|\leq2g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%5Cleq%7Cf_j-f%7C%5Cleq%7Cf_j%7C%2B%7Cf%7C%5Cleq2g) ，因此 ![g\geq\frac\varepsilon2\ \text{ a .e }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%5Cgeq%5Cfrac%5Cvarepsilon2%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a+.e+%7D) (因为 ![|f_n|\leq g\text{ a .e }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf_n%7C%5Cleq+g%5Ctext%7B+a+.e+%7D) 可证 ![|f|\leq g\text{ a .e }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf%7C%5Cleq+g%5Ctext%7B+a+.e+%7D) )
因此 ![E_n\subseteq\{x:g(x)\geq\frac\varepsilon2\}\cup N，\mu(N)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n%5Csubseteq%5C%7Bx%3Ag%28x%29%5Cgeq%5Cfrac%5Cvarepsilon2%5C%7D%5Ccup+N%EF%BC%8C%5Cmu%28N%29%3D0) 。因为 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 可积， 故![\mu(\{x:g(x)\geq\frac\varepsilon2\})<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5C%7Bx%3Ag%28x%29%5Cgeq%5Cfrac%5Cvarepsilon2%5C%7D%29%3C%5Cinfty) （[见3.3节积分性质(9)](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37233441)）。
因此 ![\mu(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty) 。

**Section 2 控制收敛定理的应用**

![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta)**定理2 **

设 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是可测函数， ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 是可积函数。若 ![|f|\leq g\ \text{ a.e.}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf%7C%5Cleq+g%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e.%7D) ，则 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 也可积。

> 因为 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 和 ![|f|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf%7C) 的可积性等价（见3.2节习题4）,我们只需证明 ![|f|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf%7C) 可积。
因为 ![|f|\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf%7C%5Cgeq0) 且可测，则由[2.2节S3定理5](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36844926)知，存在非负简单函数序列 ![\{ f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B+f_n%5C%7D) ,使得 ![f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n) 单调递增，且 ![f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n) 收敛于 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 。所以对于简单函数序列![\{ f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B+f_n%5C%7D)有 ![|f_n|\leq f \leq g \text{ a.e }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf_n%7C%5Cleq+f+%5Cleq+g+%5Ctext%7B+a.e+%7D) ，那么根据[3.3节习题1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37233441)，知 ![f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n) 是可积的。

因此，我们有可积函数序列 ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) 和可测函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ，满足： ![f_n\to f\ \text{ a.e}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D) ；存在可积函数 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) , ![|f_n|\leq g\text{ a .e }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf_n%7C%5Cleq+g%5Ctext%7B+a+.e+%7D) ；
那么根据DCT（Dominated Convergence Theorem）知 ![|f|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf%7C) 可积

引入两个定义，再证明几个有用的推论

**定义1（本质有界 essentially bounded)（本质上确界 essential supremum）**

![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是可测函数， ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 被称为是**本质有界的**（essentially bounded），如果存在常数 ![c>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%3E0) ，使得 ![|f|<c \ \text{ a.e}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf%7C%3Cc+%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D) 。

最小的常数 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) 称为 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的**本质上确界**（essential supremum），记做 ![\text{ess sup}f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bess+sup%7Df) ，即 ![\text{ess sup}f=\inf\ \{c:|f|<c\ \text{ a.e}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bess+sup%7Df%3D%5Cinf%5C+%5C%7Bc%3A%7Cf%7C%3Cc%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D%5C%7D)

**推论1**

设 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是可积函数， ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 是可测函数。如果 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 是本质有界的，那么 ![fg](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=fg) 可积。

*注： ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 没有要求是否可积*

> *因为*![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 是本质有界的，故存在常数 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) ,使得![|g|<c \ \text{ a.e}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cg%7C%3Cc+%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D) 。
故有 ![|fg|<cf \ \text{ a.e}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cfg%7C%3Ccf+%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D) 。而 ![cf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=cf) 是可积的，那么由定理1，就得 ![|fg|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cfg%7C) 可积，因此![fg](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=fg) 可积。

**推论2**

测度空间 ![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29) , ![E\in\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ,![\mu(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty)。如果可测函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 上是本质有界的，那么 ![\int_Ef](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_Ef) 存在。

注：可测函数在有限测度集上可积

> *因为*![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是本质有界的，故存在常数 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) ,使得 ![|g |<c \ \text{ a.e} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cg+%7C%3Cc+%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D+) 。
那么有 ![|\chi_Ef|<c\chi_E \ \text{ a.e}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cchi_Ef%7C%3Cc%5Cchi_E+%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D) 。又因为![\mu(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty)，故 ![c\chi_E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%5Cchi_E) 可积。
再由定理1， ![\chi_Ef](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cchi_Ef) 可积，也即 ![\int\chi_Ef=\int_Ef](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint%5Cchi_Ef%3D%5Cint_Ef) 存在。

**Section 3 单调收敛定理 & Fatou's lemma**

**定理2 （单调收敛定理 Monotone Convergence Theorem，简记MCT)**

设函数序列 ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) 如果满足：非负、单调递增、可积，且 ![f_n\to f \text{ a.e}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D) ，那么 ![\int f_n\to \int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_n%5Cto+%5Cint+f) ( ![\lim_{n\to \infty}\int f_n=\int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+f_n%3D%5Cint+f) )

> 第一种情况）如果 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可积。
因为 ![0\leq f_n\leq f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleq+f_n%5Cleq+f) ，且 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可积，那么由DCT，就得 ![\int f_n \to \int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_n+%5Cto+%5Cint+f) 。

（第二种情况）如果![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 不可积。
![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 不可积，即是 ![\int f=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f%3D%5Cinfty) ,下面我们验证 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int f_n=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+f_n%3D%5Cinfty) :
假设 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int f_n<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+f_n%3C%5Cinfty) ，那么数列 ![\left\{\int f_n\right\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%5C%7B%5Cint+f_n%5Cright%5C%7D) 是Cauchy数列。据此可推出 ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) 是Cauchy in mean： ![\int |f_m-f_m|=\underbrace{\int f_m-\int f_n \to0}_{\{\int f_n\}是Cauchy数列},\text{ as }m,n\to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+%7Cf_m-f_m%7C%3D%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cint+f_m-%5Cint+f_n+%5Cto0%7D_%7B%5C%7B%5Cint+f_n%5C%7D%E6%98%AFCauchy%E6%95%B0%E5%88%97%7D%2C%5Ctext%7B+as+%7Dm%2Cn%5Cto+%5Cinfty)
那么我们有： ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) 可积、Cauchy in mean， ![f_n \to f \text{ a.e }](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e+%7D) ,因此 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可积（[3.4节S1定理1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37274703)）。
这与 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 不可积矛盾，因此 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int f_n=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+f_n%3D%5Cinfty)

![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta)**定理3 法图引理（Fatou's Lemma）**

设函数序列 ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) 满足：(1) ![f_n\geq 0\ \text{ a.e}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cgeq+0%5C+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D) ; (2) ![f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n) 可积；

那么 ![\int\underline{\lim}f_n\leq\underline{\lim}\int f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint%5Cunderline%7B%5Clim%7Df_n%5Cleq%5Cunderline%7B%5Clim%7D%5Cint+f_n)

注：*这个定理即是说，映射 *![f \mapsto \int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f+%5Cmapsto+%5Cint+f)* 是下半连续的( lower semicontinuous)。因为映射 *![f \mapsto \int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f+%5Cmapsto+%5Cint+f)* 不是连续的，因此需要另外条件：DCT、MCT or Cauchy in mean，才能 保证当*![f_n \to f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+%5Cto+f)* 时, *![\int f_n\to \int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f_n%5Cto+%5Cint+f)

> 1)若 ![\mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } \int f_n=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cint+f_n%3D%5Cinfty) ,则不等式显然成立。
(2)设 ![\mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } \int f_n<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cint+f_n%3C%5Cinfty) .
令 ![f(x)=\mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } f_n(x)=\sup_{n\geq1}\inf_{j\geq n}\ f_j(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+f_n%28x%29%3D%5Csup_%7Bn%5Cgeq1%7D%5Cinf_%7Bj%5Cgeq+n%7D%5C+f_j%28x%29) ,再令 ![g_n(x)= \inf_{j\geq n}\ f_j(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n%28x%29%3D+%5Cinf_%7Bj%5Cgeq+n%7D%5C+f_j%28x%29) ,则 ![g_n\leq f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n%5Cleq+f_n)且 ![g_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n) 是单调递增的，因此 ![\lim_{n\to \infty}g_n=\sup_{n\geq1}g_n=\sup_{n\geq1}\inf_{j\geq n}\ f_j=f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7Dg_n%3D%5Csup_%7Bn%5Cgeq1%7Dg_n%3D%5Csup_%7Bn%5Cgeq1%7D%5Cinf_%7Bj%5Cgeq+n%7D%5C+f_j%3Df) 。

那么，对于 ![\{g_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bg_n%5C%7D) 我们有 ![g_n\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n%5Cgeq0)；单调递增； ![\lim_{n\to \infty}g_n=f \text{ a.e}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7Dg_n%3Df+%5Ctext%7B+a.e%7D) ;可积（因为 ![0\leq g_n\leq f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleq+g_n%5Cleq+f_n) , ![f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n) 可积，由定理2可知 ![g_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n) 可积）。满足MCT的条件，故 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int g_n=\int f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+g_n%3D%5Cint+f) (式1)。

另一方面，因为 ![g_n\leq f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n%5Cleq+f_n) ,所以 ![\int g_n \leq\int f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+g_n+%5Cleq%5Cint+f_n) ，两边取下极限得到 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int g_n \leq \mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } \int f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint+g_n+%5Cleq+%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cint+f_n) （式2）(思考为什么左边是用极限号，而不是下极限号？)

结合(式1)和（式2）就得到： ![\int f\leq \mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } \int f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+f%5Cleq+%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cint+f_n) ,即 ![\int\mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } f_n\leq\mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } \int f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+f_n%5Cleq%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cint+f_n) 。 ![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)

一般来说，只有 ![\int\mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } f_n<\mathop {\underline {\lim } }\limits_{n \to \infty } \int f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+f_n%3C%5Cmathop+%7B%5Cunderline+%7B%5Clim+%7D+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cint+f_n) ，即" ![=](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D) "一般不成立。
