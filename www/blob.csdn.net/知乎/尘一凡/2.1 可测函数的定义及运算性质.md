**定义1 （测度空间）**

设 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是空间， ![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的某个 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数， ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是定义在![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D)上的测度，则称三元组 ![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29) 是测度空间。在不强调![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D)和![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu)的情况下，简单记作 ![(X,\mathfrak{a})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%29) 或 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 。那么，若集合属于![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D)，则称该集合是 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 可测的（![\mu\text{-}measurable](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5Ctext%7B-%7Dmeasurable) ），有时简称可测的（measurable），要注意分辨。

> 注： ![E\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 和 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是可测的，是同一件事。

**例子1（Lebesgue测度空间）**

若取 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 为 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) ，取![\frak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrak%7Ba%7D)为Lebesgue集 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) ，取 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 为Lebesgue测度 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) ，则称三元组 ![(\mathbb{R}^n,L,m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%2CL%2Cm%29) 为Lebesgue测度空间（Lebesgue measure space）。

(i) 验证 ![\mathfrak{a}_Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y) 是一个![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数。将![\mathfrak{a}_Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y)写成集合的形式为，![\mathfrak{a}_Y=\{E: E\subseteq Y\ \& \ E\in \mathfrak{a}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y%3D%5C%7BE%3A+E%5Csubseteq+Y%5C+%5C%26+%5C+E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%5C%7D)。那么：(a) 由![\varnothing\subseteq Y \ \&\ \varnothing \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarnothing%5Csubseteq+Y+%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5Cvarnothing+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 推出 ![\varnothing \in \mathfrak{a}_Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarnothing+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y) ；(b) 由 ![Y\subseteq Y \ \&\ Y \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Y%5Csubseteq+Y+%5C+%5C%26%5C+Y+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 推出 ![Y \in \mathfrak{a}_Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Y+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y) ; (c) 若 ![A\in \mathfrak{a}_Y,B\in \mathfrak{a}_Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y%2CB%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y) ，也就是说 ![A,B\subseteq Y\ \& \ A,B\in \mathfrak{a}\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB%5Csubseteq+Y%5C+%5C%26+%5C+A%2CB%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%5C) ，则 ![A\backslash B\subseteq A\subseteq Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+B%5Csubseteq+A%5Csubseteq+Y) 且 ![A\backslash B \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+B+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，因此 ![A\backslash B \in \mathfrak{a}_Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+B+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y) ；(d) 验证若 ![A_1,A_2,...\in \mathfrak{a}_Y\Rightarrow \cup_{i=1}^{\infty}A_u \in \mathfrak{a}_Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_1%2CA_2%2C...%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y%5CRightarrow+%5Ccup_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DA_u+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_Y) (留作习题，仿照(c)做就行)

**定义3 （函数的像集和逆像(集）)**

设有函数 ![f:X\to Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AX%5Cto+Y) ，集合 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的子集，集合 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 是 ![Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Y) 的子集。

(a) 定义集合 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 在映射 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 下的像集(image)为：

![\ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ f(A)=\{f(x)\in Y:x\in A \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+f%28A%29%3D%5C%7Bf%28x%29%5Cin+Y%3Ax%5Cin+A+%5C%7D)

(b) 定义集合 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 关于 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的逆像(集)（inverse image）为：

![\ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ f^{-1}(B)=\{x\in X:f(x)\in B\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28B%29%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+X%3Af%28x%29%5Cin+B%5C%7D)

> 注：因为这里 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 不一定有逆函数，故集合 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 关于 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的逆像集不能定义为： ![\{f^{-1}(y):y\in B\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf%5E%7B-1%7D%28y%29%3Ay%5Cin+B%5C%7D) 。 ![f^{-1}(y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28y%29) 这个记号蕴含了 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 逆函数的存在。

**![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta)****![f^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D) 的良好又重要的性质：**

![f^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D) 保持集合的包含、并、交、差、补运算（![\subseteq,\cup,\cap,\backslash,\ ^c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csubseteq%2C%5Ccup%2C%5Ccap%2C%5Cbackslash%2C%5C+%5Ec) ）

(a) 若 ![A\subseteq B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+B) ，则 ![f^{-1}(A)\subseteq f^{-1}(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%29%5Csubseteq+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28B%29) ;

(b) ![f^{-1}(A\cup B)=f^{-1}(A)\cup f^{-1}(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%5Ccup+B%29%3Df%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%29%5Ccup+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28B%29) ;

(c) ![f^{-1}(A\cap B)=f^{-1}(A)\cap f^{-1}(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%5Ccap+B%29%3Df%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%29%5Ccap+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28B%29) ;

(d) ![f^{-1}(A \backslash B)=f^{-1}(A)\backslash f^{-1}(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28A+%5Cbackslash+B%29%3Df%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%29%5Cbackslash+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28B%29) ;

> 以下证明过程都是紧扣逆像集的定义。
(a)  我们只需证明：若 ![x \in f^{-1}(A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x+%5Cin+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%29) 则 ![x \in f^{-1}({B})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x+%5Cin+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%7BB%7D%29) 。设![x \in f^{-1}(A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x+%5Cin+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%29)，则 ![f(x)\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%5Cin+A) ( ![f^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D) 定义)，而 ![A\subseteq B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+B)，故 ![f(x)\in B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%5Cin+B) ，也即是 ![x \in f^{-1}(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x+%5Cin+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28B%29) (还是 ![f^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D) 定义)。 ![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)

**Section 2 可测函数（measurable function）**


**定义4 （可测函数）**

设有测度空间![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29)，函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 定义在***可测子集***![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 上， ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可以在实数上取值，也可以在广义实数上取值，以下分这两种情况对 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可测性进行定义：

(a) 函数 ![f:E\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AE%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 称作可测的（measurable），如果对 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的任意开集 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 的逆像是可测的。写成数学语言便是 :![f:E\to\mathbb{R} \text{ is measurable}\Leftrightarrow \forall \text{open set } M\subseteq\mathbb{R},f^{-1}(M)\in\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AE%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D+%5Ctext%7B+is+measurable%7D%5CLeftrightarrow+%5Cforall+%5Ctext%7Bopen+set+%7D+M%5Csubseteq%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2Cf%5E%7B-1%7D%28M%29%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)

(b) 函数 ![f:E\to\mathbb{R}\cup\{\pm\infty\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AE%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Ccup%5C%7B%5Cpm%5Cinfty%5C%7D) 称作可测的（measurable），如果对 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的任意开集 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 的逆像是可测的，并且 ![\{+\infty\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%2B%5Cinfty%5C%7D) 和 ![\{-\infty\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B-%5Cinfty%5C%7D) 逆像都是可测的。写成数学语言便是 :

![\begin{align*} f:E\to\mathbb{R}\cup{\pm\infty} \text{ is measurable}\Leftrightarrow &\forall \text{open set } M\subseteq\mathbb{R},f^{-1}(M)\in\mathfrak{a}\\ & \text{and } f^{-1}(\{+\infty\}),f^{-1}(\{-\infty\})\in\mathfrak{a} \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+f%3AE%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Ccup%7B%5Cpm%5Cinfty%7D+%5Ctext%7B+is+measurable%7D%5CLeftrightarrow+%26%5Cforall+%5Ctext%7Bopen+set+%7D+M%5Csubseteq%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2Cf%5E%7B-1%7D%28M%29%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%5C%5C+%26+%5Ctext%7Band+%7D+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5C%7B%2B%5Cinfty%5C%7D%29%2Cf%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5C%7B-%5Cinfty%5C%7D%29%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

> 注：这个定义是在测度空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的某个可测子集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 上定义 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可测。若要定义在整个空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上可测，只需在上面的定义将 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 取作 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 即可。若没有特别强调 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在哪个集合上可测，则认为是说 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在整个空间上可测。

接下来叙述可测函数的等价定义，在证明可测函数的性质时常用等价定义。

**定理1 （可测函数的等价定义）**

设有测度空间![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29)， ![E\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，函数 ![f:E\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AE%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)。则下面命题等价：

(1) ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可测；

(2) ![f^{-1}((-\infty,c))\in \mathfrak{a},\forall c\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%29%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cforall+c%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) （对任意实数 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) ， ![f^{-1}((-\infty,c))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%29%29) 可测）

(3) ![f^{-1}((-\infty,c])\in \mathfrak{a},\forall c\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%5D%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cforall+c%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ;

(4) ![f^{-1}((c,\infty))\in \mathfrak{a},\forall c\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28c%2C%5Cinfty%29%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cforall+c%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)

(5) ![f^{-1}([c,\infty))\in \mathfrak{a},\forall c\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cforall+c%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)

(6) ![f^{-1}(B)\in \mathfrak{a},\forall \text{ Borel set } B \subseteq \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28B%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cforall+%5Ctext%7B+Borel+set+%7D+B+%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)

注：上面的函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是实值函数。 若 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是取广义实数值（扩充实数值），则可测除上面的要求外，还要满足 ![f^{-1}(\{+\infty\}),f^{-1}(\{-\infty\})\in\mathfrak{a} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5C%7B%2B%5Cinfty%5C%7D%29%2Cf%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5C%7B-%5Cinfty%5C%7D%29%5Cin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+) （根据定义4）。

> (1) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (2):![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可测， ![(-\infty,c)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%29) 是 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的开集，故![f^{-1}((-\infty,c))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%29%29) 可测。 ![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)
(2) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (3)：若 ![f^{-1}((-\infty,c))\in \mathfrak{a},\forall c\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%29%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cforall+c%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，则 ![f^{-1}((-\infty,c])=\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}f^{-1}((-\infty,c+\frac1n)) \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%5D%29%3D%5Cbigcap_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Df%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%2B%5Cfrac1n%29%29+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) （把 ![(-\infty,c]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%5D) 表示为开集的交，然后利用 ![f^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D) 保持交运算）。![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)
(3) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (4)： ![f^{-1}([c,\infty))=E\backslash (f^{-1}([c,\infty)))^c =E\backslash f^{-1}([c,\infty)^c)=E\backslash f^{-1}((-\infty,c]) \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%29%3DE%5Cbackslash+%28f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%29%29%5Ec+%3DE%5Cbackslash+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%5Ec%29%3DE%5Cbackslash+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%5D%29+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)
(4) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (5)： ![f^{-1}([c,\infty))=\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}f^{-1}((c-\frac1n,\infty))\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%29%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Df%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28c-%5Cfrac1n%2C%5Cinfty%29%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)
(5) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (6)：a. 定义集合 ![e=\{A\subseteq \mathbb{R}:f^{-1}(A)\in \mathfrak{a}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%3D%5C%7BA%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%3Af%5E%7B-1%7D%28A%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%5C%7D) （即 ![e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e) 是以逆像可测的集合为元素构成的集族），可以由定义验证 ![e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e) 是 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数（事实上，可以从 ![f^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D) 保持"交并补差"看出）。
b. 因为对任意的实数 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) 有![f^{-1}([c,\infty))\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，故 ![[c,\infty)\in e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%5Cin+e) ，也即 ![\{[c,\infty):c \in \mathbb{R}\}\subseteq e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%3Ac+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+e) 。
c. 因为 ![f^{-1}((-\infty,c))=E\backslash f^{-1}([c,\infty))\in \mathfrak{a} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%29%29%3DE%5Cbackslash+f%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Bc%2C%5Cinfty%29%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+) ，故 ![\{(-\infty,c):c \in \mathbb{R}\}\subseteq e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%29%3Ac+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+e) 。
d. 因为 ![e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e) 是 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数，再由b和c知 ![\{[a,\infty)\cap(-\infty,b)\}=\{[a,b):a<b \in R\}\subseteq e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Ba%2C%5Cinfty%29%5Ccap%28-%5Cinfty%2Cb%29%5C%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Ba%2Cb%29%3Aa%3Cb+%5Cin+R%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+e) 。
e. 因为 ![(a,b)=\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}[a+\frac1n,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Ba%2B%5Cfrac1n%2Cb%29) ，故 ![\{(a,b):a<b \in R\}\subseteq e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28a%2Cb%29%3Aa%3Cb+%5Cin+R%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+e)
f. 由上面知集族 ![e\supseteq \{\text{open sets}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Csupseteq+%5C%7B%5Ctext%7Bopen+sets%7D%5C%7D)
g. 因此 ![e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e) 是包含所有开集的 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数。而 ![Borel ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Borel+)![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) -代数是包含所有开集的最小![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数。故 ![e\supseteq Borel \ \sigma \text{-}\ algebra](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Csupseteq+Borel+%5C+%5Csigma+%5Ctext%7B-%7D%5C+algebra) 。所以任意Borel集 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 都属于 ![e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e) ，即是 ![:f^{-1}(B)\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3Af%5E%7B-1%7D%28B%29%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)
(6) ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) (1)： ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上任何开集都是Borel set，故![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上开集的逆像可测，因此 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可测。 ![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)

**命题1 （集合可测当且仅当其特征函数可测）**

设有测度空间 ![(X,\mathfrak{a})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%29) ， ![E\subseteq X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubseteq+X) ，则 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 可测 ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow)![\chi_E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cchi_E) （在 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上）可测。

>  我们利用等价定义(2)来证明。
" ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) "：因为![\[{\rm{\chi_E^{-1}((-\infty,c)) = }}\left\{ {\begin{array}{*{20}{l}} {\varnothing,\text{ if }} c\leq0\\ {E^c,\text{ if } 0<c\leq1}\\ {X,\text{ if } c> 1} \end{array}} \right.\]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Crm%7B%5Cchi_E%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2Cc%29%29+%3D+%7D%7D%5Cleft%5C%7B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bl%7D%7D+%7B%5Cvarnothing%2C%5Ctext%7B+if+%7D%7D+c%5Cleq0%5C%5C+%7BE%5Ec%2C%5Ctext%7B+if+%7D+0%3Cc%5Cleq1%7D%5C%5C+%7BX%2C%5Ctext%7B+if+%7D+c%3E+1%7D+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright.%5C%5D) ，故若 ![E\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，则 ![E^c \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Ec+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 。而总有 ![\varnothing,X\in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarnothing%2CX%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，故![\chi_E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cchi_E) （在 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上）可测。

" ![\Leftarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftarrow) "：若![\chi_E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cchi_E)可测，则 ![E^c=\chi_E^{-1}((-\infty,\frac12) \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Ec%3D%5Cchi_E%5E%7B-1%7D%28%28-%5Cinfty%2C%5Cfrac12%29+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，进而推出 ![E \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 。

接下来证明，度量空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 中， ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是由measure outer measure诱导出来的测度([见1.4节最末的总结](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36438199))，则连续函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在此测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 下是可测的。

**定义 5 （度量空间的连续函数）**

**设**![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是度量空间 ![(X,\rho)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Crho%29) 上的函数 ![f:X \to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AX+%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，称 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是连续的，如果对任意 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的开集，其逆像是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的开集。写成数学语言即是：

![f:X\to \mathbb{R} \text{ is continuous}\Leftrightarrow \forall \text{open set }O\subseteq \mathbb{R},f^{-1}(O) \text{ is open}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AX%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D+%5Ctext%7B+is+continuous%7D%5CLeftrightarrow+%5Cforall+%5Ctext%7Bopen+set+%7DO%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2Cf%5E%7B-1%7D%28O%29+%5Ctext%7B+is+open%7D)

> 在 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上，此定义和高等数学中连续函数的定义是等价的。

**命题2 （Bore set上的连续函数是可测的）**

![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是度量空间， ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上metric outer measure， ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是由 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 诱导出的测度， ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 里的Borel set。

如果定义在 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 上的实值函数![f:E\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AE%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是连续的，则 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 上是可测的。

> 设有开集 ![O\subseteq \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，因为 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 上连续，则 ![f^{-1}(O)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28O%29) 是 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 里的开集。而 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的子集，由拓扑子空间开集的定义，得 ![f^{-1}(O)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28O%29) 可表示为 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 和 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 里的某个开集之交，即是 ![f^{-1}(O)=E\cap M,\color{red}{M \text{ open in X}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28O%29%3DE%5Ccap+M%2C%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7BM+%5Ctext%7B+open+in+X%7D%7D) 。
而由[1.4节Section2 定理2](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36438199)知道，Borel sets 关于metric outer measure都是可测的。
而由假设，![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 里的Borel set，故可测；而 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 是开集，自然也是Borel sets，故也可测；因此 ![f^{-1}(O)=E\cap M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28O%29%3DE%5Ccap+M) 也是可测（可测集构成 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数，因此可测集之交仍是可测集）。
即设有开集 ![O\subseteq \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)，我们证明了 ![f^{-1}(O)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D%28O%29) 可测，由可测函数的定义，知 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 上是可测的。 ![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)

**例子**

设 ![(\mathbb{R},L,m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2CL%2Cm%29) 为Lebesgue测度空间。[1.5节定理](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36594702)1告诉我们，Lebesgue外测度 ![m^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5E%2A) 是metric outer measure，由 ![m^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5E%2A) 诱导出Lebesgue测度 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 。因此根据本命题可知，定义在 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上某个Borel set的实值连续函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是可测的。
