**1.2 外代数**

切空间里的切向量如上一节所言，本质是一个箭头，可进行的运算不多，不够灵活。而从[对偶](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E5%25AF%25B9%25E5%2581%25B6%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E9%2597%25B4)的角度，考虑切空间里的线性函数，则可以灵活的多，因为函数可以做加法、乘法、标量乘法、复合等运算。一旦引入了切空间上的线性函数这个概念，再进一步，就可以考虑多重(chong,二声)线性函数，多重线性函数拥有多个参数，而且对每个参数都是线性的。矩阵的行列式就是一种多重线性函数，因为它可以看成是矩阵的列向量的函数。类似行列式的一类多重线性函数，具有交错性(alternating)或叫反称性(antisymmetric)：交换任意两个参数的位置，函数值的符号发生改变。切空间上定义的具有 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个参数的交错线性函数，就叫做 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 阶余向量（k-covector）。

**Section 1 对偶空间（Dual Space）**

设 ![V,W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%2CW) 是实向量空间，用 ![\text{Hom}(V,W)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D%28V%2CW%29) 表示所有线性映射 ![f:V \to W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AV+%5Cto+W) 构成的向量空间。

> 用 ![\text{Hom}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D) 是因为它是homomorphism的缩写,上一节已经说明向量空间之间的线性映射就是线性同态（homomorphism）.

**定义1.1 （对偶空间）**

设有向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)，![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的所有**实线性函数**构成了一个向量空间，这个向量空间就叫做 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的对偶空间，记做 ![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee) 。即是 ![V^\vee=\text{Hom}(V,\mathbb{R})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee%3D%5Ctext%7BHom%7D%28V%2C%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%29) 。

![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee) 里的元素叫作余向量(covector),或者1阶余向量(1-covector)，或者叫做对偶向量（dual vector）。

先引入一个记号，叫做Kronecker delta，即 ![\delta_j^i=\begin{cases} 1\quad \text{if }i=j,\\ 0 \quad \text{if }i\ne j \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta_j%5Ei%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+1%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bif+%7Di%3Dj%2C%5C%5C+0+%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bif+%7Di%5Cne+j+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D) 。

接下来假设 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 为 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n)**维**向量空间，设 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的基为 ![e_1,...e_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_1%2C...e_n) 。定义 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个函数 ![\alpha^i:V\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ei%3AV%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 为：对任意 ![v\in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%5Cin+V), ![\alpha^i(v)=v^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ei%28v%29%3Dv%5Ei) (**这个函数的意义是给定向量 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) ,选出其第 ![i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i) 个坐标分量![v^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%5Ei)** ),容易证明 ![\alpha^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ei) 是线性函数（用定义验证一下线性）。根据函数![\alpha^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ei) 的定义（意义），可得到 ![\alpha^i(e_j)=\delta_j^i=\begin{cases} 1\quad \text{for }i=j,\\ 0 \quad \text{for }i\ne j \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ei%28e_j%29%3D%5Cdelta_j%5Ei%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+1%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bfor+%7Di%3Dj%2C%5C%5C+0+%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bfor+%7Di%5Cne+j+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D) 。

> ![\alpha^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ei) 作用于 ![e_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_j) ,意义是选出 ![e_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_j) 的第 ![i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i) 个坐标分量。如果j刚好等于i，即是选出 ![e_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_i) 的第 ![i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i) 个坐标分量，也就是1；如果j不等于i，选出 ![e_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_j) 的第 ![i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i) 个坐标分量，也就是0.

**命题1.1**

线性函数 ![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En) 构成对偶空间![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee)的基。

> （i）先证明 ![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En) 张成（span）对偶空间![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee)（即是证明，对 ![\forall f\in V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+f%5Cin+V%5E%5Cvee) ， ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可用 ![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En) 线性表出）。
任取 ![f\in V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+V%5E%5Cvee) 和 ![v=\sum v^ie_i\in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%5Csum+v%5Eie_i%5Cin+V) 。将线性函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 作用到 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) ,得到 ![f(v)=\sum v^i f(e_i)=\sum f(e_i)\alpha^i(v)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28v%29%3D%5Csum+v%5Ei+f%28e_i%29%3D%5Csum+f%28e_i%29%5Calpha%5Ei%28v%29) （第二个等号利用定义 ![\alpha^i(v)=v^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ei%28v%29%3Dv%5Ei) ）。
即是 ![f=\sum f(e_i)\alpha^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3D%5Csum+f%28e_i%29%5Calpha%5Ei) ，因此，![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En) 张成对偶空间![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee)。
(ii)再证明 ![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En) 线性无关。
设存在某些 ![c_i\in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_i%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，使得![\sum c_i\alpha^i=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+c_i%5Calpha%5Ei%3D0) 。将它作用于基向量 ![e_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_j) ,得到 ![0=\sum_i c_i\alpha^i(e_j)=\sum_i c_i\delta^i_j=c_j,\forall j=1,2,...,n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3D%5Csum_i+c_i%5Calpha%5Ei%28e_j%29%3D%5Csum_i+c_i%5Cdelta%5Ei_j%3Dc_j%2C%5Cforall+j%3D1%2C2%2C...%2Cn)
因此![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En) 线性无关。 ![\square](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare)

对偶空间 ![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee) 满足 ![\alpha^i(e_j)=\delta_j^i=\begin{cases} 1\quad \text{for }i=j,\\ 0 \quad \text{for }i\ne j \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ei%28e_j%29%3D%5Cdelta_j%5Ei%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+1%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bfor+%7Di%3Dj%2C%5C%5C+0+%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bfor+%7Di%5Cne+j+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D) 的基 ![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En) 就说关于向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的基 ![e_1,...,e_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_1%2C...%2Ce_n) 成对偶（基 ![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En)和基 ![e_1,...,e_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_1%2C...%2Ce_n) 成对偶）。因此，对偶空间![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee)和向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 有同样的维数 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 。

**Section 2 置换（Permutations）**

这一节的置换是一个工具，服务于后面的小节。这里定义繁多，看不懂的时候可以结合例子。

**定义2.1 （置换）**

设 ![k\in \mathbb{Z}^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B) ， ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个正整数构成集合 ![E=\{1,2,...k\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3D%5C%7B1%2C2%2C...k%5C%7D) 。集合 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的**置换**（permutation）是 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 到自身的**双射**![\sigma:A\to A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3AA%5Cto+A) ，即 ![\forall i\in E,\sigma(i)\in E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+i%5Cin+E%2C%5Csigma%28i%29%5Cin+E) 。置换 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 可以用矩阵表示为： ![\sigma=\bigg(\begin{matrix} 1 & 2 & \cdots & k \\ \sigma(1) & \sigma(2) & \cdots & \sigma(k) \\ \end{matrix} \bigg)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3D%5Cbigg%28%5Cbegin%7Bmatrix%7D+1+%26+2+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+k+%5C%5C+%5Csigma%281%29+%26+%5Csigma%282%29+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+%5Csigma%28k%29+%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bmatrix%7D+%5Cbigg%29) 。

> 通俗讲，置换就是改变元素所在的位置。

**定义2.2 （轮换、对换）**

设![r\in \mathbb{Z}^+,\{a_1,a_2,...,a_r \}\subseteq \{1,2,...k\}=E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B%2C%5C%7Ba_1%2Ca_2%2C...%2Ca_r+%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+%5C%7B1%2C2%2C...k%5C%7D%3DE), ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的**轮换**(cyclic permutation)是一种置换 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 满足 ![\sigma(a_1)=a_2,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28a_1%29%3Da_2%2C)![\sigma(a_2)=a_3,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28a_2%29%3Da_3%2C)![...,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=...%2C)![\sigma(a_{r-1})=a_r,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28a_%7Br-1%7D%29%3Da_r%2C)![\sigma(a_r)=a_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28a_r%29%3Da_1)**且保持 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 的其他元素不变**。符号表示为 ![\sigma=(a_1\ a_2...a_r)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3D%28a_1%5C+a_2...a_r%29)。

因为 ![\sigma=(a_1\ a_2...a_r)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3D%28a_1%5C+a_2...a_r%29) 作用于 ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) 个元素，就叫做**![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) -轮换**。 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) -轮换是恒等映射 ![e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e) 。

![2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2) -轮换称为**对换**（transposition），即形如的 ![(a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29) 的对换表示将 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 映为 ![b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b) , ![b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b) 映为 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 。

两个轮换 ![(a_1\ a_2...a_r)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_1%5C+a_2...a_r%29) 和 ![(b_1\ b_2...b_s)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28b_1%5C+b_2...b_s%29) 称为**不相交的**（disjoint），如果集合 ![\{a_1\ a_2...a_r\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_1%5C+a_2...a_r%5C%7D) 和 ![\{b_1\ b_2...b_s\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bb_1%5C+b_2...b_s%5C%7D) 没有共同的元素。

> 轮换也可用矩阵表示为 ![\sigma=\bigg(\begin{matrix} a_1 & a_2 & \cdots &a_{r-1}& a_r \\ a_2 & a_3 & \cdots & a_r& a_1 \\ \end{matrix} \bigg)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3D%5Cbigg%28%5Cbegin%7Bmatrix%7D+a_1+%26+a_2+%26+%5Ccdots+%26a_%7Br-1%7D%26+a_r+%5C%5C+a_2+%26+a_3+%26+%5Ccdots+%26+a_r%26+a_1+%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bmatrix%7D+%5Cbigg%29) ，但对于轮换来说这种表示太麻烦，**更常用表示为 ![\sigma=(a_1\ a_2...a_r)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3D%28a_1%5C+a_2...a_r%29)**

**定义2.3 （置换的乘积）**

置换 ![\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau) 和 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 的乘积定义为它们的复合，即 ![\tau\sigma =\tau\circ\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%5Csigma+%3D%5Ctau%5Ccirc%5Csigma) ,也即对集合 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) , ![\tau\sigma(E) =\tau\circ\sigma(E)=\tau(\sigma(E))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%5Csigma%28E%29+%3D%5Ctau%5Ccirc%5Csigma%28E%29%3D%5Ctau%28%5Csigma%28E%29%29) ；注意顺序，先作用 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 再作用 ![\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau) 。

用 ![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 表示集合 ![\{1,2,...,k\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B1%2C2%2C...%2Ck%5C%7D) 上所有置换的集合，![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 又叫做[对称群](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E5%25AF%25B9%25E7%25A7%25B0%25E7%25BE%25A4_%28n%25E6%25AC%25A1%25E5%25AF%25B9%25E7%25A7%25B0%25E7%25BE%25A4%29)。

**命题2.1**

**（1）**任意**对换**![(a\ b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%5C+b%29) 的逆是其本身，即 ![(a\ b)(a \ b)=e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%5C+b%29%28a+%5C+b%29%3De) .

**（2）**两个**不相交**的轮换 ![\sigma_1,\sigma_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma_1%2C%5Csigma_2) 的乘积是可交换的，即 ![\sigma_1\sigma_2=\sigma_2\sigma_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma_1%5Csigma_2%3D%5Csigma_2%5Csigma_1)

**（3）**任一置换 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 都可以表示为一些**不相交**的**轮换**的**乘积** 。

**（4）**任一 ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) -轮换都可以写成 ![r-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r-1) 个对换的乘积，具体可以表达为： ![(a_1\ a_2...a_r)=(a_1\ a_r)(a_1\ a_{r-1})...(a_1\ a_3)(a_1\ a_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_1%5C+a_2...a_r%29%3D%28a_1%5C+a_r%29%28a_1%5C+a_%7Br-1%7D%29...%28a_1%5C+a_3%29%28a_1%5C+a_2%29)。 其中 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) -轮换看成是 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) 个对换的乘积。

> 不打算证明，知道怎么用就行。方法见下面的例子。
思考题：证明轮换 ![(1\ 2\ 3)=(2\ 3\ 1)=(3\ 1\ 2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%5C+2%5C+3%29%3D%282%5C+3%5C+1%29%3D%283%5C+1%5C+2%29)

**例子2.1**

**(4)**将置换 ![\sigma=\bigg( \begin{array} \ 1\ 2\ 3\ 4\ 5\\ 2\ 4\ 5 \ 1\ 3 \end{array} \bigg) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3D%5Cbigg%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D+%5C+1%5C+2%5C+3%5C+4%5C+5%5C%5C+2%5C+4%5C+5+%5C+1%5C+3+%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cbigg%29+) 表示成一些**不相交**轮换的乘积。

> 从元素1开始。可以看到1映成2，而2映成4，而4映成1，完成了循环，于是第一个轮换就是 ![(1 \ 2 \ 4)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281+%5C+2+%5C+4%29) 。
然后从剩下的元素重复上面操作，比如元素 ![3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3) 。3映成5，而5映成3，完成了循环，于是第二个轮换就是 ![(3\ 5)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%283%5C+5%29) 。
我们发现元素已经历遍，于是结束。最终得到 ![\sigma=\bigg( \begin{array} \ 1\ 2\ 3\ 4\ 5\\ 2\ 4\ 5 \ 1\ 3 \end{array} \bigg) =(1\ 2\ 4)(3\ 5)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3D%5Cbigg%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D+%5C+1%5C+2%5C+3%5C+4%5C+5%5C%5C+2%5C+4%5C+5+%5C+1%5C+3+%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cbigg%29+%3D%281%5C+2%5C+4%29%283%5C+5%29)

**(5) **将轮换 ![(1 \ 2\ 3\ 4)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281+%5C+2%5C+3%5C+4%29) 表示成一些对换的乘积。

> 轮换 ![(1 \ 2\ 3\ 4)=\bigg( \begin{array} \ 1\ 2\ 3\ 4\\ 2\ 3\ 4\ 1\end{array} \bigg) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281+%5C+2%5C+3%5C+4%29%3D%5Cbigg%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D+%5C+1%5C+2%5C+3%5C+4%5C%5C+2%5C+3%5C+4%5C+1%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cbigg%29+) ，也即将 ![\{1,2,3,4\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B1%2C2%2C3%2C4%5C%7D) 映为 ![\{2,3,4,1\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B2%2C3%2C4%2C1%5C%7D) 。我们可以采取这样的映射路径： ![\{1,2,3,4\}\mathop \to \limits^{(1,2)} \{2,1,3,4\}\mathop \to \limits^{(1,3)} \{2,3,1,4\}\mathop \to \limits^{(1,4)} \{2,3,4,1\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B1%2C2%2C3%2C4%5C%7D%5Cmathop+%5Cto+%5Climits%5E%7B%281%2C2%29%7D+%5C%7B2%2C1%2C3%2C4%5C%7D%5Cmathop+%5Cto+%5Climits%5E%7B%281%2C3%29%7D+%5C%7B2%2C3%2C1%2C4%5C%7D%5Cmathop+%5Cto+%5Climits%5E%7B%281%2C4%29%7D+%5C%7B2%2C3%2C4%2C1%5C%7D) 。即 ![(1\ 2\ 3\ 4)=(1 \ 4)(1 \ 3 )(1\ 2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%5C+2%5C+3%5C+4%29%3D%281+%5C+4%29%281+%5C+3+%29%281%5C+2%29) （注意**乘积**的阅读顺序是从左到右）

由上面讨论可知，任意置换 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 可以表示成一些轮换的乘积，而每一个轮换又可以表示成一些对换的乘积。因此，![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma)可以表示成一些对换的乘积，再根据**对换**个数的奇偶性来定义置换的奇偶性。

**定义2.4 （奇置换、偶置换、置换的符号）**

置换 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 称为**奇置换**，如果它能表示成奇数个对换的乘积；

置换 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 称为**偶置换**，如果它能表示成偶数个对换的乘积。

置换 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 的符号(sign)，记做 ![\text{sgn} (\sigma)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D+%28%5Csigma%29)(或![\text{sgn} \ \sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D+%5C+%5Csigma) ），定义为 ![\text{sgn} (\sigma)=\begin{cases} +1\quad \sigma是偶置换\\ -1\quad \sigma是奇置换 \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D+%28%5Csigma%29%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%2B1%5Cquad+%5Csigma%E6%98%AF%E5%81%B6%E7%BD%AE%E6%8D%A2%5C%5C+-1%5Cquad+%5Csigma%E6%98%AF%E5%A5%87%E7%BD%AE%E6%8D%A2+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D)。

容易证明 ![\text{sgn} (\sigma \tau)=\text{sgn}(\sigma)\text{sgn}(\tau),\forall \sigma,\tau\in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D+%28%5Csigma+%5Ctau%29%3D%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D%28%5Csigma%29%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D%28%5Ctau%29%2C%5Cforall+%5Csigma%2C%5Ctau%5Cin+S_k)。

**命题2.2**

(1)奇置换**乘**奇置换=偶置换；偶置换乘偶置换=偶置换；奇置换乘偶置换=奇置换

(2) ![r\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 轮换是偶置换，当且仅当， ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) 是奇数；![r\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 轮换是奇置换，当且仅当， ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) 是偶数。

> （1）根据置换的奇偶性的定义可证。
（2）根据 ![(a_1\ a_2...a_r)=(a_1\ a_r)(a_1\ a_{r-1})...(a_1\ a_3)(a_1\ a_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_1%5C+a_2...a_r%29%3D%28a_1%5C+a_r%29%28a_1%5C+a_%7Br-1%7D%29...%28a_1%5C+a_3%29%28a_1%5C+a_2%29) 可证。

**定义2.5 （逆序对）**

置换 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 中的一个**逆序对**(inversion)是指有序对 ![(\sigma(i),\sigma(j))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Csigma%28i%29%2C%5Csigma%28j%29%29) 满足 ![i<j且\sigma(i)>\sigma(j)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i%3Cj%E4%B8%94%5Csigma%28i%29%3E%5Csigma%28j%29) 。

> 找置换![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma)中所有的逆序对的方法。
方法1：观察 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 矩阵的第二行，从第一个元素开始，检查后面是否存在元素小于第一个元素，如果有，后面的元素就和第一个元素构成逆序对；接着继续检查第二个元素。。。
**（推荐）方法2：**先从第二行最小的数开始比如1，前面所有比1大的数与1构成逆序对；接着检查2，前面比2大的数与2构成逆序对，依次类推

因此，逆序对有![(2,1),(4,1),(5,1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%282%2C1%29%2C%284%2C1%29%2C%285%2C1%29) ,![(4,3),(5,3)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%284%2C3%29%2C%285%2C3%29) 。

我们发现对换 ![(5 \ 1),(4\ 1),(2\ 1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%285+%5C+1%29%2C%284%5C+1%29%2C%282%5C+1%29) 恰好对应于 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 含 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 的逆序对。
(ii)接着我们将元素 ![2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2) 复位，我们发现经过(i)步后, ![2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2) 已经在原位了。

**Section 3 多重线性函数（Multilinear Functions）**

令 ![V^k=\underbrace{V\times V\cdots\times V}_{k个}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5Ek%3D%5Cunderbrace%7BV%5Ctimes+V%5Ccdots%5Ctimes+V%7D_%7Bk%E4%B8%AA%7D) 表示 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的笛卡尔积

**定义3.1 (多重线性函数）**

函数 ![f:V^k\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AV%5Ek%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 称为 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k)**重线性函数**( ![k\text{-}linear](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7Dlinear) )，如果它关于 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个参数都是线性的，即 ![ f(..,rv+sw,..)=rf(..,v,..)+sf(..,w,..), \forall s,t\in \mathbb{R};v,w\in V ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+f%28..%2Crv%2Bsw%2C..%29%3Drf%28..%2Cv%2C..%29%2Bsf%28..%2Cw%2C..%29%2C+%5Cforall+s%2Ct%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%3Bv%2Cw%5Cin+V+) .

![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k)重线性函数也称为「 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 阶**张量（tensor）**」。可证 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)上的**所有**![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k)重线性函数构成一个向量空间，符号记做 ![L_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L_k%28V%29) 。

**例子3.1（ **![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)** 上的点积）**

设 ![e_1,e_2,...,e_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_1%2Ce_2%2C...%2Ce_n) 是 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 的标准基（即单位正交基），点积定义为： ![f(v,w)=v\cdot w=\sum_i^nv^iw^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28v%2Cw%29%3Dv%5Ccdot+w%3D%5Csum_i%5Env%5Eiw%5Ei) ,其中 ![v=\sum_iv^ie_i,w=\sum_iw^ie_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%5Csum_iv%5Eie_i%2Cw%3D%5Csum_iw%5Eie_i) 。

可以验证 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 关于 ![v,w](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%2Cw) 都是线性的，因此 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的双重线性函数。

**例子3.2 （行列式）**

设 ![v_1,v_2,...,v_n\in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v_1%2Cv_2%2C...%2Cv_n%5Cin+V) ,行列式 ![f(v_1,...,v_n)=\det[v_1\cdots v_n]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_n%29%3D%5Cdet%5Bv_1%5Ccdots+v_n%5D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 重线性函数。

**定义3.2（对称线性函数**和**交错线性函数）**

![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数 ![f:V^k\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AV%5Ek%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 称为是**对称的**(symmetric),如果对任意置换 ![\sigma \in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+%5Cin+S_k) 有 ![f(v_{\sigma(1)},...,v_{\sigma(k)})=f(v_1,...,v_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29%3Df%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_k%29) .

![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数 ![f:V^k\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AV%5Ek%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 称为是**交错的**(alternating),如果对任意置换 ![\sigma \in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+%5Cin+S_k) 有 ![f(v_{\sigma(1)},...,v_{\sigma(k)})=(\text{sgn} \ \sigma)f(v_1,...,v_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29%3D%28%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D+%5C+%5Csigma%29f%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_k%29) .

> 注1：对称线性函数通俗讲就是，任意交换参数的位置，函数值符号不变；对于交错线性函数，交换参数的位置，函数值符号改变为置换的符号。
注2：由定义看出，对于对称线性函数，交换任意两个参数的位置，函数值不变号；而对于交错线性函数，交换任意两个参数的位置，函数值变号（原因： ![f(..,v_j,..,v_i,..)=\text{sgn}(i\ j)\cdot f(..,v_i,..,v_j,..)=-f(..,v_i,..,v_j,..)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28..%2Cv_j%2C..%2Cv_i%2C..%29%3D%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D%28i%5C+j%29%5Ccdot+f%28..%2Cv_i%2C..%2Cv_j%2C..%29%3D-f%28..%2Cv_i%2C..%2Cv_j%2C..%29)

将所有 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重交错线性函数构成集合记做 ![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29) ,可以证明 ![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29) 是一个向量空间，而且是 ![L_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L_k%28V%29) 的子空间。对于 ![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29) 中的元素，称为![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重交错线性函数、或 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 阶交错张量、或 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 阶余向量。特别的，当 ![k=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D0) 时，定义 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) 阶余向量就是常数，因此 ![A_0(V)=\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_0%28V%29%3D%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 。一阶余向量就是Section 1定义的余向量。 ![\color{green}{以后提到}\color{blue}{余向量} \color{green}{就要想到它本质上是向量空间上的} \color{blue}{交错线性函数}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B%E4%BB%A5%E5%90%8E%E6%8F%90%E5%88%B0%7D%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%E4%BD%99%E5%90%91%E9%87%8F%7D+%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B%E5%B0%B1%E8%A6%81%E6%83%B3%E5%88%B0%E5%AE%83%E6%9C%AC%E8%B4%A8%E4%B8%8A%E6%98%AF%E5%90%91%E9%87%8F%E7%A9%BA%E9%97%B4%E4%B8%8A%E7%9A%84%7D+%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%E4%BA%A4%E9%94%99%E7%BA%BF%E6%80%A7%E5%87%BD%E6%95%B0%7D)

**例子3.3**

(1) ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 上的点积 ![f(v,w)=v\cdot w](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28v%2Cw%29%3Dv%5Ccdot+w) 是对称的。

(2) ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 上的行列式 ![f(v_1,...,v_n)=\det[v_1\cdots v_n]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_n%29%3D%5Cdet%5Bv_1%5Ccdots+v_n%5D) 是交错的。

(3) 设 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上有两个线性函数![f,g:V\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg%3AV%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，定义一个新的 ![2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2) 重线性函数 ![f\wedge g:V\times V\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cwedge+g%3AV%5Ctimes+V%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 为 ![(f\wedge g)(u,v)=f(u)g(v)-f(v)g(u)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%5Cwedge+g%29%28u%2Cv%29%3Df%28u%29g%28v%29-f%28v%29g%28u%29) ，则函数 ![f\wedge g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cwedge+g) 是交错的。

**Section 4 置换对多重线性函数的作用**

设 ![f\in L_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L_k%28V%29) , ![\sigma \in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+%5Cin+S_k) . ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 对 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的**作用**得到一个新的 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数 ![\sigma f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+f) ,定义为： ![\sigma f(v_1,...,v_k)=f(v_{\sigma(1)},...,v_{\sigma(k)})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+f%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_k%29%3Df%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29) .

结合对称性和交错性的定义可以知， ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是对称的当且仅当 ![\sigma f=f, \text{ for } \forall\sigma\in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+f%3Df%2C+%5Ctext%7B+for+%7D+%5Cforall%5Csigma%5Cin+S_k) ；![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是交错的当且仅当 ![\sigma f=(\text{sgn}\ \sigma) f, \text{ for } \forall\sigma\in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+f%3D%28%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D%5C+%5Csigma%29+f%2C+%5Ctext%7B+for+%7D+%5Cforall%5Csigma%5Cin+S_k)。

**例子4.1 **

根据 ![\sigma=\bigg(\begin{array} \ 1\ 2\ 3 \\ 2\ 1\ 3 \end{array} \bigg)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3D%5Cbigg%28%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D+%5C+1%5C+2%5C+3+%5C%5C+2%5C+1%5C+3+%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cbigg%29) , ![\sigma(1)=2,\sigma(1)=3,\sigma(3)=3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%281%29%3D2%2C%5Csigma%281%29%3D3%2C%5Csigma%283%29%3D3) ；![\text{sgn} \ \sigma =-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D+%5C+%5Csigma+%3D-1) .

**引理4.1 （置换对线性函数的作用满足结合律）**

设 ![\sigma,\tau\in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%2C%5Ctau%5Cin+S_k) 和 ![f\in L_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L_k%28V%29) ，则 ![\tau(\sigma f)=(\tau \sigma)f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%28%5Csigma+f%29%3D%28%5Ctau+%5Csigma%29f) .

> 这个定理是说，对 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 先作用 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 再作用 ![\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau) ，等于对 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 直接作用 ![\tau \sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+%5Csigma) 。这个定理是自然的，因为把置换对 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的作用理解成交换参数的位置，那么，先后两次交换参数位置和一步到位交换参数位置的效果是相同的。
![\begin{align} \tau(\sigma f)(v_1,..,v_k)&=(\sigma f)(v_{\tau(1)},..,\tau_{\sigma(k)})\\ &=f(v_{\tau(\sigma(1))},...,v_{\tau(\sigma(k))})\\ &=f(v_{(\tau\sigma)(1)},...,v_{(\tau\sigma)(k)})\\ &=(\tau\sigma) f(v_1,..,v_k) \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%5Ctau%28%5Csigma+f%29%28v_1%2C..%2Cv_k%29%26%3D%28%5Csigma+f%29%28v_%7B%5Ctau%281%29%7D%2C..%2C%5Ctau_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29%5C%5C+%26%3Df%28v_%7B%5Ctau%28%5Csigma%281%29%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Ctau%28%5Csigma%28k%29%29%7D%29%5C%5C+%26%3Df%28v_%7B%28%5Ctau%5Csigma%29%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%28%5Ctau%5Csigma%29%28k%29%7D%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%28%5Ctau%5Csigma%29+f%28v_1%2C..%2Cv_k%29+%5Cend%7Balign%7D)
注：这里 ![\tau(\sigma f)(v_1,..,v_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%28%5Csigma+f%29%28v_1%2C..%2Cv_k%29) 中的数字 ![1,.,k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2C.%2Ck) 代表函数 ![\tau(\sigma f)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%28%5Csigma+f%29) 参数的下标； ![(\sigma f)(v_{\tau(1)},..,\tau_{\sigma(k)})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Csigma+f%29%28v_%7B%5Ctau%281%29%7D%2C..%2C%5Ctau_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29) 中的数字 ![1,.,k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2C.%2Ck) 代表函数 ![\sigma f(v_1,..,v_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+f%28v_1%2C..%2Cv_k%29) 参数的下标； ![f(v_{\tau(\sigma(1))},...,v_{\tau(\sigma(k))})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28v_%7B%5Ctau%28%5Csigma%281%29%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Ctau%28%5Csigma%28k%29%29%7D%29) 中的数字 ![1,.,k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2C.%2Ck) 代表函数 ![ f(v_1,..,v_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+f%28v_1%2C..%2Cv_k%29) 参数的下标

**（拓展）**更一般的**，**设 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 是一个[群](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/zh/%25E7%25BE%25A4)， ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是某个集合。映射 ![ \begin{align} &G\times X\to X,\\ &\ (\sigma,x)\ \mapsto \sigma\cdot x \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%26G%5Ctimes+X%5Cto+X%2C%5C%5C+%26%5C+%28%5Csigma%2Cx%29%5C+%5Cmapsto+%5Csigma%5Ccdot+x+%5Cend%7Balign%7D) 称为群 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 对 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的**左作用**（left action），如果满足：

(i) ![e\cdot x=x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ccdot+x%3Dx) ,这里 ![e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e) 是 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 的单位元； ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的任意元素；

(ii) ![\tau\cdot(\sigma\cdot x)=(\tau\sigma)\cdot x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%5Ccdot%28%5Csigma%5Ccdot+x%29%3D%28%5Ctau%5Csigma%29%5Ccdot+x) ,对任意 ![x\in X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+X) 和 ![\tau,\sigma\in G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%2C%5Csigma%5Cin+G) 成立。

类似的，可以定义群的右作用，群的左作用和右作用统称[群作用](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E7%25BE%25A4%25E4%25BD%259C%25E7%2594%25A8)。

接下来证明几个结论，服务于下一小节。

> 只证 ![\sigma(f+g)=\sigma f+\sigma g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%28f%2Bg%29%3D%5Csigma+f%2B%5Csigma+g) . ![\begin{align} \sigma(f+g)(v_1,...,v_k)&=(f+g)(v_{\sigma(1)},...,v_{\sigma(k)})\\ &=f(v_{\sigma(1)},...,v_{\sigma(k)})+g(v_{\sigma(1)},...,v_{\sigma(k)})\\ &=\sigma f+\sigma g \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%5Csigma%28f%2Bg%29%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_k%29%26%3D%28f%2Bg%29%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29%5C%5C+%26%3Df%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29%2Bg%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Csigma+f%2B%5Csigma+g+%5Cend%7Balign%7D)

**引理4.3 （来源于群论）**

设 ![S_{k}=\{\sigma_1,....,\sigma_{k!}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_%7Bk%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Csigma_1%2C....%2C%5Csigma_%7Bk%21%7D%5C%7D) ,任取 ![\tau \in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+%5Cin+S_k) ,定义 ![\tau S_{k}=\{\tau\sigma_1,....,\tau\sigma_{k!}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+S_%7Bk%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Ctau%5Csigma_1%2C....%2C%5Ctau%5Csigma_%7Bk%21%7D%5C%7D) ,

则有 ![\tau S_k=S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+S_k%3DS_k)

**注1**：该引理是说，从**有限**个元素的群 ![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 选出某个元素 ![\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau) ，作用在该群 ![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 上，得到的群 ![\tau S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+S_k) 和原来的群 ![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 是相同的。

**注2**：集合 ![\{1,2,..,k\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B1%2C2%2C..%2Ck%5C%7D) 上的置换有 ![k!](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%21)个(想象成排列，k个数字有多少种排列？），因此集合 ![S_{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_%7Bk%7D) 的元素个数是![k!](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%21)个（有限个）。

> 证明: （i）先证明每个 ![\tau \sigma_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+%5Csigma_i) 是置换，因此 ![\tau\sigma_i\in S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%5Csigma_i%5Cin+S_k) (因为 ![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 就是所有置换组成的集合).因为 ![\sigma_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma_1) 是置换， ![\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau) 是置换，因此乘积（复合）![\tau \sigma_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+%5Csigma_i)也是置换。
(ii)证明 ![\tau \sigma_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+%5Csigma_i) 是两两不同的，因此 ![\tau \sigma_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+%5Csigma_i) 是 ![k!](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%21) 个不同的置换。如若不然，则存在 ![\tau \sigma_i=\tau \sigma_j\Rightarrow \tau^{-1}\tau \sigma_i=\tau^{-1}\tau \sigma_j\Rightarrow\sigma_i= \sigma_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+%5Csigma_i%3D%5Ctau+%5Csigma_j%5CRightarrow+%5Ctau%5E%7B-1%7D%5Ctau+%5Csigma_i%3D%5Ctau%5E%7B-1%7D%5Ctau+%5Csigma_j%5CRightarrow%5Csigma_i%3D+%5Csigma_j) （其中 ![\tau^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%5E%7B-1%7D) 是 ![\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau) 的逆，满足 ![\tau^{-1} \tau=e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%5E%7B-1%7D+%5Ctau%3De) ），这与 ![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 中任意两个元素不同矛盾。因此![\tau \sigma_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau+%5Csigma_i) 是两两不同的。
(iii) 结合 ![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 的元素是**有限的**( ![k!](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%21) 个）。 ![S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k) 是由 ![k!](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%21) 个不同的置换构成，而集合 ![\{\tau\sigma_1,....,\tau\sigma_{k!}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Ctau%5Csigma_1%2C....%2C%5Ctau%5Csigma_%7Bk%21%7D%5C%7D) 正是有 ![k!](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%21) 个不同的置换，因此 ![S_k=\{\tau\sigma_1,....,\tau\sigma_{k!}\}=\tau S_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_k%3D%5C%7B%5Ctau%5Csigma_1%2C....%2C%5Ctau%5Csigma_%7Bk%21%7D%5C%7D%3D%5Ctau+S_k) .

**Section 5 对称化算子和交错化算子（Symmetrizing and Alternating Operators）**

任给 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ，可以按以下方法构造一个**对称的**![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数 ![Sf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Sf):

![(Sf)(v_1,...,v_k)=\sum_{\sigma\in S_k}f(v_{\sigma(1)},...,v_{\sigma(k)})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Sf%29%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_k%29%3D%5Csum_%7B%5Csigma%5Cin+S_k%7Df%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29)

或简写成， ![Sf=\sum_{\sigma\in S_k}\sigma f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Sf%3D%5Csum_%7B%5Csigma%5Cin+S_k%7D%5Csigma+f) 。

同样，任给 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ，可以构造一个**交错的**![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数 ![Af](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Af):

![(Af)(v_1,...,v_k)=\sum_{\sigma\in S_k}(\text{sgn} \ \sigma )f(v_{\sigma(1)},...,v_{\sigma(k)})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Af%29%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_k%29%3D%5Csum_%7B%5Csigma%5Cin+S_k%7D%28%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D+%5C+%5Csigma+%29f%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29)

简写成， ![Af=\sum_{\sigma\in S_k}(\text{sgn} \ \sigma )\sigma f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Af%3D%5Csum_%7B%5Csigma%5Cin+S_k%7D%28%5Ctext%7Bsgn%7D+%5C+%5Csigma+%29%5Csigma+f) 。

> 注：关于符号 ![\sum_{\sigma\in S_k}\sigma f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7B%5Csigma%5Cin+S_k%7D%5Csigma+f) 的意义：**所有**置换作用于 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 得到多个函数，然后再将它们加起来。

**例子5.1 **

**Section 6 张量积**

设 ![f,g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg) 分别是向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数和 ![l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l) 重线性函数，即 ![f\in L_k(V),g\in L_l(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L_k%28V%29%2Cg%5Cin+L_l%28V%29) , ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 和 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 的**张量积**（tensor product）是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的![(k+l)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28k%2Bl%29) 重线性函数 ![f\otimes g\in L_{k+l}(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cotimes+g%5Cin+L_%7Bk%2Bl%7D%28V%29) ,定义为：

![f \otimes g(v_1,...v_{k+l})=f(v_1,...,v_k)g(v_{k+1},...,v_{k+l})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f+%5Cotimes+g%28v_1%2C...v_%7Bk%2Bl%7D%29%3Df%28v_1%2C...%2Cv_k%29g%28v_%7Bk%2B1%7D%2C...%2Cv_%7Bk%2Bl%7D%29)

**例子6.3（双线性函数）**

设 ![e_1,...,e_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_1%2C...%2Ce_n) 是向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的基，![\alpha_1,...,\alpha_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha_1%2C...%2C%5Calpha_n) 是对偶空间 ![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee) 的**对偶基，**内积 ![\langle\ ,\rangle:V\times V \to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clangle%5C+%2C%5Crangle%3AV%5Ctimes+V+%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上的双线性函数。令 ![v=\sum v^ie_i,w=\sum w^ie_i,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%5Csum+v%5Eie_i%2Cw%3D%5Csum+w%5Eie_i%2C) 记 ![\langle e_i ,e_j \rangle=g_{ij} \in\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clangle+e_i+%2Ce_j+%5Crangle%3Dg_%7Bij%7D+%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) .因为 ![\{\alpha_i\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Calpha_i%5C%7D) 是对偶基，则 ![v^i=\alpha^i(v),w^i=\alpha^i(w)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%5Ei%3D%5Calpha%5Ei%28v%29%2Cw%5Ei%3D%5Calpha%5Ei%28w%29) .

根据 ![\langle\ ,\rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clangle%5C+%2C%5Crangle) 的双线性性，可以将其表达成两个一阶线性函数的张量积： ![\langle v ,w \rangle=\sum v^iw^j\langle e_i ,e_j \rangle=\sum\alpha^i(v)\alpha^j(w)g_{ij}=\sum g_{ij}(\alpha^i\otimes \alpha^j)(v,w)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clangle+v+%2Cw+%5Crangle%3D%5Csum+v%5Eiw%5Ej%5Clangle+e_i+%2Ce_j+%5Crangle%3D%5Csum%5Calpha%5Ei%28v%29%5Calpha%5Ej%28w%29g_%7Bij%7D%3D%5Csum+g_%7Bij%7D%28%5Calpha%5Ei%5Cotimes+%5Calpha%5Ej%29%28v%2Cw%29)

因此内积 ![\langle\ ,\rangle=\sum g_{ij}\alpha^i\otimes \alpha^j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clangle%5C+%2C%5Crangle%3D%5Csum+g_%7Bij%7D%5Calpha%5Ei%5Cotimes+%5Calpha%5Ej) .

> 这个例子也是低阶张量生成高阶张量的例子

**Section 7 楔积（Wedge Product）**

设 ![f,g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg) 是两个**交错线性函数**,我们希望它们的"积"也是**交错**的,然而张量积却不能保证这一点.举例 ![f,g\in A_2(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg%5Cin+A_2%28V%29) ,张量积就是 ![f\otimes g(v_1,v_2,v_3,v_4)=f(v_1,v_2)g(v_3,v_4)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cotimes+g%28v_1%2Cv_2%2Cv_3%2Cv_4%29%3Df%28v_1%2Cv_2%29g%28v_3%2Cv_4%29) ,而不一定有 ![\sigma (f\otimes g)=(\text{sgn }\sigma)f\otimes g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma+%28f%5Cotimes+g%29%3D%28%5Ctext%7Bsgn+%7D%5Csigma%29f%5Cotimes+g) .如何实现构造一个交错的积函数呢？一个想法是先做张量积再交错化，这启发我们给出下面楔积的定义。

**定义 7.1 (楔积 Wedge Product)**

对任意 ![f \in A_k(V),g\in A_l(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f+%5Cin+A_k%28V%29%2Cg%5Cin+A_l%28V%29) ,定义**楔积**(wedge product)或外积(exterior product) ![f\wedge g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cwedge+g) 如下

![(f \wedge g)(v_1,..,v_{k+l})=\frac{1}{k!l!}\sum_{\sigma \in S_{k+l}}(\text{sgn } \sigma)f(v_{\sigma(1)},..,v_{\sigma(k)})g(v_{\sigma(k+1)},..,v_{\sigma(k+l)})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f+%5Cwedge+g%29%28v_1%2C..%2Cv_%7Bk%2Bl%7D%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%21l%21%7D%5Csum_%7B%5Csigma+%5Cin+S_%7Bk%2Bl%7D%7D%28%5Ctext%7Bsgn+%7D+%5Csigma%29f%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C..%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29g%28v_%7B%5Csigma%28k%2B1%29%7D%2C..%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%2Bl%29%7D%29)

或简写成 ![(f\wedge g)=\frac{1}{k!l!}A(f\otimes g)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%5Cwedge+g%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%21l%21%7DA%28f%5Cotimes+g%29)

> 注1：根据命题5.1可知 ![(f\wedge g)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%5Cwedge+g%29) 是交错的
注2：楔积的系数 ![\frac{1}{k!l!}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%21l%21%7D) 是为了排除重复项。将自己理解和原文贴出如下，或者根据例子7.1理解：

![\begin{align} &\quad \ (f\wedge g)(v_1,..,v_{k+l})\\ &=\sum_{\sigma \in(k,l)\text{-}shuffle}(\text{sgn } \sigma)f(v_{\sigma(1)},..,v_{\sigma(k)})g(v_{\sigma(k+1)},..,v_{\sigma(k+l)}) \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%26%5Cquad+%5C+%28f%5Cwedge+g%29%28v_1%2C..%2Cv_%7Bk%2Bl%7D%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Csum_%7B%5Csigma+%5Cin%28k%2Cl%29%5Ctext%7B-%7Dshuffle%7D%28%5Ctext%7Bsgn+%7D+%5Csigma%29f%28v_%7B%5Csigma%281%29%7D%2C..%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%29%7D%29g%28v_%7B%5Csigma%28k%2B1%29%7D%2C..%2Cv_%7B%5Csigma%28k%2Bl%29%7D%29+%5Cend%7Balign%7D)

这样每一项都是不重复的，一共有 ![C_{k+l}^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C_%7Bk%2Bl%7D%5Ek) 项。

**定义9.1  (分级代数graded algebra)**

域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 上的代数 ![(A,\times)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28A%2C%5Ctimes%29) 称为是**分级代数**（graded algebra），如果满足：

(i) ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 能写成域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 上的**向量空间**![A^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Ei)**直和**的形式: ![A=\oplus_{k=0}^\infty A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+A%5Ek) ,

(ii) 若 ![A=\oplus_{k=0}^\infty A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+A%5Ek) ，定义的乘法 ![\times](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctimes) 能将 ![A^k\times A^l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Ek%5Ctimes+A%5El) 映到 ![A^{k+l}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7Bk%2Bl%7D) ，即 ![\times :A^k\times A^l\to A^{k+l}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctimes+%3AA%5Ek%5Ctimes+A%5El%5Cto+A%5E%7Bk%2Bl%7D) .

记号![A=\oplus_{k=0}^\infty A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+A%5Ek) 的意义是， ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的每个非零的元素可以唯一表示为有限和的形式： ![a=a_{i_1}+\cdots+a_{i_m},](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Da_%7Bi_1%7D%2B%5Ccdots%2Ba_%7Bi_m%7D%2C) 这里 ![a\in A,a_{i_j}\ne0\in A^{i_j}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+A%2Ca_%7Bi_j%7D%5Cne0%5Cin+A%5E%7Bi_j%7D) . 

分级代数 ![A=\oplus_{k=0}^\infty A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+A%5Ek) 称为是**反交换的**（anticommutative）(或者是分级交换的(graded commutative)），如果对任意 ![a\in A^k,b\in A^l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+A%5Ek%2Cb%5Cin+A%5El) 有： ![ab=(-1)^{kl}ba](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=ab%3D%28-1%29%5E%7Bkl%7Dba) .

分级代数的同态就是保持阶(degree)的同态. (A homomorphism of graded algebra is an algebra homomorphism that preserves the degree.)

对于有限维的向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) (设维数为 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) ),定义 ![A_*(V):=\oplus_{k=0}^\infty A_k(V)=\oplus_{k=0}^nA_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%2A%28V%29%3A%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+A_k%28V%29%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5EnA_k%28V%29) ，且定义**楔积**![\wedge](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwedge) 为向量的乘法. 那么 ![A_*(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%2A%28V%29) 就成了反交换的分级代数**，**称为 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)上余向量的**外代数（exterior algebra）。**总结为下面定理

**引理10.1 **

设 ![e_1,...,e_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_1%2C...%2Ce_n) 是实向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的基， ![\alpha^1,...,\alpha^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5E1%2C...%2C%5Calpha%5En) 是对偶空间 ![V^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%5Cvee) 的基。如果  ![I=(1\leq i_1<\cdots<i_k\leq n )<br>](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%281%5Cleq+i_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Ci_k%5Cleq+n+%29%3Cbr%3E) 和 ![J=(1\leq j_1<\cdots<j_k\leq n )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=J%3D%281%5Cleq+j_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Cj_k%5Cleq+n+%29) 是严格升序的 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重指标，则 ![\alpha^I(e_J)=\delta^I_J:=\begin{cases} 1\quad \text{for } I=J,\\ 0\quad \text{for } I\ne J. \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI%28e_J%29%3D%5Cdelta%5EI_J%3A%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+1%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bfor+%7D+I%3DJ%2C%5C%5C+0%5Cquad+%5Ctext%7Bfor+%7D+I%5Cne+J.+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D)

> 根据命题9.2， ![\alpha^I(e_J)=\det[\alpha^i(e_j)]_{i\in I,j\in J}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI%28e_J%29%3D%5Cdet%5B%5Calpha%5Ei%28e_j%29%5D_%7Bi%5Cin+I%2Cj%5Cin+J%7D) .
如果 ![I=J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3DJ) ,那么 ![[\alpha^i(e_j)]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B%5Calpha%5Ei%28e_j%29%5D) 就是一个单位矩阵，因此它的行列式就是 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1)
如果 ![I\ne J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%5Cne+J) ，那么我们对 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 和 ![J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=J) 逐项比较，直到出现不同的项 ![i_l\not=j_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l%5Cnot%3Dj_l) ，即：
![i_1=j_1,...,i_{l-1}=j_{l-1},i_l\not=j_l,...\ .](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_1%3Dj_1%2C...%2Ci_%7Bl-1%7D%3Dj_%7Bl-1%7D%2Ci_l%5Cnot%3Dj_l%2C...%5C+.)
不妨假设 ![i_l<j_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l%3Cj_l) ( ![i_l>j_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l%3Ej_l) 讨论类似)，接下来要说明 ![i_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l) 不同于 ![J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=J) 中所有的元素。首先 ![i_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l) 不同于 ![j_1,...,j_{l-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j_1%2C...%2Cj_%7Bl-1%7D) ，因为 ![j_1,...,j_{l-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j_1%2C...%2Cj_%7Bl-1%7D) 分别等于 ![i_1,...,i_{l-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_1%2C...%2Ci_%7Bl-1%7D) 而 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 是严格升序的。 其次![i_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l) 不同于 ![j_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j_l) ，这是因为假设 ![i_l<j_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l%3Cj_l) 。最后 ![i_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l) 不同于 ![j_{l+1},..,j_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j_%7Bl%2B1%7D%2C..%2Cj_k) ,因为 ![J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=J) 是升序的， ![j_{l+1},..,j_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j_%7Bl%2B1%7D%2C..%2Cj_k) 都比 ![j_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j_l) 大，而 ![i_l<j_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l%3Cj_l) 。
综上，![i_l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i_l) 不同于 ![j_1,...,j_{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j_1%2C...%2Cj_%7Bk%7D) ，故矩阵![[\alpha^i(e_j)]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B%5Calpha%5Ei%28e_j%29%5D)的第 ![l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l) 行的元素都是0。因此 ![\det[\alpha^i(e_j)]=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdet%5B%5Calpha%5Ei%28e_j%29%5D%3D0) 。

**命题 10.1 **

设 ![I=(1\leq i_1<\cdots<i_k\leq n )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%281%5Cleq+i_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Ci_k%5Cleq+n+%29) ,交错 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数 ![\alpha^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI) 形成 ![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29) 的基.

> (i)先证明![\alpha^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI)是线性无关的。
设 ![\sum_I c_I\alpha^I=0,c_I\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_I+c_I%5Calpha%5EI%3D0%2Cc_I%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) , ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 取遍所有严格升序 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 元组。将此式作用于 ![e_J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_J) , ![J=(1\leq j_1<\cdots<j_k\leq n )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=J%3D%281%5Cleq+j_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Cj_k%5Cleq+n+%29) .根据引理10.1,得 ![0=\sum_I c_I\alpha^I(e_J)=\sum_{I}c_I\delta^I_J=c_J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3D%5Csum_I+c_I%5Calpha%5EI%28e_J%29%3D%5Csum_%7BI%7Dc_I%5Cdelta%5EI_J%3Dc_J) .因此![\alpha^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI)是线性无关的。
(ii)接着证明![\alpha^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI)可以张成 ![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29) .
设 ![f\in A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+A_k%28V%29) .先设 ![f=\sum_I c_I\alpha^I,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3D%5Csum_I+c_I%5Calpha%5EI%2C)![I=(1\leq i_1<\cdots<i_k\leq n )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%281%5Cleq+i_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Ci_k%5Cleq+n+%29) ,看能否算出系数 ![c_I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_I) .将此式作用 ![e_J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_J) , ![J=(1\leq j_1<\cdots<j_k\leq n )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=J%3D%281%5Cleq+j_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Cj_k%5Cleq+n+%29) ,得到 ![f(e_J)=\sum_I c_I\alpha^I(e_J)=\sum_{I}c_I\delta^I_J=c_J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28e_J%29%3D%5Csum_I+c_I%5Calpha%5EI%28e_J%29%3D%5Csum_%7BI%7Dc_I%5Cdelta%5EI_J%3Dc_J) 。
因此 ![f=\sum_I f(e_I)\alpha^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3D%5Csum_I+f%28e_I%29%5Calpha%5EI) ,即![\alpha^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI)可以张成 ![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29) .

**推论 10.1 **

设向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的维数是 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) ,则由 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 阶余向量构成的向量空间![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29)的维数是 ![C_n^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C_n%5Ek)

> 因为向量空间![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29)的基是![\alpha^I,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI%2C)![I=(1\leq i_1<\cdots<i_k\leq n )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%281%5Cleq+i_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Ci_k%5Cleq+n+%29) 。其中 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 的取法有![C_n^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C_n%5Ek)种

**推论10.2 **

如果 ![k>\text{dim } V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3E%5Ctext%7Bdim+%7D+V) ,则 ![A_k(V)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29%3D0)

> 对于**每个**![\alpha^I=\alpha^{i_1}\wedge\cdots\wedge\alpha^{i_k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI%3D%5Calpha%5E%7Bi_1%7D%5Cwedge%5Ccdots%5Cwedge%5Calpha%5E%7Bi_k%7D) ,因为 ![k>\text{dim } V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3E%5Ctext%7Bdim+%7D+V) ，故至少存在相同的两个对偶基余向量，假设为 ![\alpha^l=\alpha^j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5El%3D%5Calpha%5Ej) 。将 ![\alpha^l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5El) 和 ![\alpha^j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Ej) 交换位置，得到 ![\alpha^I=-\alpha^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI%3D-%5Calpha%5EI) ，推出 ![2\alpha^I=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Calpha%5EI%3D0) ，即每个 ![\alpha^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5EI) 都为 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) .故 ![A_k(V)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29%3D0)
