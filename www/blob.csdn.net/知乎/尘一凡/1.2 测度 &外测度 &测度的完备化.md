**Section 1 测度的定义及性质**

**定义1 （测度）**

设 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 为全空间， ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 为 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数，函数 ![\mu：\mathfrak{a}\rightarrow[0,\infty] ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%EF%BC%9A%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%5Crightarrow%5B0%2C%5Cinfty%5D+) 成为测度（measure），如果满足：

(1) ![\mu(\varnothing)=0;](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%3B)

(2) ![\mu ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu+) 是可数可加的（countably additive），即：

![\mu( \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n )=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu(E_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28+%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n+%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29) ,for ![E_n \in \mathfrak{a},E_i\cap E_j=\varnothing(i \ne j)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2CE_i%5Ccap+E_j%3D%5Cvarnothing%28i+%5Cne+j%29)

注：测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 的**定义域**为**![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数****；**测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 取值**非负，**允许为 ![+\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2B%5Cinfty)

**定义2 (可加性和次可加性)**

(a)可加（additive）： ![\mu(E\cup F)=\mu(E)+\mu(F) ,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%5Ccup+F%29%3D%5Cmu%28E%29%2B%5Cmu%28F%29+%2C)![for\ E,F \in \mathfrak{a}, E \cap F=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=for%5C+E%2CF+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C+E+%5Ccap+F%3D%5Cvarnothing)

(b)有限可加（finitely additive）：

![\mu( \bigcup_{n=1}^{N}E_n )=\sum_{n=1}^{N}\mu(E_n), ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28+%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7DE_n+%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29%2C+)![ for \ E_1,...,E_N \in \mathfrak{a},E_i\cap E_j=\varnothing(i \ne j)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+for+%5C+E_1%2C...%2CE_N+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2CE_i%5Ccap+E_j%3D%5Cvarnothing%28i+%5Cne+j%29)

(c)次可加（subadditive）： ![\mu(E\cup F)\leq\mu(E)+\mu(F) , \ \forall E, F \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%5Ccup+F%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%28E%29%2B%5Cmu%28F%29+%2C+%5C+%5Cforall+E%2C+F+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)

(d)有限次可加（finite subadditive） : ![\mu( \bigcup_{n=1}^{N}E_n )\leq\sum_{n=1}^{N}\mu(E_n), ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28+%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7DE_n+%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29%2C+)![ for \ E_1,...,E_N \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+for+%5C+E_1%2C...%2CE_N+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)

(e)可数次可加（countably subadditive ）: 

![\mu( \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n )\leq\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu(E_n), ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28+%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n+%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29%2C+)![ for \ E_1,E_2... \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+for+%5C+E_1%2CE_2...+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)

**定义3 （有限和**![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma)**有限）**

（3.1） ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 叫做有限（finite )测度，如果有 ![\mu(X) <\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28X%29+%3C%5Cinfty) 。

> 即测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 在全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 上的取值有限

（3.2） ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 叫做 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 有限（ ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) finite）测度，如果 ![\exists \{E_n\} \subseteq \mathfrak{a}, \ \text{s.t.} \ \mu(E_n)<\infty \ \forall n \ \&X=\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+%5C%7BE_n%5C%7D+%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C+%5C+%5Ctext%7Bs.t.%7D+%5C+%5Cmu%28E_n%29%3C%5Cinfty+%5C+%5Cforall+n+%5C+%5C%26X%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n)

> 即，虽然**全空间**![ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+)![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的测度为 ![\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cinfty) ，但 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 可以表示为可数个 ![E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n) 的并，并且每个 ![E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n) 的测度是有限的。

![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta)**测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 的性质:**

(1)测度具有有限可加性，即

![ \ E_1,...,E_N \in \mathfrak{a},E_i\cap E_j=\varnothing(i \ne j)\Rightarrow\mu( \bigcup_{n=1}^{N}E_n )=\sum_{n=1}^{N}\mu(E_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5C+E_1%2C...%2CE_N+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2CE_i%5Ccap+E_j%3D%5Cvarnothing%28i+%5Cne+j%29%5CRightarrow%5Cmu%28+%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7DE_n+%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29)

> 由测度定义 ![\mu( \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n )=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu(E_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28+%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n+%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29) ，令 ![E_{n+1}=E_{n+2}=...=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_%7Bn%2B1%7D%3DE_%7Bn%2B2%7D%3D...%3D%5Cvarnothing) 即得。

(2)测度具有单调性，即![E,F \in \mathfrak{a}, E\subseteq F\Rightarrow\mu(E)\leq \mu(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%2CF+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C+E%5Csubseteq+F%5CRightarrow%5Cmu%28E%29%5Cleq+%5Cmu%28F%29)

> ![F=E\cup(F\backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F%3DE%5Ccup%28F%5Cbackslash+E%29) ,而  ![E \cap F \backslash E =\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E+%5Ccap+F+%5Cbackslash+E+%3D%5Cvarnothing) ,由测度的可加性有 ![\mu(F)=\mu(E)+\mu(F\backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28F%29%3D%5Cmu%28E%29%2B%5Cmu%28F%5Cbackslash+E%29) ,而测度是非负的， ![\mu(F\backslash E)\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28F%5Cbackslash+E%29%5Cgeq0) ,即得 ![\mu(E)\leq\mu(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%28F%29)

(3) ![E,F\in \mathfrak{a},E\subseteq F,\mu(E) <\infty \Rightarrow\mu(F\backslash E)=\mu(F)-\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%2CF%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2CE%5Csubseteq+F%2C%5Cmu%28E%29+%3C%5Cinfty+%5CRightarrow%5Cmu%28F%5Cbackslash+E%29%3D%5Cmu%28F%29-%5Cmu%28E%29)

> 由（2）的证明过程有 ![\mu(F)=\mu(E)+\mu(F\backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28F%29%3D%5Cmu%28E%29%2B%5Cmu%28F%5Cbackslash+E%29) ，我们看这个式子的左边 ![\mu(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28F%29) 和 ![\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29) ，若 ![\mu(E)=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3D%5Cinfty) 则 ![\mu(F)=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28F%29%3D%5Cinfty) （因为由单调性 ![\mu(E)\leq\mu(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%28F%29) ），此时 ![\mu(F)-\mu(E)=\infty-\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28F%29-%5Cmu%28E%29%3D%5Cinfty-%5Cinfty) 无意义。因此要求 ![\mu(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty) ,此时就有 ![\mu(F\backslash E)=\mu(F)-\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28F%5Cbackslash+E%29%3D%5Cmu%28F%29-%5Cmu%28E%29)

(4)（测度具有可数次可加性、有限次可加性、次可加性）

![E_n \in \mathfrak{a} \Rightarrow \mu(\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n)\leq\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu(E_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%5CRightarrow+%5Cmu%28%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29)

> 令 ![F_1=E_1,F_2=E_2 \backslash E_1,F_3=E_3\backslash (E_1 \cup E_2),...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F_1%3DE_1%2CF_2%3DE_2+%5Cbackslash+E_1%2CF_3%3DE_3%5Cbackslash+%28E_1+%5Ccup+E_2%29%2C...) ,则有 ![\forall n,F_n \in \mathfrak{a} ,F_n \subseteq E_n\ \&\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}F_n=\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n,\{F_n\} \ \text{mutually disjoint}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+n%2CF_n+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%2CF_n+%5Csubseteq+E_n%5C+%5C%26%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DF_n%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%2C%5C%7BF_n%5C%7D+%5C+%5Ctext%7Bmutually+disjoint%7D)![\mu(\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n)=\mu(\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}F_n)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu(F_n)\leq\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu(E_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%29%3D%5Cmu%28%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DF_n%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28F_n%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29)

(5)(Continuity from Below)

![E_n \in \mathfrak{a} \ \&\ E_n \subseteq E_{n+1} (E_n\uparrow)\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%5C+%5C%26%5C+E_n+%5Csubseteq+E_%7Bn%2B1%7D+%28E_n%5Cuparrow%29%5CRightarrow)![\[\mathop {\lim }\limits_{n \to \infty } \mu ({E_n}) = \mu (\mathop {\lim }\limits_{n \to \infty } {E_n})\]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%5Cmu+%28%7BE_n%7D%29+%3D+%5Cmu+%28%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%7BE_n%7D%29%5C%5D)

> ![\mathop {\lim }\limits_{n \to \infty } {E_n}=\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} E_n=E_1 \cup(E_2 \backslash E_1)\cup(E_3 \backslash E_2) \cup\cdots](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%7BE_n%7D%3D%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+E_n%3DE_1+%5Ccup%28E_2+%5Cbackslash+E_1%29%5Ccup%28E_3+%5Cbackslash+E_2%29+%5Ccup%5Ccdots) (两两互斥集的并)

**Section 2   外测度（outer Measure）**

> 接着所有的阐述都围绕下面这个段话进行：

外测度 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 定义在全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的**所有**子集上，抽出 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 中满足 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A)**可测**的集合，即 ![\mathfrak{a} =\{ \mu^*\ \text{mesurable subsets of } X\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%3D%5C%7B+%5Cmu%5E%2A%5C+%5Ctext%7Bmesurable+subsets+of+%7D+X%5C%7D) ,则有结论：（1） ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 是一个 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数；（2）外测度 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 限制在 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 上就成为了测度。

**定义4（外测度的定义）**

![\mu^*: \mathscr{P}(X) \to [0, \infty]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%3A+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29+%5Cto+%5B0%2C+%5Cinfty%5D) 成为外测度（outer measure）如果满足：

![\begin{align*} &(1)\mu^*(\varnothing)=0;\\ &(2)\mu^* \text{ is countably subadditive};\\ &(3)E,F \in \mathscr{P}(X), E\subseteq F \Rightarrow \mu^*(E)\leq \mu^*(F) \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%281%29%5Cmu%5E%2A%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%3B%5C%5C+%26%282%29%5Cmu%5E%2A+%5Ctext%7B+is+countably+subadditive%7D%3B%5C%5C+%26%283%29E%2CF+%5Cin+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29%2C+E%5Csubseteq+F+%5CRightarrow+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E%29%5Cleq+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28F%29+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

> 注：外测度定义在 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是所有子集上，也就是说 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的任意子集都可以谈论外测度。这与测度不同，测度定义在 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数上，即只有一部分 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 子集可以谈论测度。

**定义5 (**![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A)**可测）**

![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A)是定义在 ![\mathscr{P}(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29) 上的外测度， ![E \in \mathscr{P}(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E+%5Cin+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29) ， ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 可测的（ ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) measurable），如果满足： ![\mu^*(A)=\mu^*(A \cap E)+\mu^*(A\backslash E), \forall A \subseteq X \color{green}{\text{(Carathéodory 判别条件)}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+E%29%2C+%5Cforall+A+%5Csubseteq+X+%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B%5Ctext%7B%28Carath%C3%A9odory+%E5%88%A4%E5%88%AB%E6%9D%A1%E4%BB%B6%29%7D%7D)

> 注：由外测度的次可加性， ![\mu^*(A)\leq\mu^*(A \cap E)+\mu^*(A\backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+E%29) 恒成立，故常常利用 ![\mu^*(A)\geq\mu^*(A \cap E)+\mu^*(A\backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cgeq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+E%29) 这一方向去验证等式成立

对于可测集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ,要求对任意的 ![ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+)![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) ，有 ![\mu^*(A)=\mu^*(A \cap E)+\mu^*(A\backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+E%29)

注意到，若 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) =无限集，则 ![A \cap E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A+%5Ccap+E) 或 ![A\backslash E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+E) 至少有一个是无限集（若不然，则 ![A=(A \cap E ) \cup(A \backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%28A+%5Ccap+E+%29+%5Ccup%28A+%5Cbackslash+E%29) A表示为有限集的不交并，则A为有限的），故上面的等式恒成立。若 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) =有限集，则 ![A \cap E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A+%5Ccap+E) 和 ![A\backslash E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cbackslash+E) 都是有限集，又由 ![A=(A \cap E ) \cup(A \backslash E)\Rightarrow \#(A)=\#(A \cap E)+\#(A \backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%28A+%5Ccap+E+%29+%5Ccup%28A+%5Cbackslash+E%29%5CRightarrow+%5C%23%28A%29%3D%5C%23%28A+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5C%23%28A+%5Cbackslash+E%29) 即 ![\mu^*(A)=\mu^*(A \cap E)+\mu^*(A\backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+E%29) 也成立。因此，任意子集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 都是可测集。

2 定义 ![\mu^*(\varnothing)=0,\mu^*(E)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%2C%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E%29%3D1) 如果 ![E\ne \varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cne+%5Cvarnothing) 。说明 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是外测度，并找出可测集。

> ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是外测度。
对于可测集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ,要求对任意的 ![ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+)![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) ，有 ![\mu^*(A)=\mu^*(A \cap E)+\mu^*(A\backslash E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+E%29)
取 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 为全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) ，则 ![\mu^*(X)=\mu^*(X \cap E)+\mu^*(X\backslash E)=\mu^*(E)+\mu^*(E^C)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28X%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28X+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28X%5Cbackslash+E%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E%5EC%29)
注意到，对上面式子要成立，只可能 ![E=X \text{ or } E=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%3DX+%5Ctext%7B+or+%7D+E%3D%5Cvarnothing) 。因此可测集为 ![\varnothing, X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarnothing%2C+X)

3 设全空间 ![X=\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，定义 ![\mu^*(E) = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{ if }E\text{ is countable} \\ 1 & \text{ if }E\text{ is uncountable} \\ \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E%29+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+0+%26+%5Ctext%7B+if+%7DE%5Ctext%7B+is+countable%7D+%5C%5C+1+%26+%5Ctext%7B+if+%7DE%5Ctext%7B+is+uncountable%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D) ,说明 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是外测度，找出可测集。

**Section 3 测度的完备化**

**定义6 （测度空间）**

设有空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) ，在其上定义了 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 和测度函数 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) ，那么称三元组![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29) 为测度空间。

**定义7  （测度完备性）**

![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29) 为测度空间，称 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是完备的（complete），如果 ![E \in \mathfrak{a},\mu(E)=0,N\subseteq E\Rightarrow N \in \mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%28E%29%3D0%2CN%5Csubseteq+E%5CRightarrow+N+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)

**定理2  **设 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 是外测度， ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 为所有**![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 可测集**( 即![\mathfrak{a}=\{ E \in \mathscr{P}(x):\mu^*(A)=\mu^*(A \cap E)+\mu^*(A\backslash E), \forall A \subseteq X \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%3D%5C%7B+E+%5Cin+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28x%29%3A%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+E%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+E%29%2C+%5Cforall+A+%5Csubseteq+X+%5C%7D) )，则 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 限制在 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 上得到的测度 ![\mu=\mu^*|_{\mathfrak{a}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%7C_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%7D) 是完备的。

> 我们只需验证， ![E \in \mathfrak{a},\mu(E)=0,N\subseteq E\Rightarrow \mu^*(A)\geq\mu^*(A \cap N)+\mu^*(A\backslash N), \forall A \subseteq X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%28E%29%3D0%2CN%5Csubseteq+E%5CRightarrow+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cgeq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+N%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+N%29%2C+%5Cforall+A+%5Csubseteq+X)

任取 ![\forall A \subseteq X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+A+%5Csubseteq+X) ，先证明 ![\mu^*(A \cap N)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+N%29%3D0) ，这是因为 ![\mu^*(A \cap N)\leq\mu^*(N)\leq\mu^*(E)=\mu(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+N%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28N%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28E%29%3D%5Cmu%28E%29%3D0)
由于 ![A\supseteq A\backslash N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csupseteq+A%5Cbackslash+N) ,故 ![\mu^*(A)\geq \mu^*(A\backslash N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cgeq+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+N%29)
进而有 ![ \mu^*(A)\geq\mu^*(A \cap N)+\mu^*(A\backslash N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cgeq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A+%5Ccap+N%29%2B%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%5Cbackslash+N%29)
这就完成了证明。

**例子**（不完备测度的例子）

![\mathfrak{a}=\{\varnothing,X\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%2CX%5C%7D) , ![\mu(\varnothing)=\mu(X)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D%5Cmu%28X%29%3D0) ，若 ![\#X\geq2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%23X%5Cgeq2) ( ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 元素个数大于2)，则测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 不完备。因为此时对于 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 有![\mu(X)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28X%29%3D0) ，我们取 ![N\subset X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%5Csubset+X) (真包含)，但 ![\mathfrak{a}=\{\varnothing,X\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%2CX%5C%7D) ，即 ![N\notin\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%5Cnotin%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)

下个定理表明，任意测度都扩充成完备的测度。

**定理3 （测度完备化）**

设 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 上的测度。

(1)令 ![\bar {\mathfrak{a}}=\{E\cup N :E \in \mathfrak{a},N\subseteq A,\text{where } A \in \mathfrak{a}\ \&\ \mu(A)=0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar+%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%7D%3D%5C%7BE%5Ccup+N+%3AE+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2CN%5Csubseteq+A%2C%5Ctext%7Bwhere+%7D+A+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5Cmu%28A%29%3D0%5C%7D) ，**则 **![\bar {\mathfrak{a}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar+%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%7D)** 是一个 **![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma)** 代数** ；

(2)令 ![\bar{\mu}(E \cup N)=\mu(E), \ \text{for} \ E \cup N \in\bar{\mathfrak{a}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7B%5Cmu%7D%28E+%5Ccup+N%29%3D%5Cmu%28E%29%2C+%5C+%5Ctext%7Bfor%7D+%5C+E+%5Ccup+N+%5Cin%5Cbar%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%7D)，则 ![\bar{\mu}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7B%5Cmu%7D) 是 ![\bar{\mathfrak{a}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%7D) 上一个**完备**的测度。

> 注：(1) ![\bar {\mathfrak{a}}\supseteq\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar+%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%7D%5Csupseteq%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ;(2) ![\bar{\mu}|_{\mathfrak{a} }=\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar%7B%5Cmu%7D%7C_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D+%7D%3D%5Cmu)
（3）这个定理是说，对于不完备的测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) ，我们可以将 ![\{N\in \mathscr{P}(X):N\subseteq A \ \&\ \mu(A)=0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BN%5Cin+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29%3AN%5Csubseteq+A+%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5Cmu%28A%29%3D0%5C%7D) (包含于测度为0的集合)添加到原来的 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D)中，就可以将测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 完备化。
