**定义1.1 余切空间（cotangent space）**

![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 里点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处的余切空间记做![T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 或者 ![T_p^*\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En),定义为切空间![T_p\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)的[对偶空间](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/40321372)![(T_p\mathbb{R}^n)^\vee](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29%5E%5Cvee) .

> 因此余切空间![T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29)的元素就是切空间![T_p\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)的线性函数（或叫余向量、1阶余向量）

与向量场类似，开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的余向量场（或者叫**微分 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 形式**）可以看做一个映射 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) ， ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 赋予![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U)上每一个点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 一个余向量 ![\omega_p \in T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega_p+%5Cin+T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) ,即 ![\begin{cases} \omega:&U\to \bigcup_{p\in U}T^*_p(\mathbb{R}^n)\\ &p\mapsto \omega_p\in T^*_p(\mathbb{R}^n) \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%5Comega%3A%26U%5Cto+%5Cbigcup_%7Bp%5Cin+U%7DT%5E%2A_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29%5C%5C+%26p%5Cmapsto+%5Comega_p%5Cin+T%5E%2A_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D) ,注意到并集 ![\bigcup_{p\in U}T^*_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcup_%7Bp%5Cin+U%7DT%5E%2A_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 里的每一个集合 ![T^*_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T%5E%2A_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 都是不相交的（无交集的）。我们将「微分 ![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式」简称为「 ![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式」。

> 也就是说，某一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处的微分![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式是对偶向量（余向量）。某个开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的微分 ![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式是对偶向量**场**（余向量**场**）。

![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta) 对任意 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数 ![f:U\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ,我们可以构造![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) -形式 ![df](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df) ,叫做 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的微分，定义如下：

对任意 ![p\in U,X_p\in T_p U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+U%2CX_p%5Cin+T_p+U) ,定义 ![(df)_p(X_p)=X_pf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28df%29_p%28X_p%29%3DX_pf) 。

> ![X_pf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_pf) 是 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点处沿向量 ![X_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p) 的方向导数，它是一个实数。对 ![X_pf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_pf) 的一种理解是向量 ![X_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p) 作用到 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 上；还有一种理解是 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 作用到 ![X_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p) 上，我们将这种作用就命名为 ![df](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df) ，得到 ![(df)_p(X_p)=X_pf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28df%29_p%28X_p%29%3DX_pf) 。

设 ![x^1,...,x^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E1%2C...%2Cx%5En) 是 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 的标准坐标系，在[1.1节S4](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/40287232)我们已经知道集合 ![\{\frac{\partial}{\partial x^1}\bigg|_p,...,\frac{\partial}{\partial x^n}\bigg|_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5E1%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%2C...%2C%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5En%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5C%7D) 是切空间![T_p\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)的基。接下来的命题要来找出余切空间![T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29)的基。

**命题1.1**

设 ![x^1,...,x^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E1%2C...%2Cx%5En) 是 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 的标准坐标系,对于![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)的每一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) ，集合 ![\{(dx^1)_p,...,(dx^n)_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28dx%5E1%29_p%2C...%2C%28dx%5En%29_p%5C%7D) 是余切空间![T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29)的(对偶)基，**且**关于切空间![T_p\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)的基 ![\{\frac{\partial}{\partial x^1}\bigg|_p,...,\frac{\partial}{\partial x^n}\bigg|_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5E1%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%2C...%2C%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5En%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5C%7D) 对偶。

> (i)先证明![\{(dx^1)_p,...,(dx^n)_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28dx%5E1%29_p%2C...%2C%28dx%5En%29_p%5C%7D)关于基 ![\{\frac{\partial}{\partial x^1}\bigg|_p,...,\frac{\partial}{\partial x^n}\bigg|_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5E1%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%2C...%2C%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5En%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5C%7D) 对偶。
![(dx^i)_p\bigg(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p\bigg)=\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_px^i=\delta^i_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28dx%5Ei%29_p%5Cbigg%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_px%5Ei%3D%5Cdelta%5Ei_j) 。因此两组基对偶。
(ii)再证明![\{(dx^1)_p,...,(dx^n)_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28dx%5E1%29_p%2C...%2C%28dx%5En%29_p%5C%7D)线性无关。
令![\sum c_i (d x^i)_p=0,c_i \in \mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+c_i+%28d+x%5Ei%29_p%3D0%2Cc_i+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En),将此式两边作用到向量![\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p)，得![0=\sum c_i (d x^i)_p\bigg(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p\bigg)=\sum c_i\delta^i_j=c_j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3D%5Csum+c_i+%28d+x%5Ei%29_p%5Cbigg%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%29%3D%5Csum+c_i%5Cdelta%5Ei_j%3Dc_j).因此![\{(dx^1)_p,...,(dx^n)_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28dx%5E1%29_p%2C...%2C%28dx%5En%29_p%5C%7D)线性无关。
(iii)再证明![\{(dx^1)_p,...,(dx^n)_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28dx%5E1%29_p%2C...%2C%28dx%5En%29_p%5C%7D)可以张成余切空间![T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29)。
任取 ![\omega_p \in T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega_p+%5Cin+T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) ，设 ![\omega_p=\sum a_i(p)(dx^i)_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega_p%3D%5Csum+a_i%28p%29%28dx%5Ei%29_p) 其中系数 ![a_i(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_i%28p%29) 是实数，接下来算出这些系数。将上式两边作用于![\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p) ，得 ![(w)_p\bigg(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p\bigg)=\sum a_i(p)\bigg(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p\bigg)(dx^i)_p=a_j(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28w%29_p%5Cbigg%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%29%3D%5Csum+a_i%28p%29%5Cbigg%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%29%28dx%5Ei%29_p%3Da_j%28p%29)
即系数 ![a_j(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_j%28p%29) 是实数 ![(w)_p\bigg(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p\bigg)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28w%29_p%5Cbigg%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%29) .这就证明了 ![\{(dx^1)_p,...,(dx^n)_p\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%28dx%5E1%29_p%2C...%2C%28dx%5En%29_p%5C%7D) 可以张成余切空间 ![T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29)

上面命题1.1(iii)的证明中 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 是一般的 ![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式，若 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 可以写成某个函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的微分，即是 ![\omega=df](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3Ddf) ,则展开系数恰好是 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的偏导数，见命题1.2.

**命题1.2**

设 ![f:U\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 里开集![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数, ![p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+U) ,则 ![(df)_p=\sum \frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}\bigg|_p(dx^i)_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28df%29_p%3D%5Csum+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%28dx%5Ei%29_p)

注： ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 里变化，就得到 ![df=\sum \frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}dx^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df%3D%5Csum+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7Ddx%5Ei)

> 任给 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 里开集![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上光滑函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ，则 ![(df)_p\in T_p^*(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28df%29_p%5Cin+T_p%5E%2A%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 。
设 ![(df)_p=\sum a_i(p)(dx^i)_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28df%29_p%3D%5Csum+a_i%28p%29%28dx%5Ei%29_p) ,其中 ![a_i(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_i%28p%29) 是实数，接下来来算出这些系数。
将上式两边作用于![\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p) ，得 ![(df)_p\bigg(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p\bigg)=\sum a_i(p)\bigg(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p\bigg)(dx^i)_p=a_j(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28df%29_p%5Cbigg%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%29%3D%5Csum+a_i%28p%29%5Cbigg%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%29%28dx%5Ei%29_p%3Da_j%28p%29) .
因此系数 ![a_j(p)=(df)_p\bigg(\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg|_p\bigg)=\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^j}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_j%28p%29%3D%28df%29_p%5Cbigg%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D) .

如果 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 是![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 开集![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的 ![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D)形式，根据命题1.1，对于每一点 ![p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+U) , ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 可以写成线性组合 ![\omega_p=\sum a_i(p)(dx^i)_p,\ a_i(p)\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega_p%3D%5Csum+a_i%28p%29%28dx%5Ei%29_p%2C%5C+a_i%28p%29%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 。当 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 取遍 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 中的点时，系数 ![a_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_i) 就成了 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的实函数。因此可以将 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 写成![\omega=\sum a_idx^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3D%5Csum+a_idx%5Ei) 。余向量**场**![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 称作在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上是 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) (光滑的），如果它所有的系数函数 ![ a_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+a_i) 在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上都是![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) (光滑的）。

如果 ![x,y,z](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%2Cy%2Cz) 是 ![\mathbb{R}^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E3) 的坐标系，那么 ![dx,dy,dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dx%2Cdy%2Cdz) 就是![\mathbb{R}^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E3)上的 ![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D)形式。这样，在微积分中，原本只是作为记号的![dx,dy,dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dx%2Cdy%2Cdz)就有了确切的意义—— ![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式。

**Section 2 微分 **![k\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7D)** 形式（Differential k-forms）**

**定义2.1 （微分 **![k\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7D)** 形式）**

![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 开集![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的**微分 **![k\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7D)** 形式**（简称![k\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式）是一个映射 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) ，它将 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 里的每一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 赋予一个切空间 ![T_p\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 上的交错 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 重线性函数（ ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 阶余向量），即 ![\omega_p\in A_k(T_p\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega_p%5Cin+A_k%28T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 。

> 因为余切空间 ![T_p^*\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 可以看成是 ![A_1(T_p\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_1%28T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) ，即 ![T_p^*\mathbb{R}^n=A_1(T_p\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5E%2A%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%3DA_1%28T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) ，因此此定义可以看成是微分**1**-形式的推广。

根据[1.2节S10的命题10.1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/40321372) , ![A_k(T_p\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 的一个基是 ![dx^I_p=dx^{i_1}_p\wedge\cdots\wedge dx^{i_k}_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dx%5EI_p%3Ddx%5E%7Bi_1%7D_p%5Cwedge%5Ccdots%5Cwedge+dx%5E%7Bi_k%7D_p) ,其中 ![1\leq i_1<\cdots<i_k\leq n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Cleq+i_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Ci_k%5Cleq+n) .（这里下标 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 表示在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点）

因此对 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 中每一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) , ![\omega_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega_p)  可以写成线性组合的形式： ![\omega_p=\sum a_I(p) dx^I_p,\quad 1\leq i_1<\cdots<i_k\leq n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega_p%3D%5Csum+a_I%28p%29+dx%5EI_p%2C%5Cquad+1%5Cleq+i_1%3C%5Ccdots%3Ci_k%5Cleq+n)

![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的 ![k\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 也可以写成线性组合的形式：

![\omega=\sum a_I dx^I, \quad a_I:U \to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3D%5Csum+a_I+dx%5EI%2C+%5Cquad+a_I%3AU+%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)

![k\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 称作在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上是 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) (光滑的），如果它所有的系数函数 ![ a_I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+a_I) 在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上都是![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) (光滑的）

将 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的所有![k\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7D)形式组成的集合（也是一个向量空间）记做 ![\Omega^k(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5Ek%28U%29) . ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的 ![0\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式给 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 里每一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 赋予一个实数 ![f\in A_0(T_p\mathbb{R}^n)=\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+A_0%28T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29%3D%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ,因此， ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的 ![0\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式就是 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的一个函数，因此 ![\Omega^0(U)=C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5E0%28U%29%3DC%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) . 此外， ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 空间上**不**存在阶数大于 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 的非零微分形式，原因见[1.2节S10的推论10.2](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/40321372)。

![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上![k\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Ctext%7B-%7D)形式 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 和![l\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l%5Ctext%7B-%7D)形式 ![\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau) 的楔积的定义是**逐点的**（pointwise）： ![(\omega\wedge \tau)_p=\omega_p\wedge\tau_p,\quad p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Comega%5Cwedge+%5Ctau%29_p%3D%5Comega_p%5Cwedge%5Ctau_p%2C%5Cquad+p%5Cin+U)

可用线性组合（分量）表示为：设 ![\omega=\sum_Ia_Idx^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3D%5Csum_Ia_Idx%5EI) , ![\tau=\sum_Jb_Jdx^J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%3D%5Csum_Jb_Jdx%5EJ) ,则 ![\omega\wedge \tau=\sum_{I,J}(a_Ib_J)dx^I\wedge dx^J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%5Cwedge+%5Ctau%3D%5Csum_%7BI%2CJ%7D%28a_Ib_J%29dx%5EI%5Cwedge+dx%5EJ)

在上面求和式中，如果 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 和 ![J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=J) 有相同的元素，则 ![dx^I\wedge dx^J=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dx%5EI%5Cwedge+dx%5EJ%3D0) .因此，上式又可以写成： ![\omega\wedge \tau=\sum_{I,J \text{ disjoint}}(a_Ib_J)dx^I\wedge dx^J](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%5Cwedge+%5Ctau%3D%5Csum_%7BI%2CJ+%5Ctext%7B+disjoint%7D%7D%28a_Ib_J%29dx%5EI%5Cwedge+dx%5EJ) .

这表明两个 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 形式的楔积仍是 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 的。由上可知，楔积是一个**双线性**映射 ![\wedge:\Omega^k(U)\times \Omega^l(U)\to\Omega^{k+l}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwedge%3A%5COmega%5Ek%28U%29%5Ctimes+%5COmega%5El%28U%29%5Cto%5COmega%5E%7Bk%2Bl%7D%28U%29) .根据[1.2节S8和S9](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/40321372) ,两个微分形式的楔积是**反交换**和满足**结合律**的。有一种特殊情形，其中一个微分形式的阶为零，比如![k=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D0) ，那么楔积 ![\wedge:\Omega^0(U)\times \Omega^l(U)\to\Omega^{l}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwedge%3A%5COmega%5E0%28U%29%5Ctimes+%5COmega%5El%28U%29%5Cto%5COmega%5E%7Bl%7D%28U%29) 就变成了一个![l\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l%5Ctext%7B-%7D)形式和一个 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数逐点相乘： ![(f\wedge\tau)_p=f(p)\wedge \tau_p=f(p)\tau_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%5Cwedge%5Ctau%29_p%3Df%28p%29%5Cwedge+%5Ctau_p%3Df%28p%29%5Ctau_p) (楔积的运算规则已在[1.2节S7](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/40321372)说明）

**例子2.3**

（a）设 ![x,y,z](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%2Cy%2Cz) 是![\mathbb{R}^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E3)的坐标系。则 ![\mathbb{R}^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E3)上的![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty)![1\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式可表示为 ![f\ dx+g\ d y+h\ dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5C+dx%2Bg%5C+d+y%2Bh%5C+dz) ，其中 ![f,g,h](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg%2Ch) 都是![\mathbb{R}^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E3)上 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数。![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty)![2\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式可表示为 ![f\ dy\wedge dz+g\ d x\wedge dz+h\ dx\wedge dy](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5C+dy%5Cwedge+dz%2Bg%5C+d+x%5Cwedge+dz%2Bh%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dy) 。 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty)![3\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 形式可表示为 ![f \ dx\wedge\ dy\wedge dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f+%5C+dx%5Cwedge%5C+dy%5Cwedge+dz) .

（b）设 ![\omega=f\ dx+g\ d y+h\ dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3Df%5C+dx%2Bg%5C+d+y%2Bh%5C+dz) ， ![\tau=g\ dx+h\ d y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%3Dg%5C+dx%2Bh%5C+d+y) ，求 ![\omega\wedge\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%5Cwedge%5Ctau)

> ![\begin{align} \omega\wedge\tau&=(f\ dx+g\ d y+h\ dz)\wedge(g\ dx+h\ d y)\\ &=\ fg\ dx\wedge dx+g^2dy\wedge dx+hg \ dz\wedge dx\\ &+fh\ dx\wedge dy+gh \ dy\wedge dy+h^2\ dz\wedge dy\\ &=(fh-g^2)\ dx\wedge dy-hg\ dx\wedge dz-h^2\ dy\wedge dz \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%5Comega%5Cwedge%5Ctau%26%3D%28f%5C+dx%2Bg%5C+d+y%2Bh%5C+dz%29%5Cwedge%28g%5C+dx%2Bh%5C+d+y%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%5C+fg%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dx%2Bg%5E2dy%5Cwedge+dx%2Bhg+%5C+dz%5Cwedge+dx%5C%5C+%26%2Bfh%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dy%2Bgh+%5C+dy%5Cwedge+dy%2Bh%5E2%5C+dz%5Cwedge+dy%5C%5C+%26%3D%28fh-g%5E2%29%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dy-hg%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dz-h%5E2%5C+dy%5Cwedge+dz+%5Cend%7Balign%7D)

![\Omega^k(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5Ek%28U%29) 是域 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的向量空间，令 ![\Omega^*(U)=\oplus_{k=0}^n\Omega^k(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5En%5COmega%5Ek%28U%29) ,在 ![\Omega^*(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29) 定义楔积 ![\wedge](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwedge) 作为乘法，微分形式的阶数作为分级代数的级数，则 ![\Omega^*(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29) 成为 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的**反交换分级代数**（[证明类似1.2节S9定理9.1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/40321372)）。另外光滑k-形式可以和光滑函数作(标量)乘法，因此 ![\Omega^k(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5Ek%28U%29) 也是环 ![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) 上的模，故而直和 ![\Omega^*(U)=\oplus_{k=0}^n\Omega^k(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5En%5COmega%5Ek%28U%29) 也是环 ![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) 上的模。

**Section 3 微分形式作为向量场上的线性函数**

在 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 的开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上， ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 是一个光滑的(![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty))**1-**形式， ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是光滑的向量场, 定义 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的函数如下：

![\omega(X)_p=\omega_p(X_p), \quad p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%28X%29_p%3D%5Comega_p%28X_p%29%2C+%5Cquad+p%5Cin+U)

写成分量的形式， ![\omega=\sum a_i dx^i,\quad X=\sum b^j\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3D%5Csum+a_i+dx%5Ei%2C%5Cquad+X%3D%5Csum+b%5Ej%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D) ,这里 ![a_i,b^j \in C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_i%2Cb%5Ej+%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29)

则 ![\omega(X)=(\sum a_i dx^i)\bigg(b^j\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}\bigg)=\sum a_ib^i \in C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%28X%29%3D%28%5Csum+a_i+dx%5Ei%29%5Cbigg%28b%5Ej%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7D%5Cbigg%29%3D%5Csum+a_ib%5Ei+%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29)

即表明 ![\omega(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%28X%29) 是开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数。因此， ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的一个![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty)的**1**-形式可以看成是从 ![\mathfrak{X}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7BX%7D%28U%29) 到 ![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) 的映射。

这个函数 ![\omega(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%28X%29) 实际上是**环**![C^\infty(U)=\mathcal{F}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29%3D%5Cmathcal%7BF%7D%28U%29) 上的线性函数，即，若 ![f\in C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) ,则 ![\omega(fX)=f\omega(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%28fX%29%3Df%5Comega%28X%29) . 要证明这个式子在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上成立，只需证明在每一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 上成立：

![\begin{align} (\omega(fX))_p&=\omega_p(f(p)X_p)\quad (\omega(fX)的定义)\\ &=f(p)\omega_p(X_p)\quad (\omega_p 是 \mathbb{R-}线性的)\\ &=(f\omega(X))_p\ \ \ \ \quad (f\omega(X)的定义) \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%28%5Comega%28fX%29%29_p%26%3D%5Comega_p%28f%28p%29X_p%29%5Cquad+%28%5Comega%28fX%29%E7%9A%84%E5%AE%9A%E4%B9%89%29%5C%5C+%26%3Df%28p%29%5Comega_p%28X_p%29%5Cquad+%28%5Comega_p+%E6%98%AF+%5Cmathbb%7BR-%7D%E7%BA%BF%E6%80%A7%E7%9A%84%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%28f%5Comega%28X%29%29_p%5C+%5C+%5C+%5C+%5Cquad+%28f%5Comega%28X%29%E7%9A%84%E5%AE%9A%E4%B9%89%29+%5Cend%7Balign%7D)

因此，![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty)的**1**-形式给出一个 ![\mathcal{F}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathcal%7BF%7D%28U%29)**-**线性函数 ![\mathfrak{X}(U)\to \mathcal{F}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7BX%7D%28U%29%5Cto+%5Cmathcal%7BF%7D%28U%29) , ![X \mapsto \omega(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X+%5Cmapsto+%5Comega%28X%29) 。同样的， ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty)的**k**-形式给出一个环 ![\mathcal{F}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathcal%7BF%7D%28U%29) 上的**k**重线性函数 ， 

![\underbrace{\mathfrak{X}(U)\times\cdots\times\mathfrak{X}(U)}_{k个} \to \mathcal{F}(U)\\ (X_1,\cdots,X_k) \mapsto \omega(X_1,..,X_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cmathfrak%7BX%7D%28U%29%5Ctimes%5Ccdots%5Ctimes%5Cmathfrak%7BX%7D%28U%29%7D_%7Bk%E4%B8%AA%7D+%5Cto+%5Cmathcal%7BF%7D%28U%29%5C%5C+%28X_1%2C%5Ccdots%2CX_k%29+%5Cmapsto+%5Comega%28X_1%2C..%2CX_k%29)

**Section 4 外导数（The Exterior Derivative）**

**定义4.1 外导数/外微分(算子）**

**外导数算子**(exterior derivative),或叫**外微分算子**(exterior differentiation)是一个映射，记作 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) ,它将一个**k-**形式映射成一个**(k+1)-**形式，即 ![d:\Omega^k(U)\to\Omega^{k+1}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%3A%5COmega%5Ek%28U%29%5Cto%5COmega%5E%7Bk%2B1%7D%28U%29) 。

在定义![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的任意**k-**形式的外导数之前，要定义0-形式（光滑函数）的外导数，接着再定义 ![k\geq1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cgeq1) 形式的外导数，见定义4.1和定义4.2

> 注：今后将混用外导数和外微分，它们都记做 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d)

在定义![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的光滑**k-形式**的外导数之前，先来定义0-形式（光滑函数）的外导数。

**定义4.2 （0-形式的外导数）**

设 ![f\in C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) ,则 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的**外导数**(exterior derivative)或叫**外微分**(exterior differentiation)定义为它的微分 ![df\in \Omega^1(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df%5Cin+%5COmega%5E1%28U%29) ,写成分量形式即是， ![df=\sum\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}dx^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df%3D%5Csum%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7Ddx%5Ei)

**定义4.3 （k-形式的外导数）**

设 ![k\geq1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cgeq1) ,若 ![\omega=\sum_I a_Idx^I\in \Omega^k(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3D%5Csum_I+a_Idx%5EI%5Cin+%5COmega%5Ek%28U%29) ,则 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 的**外导数**或**外微分**定义为

![d\ \omega=\sum_Id a_I\wedge dx^I=\sum_I\bigg(\sum_j\frac{\partial a_I}{\partial x^j}dx^j\bigg)\wedge dx^I \in \Omega^{k+1}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5C+%5Comega%3D%5Csum_Id+a_I%5Cwedge+dx%5EI%3D%5Csum_I%5Cbigg%28%5Csum_j%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+a_I%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7Ddx%5Ej%5Cbigg%29%5Cwedge+dx%5EI+%5Cin+%5COmega%5E%7Bk%2B1%7D%28U%29)

> 注1：因为系数函数 ![a_I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_I) 是**0**-形式，因此 ![d a_I=\sum_j\frac{\partial a_I}{\partial x^j}dx^j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d+a_I%3D%5Csum_j%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+a_I%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ej%7Ddx%5Ej)
注2：外导数或者外微分是一个映射，是一个过程，我们可以说“对 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 取外微分/外导数”

**例子4.1**

设 ![\mathbb{R}^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E2) 上的1-形式 ![\omega=f\ dx+g\ dy](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3Df%5C+dx%2Bg%5C+dy) ，这里 ![f,g \in C^\infty(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg+%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) . 记 ![\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}=f_x,\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}=f_y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D%3Df_x%2C%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7D%3Df_y) ，求 ![d\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5Comega) .

> ![\begin{align} d\omega&=df\wedge\ dx+dg\wedge\ dy\\ &=(f_x\ dx+f_y\ dy)\wedge dx+(g_x\ dx+g_y\ dy)\wedge\ dy\\ &=(g_x-f_x)dx\wedge dy \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+d%5Comega%26%3Ddf%5Cwedge%5C+dx%2Bdg%5Cwedge%5C+dy%5C%5C+%26%3D%28f_x%5C+dx%2Bf_y%5C+dy%29%5Cwedge+dx%2B%28g_x%5C+dx%2Bg_y%5C+dy%29%5Cwedge%5C+dy%5C%5C+%26%3D%28g_x-f_x%29dx%5Cwedge+dy+%5Cend%7Balign%7D)
注： ![dy\wedge dx=-dx \wedge dy,\quad dx\wedge dx= dy \wedge dy=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dy%5Cwedge+dx%3D-dx+%5Cwedge+dy%2C%5Cquad+dx%5Cwedge+dx%3D+dy+%5Cwedge+dy%3D0) (见1.2节交错线性函数楔积的运算规则）

接下来先介绍一般分级代数的反导子

**定义 4.4****(分级代数的反导子 )**

设 ![A=\oplus_{k=0}^\infty A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+A%5Ek) 是域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 上的**分级代数**（graded algebra）. 则 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的**反导子**(antiderivation)是一个 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) -线性映射 ![D:A\to A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3AA%5Cto+A) 满足：对任意 ![a\in A^k,b\in A^l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+A%5Ek%2Cb%5Cin+A%5El) ,有 ![D(ab)=(Da)b+(-1)^kaDb](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28ab%29%3D%28Da%29b%2B%28-1%29%5EkaDb)

如果存在整数 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) ,使得对任意的 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) ,反导子![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 将 ![A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Ek) 映到 ![A^{k+m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7Bk%2Bm%7D) ,则称 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 的阶（degree）为 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) , 或称 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 是一个 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 阶的反导子。

> 注1： ![A=\oplus_{k=0}^\infty A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+A%5Ek) 中 ![A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Ek) 的 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 代表"级数"，也就是说 ![A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Ek) 是由所有"k级"元素组成的集合，这是一个抽象的概念，具体的"级"的定义要看研究的背景。举个具体的例子，k重**交错**线性函数空间 ![A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_k%28V%29) ，这里的**重数k**就是分级代数 ![A_*(V)=\oplus_{k=0}^n A_k(V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%2A%28V%29%3D%5Coplus_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5En+A_k%28V%29) 的"级数"。
注2：一个m阶反导子,为何叫m阶？就是被m阶反导子作用后升了m级（将 ![A^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Ek) 映到 ![A^{k+m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7Bk%2Bm%7D)）

**命题4.1**

(i) 外微分(算子) ![d:\Omega^*(U)\to\Omega^*(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%3A%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29%5Cto%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29) 是一个 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 阶的反导子，即：

**命题4.2 （外微分/外导数的特征）**

命题4.1的三个性质唯一确定了 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的外微分(外导数）;即：如果映射 ![D:\Omega^*(U)\to\Omega^*(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3A%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29%5Cto%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29) 满足：(i) ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 是一个1阶的反导子(ii) ![D^2=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%5E2%3D0) (iii) 对任意 ![f\in C^\infty(U),X\in \mathfrak{X}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29%2CX%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7BX%7D%28U%29)有 ![(Df)(X)=Xf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Df%29%28X%29%3DXf) ，那么 ![D=d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3Dd)

> ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 是作用于k-形式的映射，因此要证明 ![D=d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3Dd) ，只需证明它们作用于同一k-形式的结果相等即可。此外，由于 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 和 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) 的作用是线性的，只需验证作用于 ![f \ dx^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f+%5C+dx%5EI) 即可。
根据(iii) ![(Df)(X)=Xf=(df)(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Df%29%28X%29%3DXf%3D%28df%29%28X%29) ,因此 ![Df=df](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Df%3Ddf) ;
根据(ii)和(iii) ![Ddx^i=DDx^i=D^2x^i=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Ddx%5Ei%3DDDx%5Ei%3DD%5E2x%5Ei%3D0) ,根据 ![Ddx^i=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Ddx%5Ei%3D0) 和(i) ![D(dx^i\wedge dx^j)=(Ddx^i)\wedge dx^j+(-1)dx^i\wedge Dd x^j=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28dx%5Ei%5Cwedge+dx%5Ej%29%3D%28Ddx%5Ei%29%5Cwedge+dx%5Ej%2B%28-1%29dx%5Ei%5Cwedge+Dd+x%5Ej%3D0)
同理递推下去证明 ![D(dx^I)=D(dx^{i_1}\wedge\cdots\wedge dx^{i_k})=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28dx%5EI%29%3DD%28dx%5E%7Bi_1%7D%5Cwedge%5Ccdots%5Cwedge+dx%5E%7Bi_k%7D%29%3D0) .
最后，对任意的k-形式 ![f \ dx^I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f+%5C+dx%5EI) ，
![\begin{align} D(f\ dx^I)&=(Df)\wedge dx^I+fD(dx^I)\quad(根据(\text{i}))\\ &=(df)\wedge dx^I\quad (根据Df=df和D(dx^I)=0)\\ &=d(fdx^I) \quad(根据d的定义) \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+D%28f%5C+dx%5EI%29%26%3D%28Df%29%5Cwedge+dx%5EI%2BfD%28dx%5EI%29%5Cquad%28%E6%A0%B9%E6%8D%AE%28%5Ctext%7Bi%7D%29%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%28df%29%5Cwedge+dx%5EI%5Cquad+%28%E6%A0%B9%E6%8D%AEDf%3Ddf%E5%92%8CD%28dx%5EI%29%3D0%29%5C%5C+%26%3Dd%28fdx%5EI%29+%5Cquad%28%E6%A0%B9%E6%8D%AEd%E7%9A%84%E5%AE%9A%E4%B9%89%29+%5Cend%7Balign%7D)
因此 ![D=d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3Dd)

**Section 5 闭形式和恰当形式（Closed form and Exact form）**

**定义5.2 （闭形式和恰当形式）**

![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的**k-**形式 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 称为是**闭形式**（closed form），当且仅当 ![d\ \omega=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5C+%5Comega%3D0) ;

![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的**k-**形式 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 称为是**恰当形式**（exact formm），当且仅当，存在**(k-1)-**形式 ![\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau) ，使得![\omega=d\tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3Dd%5Ctau) ;

> 注：闭形式也就是外微分 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) 的**核**(kernel)里的一个元素，恰当形式是 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) 的**像**(image)里的一个元素

**例子5.1 （带洞的平面上的闭1-形式）**

定义 ![\mathbb R^2\backslash\{0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb+R%5E2%5Cbackslash%5C%7B0%5C%7D)上的 **1-**形式 ![\omega=\frac{1}{x^2+y^2}(-y\ dx+x\ dy)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%5E2%2By%5E2%7D%28-y%5C+dx%2Bx%5C+dy%29) ,证明 ![\omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega) 是闭形式.

> 直接计算 ![d\ \omega=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5C+%5Comega%3D0) 即可

根据 ![d^2=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5E2%3D0) ,可知对于恰当形式 ![\omega=d \tau](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3Dd+%5Ctau) ,有 ![d\ \omega=d(d\ \tau)=d^2\tau=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5C+%5Comega%3Dd%28d%5C+%5Ctau%29%3Dd%5E2%5Ctau%3D0) .

**因此每一个恰当形式都是闭形式。**

更一般的，设![\{V^k\}^\infty_{k=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BV%5Ek%5C%7D%5E%5Cinfty_%7Bk%3D0%7D)为向量空间组成的集合,如果在![\{V^k\}^\infty_{k=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BV%5Ek%5C%7D%5E%5Cinfty_%7Bk%3D0%7D)上定义的线性映射 ![d_k:V^k\to V^{k+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d_k%3AV%5Ek%5Cto+V%5E%7Bk%2B1%7D) 满足 ![d_{k+1}\circ d_k=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d_%7Bk%2B1%7D%5Ccirc+d_k%3D0) ,则称![\{V^k\}^\infty_{k=0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BV%5Ek%5C%7D%5E%5Cinfty_%7Bk%3D0%7D)是一个**微分复形**(differential complex)或**上链复形**(cochain complex)**. **按照这样的定义，对 ![\mathbb R^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb+R%5En) 上任一开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) ，外微分 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) 使得向量空间 ![\Omega^*(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%5E%2A%28U%29) 成一个上链复形，也叫 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 的**de Rham复形**(de Rham complex of U):   **![0\ \mathop \to \limits^d \ \Omega^0(U)\ \mathop \to \limits^d \ \Omega^1(U)\ \mathop \to \limits^d \ \Omega^2(U)\ \mathop \to \limits^d \ \cdots](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5C+%5Cmathop+%5Cto+%5Climits%5Ed+%5C+%5COmega%5E0%28U%29%5C+%5Cmathop+%5Cto+%5Climits%5Ed+%5C+%5COmega%5E1%28U%29%5C+%5Cmathop+%5Cto+%5Climits%5Ed+%5C+%5COmega%5E2%28U%29%5C+%5Cmathop+%5Cto+%5Climits%5Ed+%5C+%5Ccdots)**

因此，闭形式也就是 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) 的核(kernel)里的一个元素，恰当形式是 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) 的像(image)里的一个元素

**Section 6 微分形式在向量计算的应用**

微分形式理论可以统一 ![\mathbb R^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb+R%5En) 里的一些定理。以下分析都 ![\mathbb R^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb+R%5En) 在进行。

![\mathbb R^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb+R%5E3) 里开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的**向量函数**(vector-valued function)是指一个映射 ![\textbf{F}=\langle P,Q,R\rangle:U\to \mathbb R^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextbf%7BF%7D%3D%5Clangle+P%2CQ%2CR%5Crangle%3AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb+R%5E3).函数 ![\textbf{F}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextbf%7BF%7D) 给 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 里的每一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 赋予了一个向量 ![\textbf{F}_p\in \mathbb R^3\simeq T_p(\mathbb R^3)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctextbf%7BF%7D_p%5Cin+%5Cmathbb+R%5E3%5Csimeq+T_p%28%5Cmathbb+R%5E3%29) . 因此 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的向量函数实际上就是 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的向量场。

![\text{div}\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P\\ Q\\ R \end{array}} \right]=\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\\ \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\\ \frac{\partial f}{\partial z} \end{array}} \right]\cdot \left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P\\ Q\\ R \end{array}} \right] =\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P_x\\ Q_y\\ R_z \end{array}} \right]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bdiv%7D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P%5C%5C+Q%5C%5C+R+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D%3D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D%5C%5C+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7D%5C%5C+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+z%7D+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D%5Ccdot+%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P%5C%5C+Q%5C%5C+R+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D+%3D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P_x%5C%5C+Q_y%5C%5C+R_z+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D)

因为 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的每一个**1-**形式是 ![dx,dy,dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dx%2Cdy%2Cdz) 和系数函数的线性组合，因此我们可以将**1-形式**和 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的向量场等同起来，即

![P\ dx+Q\ dy+R \ dz\ \leftrightarrow\ \left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P\\ Q\\ R \end{array}} \right]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5C+dx%2BQ%5C+dy%2BR+%5C+dz%5C+%5Cleftrightarrow%5C+%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P%5C%5C+Q%5C%5C+R+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D) .

同理，![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的**2-**形式也可以和 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的向量场等同起来：

![P\ dy\wedge dz+Q\ dz\wedge dx+R \ dx\wedge dy\ \leftrightarrow\ \left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P\\ Q\\ R \end{array}} \right]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5C+dy%5Cwedge+dz%2BQ%5C+dz%5Cwedge+dx%2BR+%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dy%5C+%5Cleftrightarrow%5C+%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P%5C%5C+Q%5C%5C+R+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D)

![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U)上的**3-**形式可以和 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的函数等同起来：

![f\ dx\wedge dy\wedge dz \ \leftrightarrow\ f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dy%5Cwedge+dz+%5C+%5Cleftrightarrow%5C+f)

按照上面三个等同原则，

（i)** 0-**形式(光滑函数) ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的外微分就等同于 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的梯度：

![df=\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}dx+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}dy+\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}dz\ \longleftrightarrow\ \left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} f_x\\ f_y\\ f_z \end{array}} \right]=\text{grad}\ f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7Ddx%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7Ddy%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+z%7Ddz%5C+%5Clongleftrightarrow%5C+%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+f_x%5C%5C+f_y%5C%5C+f_z+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D%3D%5Ctext%7Bgrad%7D%5C+f)

（ii）而**1-**形式的外微分是：

![d(P\ dx+Q\ dy+R\ dz)=(R_y-Q_z)dy\wedge dz-(R_x-P_z)dz\wedge dx+(Q_x-P_y)dx\wedge dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28P%5C+dx%2BQ%5C+dy%2BR%5C+dz%29%3D%28R_y-Q_z%29dy%5Cwedge+dz-%28R_x-P_z%29dz%5Cwedge+dx%2B%28Q_x-P_y%29dx%5Cwedge+dz)

它等同于 ![\text{curl}\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P\\ Q\\ R \end{array}} \right] =\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} R_y-Q_z\\ -(R_x-P_z)\\ Q_x-P_y \end{array}} \right]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bcurl%7D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P%5C%5C+Q%5C%5C+R+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D+%3D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+R_y-Q_z%5C%5C+-%28R_x-P_z%29%5C%5C+Q_x-P_y+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D)

(iii)**2-**形式的外微分是： ![d(P\ dy\wedge dz+Q\ dz\wedge dx+R \ dx\wedge dy)=(P_x+Q_y+R_z)\ dx\wedge dy \wedge dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28P%5C+dy%5Cwedge+dz%2BQ%5C+dz%5Cwedge+dx%2BR+%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dy%29%3D%28P_x%2BQ_y%2BR_z%29%5C+dx%5Cwedge+dy+%5Cwedge+dz)

它等同于 ![\text{div}\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P\\ Q\\ R \end{array}} \right] =\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P_x\\ Q_y\\ R_z \end{array}} \right]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bdiv%7D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P%5C%5C+Q%5C%5C+R+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D+%3D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P_x%5C%5C+Q_y%5C%5C+R_z+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D)

综上，0-形式、1-形式 、2-形式的外微分 ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) 分别对应于三个算子 ![\text{grad}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bgrad%7D) 、 ![\text{curl}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bcurl%7D) 、 ![\text{div}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bdiv%7D) ，如下面交换图所示：

![\begin{CD} \Omega^0(U) @>d>>\Omega^1(U) @>d>>\Omega^2(U) @>d>>\Omega^3(U) \\ @V\simeq VV@V\simeq VV@V\simeq VV@V\simeq VV\\ C^\infty(U)@>\text{grad}>>\mathfrak X(U)@>\text{curl}>>\mathfrak X(U)@>\text{div}>>C^\infty(U) \end{CD}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7BCD%7D+%5COmega%5E0%28U%29+%40%3Ed%3E%3E%5COmega%5E1%28U%29+%40%3Ed%3E%3E%5COmega%5E2%28U%29+%40%3Ed%3E%3E%5COmega%5E3%28U%29+%5C%5C+%40V%5Csimeq+VV%40V%5Csimeq+VV%40V%5Csimeq+VV%40V%5Csimeq+VV%5C%5C+C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29%40%3E%5Ctext%7Bgrad%7D%3E%3E%5Cmathfrak+X%28U%29%40%3E%5Ctext%7Bcurl%7D%3E%3E%5Cmathfrak+X%28U%29%40%3E%5Ctext%7Bdiv%7D%3E%3EC%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29+%5Cend%7BCD%7D)

在此等同之下，![\mathbb R^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb+R%5E3) 上的向量场 ![\langle P,Q,R\rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clangle+P%2CQ%2CR%5Crangle) 成为某个光滑函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的梯度,当且仅当![\langle P,Q,R\rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clangle+P%2CQ%2CR%5Crangle)对应的1-形式 ![P\ dx+Q\ dy+R\ dz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5C+dx%2BQ%5C+dy%2BR%5C+dz) 等于 ![df](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df).

接下来，证明有关梯度(grad)、旋度(curl)和散度(div)的恒等式。

**命题6.1 **

（A）梯度的旋度为零，即 ![\text{curl}(\text{grad}f)=\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} 0\\ 0\\ 0 \end{array}} \right]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bcurl%7D%28%5Ctext%7Bgrad%7Df%29%3D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+0%5C%5C+0%5C%5C+0+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D)

（B）旋度的散度为零，即 ![\text{div}\bigg(\text{curl}\left[ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} P\\ Q\\ R \end{array}} \right]\bigg)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bdiv%7D%5Cbigg%28%5Ctext%7Bcurl%7D%5Cleft%5B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+P%5C%5C+Q%5C%5C+R+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%5D%5Cbigg%29%3D0)

> 命题A和B来源于 ![\mathbb R^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb+R%5En) 开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上外微分的性质 ![d^2=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5E2%3D0)

**命题 6.2**
