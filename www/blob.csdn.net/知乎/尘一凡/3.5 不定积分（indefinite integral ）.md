**3.5 不定积分（indefinite integral ）**

**Section 1 不定积分（indefinite integral ）**

这一节以后的内容会用到，比如控制收敛定理的证明等等。

**定义1 （绝对连续 absolutely continuous）**

在测度空间 ![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29)中， **取实值**的 ![\color{green}{集合函数}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B%E9%9B%86%E5%90%88%E5%87%BD%E6%95%B0%7D)![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 被称作**绝对连续的**（absolutely continuous），如果对任意 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ,存在 ![\delta>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3E0) ,使得对任意可测子集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ，当 ![\mu(E)<\delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cdelta)时，有![|\lambda(E)|<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E%29%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon) 。

此时也称， ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda)**相对于**![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是绝对连续的（ ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) is absolutely continuous with respect to ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) ）,符号记做 ![\lambda \text{ abso. conti. w.r.t } \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda+%5Ctext%7B+abso.+conti.+w.r.t+%7D+%5Cmu) 。

**定义2 （不定积分indefinite integral****）**

在测度空间 ![(X,\mathfrak{a},\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C%5Cmu%29)中，设 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是可积函数，定义集合函数 ![\lambda:\mathfrak{a}\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%3A%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 为：对任意可测子集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ,![\lambda(E)=\int_E f\ d \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28E%29%3D%5Cint_E+f%5C+d+%5Cmu) ，则称 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的不定积分（indefinite integral of  ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ）

**定理1 （不定积分是有限的signed measure且是绝对连续的）**

定义2中的不定积分（也是集合函数） ![\lambda(E)=\int_E f\ d \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28E%29%3D%5Cint_E+f%5C+d+%5Cmu) 是：

(1) ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是有限的signed measure；

(2) ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda)**相对于**![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是绝对连续的。

> (1)证 是有限的signed measure：
(i) 先证明 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是signed measure: (a)验证 ![\lambda(\varnothing)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0) ;（b）countably addictive;
(a) ![\lambda(\varnothing)=\int_\varnothing f\ d \mu=\int0=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D%5Cint_%5Cvarnothing+f%5C+d+%5Cmu%3D%5Cint0%3D0) ;
(b) 由[3.2节S1引理1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37171224)，**集函数**![\nu(E)=\lim_{n\to\infty}\int_E f_n\ d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu%28E%29%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint_E+f_n%5C+d%5Cmu)是[countably addictive](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/35850693)，而由[积分的定义](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37171224)， ![\int_E f\ d \mu=\lim_{n\to\infty}\int_E f_n\ d\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_E+f%5C+d+%5Cmu%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint_E+f_n%5C+d%5Cmu) 。因此 ![\lambda(E)=\int_E f\ d \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28E%29%3D%5Cint_E+f%5C+d+%5Cmu) 是countably addictive。
综合(a)(b)可知![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是signed measure。

(ii)证明 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是有限的：
(*思路分析：虽然有 *![\lambda(E)=\int f\ d \mu<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28E%29%3D%5Cint+f%5C+d+%5Cmu%3C%5Cinfty)* ，但这不能说 *![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda)* 是finite的，这是因为 *![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda)* 是一个signed measure。关于带号测度有限的定义，回忆[1.6节S3定义4](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36652587)，*![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda)* 是finite的当且仅当 *![|\lambda|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%7C)* 是finite。因此我们要证*![|\lambda|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%7C)* 是finite。而 *![|\lambda|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%7C)* 的定义为*![|\lambda|=\lambda^++\lambda^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%7C%3D%5Clambda%5E%2B%2B%5Clambda%5E-)* ，而 *![\lambda^+,\lambda^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%2B%2C%5Clambda%5E-)* 是 *![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda)* 的Jordan分解，而要进行Jordan分解之前，我们需要对空间 *![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X)* 进行Hahn分解，分拆成正集 *![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A)* 和负集 *![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B)* 。我们再思考，这里正集 *![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A)* 是什么？在这里，带号测度是求可测集 *![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E)* 的积分，而正集要求所有正集的子集的带号测度是非负的，所以在这里，正集 *![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A)* 必需满足所有子集的积分是非负的，因此，只能正集 *![A=\{x\in X:f(x)\geq0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+X%3Af%28x%29%5Cgeq0%5C%7D)* ;同理，负集 *![B=\{x\in X:f(x)\leq0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+X%3Af%28x%29%5Cleq0%5C%7D)* 。思考到这里就完了，接下来就是逆着思维路线写出证明*)
（[关于正集、负集、空间的Hahn分解、测度的Jordan分解，见1.6节](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/36652587)）
定义集合 ![A=\{x\in X:f(x)\geq0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+X%3Af%28x%29%5Cgeq0%5C%7D) ， ![B=\{x\in X:f(x)\leq0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+X%3Af%28x%29%5Cleq0%5C%7D) 。
则对 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的Hahn分解为：![A,B \in \mathfrak{a}, \ X=A\cup B \ \&\ A\cap B=\varnothing](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB+%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%2C+%5C+X%3DA%5Ccup+B+%5C+%5C%26%5C+A%5Ccap+B%3D%5Cvarnothing) ，并且 ![A\geq0,B\leq0,\text{with respect to } \lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cgeq0%2CB%5Cleq0%2C%5Ctext%7Bwith+respect+to+%7D+%5Clambda) 。
对 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是Jordan分解为： ![\lambda^+(E)=\lambda(E\cap A)=\int_{E\cap A}f<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%2B%28E%29%3D%5Clambda%28E%5Ccap+A%29%3D%5Cint_%7BE%5Ccap+A%7Df%3C%5Cinfty) , ![\lambda^-(E)=-\lambda(E\cap B)=-\int_{E\cap B}f<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E-%28E%29%3D-%5Clambda%28E%5Ccap+B%29%3D-%5Cint_%7BE%5Ccap+B%7Df%3C%5Cinfty) 。而 ![|\lambda|(E)=\lambda^+(E)+\lambda^-(E)=\int_{E\cap A}f-\int_{E\cap B}f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%7C%28E%29%3D%5Clambda%5E%2B%28E%29%2B%5Clambda%5E-%28E%29%3D%5Cint_%7BE%5Ccap+A%7Df-%5Cint_%7BE%5Ccap+B%7Df) 。因此 ![|\lambda|(E)<\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%7C%28E%29%3C%5Cinfty) ,即 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 是有限的。

(2)证 ![\lambda \ll\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda+%5Cll%5Cmu) ，即是![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda)**相对于**![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 是绝对连续的。
(*思路分析： *![|\lambda(E)|=|\int_Ef|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E%29%7C%3D%7C%5Cint_Ef%7C)* 表示的是"围成的面积"， *![\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29)* 表示的是" *![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E)* 的长度"。若 *![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f)* 可积，直观来看长度*![\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29)*足够小，面积 *![|\int_Ef|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cint_Ef%7C)* 也能足够小；而面积 *![|\int_Ef|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cint_Ef%7C)* 可用简单函数的面积 *![|\int_Ef_n|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cint_Ef_n%7C)* 来近似，而简单函数的面积不超过 *![c\cdot\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%5Ccdot%5Cmu%28E%29)* （*![|f_n|\leq c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cf_n%7C%5Cleq+c)*）。因此 *![\mu(E)\rightarrow0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%5Crightarrow0)* 时有 *![|\int_Ef|\rightarrow0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cint_Ef%7C%5Crightarrow0) ）
因为 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 可积，故由可积定义知，存在简单可积函数序列 ![\{f_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bf_n%5C%7D) 满足：Cauchy in mean 且 ![f_n \to f \text{ a.e.}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+a.e.%7D) 。且由[3.4节S1引理1](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37274703)，有 ![f_n \to f \text{ in mean}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n+%5Cto+f+%5Ctext%7B+in+mean%7D) ，即 ![\int |f_n-f|\to0,n\to\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint+%7Cf_n-f%7C%5Cto0%2Cn%5Cto%5Cinfty) 。
![|\lambda(E)|=|\int_Ef|\leq\underbrace{|\int_E f-\int_Ef_n|}_{第一项}+\underbrace{|\int_Ef_n|}_{第二项}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E%29%7C%3D%7C%5Cint_Ef%7C%5Cleq%5Cunderbrace%7B%7C%5Cint_E+f-%5Cint_Ef_n%7C%7D_%7B%E7%AC%AC%E4%B8%80%E9%A1%B9%7D%2B%5Cunderbrace%7B%7C%5Cint_Ef_n%7C%7D_%7B%E7%AC%AC%E4%BA%8C%E9%A1%B9%7D) 。
第一项可以任意小： ![|\int_E f-\int_Ef_n|\leq\int|f_n-f|<\frac\varepsilon2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cint_E+f-%5Cint_Ef_n%7C%5Cleq%5Cint%7Cf_n-f%7C%3C%5Cfrac%5Cvarepsilon2)
第二项不超过 ![c\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%5Cmu%28E%29) :因为 ![f_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n) simple,故 ![\exists c\in\mathbb{R},|f_n|\leq c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+c%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2C%7Cf_n%7C%5Cleq+c) 。那么 ![|\int_Ef_n|\leq\int_Ec=c\mu(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cint_Ef_n%7C%5Cleq%5Cint_Ec%3Dc%5Cmu%28E%29) 。
因此，若要![|\lambda(E)<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon) ，可令 ![|\lambda(E)\leq\frac\varepsilon2+c\mu(E)<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E%29%5Cleq%5Cfrac%5Cvarepsilon2%2Bc%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon) 。
那么我们只需取 ![\delta=\frac\varepsilon2\cdot\frac1c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3D%5Cfrac%5Cvarepsilon2%5Ccdot%5Cfrac1c) ，就有 ![\mu(E)<\delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cdelta) 时，就有 ![|\lambda(E)<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E%29%3C%5Cvarepsilon) 。

**推论1 （不定积分是连续的）**

![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 为可积函数， ![\{E_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BE_n%5C%7D) 是可测集合序列。如果 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}E_n=E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7DE_n%3DE) 且 ![\mu(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3D0) ,那么 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int_{E_n}f \ d\mu=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint_%7BE_n%7Df+%5C+d%5Cmu%3D0) 。

> 对任意可测子集 ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) ,令集合函数![\lambda(F)=\int_F f\ d \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28F%29%3D%5Cint_F+f%5C+d+%5Cmu) 。那么由定理1， ![\lambda(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28F%29) 是有限带号测度。且 ![\lambda(F)=\lambda^+(F)-\lambda^-(F)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28F%29%3D%5Clambda%5E%2B%28F%29-%5Clambda%5E-%28F%29) ,其中 ![\lambda^+,\lambda^-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%2B%2C%5Clambda%5E-) 都是**有限（正）测度**。
因为 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}E_n=E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7DE_n%3DE) ，由[1.2节S1测度性质(9)](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/35850693),得 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\lambda^+(E_n)=\lambda^+(\lim_{n\to\infty}E_n)=\lambda^+(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%5E%2B%28E_n%29%3D%5Clambda%5E%2B%28%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7DE_n%29%3D%5Clambda%5E%2B%28E%29) ; ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\lambda^-(E_n)=\lambda^-(\lim_{n\to\infty}E_n)=\lambda^-(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%5E-%28E_n%29%3D%5Clambda%5E-%28%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7DE_n%29%3D%5Clambda%5E-%28E%29) 。
故 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\lambda(E_n)=\lim_{n\to\infty}\lambda^+(E_n)+\lim_{n\to\infty}\lambda^-(E_n)=\lambda^+(E)+\lambda^-(E)=\lambda(E)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%28E_n%29%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%5E%2B%28E_n%29%2B%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%5E-%28E_n%29%3D%5Clambda%5E%2B%28E%29%2B%5Clambda%5E-%28E%29%3D%5Clambda%28E%29)
又因为![\mu(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3D0)，由[3.3节S3定理4](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/37233441)，有 ![ \int_E f=0 ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cint_E+f%3D0+) ，即 ![\lambda(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28E%29%3D0) 。
因此 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int_{E_n}f \ d\mu=\lim_{n\to\infty}\lambda(E_n)=\lambda(E)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint_%7BE_n%7Df+%5C+d%5Cmu%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%28E_n%29%3D%5Clambda%28E%29%3D0) 。

**推论2**

![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 为可积函数， ![\{E_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BE_n%5C%7D) 是可测集合序列。如果 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\mu(E_n)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29%3D0) ,那么 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int_{E_n}f \ d\mu=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint_%7BE_n%7Df+%5C+d%5Cmu%3D0) 。

> 对任意可测子集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ,令集合函数![\lambda(E)=\int_E f\ d \mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28E%29%3D%5Cint_E+f%5C+d+%5Cmu) ,由定理1， ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 相对于 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 绝对连续。
由绝对连续的定义，即是：如果对任意 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ,存在 ![\delta>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3E0) ,使得对任意可测子集 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) ，当 ![\mu(E)<\delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E%29%3C%5Cdelta)时，有![|\lambda(E)|<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E%29%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon) 。

我们取任意 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) 。因为 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\mu(E_n)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%28E_n%29%3D0) ，故对任意 ![\delta>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta%3E0) ,存在 ![N\in \mathbb{Z}^+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%2B) ,使得 ![n\geq N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cgeq+N) 时有 ![\mu(E_n)<\delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28E_n%29%3C%5Cdelta) ，此时就有 ![|\lambda(E_n)|<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Clambda%28E_n%29%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon) 。即是 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}|\lambda(E_n)-0|=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%7C%5Clambda%28E_n%29-0%7C%3D0) ,即 ![\lim_{n\to\infty}\int_{E_n}f \ d\mu=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cint_%7BE_n%7Df+%5C+d%5Cmu%3D0)
