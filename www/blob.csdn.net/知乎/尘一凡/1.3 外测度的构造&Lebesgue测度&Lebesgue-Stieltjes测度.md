**Section 1 外测度的抽象构造**

设全空间 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) ，集族 ![K\subseteq \mathscr{P}(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29)

**定义1 （Sequential covering class)**

我们称集族 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 是 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的一个**sequential covering class**，如果满足: ![\begin{align*} &(1)\varnothing \in K\\ &(2)\forall A\subseteq X,\ \exists\{E_n\}\subseteq K\ \ni \ A\subseteq \cup_{n=1}^{\infty} E_n \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%281%29%5Cvarnothing+%5Cin+K%5C%5C+%26%282%29%5Cforall+A%5Csubseteq+X%2C%5C+%5Cexists%5C%7BE_n%5C%7D%5Csubseteq+K%5C+%5Cni+%5C+A%5Csubseteq+%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+E_n+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

> 例如，取全空间![X=\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，则 ![K=\{\text{open intervals}\}\cup\{\varnothing\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%3D%5C%7B%5Ctext%7Bopen+intervals%7D%5C%7D%5Ccup%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%5C%7D) 或者 ![K=\{\text{closed intervals}\}\cup\{\varnothing\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%3D%5C%7B%5Ctext%7Bclosed+intervals%7D%5C%7D%5Ccup%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%5C%7D) 都是sequential covering class。

定义集合函数 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 满足： ![\lambda：K\rightarrow[0,\infty] \ \&\ \lambda(\varnothing)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%EF%BC%9AK%5Crightarrow%5B0%2C%5Cinfty%5D+%5C+%5C%26%5C+%5Clambda%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0)

> 即 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 定义域为 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) ，取值为非负的广义实数，且在空集取值为0。

给定了上述的函数 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 后，我们对每一个 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 的子集，定义外测度如下： ![\forall A \in \mathscr{P}(X),\mu^*(A)=\inf \ \{\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{ \lambda(E_n)} ;A\subseteq\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n,E_n\in K,\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+A+%5Cin+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29%2C%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cinf+%5C+%5C%7B%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B+%5Clambda%28E_n%29%7D+%3BA%5Csubseteq%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%2CE_n%5Cin+K%2C%5C%7D) （式3-1）

总结为如下定理

**定理1 **For any sequential covering class ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) and for any non-negative, extended real-valued set function ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) with domain  ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) and with ![\lambda(\varnothing)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0) ,the set function ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) defined by (式3-1) is an outer measure.

> Pf: (1) ![\mu^*(\varnothing)=0;](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%3B)
(2)if ![A\subseteq B,\mu^*(A)\leq\mu^*(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq+B%2C%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29) 。
原因：定义，B的覆盖集={B的所有覆盖}，A的覆盖集={A的所有覆盖}。若B包含A，则B的覆盖也是A的覆盖，即B的覆盖集是A的覆盖集的子集（或者说B的覆盖集包含于A的覆盖集）。因为A的覆盖集 ![\supseteq](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csupseteq) B的覆盖集，因此 ![\mu^*(A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29) 能取到不小于 ![\mu^*(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29) 的下界，即 ![\mu^*(A)\leq\mu^*(B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28B%29) 。
（3）验证countable subadditivity，即 ![\mu^*(\cup_{n=1}^{\infty}A_n)\leq\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu^*(A_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28%5Ccup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DA_n%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A_n%29)
设 ![A_n \subseteq \mathscr{P}(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_n+%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29) , 因为 ![\mu^*(A_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A_n%29) 为**下确界**，因此对于 ![\mu^*(A_n)+\varepsilon/2^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A_n%29%2B%5Cvarepsilon%2F2%5En) ( ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) )，存在 ![A_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_n) 的一个覆盖，写成数学语言就是： ![\forall \varepsilon>0,\exists{E_k^{(n)}}\subseteq K \ \ni\ A_n\subseteq \cup_{k=1}^{\infty}E_k^{(n)} \&\sum_{k=1}^{\infty}\lambda(E_k^{(n)} )\leq\mu^*(A_n)+\varepsilon/2^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%2C%5Cexists%7BE_k%5E%7B%28n%29%7D%7D%5Csubseteq+K+%5C+%5Cni%5C+A_n%5Csubseteq+%5Ccup_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_k%5E%7B%28n%29%7D+%5C%26%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Clambda%28E_k%5E%7B%28n%29%7D+%29%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A_n%29%2B%5Cvarepsilon%2F2%5En)
又因为 ![\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}A_n\subseteq\bigcup_{n,k=1}^{\infty} E_k^{(n)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DA_n%5Csubseteq%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%2Ck%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+E_k%5E%7B%28n%29%7D)
则 ![\mu^*(\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}A_n)\leq \sum_{n,k=1}^{\infty} \lambda(E_k^{(n)})\leq \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(\mu^*(A_n)+\varepsilon/2^n)\leq \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu^*(A_n)+\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DA_n%29%5Cleq+%5Csum_%7Bn%2Ck%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+%5Clambda%28E_k%5E%7B%28n%29%7D%29%5Cleq+%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%28%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A_n%29%2B%5Cvarepsilon%2F2%5En%29%5Cleq+%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A_n%29%2B%5Cvarepsilon)
令 ![\varepsilon \to0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon+%5Cto0) ,则可得 ![\mu^*(\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}A_n)\leq\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\mu^*(A_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DA_n%29%5Cleq%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A_n%29)

**例子 **

设 ![K=\{\varnothing,X,\text{singletons}\};](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%3D%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%2CX%2C%5Ctext%7Bsingletons%7D%5C%7D%3B)![\lambda(\varnothing)=0,\lambda(X)=\#X,\lambda(E)=1\ \text{if} \ E \ \text{singleton}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%2C%5Clambda%28X%29%3D%5C%23X%2C%5Clambda%28E%29%3D1%5C+%5Ctext%7Bif%7D+%5C+E+%5C+%5Ctext%7Bsingleton%7D) 。

则 ![\mu^*(A)=\#A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5C%23A) 是外测度， ![\mathfrak{a}=\mathscr{P}(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%3D%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29) 为 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数。故 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 限制在 ![\mathscr{P}(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28X%29) 上就是测度，这个测度叫做counting measure。

**Section 2  ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 上的Lebesgue外测度 &Lebesgue测度**

（这一小节讲述 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 上最常用的测度）（将上一小节抽象的外测度构造落实到具体的 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 空间上来）

设 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 为 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维的欧几里得空间， ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 里的点表示为 ![x=(x_1,x_2,...,x_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D%28x_1%2Cx_2%2C...%2Cx_n%29) 。 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 里的开区间（open interval）定义为 ![I_{a,b}=\{x=(x_1,x_2,...,x_n):a_i<x_i<b_i, \text{for} \ i=1,2,...n \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_%7Ba%2Cb%7D%3D%5C%7Bx%3D%28x_1%2Cx_2%2C...%2Cx_n%29%3Aa_i%3Cx_i%3Cb_i%2C+%5Ctext%7Bfor%7D+%5C+i%3D1%2C2%2C...n+%5C%7D) ( ![a=(a_1,...,a_n),b=(b_1,...,b_n) \in R^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3D%28a_1%2C...%2Ca_n%29%2Cb%3D%28b_1%2C...%2Cb_n%29+%5Cin+R%5En) )

![K=\{\text{open intervals in }R^n\} \cup \{\varnothing\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%3D%5C%7B%5Ctext%7Bopen+intervals+in+%7DR%5En%5C%7D+%5Ccup+%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%5C%7D) ， ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 就是sequential covering class。

我们构造函数![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) ，使之满足：

![\begin{align*} &(1) \ \lambda(\varnothing)=0\\ &(2) \ \lambda(I_{a,b})=\prod_{i=1}^{n}(b_i-a_i), \text{if} \ a_i\ne b_i \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%281%29+%5C+%5Clambda%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%5C%5C+%26%282%29+%5C+%5Clambda%28I_%7Ba%2Cb%7D%29%3D%5Cprod_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%28b_i-a_i%29%2C+%5Ctext%7Bif%7D+%5C+a_i%5Cne+b_i+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

有了上述的 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 和 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) ，那么由Section 1的定理1，定义 ![R^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%5En)上的**Lebesgue 外测度**（

Lebesgue outer measure）![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 为：![\forall A \in \mathscr{P}(R^n),\mu^*(A)=\inf \ \{\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{ \lambda(E_n)} ;A\subseteq\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n,E_n\in K,\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+A+%5Cin+%5Cmathscr%7BP%7D%28R%5En%29%2C%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cinf+%5C+%5C%7B%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B+%5Clambda%28E_n%29%7D+%3BA%5Csubseteq%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%2CE_n%5Cin+K%2C%5C%7D) 。那么将此 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 限制在 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 上，就得到测度 ![\mu=\mu^*|_{\mathfrak{a}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%3D%5Cmu%5E%2A%7C_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%7D) 。我们将此由Lebesgue外测度 ![\mu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A) 产生的测度定义为**Lebesgue测度**(Lebesgue measure)，且Lebesgue测度是**完备**(complete)的 (思考为什么)。此时，我们将可测集称为**Lebesgue可测**，或者Lebesgue集。

> 注1：上面外测度就是定义想法就是：先给一些开区间 ![E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_n) 指定一种“长度” ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 。对要测量的集合 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) ,我们找到一系列开区间去覆盖 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) ,即 ![A\subseteq\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Csubseteq%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n) 。对**每一种**覆盖方式，计算这些开区间的"总长度"，即是 ![\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{ \lambda(E_n)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B+%5Clambda%28E_n%29%7D) 。由于覆盖 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的方式有多种，算出来的"总长度"就有**多个值**，我们选最小的那一个值，作为 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的测度，即是 ![\text{inf}(\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{ \lambda(E_n)})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Binf%7D%28%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B+%5Clambda%28E_n%29%7D%29) 。这个理解对于下面的证明很重要，总之要理解**外测度是最小值**。

![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta) 注2：在上面还没有说清楚 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 是什么，因为需要拓扑结构，将在1.5节说明。现在结论如下：(i) Borel sets都是Lebesgue可测；(ii)Lebesgue可测集(即 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D))都是形如 ![B\cup N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%5Ccup+N) 的集合，其中 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 是Borel set， ![N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N) 是零测集，即 ![\mu(N)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28N%29%3D0) 。

![\Delta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta)![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上Lebesgue测度 ![\mu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu) 的一些结论

（1）单点集的测度为0，即 ![\mu(\{a\})=0,a\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5C%7Ba%5C%7D%29%3D0%2Ca%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)

> 取 ![\varepsilon>0,\{a\}\subseteq(a-\varepsilon,a+\varepsilon)\Rightarrow ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0%2C%5C%7Ba%5C%7D%5Csubseteq%28a-%5Cvarepsilon%2Ca%2B%5Cvarepsilon%29%5CRightarrow+)![\mu^*(A)=\inf \ \{\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{ \lambda(E_n)} ;A\subseteq\bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}E_n,E_n\in K,\}\leq \lambda((a-\varepsilon,a+\varepsilon))=2\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D%5Cinf+%5C+%5C%7B%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B+%5Clambda%28E_n%29%7D+%3BA%5Csubseteq%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7DE_n%2CE_n%5Cin+K%2C%5C%7D%5Cleq+%5Clambda%28%28a-%5Cvarepsilon%2Ca%2B%5Cvarepsilon%29%29%3D2%5Cvarepsilon)
即 ![0\leq\mu^*(A)\leq2\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleq%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%5Cleq2%5Cvarepsilon) 对一切 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) 恒成立，令 ![\varepsilon \to0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon+%5Cto0) ，即得 ![\mu^*(A)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A%28A%29%3D0)

（2）可数点集的测度为0，即 ![\mu(\{a_1,a_2,...\})=0,a_i\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5C%7Ba_1%2Ca_2%2C...%5C%7D%29%3D0%2Ca_i%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)

> 由Lebesgue测度的可数可加性即得。

（3）闭区间的测度等于其长度，即 ![\mu([a,b])=b-a,b>a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%3Db-a%2Cb%3Ea)

> （先复习一下
需要用到海涅-傅雷尔定理（Heine-Borel theorem）：设 ![E\subseteq \mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 中，则 ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是有界闭集 ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow)![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是紧集（compact）。所以， ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 是有界的闭集，所以是紧集的。由紧集的定义， ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的每个开覆盖都存在**有限**子覆盖。）

(i) 先证 ![ \mu([a,b])\leq b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%5Cleq+b-a)
因为对 ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0) ,开区间 ![(a-\varepsilon,b+\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a-%5Cvarepsilon%2Cb%2B%5Cvarepsilon%29) 是 ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的开覆盖，即是 ![[a,b]\subseteq (a-\varepsilon ,b+\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D%5Csubseteq+%28a-%5Cvarepsilon+%2Cb%2B%5Cvarepsilon%29),由Lebesgue测度的定义有 ![\mu([a,b])\leq \lambda (a-\varepsilon ,b+\varepsilon)=b-a+2\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%5Cleq+%5Clambda+%28a-%5Cvarepsilon+%2Cb%2B%5Cvarepsilon%29%3Db-a%2B2%5Cvarepsilon)
令 ![\varepsilon \to0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon+%5Cto0) ,即得 ![ \mu([a,b])\leq b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%5Cleq+b-a)

(ii)再证 ![ \mu([a,b])\geq b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%5Cgeq+b-a)
要证 ![ \mu([a,b])\geq b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%5Cgeq+b-a) ，等价于证：若 ![\{I_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_n%5C%7D_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D) 是 ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的任意开覆盖(即是![[a,b] \subseteq \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty} I_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D+%5Csubseteq+%5Cbigcup_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+I_n) )，则有 ![\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{ \lambda(I_n)} \geq b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B+%5Clambda%28I_n%29%7D+%5Cgeq+b-a) 。而 ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 是 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 上的有界闭集，故是紧集，故对于 ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的任意开覆盖 ![\{I_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_n%5C%7D_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D) ，存在**有限**的子覆盖 ![\{I_{n_k}\}_{k=1}^{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_%7Bn_k%7D%5C%7D_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D) 。**如果**能证明这个有限子覆盖 ![\{I_{n_k}\}_{k=1}^{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_%7Bn_k%7D%5C%7D_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D) 的总长度也大于等于 ![b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b-a) ，得到 ![\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{ \lambda(I_n)}\geq \sum_{k=1}^{N} \lambda( I_{n_k} )\geq b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B+%5Clambda%28I_n%29%7D%5Cgeq+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D+%5Clambda%28+I_%7Bn_k%7D+%29%5Cgeq+b-a) ,那么就完成了证明。

接下来证明，如果有限子覆盖 ![\{I_{n_k}\}_{k=1}^{N}\supseteq[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_%7Bn_k%7D%5C%7D_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D%5Csupseteq%5Ba%2Cb%5D) ，有 ![\sum_{k=1}^{N} \lambda( I_{n_k} )\geq b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D+%5Clambda%28+I_%7Bn_k%7D+%29%5Cgeq+b-a) 。
因为 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 落在 ![\{I_{n_k}\}_{k=1}^{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_%7Bn_k%7D%5C%7D_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D) ， ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 落在 ![I_{n_k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_%7Bn_k%7D) 的某个开区间，令这个区间为 ![(a_1,b_1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_1%2Cb_1%29) ,即 ![a_1<a<b_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_1%3Ca%3Cb_1) 。若 ![b_1\leq b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1%5Cleq+b) ,则 ![b_1 \in [a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1+%5Cin+%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 。但 ![b_1\notin(a_1,b_1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1%5Cnotin%28a_1%2Cb_1%29) ,故一定存在开区间 ![(a_2,b_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_2%2Cb_2%29) 使得 ![b_1\in (a_2,b_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1%5Cin+%28a_2%2Cb_2%29) ,即 ![a_2<b_1<b_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_2%3Cb_1%3Cb_2) 。重复上述步骤，得到 ![\{I_{n_k}\}_{k=1}^{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_%7Bn_k%7D%5C%7D_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D) 的一个开区间序列 ![(a_1,b_1),\cdots,(a_i,b_i),...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_1%2Cb_1%29%2C%5Ccdots%2C%28a_i%2Cb_i%29%2C...) 且有 ![a_i<b_{i-1}<b_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_i%3Cb_%7Bi-1%7D%3Cb_i) 。
而 ![\{I_{n_k}\}_{k=1}^{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_%7Bn_k%7D%5C%7D_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D) 是有限的，因此上述序列必终止于某个开区间，命名为 ![(a_N,b_N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_N%2Cb_N%29) ，且终止时有 ![b \in(a_N,b_N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b+%5Cin%28a_N%2Cb_N%29) ，即是 ![a_N<b<b_N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_N%3Cb%3Cb_N) （不然没法覆盖 ![b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b) ）。那么我们计算这个有限子覆盖的长度： ![\begin{align*} \sum_{k=1}^{N} \lambda(I_{n_k})&=\sum_{i=1}^{N}\lambda((a_i,b_i))\\ &=(b_N-a_N)+(b_{N-1}-a_{N-1})+\cdots+(b_1-a_1)\\ &=b_N-(a_N-b_{N-1})-\cdots-(a_2-b_1)-a_1\\ &>b_N-a_1>b-a \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D+%5Clambda%28I_%7Bn_k%7D%29%26%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7BN%7D%5Clambda%28%28a_i%2Cb_i%29%29%5C%5C+%26%3D%28b_N-a_N%29%2B%28b_%7BN-1%7D-a_%7BN-1%7D%29%2B%5Ccdots%2B%28b_1-a_1%29%5C%5C+%26%3Db_N-%28a_N-b_%7BN-1%7D%29-%5Ccdots-%28a_2-b_1%29-a_1%5C%5C+%26%3Eb_N-a_1%3Eb-a+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)
也就是说,对 ![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的任意开覆盖 ![\{I_n\}_{n=1}^{\infty}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BI_n%5C%7D_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D) ，有 ![\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{ \lambda(I_n)}>b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B+%5Clambda%28I_n%29%7D%3Eb-a) ，即 ![ \mu([a,b])> b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%3E+b-a) 。

综合(i)(ii)，即得 ![\mu([a,b])=b-a,b>a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%3Db-a%2Cb%3Ea)

（4） ![\mu((a,b))=\mu((a,b])=\mu([a,b))=b-a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%28%28a%2Cb%29%29%3D%5Cmu%28%28a%2Cb%5D%29%3D%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%29%29%3Db-a)

> ![[a,b]=[a,b)\cup\{b\}\Rightarrow \mu([a,b])= \mu([a,b))+ \mu(\{b\})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D%3D%5Ba%2Cb%29%5Ccup%5C%7Bb%5C%7D%5CRightarrow+%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%5D%29%3D+%5Cmu%28%5Ba%2Cb%29%29%2B+%5Cmu%28%5C%7Bb%5C%7D%29) (可加性)

**Section 3 Lebesgue-Stieltjes Measure**

Lebesgue-Stieltjes测度是 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的一种测度，构造如下:

设有函数 ![f:\mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3A%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D+%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ，单调递增且右连续。

![K=\{\text{open intervals} \ (a,b)\ \text{in}\ \mathbb{R} \}\cup\{\varnothing\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%3D%5C%7B%5Ctext%7Bopen+intervals%7D+%5C+%28a%2Cb%29%5C+%5Ctext%7Bin%7D%5C+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D+%5C%7D%5Ccup%5C%7B%5Cvarnothing%5C%7D) ，则 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 是sequential covering class。

定义函数![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) ，满足：

![\begin{align*} &(1) \ \lambda(\varnothing)=0\\ &(2) \ \lambda((a,b))=f(b)-f(a), (a,b) \text{ is open intervals} \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%281%29+%5C+%5Clambda%28%5Cvarnothing%29%3D0%5C%5C+%26%282%29+%5C+%5Clambda%28%28a%2Cb%29%29%3Df%28b%29-f%28a%29%2C+%28a%2Cb%29+%5Ctext%7B+is+open+intervals%7D+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

那么由上面 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 和 ![\lambda](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda) 决定的的外测度 ![\mu_f^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_f%5E%2A) 称为由函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 诱导出来的**Lebesgue-Stieltjes外测度。**同样将此 ![\mu_f^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_f%5E%2A) 限制在 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数 ![\mathfrak{a}_f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D_f) 上，就得到测度 ![\mu_f=\mu_f^*|_{\mathfrak{a_f}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_f%3D%5Cmu_f%5E%2A%7C_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Ba_f%7D%7D) 。将此由Lebesgue-Stieltjes外测度 ![\mu_f^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_f%5E%2A) 产生的测度 ![\mu_f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu_f) 定义为**Lebesgue-Stieltjes测度**(Lebesgue-Stieltjes measure)，且该测度也是**完备**(complete)的 

> 这一段**没有指出**![\mu^*_f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%5E%2A_f) 的计算方式，请思考怎么定义。同样上面还没有说清楚 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 代数 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 是什么，因为需要拓扑结构，将在以后说明。
