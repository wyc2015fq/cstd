**1.1 光滑函数、导数、切向量、和切空间**

第一章来重新审视欧几里得空间的一些对象。第一节接受审视的对象有光滑函数、导数、切向量、和切空间。

**Section 1 光滑函数**

![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 是 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 维欧几里得空间。为和微分几何的传统记法一致，坐标不用 ![x,y,z..](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%2Cy%2Cz..) 表示，而用 ![x^1,x^2,x^3,...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E1%2Cx%5E2%2Cx%5E3%2C...) （用上标表示）。那么点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的坐标表示就是 ![p=(p^1,p^2,...,p^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%3D%28p%5E1%2Cp%5E2%2C...%2Cp%5En%29) 。

**定义1.1 (![C^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5Ek)****实函数)**

设 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 是![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)的开集，![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 是非负整数。实函数 ![f:U\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 被称为在点 ![p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+U) 是 ![C^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5Ek) 函数，如果该函数所有 ![j (\leq k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j+%28%5Cleq+k%29) 阶偏导数**存在**并且**在****![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p)****点连续。**

特别的，如果任意阶偏导数**都存在且连续**，就称实函数 ![f:U\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 在点 ![p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+U) 是![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty)函数，也叫做光滑函数。 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 和光滑是同义词。

> 通俗讲，在某点处，k阶偏导存在且连续就是 ![C^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5Ek) ;无穷阶偏导存在且连续就是光滑。

**例子1.1**

1 开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的 ![C^0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E0) 函数就是连续函数。

2 函数 ![f(x)=|x|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D%7Cx%7C) 在点 ![x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0) 处是 ![C^0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E0) 但不是 ![C^1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E1) ，因为一阶导数不存在。

3 函数![f(x^1,x^2)=\sin(x_1+x_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%5E1%2Cx%5E2%29%3D%5Csin%28x_1%2Bx_2%29) 在所有点都是光滑的。

**定义1.2 (**![C^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5Ek)**向量函数)**

设 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 是![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)的开集，![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 是非负整数。向量函数 ![f:U\to \mathbb{R}^m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Em) 被称为在点 ![p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+U) 是 ![C^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5Ek)的，如果 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的所有分量 ![f^1,...,f^m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E1%2C...%2Cf%5Em) 都是 ![C^k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5Ek) 的。

同理，向量函数 ![f:U\to \mathbb{R}^m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Em) 被称为在点 ![p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+U) 是 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 的，如果 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的所有分量 ![f^1,...,f^m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E1%2C...%2Cf%5Em) 都是 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 的。

> 向量函数意思就是，f的取值是一个向量而不是实数。

**例子1.2**

1 向量函数 ![h(x^1,x^2,x^3)=\langle x^1\sin(x^2+x^3),x^1+x^3\rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%28x%5E1%2Cx%5E2%2Cx%5E3%29%3D%5Clangle+x%5E1%5Csin%28x%5E2%2Bx%5E3%29%2Cx%5E1%2Bx%5E3%5Crangle) 是![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty)。

> 注：今后用尖括号 ![\langle\ \ \rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clangle%5C+%5C+%5Crangle) 表示列向量，用圆括号 ![(\ \ )](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5C+%5C+%29) 表示点的坐标。

**定义1.3 （解析函数）**

[解析函数-维基百科](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E8%25A7%25A3%25E6%259E%2590%25E5%2587%25BD%25E6%2595%25B0)

> 函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在某点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处解析，要求：在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的**某个邻域****![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U)****内的所有点**![x\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+U) ， ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 可以用泰勒级数表示（也就是说，这个泰勒级数邻域![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U)是收敛的）。解析不等于光滑，解析强于光滑，光滑可能不解析，见下面例子。

**例子1.3 （在某点光滑但不解析）**

定义函数 ![f(x)= \begin{cases} e^{-1/x} & x>0\\ 0& x\leq0 \end{cases}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+e%5E%7B-1%2Fx%7D+%26+x%3E0%5C%5C+0%26+x%5Cleq0+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D) ,函数图像如下。
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-5b9feb1652a0f14f47e4258a368a636e_b.jpg)例3函数的图像，光滑但不解析
> 在点 ![x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0) 处，计算可知 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 各阶导数存在且连续，且都等于0,即： ![f(0)=f'(0)=f^{''}(x)=...=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%280%29%3Df%27%280%29%3Df%5E%7B%27%27%7D%28x%29%3D...%3D0) 。因此, ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在点 ![x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0) 处是光滑的。
接下来说明在0处不解析。若 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在点 ![x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0) 处解析，则存在 ![x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0) 的一个邻域 ![(-\varepsilon,\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28-%5Cvarepsilon%2C%5Cvarepsilon%29)( ![\varepsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0)),使得邻域内所有点 ![t \in (-\varepsilon,\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t+%5Cin+%28-%5Cvarepsilon%2C%5Cvarepsilon%29) ，有![f(t)=f(0)+f'(0)t+\frac{f''(0)}{2!}t^2+....](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28t%29%3Df%280%29%2Bf%27%280%29t%2B%5Cfrac%7Bf%27%27%280%29%7D%7B2%21%7Dt%5E2%2B....) 。而这是不成立的，因为取 ![0<t<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3Ct%3C%5Cvarepsilon) ,左边不等于0（函数定义知 ![f(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28t%29) 大于0），而右边等于0(因为各阶导数都是0)。

**引理1.1 （带积分余项的泰勒定理）**

设 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是定义在点 ![p=(p^1,...,p^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%3D%28p%5E1%2C...%2Cp%5En%29) 星型域 ![U\subseteq \mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%5Csubseteq+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 上的 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数。则对任意 ![x\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+U) ,存在函数 ![g_1,...,g_n\in C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_1%2C...%2Cg_n%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) ,使得 ![f(x)=f(p)+\sum_{i=1}^n(x^i-p^i)g_i(x),\quad g_i(p)=\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Df%28p%29%2B%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%28x%5Ei-p%5Ei%29g_i%28x%29%2C%5Cquad+g_i%28p%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29)
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-7c83e430730a79f88eb3ba648bd29a47_b.jpg)U是p的星型域，连接x和p直线段的任意一点都在U内
> 注1： ![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) 是在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 内每一点p都光滑的函数的集合，即 ![C^\infty(U):=\{f:f \text{ is } C^\infty \text{ at } p,\forall p\in U\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29%3A%3D%5C%7Bf%3Af+%5Ctext%7B+is+%7D+C%5E%5Cinfty+%5Ctext%7B+at+%7D+p%2C%5Cforall+p%5Cin+U%5C%7D) 。
注2： ![\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29) 表示函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 对 ![x^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Ei) 的偏导数。

证明：因为 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 是 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的星型域，任取 ![x\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+U) 。 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 和 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 之间的直线段任意一点，表示为 ![p+t(x-p),0\leq t\leq1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%2Bt%28x-p%29%2C0%5Cleq+t%5Cleq1) ，也落在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 内。故 ![f(p+t(x-p))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28p%2Bt%28x-p%29%29) 有定义。
根据链式法则， ![\frac{d}{dt}f(p+t(x-p))=\sum(x^i-p^i)\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}({p+t(x-p)})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdt%7Df%28p%2Bt%28x-p%29%29%3D%5Csum%28x%5Ei-p%5Ei%29%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28%7Bp%2Bt%28x-p%29%7D%29)

上式两边对 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 从0到1积分，得到
![f(p+t(x-p))|_0^1=\sum(x^i-p^i)\int_0^1\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}({p+t(x-p)})\ dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28p%2Bt%28x-p%29%29%7C_0%5E1%3D%5Csum%28x%5Ei-p%5Ei%29%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28%7Bp%2Bt%28x-p%29%7D%29%5C+dt)
令函数 ![g_i(x)=\int_0^1\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}({p+t(x-p)})\ dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_i%28x%29%3D%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28%7Bp%2Bt%28x-p%29%7D%29%5C+dt) ，
则有： ![f(x)-f(p)=\sum_{i=1}^n(x^i-p^i)g_i(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29-f%28p%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%28x%5Ei-p%5Ei%29g_i%28x%29) 。
并且 ![g_i(p)=\int_0^1\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}({p})\ dt=\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_i%28p%29%3D%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28%7Bp%7D%29%5C+dt%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29) 。证毕。

**Section 2 切向量与导数**

回忆一下微积分里，我们是怎么表示切向量的，以 ![\mathbb{R}^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E3) 曲面的切向量为例。

![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-fb7c2e3b88ce3cbcb6d653038a8f2b38_b.jpg)曲面在p点处的切平面和切向量
首先，取曲面三个点，过这三个点确定一个平面，叫做曲面的割平面。令这三个点在曲面上都趋于某个定点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) ，那么相应的割平面也会变化地趋于某个平面，这个极限平面就叫做曲面在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点的切平面。直观看起来，这个切平面与曲面只相交于一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 。曲面在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点处的切向量，就是位于切平面里，起点位于![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点的向量（如上图）。也就是说，二维曲面在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点处的切向量是三维欧式空间中的一个"箭头"，也对应于一个三维的列数组。

切向量的这种表示方法是有局限的。因为它假定曲面总是能嵌入到高维的欧式空间（如上面例子是二维的曲面嵌入到三维的欧式空间），那么遇到不能嵌入的例子，就不适用了。打个比方，我们在外太空可以清楚看到地球表面是个球面，而在地球上我们却是很难判断它的形状的，问题是，只在地球上有没有方法推测出地球的形状呢？与此类似，给一个曲面能否找到切向量不依赖高维空间的表示呢？接下来就来寻找这个答案，秘密就在于（方向）导数。

**定义2.1 (方向导数）**

设 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 是![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En)的开集，函数![f：U\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%EF%BC%9AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 。给定 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 内一点 ![p=(p^1,...,p^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%3D%28p%5E1%2C...%2Cp%5En%29) 和一向量 ![v=\langle v^1,...,v^n\rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%5Clangle+v%5E1%2C...%2Cv%5En%5Crangle) 。那么过点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 沿 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 方向的直线上的点可以表示为 ![c(t)=(p^1+tv^1,...,p^n+tv^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%28t%29%3D%28p%5E1%2Btv%5E1%2C...%2Cp%5En%2Btv%5En%29) ,分量 ![c^i(t)=p^i+tv^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%5Ei%28t%29%3Dp%5Ei%2Btv%5Ei) 。

定义函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点沿向量 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 的方向导数为：

![D_vf|_p=\lim_{t\to 0}\frac{f(c(t))-f(p)}{t}=\frac{d}{dt}\bigg|_{t=0}f(c(t))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf%7C_p%3D%5Clim_%7Bt%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28c%28t%29%29-f%28p%29%7D%7Bt%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdt%7D%5Cbigg%7C_%7Bt%3D0%7Df%28c%28t%29%29) 。

根据链式法则，得到

![D_vf|_p=\sum_{i=1}^n\frac{dc^i(t)}{dt}(0)\cdot\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)=\sum_{i=1}^n v^i\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf%7C_p%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7Bdc%5Ei%28t%29%7D%7Bdt%7D%280%29%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En+v%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29)

> 注1：有些书会严格地定义方向导数为：函数在某一点沿**单位向量**的方向导数。为体现“给定一个向量对应一个方向导数”的核心思想，本文不采用这种定义，两者只是相差某个倍数而已。
注2： ![D_vf|_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf%7C_p) 是一个实数，在不混淆情况下，简单记做 ![D_vf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf) 。

**例2.1** 求函数 ![f(x,y,z)=x^2+3y+4z^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%2Cy%2Cz%29%3Dx%5E2%2B3y%2B4z%5E3) 在点 ![p=(1,1,1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%3D%281%2C1%2C1%29) 处沿 ![v=<1,2,3>](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%3C1%2C2%2C3%3E) 的方向导数 ![D_vf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf) 。

> ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处的偏导数为 ![\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\bigg|_p=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%3D2) , ![\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\bigg|_p=3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%3D3) , ![\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}\bigg|_p=12](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+z%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%3D12) 。则由 ![D_vf=\sum_{i=1}^n v^i\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En+v%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29) 得到 ![D_vf=1\times2+2\times3+3\times12=44](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf%3D1%5Ctimes2%2B2%5Ctimes3%2B3%5Ctimes12%3D44)

![D_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v) 可以看做一个映射 ![D_v:f\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v%3Af%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ,即给定一个函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ,得到一个实数 ![D_vf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf) 。故 ![D_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v) 可以记做 ![D_v=\sum_{i=1}^n v^i\frac{\partial }{\partial x^i}\bigg|_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En+v%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p) 。至此，我们发现向量 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 和方向导数映射 ![D_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v) 可以联系起来，给定向量 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 可以确定一个 ![D_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v) ,即有映射 ![v\to D_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%5Cto+D_v) ,这表明切向量 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 可以看成函数上的某种操作（operator）。我们继续沿着这个思路探索，为了严谨性，下面引入函数芽的概念。

**Section 3 函数芽(germs of function)**

如果两个函数在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的某个邻域内每点取值都相等，则它们有相同的方向导数（由方向导数定义可以得出），也就是说，这两个函数在方向导数的意义下是等价的。这启发我们定义下面的等价类。

**定义3.1 （函数芽）**

![(f,U_p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2CU_p%29) 表示 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点某个领域内的 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 实函数。

定义等价关系：称 ![(f,U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2CU%29) 等价于 ![(g,V)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28g%2CV%29) ,如果存在开集 ![W\subseteq U\cap V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W%5Csubseteq+U%5Ccap+V) 且 ![p\in W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+W) ,使得限制在 ![W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W) 上有 ![f=g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3Dg) 。那么在这个等价关系下，函数 ![(f,U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2CU%29) 的**等价类**就叫做 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点处的芽（germ）,记做 ![[f]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bf%5D) 。

我们记 ![C^\infty_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 或 ![C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p) 为： ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 空间中，点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处所有 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数的芽的集合，即是 ![C^\infty_p(\mathbb{R}^n)=\{[h]:h \text{ is }C^\infty \text{ at }p \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29%3D%5C%7B%5Bh%5D%3Ah+%5Ctext%7B+is+%7DC%5E%5Cinfty+%5Ctext%7B+at+%7Dp+%5C%7D)

> 注1：通俗讲，函数f的芽就是那些在某个小邻域内与f相等的函数
注2：定义函数芽使得讨论的函数减少了，因为属于同个芽的函数都是等价的，讨论其中一个代表就行。
注3：今后若写 ![f\in C^\infty_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) ,实际上是讨论芽 ![[f]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bf%5D) ,只不过选了其中一个代表 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 。

**例子3.1**

设在 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的一元函数 ![f_1,f_2,f_3,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_1%2Cf_2%2Cf_3%2C) 在点 ![x_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0) 处光滑，由图看出在邻域 ![(x_0-\varepsilon,x_0+\varepsilon)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_0-%5Cvarepsilon%2Cx_0%2B%5Cvarepsilon%29) 内有![f_1=f_2=f_3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_1%3Df_2%3Df_3) 。因此函数 ![f_1,f_2,f_3,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_1%2Cf_2%2Cf_3%2C)是等价的，它们同属于一个芽，这个芽可以记做 ![[f_1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bf_1%5D)或 ![[f_2]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bf_2%5D) 或 ![[f_3]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bf_3%5D) 等等
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-ff1f3e0cd86c40804bd5da794c33a67f_b.jpg)函数芽
接下来证明 ![C^\infty_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 构成一个 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) -[向量空间](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E5%2590%2591%25E9%2587%258F%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E9%2597%25B4) (即标量取实数的向量空间)

**命题3.1**

![C^\infty_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 是域 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的的[向量空间](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E5%2590%2591%25E9%2587%258F%25E7%25A9%25BA%25E9%2597%25B4)

注：通俗地讲，![C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p) 是一个![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)-向量空间，就是说把**整个光滑函数**当做**一个元素**，配备了如上定义的向量加法和标量乘法后，这个元素就成为一个向量。

> 证明：要证明 ![C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p) 是一个向量空间，我们只需验证满足向量空间的定义。

(i) 设 ![f,g\in C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) , ![r\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 。我们先定义 ![C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p) 的"向量加法"和"标量乘法"。
(a) 向量加法 ![+:C^\infty_p\times C^\infty_p \rightarrow C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2B%3AC%5E%5Cinfty_p%5Ctimes+C%5E%5Cinfty_p+%5Crightarrow+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) ，![(f+g)(x)=f(x)+g(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2Bg%29%28x%29%3Df%28x%29%2Bg%28x%29) （ ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 属于 ![f,g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg) 定义域的交集），显然 ![f+g \in C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Bg+%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) 。
(b) 标量乘法 ![\cdot:\mathbb{R}\times C^\infty_p \rightarrow C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccdot%3A%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Ctimes+C%5E%5Cinfty_p+%5Crightarrow+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) , ![(r\cdot f)(x)=r\times f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28r%5Ccdot+f%29%28x%29%3Dr%5Ctimes+f%28x%29) ，显然 ![r\cdot f \in C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Ccdot+f+%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) .
*（注1：这里 *![\times](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctimes)* 是普通的乘法; 经常把*![\cdot](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccdot)* 省略，为强调不同的乘法，下面证明不省略，以后会省略*
*注2：此时向量空间*![C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p)*可表示为*![(C^\infty_p,+,\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28C%5E%5Cinfty_p%2C%2B%2C%5Ccdot%29)*）*

(ii) 设 ![f,g,h \in C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg%2Ch+%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) 和 ![r,s \in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%2Cs+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 。下面验证在此加法和乘法下，满足向量空间的定义。

(1) 验证向量加法的结合律，即 ![(f+g)+h=f+(g+h)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2Bg%29%2Bh%3Df%2B%28g%2Bh%29) 。
根据加法的定义，左边的函数就是： ![((f+g)+h)(x)=(f+g)(x)+h(x)=f(x)+g(x)+h(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%28f%2Bg%29%2Bh%29%28x%29%3D%28f%2Bg%29%28x%29%2Bh%28x%29%3Df%28x%29%2Bg%28x%29%2Bh%28x%29) 。同理证明右边的函数也是如此。
(2) 验证向量加法的交换律，即 ![f+g=g+f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Bg%3Dg%2Bf) 。(读者仿照上面可证）
(3) 验证向量加法的单位元，零函数就是单位元。即 ![\forall f \in C^\infty_p, 0+f=f+0=f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+f+%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p%2C+0%2Bf%3Df%2B0%3Df) 。
(4) 验证向量加法的逆元素。对任意 ![f\in C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) ,其加法的逆就是 ![-f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-f) 。
(5) 验证标量乘法与标量的域乘法相容，即 ![(r\times s)\cdot f=r\cdot(s\cdot f)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28r%5Ctimes+s%29%5Ccdot+f%3Dr%5Ccdot%28s%5Ccdot+f%29) 。
根据标量乘法的定义，左边的函数就是 ![((r\times s)\cdot f)(x)=r\times s\times f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%28r%5Ctimes+s%29%5Ccdot+f%29%28x%29%3Dr%5Ctimes+s%5Ctimes+f%28x%29) 。
右边的函数 ![(r\cdot(s\cdot f))(x)=r\times (s\cdot f)(x)=r\times s\times f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28r%5Ccdot%28s%5Ccdot+f%29%29%28x%29%3Dr%5Ctimes+%28s%5Ccdot+f%29%28x%29%3Dr%5Ctimes+s%5Ctimes+f%28x%29) 。即 ![((r\times s)\cdot f)(x)=(r\cdot(s\cdot f))(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%28r%5Ctimes+s%29%5Ccdot+f%29%28x%29%3D%28r%5Ccdot%28s%5Ccdot+f%29%29%28x%29) ，故 ![(r\times s)\cdot f=r\cdot(s\cdot f)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28r%5Ctimes+s%29%5Ccdot+f%3Dr%5Ccdot%28s%5Ccdot+f%29) 。
(6)验证标量乘法的单位元。实数 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 就是单位元，即 ![1\cdot f=f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Ccdot+f%3Df) 。
(7)验证标量乘法对向量加法的分配律，即 ![s\cdot(f+g)=s\cdot f+s\cdot g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s%5Ccdot%28f%2Bg%29%3Ds%5Ccdot+f%2Bs%5Ccdot+g) 。
证明套路如(5),左边函数是 ![(s\cdot(f+g))(x)=s\times(f+g)(x)=s\times(f(x)+g(x))=s\times f(x)+s\times g(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28s%5Ccdot%28f%2Bg%29%29%28x%29%3Ds%5Ctimes%28f%2Bg%29%28x%29%3Ds%5Ctimes%28f%28x%29%2Bg%28x%29%29%3Ds%5Ctimes+f%28x%29%2Bs%5Ctimes+g%28x%29)
右边的函数留给读者。
(8) 验证标量乘法对**域加法**的分配律，即 ![(s+t)\cdot f=s\cdot f+t\cdot f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28s%2Bt%29%5Ccdot+f%3Ds%5Ccdot+f%2Bt%5Ccdot+f) 。
*（注：上面式子两个"+"的含义是不同的，左边是标量的加法，右边是向量的加法）*
左边函数是 ![((s+t)\cdot f)(x)=(s+t)\times f(x)=\underbrace{s\times f(x)+t\times f(x)}_{思考这里+是何种加法？}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%28s%2Bt%29%5Ccdot+f%29%28x%29%3D%28s%2Bt%29%5Ctimes+f%28x%29%3D%5Cunderbrace%7Bs%5Ctimes+f%28x%29%2Bt%5Ctimes+f%28x%29%7D_%7B%E6%80%9D%E8%80%83%E8%BF%99%E9%87%8C%2B%E6%98%AF%E4%BD%95%E7%A7%8D%E5%8A%A0%E6%B3%95%EF%BC%9F%7D)
右边的函数是 ![(s\cdot f+t\cdot f)(x)=(s\cdot f)(x)+(t\cdot f)(x)=s\times f(x)+t\times f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28s%5Ccdot+f%2Bt%5Ccdot+f%29%28x%29%3D%28s%5Ccdot+f%29%28x%29%2B%28t%5Ccdot+f%29%28x%29%3Ds%5Ctimes+f%28x%29%2Bt%5Ctimes+f%28x%29) 。

至此，![C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p) 是一个![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)-向量空间是证毕。

**例子 3.2**

在 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上，取 ![p=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%3D0) ，那么函数 ![f=\sin(x),g=\cos(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3D%5Csin%28x%29%2Cg%3D%5Ccos%28x%29) 都是 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处的光滑函数，因此 ![f,g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg) 都是![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)-向量空间 ![C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p) 的向量，或者说 ![\sin](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin)函数和 ![\cos](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos) 函数都是向量。请问，向量 ![(\sin+\cos)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Csin%2B%5Ccos%29) 是哪一个光滑函数？

> 根据向量的加法，对每一点 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 有![(\sin+\cos)(x)=\sin(x)+\cos(x)=\sqrt{2}\sin(x+\frac\pi4)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Csin%2B%5Ccos%29%28x%29%3D%5Csin%28x%29%2B%5Ccos%28x%29%3D%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%5Csin%28x%2B%5Cfrac%5Cpi4%29) ，也就是说，向量 ![(\sin+\cos)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Csin%2B%5Ccos%29) 是函数 ![\sqrt{2}\sin(x+\frac\pi4)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%5Csin%28x%2B%5Cfrac%5Cpi4%29) 。

向量空间的元素之间并没有“乘法”运算，指定了满足一定规则的乘法，就成了新的代数系统称为**代数**（algebra）。

**定义3.2 （代数）**

域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 上的[代数](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Algebra_over_a_field)是指：域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 上的向量空间 ![(A,+,\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28A%2C%2B%2C%5Ccdot%29) 且配备了向量乘法 ![\ast](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cast) ，满足：

(i) 结合律： ![(f\ast g )\ast h=f\ast (g \ast h)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%5Cast+g+%29%5Cast+h%3Df%5Cast+%28g+%5Cast+h%29) , ![\forall f,g,h \in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+f%2Cg%2Ch+%5Cin+A)

(ii) 分配律： ![(f+g)\ast h=f\ast h+g\ast h](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2Bg%29%5Cast+h%3Df%5Cast+h%2Bg%5Cast+h) 和 ![f\ast(g+h)=f\ast g+f\ast h](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cast%28g%2Bh%29%3Df%5Cast+g%2Bf%5Cast+h) , ![\forall f,g,h \in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+f%2Cg%2Ch+%5Cin+A) 。

(iii)齐次性(homogeneity)： ![r\cdot (f\ast g)=(r\cdot f)\ast g=f\ast(r\cdot g)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Ccdot+%28f%5Cast+g%29%3D%28r%5Ccdot+f%29%5Cast+g%3Df%5Cast%28r%5Ccdot+g%29) , ![\forall f,g \in A,r\in\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+f%2Cg+%5Cin+A%2Cr%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 。

> 注1：这个定义实际上是满足[结合律的代数](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Associative_algebra)，有些代数的定义是可以不满足结合律的（见[Algebra over a field](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Algebra_over_a_field)）

**定义3.3 （线性映射）**

设域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 上有两向量空间 ![V,W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%2CW) ,称映射 ![L:V \to W](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%3AV+%5Cto+W) 是线性映射或线性算子，如果对任意 ![r\in \mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 和 ![u,v\in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%2Cv%5Cin+V) 有：

(i) ![L(u+v)=L(u)+L(v)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%28u%2Bv%29%3DL%28u%29%2BL%28v%29) ;

(ii) ![L(rv)=rL(v)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%28rv%29%3DrL%28v%29)

当强调标量 ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) 的域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 时，就称映射 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 是 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D)-线性映射，或![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D)-线性的。

**定义3.4 (同态和同构）**

[同态](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E5%2590%258C%25E6%2580%2581)是保持运算结构的的映射；[同构](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isomorphism)即是保持运算结构的双射，即同构不仅是同态，而且是双射，因此两个同构的对象可以看成是相同的（**运算的意义下**）。同态(或者同构)是一个框架，讨论的对象是[群](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Group_%28mathematics%29)时，就叫[群同态](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E7%25BE%25A4%25E5%2590%258C%25E6%2585%258B)（群同构）；讨论的对象是向量空间时，就叫线性映射（线性同构）；讨论的对象是代数时，就叫代数同态(代数同构）。原因在于，线性空间的加法运算可以看成是加法群，而代数的乘法运算可以看成是乘法群。

线性映射见定义3.3，代数同态的定义如下。

设 ![A,A'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CA%27) 是域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 上的代数，如果线性映射 ![L:A\to A'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%3AA%5Cto+A%27) 保持代数的乘法： ![L(ab)=L(a)L(b),\forall a,b \in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%28ab%29%3DL%28a%29L%28b%29%2C%5Cforall+a%2Cb+%5Cin+A) ,则称线性映射 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 是**代数同态**。

**例子3.3**

复数、普通向量、[相量](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E7%259B%25B8%25E9%2587%258F)（电气工程）的区别和联系是什么？

> （1）首先，仿照命题3.1做法，可以验证复数集 ![\mathbb{C}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D) 是一个 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) -向量空间，不仅如此，复数集 ![\mathbb{C}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D)还是一个代数（定义3.2），因为复数的乘法可验证满足定义3.2。
（2）对任意复数 ![z\in \mathbb{C}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D) ,可定义映射 ![L:\mathbb{C\to R^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%3A%5Cmathbb%7BC%5Cto+R%5E2%7D) 为 ![z=x+y\text{i} \rightarrow (x,y)\in \mathbb{R}^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z%3Dx%2By%5Ctext%7Bi%7D+%5Crightarrow+%28x%2Cy%29%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E2) 。
可以验证这个映射 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 是线性映射，而且是双射，因此复数集![\mathbb{C}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D)和 ![\mathbb{R}^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E2) 是线性同构。
（3）我们给向量空间 ![\mathbb{R}^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E2) 配备一种向量的乘法 ![\odot](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Codot) ，定义为 ![(a,b)\odot(c,d)=(ac-bd,ad+bc)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29%5Codot%28c%2Cd%29%3D%28ac-bd%2Cad%2Bbc%29) 。那么，线性映射 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 就成了代数同构，证明：对 ![\forall z_1=a+b\text{i},z_2=c+d\text{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+z_1%3Da%2Bb%5Ctext%7Bi%7D%2Cz_2%3Dc%2Bd%5Ctext%7Bi%7D) ,有![L(z_1 z_2)=L(ac-bd+(ad+bc)\text{i})=(ac-bd,ad+bc)=(a,b)\odot(c,d)=L(z_1)\odot L(z_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%28z_1+z_2%29%3DL%28ac-bd%2B%28ad%2Bbc%29%5Ctext%7Bi%7D%29%3D%28ac-bd%2Cad%2Bbc%29%3D%28a%2Cb%29%5Codot%28c%2Cd%29%3DL%28z_1%29%5Codot+L%28z_2%29)
（4）把 ![\mathbb{R}^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E2) 视作 ![\mathbb{R}^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E3) 的子空间，则可定义令一种向量的乘法 ![\times](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctimes) ，叫做叉乘（[cross product](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cross_product)），定义为：设 ![u,v\in\mathbb{R}^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%2Cv%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E3) ,![u=(u_1,u_2,u_3),v=(v_1,v_2,v_3)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%3D%28u_1%2Cu_2%2Cu_3%29%2Cv%3D%28v_1%2Cv_2%2Cv_3%29) ,则 ![u\times v= \left| {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} {\rm{i}}&{\rm{j}}&{\rm{k}}\\ {{u_1}}&{{u_2}}&{{u_3}}\\ {{v_1}}&{{v_2}}&{{v_3}} \end{array}} \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%5Ctimes+v%3D+%5Cleft%7C+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+%7B%5Crm%7Bi%7D%7D%26%7B%5Crm%7Bj%7D%7D%26%7B%5Crm%7Bk%7D%7D%5C%5C+%7B%7Bu_1%7D%7D%26%7B%7Bu_2%7D%7D%26%7B%7Bu_3%7D%7D%5C%5C+%7B%7Bv_1%7D%7D%26%7B%7Bv_2%7D%7D%26%7B%7Bv_3%7D%7D+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright%7C) 。则 ![\mathbb{R}^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E2) 在此乘法下成了另一种代数，运算为![(a,b,0)\times(c,d,0)=(0,0,ad-bc)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%2C0%29%5Ctimes%28c%2Cd%2C0%29%3D%280%2C0%2Cad-bc%29) 。这种乘法，也正是普通向量所定义的乘法。
（5）相量是电气工程的概念，可以验证相量和复数可建立线性同构和代数同构，所以在运算的意义下是一个东西，
（6）总结：从向量空间角度看，普通向量、复数和相量都是向量空间，因为都满足向量空间的8个性质；从代数来看，复数和相量是一个东西，它们和普通向量不是一个东西，因为定义的乘法不一样。

上命题3.1已证明 ![C^\infty_p(R^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p%28R%5En%29) 是一个 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 向量空间，不仅如此，它还是一个代数。

**命题3.2 **

![C^\infty_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 是域 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的代数。

> 对 ![\forall f,g\in C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+f%2Cg%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) ,定义乘法 ![\ast](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cast) ： ![(f*g)(x)=f(x)g(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2Ag%29%28x%29%3Df%28x%29g%28x%29)。然后验证代数的定义即可。

**Section 4  在某点的导数**

这一小节推广方向导数的定义和建立切向量和导数映射之间的同构。

在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点处所有切向量构成的集合叫做切空间（tangent space），用 ![T_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29)或 ![T_p\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 表示。设对任意 ![\nu \in T_p\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu+%5Cin+T_p%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) ,导数 ![D_vf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf) 可以看成一个映射 ![D_v:C^\infty_p\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v%3AC%5E%5Cinfty_p%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ,根据 ![D_vf|_p=\sum_{i=1}^n v^i\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_vf%7C_p%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En+v%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29) ，可以证明方向导数映射 ![D_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v) 是 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) -线性映射（证明留作习题），且满足莱布尼兹律（Leibniz rule）： ![D_v(fg)=(D_vf)g(p)+f(p)D_vg](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v%28fg%29%3D%28D_vf%29g%28p%29%2Bf%28p%29D_vg) 。据此，我们推广方向导数至一般导数，如下定义：

** 定义4.1 （导数映射）**

任何**线性映射**![D:C^\infty_p\to\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3AC%5E%5Cinfty_p%5Cto%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) ,只要满足莱布尼兹律 ![D(fg)=(Df)g(p)+f(p)Dg](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28fg%29%3D%28Df%29g%28p%29%2Bf%28p%29Dg) ，就叫做 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点处的导数（或导数算符，或导数映射）或者叫 ![C_p^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C_p%5E%5Cinfty) 的点导数。记在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点处所有导数算符的集合为 ![\mathcal{ D}_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathcal%7B+D%7D_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 。

> 注1：方向导数和偏导数符合此定义，因此它们都是导数。
注2：![\mathcal{ D}_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathcal%7B+D%7D_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 也是一个 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) -向量空间。因为两个导数算符的和也是导数算符，导数算符乘以标量也是导数算符。证明也留作习题。

Section 2 末尾提到，给定向量 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 可以确定一个 ![D_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v) 。因此，我们来定义切空间![T_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29)到 ![\mathcal{ D}_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathcal%7B+D%7D_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 的映射 

![\begin{align} &\phi :T_p(\mathbb{R}^n) \to \mathcal{ D}_p(\mathbb{R}^n)\\ & v\mapsto D_v=\sum v^i \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i}\bigg |_p \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%26%5Cphi+%3AT_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29+%5Cto+%5Cmathcal%7B+D%7D_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29%5C%5C+%26+v%5Cmapsto+D_v%3D%5Csum+v%5Ei+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x_i%7D%5Cbigg+%7C_p+%5Cend%7Balign%7D) （式4-1）

接下来，我们来证明映射 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 是同构映射，即 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 不但是线性映射，而且是双射（即是单射又是满射）。我们先来证明一个简单的引理。

**引理4.1 (导数作用于常函数为0）**

如果 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 是 ![C_p^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C_p%5E%5Cinfty) 的点导数算符，则对任意常函数 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) ,有 ![D(c)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28c%29%3D0) 。

> 证明：我们不知道 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 的具体表达式，只能利用定义的线性和莱布尼兹律来证明。
因为映射 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 是线性的，故 ![D(c)=cD(1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28c%29%3DcD%281%29) 。我们只需证明 ![D(1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%281%29%3D0)  。
由莱布尼兹律 ![D(1)=D(1\cdot 1)=D(1)\cdot1+1\cdot D(1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%281%29%3DD%281%5Ccdot+1%29%3DD%281%29%5Ccdot1%2B1%5Ccdot+D%281%29) ,即得 ![D(1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%281%29%3D0) 。

**定理4.1 **

对任意 ![v\in T_p(\mathbb{R}^n),](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%5Cin+T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29%2C) 定义映射 ![\phi :T_p(\mathbb{R}^n) \to \mathcal{ D}_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi+%3AT_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29+%5Cto+%5Cmathcal%7B+D%7D_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 为：

![\phi(v)=D_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%28v%29%3DD_v) ,其中 ![D_v \in \mathcal{D}_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v+%5Cin+%5Cmathcal%7BD%7D_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 。则映射 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 是同构映射。

> 证明：要证明 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 是同构映射，只需证明 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 既是线性映射又是双射（即是单射又是满射）。

（i）证明是线性映射。任取 ![u,v \in T_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%2Cv+%5Cin+T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 和 ![r\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 。
（a）则对任意 ![f\in C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) ,
![\begin{align} \phi(u+v)f=D_{u+v}f&=\sum (u+v)^i \frac{\partial }{\partial x_i}\bigg |_p f\\ &=\sum (u^i+v^i) \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i}\bigg |_pf\\ &=\sum u^i \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i}\bigg |_p f+\sum v^i\frac{\partial}{\partial x_i}\bigg |_pf\\ &=(D_u+D_v)f=(\phi(u)+\phi(v))f \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%5Cphi%28u%2Bv%29f%3DD_%7Bu%2Bv%7Df%26%3D%5Csum+%28u%2Bv%29%5Ei+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x_i%7D%5Cbigg+%7C_p+f%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Csum+%28u%5Ei%2Bv%5Ei%29+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x_i%7D%5Cbigg+%7C_pf%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Csum+u%5Ei+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x_i%7D%5Cbigg+%7C_p+f%2B%5Csum+v%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x_i%7D%5Cbigg+%7C_pf%5C%5C+%26%3D%28D_u%2BD_v%29f%3D%28%5Cphi%28u%29%2B%5Cphi%28v%29%29f+%5Cend%7Balign%7D)
因此， ![\phi(u+v)=\phi(u)+\phi(v)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%28u%2Bv%29%3D%5Cphi%28u%29%2B%5Cphi%28v%29) 。
（b） 仿上可证， ![\phi(rv)=r\phi(v)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%28rv%29%3Dr%5Cphi%28v%29) 。
（c）综合(a)和(b)可知![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi)是线性映射。

（ii）证明线性映射 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 是单射（利用技巧：[要证明某个线性映射是单射，只需证明该线性映射的核为0](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//math.stackexchange.com/questions/2193333/showing-a-linear-map-is-injective-if-and-only-if-kernel-is-0)，即 ![\phi(v)=0\Rightarrow v=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%28v%29%3D0%5CRightarrow+v%3D0) ）。
设 ![\phi(v)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%28v%29%3D0) ，即是 ![D_v=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v%3D0) ，其中 ![v\in T_p(\mathbb{R}^n)。](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%5Cin+T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29%E3%80%82) 将 ![D_v=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D_v%3D0) 作用于坐标函数 ![x^j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Ej) ,得到 ![0=D_v(x^j)=\sum_iv^i\frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}\bigg|_px^j=v^j,\forall v^j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3DD_v%28x%5Ej%29%3D%5Csum_iv%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%5Cbigg%7C_px%5Ej%3Dv%5Ej%2C%5Cforall+v%5Ej) 。因此 ![v=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D0) 。
（iii）证明线性映射 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 是满射（即证明对任意导数 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) ,总能找到切向量 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 与之对应）。
任取 ![D\in \mathcal{ D}_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%5Cin+%5Cmathcal%7B+D%7D_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 。设 ![(f,V)\in C_p^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28f%2CV%29%5Cin+C_p%5E%5Cinfty) ,其中 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 是 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 足够小的开邻域且是星型的。
那么根据泰勒引理（引理1.1），则存在函数 ![g_i\in C^\infty_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_i%5Cin+C%5E%5Cinfty_p) ，使得
![f(x)=f(p)+\sum_{i=1}^n(x^i-p^i)g_i(x),\quad g_i(p)=\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Df%28p%29%2B%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%28x%5Ei-p%5Ei%29g_i%28x%29%2C%5Cquad+g_i%28p%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29) 。
将 ![D](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D) 作用于上式两边，并注意 ![D(f(p))=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28f%28p%29%29%3D0) 和 ![D(p^i)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28p%5Ei%29%3D0) (根据引理4.1)，再利用莱布尼兹律，得 ![Df(x)=\sum_{i=1}^n(Dx^i)g_i(p)+\sum_{i=1}^n(p^i-p^i)Dg_i(x)=\sum_{i=1}^n(Dx^i)\frac{\partial f}{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Df%28x%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%28Dx%5Ei%29g_i%28p%29%2B%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%28p%5Ei-p%5Ei%29Dg_i%28x%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5En%28Dx%5Ei%29%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29)
这表明 ![D=D_v=\phi(v)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3DD_v%3D%5Cphi%28v%29) ,其中 ![v=\langle Dx^1,...,Dx^n\rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%5Clangle+Dx%5E1%2C...%2CDx%5En%5Crangle) 。故 ![\phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi) 是满射。

上面定理表明，可以将点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处的切向量和点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处的导数**等同**起来。在向量空间同构 ![T_p(\mathbb{R}^n)\simeq \mathcal{D}_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29%5Csimeq+%5Cmathcal%7BD%7D_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 的意义下， ![T_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 的标准基 ![e_1,...,e_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e_1%2C...%2Ce_n) 对应于偏导数集 ![\frac{\partial }{\partial x^1},...,\frac{\partial }{\partial x^n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5E1%7D%2C...%2C%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5En%7D) 。从此以后，我们将它们看做一个东西，并将 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点的切向量 ![v=\langle v^1,...,v^n\rangle=\sum v^i e_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%5Clangle+v%5E1%2C...%2Cv%5En%5Crangle%3D%5Csum+v%5Ei+e_i) 写成 ![v=\sum v^i\frac{\partial }{\partial x^i}\bigg|_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%5Csum+v%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p) 。

这就是我们所要找的不依赖于高维空间的切向量的表达方式，将某点处的切向量表达成导数，这种表达方式便于推广至一般的流形。

> 表示函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 在 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点沿着 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 的方向导数，也就是说切向量作用于函数得到一个实数。

**Section 5 向量场、向量场作为导数**

**定义5.1 (向量场)**

![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 上开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 的向量场 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是一个映射，它将开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 里的每一点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 对应到一个切向量 ![X_p \in T_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p+%5Cin+T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 。因为 ![T_p(\mathbb{R}^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_p%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%29) 的基是 ![\bigg\{\frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}\bigg|_p\bigg\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbigg%5C%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%5Cbigg%5C%7D) ,因此切向量 ![X_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p) 可以写成 ![X_p=\sum a^i(p) \frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}\bigg|_p,\quad p\in U,a^i(p)\in \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p%3D%5Csum+a%5Ei%28p%29+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%5Cbigg%7C_p%2C%5Cquad+p%5Cin+U%2Ca%5Ei%28p%29%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 。

略去 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) ,向量场可写成 ![X=\sum a^i \frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D%5Csum+a%5Ei+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D) ，其中 ![a^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Ei) 是 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的实函数。我们称向量场 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上是光滑的（ ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) ），如果系数函数 ![a^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Ei) 在 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上都是光滑的。

Section 4 我们知道切向量作用于函数得到一个实数，那么光滑向量场作用于函数得到什么呢？接下来研究这个问题。

设 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是开集 ![U\subseteq\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%5Csubseteq%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 上的光滑向量场， ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的光滑函数。

在开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上定义新函数 ![(Xf)：U\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Xf%29%EF%BC%9AU%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 为 ![(Xf)(p)=X_p(f),\forall p \in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Xf%29%28p%29%3DX_p%28f%29%2C%5Cforall+p+%5Cin+U) 。

> 来理解一下这个定义，对于固定的 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) , ![X_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p) 是一个确定的切向量， ![X_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p) 作用到函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 上得到一方向导数，因此 ![X_p(f)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p%28f%29) 是一个实数。当 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 变化时， ![X_p(f)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p%28f%29) 就成为关于 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 的函数，这个函数的名字就叫 ![Xf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Xf) 。

将 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 写成 ![X=\sum a_i \frac{\partial }{\partial x^i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D%5Csum+a_i+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D) ,得到 ![(Xf)(p)=X_p(f)=\sum a_i(p) \frac{\partial f }{\partial x^i}(p)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Xf%29%28p%29%3DX_p%28f%29%3D%5Csum+a_i%28p%29+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D%28p%29) 。

略去 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 即是 ![Xf=\sum a^i\frac{\partial f }{\partial x^i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Xf%3D%5Csum+a%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D) ，这表明 ![Xf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Xf) 是 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的光滑函数（为什么？因为 ![a^i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Ei) 光滑，而 ![\frac{\partial f }{\partial x^i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D) 是一个实数）。

因此一个 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 的向量场 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 可以看成一个 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) -线性映射： ![C^\infty(U)\to C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29%5Cto+C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) , ![f \mapsto Xf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f+%5Cmapsto+Xf) ，即 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 将一个光滑函数映射成一个光滑函数。

开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的所有 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数构成一个「**环(ring)**」，记做 ![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) 或者 ![\mathcal{F}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathcal%7BF%7D%28U%29) .向量场 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 和 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的**乘法**定义为（注意是逐点定义的）： ![(fX)_p=f(p)X_p,p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28fX%29_p%3Df%28p%29X_p%2Cp%5Cin+U) 。

显然，如果 ![X=\sum a^i\frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D%5Csum+a%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D) 是一个 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 向量场， ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 是 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 函数，则 ![fX=\sum (fa^i)\frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=fX%3D%5Csum+%28fa%5Ei%29%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D) 是 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 向量场。将所有![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 向量场记做 ![\mathfrak{X}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7BX%7D%28U%29) ,则![\mathfrak{X}(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7BX%7D%28U%29)不仅是域 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的向量空间，还是环![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29)上的「[模(module)](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E6%25A8%25A1)」。

代数环和模的定义如下：

**定义5.2（环）**

集合 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 和定义于其上的二元运算 ![+](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2B) 和 ![\cdot](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccdot) , ![(R, +, ·)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28R%2C+%2B%2C+%C2%B7%29) 构成一个**环(ring)**，若满足：

(i) ![(R, +)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28R%2C+%2B%29) 形成一个[交换群](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E9%2598%25BF%25E8%25B4%259D%25E5%25B0%2594%25E7%25BE%25A4)，其单位元称为**零元**，记作 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) ,即：
- ![(R, +)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28R%2C+%2B%29)是封闭的： ![a\in R,b\in R\Rightarrow a+b\in R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+R%2Cb%5Cin+R%5CRightarrow+a%2Bb%5Cin+R)
- 满足交换律： ![(a + b) = (b + a)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a+%2B+b%29+%3D+%28b+%2B+a%29)
- 满足结合律： ![(a + b) + c = a + (b + c)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a+%2B+b%29+%2B+c+%3D+a+%2B+%28b+%2B+c%29)
- 存在零元 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) ： ![0 + a = a + 0 = a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0+%2B+a+%3D+a+%2B+0+%3D+a)
- 任意元素存在逆元素： ![\text{for }∀a, ∃(−a) \text{such that } a + −a = −a + a = 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bfor+%7D%E2%88%80a%2C+%E2%88%83%28%E2%88%92a%29+%5Ctext%7Bsuch+that+%7D+a+%2B+%E2%88%92a+%3D+%E2%88%92a+%2B+a+%3D+0)

(ii) ![(R, ·)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28R%2C+%C2%B7%29) 形成一个[半群](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E5%258D%258A%25E7%25BE%25A4),即：
- ![(R, ·)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28R%2C+%C2%B7%29)是封闭的: ![a\in R,b\in R\Rightarrow ab\in R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+R%2Cb%5Cin+R%5CRightarrow+ab%5Cin+R)
- 满足结合律： ![(a·b)·c = a·(b·c)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%C2%B7b%29%C2%B7c+%3D+a%C2%B7%28b%C2%B7c%29)

(iii)乘法关于加法满足分配律,即：
- ![a·(b + c) = (a·b) + (a·c)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%C2%B7%28b+%2B+c%29+%3D+%28a%C2%B7b%29+%2B+%28a%C2%B7c%29)
- ![(a + b)·c = (a·c) + (b·c)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a+%2B+b%29%C2%B7c+%3D+%28a%C2%B7c%29+%2B+%28b%C2%B7c%29)

如果环 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 对乘法也含有**单位元**，则该环称作**幺环**（unitary ring）；如果环![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 对乘法满足交换律，则该环称作交换环（commutative ring）。

容易证明![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29)是一个带单位元的交换环（可以动手验证一下）。

**定义5.3（模）**

设 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 是一个带单位元的交换环，则一个 ![R\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 模是一个交换群 ![(A,+)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28A%2C%2B%29) 配备了标量乘法，满足：

(i)结合律： ![(rs)a=r(sa)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28rs%29a%3Dr%28sa%29) , ![r,s\in R;a\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%2Cs%5Cin+R%3Ba%5Cin+A)

(ii)单位元：设 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 是 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 的乘法单位元，有 ![1a=a,a\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1a%3Da%2Ca%5Cin+A)

(iii)分配律： ![(r+s)a=ra+sa,r(a+b)=ra+rb](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28r%2Bs%29a%3Dra%2Bsa%2Cr%28a%2Bb%29%3Dra%2Brb) , ![r,s\in R;a,b\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%2Cs%5Cin+R%3Ba%2Cb%5Cin+A)

如果 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 是一个域，则![R\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 模就成了 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 上的向量空间。因此，![R\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 模可以看成是向量空间的推广，它允许将标量放宽到环上.

**命题5.1 （向量场的莱布尼兹律）**

在 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) 的开集 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上，设 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是光滑向量场， ![f,g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%2Cg)是光滑函数，则 ![X(fg)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%28fg%29) 满足莱布尼兹律： ![X(fg)=(Xf)g+f(Xg)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%28fg%29%3D%28Xf%29g%2Bf%28Xg%29) 。

> 证明：对每一点 ![p\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5Cin+U) ,切向量 ![X_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p) 满足莱布尼兹律： ![X_p(fg)=(X_pf)g(p)+f(p)(X_pg)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_p%28fg%29%3D%28X_pf%29g%28p%29%2Bf%28p%29%28X_pg%29) 。
而 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 任意的， 因此 ![X(fg)=(Xf)g+f(Xg)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%28fg%29%3D%28Xf%29g%2Bf%28Xg%29)

**例子5.1 **

设 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是向量场 ![X=\langle x,2y,0\rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D%5Clangle+x%2C2y%2C0%5Crangle) ，函数 ![f(x,y,z)=x^2+y^2+z^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%2Cy%2Cz%29%3Dx%5E2%2By%5E2%2Bz%5E2) ,计算 ![Xf](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Xf) 。

> ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的偏导数为： ![\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}=2x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D%3D2x) , ![\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}=2y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7D%3D2y) , ![\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}=2z](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f%7D%7B%5Cpartial+z%7D%3D2z) 。
根据 ![Xf=\sum a^i\frac{\partial f }{\partial x^i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Xf%3D%5Csum+a%5Ei%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+f+%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%5Ei%7D) ，
![Xf=](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Xf%3D)![x\cdot 2x+2y\cdot2y+0\cdot 2z](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Ccdot+2x%2B2y%5Ccdot2y%2B0%5Ccdot+2z) = ![2x^2+4y^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2x%5E2%2B4y%5E2)

更一般的，设 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是域 ![\mathbb{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D) 上的代数， ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的「导数」是一个 ![\mathbb{K}\text{-}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BK%7D%5Ctext%7B-%7D) 线性映射 ![D:A\to A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%3AA%5Cto+A) ,使得 ![D(ab)=(Da)b+aDb\quad\text{for }\forall a,b\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28ab%29%3D%28Da%29b%2BaDb%5Cquad%5Ctext%7Bfor+%7D%5Cforall+a%2Cb%5Cin+A)

**所有 **![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A)**的导数构成的集合**在「向量加法」和「标量乘法」 下封闭，因此构成一个向量空间，记做 ![\text{Der}(A)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BDer%7D%28A%29) .因此，给定一个 ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的 ![C^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty) 向量场，就给定了 ![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) 的导数，故我们得到映射 ![\begin{align} \varphi:\mathfrak{X}(&U)\to \text{Der}(C^\infty(U))\\ &X \mapsto D \end{align}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%5Cvarphi%3A%5Cmathfrak%7BX%7D%28%26U%29%5Cto+%5Ctext%7BDer%7D%28C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29%29%5C%5C+%26X+%5Cmapsto+D+%5Cend%7Balign%7D) . 正如在点 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 处的切向量可以和 ![C_p^\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C_p%5E%5Cinfty) 的 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 点导数等同起来， ![U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U) 上的向量场也可以等同于![C^\infty(U)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%5Cinfty%28U%29) 的导数；也即映射 ![\varphi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi) 是一个线性同构。
