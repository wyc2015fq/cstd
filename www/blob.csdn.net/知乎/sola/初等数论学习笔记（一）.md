**整除等数论基本概念**

*一、整除*

**定义：**

![a|b(a\neq 0)\Leftrightarrow \exists l,s.t. b=al](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%7Cb%28a%5Cneq+0%29%5CLeftrightarrow+%5Cexists+l%2Cs.t.+b%3Dal)
容易知道整除有着以下性质：（证明略）

1、 ![a|b,b|c\Rightarrow a|c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%7Cb%2Cb%7Cc%5CRightarrow+a%7Cc)
2、 ![a_1+a_2+...+a_n=0,k|a_2,k|a_3,...,k|a_n\Rightarrow k|a_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_1%2Ba_2%2B...%2Ba_n%3D0%2Ck%7Ca_2%2Ck%7Ca_3%2C...%2Ck%7Ca_n%5CRightarrow+k%7Ca_1)
3、（带余除法） ![\forall a,b(b>0),\exists q,r,s.t.a=bq+r(0\leq r<b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+a%2Cb%28b%3E0%29%2C%5Cexists+q%2Cr%2Cs.t.a%3Dbq%2Br%280%5Cleq+r%3Cb%29)

*二、最大公因数（gcd）*
定义：

给定 ![a_1,a_2,...,a_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_1%2Ca_2%2C...%2Ca_n) 不全为0，易知集合 ![\left \{ d:d>0,d|a_1,d|a_2,...d|a_n \right \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft+%5C%7B+d%3Ad%3E0%2Cd%7Ca_1%2Cd%7Ca_2%2C...d%7Ca_n+%5Cright+%5C%7D) 为有限集合，则其极大元存在且唯一，称为 ![a_1,a_2,...,a_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_1%2Ca_2%2C...%2Ca_n)**最大公因数（gcd），记为 **![(a_1,a_2,...,a_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_1%2Ca_2%2C...%2Ca_n%29) 。

下面给出gcd的比较重要的性质：

1、在带余除法 ![\forall a,b(b>0),\exists q,r,s.t.a=bq+r(0\leq r<b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+a%2Cb%28b%3E0%29%2C%5Cexists+q%2Cr%2Cs.t.a%3Dbq%2Br%280%5Cleq+r%3Cb%29) 中， ![(a,b)=(b,r)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29%3D%28b%2Cr%29)

证明：

记 ![S_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_1) 为a与b所有公因子组成的集合， ![S_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_2) 为b与r所有公因子组成的集合。

若 ![d\in S_1,\ d|a,d|b\Rightarrow d|r\Rightarrow d\in S_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5Cin+S_1%2C%5C+d%7Ca%2Cd%7Cb%5CRightarrow+d%7Cr%5CRightarrow+d%5Cin+S_2)

同理地， ![d\in S_2,\ d|r,d|b\Rightarrow d|a\Rightarrow d\in S_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5Cin+S_2%2C%5C+d%7Cr%2Cd%7Cb%5CRightarrow+d%7Ca%5CRightarrow+d%5Cin+S_1)
故 ![S_1=S_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_1%3DS_2) ，极大元相同， ![(a,b)=(b,r)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29%3D%28b%2Cr%29)

2、基于性质1，可以得到求最大公因数的**Euclid算法**（辗转相除法）。

![a=bq_1+r_1(0\leq r_1<b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Dbq_1%2Br_1%280%5Cleq+r_1%3Cb%29)
若 ![r_1\ne0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r_1%5Cne0) ， ![b=r_1q_2+r_2(0\leq r_2<r_1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%3Dr_1q_2%2Br_2%280%5Cleq+r_2%3Cr_1%29)
以此类推，直到 ![r_{n-1}=r_nq_{n+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r_%7Bn-1%7D%3Dr_nq_%7Bn%2B1%7D) 终止，则 ![r_n=(a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r_n%3D%28a%2Cb%29) 。

3、 ![(a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29) 为a与b的公因数（虽然简单但是很常用）


*三、最小公倍数（lcm）*

**定义：**

**![[a_1,a_2,...,a_n]=min\left \{ m:a_1|m,a_2|m,...,a_n|m \right \}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba_1%2Ca_2%2C...%2Ca_n%5D%3Dmin%5Cleft+%5C%7B+m%3Aa_1%7Cm%2Ca_2%7Cm%2C...%2Ca_n%7Cm+%5Cright+%5C%7D)**

那么问题是，最大公因数与最小公倍数与两个数之间有什么联系呢？

**定理：**![(a,b)[a,b]=ab](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a%2Cb%29%5Ba%2Cb%5D%3Dab)

![M=[a,b],\exists k,\ M=ak](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%3D%5Ba%2Cb%5D%2C%5Cexists+k%2C%5C+M%3Dak)

![d=(a,b),a_1=\frac{a}{d},b_1=\frac{b}{d}\Rightarrow \frac{ak}{b}=\frac{a_1k}{b_1}\in \mathbb{N}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%3D%28a%2Cb%29%2Ca_1%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bd%7D%2Cb_1%3D%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7Bd%7D%5CRightarrow+%5Cfrac%7Bak%7D%7Bb%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba_1k%7D%7Bb_1%7D%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BN%7D)

又 ![(a_1,b_1)=(\frac{a}{(a,b)},\frac{b}{(a,b)})=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_1%2Cb_1%29%3D%28%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7B%28a%2Cb%29%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7B%28a%2Cb%29%7D%29%3D1) （此性质会在习题中证明）

![b_1|a_1k\Rightarrow b_1|k\Rightarrow \exists t,\ k=b_1t\Rightarrow \frac{ak}{b}=a_1t\Rightarrow M=a_1bt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1%7Ca_1k%5CRightarrow+b_1%7Ck%5CRightarrow+%5Cexists+t%2C%5C+k%3Db_1t%5CRightarrow+%5Cfrac%7Bak%7D%7Bb%7D%3Da_1t%5CRightarrow+M%3Da_1bt)

而 ![M=\frac{ab}{(a,b)}t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%3D%5Cfrac%7Bab%7D%7B%28a%2Cb%29%7Dt) ，M为a与b的公倍数

而当 ![t=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D1) 时，M即为所有公倍数中最小的，为最小公倍数，故得证。

这个定理虽然简短，但是却告诉了我们gcd与lcm之间深刻的内在联系。（在讲到算数基本定理时，我们会有办法更简单地重新审视这个定理）


*四、连分数的简要探讨*

**定义：**

**![a\in \mathbb{R}, \ q_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2C+%5C+q_1) 为小于 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 的最大整数**

**则 ![0<a-q_1<1,a=q_1+\frac{1}{a_2}(a_2>1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3Ca-q_1%3C1%2Ca%3Dq_1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Ba_2%7D%28a_2%3E1%29)**

**同样地 ![a_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_2) 不为整数时， ![a_2=q_2+\frac{1}{a_3}(a_3>1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_2%3Dq_2%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Ba_3%7D%28a_3%3E1%29)**

**以此类推， ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 可以写成 ![a=q_1+\frac{1}{q_2+\frac{1}{q_3+...}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Dq_1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_2%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_3%2B...%7D%7D) 的连分数形式。 ![(\forall i,q_i\in \mathbb{Z})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cforall+i%2Cq_i%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%29)**

**当 ![a_n\in \mathbb{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D) 时    过程终止。**

容易发现的是，当一个数的连分数表达式有限，即 ![a=q_1+\frac{1}{q_2+\frac{1}{q_3+...+\frac{1}{q_n}}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Dq_1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_2%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_3%2B...%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_n%7D%7D%7D) 时， ![a\in \mathbb{Q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D) 。问题是：逆命题是否成立呢？答案是肯定的。我们可以利用上文提及的Euclid算法给出证明。

**定理：**![a\in \mathbb{Q}\Leftrightarrow \exists q_n,s.t.\ a=q_1+\frac{1}{q_2+\frac{1}{q_3+...+\frac{1}{q_n}}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D%5CLeftrightarrow+%5Cexists+q_n%2Cs.t.%5C+a%3Dq_1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_2%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_3%2B...%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_n%7D%7D%7D)

证明：（仅证 ![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow) ）

![\alpha=\frac{a}{b}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bb%7D) 不妨设 ![a>b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Eb) ，由Euclid算法， ![a=bq_1+r_2(0 \leq r_2<b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Dbq_1%2Br_2%280+%5Cleq+r_2%3Cb%29) ，故 ![\frac{a}{b}=q_1+\frac{r_2}{b}(0 \leq \frac{r_2}{b}<1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bb%7D%3Dq_1%2B%5Cfrac%7Br_2%7D%7Bb%7D%280+%5Cleq+%5Cfrac%7Br_2%7D%7Bb%7D%3C1%29)

同理地， ![\frac{b}{r_2}=q_2+\frac{r_3}{r_2}(0 \leq \frac{r_3}{r_2}<1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7Br_2%7D%3Dq_2%2B%5Cfrac%7Br_3%7D%7Br_2%7D%280+%5Cleq+%5Cfrac%7Br_3%7D%7Br_2%7D%3C1%29)

则 ![\frac{a}{b}=q_1+\frac{1}{q_2+\frac{r_3}{r_2}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bb%7D%3Dq_1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bq_2%2B%5Cfrac%7Br_3%7D%7Br_2%7D%7D)

以此类推，由于Euclid算法总会在有限步终止，故 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 的  连分数    表达式    有限。  

特别地，任何一个正数都能写成连分数形式，故都对应着唯一的一串自然数。有理数对应的自然数串是有限的，无理数对应的自然数串则是无限的。

例： ![\frac{105}{38}=2+\frac{1}{1+\frac{1}{3+\frac{1}{4+\frac{1}{2}}}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B105%7D%7B38%7D%3D2%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B4%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%7D%7D%7D)
对应着自然数串 ![\left \langle 2,1,3,4,2 \right \rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft+%5Clangle+2%2C1%2C3%2C4%2C2+%5Cright+%5Crangle) （事实上就是Euclid算法中每一步得到的商）



*五、素数*

**定义：**

**若一个数恰好有两个正整数因子，则为素数。**

素数的性质：

1、一个大于1的正整数的大于1的最小因子为素数。（反证，证略）

这条性质看上去十分简单，然而在之后**构造素数**进行证明时十分常用。

2、若 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 为合数， ![d](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d) 为其大于1的最小因子，则 ![d \leq \sqrt{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d+%5Cleq+%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D) 。（注意到 ![\frac{n}{d}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7Bd%7D) 为小于 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 的最大因子，证略）


接下来，我们给出一个重要的定理：素数是有无穷多个的。（这里仅用初等方法证明，类似的方法可用来证明有无穷多个 ![4n+3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=4n%2B3) 与 ![6n+5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=6n%2B5) 型素数，之后会用黎曼 ![\zeta ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Czeta+) 函数另给出一个证明）

**定理：存在无穷多个素数**

证明：

![\forall p_1,p_2,...,p_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+p_1%2Cp_2%2C...%2Cp_n) 均为素数，则仅需证明能够利用这些素数构造出一个新的素数，即可无限多次进行这个过程，从而得到无穷多个素数。

![N=p_1p_2...p_n+1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%3Dp_1p_2...p_n%2B1) ，由性质1，取 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 为 ![N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N)  的  大于1的最小因子，则 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 为素数。

若 ![\exists i,\ s.t.\ p=p_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexists+i%2C%5C+s.t.%5C+p%3Dp_i) ，由 ![p|N\Rightarrow p_i|N\Rightarrow p_i|p_1p_2...p_n+1\Rightarrow p_i|1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%7CN%5CRightarrow+p_i%7CN%5CRightarrow+p_i%7Cp_1p_2...p_n%2B1%5CRightarrow+p_i%7C1) ，矛盾。

故 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 为新构造出的异于所有 ![p_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p_i) 的素数，得证。

*六、算数基本定理*

下设 ![p,q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%2Cq) 为素数

先给出两个关于素数性质的命题

命题1、 ![\forall a,p,\ p|a\ or\ (p,a)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+a%2Cp%2C%5C+p%7Ca%5C+or%5C+%28p%2Ca%29%3D1) （证略）

命题2、若 ![p|a_1a_2...a_n\Rightarrow \exists a_i,\ p|a_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%7Ca_1a_2...a_n%5CRightarrow+%5Cexists+a_i%2C%5C+p%7Ca_i)

证明：

反证。若 ![\forall 1\leq i \leq n,p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+1%5Cleq+i+%5Cleq+n%2Cp) 不整除 ![a_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_i)![\Rightarrow \forall 1\leq i \leq n,(p,a_i)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow+%5Cforall+1%5Cleq+i+%5Cleq+n%2C%28p%2Ca_i%29%3D1)
则 ![(p,a_1a_2...a_n)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28p%2Ca_1a_2...a_n%29%3D1) （习题中证明此结论），矛盾 。

上述两个命题都向我们展示了素数的不可分性，而这恰恰是算数基本定理成立的基础。

下面用数学归纳法给出非常重要的算数基本定理的简略证明。

**定理（算数基本定理）：任意一个大于1的正整数都能表示成 ![a=p_1^{\alpha_1}p_2^{\alpha_2}...p_k^{\alpha_k}(\alpha_1,\alpha_2,...,\alpha_k>0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Dp_1%5E%7B%5Calpha_1%7Dp_2%5E%7B%5Calpha_2%7D...p_k%5E%7B%5Calpha_k%7D%28%5Calpha_1%2C%5Calpha_2%2C...%2C%5Calpha_k%3E0%29) 的形式，其中 ![p_1<p_2<...<p_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p_1%3Cp_2%3C...%3Cp_k) 均为素数，且这种表示方法是唯一的。**

证明：

存在性：

若对于 ![b_1>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1%3E1) ， ![\forall b\in[1,b_1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+b%5Cin%5B1%2Cb_1%5D) 能进行上述表示， ![a=b_1+1,p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Db_1%2B1%2Cp) 为 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 的大于1的最小因子。则 ![p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p) 为素数，且 ![1\leq \frac{a}{p} \leq b_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Cleq+%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bp%7D+%5Cleq+b_1)

若 ![\frac{a}{p}=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bp%7D%3D1) ，结论显然成立。

若 ![\frac{a}{p}>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bp%7D%3E1) ， ![\frac{a}{p}=p_1^{\alpha_1}p_2^{\alpha_2}...p_k^{\alpha_k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bp%7D%3Dp_1%5E%7B%5Calpha_1%7Dp_2%5E%7B%5Calpha_2%7D...p_k%5E%7B%5Calpha_k%7D) ，则 ![a=pp_1^{\alpha_1}p_2^{\alpha_2}...p_k^{\alpha_k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Dpp_1%5E%7B%5Calpha_1%7Dp_2%5E%7B%5Calpha_2%7D...p_k%5E%7B%5Calpha_k%7D) 可以被表示。

唯一性：

若 ![a=p_1^{\alpha_1}p_2^{\alpha_2}...p_k^{\alpha_k}=q_1^{\beta_1}q_2^{\beta_2}...q_l^{\beta_l}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Dp_1%5E%7B%5Calpha_1%7Dp_2%5E%7B%5Calpha_2%7D...p_k%5E%7B%5Calpha_k%7D%3Dq_1%5E%7B%5Cbeta_1%7Dq_2%5E%7B%5Cbeta_2%7D...q_l%5E%7B%5Cbeta_l%7D)

记 ![\alpha=\alpha_1+...+\alpha_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3D%5Calpha_1%2B...%2B%5Calpha_k) ，对  ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 归纳。

若结论对 ![\alpha<A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3CA) 成立，当 ![\alpha=A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3DA) 时，

由归纳假设， ![\frac{a}{p_1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bp_1%7D) 表达式唯一 ![\Rightarrow a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow+a) 表达式唯一。
