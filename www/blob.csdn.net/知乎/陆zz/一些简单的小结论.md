自然要问，**可不可以把其他两个流形做连通和变成一个球面**？

1维时,我们有1维紧连通流形都是![S^1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5E1),没有什么其他的流形；

2维时,我们有闭曲面分类定理，所有2维紧可定向连通流形都形如g个洞的甜甜圈(g叫拓扑亏格)，下图为g=2的样子：

![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/88ef758e4170f99ac4a17d40e0be3d2b_b.png)做连通和之后亏格=两流形亏格直和，所以如果![M+N=S^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%2BN%3DS%5E2),那么M，N亏格都是0，也都是球面。一般地，我们有
![M+N=S^n\Rightarrow M,N=S^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%2BN%3DS%5En%5CRightarrow+M%2CN%3DS%5En)

证：（Mazure）

考虑无穷连通和![(M+N)+(M+N)+(M+N)+\hdots](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28M%2BN%29%2B%28M%2BN%29%2B%28M%2BN%29%2B%5Chdots)其同胚于![S^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5En)去掉一点(无穷远点)

(把![S^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5En)嵌到![R^{n+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D)里面，考虑![R^{n+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D)加上无穷远点紧化得到S^{n+1},在S^{n+1}里看)

但是![(M+N)+(M+N)+(M+N)+\hdots=M+(N+M)+(N+M)+\hdots=M+(M+N)+(M+N)+\hdots](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28M%2BN%29%2B%28M%2BN%29%2B%28M%2BN%29%2B%5Chdots%3DM%2B%28N%2BM%29%2B%28N%2BM%29%2B%5Chdots%3DM%2B%28M%2BN%29%2B%28M%2BN%29%2B%5Chdots)

所以M与![S^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5En)去掉一点做连通和得到的还是![S^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5En)去除一点，

但任何一个流形M与![S^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5En)去掉一点做连通和得到的是M去除一点，所以M去除一点同胚于![S^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5En)去除一点，也就是n维欧氏空间，M是n维欧式空间的一点紧化，即![M=S^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%3DS%5En)，同理N。

注：M，N不一定光滑同胚于S^n，we should notice the existence of extoic sphere。
