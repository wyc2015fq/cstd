# 试证明对于任意三角形，内接圆半径不超过外接圆半径的一半——京都大学2006年高考第四题（理科） - 知乎

![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-d431c3b6a1cb43182ae093296fbad0e5_b.jpg)

平面上有一个半径为 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1)  的圆周，圆周上有不同的 ![A,B,C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB%2CC) 三个点。试证明三角形 ![ABC](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=ABC) 的内切圆的半径 ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) 满足 ![r\leq\frac{1}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D)


![\sin(A+B)=\sin C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin%28A%2BB%29%3D%5Csin+C) ，同理 ![\sin\frac{A+B}{2}=\cos\frac{C}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BA%2BB%7D%7B2%7D%3D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D)

![BC=2\sin A,CA=2\sin B, AB=2\sin C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=BC%3D2%5Csin+A%2CCA%3D2%5Csin+B%2C+AB%3D2%5Csin+C)

面积有两种表达方法，一种是正弦定理，另外一种通过内接圆半径和周长计算。

前者 ![S=\frac{1}{2}\sin A\cdot AB\cdot AC=2\sin A \sin B\sin C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin+A%5Ccdot+AB%5Ccdot+AC%3D2%5Csin+A+%5Csin+B%5Csin+C)

后者 ![S=\frac{r}{2}(AB+BC+CA)=r(\sin A+\sin B+\sin C)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%3D%5Cfrac%7Br%7D%7B2%7D%28AB%2BBC%2BCA%29%3Dr%28%5Csin+A%2B%5Csin+B%2B%5Csin+C%29)

整理得到 ![r=\frac{2\sin A\sin B\sin C}{\sin A+\sin B+\sin C}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%5Csin+A%5Csin+B%5Csin+C%7D%7B%5Csin+A%2B%5Csin+B%2B%5Csin+C%7D)

要证明 ![r\leq\frac{1}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D) ，也就是证明 ![\frac{2\sin A\sin B\sin C}{\sin A+\sin B+\sin C}\leq\frac{1}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2%5Csin+A%5Csin+B%5Csin+C%7D%7B%5Csin+A%2B%5Csin+B%2B%5Csin+C%7D%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D)

也就是证明 ![4\sin A\sin B\sin C\leq\sin A+\sin B+\sin C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=4%5Csin+A%5Csin+B%5Csin+C%5Cleq%5Csin+A%2B%5Csin+B%2B%5Csin+C)

利用和差化积，积化和差，和最开始讨论的公式对右侧进行变形

![\sin A+\sin B+\sin C=2\sin\frac{A+B}{2}\cos\frac{A-B}{2}+2\sin\frac{C}{2}\cos\frac{C}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin+A%2B%5Csin+B%2B%5Csin+C%3D2%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BA%2BB%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D%2B2%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D)

![=2\cos\frac{C}{2}\cos\frac{A-B}{2}+2\sin\frac{C}{2}\cos\frac{C}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D2%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D%2B2%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D)

![=2\cos\frac{C}{2}\cos\frac{A-B}{2}+2\cos\frac{A+B}{2}\cos\frac{C}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D2%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D%2B2%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA%2BB%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D)

![=2\cos\frac{C}{2}(\cos\frac{A-B}{2}+\cos\frac{A+B}{2})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D2%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%28%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D%2B%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA%2BB%7D%7B2%7D%29)

![=4\cos\frac{A}{2}\cos\frac{B}{2}\cos\frac{C}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D4%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BB%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D)

另外 ![\sin A\sin B \sin C=8\sin\frac{A}{2}\cos\frac{A}{2}\sin\frac{B}{2}\cos\frac{B}{2}\sin\frac{C}{2}\cos\frac{A}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin+A%5Csin+B+%5Csin+C%3D8%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BA%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BB%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BB%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA%7D%7B2%7D)

约去一样的东西（注意符号是正的），原题等价于证明 ![\sin\frac{A}{2}\sin\frac{B}{2}\sin\frac{C}{2}\leq\frac{1}{8}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BA%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BB%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B8%7D)

继续利用和差化积变形并配方， ![\sin\frac{A}{2}\sin\frac{B}{2}\sin\frac{C}{2}=\frac{1}{2}(\cos\frac{A-B}{2}-\cos\frac{A+B}{2})\sin\frac{C}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BA%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BB%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%28%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D-%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA%2BB%7D%7B2%7D%29%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D)

![=\frac{1}{2}(\cos\frac{A-B}{2}-\sin\frac{C}{2})\sin\frac{C}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%28%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D-%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D%29%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D)

![=\frac{1}{8}\cos^2\frac{A-B}{2}-\frac{1}{2}(\sin\frac{C}{2}-\frac{1}{2}\cos\frac{A-B}{2})^2\leq\frac{1}{8}\cos^2\frac{A-B}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B8%7D%5Ccos%5E2%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%28%5Csin%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7B2%7D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D%29%5E2%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B8%7D%5Ccos%5E2%5Cfrac%7BA-B%7D%7B2%7D)

![\leq\frac{1}{8}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B8%7D)
