自然想到积分，也就是Wallis（华莱士）公式

通过部分积分法，我们可以得到递推公式

记 ![I_n=\int_0^\frac{\pi}{2}\sin^nxdx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_n%3D%5Cint_0%5E%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin%5Enxdx) ，其中 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 是自然数

![I_n=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^nxdx=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}(-\cos x)\prime \sin^{n-1}xdx=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\cos x(\sin^{n-1}x)\prime dx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_n%3D%5Cint_0%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%7D%5Csin%5Enxdx%3D%5Cint_0%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%7D%28-%5Ccos+x%29%5Cprime+%5Csin%5E%7Bn-1%7Dxdx%3D%5Cint_0%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%7D%5Ccos+x%28%5Csin%5E%7Bn-1%7Dx%29%5Cprime+dx)

![=(n-1)\int_0^\frac{\pi}{2}\cos^2x\sin^{n-2}xdx=(n-1)\int_0^\frac{\pi}{2}\sin^{n-2}x-\sin^nxdx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%28n-1%29%5Cint_0%5E%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%5Ccos%5E2x%5Csin%5E%7Bn-2%7Dxdx%3D%28n-1%29%5Cint_0%5E%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%5Csin%5E%7Bn-2%7Dx-%5Csin%5Enxdx)

所以 ![I_n=(n-1)(I_{n-2}-I_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_n%3D%28n-1%29%28I_%7Bn-2%7D-I_n%29)

也就是 ![I_n=\frac{n-1}{n}I_{n-2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_n%3D%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7Bn%7DI_%7Bn-2%7D)

由于我们知道只有当 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 为偶数时，积分结果才会出现 ![\pi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi)

![=\frac{(2n-1)(2n-3)\cdot\cdot\cdot3\cdot1}{2n(2n-2)(2n-4)\cdot\cdot\cdot4\cdot2}\cdot \frac{\pi}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B%282n-1%29%282n-3%29%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot3%5Ccdot1%7D%7B2n%282n-2%29%282n-4%29%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot4%5Ccdot2%7D%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D)

注意题目中要凑出 ![(2n)!](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%282n%29%21) 的形式，所以在分子分母上同时乘 ![2n(2n-2)(2n-4)\cdot\cdot\cdot4\cdot2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2n%282n-2%29%282n-4%29%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot4%5Ccdot2) 的话，就可以使分子变成我们想要的形式

也就是 ![\lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{a_n}{a_{2n}}=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Ba_n%7D%7Ba_%7B2n%7D%7D%3D1)


第四问

最后把 ![a_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n) 和 ![a_{2n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7B2n%7D) 代入

![\frac{a_n}{a_{2n}}=\frac{n!}{\sqrt{n}n^ne^{-n}}\cdot\frac{\sqrt{2n}(2n)^{2n}e^{-2n}}{(2n)!}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba_n%7D%7Ba_%7B2n%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bn%21%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7Dn%5Ene%5E%7B-n%7D%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2n%7D%282n%29%5E%7B2n%7De%5E%7B-2n%7D%7D%7B%282n%29%21%7D)

约分，并考虑凑出 ![b_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_n) 的形式

![=\frac{\sqrt{2n}e^{-n}(n!)^22^{2n}n^n}{(2n)!n!\sqrt n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2n%7De%5E%7B-n%7D%28n%21%29%5E22%5E%7B2n%7Dn%5En%7D%7B%282n%29%21n%21%5Csqrt+n%7D)

![=b_n\cdot\frac{\sqrt{2n}n^ne^{-n}}{n!}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3Db_n%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2n%7Dn%5Ene%5E%7B-n%7D%7D%7Bn%21%7D)

观察剩下的东西

发现正好和 ![a_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n) 的形式如出一辙

所以 ![\frac{a_n}{a_{2n}}=\frac{\sqrt2b_n}{a_n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba_n%7D%7Ba_%7B2n%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt2b_n%7D%7Ba_n%7D)

也就是 ![\lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{a_n}{a_{2n}}=\lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{\sqrt2b_n}{a_n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Ba_n%7D%7Ba_%7B2n%7D%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt2b_n%7D%7Ba_n%7D)

把第一问以及第三问的结果代入

可得

![\lim_{n \to \infty}a_n=\sqrt{2\pi}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7Da_n%3D%5Csqrt%7B2%5Cpi%7D)
