# 利用书上的导数定义解决高考导数题 - 知乎
# 

在笔者印象中，高中数学书中给出的唯一一个极限，也就是高考中可以用的极限大概就只有下面这个式子了。

这个式子是为了讲导数的定义才在书中出现的，但其他关于极限的式子一个都没有……

![f\prime(x_1)=\lim_{x_2 \to x_1}\frac{f(x_2)-f(x_1)}{x_2-x_1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cprime%28x_1%29%3D%5Clim_%7Bx_2+%5Cto+x_1%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x_2%29-f%28x_1%29%7D%7Bx_2-x_1%7D)

也就是导数定义式。

既然书上给了，我们为什么不好好利用一下这个式子呢？

下面我来展现一下这个式子在高考导数题中怎么使用


![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-25fb03e6fea1dbbecd228d0b8a3e8f78_b.jpg)

前面的步骤是铺垫，就不细说了


> 分参得到 ![k<1-\frac{2x\ln x}{x^2-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3C1-%5Cfrac%7B2x%5Cln+x%7D%7Bx%5E2-1%7D) ，接着求导 ![(\frac{2x\ln x}{x^2-1})\prime=\frac{2[x^2(1-\ln x)-(1+\ln x)]}{(x^2-1)^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cfrac%7B2x%5Cln+x%7D%7Bx%5E2-1%7D%29%5Cprime%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%5Bx%5E2%281-%5Cln+x%29-%281%2B%5Cln+x%29%5D%7D%7B%28x%5E2-1%29%5E2%7D)
当 ![x<1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3C1) 时括号内 ![x^2(1-\ln x)>0, \;(1+\ln x)<0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E2%281-%5Cln+x%29%3E0%2C+%5C%3B%281%2B%5Cln+x%29%3C0) ，所以恒为正
当 ![x>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3E1) 时同理，恒为负。根据增减性可知，本题等同于求 ![\lim_{x\to1}\;1-\frac{2x\ln x}{x^2-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx%5Cto1%7D%5C%3B1-%5Cfrac%7B2x%5Cln+x%7D%7Bx%5E2-1%7D) 的值

## 这里为了求这个极限开始用导数的定义

为了凑成![f\prime(x_1)=\lim_{x_2 \to x_1}\frac{f(x_2)-f(x_1)}{x_2-x_1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cprime%28x_1%29%3D%5Clim_%7Bx_2+%5Cto+x_1%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x_2%29-f%28x_1%29%7D%7Bx_2-x_1%7D)的形式，我们可以设 ![f(x)=\sqrt x\ln x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D%5Csqrt+x%5Cln+x)

那么这里 ![f(1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%281%29%3D0) ， ![f\prime(x)=\frac{\ln x}{2\sqrt x}+\frac{1}{\sqrt x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cprime%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cln+x%7D%7B2%5Csqrt+x%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt+x%7D) ，注意代换 ![t=x^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3Dx%5E2)

![\lim_{x\to1}\frac{2x\ln x}{x^2-1}=\lim_{x\to1}\frac{2x\ln x-0}{x^2-1}=\lim_{t\to1}\frac{f(t)-f(1)}{t-1}=f\prime(1)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx%5Cto1%7D%5Cfrac%7B2x%5Cln+x%7D%7Bx%5E2-1%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bx%5Cto1%7D%5Cfrac%7B2x%5Cln+x-0%7D%7Bx%5E2-1%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bt%5Cto1%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28t%29-f%281%29%7D%7Bt-1%7D%3Df%5Cprime%281%29%3D1)

所以 ![\lim_{x\to1}\;1-\frac{2x\ln x}{x^2-1}=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx%5Cto1%7D%5C%3B1-%5Cfrac%7B2x%5Cln+x%7D%7Bx%5E2-1%7D%3D0) ，所以最后答案是 ![k\leq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cleq0)



同样这个方法也可以用课本中（也就是高考中能用）的知识，直接破解很多难以解决的极限

比如 ![\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csin+x%7D%7Bx%7D) ， ![\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{e^x-1}{x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7Be%5Ex-1%7D%7Bx%7D) ，…………


对于 ![f(x)=\sin x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D%5Csin+x)

![\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin x-0}{x-0}=f\prime(0)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csin+x%7D%7Bx%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csin+x-0%7D%7Bx-0%7D%3Df%5Cprime%280%29%3D1)

对于 ![f(x)=e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3De%5Ex)

![\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{e^x-1}{x}=\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{e^x-1}{x-0}=f\prime(0)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7Be%5Ex-1%7D%7Bx%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7Be%5Ex-1%7D%7Bx-0%7D%3Df%5Cprime%280%29%3D1)

可以看到，这个方法能够完美绕开洛必达法则

这里最后出一道思考题

![\lim_{x \to 2}\frac{\ln\frac{x}{2}}{x-2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+2%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cln%5Cfrac%7Bx%7D%7B2%7D%7D%7Bx-2%7D)

这个该如何用导数的定义解呢？





答案：

设 ![f(x)=\ln x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D%5Cln+x)

![\lim_{x \to 2}\frac{\ln\frac{x}{2}}{x-2}=\lim_{x \to 2}\frac{\ln x-\ln 2}{x-2}=f\prime(2)=\frac{1}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+2%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cln%5Cfrac%7Bx%7D%7B2%7D%7D%7Bx-2%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bx+%5Cto+2%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cln+x-%5Cln+2%7D%7Bx-2%7D%3Df%5Cprime%282%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D)
