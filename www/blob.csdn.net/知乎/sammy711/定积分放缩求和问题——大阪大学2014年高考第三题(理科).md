翻译过来： 求![\sum_{n=1}^{40000}\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%7B40000%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D) 的整数部分

一道典型的积分放缩题，对于对定积分有一定理解的同学来说应该是正常难度




————————————————————————————————————



![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-8f23b6534a3342455cdc46d487046f93_b.png)

在画出 ![\frac{1}{\sqrt{1}},\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}},\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}.....\frac{1}{\sqrt{n-1}},\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B1%7D%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B3%7D%7D.....%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn-1%7D%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D)

图中涂成黄色的部分就是我们要求的和 ![\sum\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D)

很明显，（**横坐标从0到n的红色曲线的下方的面积**）

<**黄色部分的面积**

<1+（**横坐标从1到n的蓝色曲线下方的面积**）

那么现在就可以计算了

![\int_0^n\sqrt{\frac{1}{x+1}}dx<\sum_1^n\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}<1+\int_1^n\sqrt{\frac{1}{x}}dx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5En%5Csqrt%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%2B1%7D%7Ddx%3C%5Csum_1%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D%3C1%2B%5Cint_1%5En%5Csqrt%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%7D%7Ddx)

本题里n的值是40000

所以 ![\int_0^{40000}\sqrt{\frac{1}{x+1}}dx<\sum_1^{40000}\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}<1+\int_1^{40000}\sqrt{\frac{1}{x}}dx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5E%7B40000%7D%5Csqrt%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%2B1%7D%7Ddx%3C%5Csum_1%5E%7B40000%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D%3C1%2B%5Cint_1%5E%7B40000%7D%5Csqrt%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%7D%7Ddx)

积出来 ![2\sqrt{40001}-2<\sum_0^{40000}\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}<1+400-2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Csqrt%7B40001%7D-2%3C%5Csum_0%5E%7B40000%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D%3C1%2B400-2)

![2\sqrt{40001}-2<\sum_0^{40000}\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}<399](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Csqrt%7B40001%7D-2%3C%5Csum_0%5E%7B40000%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D%3C399)

接下来比较 ![398](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=398)  与 ![2\sqrt{40001}-2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Csqrt%7B40001%7D-2) 的大小

![2\sqrt{40001}-2-398=2(\sqrt{40001}-200)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Csqrt%7B40001%7D-2-398%3D2%28%5Csqrt%7B40001%7D-200%29%3E0)

因此 ![398<2\sqrt{40001}-2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=398%3C2%5Csqrt%7B40001%7D-2)

![398<\sum_0^{40000}\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}<399](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=398%3C%5Csum_0%5E%7B40000%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D%3C399)

答： ![\sum_0^{40000}\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_0%5E%7B40000%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D) 的整数部分为398
