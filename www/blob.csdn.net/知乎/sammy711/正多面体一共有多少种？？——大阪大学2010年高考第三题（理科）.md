![(\frac{n}{m}-\frac{n}{2}+1)\;l=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7Bm%7D-%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7B2%7D%2B1%29%5C%3Bl%3D2)

题目背景：

欧拉定理告诉我们，对于多面体有 ![V+F-E=X(P)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%2BF-E%3DX%28P%29) 成立

![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 是顶点的个数， ![F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F) 是面数， ![E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E) 是棱数。 ![X(P)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%28P%29) 是 ![P ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P+) 的拓扑不变量，对于简单多面体来说， ![X(P)=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%28P%29%3D2)，也就是 

![V-E+F=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V-E%2BF%3D2)

设正多面体有 ![l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l) 个正 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 边形的面，每个顶点被 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 条线段所共有。

那么顶点的个数就是 ![\frac{nl}{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bnl%7D%7Bm%7D) （每一个正多边形分开算，一共有 ![nl](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=nl) 个顶点，但实际上每个顶点被 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 个线段共有）

面的个数是 ![l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l)

棱的个数是 ![\frac{nl}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bnl%7D%7B2%7D) （分开算是 ![nl](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=nl) ，但每个棱被两个面共有）

所以就有

![\frac{nl}{m}-\frac{nl}{2}+l=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bnl%7D%7Bm%7D-%5Cfrac%7Bnl%7D%7B2%7D%2Bl%3D2)

也就是 ![(\frac{n}{m}-\frac{n}{2}+1)\;l=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7Bm%7D-%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7B2%7D%2B1%29%5C%3Bl%3D2) ，其中 ![m,n,l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2Cn%2Cl) 是整数

接下来只要解 ![l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l) ，就可以知道正多面体一共有多少种了。

—————————————————————————————————————————

作为数论是很简单的题，但这道题出在试卷上的背景，其实是正多面体问题。

笔者很喜欢这种虽然简单但是很有意思的题。

—————————————————————————————————————————

![(\frac{n}{m}-\frac{n}{2}+1)\;l=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7Bm%7D-%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7B2%7D%2B1%29%5C%3Bl%3D2)

先去掉分数

![(2n-mn+2m)\;l=4m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%282n-mn%2B2m%29%5C%3Bl%3D4m)

然后合并同类项

![(m-2)nl=2(l-2)m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28m-2%29nl%3D2%28l-2%29m)

![\frac{2m}{m-2}=\frac{nl}{(l-2)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bnl%7D%7B%28l-2%29%7D)


- 从 ![m=3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D3) 开始枚举

![6(l-2)=nl](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=6%28l-2%29%3Dnl)

![n=6-\frac{12}{l}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D6-%5Cfrac%7B12%7D%7Bl%7D) ，考虑到 ![m,n,l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2Cn%2Cl) 是大于等于 ![3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3) 的整数，另外对 ![12](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=12) 因数分解，解得

![(m,n,l)=(3,3,4)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28m%2Cn%2Cl%29%3D%283%2C3%2C4%29)

![(m,n,l)=(3,4,6)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28m%2Cn%2Cl%29%3D%283%2C4%2C6%29)

![(m,n,l)=(3,5,12)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28m%2Cn%2Cl%29%3D%283%2C5%2C12%29)
- ![m=4](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D4) 时

![4(l-2)=nl](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=4%28l-2%29%3Dnl)

![n=4-\frac{8}{l}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D4-%5Cfrac%7B8%7D%7Bl%7D) ，同样对 ![8](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=8) 分解

![(m,n,l)=(4,3,8)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28m%2Cn%2Cl%29%3D%284%2C3%2C8%29)
- ![m=5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D5) 时

![\frac{10}{3}(l-2)=nl](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B10%7D%7B3%7D%28l-2%29%3Dnl)

![n=\frac{10}{3}-\frac{20}{3l}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D%5Cfrac%7B10%7D%7B3%7D-%5Cfrac%7B20%7D%7B3l%7D)

注意 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 的范围， ![n=3,l=20](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D3%2Cl%3D20) 是唯一解

![(m,n,l)=(5,3,20)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28m%2Cn%2Cl%29%3D%285%2C3%2C20%29)
- ![m\geq6](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5Cgeq6) 时

![\frac{2m}{m-2}(l-2)=nl](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D%28l-2%29%3Dnl)

![n=\frac{2m}{m-2}-\frac{2m}{m-2}\cdot\frac{2}{l}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D-%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7Bl%7D)

注意， ![\frac{2m}{m-2}=\frac{2m-4+4}{m-2}=2+\frac{4}{m-2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B2m-4%2B4%7D%7Bm-2%7D%3D2%2B%5Cfrac%7B4%7D%7Bm-2%7D)

当 ![m\geq6](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5Cgeq6) 的时候 ![\frac{2m}{m-2}\leq3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D%5Cleq3)

然而 ![\frac{2m}{m-2}\cdot\frac{2}{l}>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7Bl%7D%3E0) 恒成立

所以 ![\frac{2m}{m-2}-\frac{2m}{m-2}\cdot\frac{2}{l}<3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D-%5Cfrac%7B2m%7D%7Bm-2%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7Bl%7D%3C3) ，也就是 ![m\geq6](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5Cgeq6) 时 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 没有正整数解。


答：解有
