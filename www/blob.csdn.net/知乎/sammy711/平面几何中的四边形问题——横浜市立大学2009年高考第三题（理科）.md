本题用梅涅劳斯定理很简单的哦~

延长 ![BC,AD;BA,CD](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=BC%2CAD%3BBA%2CCD)

![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-da180ad531eb26728f94cc29a07cd499_b.png)
考虑 ![AB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AB) 的中点 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) ，我们知道 ![EAcos\theta=AP](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=EAcos%5Ctheta%3DAP) 。又因为 ![EA=EB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=EA%3DEB)

所以 ![EA=EB=\frac{a}{2cos\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=EA%3DEB%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D)

同理可得 ![FB=\frac{b}{2cos\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=FB%3D%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D)

在三角形 ![BCF](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=BCF) 中，根据梅涅劳斯定理有

![\frac{\frac{a}{2cos\theta}}{\frac{a}{2cos\theta}-b}\cdot\frac{c}{\frac{b}{2cos\theta}-c}\cdot\frac{\frac{b}{2cos\theta}-a}{a}=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D-b%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7Bc%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D-c%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D-a%7D%7Ba%7D%3D1)

![\frac{c}{2cos\theta}(\frac{b}{2cos\theta}-a)=(\frac{a}{2cos\theta}-b)(\frac{b}{2cos\theta}-c)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bc%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D%28%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D-a%29%3D%28%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D-b%29%28%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D-c%29)

![bc-2cos\theta ac=ab-2b^2cos\theta-2cos\theta ac+4cos^2\theta bc](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=bc-2cos%5Ctheta+ac%3Dab-2b%5E2cos%5Ctheta-2cos%5Ctheta+ac%2B4cos%5E2%5Ctheta+bc)

整理

![c=a-2bcos\theta+4cos^2\theta c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%3Da-2bcos%5Ctheta%2B4cos%5E2%5Ctheta+c)

![c=\frac{a-2bcos\theta}{1-4cos^2\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba-2bcos%5Ctheta%7D%7B1-4cos%5E2%5Ctheta%7D)

注：所给的条件 ![a<\frac{b}{2cos\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3C%5Cfrac%7Bb%7D%7B2cos%5Ctheta%7D) 是为了保证线段 ![AB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AB) 和 ![BC](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=BC) 不会相交。

（2）

设 ![cos\theta=t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=cos%5Ctheta%3Dt)

则 ![c=\frac{a-2bt}{1-4t^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba-2bt%7D%7B1-4t%5E2%7D)

![\frac{dc}{dt}=\frac{-2b(1-4t^2)+8t(a-2bt)}{(1-4t^2)^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdc%7D%7Bdt%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B-2b%281-4t%5E2%29%2B8t%28a-2bt%29%7D%7B%281-4t%5E2%29%5E2%7D)

分母为正，化简分子得到

![-2(4bt^2-4at+b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-2%284bt%5E2-4at%2Bb%29)

考虑到余弦函数的取值范围和增减，当 ![t=\frac{a-\sqrt{a^2-b^2}}{2b}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba-%5Csqrt%7Ba%5E2-b%5E2%7D%7D%7B2b%7D) 时有最小值

计算可得， ![c=\frac{a+\sqrt{a^2-b^2}}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%3D%5Cfrac%7Ba%2B%5Csqrt%7Ba%5E2-b%5E2%7D%7D%7B2%7D)
