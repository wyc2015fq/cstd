- ![f(x^3)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%5E3%29) 可以被![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 整除（多项式的整除）
- ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 的系数 ![a,b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%2Cb) 不能同时为实数

因为 ![f(x^3)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%5E3%29) 能被 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 整除，所以 ![f(x^3)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%5E3%29) 可以写成

![f(x^3)=P(x)f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%5E3%29%3DP%28x%29f%28x%29) ，其中 ![P(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%28x%29) 是四次多项式。

设 ![x^2+ax+b=(x-\alpha)(x-\beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E2%2Bax%2Bb%3D%28x-%5Calpha%29%28x-%5Cbeta%29)

因为 ![f(\alpha)=f(\beta)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Calpha%29%3Df%28%5Cbeta%29%3D0) ，而且 ![f(x^3)=P(x)f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%5E3%29%3DP%28x%29f%28x%29)

所以 ![f(\alpha^3),f(\beta^3)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Calpha%5E3%29%2Cf%28%5Cbeta%5E3%29) 也为 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0)

但 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 的解只有![\alpha,\beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta) 两个

所以 ![ \alpha^3,\beta^3 \in \left\{ \alpha,\beta \right\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Calpha%5E3%2C%5Cbeta%5E3+%5Cin+%5Cleft%5C%7B+%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta+%5Cright%5C%7D)
