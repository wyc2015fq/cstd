翻译过来就是问

边长为1的正方体沿对角线旋转得到的图形，体积为多少。

类似的问题在东京工业大学1993年高考中也有出现

![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3d9a59001f9e90c823d3fabc10e86a24_b.jpg)

[正方体绕其体对角线旋转一周得到的几何体是啥样的？](https://www.zhihu.com/question/24201486/answer/124561826)


![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/50fe8aa37f1cc1a7d1fec409124e9b90_b.jpg)
假定正方体沿对角线OF旋转

由于正方体的对称性，我们可以只考虑半个对角线

现在我们需要寻找的就是 垂直于对角线且过点![(t,t,t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t%2Ct%2Ct%29)的平面与正方体交面的情况

易知，这个平面与x y z轴的交点坐标为 ![(3t ,0,0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%283t+%2C0%2C0%29)![(0,3t,0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%280%2C3t%2C0%29)![(0,0,3t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%280%2C0%2C3t%29)

记这三个交点为![M,N,P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%2CN%2CP)

①

当![t<\frac{1}{3} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D+)时 这个平面与正方体的交面是一个正三角形，如图![\triangle QRS](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctriangle+QRS)

②当![\frac{1}{3} <t<\frac{1}{2} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D+%3Ct%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D+)时，这个平面与正方体的交面的话

记这个平面的表达式为 ![x+y+z=3t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%2By%2Bz%3D3t)

带入各边表达式，如线段![AB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AB)的表达式

![x=1,z=0,0<y<1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D1%2Cz%3D0%2C0%3Cy%3C1)

解得

![y=3t-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3D3t-1)，![\frac{1}{3} <t<\frac{2}{3} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D+%3Ct%3C%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B3%7D+)

考虑到![\frac{1}{3} <t<\frac{1}{2} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D+%3Ct%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D+)，此平面与![AB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AB)有交点![(1,3t-1,0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%2C3t-1%2C0%29)，在图中画出

带入各边方程可知，在![\frac{1}{3} <t<\frac{1}{2} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D+%3Ct%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D+)范围内有交点的只有![AB,BC,CG,GD,DE,EA](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AB%2CBC%2CCG%2CGD%2CDE%2CEA)六条线段，

————————————————————————————————

对①来说，点![(t,t,t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t%2Ct%2Ct%29)到这个三角形的最远距离是三个顶点

![V_{1} =\pi \int_{0}^{\frac{1}{3} } ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B1%7D+%3D%5Cpi+%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D+%7D+)![(3t-t)^2+(t-0)^2+(t-0)^2 d(\sqrt{3}t) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%283t-t%29%5E2%2B%28t-0%29%5E2%2B%28t-0%29%5E2+d%28%5Csqrt%7B3%7Dt%29+)

![=\pi \int_{0}^{\frac{1}{3} } ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cpi+%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D+%7D+)![6\sqrt{3} t^2dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=6%5Csqrt%7B3%7D+t%5E2dt)

=![2\sqrt{3} \pi(\frac{1}{3})^3 =\frac{2}{27}\sqrt{3} \pi ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Csqrt%7B3%7D+%5Cpi%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D%29%5E3+%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B27%7D%5Csqrt%7B3%7D+%5Cpi+)

其中![\sqrt{3}t ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B3%7Dt+)是点O到![(t,t,t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t%2Ct%2Ct%29)的距离

对②来说 距离最远的点是六边形的六个顶点

计算发现，![(t,t,t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t%2Ct%2Ct%29)到六个顶点的距离相同，事实上截面是一个以![(t,t,t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t%2Ct%2Ct%29)为中心的六边形

![V_{2} =\pi \int_{\frac{1}{3}}^{\frac{1}{2} } ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7B2%7D+%3D%5Cpi+%5Cint_%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D%7D%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D+%7D+)![(t-1)^2+[(3t-1)-t]^2+(t-0)^2 d(\sqrt{3}t) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t-1%29%5E2%2B%5B%283t-1%29-t%5D%5E2%2B%28t-0%29%5E2+d%28%5Csqrt%7B3%7Dt%29+)

![=\sqrt{3}\pi \int_{\frac{1}{3}}^{\frac{1}{2} } ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Csqrt%7B3%7D%5Cpi+%5Cint_%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D%7D%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D+%7D+)![6t^2-6t+2 ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=6t%5E2-6t%2B2+)![dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dt)

=![\frac{5}{54} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B5%7D%7B54%7D+)![\sqrt{3}\pi ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B3%7D%5Cpi+)

所以![V=2V_{1} +2V_{2}=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{3}\pi ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%3D2V_%7B1%7D+%2B2V_%7B2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B3%7D%7D%7B3%7D%5Cpi+)
