这题做到最后一步不知道怎么判断的人很多……

思路在于函数的凹凸性

下面是函数 ![y=e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3De%5Ex) 的图像

我们知道 ![e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex) 是下凸函数(二阶导数恒大于0)，所以在某点 ![(t,e^t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t%2Ce%5Et%29) 引出的切线 ![g(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29) 有 ![g(x)<e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29%3Ce%5Ex) 恒成立

通俗的说，就是切线永远在原函数下方

这里开始的步骤就是国内高考压轴导数题的惯用套路了


具体做法

记从点 ![(t,e^t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t%2Ce%5Et%29) 引出的切线为 ![g(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29)

设 ![e^x-g(x)=h(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex-g%28x%29%3Dh%28x%29)

![h^\prime(x)=e^x-g^\prime(x)=e^x-C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5E%5Cprime%28x%29%3De%5Ex-g%5E%5Cprime%28x%29%3De%5Ex-C) ，其中C为切线的斜率

由于切线是从 ![(t,e^t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28t%2Ce%5Et%29) 引的，因此C就是 ![e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex) 在 ![x=t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3Dt) 处的导数

我们知道， ![(e^x)^\prime=e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28e%5Ex%29%5E%5Cprime%3De%5Ex) ，所以C= ![e^t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Et)

也就是说， ![h^\prime(x)=e^x-e^t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5E%5Cprime%28x%29%3De%5Ex-e%5Et)

再次求导， ![h^{\prime\prime}(x)=e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5E%7B%5Cprime%5Cprime%7D%28x%29%3De%5Ex)

![h^{\prime\prime}(x)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5E%7B%5Cprime%5Cprime%7D%28x%29%3E0) 恒成立，且 ![h^\prime(t)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5E%5Cprime%28t%29%3D0)

因此当 ![x>t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3Et) 的时候， ![h^\prime(x)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5E%5Cprime%28x%29%3E0)

![h(t)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%28t%29%3D0) （因为是从 ![x=t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3Dt) 引的切线）

因此当 ![x>t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3Et) 的时候， ![h(x)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%28x%29%3E0)

在本题中，为了方便计算，我们设 ![t=3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D3) ，那么我们想要求的 ![x=\pi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D%5Cpi) 处的值有如下关系

![e^\pi-g(\pi)>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%5Cpi-g%28%5Cpi%29%3E0)

函数 ![g(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29) 是 ![e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex) 在![(3,e^3)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%283%2Ce%5E3%29) 处引出的切线，斜率C为 ![e^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E3)

因此 ![g(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29) 的表达式为 ![g(x)=e^3(x-3)+e^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29%3De%5E3%28x-3%29%2Be%5E3)

![x=\pi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D%5Cpi) 时，可得

![e^\pi-e^3\times(\pi-3)-e^3>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%5Cpi-e%5E3%5Ctimes%28%5Cpi-3%29-e%5E3%3E0)

![e^\pi>\pi e^3-2e^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%5Cpi%3E%5Cpi+e%5E3-2e%5E3)

在这里就可以放缩了， ![\pi=3.1,e=2.7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi%3D3.1%2Ce%3D2.7)

![e^\pi>(3.1-2)\times2.7^3=1.1\times2.7^3=21.6513](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%5Cpi%3E%283.1-2%29%5Ctimes2.7%5E3%3D1.1%5Ctimes2.7%5E3%3D21.6513)

因此 ![e^\pi>21](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%5Cpi%3E21) 成立

证毕！

简单来说，本题的思路就是寻找一个函数，它能够在 ![x=\pi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D%5Cpi) 的时候大于21，又能使得这个函数可以计算（不无理数次方）

这里，我们选择的就是 ![e^x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex) 在 ![x=3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D3) 处的切线，就是图里的那条切线
