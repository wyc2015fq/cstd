> 2. 考虑椭圆 ![C:\frac{x^2}{a^2}+\frac{y^2}{b^2}=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%3A%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E2%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7By%5E2%7D%7Bb%5E2%7D%3D1) ，其中 ![a+b=1,a>0,b>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%2Bb%3D1%2Ca%3E0%2Cb%3E0)
试求当 ![a,b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%2Cb) 变化时，椭圆 ![C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C) 扫过的区域。

（本题面向国内高中生，放到国内高考可以作为圆锥曲线压轴题。）

和滑落的棒那题一样，固定 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 求 ![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) 的最值。

首先把 ![b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b) 消去

![C:\frac{x^2}{a^2}+\frac{y^2}{(1-a)^2}=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%3A%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E2%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7By%5E2%7D%7B%281-a%29%5E2%7D%3D1)

![y^2=(a^2-2a+1)(1-\frac{x^2}{a^2})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5E2%3D%28a%5E2-2a%2B1%29%281-%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E2%7D%29)

设 ![f(a)=(a^2-2a+1)(1-\frac{x^2}{a^2})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28a%29%3D%28a%5E2-2a%2B1%29%281-%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E2%7D%29)

那么 ![f\prime(a)=(2a-2)\cdot(1-\frac{x^2}{a^2})+(a^2-2a+1)\cdot\frac{2x^2}{a^3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cprime%28a%29%3D%282a-2%29%5Ccdot%281-%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E2%7D%29%2B%28a%5E2-2a%2B1%29%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B2x%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E3%7D)

![=(a-1)(2-\frac{2x^2}{a^2}+\frac{2x^2}{a^2}-\frac{2x^2}{a^3})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%28a-1%29%282-%5Cfrac%7B2x%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E2%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B2x%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E2%7D-%5Cfrac%7B2x%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E3%7D%29)

![=2(a-1)\cdot(1-\frac{x^2}{a^3})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D2%28a-1%29%5Ccdot%281-%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7Ba%5E3%7D%29)

所以 ![x^2=a^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E2%3Da%5E3) 时， ![y^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5E2) 有最值

计算可得此时 ![y^2=(1-a)^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5E2%3D%281-a%29%5E3)

易知此时 ![x^\frac{2}{3}+y^\frac{2}{3}=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B3%7D%2By%5E%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B3%7D%3D1)

![x^2+y^2+1-2\sqrt{x^2+y^2}=x^2+y^2-2ax-2y+a^2+1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E2%2By%5E2%2B1-2%5Csqrt%7Bx%5E2%2By%5E2%7D%3Dx%5E2%2By%5E2-2ax-2y%2Ba%5E2%2B1)

![4x^2+4y^2=4a^2x^2+4y^2+a^4+8axy-4a^2y-4a^3x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=4x%5E2%2B4y%5E2%3D4a%5E2x%5E2%2B4y%5E2%2Ba%5E4%2B8axy-4a%5E2y-4a%5E3x)

另外 ![2\sqrt{x^2+y^2}=2ax+2y-a^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Csqrt%7Bx%5E2%2By%5E2%7D%3D2ax%2B2y-a%5E2) ，所以等式右侧为非负

第三问：

观察分子分母有相同的部分

设 ![t(t-2)=m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%28t-2%29%3Dm) ，其中 ![-1\leq m<0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1%5Cleq+m%3C0)

那么 ![y=g(m)=\frac{m^2x^2-4}{4m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dg%28m%29%3D%5Cfrac%7Bm%5E2x%5E2-4%7D%7B4m%7D)

固定 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x)  求 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 变化时 ![y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y) 的最大值

![g\prime(m)=\frac{2mx^2\cdot4m-(m^2x^2-4)\cdot4}{16m^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%5Cprime%28m%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B2mx%5E2%5Ccdot4m-%28m%5E2x%5E2-4%29%5Ccdot4%7D%7B16m%5E2%7D)

![=\frac{m^2x^2+4}{4m^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7Bm%5E2x%5E2%2B4%7D%7B4m%5E2%7D)

![g(-1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28-1%29) 是最小值 ![g(-1)=1-\frac{x^2}{4}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28-1%29%3D1-%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7B4%7D)
