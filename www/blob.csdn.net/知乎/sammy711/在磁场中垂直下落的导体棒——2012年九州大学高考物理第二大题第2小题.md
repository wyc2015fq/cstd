在回路中另有一个导体棒 ![XY](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=XY) ，其长度和 ![ps](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=ps) 边相同。导体棒的质量为 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) ，长度和 ![ps](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=ps) 一样为 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 。设本题中导体棒可以垂直无摩擦地上下无摩擦地移动并保持和电路接触。

另外，整个电路的平面内有从纸外朝向纸内方向的磁场。磁感应强度为 ![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 。

![t=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D0) 时，导体棒从电路中的某处开始垂直下落。计此时导体棒的位置为 ![x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0) ，并规定朝下为正。

设时间 ![t\;(t>0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%5C%3B%28t%3E0%29) 时，导体棒的速度和加速度分别为 ![v,a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%2Ca) 。另外，重力加速度为 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g)。另外， ![t=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D0)时电路中没有电流。不考虑电场和磁场的溢出部分，电路和导体棒的电阻和空气阻力。

1. 导体棒下落时，由于电磁感应，环路中会有电流产生。请问电流的方向是 ![X \to Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X+%5Cto+Y) 还是 ![Y \to X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Y+%5Cto+X) ？


设流过线圈的电流为 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) ，电容器的电流为 ![I\prime](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%5Cprime)。电流的方向以图中箭头的方向为正

2.考虑由导体棒和电容器组成的电路 ![X \to Y \to s \to p \to X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X+%5Cto+Y+%5Cto+s+%5Cto+p+%5Cto+X)

(a)  设![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 时刻，电容器![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) 侧电极所带的电荷为 ![Q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Q) ，请用 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 和其它需要的物理量表示 ![Q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Q)。

(b)  设从![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 时刻开始经过了很短的时间 ![\Delta t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+t) ，这时 ![Q,v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Q%2Cv) 的变化量分别计作 ![\Delta Q,\Delta v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+Q%2C%5CDelta+v) 。这时设电流的大小 ![I\prime](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%5Cprime) 满足 ![I\prime=\frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta t}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%5Cprime%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5CDelta+Q%7D%7B%5CDelta+t%7D) ，导体棒的加速度 ![a=\frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5CDelta+v%7D%7B%5CDelta+t%7D) 。请用 ![a,B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%2CB) 和其它所需要的物理量表示 ![I\prime](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%5Cprime) 。

3.考虑由导体棒和线圈组成的电路 ![X \to Y \to r \to q \to X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X+%5Cto+Y+%5Cto+r+%5Cto+q+%5Cto+X) 。设从 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 时刻开始经过了很短的时间 ![\Delta t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+t) 。这时 ![I,x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%2Cx) 的变化量分别为 ![\Delta I,\Delta x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+I%2C%5CDelta+x) 。这时速度满足 ![v=\frac{\Delta x}{\Delta t}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%7B%5CDelta+t%7D) 。另外， ![t=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D0) 时 ![I=x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3Dx%3D0)  。请用 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 和其他需要的物理量表示 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 。

6.请用 ![M,g,B,L,C,l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%2Cg%2CB%2CL%2CC%2Cl) 中必要的物理量表示 ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 的最大值和最小值。

1. 不用说，右手楞次定律，方向是 ![X \to Y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X+%5Cto+Y)

2.

(a)感应电动势是 ![BLv](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=BLv) ，电容是 ![C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C) ，所以电荷 ![Q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Q) 就是 ![Blv\cdot C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Blv%5Ccdot+C)

(b) ![\Delta Q=BlC\Delta v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+Q%3DBlC%5CDelta+v)，注意到方向为正

![I\prime=\frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta t}=BlCa](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%5Cprime%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5CDelta+Q%7D%7B%5CDelta+t%7D%3DBlCa)

3.

根据电感的定义式 ，考虑到电流的方向

![\frac{\Delta I}{\Delta t}L=Blv](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5CDelta+I%7D%7B%5CDelta+t%7DL%3DBlv)

两边关于 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 积分，就有

![I=\frac{Bl}{L}x+C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%5Cfrac%7BBl%7D%7BL%7Dx%2BC) （ ![C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C) 为常数）

接着 ![t=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D0) 时， ![I=x=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3Dx%3D0) ，所以 ![C=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%3D0)

所以答案是 ![I=\frac{Bl}{L}x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%5Cfrac%7BBl%7D%7BL%7Dx)

4.

导杆受的力有：重力，安培力，考虑到方向。

所以有 ![Ma=Mg-BI_总\;l,\;\;](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Ma%3DMg-BI_%E6%80%BB%5C%3Bl%2C%5C%3B%5C%3B)

![Ma=Mg-Bl(I+I\prime)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Ma%3DMg-Bl%28I%2BI%5Cprime%29)

5.

带入2和3的结果

![Ma=Mg-B^2l^2(Ca+\frac{x}{L})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Ma%3DMg-B%5E2l%5E2%28Ca%2B%5Cfrac%7Bx%7D%7BL%7D%29)

考虑到是一个二阶的微分方程，我们把 ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 和 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 分到等式两边

![(M+CB^2l^2)\frac{d^2x}{dt^2}=Mg- \frac{B^2l^2}{L}x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28M%2BCB%5E2l%5E2%29%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E2x%7D%7Bdt%5E2%7D%3DMg-+%5Cfrac%7BB%5E2l%5E2%7D%7BL%7Dx)

解这个方程，我们设 ![Mg- \frac{B^2l^2}{L}x=u](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Mg-+%5Cfrac%7BB%5E2l%5E2%7D%7BL%7Dx%3Du)

对 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 微分两次

![- \frac{B^2l^2}{L}\frac{d^2x}{dt^2}=\frac{d^2u}{dt^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-+%5Cfrac%7BB%5E2l%5E2%7D%7BL%7D%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E2x%7D%7Bdt%5E2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E2u%7D%7Bdt%5E2%7D)

原来的微分方程变为

![(M+CB^2l^2)\frac{d^2u}{dt^2}=- \frac{B^2l^2}{L}u](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28M%2BCB%5E2l%5E2%29%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E2u%7D%7Bdt%5E2%7D%3D-+%5Cfrac%7BB%5E2l%5E2%7D%7BL%7Du)

![\frac{d^2u}{dt^2}=- \frac{B^2l^2}{L(M+CB^2l^2)}u](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E2u%7D%7Bdt%5E2%7D%3D-+%5Cfrac%7BB%5E2l%5E2%7D%7BL%28M%2BCB%5E2l%5E2%29%7Du)

所以通解是

![u=Asin(\frac{Bl}{\sqrt{L(M+CB^2l^2)}}t+P)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%3DAsin%28%5Cfrac%7BBl%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7BL%28M%2BCB%5E2l%5E2%29%7D%7Dt%2BP%29) ，其中 ![A,P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CP) 是参数

因此 ![x=(Mg-Asin(\frac{Bl}{\sqrt{L(M+CB^2l^2)}}t+P))\frac{L}{B^2l^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D%28Mg-Asin%28%5Cfrac%7BBl%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7BL%28M%2BCB%5E2l%5E2%29%7D%7Dt%2BP%29%29%5Cfrac%7BL%7D%7BB%5E2l%5E2%7D)

所以角速度 ![\omega=\frac{Bl}{\sqrt{L(M+CB^2l^2)}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Comega%3D%5Cfrac%7BBl%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7BL%28M%2BCB%5E2l%5E2%29%7D%7D)

振动中心处时, ![\frac{d^2x}{dt^2}=\frac{d^2u}{dt^2}=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E2x%7D%7Bdt%5E2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E2u%7D%7Bdt%5E2%7D%3D0) ，根据 ![\frac{d^2u}{dt^2}=- \frac{B^2l^2}{L(M+CB^2l^2)}u](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bd%5E2u%7D%7Bdt%5E2%7D%3D-+%5Cfrac%7BB%5E2l%5E2%7D%7BL%28M%2BCB%5E2l%5E2%29%7Du) ，有 ![u=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%3D0)

所以振动中心的 ![x_0=\frac{MgL}{B^2l^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%3D%5Cfrac%7BMgL%7D%7BB%5E2l%5E2%7D)

6.

因此 ![A=Mg](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3DMg)

![I=(Mg-Mgsin(\frac{Bl}{\sqrt{L(M+CB^2l^2)}}t+P))\frac{1}{Bl}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%28Mg-Mgsin%28%5Cfrac%7BBl%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7BL%28M%2BCB%5E2l%5E2%29%7D%7Dt%2BP%29%29%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7BBl%7D)

根据三角函数的性质，最大值 ![I_M=\frac{2Mg}{Bl}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_M%3D%5Cfrac%7B2Mg%7D%7BBl%7D) ，最小值 ![I_m=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_m%3D0)
