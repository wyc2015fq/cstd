![l_1,l_2,l_3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l_1%2Cl_2%2Cl_3) 是空间中起点均为 ![O](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O) 的三条不同的射线。已知 ![l_1,l_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l_1%2Cl_2) 所成的角和 ![l_1,l_3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l_1%2Cl_3) 所成的角均为 ![\frac{\pi}{3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B3%7D) ， ![l_2,l_3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l_2%2Cl_3) 所成的角为   ![\theta\;\;(0<\theta\leq\frac{2\pi}{3})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctheta%5C%3B%5C%3B%280%3C%5Ctheta%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cpi%7D%7B3%7D%29) 。求满足下面条件的 ![\theta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctheta) 的范围。
- 三条射线上分别存在一个不与 ![O](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O) 重合的点 ![P_1,P_2,P_3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1%2CP_2%2CP_3) ，并且这三个点形成正三角形。


————————————————————————————————————————

设 ![OP_1=x,OP_2=y,OP_3=z](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OP_1%3Dx%2COP_2%3Dy%2COP_3%3Dz)

根据余弦定理

![P_1P_2=\sqrt{x^2+y^2-xy}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1P_2%3D%5Csqrt%7Bx%5E2%2By%5E2-xy%7D)

![P_1P_3=\sqrt{x^2+z^2-xz}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1P_3%3D%5Csqrt%7Bx%5E2%2Bz%5E2-xz%7D)

![P_2P_3=\sqrt{y^2+z^2-2yz\cos\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_2P_3%3D%5Csqrt%7By%5E2%2Bz%5E2-2yz%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D)

先考虑第一个和第二个相等的条件

![y^2-xy=z^2-xz](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5E2-xy%3Dz%5E2-xz)

![(y-z)(y+z-x)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28y-z%29%28y%2Bz-x%29%3D0)

分两种情况讨论

- ![y=z](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dz) 时

![P_2P_3=\sqrt{2y^2-2y^2\cos\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_2P_3%3D%5Csqrt%7B2y%5E2-2y%5E2%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D)

和第一个方程联立

![y^2-x^2+xy-2y^2\cos\theta=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5E2-x%5E2%2Bxy-2y%5E2%5Ccos%5Ctheta%3D0)

注意到这里式子的次数是平齐的，这里考虑 ![t=\frac{y}{x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D%5Cfrac%7By%7D%7Bx%7D)的存在性，等式两边同时除以 ![x^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E2)

![(1-2\cos\theta)t^2+t-1=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281-2%5Ccos%5Ctheta%29t%5E2%2Bt-1%3D0)

注意到 ![x>0,y>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3E0%2Cy%3E0)

方程应该在 ![t>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3E0) 的范围内有解

也就是方程最大的根要大于零

![1-2\cos\theta>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1-2%5Ccos%5Ctheta%3E0) 时，最大的根为 ![\frac{-1+\sqrt{5-8\cos\theta}}{2-4\cos\theta}>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B-1%2B%5Csqrt%7B5-8%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D%7D%7B2-4%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D%3E0)

解得  ![\cos\theta<\frac{1}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos%5Ctheta%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D)

![1-2\cos\theta<0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1-2%5Ccos%5Ctheta%3C0) 时，最大的根为 ![\frac{-1-\sqrt{5-8\cos\theta}}{2-4\cos\theta}>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B-1-%5Csqrt%7B5-8%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D%7D%7B2-4%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D%3E0)

解得 ![\frac{1}{2}<\cos\theta\leq\frac{5}{8}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%3C%5Ccos%5Ctheta%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B5%7D%7B8%7D)

![1-2\cos\theta=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1-2%5Ccos%5Ctheta%3D0)  时， ![t=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D1) ，成立

解得  ![\cos\theta=\frac{1}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos%5Ctheta%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D)

- ![x=y+z](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3Dy%2Bz) 时

![P_1P_2=\sqrt{x^2+y^2-xy}=\sqrt{y^2+z^2+yz}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1P_2%3D%5Csqrt%7Bx%5E2%2By%5E2-xy%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7By%5E2%2Bz%5E2%2Byz%7D)

![P_2P_3=\sqrt{y^2+z^2-2yz\cos\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_2P_3%3D%5Csqrt%7By%5E2%2Bz%5E2-2yz%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D)

这两个相等，解得 ![\cos\theta=-\frac{1}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos%5Ctheta%3D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D)

另外考虑到 ![0<\theta\leq\frac{2\pi}{3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3C%5Ctheta%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cpi%7D%7B3%7D)

综上所述，结果是

![\arccos\frac{5}{8}\leq\theta\leq\frac{2\pi}{3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Carccos%5Cfrac%7B5%7D%7B8%7D%5Cleq%5Ctheta%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cpi%7D%7B3%7D)
