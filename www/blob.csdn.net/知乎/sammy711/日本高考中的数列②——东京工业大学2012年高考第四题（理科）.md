![=-\frac{1}{n+3}+\frac{n}{2}(\frac{2n+2}{n(n+1)(n+2)})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%2B3%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7B2%7D%28%5Cfrac%7B2n%2B2%7D%7Bn%28n%2B1%29%28n%2B2%29%7D%29)

第三问

![b_n=\frac{1}{\sqrt{n(n+1)}}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{(n+1)(n+2)}}+\cdot\cdot\cdot+\frac{1}{\sqrt{(n+k-1)(n+k)}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_n%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%28n%2B1%29%7D%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B%28n%2B1%29%28n%2B2%29%7D%7D%2B%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B%28n%2Bk-1%29%28n%2Bk%29%7D%7D)

看到这个形式，考虑转化成定积分

![=\frac{1}{n}[\frac{1}{\sqrt{(1+\frac{1}{n})}}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{(1+\frac{1}{n})(1+\frac{2}{n})}}+\cdot\cdot\cdot+\frac{1}{\sqrt{(1+\frac{k-1}{n})(1+\frac{k}{n})}}]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%5B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%7D%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7Bn%7D%29%7D%7D%2B%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7Bk-1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%281%2B%5Cfrac%7Bk%7D%7Bn%7D%29%7D%7D%5D)

为了利用定积分，我们需要先搞定这个分母，放缩一下

![\frac{1}{n}(\frac{1}{1+\frac{1}{n}}+\frac{1}{1+\frac{2}{n}}+\cdot\cdot\cdot+\frac{1}{1+\frac{k}{n}})<b_n<\frac{1}{n}(\frac{1}{1}+\frac{1}{1+\frac{1}{n}}+\cdot\cdot\cdot+\frac{1}{1+\frac{k-1}{n}})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%2B%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7Bk%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%29%3Cb_n%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%2B%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7Bk-1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%29)

然后我们就可以转换成定积分了

![\int_0^1\frac{1}{1+x}-\frac{1}{n}<b_n<\int_0^1\frac{1}{x+1}-\frac{1}{2n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%2Bx%7D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%3Cb_n%3C%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%2B1%7D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2n%7D)
