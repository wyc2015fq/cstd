![e^{ikx},\cos(kx),k,kx,e^{-ax^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7Bikx%7D%2C%5Ccos%28kx%29%2Ck%2Ckx%2Ce%5E%7B-ax%5E2%7D)

![\cos(kx)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos%28kx%29) ：  ![-k^2\cos(kx)=A\cos(kx)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-k%5E2%5Ccos%28kx%29%3DA%5Ccos%28kx%29) 所以是特征函数且特征值是 ![-k^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-k%5E2)

![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) ： ![0=Ak](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3DAk) 所以是特征函数且特征值是 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0)

![kx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=kx) ：不是特征函数
