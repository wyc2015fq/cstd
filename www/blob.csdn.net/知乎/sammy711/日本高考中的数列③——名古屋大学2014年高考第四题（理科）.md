对于 ![a_{n+1}=[\frac{a_n}{2}]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bn%2B1%7D%3D%5B%5Cfrac%7Ba_n%7D%7B2%7D%5D) ，它的本质是去掉 ![a_{n+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bn%2B1%7D) 二进制数中最后一位

![a_3=1_{(10)}=1_{(2)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_3%3D1_%7B%2810%29%7D%3D1_%7B%282%29%7D)

第二问：

注意，以下说明中的位数均从左开始第一个不是 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) 的位数开始计算

显然，本文等同于 ![N_{(2)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_%7B%282%29%7D) 中前两位是 ![10](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=10)

![0\leq N<2^{10}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Cleq+N%3C2%5E%7B10%7D) ，所以 ![N_{(2)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_%7B%282%29%7D) 最多只有 ![10](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=10) 位

当 ![N_{(2)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_%7B%282%29%7D) 有 ![10](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=10) 位时，满足条件的 ![N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N) 有 ![2^{10-2}=2^8](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E%7B10-2%7D%3D2%5E8) 个

![9](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=9) 位时，有 ![2^7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E7) 个

………………

![2^8+2^7+\cdot\cdot\cdot+2^0=2^9-1=511](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E8%2B2%5E7%2B%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2B2%5E0%3D2%5E9-1%3D511) 个


第三问

这时 ![N_{(2)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_%7B%282%29%7D) 最多有 ![100](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=100) 位

由第二问的算法我们可以明白，对于位数为 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 的数 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 来说

无论 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 是多少，使得 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 至少出现一次的 ![N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N) 的个数都是 ![2^{101-k}-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E%7B101-k%7D-1)

注意 ![\frac{2^{101-k}-1}{2^{100}}\leq\frac{1}{100}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2%5E%7B101-k%7D-1%7D%7B2%5E%7B100%7D%7D%5Cleq%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B100%7D)

当 ![k=7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D7) 时

![\frac{2^{101-k}-1}{2^{100}}=\frac{1}{2^6}-\frac{1}{2^{100}}>\frac{1}{100}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2%5E%7B101-k%7D-1%7D%7B2%5E%7B100%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5E6%7D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5E%7B100%7D%7D%3E%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B100%7D)

当 ![k=8](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D8) 时

![\frac{2^{101-k}-1}{2^{100}}=\frac{1}{2^7}-\frac{1}{2^{100}}<\frac{1}{100}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2%5E%7B101-k%7D-1%7D%7B2%5E%7B100%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5E7%7D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5E%7B100%7D%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B100%7D)

因此当 ![k=8](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D8) 时 ![N_{(2)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_%7B%282%29%7D) 的位数最小，为 ![8](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=8)

此时 ![N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N) 的最小值是 ![N_{(2)}=10000000](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_%7B%282%29%7D%3D10000000)

换算成十进制， ![N_{(10)}=128](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_%7B%2810%29%7D%3D128)

所以最小时， ![m=128](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D128)
