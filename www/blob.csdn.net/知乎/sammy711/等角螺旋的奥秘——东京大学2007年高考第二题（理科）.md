![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 是大于等于 ![2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2) 的整数。平面上有 ![n+2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B2) 个点，分别为 ![O,P_0,P_1,\cdot\cdot\cdot P_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O%2CP_0%2CP_1%2C%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot+P_n) 。这些点满足下列条件。

- ![\angle P_{k-1}OP_k=\frac{\pi}{n}(1\leq k\leq n)，\angle OP_{k-1}P_k=\angle OP_{0}P_1(2\leq k\leq n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cangle+P_%7Bk-1%7DOP_k%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%281%5Cleq+k%5Cleq+n%29%EF%BC%8C%5Cangle+OP_%7Bk-1%7DP_k%3D%5Cangle+OP_%7B0%7DP_1%282%5Cleq+k%5Cleq+n%29)
- 线段 ![OP_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OP_0) 长为 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) ，线段 ![OP_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OP_1) 的长度为 ![1+\frac{1}{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D)

设线段 ![P_{k-1}P_{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_%7Bk-1%7DP_%7Bk%7D) 的长度为 ![a_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_k) ， ![s_n=\sum_{k=1}^na_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s_n%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5Ena_k)

先来常规做法

用余弦定理解三角形  ![OP_0P_1 ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OP_0P_1+)

![P_0P_1=\sqrt{1+(1+\frac{1}{n})^2-2cos\frac{\pi}{n}(1+\frac{1}{n})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_0P_1%3D%5Csqrt%7B1%2B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E2-2cos%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%7D)

我们知道，图中三角形 ![OP_{k-1}P_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OP_%7Bk-1%7DP_k) 都是相似三角形，相邻两个三角形的比例关系是 ![(1+\frac{1}{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29) 。

所以![\sum_{k=1}^na_k=[1+(1+\frac{1}{n})+(1+\frac{1}{n})^2+\cdot\cdot\cdot+(1+\frac{1}{n})^n]P_0P_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5Ena_k%3D%5B1%2B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%2B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E2%2B%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5En%5DP_0P_1)

根据等比数列求和公式

![1+(1+\frac{1}{n})+(1+\frac{1}{n})^2+\cdot\cdot\cdot+(1+\frac{1}{n})^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%2B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E2%2B%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5En)

![=\frac{(1+\frac{1}{n})^n-1}{1+\frac{1}{n}-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5En-1%7D%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D-1%7D)

![=n[(1+\frac{1}{n})^n-1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3Dn%5B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5En-1%5D)

所以 ![s_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s_n)

![=n[(1+\frac{1}{n})^n-1]\sqrt{1+(1+\frac{1}{n})^2-2cos\frac{\pi}{n}(1+\frac{1}{n})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3Dn%5B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5En-1%5D%5Csqrt%7B1%2B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E2-2cos%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%7D)

![=[(1+\frac{1}{n})^n-1]\sqrt{2n+2+\frac{1}{n}-2cos\frac{\pi}{n}(n+1)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5B%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5En-1%5D%5Csqrt%7B2n%2B2%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D-2cos%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%28n%2B1%29%7D)

当 ![n \to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n+%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 时

前半部分

![(1+\frac{1}{n})^n-1=e-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5En-1%3De-1)

后半部分 ![\sqrt{2n^2+2n+1-2cos\frac{\pi}{n}(n^2+n)}=\sqrt{1+2n(n+1)(1-cos\frac{\pi}{n})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B2n%5E2%2B2n%2B1-2cos%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%28n%5E2%2Bn%29%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7B1%2B2n%28n%2B1%29%281-cos%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%29%7D)

根据和角公式 ![1-cos2\theta=2sin^2\theta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1-cos2%5Ctheta%3D2sin%5E2%5Ctheta)

所以 ![1-cos\frac{\pi}{n}=2sin^2\frac{\pi}{2n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1-cos%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%3D2sin%5E2%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D)

![\lim_{n \to \infty}\sqrt{1+2n(n+1)(1-cos\frac{\pi}{n})}=\lim_{n \to \infty}\sqrt{1+4n(n+1)sin^2\frac{\pi}{2n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Csqrt%7B1%2B2n%28n%2B1%29%281-cos%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%29%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Csqrt%7B1%2B4n%28n%2B1%29sin%5E2%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D)

![=\lim_{n \to \infty}\sqrt{1+4n(n+1)\frac{\pi^2}{4n^2}\frac{sin^2\frac{\pi}{2n}}{(\frac{\pi}{2n})^2}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Csqrt%7B1%2B4n%28n%2B1%29%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7B4n%5E2%7D%5Cfrac%7Bsin%5E2%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D%7B%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%29%5E2%7D%7D)

![=\sqrt{\pi^2+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Csqrt%7B%5Cpi%5E2%2B1%7D)

综上可知

![\lim_{n \to \infty}s_n=(e-1)\sqrt{\pi^2+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7Ds_n%3D%28e-1%29%5Csqrt%7B%5Cpi%5E2%2B1%7D)

## 关于等角螺线

注意，所有 ![\angle OP_kP_{k+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cangle+OP_kP_%7Bk%2B1%7D) 的角度都是相同的。这完全满足等角螺线 ![r=ae^{b\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%3Dae%5E%7Bb%5Ctheta%7D) 的定义

也就是说，当 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 趋近无穷大时，最外面的一圈 ![P_kP_{k+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_kP_%7Bk%2B1%7D) 是等角螺线的一部分。

也就是图中用青色箭头连接起来的部分

![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-74ed40f5e4ed7b994e646d7f8877b277_b.png)


详见：[张英锋：昆虫为什么不会因趋光性齐刷刷地奔向太阳？](https://www.zhihu.com/question/21260723/answer/29561931)

根据 ![\lim_{n \to \infty}(1+\frac{1}{n})^n=e](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%281%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5En%3De) ， ![r=ae^{b\theta}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%3Dae%5E%7Bb%5Ctheta%7D) 过 ![(1,0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%2C0%29) 和 ![(e,\pi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28e%2C%5Cpi%29)

所以 ![a=1,b=\frac{1}{\pi}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3D1%2Cb%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D) ，也就是 ![r=e^{\frac{\theta}{\pi}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%3De%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%7D)

在定义域内对曲线长度积分

![\int_0^\pi \sqrt{dr^2+(rd\theta)^2}=\int_0^\pi \sqrt{(\frac{dr}{d\theta})^2+r^2} d\theta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5E%5Cpi+%5Csqrt%7Bdr%5E2%2B%28rd%5Ctheta%29%5E2%7D%3D%5Cint_0%5E%5Cpi+%5Csqrt%7B%28%5Cfrac%7Bdr%7D%7Bd%5Ctheta%7D%29%5E2%2Br%5E2%7D+d%5Ctheta)

![=\int_0^\pi \sqrt{(\frac{1}{\pi}e^{\frac{\theta}{\pi}})^2+(e^{\frac{\theta}{\pi}})^2} d\theta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cint_0%5E%5Cpi+%5Csqrt%7B%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7De%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%7D%29%5E2%2B%28e%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%7D%29%5E2%7D+d%5Ctheta)

![=\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{\pi^2}}\int_0^\pi e^{\frac{\theta}{\pi}} d\theta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Csqrt%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7D%5Cint_0%5E%5Cpi+e%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%7D+d%5Ctheta)

![=\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{\pi^2}}\cdot\pi [e^{\frac{\theta}{\pi}}]_0^\pi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Csqrt%7B1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7D%5Ccdot%5Cpi+%5Be%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%7D%5D_0%5E%5Cpi)

![=\sqrt{{\pi^2}+1}\cdot (e-1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Csqrt%7B%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%2B1%7D%5Ccdot+%28e-1%29)
