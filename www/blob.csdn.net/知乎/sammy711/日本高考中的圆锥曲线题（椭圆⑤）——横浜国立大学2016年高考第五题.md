平面上有椭圆 ![C:\frac{x^2}{4}+y^2=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%3A%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7B4%7D%2By%5E2%3D1)

1.

如果经过点 ![P(a,b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%28a%2Cb%29) 的椭圆的两条切线垂直，求 ![a,b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%2Cb) 满足的条件

2.

![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 是 ![C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C) 的一个外切长方形，如果 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 的一个顶点的横坐标是 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 且纵坐标为正 ，求 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 的面积。

——————————————————————————————————————


经过 ![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 点的直线可以写成 ![y=m(x-a)+b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dm%28x-a%29%2Bb)

它与椭圆相切，所以联立方程，判别式为 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0)

![\frac{x^2}{4}+(mx-ma+b)^2=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7B4%7D%2B%28mx-ma%2Bb%29%5E2%3D1)

![(\frac{1}{4}+m^2)x^2+2m(b-ma)x+(ma-b)^2-1=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B4%7D%2Bm%5E2%29x%5E2%2B2m%28b-ma%29x%2B%28ma-b%29%5E2-1%3D0)

计算判别式

![4m^2(b^2-2mab+m^2a^2)-(4m^2+1)(m^2a^2-2abm+b^2-1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=4m%5E2%28b%5E2-2mab%2Bm%5E2a%5E2%29-%284m%5E2%2B1%29%28m%5E2a%5E2-2abm%2Bb%5E2-1%29%3D0)

![4m^2(b^2-2mab+m^2a^2)-4m^2\cdot(m^2a^2-2abm+b^2-1)-(m^2a^2-2abm+b^2-1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=4m%5E2%28b%5E2-2mab%2Bm%5E2a%5E2%29-4m%5E2%5Ccdot%28m%5E2a%5E2-2abm%2Bb%5E2-1%29-%28m%5E2a%5E2-2abm%2Bb%5E2-1%29%3D0)

![(4-a^2)m^2+2abm-b^2+1=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%284-a%5E2%29m%5E2%2B2abm-b%5E2%2B1%3D0)

当 ![a\neq\pm2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cneq%5Cpm2) 时

由题两条切线垂直可以知道 ![m_1m_2=-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m_1m_2%3D-1)

因此 ![\frac{b^2-1}{a^2-4}=-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bb%5E2-1%7D%7Ba%5E2-4%7D%3D-1)

解得 ![a^2+b^2=5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5E2%2Bb%5E2%3D5)

当 ![a=\pm2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3D%5Cpm2) ，垂直关系仍然成立

所以范围是 ![a^2+b^2=5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5E2%2Bb%5E2%3D5)

第二问

由第一问可知长方形的一个顶点是 ![(1,2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%2C2%29)

根据对称性，和其相对的顶点是 ![(-1,-2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28-1%2C-2%29)

在第一问的直线方程中代入 ![a=1,b=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3D1%2Cb%3D2)

![3m^2+4m-3=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3m%5E2%2B4m-3%3D0)

![m=\frac{-4\pm\sqrt{52}}{6}=\frac{-2\pm\sqrt{13}}{3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D%5Cfrac%7B-4%5Cpm%5Csqrt%7B52%7D%7D%7B6%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B-2%5Cpm%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B3%7D)

联立 ![x^2+y^2=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E2%2By%5E2%3D1) 和 ![y=m(x-1)+2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dm%28x-1%29%2B2)

![x^2+m^2(x-1)^2+4m(x-1)+4=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E2%2Bm%5E2%28x-1%29%5E2%2B4m%28x-1%29%2B4%3D1)

![(m^2+1)x^2-(2m^2-4m)x+m^2-4m+3=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28m%5E2%2B1%29x%5E2-%282m%5E2-4m%29x%2Bm%5E2-4m%2B3%3D0)

根据韦达定理 ![x_1+x_2=\frac{2m^2-4m}{m^2+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_1%2Bx_2%3D%5Cfrac%7B2m%5E2-4m%7D%7Bm%5E2%2B1%7D)

取其中一个 ![m=\frac{\sqrt{13}-2}{3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B13%7D-2%7D%7B3%7D)

![m^2=\frac{17-4\sqrt{13}}{9}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5E2%3D%5Cfrac%7B17-4%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B9%7D)

![x_1+x_2=\frac{2m^2-4m}{m^2+1}=\frac{\frac{34-8\sqrt{13}}{9}+\frac{8-4\sqrt{13}}{3}}{\frac{26-4\sqrt{13}}{9}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_1%2Bx_2%3D%5Cfrac%7B2m%5E2-4m%7D%7Bm%5E2%2B1%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cfrac%7B34-8%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B9%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B8-4%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B3%7D%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7B26-4%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B9%7D%7D)

![=\frac{58-20\sqrt{13}}{26-4\sqrt{13}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B58-20%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B26-4%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D)

![=\frac{468-288\sqrt{13}}{468}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B468-288%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B468%7D)

我们知道 ![x_1=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_1%3D1)

所以 ![x_2=-\frac{8\sqrt{13}}{13}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_2%3D-%5Cfrac%7B8%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B13%7D)

考虑长方形对角线的长度，和 ![(-\frac{8\sqrt{13}}{13},y_2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28-%5Cfrac%7B8%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B13%7D%2Cy_2%29) 到这条对角线的距离

对角线的长度 ![=2\sqrt{1^2+2^2}=2\sqrt5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D2%5Csqrt%7B1%5E2%2B2%5E2%7D%3D2%5Csqrt5)

![x_2^2+y_2^2=5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_2%5E2%2By_2%5E2%3D5)

所以 ![y_2=\sqrt{5-\frac{64}{13}}=\frac{\sqrt{13}}{13}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y_2%3D%5Csqrt%7B5-%5Cfrac%7B64%7D%7B13%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B13%7D)

对角线的解析式为 ![y=2x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3D2x) ，也就是 ![2x-y=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2x-y%3D0)

根据点到直线距离公式

距离 ![=\frac{\left| -\frac{16}{13}\sqrt{13}-\frac{\sqrt{13}}{13}\right|}{\sqrt{2^2+1^2}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cleft%7C+-%5Cfrac%7B16%7D%7B13%7D%5Csqrt%7B13%7D-%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B13%7D%5Cright%7C%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%5E2%2B1%5E2%7D%7D)

![=\frac{17\sqrt{13}}{13\sqrt5}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B17%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B13%5Csqrt5%7D)

因此长方形的面积就是

![\frac{17\sqrt{13}}{13\sqrt5}\times2\sqrt5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B17%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B13%5Csqrt5%7D%5Ctimes2%5Csqrt5)

![=\frac{34\sqrt{13}}{13}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B34%5Csqrt%7B13%7D%7D%7B13%7D)
