# 椭圆的外切矩形中，面积最大的是多少？（圆锥曲线，椭圆⑤）——首都大学东京2015年高考第三题（理科） - 知乎

## 本题答题时间为25分钟


考虑椭圆 ![\frac{x^2}{16}+\frac{y^2}{9}=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7B16%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7By%5E2%7D%7B9%7D%3D1) ，回答以下问题
- 求斜率为 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 并与椭圆相切的所有直线的方程
- 设所有边都相切与椭圆的矩形的一边的斜率是 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) ，求这个矩形的面积 ![S(m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%28m%29)
- 求 ![S(m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%28m%29) 的最大值


1.

![y=mx+k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dmx%2Bk) 和 ![\frac{x^2}{16}+\frac{y^2}{9}=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7B16%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7By%5E2%7D%7B9%7D%3D1) 联立

![(16m^2+9)x^2+32mkx+16k^2-144=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2816m%5E2%2B9%29x%5E2%2B32mkx%2B16k%5E2-144%3D0)

![\Delta=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta%3D0) ，也就是 ![16m^2k^2-(16m^2+9)(k^2-9)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=16m%5E2k%5E2-%2816m%5E2%2B9%29%28k%5E2-9%29%3D0)

![k^2=16m^2+9](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5E2%3D16m%5E2%2B9)

![k=\pm\sqrt{16m^2+9}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D%5Cpm%5Csqrt%7B16m%5E2%2B9%7D)

所以答案是

![y=mx+\sqrt{16m^2+9}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dmx%2B%5Csqrt%7B16m%5E2%2B9%7D)

![y=mx-\sqrt{16m^2+9}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dmx-%5Csqrt%7B16m%5E2%2B9%7D)

2.

![m=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D0) 时
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-e2951c041c4b25a81c24496fb6dda8b6_b.jpg)
![S(m)=48](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%28m%29%3D48)

不为 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) 时，一条直线是 ![y=mx\pm\sqrt{16m^2+9}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dmx%5Cpm%5Csqrt%7B16m%5E2%2B9%7D)

另一条直线，只需要把 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 替换成 ![-\frac{1}{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bm%7D) 即可

也就是 ![y=-\frac{x}{m}\pm\sqrt{\frac{16}{m^2}+9}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3D-%5Cfrac%7Bx%7D%7Bm%7D%5Cpm%5Csqrt%7B%5Cfrac%7B16%7D%7Bm%5E2%7D%2B9%7D)
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-2e3688e04f3dc8108cee81a0a823a6e7_b.jpg)

考虑上边和右边的两条边，考虑长和宽，也就是和原点 ![O](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O) 的距离

![d_1=\frac{\sqrt{16m^2+9}}{\sqrt{m^2+1}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d_1%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B16m%5E2%2B9%7D%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bm%5E2%2B1%7D%7D)

![d_2=\frac{\sqrt{9m^2+16}}{\sqrt{m^2+1}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d_2%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B9m%5E2%2B16%7D%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bm%5E2%2B1%7D%7D)

![S(m)=4d_1d_2=\frac{4\sqrt{16m^2+9}\sqrt{9m^2+16}}{m^2+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%28m%29%3D4d_1d_2%3D%5Cfrac%7B4%5Csqrt%7B16m%5E2%2B9%7D%5Csqrt%7B9m%5E2%2B16%7D%7D%7Bm%5E2%2B1%7D)

计算可知 ![S(0)=48](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%280%29%3D48)

所以答案就是 ![\frac{4\sqrt{16m^2+9}\sqrt{9m^2+16}}{m^2+1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B4%5Csqrt%7B16m%5E2%2B9%7D%5Csqrt%7B9m%5E2%2B16%7D%7D%7Bm%5E2%2B1%7D)

3.

![S(m)=4\sqrt{\frac{(16m^2+16-7)(9m^2+9+7)}{(m^2+1)^2}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%28m%29%3D4%5Csqrt%7B%5Cfrac%7B%2816m%5E2%2B16-7%29%289m%5E2%2B9%2B7%29%7D%7B%28m%5E2%2B1%29%5E2%7D%7D)

设 ![m^2+1=t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5E2%2B1%3Dt) ，考虑根号内的

![\frac{(16t-7)(9t+7)}{t^2}=144+\frac{49}{t}-\frac{49}{t^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%2816t-7%29%289t%2B7%29%7D%7Bt%5E2%7D%3D144%2B%5Cfrac%7B49%7D%7Bt%7D-%5Cfrac%7B49%7D%7Bt%5E2%7D)

求导得 ![-\frac{49}{t^2}+\frac{98}{t^3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-%5Cfrac%7B49%7D%7Bt%5E2%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B98%7D%7Bt%5E3%7D) ，调查极值可知最大值时 ![t=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D2)

代入得到 ![S(m)=50](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%28m%29%3D50) 为最大值
