等等先别急，等我慢慢道来这道题的出题背景


我们知道在 ![(0,\frac{\pi}{2})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%280%2C%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%29) 的范围中 ![\sin x<x<\tan x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin+x%3Cx%3C%5Ctan+x) 是恒成立的
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-fe7ac29f73073e9f75c6437aa6568978_b.jpg)
证明方法参考三角形 ![OAB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OAB) ，扇形 ![OAB](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OAB) ，三角形 ![OBC](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OBC) 的面积关系

现在我们开个脑洞，把这个强行凑到平方倒数和的形式

![\sin x<x<\tan x\to\sin^2 x<x^2<\tan^2 x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csin+x%3Cx%3C%5Ctan+x%5Cto%5Csin%5E2+x%3Cx%5E2%3C%5Ctan%5E2+x)

也就是 ![\frac{1}{\tan^2 x}<\frac{1}{x^2}<\frac{1}{\sin^2 x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Ctan%5E2+x%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%5E2%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csin%5E2+x%7D)

注意不等式的成立范围是 ![(0,\frac{\pi}{2})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%280%2C%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%29) ，而 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 是从 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 到正无穷的整数。

这里是关键：**为了让这两个区间能够相互对应**，我们需要把 ![\frac{\pi}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D) 切割成 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个区间，然后取第 ![1,2,3,\cdot\cdot\cdot,k,\cdot\cdot\cdot,n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2C2%2C3%2C%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2Ck%2C%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2Cn)   个区间

也就是设 ![x=\frac{k\pi}{2n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D%5Cfrac%7Bk%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D) ，其中 ![k=1,2,3,\cdot\cdot\cdot,n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D1%2C2%2C3%2C%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2Cn)

最后再依次把这些区间内的不等式加起来

也就是

![\frac{1}{\tan^2 \frac{\pi}{2n}}<\frac{4n^2}{\pi^2}<\frac{1}{\sin^2 \frac{\pi}{2n}} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Ctan%5E2+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B4n%5E2%7D%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csin%5E2+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D+)  ，   ![\frac{1}{\tan^2 \frac{2\pi}{2n}} <\frac{4n^2}{2^2\pi^2}<\frac{1}{\sin^2 \frac{2\pi}{2n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Ctan%5E2+%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D+%3C%5Cfrac%7B4n%5E2%7D%7B2%5E2%5Cpi%5E2%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csin%5E2+%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D)………………

把这些不等式全都加起来，再取一个极限就有

![\lim_{n \to \infty}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{1}{\tan^2 \frac{k\pi}{2n}}<\lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{4n^2}{\pi^2}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{1}{k^2}<\lim_{n \to \infty}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{1}{\sin^2 \frac{k\pi}{2n}} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Ctan%5E2+%5Cfrac%7Bk%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D%3C%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B4n%5E2%7D%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%5E2%7D%3C%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csin%5E2+%5Cfrac%7Bk%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D+)

变个形，再利用三角函数的公式 ![\frac{1}{\sin^2x}-1=\frac{1}{\tan^2x}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csin%5E2x%7D-1%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Ctan%5E2x%7D)

![\lim_{n \to \infty}[(\frac{\pi^2}{4n^2}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{1}{\sin^2 \frac{k\pi}{2n}} )-\frac{\pi}{4n}]<\lim_{n \to \infty}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{1}{k^2}<\lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{\pi^2}{4n^2}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{1}{\sin^2 \frac{k\pi}{2n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5B%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7B4n%5E2%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csin%5E2+%5Cfrac%7Bk%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D+%29-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B4n%7D%5D%3C%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%5E2%7D%3C%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7B4n%5E2%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csin%5E2+%5Cfrac%7Bk%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D)

![\frac{\pi}{4n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B4n%7D)在 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 趋近无穷时极限是零

根据夹逼定理，最后转化成求 ![\lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{1}{n^2}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{1}{\sin^2 \frac{k\pi}{2n}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%5E2%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csin%5E2+%5Cfrac%7Bk%5Cpi%7D%7B2n%7D%7D)的值

在本题我们将会证明这个极限是 ![\frac{2}{3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B3%7D)


因此 ![\lim_{n \to \infty}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{1}{k^2}=\frac{2}{3}\cdot\frac{\pi^2}{4}=\frac{\pi^2}{6}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5En%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%5E2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B3%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7B4%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%5E2%7D%7B6%7D)
