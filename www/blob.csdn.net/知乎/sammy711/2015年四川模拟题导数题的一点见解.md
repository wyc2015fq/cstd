————————————————————————————————————

易知，![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29)  在定义域内导数均存在，并且 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 的定义域是 ![x>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3E0)

所以函数取最小值时，导数必为零

因此我们把 ![f\prime(x)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cprime%28x%29%3D0) 的解中，代表函数取得最小值的 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 设为 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t)

首先因为导数为 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) ，![me^{mt-1}-\frac{1-lnt}{t^2}=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=me%5E%7Bmt-1%7D-%5Cfrac%7B1-lnt%7D%7Bt%5E2%7D%3D0)

另外根据最小值为 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) ，有![e^{mt-1}-\frac{lnt}{t}=m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7Bmt-1%7D-%5Cfrac%7Blnt%7D%7Bt%7D%3Dm)

联立等式，解得 ![(mt+1)(lnt+mt-1)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28mt%2B1%29%28lnt%2Bmt-1%29%3D0)

![m\neq 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5Cneq+0) 时

如果 ![lnt+mt-1=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=lnt%2Bmt-1%3D0) ，那么![f(t)=m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28t%29%3Dm) 恒成立

我们知道 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 是实数，所以需要 ![g(t)=lnt+mt-1=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28t%29%3Dlnt%2Bmt-1%3D0) 有解

为了求 ![m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m) 的最小值，我们先考虑 ![m<0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3C0) 时的情况

对 ![g(t)=lnt+mt-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28t%29%3Dlnt%2Bmt-1) 求导得到 ![\frac{mt+1}{t}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bmt%2B1%7D%7Bt%7D) 。其为 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) 时 ![t=-\frac{1}{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bm%7D)

![t<-\frac{1}{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3C-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bm%7D) 时导数为正，单调增加。 ![t>-\frac{1}{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3E-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bm%7D) 时导数为负，单调减少

因此 ![g(-\frac{1}{m})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bm%7D%29) 是最大值，为了保证 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 有解，必须要有 ![g(-\frac{1}{m})\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bm%7D%29%5Cgeq0) ，也就是 

![-ln(-m)-1-1\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-ln%28-m%29-1-1%5Cgeq0)

解得 ![m\geq-\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5Cgeq-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D)


如果是 ![mt+1=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=mt%2B1%3D0) 的话，那么因为有 ![f(t)=m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28t%29%3Dm) ，所以 ![m(1+ln(-m))=\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%281%2Bln%28-m%29%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D)

根据上一种情况结果猜测解是 ![m=-\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D) 并加以证明，带入发现成立

左边求导是 ![2+ln(-m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%2Bln%28-m%29)

![m=-\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D) 时导数为 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0)

![m<-\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3C-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D)  时导数为正，单调增加

![m>-\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3E-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D) 时导数为负，单调减少

因此 ![m=-\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D) 是最大值，所以是唯一解

![m=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%3D0) 时，无论是否成立，最小值都无法小于 ![-\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D)

综上所述 ![m\geq-\frac{1}{e^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%5Cgeq-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E2%7D)
