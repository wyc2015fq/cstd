![\left| a_1-c_1 \right|,\left| a_2-c_2 \right|,\cdot\cdot\cdot,\left| a_n-c_{n} \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+a_1-c_1+%5Cright%7C%2C%5Cleft%7C+a_2-c_2+%5Cright%7C%2C%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2C%5Cleft%7C+a_n-c_%7Bn%7D+%5Cright%7C)

这 ![3n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3n) 个数分别对应 ![[1,3n]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B1%2C3n%5D) 中的所有整数…………结论①


第一题就只有 ![1,2,3,4](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2C2%2C3%2C4) 四个数，而且 ![a_1=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_1%3D1)

根据 ![\left| a_1-b_1 \right|>\left| a_1-c_1 \right|>\left| a_1-a_{2} \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+a_1-b_1+%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7C+a_1-c_1+%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7C+a_1-a_%7B2%7D+%5Cright%7C)

易知 ![b_1=4,c_1=3,a_2=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1%3D4%2Cc_1%3D3%2Ca_2%3D2)

第二题有 ![1\to7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Cto7) 一共七个数，而且 ![a_1=7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_1%3D7)

![\left| 7-b_1 \right|>\left| 7-c_1 \right|>\left| 7-a_{2} \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+7-b_1+%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7C+7-c_1+%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7C+7-a_%7B2%7D+%5Cright%7C)

另外有 ![\left| a_2-b_2 \right|>\left| a_2-c_2 \right|>\left| a_2-a_{3} \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+a_2-b_2+%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7C+a_2-c_2+%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7C+a_2-a_%7B3%7D+%5Cright%7C)

显然这六个差值里最大的一定是 ![\left| 7-b_1 \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+7-b_1+%5Cright%7C) 或者 ![\left| a_2-b_2 \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+a_2-b_2+%5Cright%7C)

根据结论①，我们知道差的最大值是 ![6](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=6) ，这只能由 ![\left| 7-b_1 \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+7-b_1+%5Cright%7C) 得到

因此 ![b_1=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1%3D1)

所以剩下的 ![a_2,b_2,c_3,a_3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_2%2Cb_2%2Cc_3%2Ca_3) 只能从 ![2,3,4,5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%2C3%2C4%2C5) 中挑选

第二大的数是 ![\left| 7-c_1 \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+7-c_1+%5Cright%7C) 或者 ![\left| a_2-b_2 \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+a_2-b_2+%5Cright%7C)

但差的第二大的值是 ![5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=5) ，而 ![2,3,4,5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%2C3%2C4%2C5) 中无论如何选取都不可能得到差值 ![5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=5)

所以 ![c_1=2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_1%3D2)

同理可得其他数

因此 ![(a_2,a_3,b_1,b_2,c_1,c_2)=(3,4,1,6,2,5)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_2%2Ca_3%2Cb_1%2Cb_2%2Cc_1%2Cc_2%29%3D%283%2C4%2C1%2C6%2C2%2C5%29)

第三题参考第二题的思路

显然最大的差值 ![3n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3n)

根据条件 ![C_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C_2) ，,这个值一定出自

![\left| 1-b_1 \right|,\left| a_2-b_2 \right|,\cdot\cdot\cdot\left| a_n-b_n \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+1-b_1+%5Cright%7C%2C%5Cleft%7C+a_2-b_2+%5Cright%7C%2C%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Cleft%7C+a_n-b_n+%5Cright%7C)  中

这之中能取到 ![3n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3n) 的只有 ![\left| 1-b_1 \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+1-b_1+%5Cright%7C)

所以 ![b_1=3n+1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_1%3D3n%2B1)

同第二题， ![c_1=3n,a_2=3n-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_1%3D3n%2Ca_2%3D3n-1)

接着根据 ![\left| (3n-1)-b_2 \right|>\left| (3n-1)-c_2 \right|>\left| (3n-1)-a_3 \right|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C+%283n-1%29-b_2+%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7C+%283n-1%29-c_2+%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7C+%283n-1%29-a_3+%5Cright%7C)

可得这三个差值分别是 ![3n-3,3n-4,3n-5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3n-3%2C3n-4%2C3n-5)

因此 ![b_2=2,c_2=3,a_3=4](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b_2%3D2%2Cc_2%3D3%2Ca_3%3D4)

由第三题的推论，易知

![k=2j-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D2j-1) 时， ![(b_{2j-1},c_{2j-1},a_{2j})=(3n-[3(j-1)-1],3n-3(j-1),3n-[3(j-1)+1])](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28b_%7B2j-1%7D%2Cc_%7B2j-1%7D%2Ca_%7B2j%7D%29%3D%283n-%5B3%28j-1%29-1%5D%2C3n-3%28j-1%29%2C3n-%5B3%28j-1%29%2B1%5D%29)

![k=2j](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D2j) 时， ![(a_{2j},b_{2j},a_{2j+1})=(3j-1,3j,3j+1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_%7B2j%7D%2Cb_%7B2j%7D%2Ca_%7B2j%2B1%7D%29%3D%283j-1%2C3j%2C3j%2B1%29)

所以 ![(a_{29},b_{29},c_{29})=(43,6010,6009)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_%7B29%7D%2Cb_%7B29%7D%2Cc_%7B29%7D%29%3D%2843%2C6010%2C6009%29)
