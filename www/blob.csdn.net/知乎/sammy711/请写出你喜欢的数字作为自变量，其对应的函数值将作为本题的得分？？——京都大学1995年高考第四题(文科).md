设自变量n为自然数的函数

![f(n)=](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28n%29%3D)n除以7所得的余数

![g(n)=3f(\sum_{k=1}^7 k^n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28n%29%3D3f%28%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E7+k%5En%29)

(1) 试证明对任何自然数，都有 ![f(n^7)=f(n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28n%5E7%29%3Df%28n%29) 成立

(2) 请任选一个你喜欢的自然数作为n的取值，所对应的 ![g(n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28n%29) 将作为问![(2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%282%29)的得分

——————————————————————————————

第 ![(1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%29) 问

有 ![n^7-n=n(n^6-1)=n(n-1)(n^2+n+1)(n+1)(n^2-n+1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5E7-n%3Dn%28n%5E6-1%29%3Dn%28n-1%29%28n%5E2%2Bn%2B1%29%28n%2B1%29%28n%5E2-n%2B1%29)

这时设 ![n=7k,7k+1,7k+2,......,7k+6](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D7k%2C7k%2B1%2C7k%2B2%2C......%2C7k%2B6) (k为整数) ，再分别带入算余数

可以发现，无论 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 除以7余数为多少，![n^7-n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5E7-n)都 为7的倍数

因此 ![n^7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5E7) 与 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 同余

得证

第 ![(2)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%282%29) 问

其实没什么技巧...

于是只要计算从1到6的情况就可以了
