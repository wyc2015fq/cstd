根据 ![(\cos n\theta)\prime=f_n\prime(\cos\theta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Ccos+n%5Ctheta%29%5Cprime%3Df_n%5Cprime%28%5Ccos%5Ctheta%29)

![-ng_n(\cos\theta)\sin\theta=f_n\prime(\cos\theta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-ng_n%28%5Ccos%5Ctheta%29%5Csin%5Ctheta%3Df_n%5Cprime%28%5Ccos%5Ctheta%29)

设 ![\cos\theta=x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos%5Ctheta%3Dx)

![-ng_n(x)\sin\theta=f_n\prime(x)\cdot(-\sin\theta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-ng_n%28x%29%5Csin%5Ctheta%3Df_n%5Cprime%28x%29%5Ccdot%28-%5Csin%5Ctheta%29)

解得 ![f_n\prime(x)=ng_n(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cprime%28x%29%3Dng_n%28x%29)  ，其中 ![-1\leq x\leq 1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1%5Cleq+x%5Cleq+1)

**由于 ![f_n(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%28x%29) 和 ![g_n(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_n%28x%29) 是多项式，函数图像可以由有限个点所确定，所以在某一区间恒成立代表它在实数域内均成立**

因此对于任意的 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) ， ![f_n\prime(x)=ng_n(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%5Cprime%28x%29%3Dng_n%28x%29) 成立
