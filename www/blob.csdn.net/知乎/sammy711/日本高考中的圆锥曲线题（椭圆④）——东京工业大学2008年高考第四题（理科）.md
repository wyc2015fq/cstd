第一问送分。。

很明显 ![P,Q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%2CQ) 的横坐标是 ![\frac{\frac{1}{2}}{\tan\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%7D%7B%5Ctan%5Calpha%7D) ，也就是 ![\frac{1}{2\tan\alpha}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5Ctan%5Calpha%7D)

所以点 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 的坐标是 ![(\frac{1}{2\tan\alpha}+\frac{\sqrt3}{2},0)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5Ctan%5Calpha%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt3%7D%7B2%7D%2C0%29)
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-fa2c67ed28d4d2c24b058deed84d2041_b.jpg)

第二问考虑矩阵变换

![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-bce178d4e5243d33ca929474683dd643_b.jpg)

设 ![P,Q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%2CQ) 的坐标分别是 ![(p,-p\tan\alpha),(q,q\tan\alpha)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28p%2C-p%5Ctan%5Calpha%29%2C%28q%2Cq%5Ctan%5Calpha%29)

由 ![PQ=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=PQ%3D1)，可以得到

![(p-q)^2+\tan^2\alpha(p+q)^2=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28p-q%29%5E2%2B%5Ctan%5E2%5Calpha%28p%2Bq%29%5E2%3D1)

考虑 ![PQ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=PQ) 的中点 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) ，显然 ![\vec{MR}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BMR%7D) 是 ![\vec{PQ}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BPQ%7D) 顺时针旋转 ![\frac{\pi}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D) 并放缩 ![\frac{\sqrt3}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt3%7D%7B2%7D) 倍所得到的向量

写出旋转矩阵，另外计算得到 ![\vec{PQ}=\left( \begin{array}{c} q-p\\ m(q+p)\end{array} \right)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BPQ%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7D+q-p%5C%5C+m%28q%2Bp%29%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cright%29)

所以

![\vec{MR}= \frac{\sqrt3}{2}\left( \begin{array}{cc} \cos(-\frac{\pi}{2})& -\sin(-\frac{\pi}{2})\\ \\ \sin(-\frac{\pi}{2})&\cos(-\frac{\pi}{2}) \end{array} \right)\vec{PQ}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BMR%7D%3D+%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt3%7D%7B2%7D%5Cleft%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D+%5Ccos%28-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%29%26+-%5Csin%28-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%29%5C%5C+%5C%5C+%5Csin%28-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%29%26%5Ccos%28-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%29+%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cright%29%5Cvec%7BPQ%7D)

![=](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D)![\frac{\sqrt3}{2}\left( \begin{array}{cc} 0& 1\\ \\ -1&0\end{array} \right)\left( \begin{array}{c} q-p\\\\ m(q+p)\end{array} \right)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt3%7D%7B2%7D%5Cleft%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D+0%26+1%5C%5C+%5C%5C+-1%260%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cright%29%5Cleft%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7D+q-p%5C%5C%5C%5C+m%28q%2Bp%29%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cright%29)

![=\frac{\sqrt3}{2}\left( \begin{array}{c} m(q+p)\\ \\p-q\end{array} \right)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt3%7D%7B2%7D%5Cleft%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7D+m%28q%2Bp%29%5C%5C+%5C%5Cp-q%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cright%29)

因此 ![\vec{OR}=\vec{OM}+\vec{MR}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BOR%7D%3D%5Cvec%7BOM%7D%2B%5Cvec%7BMR%7D)

![=\frac{1}{2}\left( \begin{array}{c} (q+p)\\ \\m(p-q)\end{array} \right)+\frac{\sqrt3}{2}\left( \begin{array}{c} m(q+p)\\ \\p-q\end{array} \right) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%5Cleft%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7D+%28q%2Bp%29%5C%5C+%5C%5Cm%28p-q%29%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cright%29%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt3%7D%7B2%7D%5Cleft%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7D+m%28q%2Bp%29%5C%5C+%5C%5Cp-q%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cright%29+)![=\left( \begin{array}{c} \frac{1+\sqrt3m}{2}(q+p)\\ \\\frac{m+\sqrt3}{2}(p-q)\end{array} \right)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cleft%28+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7D+%5Cfrac%7B1%2B%5Csqrt3m%7D%7B2%7D%28q%2Bp%29%5C%5C+%5C%5C%5Cfrac%7Bm%2B%5Csqrt3%7D%7B2%7D%28p-q%29%5Cend%7Barray%7D+%5Cright%29)


由刚开始的式子 ![(p-q)^2+\tan^2\alpha(p+q)^2=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28p-q%29%5E2%2B%5Ctan%5E2%5Calpha%28p%2Bq%29%5E2%3D1)

设 ![\frac{1+\sqrt3m}{2}=s,\frac{m+\sqrt3}{2}=t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%2B%5Csqrt3m%7D%7B2%7D%3Ds%2C%5Cfrac%7Bm%2B%5Csqrt3%7D%7B2%7D%3Dt)

可得 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 的轨迹

![\frac{\tan^2\alpha }{s^2}x^2+\frac{1}{t^2}y^2=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctan%5E2%5Calpha+%7D%7Bs%5E2%7Dx%5E2%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bt%5E2%7Dy%5E2%3D1)

显然轨迹是椭圆的一部分
