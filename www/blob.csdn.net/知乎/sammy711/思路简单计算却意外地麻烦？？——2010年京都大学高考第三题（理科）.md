评论区中有读者说了用圆的方法做的了，那我就写一个硬算的

![cos\angle APB=\frac{x^2+(x-1)(x-2)}{\sqrt{x^2+(x-1)^2}\sqrt{x^2+(x-2)^2}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=cos%5Cangle+APB%3D%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%2B%28x-1%29%28x-2%29%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bx%5E2%2B%28x-1%29%5E2%7D%5Csqrt%7Bx%5E2%2B%28x-2%29%5E2%7D%7D)

求这个的最大值，我们把它平方

![\frac{(2x^2-3x+2)^2}{(2x^2-2x+1)(2x^2-4x+4)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%282x%5E2-3x%2B2%29%5E2%7D%7B%282x%5E2-2x%2B1%29%282x%5E2-4x%2B4%29%7D)

![=\frac{4x^4-12x^3+17x^2-12x+4}{4x^4-12x^3+18x^2-12x+4}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B4x%5E4-12x%5E3%2B17x%5E2-12x%2B4%7D%7B4x%5E4-12x%5E3%2B18x%5E2-12x%2B4%7D)

注意到很多项的次数相同，考虑用1减

![=1-\frac{x^2}{4x^4-12x^3+18x^2-12x+4}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D1-%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E2%7D%7B4x%5E4-12x%5E3%2B18x%5E2-12x%2B4%7D)

![=1-\frac{1}{4x^2-12x+18-\frac{12}{x}+\frac{4}{x^2}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D1-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B4x%5E2-12x%2B18-%5Cfrac%7B12%7D%7Bx%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B4%7D%7Bx%5E2%7D%7D)

对分母求导，结果为

![8x-12+\frac{12}{x^2}-\frac{8}{x^3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=8x-12%2B%5Cfrac%7B12%7D%7Bx%5E2%7D-%5Cfrac%7B8%7D%7Bx%5E3%7D)

![=\frac{8(x^4-1)-12x(x-1)}{x^3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B8%28x%5E4-1%29-12x%28x-1%29%7D%7Bx%5E3%7D)

![=\frac{4(2x^3+2x^2-x+2)}{x^3}(x-1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5Cfrac%7B4%282x%5E3%2B2x%5E2-x%2B2%29%7D%7Bx%5E3%7D%28x-1%29)

![2x^3+2x^2-x+1=2x^3+2(x-\frac{1}{4})^2+\frac{7}{8}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2x%5E3%2B2x%5E2-x%2B1%3D2x%5E3%2B2%28x-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B4%7D%29%5E2%2B%5Cfrac%7B7%7D%7B8%7D) ，在 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 为正时，恒大于 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0)

因此 ![x=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D1) 时，有最小值 ![cos\angle APB=\frac{\sqrt2}{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=cos%5Cangle+APB%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt2%7D%7B2%7D)
