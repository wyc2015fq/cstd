![12_{(n)}\to n+2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=12_%7B%28n%29%7D%5Cto+n%2B2)

![1331_{(n)}\to n^3+3n^2+3n+1\to(n+1)^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1331_%7B%28n%29%7D%5Cto+n%5E3%2B3n%5E2%2B3n%2B1%5Cto%28n%2B1%29%5E3)

所以原式化为 ![2^{n+2}=(n+1)^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D%3D%28n%2B1%29%5E3)

考虑到 ![n+1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B1) 是 ![2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2) 的倍数，显然 ![n=7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D7) 是一个解

## **下面证明唯一性（重要！！）**

![n=4,5,6](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D4%2C5%2C6) 时，带入式子显然不满足

考虑数学归纳法

假设对于 ![8,9,10,\cdot\cdot\cdot,n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=8%2C9%2C10%2C%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%2Cn) ，有 ![2^{n+2}>(n+1)^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D%3E%28n%2B1%29%5E3) 成立

显然 ![n=8](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D8) 时成立

下面我们来证明对于 ![n+1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B1) 也成立

![2^{n+3}-(n+2)^3=2^{n+2}+2^{n+2}-(n+1)^3-3(n+1)^2-3(n+1)-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E%7Bn%2B3%7D-%28n%2B2%29%5E3%3D2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D%2B2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D-%28n%2B1%29%5E3-3%28n%2B1%29%5E2-3%28n%2B1%29-1)

![=[2^{n+2}-(n+1)^3]+2^{n+2}-(3n^2+9n+7)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3D%5B2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D-%28n%2B1%29%5E3%5D%2B2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D-%283n%5E2%2B9n%2B7%29)

我们知道对于范围内的 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) ，有![n^3>3n^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5E3%3E3n%5E2) 成立

注意 ![n^2-9n-7>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5E2-9n-7%3E0) 的解集完全包括 ![n>7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3E7) ，因此有 ![n^3>n^2>9n+7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5E3%3En%5E2%3E9n%2B7)

![[2^{n+2}-(n+1)^3]+2^{n+2}-(3n^2+9n+7)>[2^{n+2}-(n+1)^3]+2^{n+2}-2n^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D-%28n%2B1%29%5E3%5D%2B2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D-%283n%5E2%2B9n%2B7%29%3E%5B2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D-%28n%2B1%29%5E3%5D%2B2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D-2n%5E3)

![2^{n+2}-2n^3=2(2^{n+1}-n^3)\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D-2n%5E3%3D2%282%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D-n%5E3%29%5Cgeq0)

因此 ![2^{n+3}>(n+2)^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E%7Bn%2B3%7D%3E%28n%2B2%29%5E3) 也成立

根据数学归纳法， ![2^{n+2}>(n+1)^3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D%3E%28n%2B1%29%5E3) 对于大于 ![7](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=7) 的整数 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 均成立

唯一性证明完了~
