本题答题时间为**90**分钟


考虑定义域为全体实数，且不一定连续的函数 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 。假设 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 满足：
- ![f(x+y)=f(x)+f(y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%2By%29%3Df%28x%29%2Bf%28y%29)
- ![f(xy)=f(x)f(y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28xy%29%3Df%28x%29f%28y%29)
- ![f(1)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%281%29%3D1)

试证明：
- 对于任何有理数 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) ，都有 ![f(x)=x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Dx)
- 对于实数 ![x,y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%2Cy) ，如果 ![x\leq y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cleq+y) 则 ![f(x)\leq f(y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%5Cleq+f%28y%29)
- 对于任何实数 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) ，都有 ![f(x)=x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Dx)
