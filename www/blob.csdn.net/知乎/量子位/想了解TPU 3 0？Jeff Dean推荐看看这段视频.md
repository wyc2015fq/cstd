昨天，[Google I/O 2018大会首日上](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMzIzNjc1NzUzMw%3D%3D%26mid%3D2247498171%26idx%3D1%26sn%3D6af8bfbcf42265f5240cec37c70cb7eb%26chksm%3De8d04ec9dfa7c7dff88c2b71f18f93e23903ed74b429adabb5830844d8d3c9504b4265e2484a%26scene%3D21%23wechat_redirect)，TPU 3.0正式披露。
