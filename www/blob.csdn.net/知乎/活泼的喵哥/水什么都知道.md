Empty your mind,  

be formless. Shapeless, 

like water. 

If you put water into a cup,

it becomes the cup. 

You put water into a bottle 

and it becomes the bottle. 

You put it in a teapot, 

it becomes the teapot. 

water can flow or it can crash. 

Be water, my friend.
