2. **Restriction****and****corestriction homomorphism**

回忆我们在上一篇note中给出的Res map，![\text{Res}:H^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow H^{r}(H,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BRes%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28H%2CM%29) ，其中，![\alpha:H\rightarrow G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3AH%5Crightarrow+G) 是inclusion， ![\beta:M\rightarrow M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%3AM%5Crightarrow+M) 是identity。然而Shapiro's lemma 告诉我们：

![H^{r}(G,\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(M))\simeq H^{r}(H,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2C%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28M%29%29%5Csimeq+H%5E%7Br%7D%28H%2CM%29) 。于是我们把Res写成如下![\text{Res}:H^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow H^{r}(G,\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(M))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BRes%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2C%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28M%29%29)

具体地，compatible pair ![(\alpha,\beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta%29) 定义为：

(1) ![\alpha:G\rightarrow G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3AG%5Crightarrow+G) ， ![\beta:M\rightarrow\text{Ind}_{H}^{G}(M),m\mapsto\varphi_{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%3AM%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7BH%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28M%29%2Cm%5Cmapsto%5Cvarphi_%7Bm%7D) ，其中 ![\varphi_{m}(g)=gm](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi_%7Bm%7D%28g%29%3Dgm)**（**）**。

这个 ![\beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta) 是well-defined，因为有交换图：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-dc7ba52884751d03d567ced5e29b1119_b.jpg)
它由以下的交换图诱导出：
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a30d11cdb1c294ecc927efafe64df080_b.jpg)
交换是因为**（**）**： ![\varphi_{m}(1_{G})=1_{G}\cdot m=m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi_%7Bm%7D%281_%7BG%7D%29%3D1_%7BG%7D%5Ccdot+m%3Dm) 。

**我们有时在使用Res map时需要使用上面这种等价定义**。

现在我们给出corestrction的定义， ![\text{Cor}:H^{r}(H,M)\simeq H^{r}(G,\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(M))\rightarrow H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BCor%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28H%2CM%29%5Csimeq+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2C%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28M%29%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29)

其中compatible pair ![(\alpha,\beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta%29) 定义为

(1) ![\alpha:G\rightarrow G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3AG%5Crightarrow+G) 是identity

(2) ![\beta:\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(M)\rightarrow M,\varphi\mapsto\Sigma_{s\in S}s\varphi(s^{-1}) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%3A%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28M%29%5Crightarrow+M%2C%5Cvarphi%5Cmapsto%5CSigma_%7Bs%5Cin+S%7Ds%5Cvarphi%28s%5E%7B-1%7D%29+) ，其中 ![G=\cup_{s\in S}sH](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G%3D%5Ccup_%7Bs%5Cin+S%7DsH) 是left coset的disjoint union。

3. **The ![{\rm Cor}\circ{\rm Res}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7B%5Crm+Cor%7D%5Ccirc%7B%5Crm+Res%7D) map **

现在我们给出这篇note最重要的一个结果。

**Proposition 2**. 设H是G的finite-index subgroup，于是

![{\rm Cor}\circ{\rm Res}:H^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7B%5Crm+Cor%7D%5Ccirc%7B%5Crm+Res%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29)
是multiplication by ![(G:H)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28G%3AH%29) 。

*Proof*. 只用写成compatible pair 中的 ![\beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta) 即可：

![{\rm Cor}\circ{\rm Res}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7B%5Crm+Cor%7D%5Ccirc%7B%5Crm+Res%7D) 由 ![C^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow C^{r}(G,\text{Ind}_{H}^{G}(M))\rightarrow C^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+C%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2C%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7BH%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28M%29%29%5Crightarrow+C%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29) 给出 ，**而Res和Cor中的 ![\beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta) 怎么定义我们在上面已经讨论清楚了**：

![M\rightarrow\text{Ind}_{H}^{G}(M)\rightarrow M,m\mapsto\varphi_{m}\mapsto\sum_{s\in S}s\varphi_{m}(s^{-1})=\sum_{s\in S}s\cdot s^{-1}m=|S|\cdot m=(G:H)\cdot m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7BH%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28M%29%5Crightarrow+M%2Cm%5Cmapsto%5Cvarphi_%7Bm%7D%5Cmapsto%5Csum_%7Bs%5Cin+S%7Ds%5Cvarphi_%7Bm%7D%28s%5E%7B-1%7D%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bs%5Cin+S%7Ds%5Ccdot+s%5E%7B-1%7Dm%3D%7CS%7C%5Ccdot+m%3D%28G%3AH%29%5Ccdot+m)

于是证毕。

由上面这个性质我们可以得到一些简单的观察：

**[1].** 若 ![|G|=n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7CG%7C%3Dn) ，那么 ![nH^{r}(G,M)=0,r\geq0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=nH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%3D0%2Cr%5Cgeq0) 。

事实上， ![{\rm Cor}\circ{\rm Res}:H^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow H^{r}(\{1\},M)=0\rightarrow H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7B%5Crm+Cor%7D%5Ccirc%7B%5Crm+Res%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28%5C%7B1%5C%7D%2CM%29%3D0%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29) 。

**[2].** 若G是有限群，M是有限生成G-module，则 ![H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29) 是有限群。

*Proof*. [1]说明 ![H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29) 中每个元素阶有限，我们**只用说明 ![H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29) 有限生成**：

G是有限群，那么Z[G]是有限生成abelian group。又因为M是有限生成G-module，所以M是有限生成abelian group，我们回忆上同调群的定义：

![0\rightarrow...\rightarrow\text{Hom}_{G}(F_{r},M)\rightarrow\text{Hom}_{G}(F_{r+1},M)\rightarrow... ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Crightarrow...%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28F_%7Br%7D%2CM%29%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28F_%7Br%2B1%7D%2CM%29%5Crightarrow...+) ， ![H^{n}(G,M)=\frac{\text{Ker}d^{n}}{\text{Im}d^{n-1}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Bn%7D%28G%2CM%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctext%7BKer%7Dd%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%7B%5Ctext%7BIm%7Dd%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D) 。

现在注意到，![\text{Hom}_{G}(F_{r},M)\subseteq \text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(F_{r},M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28F_%7Br%7D%2CM%29%5Csubseteq+%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28F_%7Br%7D%2CM%29) ，其中![\text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(F_{r},M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28F_%7Br%7D%2CM%29) 是有限生成abelian group：

![F_{r}=\bigoplus_{(g_{1},...,g_{r})\in G^{r}}\mathbb{Z}\cdot (g_{1},...,g_{r})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F_%7Br%7D%3D%5Cbigoplus_%7B%28g_%7B1%7D%2C...%2Cg_%7Br%7D%29%5Cin+G%5E%7Br%7D%7D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5Ccdot+%28g_%7B1%7D%2C...%2Cg_%7Br%7D%29)

![M\simeq\mathbb{Z}^{n}\oplus\mathbb{Z}_{q1}\oplus\cdot\cdot\cdot\mathbb{Z}_{qt}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%5Csimeq%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Coplus%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7Bq1%7D%5Coplus%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7Bqt%7D)

![\text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(F_{r},M)\simeq \bigoplus_{(g_{1},...,g_{r})\in G^{r}}\text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Z}\cdot (g_{1},...,g_{r}),\mathbb{Z}^{n}\oplus\mathbb{Z}_{q1}\oplus\cdot\cdot\cdot\mathbb{Z}_{qt})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28F_%7Br%7D%2CM%29%5Csimeq+%5Cbigoplus_%7B%28g_%7B1%7D%2C...%2Cg_%7Br%7D%29%5Cin+G%5E%7Br%7D%7D%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5Ccdot+%28g_%7B1%7D%2C...%2Cg_%7Br%7D%29%2C%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Coplus%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7Bq1%7D%5Coplus%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7Bqt%7D%29) 。

G有限，那么direct sum下标有限，另一方面direct summand同构于

![\big(\bigoplus_{n}\text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Z},\mathbb{Z})\big)\oplus\big(\bigoplus_{1\leq i\leq t}\text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Z},\mathbb{Z}_{q_{t}})\big)=\{1\}^{n}\oplus\big(\bigoplus_{1\leq i \leq t}\mathbb{Z}_{q_{i}}^{\times}\big)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbig%28%5Cbigoplus_%7Bn%7D%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2C%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%29%5Cbig%29%5Coplus%5Cbig%28%5Cbigoplus_%7B1%5Cleq+i%5Cleq+t%7D%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2C%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7Bq_%7Bt%7D%7D%29%5Cbig%29%3D%5C%7B1%5C%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Coplus%5Cbig%28%5Cbigoplus_%7B1%5Cleq+i+%5Cleq+t%7D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7Bq_%7Bi%7D%7D%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%5Cbig%29)

所以得到：

所以 ![\text{Hom}_{G}(F_{r},M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28F_%7Br%7D%2CM%29) ， ![\text{Ker}d^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BKer%7Dd%5E%7Bn%7D) 以及 ![H^{n}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Bn%7D%28G%2CM%29) 都是有限生成abelian group。

**[3].** 设 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 是有限群， ![G_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G_%7Bp%7D) 是它的Sylow p-subgroup，![H^{r}(G,M)_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29_%7Bp%7D) 是 ![H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29) 的p-primary component。那么

(1) ![\text{Res}:H^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow H^{r}(G_{p},M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BRes%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G_%7Bp%7D%2CM%29) 限制在 ![H^{r}(G,M)_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29_%7Bp%7D) 为单射，

(2) 存在injection ![\theta:H^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow\oplus_{p}H^{r}(G_{p},M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctheta%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow%5Coplus_%7Bp%7DH%5E%7Br%7D%28G_%7Bp%7D%2CM%29) 。
Proof. 假设![x\in H^{r}(G,M)_{p},{\rm Res}_{p}(x)=0, (G:G_{p})=m,(p,m)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29_%7Bp%7D%2C%7B%5Crm+Res%7D_%7Bp%7D%28x%29%3D0%2C+%28G%3AG_%7Bp%7D%29%3Dm%2C%28p%2Cm%29%3D1)。考虑

![{\rm Cor}\circ{\rm Res}_{p}:H^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow H^{r}(G_{p},M)\rightarrow H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7B%5Crm+Cor%7D%5Ccirc%7B%5Crm+Res%7D_%7Bp%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G_%7Bp%7D%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29)
