# 局部域（二）完备化 - 知乎
# 

**（一）完备化（metric）**

首先回忆度量空间的完备化，key idea是把“等价的Cauchy列 ![\overline{(a_{n})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7B%28a_%7Bn%7D%29%7D) ”当作元素，两个元素的距离定义为 ![d(\overline{(a_{n})},\overline{(b_{n})})=\lim_{n\rightarrow \infty}d(a_{n},b_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28%5Coverline%7B%28a_%7Bn%7D%29%7D%2C%5Coverline%7B%28b_%7Bn%7D%29%7D%29%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Dd%28a_%7Bn%7D%2Cb_%7Bn%7D%29) ，**后者存在是因为可以验证 ![d(a_{n},b_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28a_%7Bn%7D%2Cb_%7Bn%7D%29) 是 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 中的Cauchy列，而 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 在绝对值诱导的度量下是完备的**。即以下定理：

**Th1.**![(X,d)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2Cd%29) 是一个度量空间，完备化是 ![(\widehat{X},{\varphi}:X\rightarrow\widehat{X})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cwidehat%7BX%7D%2C%7B%5Cvarphi%7D%3AX%5Crightarrow%5Cwidehat%7BX%7D%29) 满足：

（1） ![(\widehat{X},\widehat{d})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cwidehat%7BX%7D%2C%5Cwidehat%7Bd%7D%29) 是完备度量空间。（2） ![\varphi(X)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%28X%29) 在 ![\widehat{X}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BX%7D) 中稠密：对于任意 ![\widehat{x}\in \widehat{X}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%5Cin+%5Cwidehat%7BX%7D)，存在 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 中的Cauchy列 ![(a_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_%7Bn%7D%29) 使得 ![\widehat{d}(\varphi(x_{n}),\widehat{x})\rightarrow0,n\rightarrow\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7Bd%7D%28%5Cvarphi%28x_%7Bn%7D%29%2C%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%29%5Crightarrow0%2Cn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty) 。

（3） ![\varphi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi) 是保距映射： ![\widehat{d}(\varphi(x),\varphi(y))=d(x,y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7Bd%7D%28%5Cvarphi%28x%29%2C%5Cvarphi%28y%29%29%3Dd%28x%2Cy%29) 。

并且完备化在保距双射意义下唯一。

我们把赋值域完备化，并且最关心的是nonarchimedean的情形。为了了解具体构造，我们写一个略微详细的证明。

**Th2. **![(K,|\cdot|)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28K%2C%7C%5Ccdot%7C%29) 是一个赋值域， ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 是一个**离散的、nonarchimedean的绝对值**，**![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 是由 ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 诱导的normalized的离散赋值（假设存在）**。那么存在唯一（在保绝对值同构意义下）的完备化 ![(\widehat{K},\varphi:K\rightarrow\widehat{K})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D%2C%5Cvarphi%3AK%5Crightarrow%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D%29) :

（1） ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 是一个域， ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 延拓成 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 上的离散的、nonarchimedean的绝对值。

（2） ![{\varphi}:K\rightarrow\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7B%5Cvarphi%7D%3AK%5Crightarrow%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 是保绝对值的， ![\varphi(K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%28K%29) 在 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 中稠密。

**Proof.（a detailed sketch）**

** Step1 定义**![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) :

![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 的元素定义为 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 中等价的Cauchy列 ![\overline{(a_{n})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7B%28a_%7Bn%7D%29%7D) ： ![(a_{n})\sim (b_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_%7Bn%7D%29%5Csim+%28b_%7Bn%7D%29) 当且仅当 ![|a_{n}-b_{n}|\rightarrow0,n\rightarrow\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Ca_%7Bn%7D-b_%7Bn%7D%7C%5Crightarrow0%2Cn%5Crightarrow%5Cinfty) 。（容易验证 ![\sim ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csim+) 是 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 中Cauchy列全体上的等价关系）

**Step2 延拓**![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) ，**说明**![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D)**是域**：

对于 ![\widehat{x}=\overline{(a_{n})}\in\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%3D%5Coverline%7B%28a_%7Bn%7D%29%7D%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D)，定义 ![|\widehat{x}|=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}|a_{n}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%7C%3D%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C) 。

**Ex1. **验证 ![|\widehat{x}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%7C) 是well defined的，即 ![\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}|a_{n}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C) 存在并且与代表元的选取无关。

（**Hint：**![\{|a_{n}|\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C%5C%7D) 是 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 中的Cauchy列，用三角不等式证明 ![||a_{n}|-|b_{n}||\leq|a_{n}-b_{n}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C-%7Cb_%7Bn%7D%7C%7C%5Cleq%7Ca_%7Bn%7D-b_%7Bn%7D%7C) ，左边那个外层的绝对值是实数中的绝对值，以及 ![||b_{n}|-a|\leq||b_{n}|-|a_{n}||+||a_{n}|-a|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%7Cb_%7Bn%7D%7C-a%7C%5Cleq%7C%7Cb_%7Bn%7D%7C-%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C%7C%2B%7C%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C-a%7C) 。）

**Ex2. ![|\widehat{x}|=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}|a_{n}|=a>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%7C%3D%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C%3Da%3E0) 时，说明实际上从某项 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 开始 ![|\widehat{x}|=|a_{n}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%7C%3D%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C)** 。

（**Hint：**利用nonarchimedean绝对值的一个性质： ![|a+b|\ne\text{max}\{|a|,|b|\}\Rightarrow|a|=|b|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Ca%2Bb%7C%5Cne%5Ctext%7Bmax%7D%5C%7B%7Ca%7C%2C%7Cb%7C%5C%7D%5CRightarrow%7Ca%7C%3D%7Cb%7C) 以及从某项开始 ![|a_{n}|,|a_{m}|\in(a-\varepsilon,a+\varepsilon),|a_{n}-a_{m}|<\varepsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Ca_%7Bn%7D%7C%2C%7Ca_%7Bm%7D%7C%5Cin%28a-%5Cvarepsilon%2Ca%2B%5Cvarepsilon%29%2C%7Ca_%7Bn%7D-a_%7Bm%7D%7C%3C%5Cvarepsilon) ，其中取 ![\varepsilon<a/3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3Ca%2F3) 。）

![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 是一个域： ![\widehat{x}=\overline{(x_{n})},\widehat{y}=\overline{(y_{n})}\in\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%3D%5Coverline%7B%28x_%7Bn%7D%29%7D%2C%5Cwidehat%7By%7D%3D%5Coverline%7B%28y_%7Bn%7D%29%7D%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 定义加法和乘法为

![\widehat{x}+\widehat{y}=\overline{(x_{n}+y_{n})},\widehat{x}\cdot\widehat{y}=\overline{(x_{n}y_{n})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%2B%5Cwidehat%7By%7D%3D%5Coverline%7B%28x_%7Bn%7D%2By_%7Bn%7D%29%7D%2C%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%5Ccdot%5Cwidehat%7By%7D%3D%5Coverline%7B%28x_%7Bn%7Dy_%7Bn%7D%29%7D) 。

**Ex3. **证明 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 在上述加法和乘法下构成一个域。

在说明了 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 是一个域以后，我们要说明 ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 的确是一个离散的、nonarchimedean的绝对值。这里只有“离散”要说明一下：**利用Ex2证明 ![|\widehat{K}^{\times}|=|K^{\times}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%7C%3D%7CK%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%7C) 即可**。

**Ex4.**![|\widehat{K}^{\times}|=|K^{\times}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%7C%3D%7CK%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%7C) ，于是 ![|\widehat{K}^{\times}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%7C) 在 ![\mathbb{R}_{>0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D_%7B%3E0%7D) 中离散。

**Step3 **定义嵌入 ![\varphi:K\rightarrow\widehat{K},a\mapsto\overline{(a,a,a,...,a...)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%3AK%5Crightarrow%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D%2Ca%5Cmapsto%5Coverline%7B%28a%2Ca%2Ca%2C...%2Ca...%29%7D) ，以及 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 上度量 ![\widehat{d}(\widehat{x},\widehat{y})=|\widehat{x}-\widehat{y}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7Bd%7D%28%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%2C%5Cwidehat%7By%7D%29%3D%7C%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D-%5Cwidehat%7By%7D%7C) 。

那么显然 ![\varphi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi) 是保绝对值的单同态。

我们注意到![\widehat{d}(\widehat{x},\widehat{y})=|\widehat{x}-\widehat{y}|=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}|x_{n}-y_{n}|=\lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}d(x_{n},y_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7Bd%7D%28%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D%2C%5Cwidehat%7By%7D%29%3D%7C%5Cwidehat%7Bx%7D-%5Cwidehat%7By%7D%7C%3D%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7D%7Cx_%7Bn%7D-y_%7Bn%7D%7C%3D%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Crightarrow+%5Cinfty%7Dd%28x_%7Bn%7D%2Cy_%7Bn%7D%29) 。也就是说，通过**延拓绝对值而诱导的度量和度量空间完备化得到的度量是一样的，**所以由Th1可知 ![\varphi(K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%28K%29) 在 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 中稠密，并且 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 在保绝对值同构下唯一。

**（二）赋值环**

考虑： ![\widehat{A}=\{a\in\widehat{K}~|~|a|\leq1\},\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}=\{a\in\widehat{K}~|~|a|<1\} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D%3D%5C%7Ba%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D~%7C~%7Ca%7C%5Cleq1%5C%7D%2C%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%3D%5C%7Ba%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D~%7C~%7Ca%7C%3C1%5C%7D+)

**Claim1.**![\widehat{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D) （ ![\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D) ）是 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A)（ ![\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) ） 在 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D) 中的闭包。

**Proof.****注意到 ![\widehat{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D) 即 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 中所有Cauchy列极限构成的集合**： 显然![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 中所有Cauchy列的极限都在 ![\widehat{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D) 中；反过来，如果 ![a=\overline{(a_{n})}\in\widehat{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3D%5Coverline%7B%28a_%7Bn%7D%29%7D%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D) ，那么从某一项开始 ![a_{n}\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7Bn%7D%5Cin+A) ，前面的项无关紧要，自然可以把 ![(a_{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28a_%7Bn%7D%29) 看作 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 中的Cauchy列。

**于是 ![\widehat{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D) 由 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 在**![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D)**中的所有极限点构成，是其在**![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D)**中闭包。**同样可说明 ![\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D) 是 ![\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) 的闭包。

注意到如果 ![\mathfrak{m}=(\pi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%3D%28%5Cpi%29) ，那么 ![\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}=(\pi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%3D%28%5Cpi%29) 。

**Claim2.** 对于每个正整数 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) ， ![A/\mathfrak{m}^{n}=\widehat{A}/\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2F%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%3D%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D%2F%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%5E%7Bn%7D) 。

**Proof. **注意到 ![A/\mathfrak{m}^{n}\rightarrow\widehat{A}/\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}^{n}~\text{injective}\Leftrightarrow~A\cap\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}^{n}=\mathfrak{m}^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2F%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Crightarrow%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D%2F%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%5E%7Bn%7D~%5Ctext%7Binjective%7D%5CLeftrightarrow~A%5Ccap%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%3D%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%5E%7Bn%7D)。

**其次由定义可知****![A ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A+) 在 ![\widehat{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D) 中稠密**：即对于 ![a\in\widehat{A}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D) ，可找到 ![a_{0}\in A ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7B0%7D%5Cin+A+) 使得 ![v(a-a_{0})>n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28a-a_%7B0%7D%29%3En) 。

那么 ![a-a_{0}\in\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a-a_%7B0%7D%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%5E%7Bn%7D) ，所以 ![a=a_{0}+(a-a_{0})\in A+\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Da_%7B0%7D%2B%28a-a_%7B0%7D%29%5Cin+A%2B%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%5E%7Bn%7D) ，即 ![A/\mathfrak{m}^{n}\rightarrow\widehat{A}/\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2F%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Crightarrow%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D%2F%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%5E%7Bn%7D) 满射。

**Example.**![\mathbb{Z}/p^{n}\mathbb{Z}\simeq\mathbb{Z}_{(p)}/p^{n}\mathbb{Z}_{(p)}\simeq\mathbb{Z}_{p}/p^{n}\mathbb{Z}_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2Fp%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5Csimeq%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7B%28p%29%7D%2Fp%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7B%28p%29%7D%5Csimeq%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7Bp%7D%2Fp%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7Bp%7D)

（第一个同构是局部化的标准结果）

**（三） ![\mathfrak{m}-\text{adic}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D-%5Ctext%7Badic%7D) 展开**

**Claim3. **设 ![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) 是 ![A/\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2F%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) 的代表构成的集合，并且 ![\mathfrak{m}=(\pi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%3D%28%5Cpi%29) 。那么 ![\{s_{M}=\sum_{i=-n}^{M}a_{i}\pi^{i}~|~a_{i}\in S\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bs_%7BM%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D-n%7D%5E%7BM%7Da_%7Bi%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bi%7D~%7C~a_%7Bi%7D%5Cin+S%5C%7D) 是Cauchy列。并且 ![\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D)**中任一个元素 ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 都可以唯一表示成 ![a_{0}\pi^{n}+a_{1}\pi^{n+1}+a_{2}\pi^{n+2}...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7B0%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D%2Ba_%7B1%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%2Ba_%7B2%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D...) ，其中：**

**![a_{0}\ne0,v(\alpha)=n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7B0%7D%5Cne0%2Cv%28%5Calpha%29%3Dn) 。**

**Proof.** 第一个断言是trivial的。

任取 ![\alpha\in\widehat{K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BK%7D)，假设 ![v(\alpha)=n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28%5Calpha%29%3Dn) ，则 ![v(\pi^{-n}\alpha)=0\Rightarrow\pi^{-n}\alpha\in\widehat{A}^{\times}\Rightarrow\alpha=\pi^{n}\alpha_{0},\alpha_{0}\in\widehat{A}^{\times}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28%5Cpi%5E%7B-n%7D%5Calpha%29%3D0%5CRightarrow%5Cpi%5E%7B-n%7D%5Calpha%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%5CRightarrow%5Calpha%3D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D%5Calpha_%7B0%7D%2C%5Calpha_%7B0%7D%5Cin%5Cwidehat%7BA%7D%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D) 。

......

![\alpha_{0}-a_{0}-a_{1}\pi-...-a_{n}\pi^{n}\in\pi^{n}\widehat{\mathfrak{m}}\Rightarrow|\alpha_{0}-\sum_{i=0}^{n}a_{i}\pi^{i}|\leq|\pi^{n}|\rightarrow0\Rightarrow\alpha_{0}=\sum_{i=0}^{\infty}a_{i}\pi^{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha_%7B0%7D-a_%7B0%7D-a_%7B1%7D%5Cpi-...-a_%7Bn%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cin%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cwidehat%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%7D%5CRightarrow%7C%5Calpha_%7B0%7D-%5Csum_%7Bi%3D0%7D%5E%7Bn%7Da_%7Bi%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bi%7D%7C%5Cleq%7C%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D%7C%5Crightarrow0%5CRightarrow%5Calpha_%7B0%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Da_%7Bi%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bi%7D) 。

于是， ![\alpha=a_{0}\pi^{n}+a_{1}\pi^{n+1}+a_{2}\pi^{n+2}...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3Da_%7B0%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D%2Ba_%7B1%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%2Ba_%7B2%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%2B2%7D...) ，或者记为 ![\alpha=\sum_{i=n}^{\infty}c_{i}\pi^{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3Dn%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7Dc_%7Bi%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bi%7D)

**注意到，如果令 ![s_{m}=\sum_{i=n}^{m}c_{i}\pi^{i},m\geq n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s_%7Bm%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3Dn%7D%5E%7Bm%7Dc_%7Bi%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bi%7D%2Cm%5Cgeq+n) ，那么由构造可知：**

**唯一性：**

注意到上面的证明说明了：**如果 ![\alpha=\sum_{i}c_{i}\pi^{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%7Dc_%7Bi%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bi%7D) ，第一个不为 ![0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0) 的 ![c_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_%7Bi%7D) 是 ![c_{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_%7Bm%7D) ，则 ![v(\alpha)=m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28%5Calpha%29%3Dm) ，那么 ![\alpha=\sum_{i}c_{i}\pi^{i}=0\Leftrightarrow c_{i}=0,\text{for all}~i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%7Dc_%7Bi%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bi%7D%3D0%5CLeftrightarrow+c_%7Bi%7D%3D0%2C%5Ctext%7Bfor+all%7D~i) 。**
