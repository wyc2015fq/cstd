于是，我们要得到上同调群 ![H^{r}(G,M),H^{r}(G^{\prime},M^{\prime})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%2CH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2CM%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%29) 之间的morphism，只需要给出complexes（resolutions）之间的morphisms即可 ![C^{r}(G,M),C^{r}(G^{\prime},M^{\prime})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%2CC%5E%7Br%7D%28G%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2CM%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%29) 。即有以下交换图：

现在我们的问题是，**我们应该去研究哪些morphisms/compatible pairs ![(\alpha,\beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta%29) ?**

回到**我们的主要目的——研究Galois group G，所以我们先考虑Galois groups之间、fields之间有哪些morphisms？**

设L/K是Galois extension，那么根据Galois correspondence： ![L\supseteq E\supseteq K\leftrightarrow1\leq H\leq G ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Csupseteq+E%5Csupseteq+K%5Cleftrightarrow1%5Cleq+H%5Cleq+G+) ， ![G,H](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G%2CH) acts on ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 。那么自然可以考虑                             (1) ![\alpha:H\rightarrow G,\beta:L\rightarrow L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3AH%5Crightarrow+G%2C%5Cbeta%3AL%5Crightarrow+L)

(2) 特别地，如果 ![H](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H) 是 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 的正规子群，那么![ H=\text{Gal}(L/E),G/H=\text{Gal}(E/K),L^{H}=E](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+H%3D%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FE%29%2CG%2FH%3D%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28E%2FK%29%2CL%5E%7BH%7D%3DE) ， ![G/H](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G%2FH) acts on ![L^{H}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5E%7BH%7D) 。

于是可以考虑： ![\alpha:G\rightarrow G/H,\beta:L^{H}\rightarrow L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3AG%5Crightarrow+G%2FH%2C%5Cbeta%3AL%5E%7BH%7D%5Crightarrow+L) 。

容易验证 ![(\alpha,\beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta%29) 是compatible pair。

于是，我们可以考虑：

**Def 1**. *Restriction homomorphism*设 ![H](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H) 是 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 的子群， ![\alpha:H\rightarrow G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3AH%5Crightarrow+G) 是inclusion， ![\beta:M\rightarrow M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%3AM%5Crightarrow+M) 是identity G-homomorphism。我们得到

![\text{Res}:H^{r}(G,M)\rightarrow H^{r}(H,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BRes%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28H%2CM%29)

**Def 2**. *Inflation homomorphism*设 ![H](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H) 是 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 的正规子群， ![\alpha:G\rightarrow G/H](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3AG%5Crightarrow+G%2FH) 是quotient map， ![\beta:M^{H}\rightarrow M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%3AM%5E%7BH%7D%5Crightarrow+M) 是inclusion，于是得到

![\text{Inf}:H^{r}(G/H,M^{H})\rightarrow H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInf%7D%3AH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2FH%2CM%5E%7BH%7D%29%5Crightarrow+H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29)

当然，为了实现我们的主要目的，我们还需要处理其他的compatible pair ![(\alpha,\beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta%29) 。这些内容是我们接下来几篇notes着重讨论的部分。

这篇note的剩余部分我们介绍*Shapiro's Lemma*：

(1)它揭示了 ![H^{r}(G,\cdot),H^{r}(H,\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2C%5Ccdot%29%2CH%5E%7Br%7D%28H%2C%5Ccdot%29) 之间的关系。**（实际上后面会说明，该引理就是说：两个函子 ![(~\cdot~)^{H},(~\cdot~)^{G}\circ\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(~\cdot~)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BH%7D%2C%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BG%7D%5Ccirc%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28~%5Ccdot~%29) 分别的导出函子同构）**

(2)方便用它来定义一些compatible pair ![(\alpha,\beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Calpha%2C%5Cbeta%29) ，例如*corestriction*.

首先我们需要给出*induced module*的定义。

2.** Induced modules**

(1) objects： ![(M,\beta)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28M%2C%5Cbeta%29) ，其中 ![M\in\text{obj}(\text{Mod}_{G})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%5Cin%5Ctext%7Bobj%7D%28%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BG%7D%29) ， ![\beta\in\text{Hom}_{H}(M,N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%5Cin%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BH%7D%28M%2CN%29) 。

(2) morphisms: ![f:(M,\beta)\rightarrow (M^{\prime},\beta^{\prime})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3A%28M%2C%5Cbeta%29%5Crightarrow+%28M%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2C%5Cbeta%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%29) ，其中 ![f\in\text{Hom}_{G}(M,M^{\prime})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28M%2CM%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%29) ，并且有交换图
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a383cfaaf76ffb386a6428d4706f51c2_b.jpg)
我们来给出 ![\mathcal{C}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathcal%7BC%7D) 中的**universal object **![(\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(N),\phi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28N%29%2C%5Cphi%29) ：

定义 ![\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(N)=\{\varphi:G\rightarrow N~|~\varphi(hg)=h\varphi(g),h\in H\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28N%29%3D%5C%7B%5Cvarphi%3AG%5Crightarrow+N~%7C~%5Cvarphi%28hg%29%3Dh%5Cvarphi%28g%29%2Ch%5Cin+H%5C%7D) ，以及

同时，定义 ![\phi:\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(N)\rightarrow N,\phi(\varphi)=\varphi(1_{G})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%3A%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28N%29%5Crightarrow+N%2C%5Cphi%28%5Cvarphi%29%3D%5Cvarphi%281_%7BG%7D%29) 。

**Exercise 2.**![\phi\in\text{Hom}_{H}(\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(N),N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%5Cin%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BH%7D%28%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28N%29%2CN%29) 。

为了说明universal property。只用说明以下引理。

事实上，对于任意 ![\alpha\in\text{Hom}_{G}(M,\text{Ind}_{G}^{H}(N))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Cin%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28M%2C%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7BG%7D%5E%7BH%7D%28N%29%29) ， ![\alpha\mapsto\beta](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Cmapsto%5Cbeta) ，

定义 ![\beta(m)=\alpha(m)(1_{G})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%28m%29%3D%5Calpha%28m%29%281_%7BG%7D%29) ；反过来，任给 ![\beta\in\text{Hom}_{H}(M,N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%5Cin%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BH%7D%28M%2CN%29) ， ![\beta\mapsto\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%5Cmapsto%5Calpha) ，定义

![\alpha(m)(g)=\beta(gm)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%28m%29%28g%29%3D%5Cbeta%28gm%29) 。

**[2]. Description of **![\text{Ind}_{H}^{G}(N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7BH%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29)

定义 ![\text{Hom}_{H}(\mathbb{Z}[G],N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BH%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%2CN%29) 上的G-module structure：

(1) ![(\varphi+\varphi^{\prime})(g)=\varphi(g)+\varphi^{\prime}(g)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cvarphi%2B%5Cvarphi%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%29%28g%29%3D%5Cvarphi%28g%29%2B%5Cvarphi%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%28g%29) ，

(2) ![(g\varphi)(x)=g(\varphi(g^{-1}x))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28g%5Cvarphi%29%28x%29%3Dg%28%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7Dx%29%29) 。

**Proposition 1. **存在G-module之间的同构

![\text{Ind}_{H}^{G}(N)\simeq\text{Hom}_{H}(\mathbb{Z}[G],N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7BH%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29%5Csimeq%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BH%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%2CN%29)

*Proof. *事实上，任给 ![\varphi\in\text{Ind}_{H}^{G}(N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%5Cin%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7BH%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29) ，可延拓成 ![\varphi:\mathbb{Z}[G]\rightarrow N,\varphi(\sum_{g}n_{g}g)=\sum_{g}n_{g}\varphi(g)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%3A%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%5Crightarrow+N%2C%5Cvarphi%28%5Csum_%7Bg%7Dn_%7Bg%7Dg%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%7Dn_%7Bg%7D%5Cvarphi%28g%29) ，并且 ![\varphi(hg)=h\varphi(g),h\in H](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%28hg%29%3Dh%5Cvarphi%28g%29%2Ch%5Cin+H) ，所以 ![\varphi\in \text{Hom}_{H}(\mathbb{Z}[G],N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%5Cin+%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BH%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%2CN%29) 。反过来，任给 ![\varphi\in \text{Hom}_{H}(\mathbb{Z}[G],N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%5Cin+%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BH%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%2CN%29) ，自然可看作 ![\varphi\in\text{Ind}_{H}^{G}(N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%5Cin%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7BH%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29) 。

特别地，如果H=1，那么**N即abelian group（没有module structure）**， ![\text{Ind}_{\{1\}}^{G}(N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D_%7B%5C%7B1%5C%7D%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29) 简记为 ![\text{Ind}^{G}(N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29) ， ![\text{Ind}^{G}(N)=\{\varphi:G\rightarrow N\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29%3D%5C%7B%5Cvarphi%3AG%5Crightarrow+N%5C%7D) 。此时上述同构变成： ![\text{Ind}^{G}(N)\simeq\text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Z}[G],N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29%5Csimeq%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%2CN%29) 。

**Def 3.** 若一个G-module M， ![M=\text{Ind}^{G}(N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%3D%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29) ，N是一个abelian group，则称M为induced module。

对于abelian group N，考虑 ![\mathbb{Z}[G]\otimes_{\mathbb{Z}}N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%5Cotimes_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7DN) ，容易验证，上面有一个G-module structure

![g(z\otimes n)=gz\otimes n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28z%5Cotimes+n%29%3Dgz%5Cotimes+n) 。

**Proposition 2.** 如果G是有限群，存在G-module之间的同构

![\alpha:\text{Ind}^{G}(N)\rightarrow\mathbb{Z}[G]\otimes_{\mathbb{Z}}N,\varphi\mapsto\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes\varphi(g^{-1})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%3A%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29%5Crightarrow%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%5Cotimes_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7DN%2C%5Cvarphi%5Cmapsto%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29)

*Proof. *G有限，那么 ![\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes\varphi(g^{-1})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29) 是well-defined。

(1) ![\alpha](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha) 是G-module同态：

![\alpha(\varphi+\phi)=\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes(\varphi+\phi)(g^{-1})=\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes(\varphi(g^{-1})+\phi(g^{-1}))\\=\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes\varphi(g^{-1})+\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes\phi(g^{-1})=\alpha(\varphi)+\alpha(\phi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%28%5Cvarphi%2B%5Cphi%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%28%5Cvarphi%2B%5Cphi%29%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%28%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%2B%5Cphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%29%5C%5C%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%2B%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%5Cphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%3D%5Calpha%28%5Cvarphi%29%2B%5Calpha%28%5Cphi%29)

![\alpha(x\varphi)=\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes(x\varphi)(g^{-1})=\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes\varphi(g^{-1}x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%28x%5Cvarphi%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%28x%5Cvarphi%29%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7Dx%29) ，做替换 ![g^{-1}x\mapsto y^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%5E%7B-1%7Dx%5Cmapsto+y%5E%7B-1%7D) ，于是 ![\alpha(x\varphi)=\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes\varphi(g^{-1}x)=\sum_{y\in G}yx\otimes\varphi(y^{-1})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%28x%5Cvarphi%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7Dx%29%3D%5Csum_%7By%5Cin+G%7Dyx%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28y%5E%7B-1%7D%29) 。所以，

![x(\alpha(\varphi))=x\big(\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes\varphi(g^{-1})\big)=\sum_{g\in G}x(g\otimes\varphi(g^{-1}))=\sum_{g\in G}xg\otimes\varphi(g^{-1})=\alpha(x\varphi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%28%5Calpha%28%5Cvarphi%29%29%3Dx%5Cbig%28%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%5Cbig%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dx%28g%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dxg%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%3D%5Calpha%28x%5Cvarphi%29)

(2)injective: 若 ![\alpha(\varphi)=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%28%5Cvarphi%29%3D0) ，那么 ![\sum_{g\in G}g\otimes\varphi(g^{-1})=0\Rightarrow\varphi(g)=0,{\rm for~all}~g\in G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bg%5Cin+G%7Dg%5Cotimes%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7D%29%3D0%5CRightarrow%5Cvarphi%28g%29%3D0%2C%7B%5Crm+for~all%7D~g%5Cin+G) 。即 ![\varphi=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%3D0) 。

(3)surjective: 首先注意到 ![\mathbb{Z}[G]\otimes_{\mathbb{Z}}N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%5Cotimes_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7DN)中任一个元素可以写成 ![\sum_{g}g\otimes n_{g}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_%7Bg%7Dg%5Cotimes+n_%7Bg%7D)  。（一般地，假设E是一个free R-module with basis {vi}，F是任意一个R-module，则 ![E\otimes_{R}F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cotimes_%7BR%7DF) 中任意一个元素可以写成 ![x=\sum_{i}v_{i}\otimes y_{i},y_{i}\in F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%7Dv_%7Bi%7D%5Cotimes+y_%7Bi%7D%2Cy_%7Bi%7D%5Cin+F) 。）

那么对于 ![\varphi\in\text{Ind}^{G}(N),\varphi(g)=n_{g}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%5Cin%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D%28N%29%2C%5Cvarphi%28g%29%3Dn_%7Bg%7D) ，自然满足 ![\alpha(\varphi)=\sum_{g}g\otimes n_{g}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%28%5Cvarphi%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bg%7Dg%5Cotimes+n_%7Bg%7D) 。

3.** Shapiro's lemma**

**Shapiro's lemma. **设 ![H\leq G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5Cleq+G) ，那么对每个 ![H-\text{module}~N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H-%5Ctext%7Bmodule%7D~N) ，成立

![H^{r}(G,\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(N))\simeq H^{r}(H,N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2C%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28N%29%29%5Csimeq+H%5E%7Br%7D%28H%2CN%29)

**Lemma 2.** The functor ![\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}:\text{Mod}_{H}\rightarrow \text{Mod}_{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%3A%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BH%7D%5Crightarrow+%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BG%7D) is exact.

**Lemma 3. **If ![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) is an** injective** G-module, then so is ![\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(I)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28I%29) .

**Exercise 4.** Prove Lemma 2 and Lemma 3.

*Proof* of Shapiro's lemma. 我们已经说明，有函子同构：

![(~\cdot~)^{H}\simeq(~\cdot~)^{G}\circ\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(~\cdot~)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BH%7D%5Csimeq%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BG%7D%5Ccirc%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28~%5Ccdot~%29)**(**)**

现在任给一个injective resolutions： ![N\rightarrow I^{\bullet}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%5Crightarrow+I%5E%7B%5Cbullet%7D) ，Lemma 2，Lemma 3 分别说明

**(1) ![\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(N)\rightarrow\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(I^{\bullet})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28N%29%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28I%5E%7B%5Cbullet%7D%29) 仍是resolution，**

**(2) ![\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(N)\rightarrow\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(I^{\bullet})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28N%29%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28I%5E%7B%5Cbullet%7D%29) 还是injective resolution。**

于是由导出函子的定义以及(**)得到：

![H^{r}(H,N)=L^{r}N^{G}=H^{r}(I^{\bullet H})=H^{r}((\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(I^{\bullet}))^{G})=L^{r}\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(I^{\bullet})^{G}=H^{r}(G,\text{Ind}^{G}_{H}(N))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28H%2CN%29%3DL%5E%7Br%7DN%5E%7BG%7D%3DH%5E%7Br%7D%28I%5E%7B%5Cbullet+H%7D%29%3DH%5E%7Br%7D%28%28%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28I%5E%7B%5Cbullet%7D%29%29%5E%7BG%7D%29%3DL%5E%7Br%7D%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28I%5E%7B%5Cbullet%7D%29%5E%7BG%7D%3DH%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2C%5Ctext%7BInd%7D%5E%7BG%7D_%7BH%7D%28N%29%29)
