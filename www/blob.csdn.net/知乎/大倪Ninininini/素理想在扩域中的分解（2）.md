# 素理想在扩域中的分解（2） - 知乎
# 

上一篇我们总结了在Galois扩张 ![L/K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%2FK) 中，素理想如何分解：分解群和惯性群分别描述了分裂和分歧；以及分裂、剩余域扩张、分歧分别在哪些中间域发生。

现在我们考虑素理想非分歧的情形，那么只需要考虑剩余域扩张 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 和分裂 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) ，这种情况下 ![\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) 群中一些（或一个）元素 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},K}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2CK%7D) （Frobenius element）决定 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 和 ![g](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g) 。也就是说**Frobenius element“掌管了”素理想 ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 的分解情况。**

**（一）局部域中的Frobenius element**

首先我们回忆局部域的非分歧扩张：

**定理. **设 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 是一个局部域， ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 是其剩余域， ![L/K ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%2FK+) 是有限可分扩张。那么我们有一一对应：

{包含在 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 中的 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 的非分歧扩张 ![K^{\prime}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D) } ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow) {包含在 ![l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l) 中的 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 的有限可分扩张 ![l^{\prime}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D) }

并且成立： ![[K^{\prime}:K]=[k^{\prime}:k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5BK%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%3AK%5D%3D%5Bk%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%3Ak%5D) ; ![K^{\prime}/K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2FK) Galois ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow)![k^{\prime}/k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2Fk) Galois，![\text{Gal}(K^{\prime}/K)\simeq\text{Gal}(k^{\prime}/k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28K%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2FK%29%5Csimeq%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28k%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2Fk%29) 。

局部域的非分歧扩张是“简单”的扩张，这是因为：

**（1）素理想不发生分裂和分歧，只发生剩余域扩张（只有 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 需要考虑）。**

**（2）![\text{Gal}(K^{\prime}/K)\simeq\text{Gal}(k^{\prime}/k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28K%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2FK%29%5Csimeq%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28k%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2Fk%29) 反映 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 。剩余域有限时， ![k^{\prime}/k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2Fk) 即有限域的有限扩张（easy）。**

假设 ![K_{n}/K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K_%7Bn%7D%2FK) 是局部域 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 的 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 次非分歧扩张，剩余域扩张为 ![k_{n}/k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7Bn%7D%2Fk)， ![\#k=q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%23k%3Dq) ， ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 是 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 的赋值环 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 中的唯一非零素理想。那么有： ![\text{Gal}(K_{n}/K)\simeq\text{Gal}(k_{n}/k)\simeq\text{Gal}(\text{F}_{q^{n}}/\text{F}_{q})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28K_%7Bn%7D%2FK%29%5Csimeq%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28k_%7Bn%7D%2Fk%29%5Csimeq%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28%5Ctext%7BF%7D_%7Bq%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%2F%5Ctext%7BF%7D_%7Bq%7D%29) 。

![\text{Gal}(\text{F}_{q^{n}}/\text{F}_{q})=\langle\sigma\rangle](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28%5Ctext%7BF%7D_%7Bq%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%2F%5Ctext%7BF%7D_%7Bq%7D%29%3D%5Clangle%5Csigma%5Crangle) 是 ![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 阶循环群，其中 ![\sigma: \text{F}_{q^{n}}\rightarrow\text{F}_{q} ; a \mapsto a^{q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%3A+%5Ctext%7BF%7D_%7Bq%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BF%7D_%7Bq%7D+%3B+a+%5Cmapsto+a%5E%7Bq%7D) 是有限域的Frobenius置换。在上面的同构下， ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma) 在 ![\text{Gal}(K_{n}/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28K_%7Bn%7D%2FK%29) 中像记为 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%7D)，它是 ![\text{Gal}(K_{n}/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28K_%7Bn%7D%2FK%29) 的生成元，其阶数即 ![\text{Gal}(K_{n}/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28K_%7Bn%7D%2FK%29) 的阶数 ![=f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%3Df) 。并且，对于任意 ![\beta\in B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%5Cin+B) ( ![K ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K+) 的赋值环）， ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p}}\beta\equiv\beta^{q}\text{mod}\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%7D%5Cbeta%5Cequiv%5Cbeta%5E%7Bq%7D%5Ctext%7Bmod%7D%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 。

**（二）整体域中的Frobenius element**

在整体域中，也可以去定义Frobenius element。假设 ![L/K ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%2FK+) 是Galois扩张， ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 是数域； ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 是 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 中素理想，![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是 ![\mathbb{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D) 在 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 中的整闭包，![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 是 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 在 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 中的整闭包； ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 在 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 中非分歧， ![\mathfrak{q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D) 是 ![L ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L+) 中 ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 上的素理想； ![L,K ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%2CK+) 的剩余域分别为 ![l,k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l%2Ck) , ![\#k=q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%23k%3Dq) 。

我们知道剩余域和局部化的剩余域是同构的，那么利用局部域非分歧扩张的结果，得到同构以及嵌入： ![\text{Gal}(l/k)\simeq\text{Gal}(l_{\mathfrak{q}}/k_{\mathfrak{p}})\simeq\text{Gal}(L_{\mathfrak{q}}/K_{\mathfrak{p}})\simeq D_{\mathfrak{q}}\hookrightarrow\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28l%2Fk%29%5Csimeq%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28l_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%2Fk_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%7D%29%5Csimeq%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%2FK_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%7D%29%5Csimeq+D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%5Chookrightarrow%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) 。这样一来，**局部对象 ![L_{\mathfrak{q}}/K_{\mathfrak{p}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%2FK_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%7D) 中的Frobenius element便可以在整体对象 ![ D_{\mathfrak{q}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D) 中（自然就嵌入到 ![\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) ）表现出来，我们假设 ![L_{\mathfrak{q}}/K_{\mathfrak{p}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%2FK_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%7D) 中的Frobenius element在 ![\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) 中的像为 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D)**。那么根据上面的同构： ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}\mathfrak{q}=\mathfrak{q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%3D%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D) ，且对于任意 ![\alpha\in B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Cin+B) ， ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}\alpha\equiv\alpha^{q}\text{mod}\mathfrak{q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%5Calpha%5Cequiv%5Calpha%5E%7Bq%7D%5Ctext%7Bmod%7D%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D) 。

**现在定义 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},L}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2CL%7D) 为 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D) 在 ![\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) 中的共轭类。**

**Fact1.**![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},L}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2CL%7D) 与 ![L ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L+) 中 ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 上的素理想的选取无关：

**Proof. **首先注意到对于任意 ![\sigma\in\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%5Cin%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) ，![\sigma\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}\sigma^{-1}=\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\sigma(\mathfrak{q})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%5Csigma%5E%7B-1%7D%3D%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Csigma%28%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%29%7D)：

 对于任意 ![\beta\in B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%5Cin+B) ，![\sigma\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}\sigma^{-1}(\beta)=\sigma((\sigma^{-1}\beta)^{q}+\alpha)=\beta^{q}+\sigma\alpha\equiv\beta^{q}\text{mod}\mathfrak\sigma{q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%5Csigma%5E%7B-1%7D%28%5Cbeta%29%3D%5Csigma%28%28%5Csigma%5E%7B-1%7D%5Cbeta%29%5E%7Bq%7D%2B%5Calpha%29%3D%5Cbeta%5E%7Bq%7D%2B%5Csigma%5Calpha%5Cequiv%5Cbeta%5E%7Bq%7D%5Ctext%7Bmod%7D%5Cmathfrak%5Csigma%7Bq%7D)， ![\alpha\in \mathfrak{q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha%5Cin+%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D) 。 

另一方面![\text{Gal}(L/K)\times\{\text{nonzero prime ideals over}~\mathfrak{p}\}\rightarrow \{\text{nonzero prime ideals over}~\mathfrak{p}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29%5Ctimes%5C%7B%5Ctext%7Bnonzero+prime+ideals+over%7D~%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%5C%7D%5Crightarrow+%5C%7B%5Ctext%7Bnonzero+prime+ideals+over%7D~%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%5C%7D)是一个可迁作用，于是对于任意 ![\mathfrak{q}^{\prime}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D) ，总可以找到 ![\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma)， ![\mathfrak{q}^{\prime}=\sigma\mathfrak{q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%3D%5Csigma%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D) 。 于是 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}^{\prime}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%7D) 的共轭类为：

![\{\tau\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}^{\prime}}\tau^{-1}~|~\tau\in\text{Gal}(L/K)\}=\{\tau\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\sigma(\mathfrak{q})}\tau^{-1}~|~\tau\in\text{Gal}(L/K)\}=\{\tau\sigma\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}\sigma^{-1}\tau^{-1}~|~\tau\in\text{Gal}(L/K)\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Ctau%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%7D%5Ctau%5E%7B-1%7D~%7C~%5Ctau%5Cin%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29%5C%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Ctau%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Csigma%28%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%29%7D%5Ctau%5E%7B-1%7D~%7C~%5Ctau%5Cin%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29%5C%7D%3D%5C%7B%5Ctau%5Csigma%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%5Csigma%5E%7B-1%7D%5Ctau%5E%7B-1%7D~%7C~%5Ctau%5Cin%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29%5C%7D)

而 ![\tau\sigma](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctau%5Csigma) 跑遍 ![\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) ，所以 ![\{\tau\sigma\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}\sigma^{-1}\tau^{-1}~|~\tau\in\text{Gal}(L/K)\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7B%5Ctau%5Csigma%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D%5Csigma%5E%7B-1%7D%5Ctau%5E%7B-1%7D~%7C~%5Ctau%5Cin%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29%5C%7D) 即 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},L}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2CL%7D) 。

**Fact2. **![L/K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%2FK) 是abelian extension时， ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},L}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2CL%7D) 为一个元素。

**Proof.** 注意到交换群的每个共轭类只有一个元素。

![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 的分解情况可以被 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},L}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2CL%7D) 反映出来：

**定理. **假设 ![L/K ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%2FK+) 是Galois扩张， ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 是数域； ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 是 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 中素理想，![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是 ![\mathbb{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D) 在 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 中的整闭包，![B](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B) 是 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 在 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 中的整闭包； ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 在 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 中非分歧， ![\mathfrak{q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D) 是 ![L ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L+) 中 ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 上的素理想； ![L,K ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%2CK+) 的剩余域分别为 ![l,k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l%2Ck) , ![\#k=q](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%23k%3Dq) 。那么：

（1） ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},L}=\{1\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2CL%7D%3D%5C%7B1%5C%7D)![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow)![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 在 ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 中完全分解。

（2） ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},\mathfrak{q}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bq%7D%7D) 在 ![\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) 中阶数为 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ，那么 ![\mathfrak{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D) 上的素理想个数为 ![[L:K]/f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5BL%3AK%5D%2Ff) 。

（3） ![K\subset L^{\prime}\subset L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%5Csubset+L%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%5Csubset+L)， ![L^{\prime}/K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2FK) Galois，则 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p},L}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%2CL%7D) 限制在 ![\text{Gal}(L^{\prime}/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2FK%29) 上为 ![\text{Frob}_{\mathfrak{p}^{\prime},L}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BFrob%7D_%7B%5Cmathfrak%7Bp%7D%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%2CL%7D) 。
