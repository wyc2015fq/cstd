# 局部域（一）绝对值和赋值的定义 - 知乎
# 

**（一）绝对值（absolute value）与离散赋值（discrete valuation）**

给定一个域 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) ，我们希望在上面定义一个度量。这个度量是由**绝对值（absolute value）**诱导的，就像线性空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 上一个范数可以诱导一个度量。

**Def. **域 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 上的绝对值是一个乘法同态， ![|\cdot|:K^{\times}\rightarrow\mathbb{R}_{>0},|xy|=|x||y|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C%3AK%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%5Crightarrow%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D_%7B%3E0%7D%2C%7Cxy%7C%3D%7Cx%7C%7Cy%7C) 。并且满足：

（1） ![|0|=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C0%7C%3D0) 。（2） ![|x+y|\leq|x|+|y|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cx%2By%7C%5Cleq%7Cx%7C%2B%7Cy%7C) 。

如果还满足（3） ![|x+y|\leq\text{max}\{|x|,|y|\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cx%2By%7C%5Cleq%5Ctext%7Bmax%7D%5C%7B%7Cx%7C%2C%7Cy%7C%5C%7D) ，则称之为**nonarchimedean**的，否则称为archimedean的。如果 ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 把非零元都映成 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) ，则称之为trivial absolute value。

**Remark**：容易验证 ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 将 ![K^{\times}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D) 中的单位根映到 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) ，并且 ![|-x|=|x| ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C-x%7C%3D%7Cx%7C+) 。 ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 诱导出 ![K ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K+) 上的度量![d(x,y)=|x-y|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%28x%2Cy%29%3D%7Cx-y%7C) 。（这个度量又诱导出 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 上的拓扑：任给 ![a\in K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Cin+K) ， ![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a) 的开领域系由 ![U(a,\epsilon)=\{x\in K~|~|x-a|<\epsilon\},\epsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%28a%2C%5Cepsilon%29%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+K~%7C~%7Cx-a%7C%3C%5Cepsilon%5C%7D%2C%5Cepsilon%3E0) 构成。）

在数论里我们更多地考虑nonarchimedean的情形，比如学过初等数论的同学应该熟知 ![\mathbb{Q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D) 上的p-adic绝对值 ![|\cdot|_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C_%7Bp%7D) 就是第一个重要的例子。

对于nonarchimedean的绝对值，有个简单的判别条件：

**Ex1. **![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 是nonarchimedean的绝对值 ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow)![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 在 ![\{m\cdot1~|~m\in\mathbb{Z}\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bm%5Ccdot1~%7C~m%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5C%7D) 上取值有界。例如 ![\text{char}K=p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bchar%7DK%3Dp) 时， ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 上的绝对值一定是nonarchimedean的。

与绝对值相对应的是域上的离散赋值，即同态 ![v:K^{\times}\rightarrow\mathbb{Z},v(ab)=v(a)+v(b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3AK%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%5Crightarrow%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2Cv%28ab%29%3Dv%28a%29%2Bv%28b%29)。并且还要满足：（1） ![v(0)=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%280%29%3D%5Cinfty) 。（2） ![v(a+b)\geq\text{min}\{v(a),v(b)\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28a%2Bb%29%5Cgeq%5Ctext%7Bmin%7D%5C%7Bv%28a%29%2Cv%28b%29%5C%7D) 。当**同态为满射时，称之是normalized的**。（比如 ![\mathbb{Q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D) 上的p-adic赋值 ![v_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v_%7Bp%7D) ）

注意到 ![\mathbb{Q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D) 上的p-adic赋值与绝对值的关系： ![|a|_{p}=(1/p)^{v_{p}(a)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Ca%7C_%7Bp%7D%3D%281%2Fp%29%5E%7Bv_%7Bp%7D%28a%29%7D) 是由指数与对数确定的。对于一般情形，我们有：

**（1）一个离散赋值可确定一个nonarchimedean的绝对值，定义**![|x|=c^{-v(x)},c>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cx%7C%3Dc%5E%7B-v%28x%29%7D%2Cc%3E1) ；

**（2）一个（nontrivial的）nonarchimedean的绝对值可以确定一个加法赋值**（ 加法赋值的定义是，![v:K^{\times}\rightarrow\mathbb{R},v(ab)=v(a)+v(b)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%3AK%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%5Crightarrow%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2Cv%28ab%29%3Dv%28a%29%2Bv%28b%29) 且 ![v(0)=\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%280%29%3D%5Cinfty) ， ![v(a+b)\geq\text{min}\{v(a),v(b)\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28a%2Bb%29%5Cgeq%5Ctext%7Bmin%7D%5C%7Bv%28a%29%2Cv%28b%29%5C%7D) ），定义 ![v(x)=-\log_{c}|x|,c>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28x%29%3D-%5Clog_%7Bc%7D%7Cx%7C%2Cc%3E1) 。

特别注意的是，如果在（2）中，**如果 ![v(K^{\times})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28K%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%29) 在 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) （加法群）中离散， ![v(K^{\times})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28K%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%29) 一定是lattice，即**![v(K^{\times})=\mathbb{Z}c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28K%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%29%3D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7Dc) ，其中 ![c](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c) 是某个非零实数。此时令 ![\text{ord}=c^{-1}v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bord%7D%3Dc%5E%7B-1%7Dv) ，它就成为一个normalized discrete valuation。

**Remark：**![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 称为离散的，如果 ![|K^{\times}|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7CK%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%7C) 在 **![\mathbb{R}_{>0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D_%7B%3E0%7D) （乘法群）**中离散。

**Ex2. **![|\mathbb{Q}^{\times}|_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%7C_%7Bp%7D) 在 ![\mathbb{R}_{>0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D_%7B%3E0%7D) 中离散，但是 ![|\mathbb{Q}|_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D%7C_%7Bp%7D) 不在 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 中离散。（hint：利用离散子空间的定义即可）

**（二）nonarchimedean的拓扑与archimedean的不同**

![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 是一个域，上面有非平凡的**nonarchimedean绝对值**![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 。

**Claim1. **若 ![|a|\ne|b|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Ca%7C%5Cne%7Cb%7C) ，则 ![|a+b|=\text{max}\{|a|,|b|\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Ca%2Bb%7C%3D%5Ctext%7Bmax%7D%5C%7B%7Ca%7C%2C%7Cb%7C%5C%7D)

（如果 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 是离散赋值， ![v(a)\ne v(b)\Rightarrow v(a+b)=\text{min}\{v(a),v(b)\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28a%29%5Cne+v%28b%29%5CRightarrow+v%28a%2Bb%29%3D%5Ctext%7Bmin%7D%5C%7Bv%28a%29%2Cv%28b%29%5C%7D) ） 

利用三角不等式很容易证明。我们发现由 ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 诱导的度量拓扑与archimedean

情形十分不同：

**Claim2.  ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 是nonarchimedean绝对值，定义开球**![D(a,r)=\{x\in K~|~|x-a|<r\},r>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28a%2Cr%29%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+K~%7C~%7Cx-a%7C%3Cr%5C%7D%2Cr%3E0) 。如果 ![b\in D(a,r) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%5Cin+D%28a%2Cr%29+) ，那么 ![D(a,r)=D(b,r)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28a%2Cr%29%3DD%28b%2Cr%29) 。

这就是说，**在一个半径为 ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) 的开球中任取一点再做一个半径为 ![r](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r) 的球，那么这两个球重合！显然这在archimedean情形是不可能的。**

**Ex3. **![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 是一个域， ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 是非平凡绝对值，如果满足：对于任意 ![D(a,r)=\{x\in K~|~|x-a|<r\},r>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28a%2Cr%29%3D%5C%7Bx%5Cin+K~%7C~%7Cx-a%7C%3Cr%5C%7D%2Cr%3E0) ， ![b\in D(a,r) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%5Cin+D%28a%2Cr%29+)![\Rightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CRightarrow)![D(a,r)=D(b,r)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28a%2Cr%29%3DD%28b%2Cr%29) 。那么 ![|\cdot|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7C%5Ccdot%7C) 是nonarchimedean的。

（hint：反证，如果有 ![x,y](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%2Cy) 使得 ![|x+y|>\text{max}\{|x|,|y|\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cx%2By%7C%3E%5Ctext%7Bmax%7D%5C%7B%7Cx%7C%2C%7Cy%7C%5C%7D) ，考虑 ![D(0,\text{max}\{|x|,|y|\})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%280%2C%5Ctext%7Bmax%7D%5C%7B%7Cx%7C%2C%7Cy%7C%5C%7D%29) ）

**（三）赋值环**

**设**![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 是 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 上的**normalized**离散赋值，考虑以下：

![A=\{a\in K~|~v(a)\geq0\},U=\{a\in K~|~v(a)=0\},\mathfrak{m}=\{a\in K~|~v(a)>0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5C%7Ba%5Cin+K~%7C~v%28a%29%5Cgeq0%5C%7D%2CU%3D%5C%7Ba%5Cin+K~%7C~v%28a%29%3D0%5C%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%3D%5C%7Ba%5Cin+K~%7C~v%28a%29%3E0%5C%7D)

**Claim3. **![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 的子环，并且 ![\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) 是 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的唯一极大理想， ![U=A^{\times}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%3DA%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D)， ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是离散赋值环。

**Proof**. 显然 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是 ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) 的子环， ![\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) 是 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的理想，并且 ![U=A^{\times}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%3DA%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D) 。注意到 ![A^{\times}=A-\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%3DA-%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) ，所以 ![\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) 是极大理想，并且 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 中其他proper的理想一定包含在 ![\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) 中，故 ![\mathfrak{m}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D) 是唯一的极大理想。于是 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是一个local ring。所以，为了说明 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是离散赋值环，**只用说明 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是PID的**：

**（1）假设 ![\pi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi) 是 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 中满足 ![v(\pi)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28%5Cpi%29%3D1) 的元素，那么 ![K^{\times}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D) 中任意一个元素 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 可以表示成 ![x=u\pi^{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3Du%5Cpi%5E%7Bk%7D) ，其中 ![u\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%5Cin+U) ：**

![v(x)=k\Rightarrow v(x\pi^{-k})=0\Rightarrow u=x\pi^{-k}\in U](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28x%29%3Dk%5CRightarrow+v%28x%5Cpi%5E%7B-k%7D%29%3D0%5CRightarrow+u%3Dx%5Cpi%5E%7B-k%7D%5Cin+U) 。

**（2）任取 ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 的理想 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ，设 ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 中赋值最小的元素为 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) ， ![v(x)=n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28x%29%3Dn) ，我们说明 ![\mathfrak{a}=(\pi^{n})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D%3D%28%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D%29) ：**

由（1）可知， ![x=u\pi^{n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3Du%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D) ，那么 ![\pi^{n}A\subseteq\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7DA%5Csubseteq%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) ；反过来， ![\mathfrak{a}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Ba%7D) 中任意一个元素形如 ![y=\varepsilon\pi^{m},m\geq n,\varepsilon\in U\Rightarrow\pi^{m-n}\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3D%5Cvarepsilon%5Cpi%5E%7Bm%7D%2Cm%5Cgeq+n%2C%5Cvarepsilon%5Cin+U%5CRightarrow%5Cpi%5E%7Bm-n%7D%5Cin+A) ，注意到 ![\pi^{m-n}\in A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi%5E%7Bm-n%7D%5Cin+A) ，于是 ![y=\varepsilon\pi^{m}=\varepsilon\pi^{m-n}\pi^{n}\in\pi^{n}A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3D%5Cvarepsilon%5Cpi%5E%7Bm%7D%3D%5Cvarepsilon%5Cpi%5E%7Bm-n%7D%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cin%5Cpi%5E%7Bn%7DA) 。

于是， ![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) 是离散赋值环，**有唯一（up to a unit）的prime element，记为 ![\pi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi) 。于是 ![\mathfrak{m}=(\pi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%3D%28%5Cpi%29) , ![v(\pi)=1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28%5Cpi%29%3D1) 。**

**Remark：**此时诱导出的nonarchimedean绝对值，**![|x|=c^{-v(x)},c>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Cx%7C%3Dc%5E%7B-v%28x%29%7D%2Cc%3E1) 是离散的**。

事实上，注意到对于任意 ![x\in K^{\times}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin+K%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D) ， ![v(x)=k\Rightarrow|x|=c^{-kv(\pi)}=|\pi|^{k}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28x%29%3Dk%5CRightarrow%7Cx%7C%3Dc%5E%7B-kv%28%5Cpi%29%7D%3D%7C%5Cpi%7C%5E%7Bk%7D) ，即 ![|K^{\times}|=(|\pi|)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7CK%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%7C%3D%28%7C%5Cpi%7C%29) 。而显然， ![(|\pi|)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%7C%5Cpi%7C%29) 在 ![\mathbb{R}_{>0}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D_%7B%3E0%7D) 中离散。

**First Example**： ![K=\mathbb{Q},v=v_{p},|\cdot|=|\cdot|_{p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K%3D%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D%2Cv%3Dv_%7Bp%7D%2C%7C%5Ccdot%7C%3D%7C%5Ccdot%7C_%7Bp%7D) ，那么 ![A=\mathbb{Z}_{(p)},U=\mathbb{Z}_{(p)}^{\times},\mathfrak{m}=p\cdot\mathbb{Z}_{(p)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7B%28p%29%7D%2CU%3D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7B%28p%29%7D%5E%7B%5Ctimes%7D%2C%5Cmathfrak%7Bm%7D%3Dp%5Ccdot%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D_%7B%28p%29%7D) 。
