# 局部类域论 1 上同调群的定义 - 知乎
# 

粗略地讲，对于一个local field ![K](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=K) ，![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 是它的一个abelian extension，我们的**主要目标**是了解 ![G=\text{Gal}(L/K)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G%3D%5Ctext%7BGal%7D%28L%2FK%29) ，包括关于它的一些同构映射。我们的做法是可以通过研究它的上同调群 ![H^{r}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CM%29) 来得到 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 的性质（这里我们会考虑它的Tate cohomology）。

之所以可以做到大体是因为两个原因：
- (Tate) cohomology group本身具有一套漂亮的性质。
- ![L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L) 作为local field的arithmetic information比较清晰 ![L=U_{L}\times\mathbb{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%3DU_%7BL%7D%5Ctimes%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D)（G-module isomorphism)，对![H^{r}(G,L)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CL%29) 的研究直接变成对 ![H^{r}(G,U_{L})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2CU_%7BL%7D%29) 和 ![H^{r}(G,\mathbb{Z})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Br%7D%28G%2C%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%29) 的研究。这二者在(Tate) cohomology 里都是“好”的对象。

（这个philosophy可以看作从表示论里借鉴的，有知友也在其他答案里提过一个回答

[Tannakian Formalism](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mathoverflow.net/questions/3446/tannakian-formalism/3467%233467)）

第一篇note复习一下cohomology group的定义，我们从G-module范畴开始，至于该范畴里那些重要的morphism，functor 和natural transformation都放在以后的notes里。
- **The category **![\text{Mod}_{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BG%7D)

**Def 1. **一个abel群 ![M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M) 称作是 ![G-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G-) module，如果G在M上有一个group action。

**Def 2. **两个G-module M，N之间的morphism f 是它们之间的一个群同态，并且满足对任意G中元素g，成立：![f(gm)=gf(m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28gm%29%3Dgf%28m%29) 。

上述构成的范畴称为G-module 范畴，记作 ![\text{Mod}_{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BG%7D) 。

特别地，我们规定G acts trivially on ![\mathbb{Z},\mathbb{Q}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2C%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D) ，于是它们也成为G-module。

**Fact 1. **设![\mathbb{Z}[G]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D) 是group ring，那么![\text{Mod}_{G}=\text{Mod}_{\mathbb{Z}[G]}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BG%7D%3D%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%7D) 。右边是 ![\mathbb{Z}[G]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D) -模范畴。

事实上容易验证：

(1)任给一个G-module M，可以诱导一个![\mathbb{Z}[G]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D) -模：

![\mathbb{Z}[G]\times M\rightarrow M,(\Sigma n_{i}g_{i},m)\mapsto\Sigma n_{i}(g_{i}m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%5Ctimes+M%5Crightarrow+M%2C%28%5CSigma+n_%7Bi%7Dg_%7Bi%7D%2Cm%29%5Cmapsto%5CSigma+n_%7Bi%7D%28g_%7Bi%7Dm%29)

右边的sum是M中的加法。反过来一个![\mathbb{Z}[G]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D) -模自然是一个G-module。

(2)G-module 之间的morphism f extends to  ![f(\Sigma n_{i}g_{i}\cdot m)=\sum n_{i}g_{i}f(m)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5CSigma+n_%7Bi%7Dg_%7Bi%7D%5Ccdot+m%29%3D%5Csum+n_%7Bi%7Dg_%7Bi%7Df%28m%29) 。

反过来，一个![\mathbb{Z}[G]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D) -模间的同态自然是G-module 同态。

两个G-module M，N之间的G-同态我们记作 ![\text{Hom}_{G}(M,N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28M%2CN%29) 。（注意和下面的区别）

**Fact 2.**![\text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(M,N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28M%2CN%29) 中元素为 ![\varphi:M\rightarrow N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarphi%3AM%5Crightarrow+N) （看作abel群的同态），定义 ![(\varphi+\varphi^ {\prime})(m)=\varphi(m)+\varphi^{\prime}(m),(g\varphi)(m)=g(\varphi(g^{-1}m))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cvarphi%2B%5Cvarphi%5E+%7B%5Cprime%7D%29%28m%29%3D%5Cvarphi%28m%29%2B%5Cvarphi%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D%28m%29%2C%28g%5Cvarphi%29%28m%29%3Dg%28%5Cvarphi%28g%5E%7B-1%7Dm%29%29) 。那么 ![\text{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(M,N)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%7D%28M%2CN%29) 具有G-module structure。

2.** Definition of the cohomology group**

**Def 3.**![(~\cdot~)^{G}:\text{Mod}_{G}\rightarrow\text{Ab},M\mapsto M^{G}=\{m\in M~|~gm=m~{\rm all}~g\in G\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BG%7D%3A%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BG%7D%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BAb%7D%2CM%5Cmapsto+M%5E%7BG%7D%3D%5C%7Bm%5Cin+M~%7C~gm%3Dm~%7B%5Crm+all%7D~g%5Cin+G%5C%7D)

其中Ab是category of abelian group。

**Fact 3.**![(~\cdot~)^{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BG%7D) 是left exact的。（直接按照exact的定义验证）

**Fact 4. **![\text{Mod}_{G}=\text{Mod}_{\mathbb{Z}[G]}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BG%7D%3D%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%7D) has enough injectives。（Clear, since every module is a submodule of an injective module)

由于上面两个事实成立，所以可以用derived functor给出cohomology group的定义了。(对于derived functor不太熟悉可直接参考S.Lang, Algebra, page 793 Theorem 6.1，或直接wikipedia [Derived Functor](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Derived_functor)）

**Def 4.** 设 ![(~\cdot~)^{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BG%7D) 的right derived functor 是 ![R^{n}(~\cdot~)^{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%5E%7Bn%7D%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BG%7D) ，那么我们定义G的n阶上同调群为 ![H^{n}(G,M)=R^{n}M^{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Bn%7D%28G%2CM%29%3DR%5E%7Bn%7DM%5E%7BG%7D) 。

具体地，根据derived functor的定义，给一个M的injective resolution：

![0\rightarrow M\rightarrow I^{0}\rightarrow I^{1}\rightarrow...\rightarrow I^{n}\rightarrow I^{n+1}\rightarrow...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Crightarrow+M%5Crightarrow+I%5E%7B0%7D%5Crightarrow+I%5E%7B1%7D%5Crightarrow...%5Crightarrow+I%5E%7Bn%7D%5Crightarrow+I%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%5Crightarrow...) apply the functor ![(~\cdot~)^{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28~%5Ccdot~%29%5E%7BG%7D) 得到

![0\rightarrow (I^{0})^{G}\rightarrow (I^{1})^{G}\rightarrow...\rightarrow (I^{n})^{G}\rightarrow (I^{n+1})^{G}\rightarrow...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Crightarrow+%28I%5E%7B0%7D%29%5E%7BG%7D%5Crightarrow+%28I%5E%7B1%7D%29%5E%7BG%7D%5Crightarrow...%5Crightarrow+%28I%5E%7Bn%7D%29%5E%7BG%7D%5Crightarrow+%28I%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%29%5E%7BG%7D%5Crightarrow...)

其中微分是 ![d^{n}:(I^{n})^{G}\rightarrow (I^{n+1})^{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5E%7Bn%7D%3A%28I%5E%7Bn%7D%29%5E%7BG%7D%5Crightarrow+%28I%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%29%5E%7BG%7D) ，那么 ![H^{n}(G,M)=\frac{\text{Ker}d^{n}}{\text{Im}d^{n-1}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Bn%7D%28G%2CM%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctext%7BKer%7Dd%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%7B%5Ctext%7BIm%7Dd%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D) 。

特别地，当n=0时，根据derived functor的性质 ![H^{0}(G,M)=R^{0}M^{G}=M^{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7B0%7D%28G%2CM%29%3DR%5E%7B0%7DM%5E%7BG%7D%3DM%5E%7BG%7D) 。

**3. Description**

根据derived functor的性质我们知道，计算/描述cohomology group ![H^{n}(G,M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Bn%7D%28G%2CM%29) 和injective resolutions的选取没有关系，因此我们希望用一个“比较好”的resolution来描述上同调群。

**[1] *首先注意一个key observation。***

**Fact 5.**![(~\dot~~)^{G}=\text{Hom}_{G}(\mathbb{Z},\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28~%5Cdot~~%29%5E%7BG%7D%3D%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2C%5Ccdot%29) 。于是 ![(~\dot~~)^{G}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28~%5Cdot~~%29%5E%7BG%7D) 与 ![\text{Hom}_{G}(\mathbb{Z},\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2C%5Ccdot%29) 的derived functor同构。（右边的Hom functor看作 ![\text{Mod}_{\mathbb{Z}[G]}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%7D) 上的）

Proof. 对于任意一个G-module M，定义 ![\text{Hom}_{G}(\mathbb{Z},M)\rightarrow M^{G}; f\mapsto f(1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2CM%29%5Crightarrow+M%5E%7BG%7D%3B+f%5Cmapsto+f%281%29) ，容易验证它是group isomorphism。

然而注意到![\text{Hom}_{G}(\mathbb{Z},\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BG%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2C%5Ccdot%29) 的derived functor就是Ext functor ![\text{Ext}_{G}^{r}(A,~\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BExt%7D_%7BG%7D%5E%7Br%7D%28A%2C~%5Ccdot%29) 。（参见wikipedia [Ext functor](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ext_functor%23Definition)）这里我们需要Ext functor的一个事实，即两种定义Ext的方式等价：

**Fact 6.** 在任一个模范畴 ![\text{Mod}_{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BMod%7D_%7BR%7D) 中， 设![\text{Ext}_{R}^{r}(A,~\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BExt%7D_%7BR%7D%5E%7Br%7D%28A%2C~%5Ccdot%29) 是 left exact functor ![\text{Hom}_{R}(A,~\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BR%7D%28A%2C~%5Ccdot%29) 的r-th right derived functor； ![\text{Ext}_{R}^{r}(\cdot~,B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BExt%7D_%7BR%7D%5E%7Br%7D%28%5Ccdot~%2CB%29) 是 *contravariant* left exact functor![\text{Hom}_{R}(\cdot,B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BR%7D%28%5Ccdot%2CB%29) 的r-th derived functor。那么两种方式得到的 ![\text{Ext}_{R}^{r}(A,B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BExt%7D_%7BR%7D%5E%7Br%7D%28A%2CB%29) 一样。

具体地，对于第一种定义，给出一个B的一个injective resolution

![0\rightarrow B\rightarrow I^{0}\rightarrow...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Crightarrow+B%5Crightarrow+I%5E%7B0%7D%5Crightarrow...) ，apply ![\text{Hom}_{R}(A,~\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BR%7D%28A%2C~%5Ccdot%29) ：

![0\rightarrow\text{Hom}_{R}(A,I^{0})\rightarrow\text{Hom}_{R}(A,I^{1})\rightarrow...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BR%7D%28A%2CI%5E%7B0%7D%29%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BR%7D%28A%2CI%5E%7B1%7D%29%5Crightarrow...) ，![\text{Ext}_{R}^{r}(A,B)=\frac{\text{Ker}d^{r}}{\text{Im}d^{r-1}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BExt%7D_%7BR%7D%5E%7Br%7D%28A%2CB%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctext%7BKer%7Dd%5E%7Br%7D%7D%7B%5Ctext%7BIm%7Dd%5E%7Br-1%7D%7D) 。其中 ![d^{r}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5E%7Br%7D) 是微分。

第二种定义，给出A的一个projective resolution

![...\rightarrow P^{1}\rightarrow P^{0}\rightarrow A\rightarrow0 ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=...%5Crightarrow+P%5E%7B1%7D%5Crightarrow+P%5E%7B0%7D%5Crightarrow+A%5Crightarrow0+) ，apply ![\text{Hom}_{R}(\cdot,B)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BR%7D%28%5Ccdot%2CB%29) ：

![0\rightarrow\text{Hom}_{R}(P^{0},B)\rightarrow\text{Hom}_{R}(P^{1},B)\rightarrow...](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BR%7D%28P%5E%7B0%7D%2CB%29%5Crightarrow%5Ctext%7BHom%7D_%7BR%7D%28P%5E%7B1%7D%2CB%29%5Crightarrow...) ， ![\text{Ext}_{R}^{r}(A,B)=\frac{\text{Ker}d^{r}}{\text{Im}d^{r-1}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7BExt%7D_%7BR%7D%5E%7Br%7D%28A%2CB%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctext%7BKer%7Dd%5E%7Br%7D%7D%7B%5Ctext%7BIm%7Dd%5E%7Br-1%7D%7D) 。

**[2] *由以上讨论，***![H^{n}(G,M)=\text{Ext}_{G}^{n}(\mathbb{Z},M)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%7Bn%7D%28G%2CM%29%3D%5Ctext%7BExt%7D_%7BG%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%28%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2CM%29)*** 。我们只用找 ***![\mathbb{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D)*** 的一个projective resolution即可。***

**proposition 1. **(Standard complex) 设 ![E_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_%7Bi%7D) 是basis为 ![(x_{0},...,x_{i-1},x_{i}),x_{i}\in G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_%7B0%7D%2C...%2Cx_%7Bi-1%7D%2Cx_%7Bi%7D%29%2Cx_%7Bi%7D%5Cin+G) 的free Z-module。那么 ![E_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_%7Bi%7D) 可看作free Z[G]-module，group action 定义为： ![g\cdot(x_{0},...,x_{i})=(gx_{0},...,gx_{i})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%5Ccdot%28x_%7B0%7D%2C...%2Cx_%7Bi%7D%29%3D%28gx_%7B0%7D%2C...%2Cgx_%7Bi%7D%29) 。并且有projective resolution：

![...\rightarrow E_{i}\rightarrow E_{i-1}\rightarrow...\rightarrow E_{0}\rightarrow\mathbb{Z}\rightarrow0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=...%5Crightarrow+E_%7Bi%7D%5Crightarrow+E_%7Bi-1%7D%5Crightarrow...%5Crightarrow+E_%7B0%7D%5Crightarrow%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5Crightarrow0) ，其中微分 ![d_{i+1}:E_{i+1}\rightarrow E_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d_%7Bi%2B1%7D%3AE_%7Bi%2B1%7D%5Crightarrow+E_%7Bi%7D) 为

![d_{i+1}(x_{0},...,x_{i})=\sum_{j=0}^{i+1}(-1)^{j}(x_{0},...,\widehat{x_{j}},...,x_{i+1})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d_%7Bi%2B1%7D%28x_%7B0%7D%2C...%2Cx_%7Bi%7D%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bj%3D0%7D%5E%7Bi%2B1%7D%28-1%29%5E%7Bj%7D%28x_%7B0%7D%2C...%2C%5Cwidehat%7Bx_%7Bj%7D%7D%2C...%2Cx_%7Bi%2B1%7D%29) 。特别地， ![E_{0}=\mathbb{Z}[G]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_%7B0%7D%3D%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D) ， ![d_{0}:\mathbb{Z}[G]\rightarrow\mathbb{Z},d_{0}(x_{0})=1,x_{0}\in G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d_%7B0%7D%3A%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5BG%5D%5Crightarrow%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2Cd_%7B0%7D%28x_%7B0%7D%29%3D1%2Cx_%7B0%7D%5Cin+G) 。

Proof. (good exercise, see S. Lang. Algebra, page 826 for hint)

我们使用的是下面这个resolution。

**proposition 2.** (Standard complex modified) 设 ![F_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F_%7Bi%7D) 是free Z[G]-module over basis ![(x_{1},...,x_{i}),x_{i}\in G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_%7B1%7D%2C...%2Cx_%7Bi%7D%29%2Cx_%7Bi%7D%5Cin+G) 。那么有projective resolution：

![...\rightarrow F_{i}\rightarrow F_{i-1}\rightarrow...\rightarrow F_{0}\rightarrow\mathbb{Z}\rightarrow0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=...%5Crightarrow+F_%7Bi%7D%5Crightarrow+F_%7Bi-1%7D%5Crightarrow...%5Crightarrow+F_%7B0%7D%5Crightarrow%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%5Crightarrow0) ，其中微分 ![d^{\prime}_{i+1}:F_{i+1}\rightarrow F_{i}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5E%7B%5Cprime%7D_%7Bi%2B1%7D%3AF_%7Bi%2B1%7D%5Crightarrow+F_%7Bi%7D) 为
