# 凸优化问题介绍(二) - 知乎
# 

## 简介

凸优化是所有优化问题最容易求解的一类问题，原因是凸函数和凸集提供了很好的最优性保障。

标准形式的凸优化问题：

![\begin{align} \min_{x \in \mathbb{R^n}}\, &f_0(x)  \\   \mathrm{subject \,\, to\,} &f_i(x) \leq 0, i = 1, \cdots, m \\ & h_i(x) = 0, i = 1, \cdots, p  \end{align}  \tag{1} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%5Cmin_%7Bx+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%5En%7D%7D%5C%2C+%26f_0%28x%29++%5C%5C+++%5Cmathrm%7Bsubject+%5C%2C%5C%2C+to%5C%2C%7D+%26f_i%28x%29+%5Cleq+0%2C+i+%3D+1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5C%5C+%26+h_i%28x%29+%3D+0%2C+i+%3D+1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+p++%5Cend%7Balign%7D++%5Ctag%7B1%7D+)

其中，
- 目标函数 ![f_0(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_0%28x%29) 是凸函数。
- 不等式约束 ![f_i(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_i%28x%29) 是凸函数， ![i=1, \cdots, m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i%3D1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m) 。
- 等式约束 ![h_i(x) = a_i^Tx - b_i](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h_i%28x%29+%3D+a_i%5ETx+-+b_i) 是仿射函数， ![i = 1, \cdots, p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i+%3D+1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+p) 。

对于凸优化问题，定义域记作 ![D = \bigcap_{i=0}^m \mathrm{dom}(f_i) \cap \bigcap_{i=1}^p \mathrm{dom}(h_i)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D+%3D+%5Cbigcap_%7Bi%3D0%7D%5Em+%5Cmathrm%7Bdom%7D%28f_i%29+%5Ccap+%5Cbigcap_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Ep+%5Cmathrm%7Bdom%7D%28h_i%29) ，最优解记作 ![x^* = \arg\min_{x \in D} f_0(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A+%3D+%5Carg%5Cmin_%7Bx+%5Cin+D%7D+f_0%28x%29) ，最优值记作 ![p^* = f_0(x^*)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5E%2A+%3D+f_0%28x%5E%2A%29) 。

下面介绍一些凸分析术语，以后的算法分析以及最优解存在条件，都将用到凸函数的特性。

## 凸集

集合 ![C \in \mathbb{R^n}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%5En%7D) 是凸集当且仅当， ![\forall x, y \in C, \forall \alpha \in[0,1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x%2C+y+%5Cin+C%2C+%5Cforall+%5Calpha+%5Cin%5B0%2C1%5D) ， ![\alpha x + (1-\alpha) y \in C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+x+%2B+%281-%5Calpha%29+y+%5Cin+C) 。

凸集的几何解释是凸集内任意两点的连线(线段)上的点，仍属于这个集合。也就说，这个集合是“连通”的。
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-9dc887563532d90d5b74f4e56cd031ea_b.jpg)左图：凸集；右图：不是凸集

## 凸函数

凸函数是定义域 ![dom(f)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dom%28f%29) 为凸集，且满足如下性质的函数：

![\forall x, y \in dom(f), \forall \alpha \in [0, 1], f(\alpha x + (1-\alpha)y) \leq \alpha f(x) + (1-\alpha) f(y) \tag{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall+x%2C+y+%5Cin+dom%28f%29%2C+%5Cforall+%5Calpha+%5Cin+%5B0%2C+1%5D%2C+f%28%5Calpha+x+%2B+%281-%5Calpha%29y%29+%5Cleq+%5Calpha+f%28x%29+%2B+%281-%5Calpha%29+f%28y%29+%5Ctag%7B2%7D)

![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3eee2b751c97aa501f212235ee53fadc_b.jpg)(a)图：凸函数的定义的解释：弦位于函数的上方。(b)图：凸函数不可能有局部极小值的直观解释。
**凸函数没有局部最优解**。

> ![\mathit{Proof:}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathit%7BProof%3A%7D)  假设 ![x^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A) 是凸函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的全局最优解；如果 ![x^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A) 是局部最优解，那么必存在 ![\bar x \ne x^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar+x+%5Cne+x%5E%2A) 使得 ![f(\bar x) \leq f(x^*)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Cbar+x%29+%5Cleq+f%28x%5E%2A%29) 。根据凸函数的定义：  ![\begin{split} f(\alpha \bar x+ (1- \alpha) x^*) &\leq \alpha f(\bar x) + (1-\alpha) f(x^*) \\ &\leq \alpha f(x^*) + (1 - \alpha) f(x^*) \\ & \leq f(x^*) \end{split}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bsplit%7D+f%28%5Calpha+%5Cbar+x%2B+%281-+%5Calpha%29+x%5E%2A%29+%26%5Cleq+%5Calpha+f%28%5Cbar+x%29+%2B+%281-%5Calpha%29+f%28x%5E%2A%29+%5C%5C+%26%5Cleq+%5Calpha+f%28x%5E%2A%29+%2B+%281+-+%5Calpha%29+f%28x%5E%2A%29+%5C%5C+%26+%5Cleq+f%28x%5E%2A%29+%5Cend%7Bsplit%7D)  也就是说，位于 ![\bar x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbar+x) 和 ![x^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A) 之间的线段上的函数值都小于 ![f(x^*)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%5E%2A%29) ，这与 ![x^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A) 是局部最优解矛盾，因此 ![x^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A) 只能是全局最优解。

对于分析最优性而言，我们更喜欢凸函数的另一种等价定义。

**(一阶Lower Bound)**对于定义域 ![dom(f)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dom%28f%29) 是凸集的函数 ![f: \mathbb{R^n} \mapsto \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%3A+%5Cmathbb%7BR%5En%7D+%5Cmapsto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D)，且 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 一阶可导。 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 是凸函数 ![\Leftrightarrow](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLeftrightarrow)![f(y) \geq f(x) + \nabla f(x)^T (y-x) \quad \forall x, y \in dom(f) \tag{3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28y%29+%5Cgeq+f%28x%29+%2B+%5Cnabla+f%28x%29%5ET+%28y-x%29+%5Cquad+%5Cforall+x%2C+y+%5Cin+dom%28f%29+%5Ctag%7B3%7D)
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-d5580b5ccaf58b81eee3e42660c9d07c_b.jpg)可导凸函数的一阶Lower Bound：任意一点的切线位于函数曲线的下方。
对于强凸函数而言，其还存在Quadratic Bound。

**(Quadratic Bound)**假设函数 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 是强凸的，强凸函数 ![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29) 有如下性质：![ f(y) \geq f(x) + \nabla f(x)^T(y-x) + \frac{m}{2} ||y-x||^2    \quad\quad\quad \tag{4} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+f%28y%29+%5Cgeq+f%28x%29+%2B+%5Cnabla+f%28x%29%5ET%28y-x%29+%2B+%5Cfrac%7Bm%7D%7B2%7D+%7C%7Cy-x%7C%7C%5E2++++%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad+%5Ctag%7B4%7D+)

![ f(y) \leq f(x) + \nabla f(x)^T(y-x) + \frac{M}{2} ||y-x||^2 \quad\quad\quad  \tag{5}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+f%28y%29+%5Cleq+f%28x%29+%2B+%5Cnabla+f%28x%29%5ET%28y-x%29+%2B+%5Cfrac%7BM%7D%7B2%7D+%7C%7Cy-x%7C%7C%5E2+%5Cquad%5Cquad%5Cquad++%5Ctag%7B5%7D)
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-ece185a65788688f0f7ea87ed784ff13_b.jpg)强凸函数位于Lower Bound和Quadratic Bound之间。
