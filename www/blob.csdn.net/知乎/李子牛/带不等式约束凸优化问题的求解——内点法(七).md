# 带不等式约束凸优化问题的求解——内点法(七) - 知乎
# 

## 简介

这篇文章，我们考虑如下带等式约束和不等式约束的凸优化问题：

![\begin{align} \min \, & f_0(x) \\ \mathrm{subject\,\, to}\, & f_i(x) \leq 0 \quad i=1, \cdots, m \\ & Ax = b \end{align} \tag{1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%5Cmin+%5C%2C+%26+f_0%28x%29+%5C%5C+%5Cmathrm%7Bsubject%5C%2C%5C%2C+to%7D%5C%2C+%26+f_i%28x%29+%5Cleq+0+%5Cquad+i%3D1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5C%5C+%26+Ax+%3D+b+%5Cend%7Balign%7D+%5Ctag%7B1%7D)

其中， ![f_0, \cdots, f_m: \mathbb{R}^n \mapsto \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_0%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+f_m%3A+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En+%5Cmapsto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是二次可微凸函数， ![A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times p}, \mathrm{rank}(A) = n \leq p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A+%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7Bn+%5Ctimes+p%7D%2C+%5Cmathrm%7Brank%7D%28A%29+%3D+n+%5Cleq+p) 。假设最优解用 ![x^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A) 表示，最优值用 ![f_0(x^*)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_0%28x%5E%2A%29) 表示。

我们还假定该问题严格可行，即存在 ![x \in dom(f)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x+%5Cin+dom%28f%29) 满足 ![Ax = b ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Ax+%3D+b+) 和 ![f_i(x) < 0, i =1, \cdots, m](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_i%28x%29+%3C+0%2C+i+%3D1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m) 。这意味着存在最优对偶 ![\lambda^*, \nu^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%2A%2C+%5Cnu%5E%2A) 满足KKT条件(KKT条件的介绍，参见[带约束凸优化最优解的存在条件——KKT条件](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/50230049))：

![\begin{align} f_i(x^*) &\leq 0, \quad i = 1, \cdots, m \\ h_i(x^*) &= 0, \quad i = 1, \cdots, p \\ \lambda_i^* &\ge 0, \quad i = 1, \cdots, m \\ \lambda_i^* f_i(x^*) &= 0, \quad i = 1, \cdots, m \\ \nabla f_0(x^*) + \sum_{i=1}^m \lambda_i^* \nabla f_i(x^*) + \sum_{i=1}^{p} \nu_i^* \nabla h_i(x^*) &= 0 \end{align} \tag{2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+f_i%28x%5E%2A%29+%26%5Cleq+0%2C+%5Cquad+i+%3D+1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5C%5C+h_i%28x%5E%2A%29+%26%3D+0%2C+%5Cquad+i+%3D+1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+p+%5C%5C+%5Clambda_i%5E%2A+%26%5Cge+0%2C+%5Cquad+i+%3D+1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5C%5C+%5Clambda_i%5E%2A+f_i%28x%5E%2A%29+%26%3D+0%2C+%5Cquad+i+%3D+1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5C%5C+%5Cnabla+f_0%28x%5E%2A%29+%2B+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Clambda_i%5E%2A+%5Cnabla+f_i%28x%5E%2A%29+%2B+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bp%7D+%5Cnu_i%5E%2A+%5Cnabla+h_i%28x%5E%2A%29+%26%3D+0+%5Cend%7Balign%7D+%5Ctag%7B2%7D)

所以用内点法求解问题(1)，就等价于求解问题(2)。显然直接求解(2)是非常困难的，所以我们采用一定的技巧(障碍法)，将不等式约束加入的目标函数，从而**使问题转化为带等式约束的凸优化问题**，从而可以利用[无约束凸优化问题求解——Newton Method(四)](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/50348622)介绍的办法进行求解。

## 对数障碍

我们重新表述问题(1)，将其转化为等式约束问题：

![\begin{split} \min \, & f_0(x) + \sum_{i=0}^m I_{-}(f_i(x)) \\ \mathrm{subject \, to} \, & Ax = b \end{split} \tag{3}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bsplit%7D+%5Cmin+%5C%2C+%26+f_0%28x%29+%2B+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D0%7D%5Em+I_%7B-%7D%28f_i%28x%29%29+%5C%5C+%5Cmathrm%7Bsubject+%5C%2C+to%7D+%5C%2C+%26+Ax+%3D+b+%5Cend%7Bsplit%7D+%5Ctag%7B3%7D)

其中 ![I_{-}: \mathbb{R} \mapsto \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_%7B-%7D%3A+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D+%5Cmapsto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是非正实数的示例函数，

![I_{-}(x) =  \begin{cases} 0 & \textrm{ if} \,\, u \leq 0 \\ \infty & \textrm{otherwise} \end{cases}  \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_%7B-%7D%28x%29+%3D++%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+0+%26+%5Ctextrm%7B+if%7D+%5C%2C%5C%2C+u+%5Cleq+0+%5C%5C+%5Cinfty+%26+%5Ctextrm%7Botherwise%7D+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D++%5C%5C)

但是可以看到 ![I_{-}(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I_%7B-%7D%28x%29) 是不可微的函数，不能直接应用Newton Method。但是，我们可以近似示例函数：

![\hat{I_{-}}(x) = \phi(x) =- (1/t) \log(-x), \quad dom(\hat{I_{-}}) = - \mathbb{R}_{++} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Chat%7BI_%7B-%7D%7D%28x%29+%3D+%5Cphi%28x%29+%3D-+%281%2Ft%29+%5Clog%28-x%29%2C+%5Cquad+dom%28%5Chat%7BI_%7B-%7D%7D%29+%3D+-+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D_%7B%2B%2B%7D+%5C%5C)
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-c6031f6aae9028a96b139603445424b0_b.jpg)对数障碍函数。t越大，精度越高，越接近示例函数。
可以看到上面的近似函数，其是可微的凸函数。下面我们求解如下近似问题，并且我们将给予证明当 ![t \to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t+%5Cto+%5Cinfty) 时，近似问题的解与原问题的解是一样的。

![\begin{split} \min \, & f_0(x) + \sum_{i=0}^m -(1/t) \log(-f_i(x)) \\ \mathrm{subject \, to} \, & Ax = b \end{split} \tag{4}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bsplit%7D+%5Cmin+%5C%2C+%26+f_0%28x%29+%2B+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D0%7D%5Em+-%281%2Ft%29+%5Clog%28-f_i%28x%29%29+%5C%5C+%5Cmathrm%7Bsubject+%5C%2C+to%7D+%5C%2C+%26+Ax+%3D+b+%5Cend%7Bsplit%7D+%5Ctag%7B4%7D)

为了方便以后讨论，我们定义 ![\phi(x) = -\sum_{i=1}^m \log(-f_i(x))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cphi%28x%29+%3D+-%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Clog%28-f_i%28x%29%29) 为**对数障碍函数** ，并直接给出其梯度和Hessian矩阵。

![\nabla \phi(x) = \sum_{i=1}^m \frac{1}{-f_i(x)} \nabla f_i(x) \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnabla+%5Cphi%28x%29+%3D+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B-f_i%28x%29%7D+%5Cnabla+f_i%28x%29+%5C%5C)

![\nabla^2 \phi(x) = \sum_{i=1}^m \frac{1}{-f_i(x)} \nabla^2 f_i(x) + \sum_{i=1}^m \frac{1}{f_i(x)^2} \nabla f_i(x) \nabla f_i(x)^T \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnabla%5E2+%5Cphi%28x%29+%3D+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B-f_i%28x%29%7D+%5Cnabla%5E2+f_i%28x%29+%2B+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bf_i%28x%29%5E2%7D+%5Cnabla+f_i%28x%29+%5Cnabla+f_i%28x%29%5ET+%5C%5C)

## 中心路径

下面我们考虑如何求解问题(4)，为了简化符号表达，对目标函数乘以 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) ，考虑如下等价问题：

![\begin{split} \min \,\, &t f_0(x) + \phi(x) \\ \mathrm{subject \, to} \,\, & Ax = b \end{split} \tag{5}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bsplit%7D+%5Cmin+%5C%2C%5C%2C+%26t+f_0%28x%29+%2B+%5Cphi%28x%29+%5C%5C+%5Cmathrm%7Bsubject+%5C%2C+to%7D+%5C%2C%5C%2C+%26+Ax+%3D+b+%5Cend%7Bsplit%7D+%5Ctag%7B5%7D)

对任意的 ![t > 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t+%3E+0) ，上述优化问题存在唯一的最优解 ![x^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A%28t%29) ，将这一系列最优解组成的集合 ![\{x^*(t)\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bx%5E%2A%28t%29%5C%7D) 称为**中心路径**。中心路径上的点满足 ![x^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A%28t%29) 是严格可行的，即：

![Ax^*(t) = b, \quad f_i(x^*(t))<0, \quad i =1, \cdots, m \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Ax%5E%2A%28t%29+%3D+b%2C+%5Cquad+f_i%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29%3C0%2C+%5Cquad+i+%3D1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5C%5C)

并且存在 ![\hat \nu \in  \mathbb{R}^p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Chat+%5Cnu+%5Cin++%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Ep) 使得

![\begin{align} 0 &= t \nabla f_0(x^*(t)) + \nabla \phi(x^*(t)) + A^T \hat \nu \\ & =  t \nabla f_0(x^*(t)) +  \sum_{i=1}^m \frac{1}{-f_i(x^*)} \nabla f_i(x^*) + A^T \hat \nu  \end{align} \tag{6}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+0+%26%3D+t+%5Cnabla+f_0%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29+%2B+%5Cnabla+%5Cphi%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29+%2B+A%5ET+%5Chat+%5Cnu+%5C%5C+%26+%3D++t+%5Cnabla+f_0%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29+%2B++%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B-f_i%28x%5E%2A%29%7D+%5Cnabla+f_i%28x%5E%2A%29+%2B+A%5ET+%5Chat+%5Cnu++%5Cend%7Balign%7D+%5Ctag%7B6%7D)

(6)提供了一个重要的性质：每个中心点产生对偶可行解，从而给出最优值 ![p^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5E%2A) 的一个下界。更确切地说，定义

![\lambda^*(t) = - \frac{1}{tf_i(x^*(t))}, \quad i =1, \cdots, m, \quad \nu^*(t) = \hat \nu/t \tag{7}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29+%3D+-+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Btf_i%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29%7D%2C+%5Cquad+i+%3D1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m%2C+%5Cquad+%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29+%3D+%5Chat+%5Cnu%2Ft+%5Ctag%7B7%7D)

我们要说明 ![\lambda^*(t), \nu^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29%2C+%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29) 是原问题(1)的对偶可行解。

首先验证 ![\lambda^*(t) >0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29+%3E0) ，这点由 ![f_i(x^*(t)) < 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_i%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29+%3C+0) 保证。其次，我们重新整理(6)得到：

![  \nabla f_0(x^*(t)) +  \sum_{i=1}^m \lambda^*(t) \nabla f_i(x^*) + A^T \nu^*(t) = 0 \\ ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=++%5Cnabla+f_0%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29+%2B++%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29+%5Cnabla+f_i%28x%5E%2A%29+%2B+A%5ET+%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29+%3D+0+%5C%5C+)

因此 ![x^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A%28t%29) 使得 ![\lambda = \lambda^*(t), \nu = \nu^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda+%3D+%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29%2C+%5Cnu+%3D+%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29) 时的Lagrange函数

![L(x,\lambda, \nu) = f_0(x) + \sum_{i=1}^m \lambda_i f_i(x) + \sum_{i=1}^p \nu_i h_i(x) \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%28x%2C%5Clambda%2C+%5Cnu%29+%3D+f_0%28x%29+%2B+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Clambda_i+f_i%28x%29+%2B+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Ep+%5Cnu_i+h_i%28x%29+%5C%5C)

达到极小，这意味着 ![ \lambda^*(t), \nu^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29%2C+%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29)是对偶可行解。因此对偶函数 ![g( \lambda^*(t),  \nu^*(t))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28+%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29%2C++%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29%29) 是有界的：

![\begin{align} g( \lambda^*(t),  \nu^*(t)) &= f_0(x^*(t)) +  \sum_{i=1}^m \lambda^*(t)  f_i(x^*) + \nu^*(t)^T(Ax^*(t) - b) \\ &=  f_0(x^*(t)) - m /t   \\ \end{align} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+g%28+%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29%2C++%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29%29+%26%3D+f_0%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29+%2B++%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em+%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29++f_i%28x%5E%2A%29+%2B+%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29%5ET%28Ax%5E%2A%28t%29+-+b%29+%5C%5C+%26%3D++f_0%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29+-+m+%2Ft+++%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Balign%7D+%5C%5C)

即，近似解 ![g( \lambda^*(t),  \nu^*(t))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28+%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29%2C++%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29%29) 与原问题最优解 ![p^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%5E%2A) 之间的间隙就是 ![m/t ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2Ft+) 。随着 ![t \to \infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t+%5Cto+%5Cinfty) ， ![m/t \to 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2Ft+%5Cto+0) 。

![g( \lambda^*(t),  \nu^*(t)) \leq p^*  \Rightarrow  f_0(x^*(t)) -p^* \leq m/t \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28+%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29%2C++%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29%29+%5Cleq+p%5E%2A++%5CRightarrow++f_0%28x%5E%2A%28t%29%29+-p%5E%2A+%5Cleq+m%2Ft+%5C%5C)

> 这里，我们补充给出基于KKT条件的解释。
我们可以将中心路径条件(6)解释为KKT最优性(2)的变形。
点 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 是等于 ![x^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A%28t%29) 的充要条件是存在 ![\lambda, \nu](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%2C+%5Cnu) 满足
![\begin{align} Ax = b, \quad f_i(x) &\leq 0, \quad i=1, \cdots, m \\  \lambda_i &\ge 0, \quad i=1, \cdots, m \\ \nabla f_0(x) + \sum_{i=1}^m\lambda_i f_i(x) + A^T\nu &= 0 \\ -\lambda_i f_i(x) &=1/t, \quad i=1, \cdots, m \end{align} \tag{8}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+Ax+%3D+b%2C+%5Cquad+f_i%28x%29+%26%5Cleq+0%2C+%5Cquad+i%3D1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5C%5C++%5Clambda_i+%26%5Cge+0%2C+%5Cquad+i%3D1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5C%5C+%5Cnabla+f_0%28x%29+%2B+%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5Em%5Clambda_i+f_i%28x%29+%2B+A%5ET%5Cnu+%26%3D+0+%5C%5C+-%5Clambda_i+f_i%28x%29+%26%3D1%2Ft%2C+%5Cquad+i%3D1%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+m+%5Cend%7Balign%7D+%5Ctag%7B8%7D)
近似问题的KKT条件(8)与原始KKT条件(2)的唯一的差别是 ![-\lambda_i f_i(x) =0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-%5Clambda_i+f_i%28x%29+%3D0) 被 ![-\lambda_i f_i(x) =1/t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-%5Clambda_i+f_i%28x%29+%3D1%2Ft) 替代。特别地，当 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 很大时， ![x^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A%28t%29) 和对应的对偶解 ![\lambda^*(t), \nu^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%2A%28t%29%2C+%5Cnu%5E%2A%28t%29) "几乎"满足原始问题(1)的KKT条件。

这里小结一下。我们在中心路径这部分给出了证明**近似问题(5)的最优解与原始问题(1)的最优解的误差不超过 ![m/t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2Ft)** ，因此我们可以放心的求解近似问题(5)。

## 障碍方法

下面给出Newton Method求解近似问题(5)的算法框架

> 重复进行：
1. 中心点步骤：
          从初始值 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) 开始，采用Newton Method在 ![Ax=b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Ax%3Db) 的约束下极小化 ![tf_0 + \phi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=tf_0+%2B+%5Cphi) ，最终确定 ![x^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A%28t%29)
2. 改进： ![x:= x^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3A%3D+x%5E%2A%28t%29)
3. 停止准则。 如果 ![m/t < \epsilon](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2Ft+%3C+%5Cepsilon) 则退出
4. 增加 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) ： ![t:= \mu t, \quad \mu>1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3A%3D+%5Cmu+t%2C+%5Cquad+%5Cmu%3E1)

根据在中心路径小节中的分析，近似问题(5)的最优解与原始问题(1)的最优解的误差不超过 ![m/t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2Ft)，所以在求解时，我们从较小的 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 开始，不断增大 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 直至收敛。此外，我们把每次求出的最优解 ![x^*(t)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%2A%28t%29) 当作下一个 ![t:= \mu t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3A%3D+%5Cmu+t) 的初值，这样Newton Method会收敛更快，即Step1.中心点步骤的运行时间可以当作常数对待，而不是一个耗时的迭代循环。

## 修改KKT方程的Newton下降方向

整篇文章到这里，只剩下Step1没有具体讲。读者可以自行参照[带等式约束凸优化问题的求解——Newton Method(六)](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/50411305)自己推导，或者参照[《Convex Optimization》](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//book.douban.com/subject/21249088/)的P550-552页了解详细的计算步骤。

## 总结

在这篇文章里，我们介绍了带等式约束和不等式约束的凸优化问题。**通过引入对数障碍函数，我们把原问题近似为带等式约束的凸优化问题**，从而可以应用Newton Method求解KKT最优条件，找到最优解；此外，我们也给出了近似问题的最优解和原问题的最优解之间的误差，证明当 ![t](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t) 不断增大时，精度会不断提高。

到这里，整体凸优化系列已经全部讲完了。对于最简单的无约束凸优化问题，我们可以采用Gradient Descent或者Newton Method求解；对于稍复杂的等式约束凸优化问题，我们对目标函数进行二阶Taylor近似，然后采用Newton Method求解KKT最优条件；对于最复杂的带不等式约束问题，则时引入对数障碍函数，转化为带等式约束的凸优化问题。
