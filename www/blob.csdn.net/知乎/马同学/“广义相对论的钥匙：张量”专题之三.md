**1 余向量**

**1.1 什么是余向量（covector）？**

空间中的直线、平面这些“线性”的几何对象，也是线性代数所关心的。

比如平面中的一条直线，很显然与坐标无关，是一个几何对象：
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-a2c36451e78d4b17217bf291998c118b_b.jpg)
此直线在不同的基下有不同的代数表示：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-84505c89d084fbba3460c8b7a4d22b25_b.jpg)
直线符合之前说的张量的特点：
- 直线是一个几何对象，它与基无关
- 不同的基下，有不同的代数表达
- 并且，不同的坐标值之间有明确的转换规则

表示直线、平面这些“线性”的几何对象的就是余向量，也是张量的一种。

**1.2 余向量的细节**

刚才的直线，在直角坐标系下：
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-94273b0a33de2216af5d6451350fea53_b.jpg)
对应的直线方程为：

![2x+y=0\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2x%2By%3D0%5C%5C)

在正交单位基下，可以用这个式子来表示：
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-54f8d91597a281ef07fc047d541bad7f_b.jpg)
此处，可认为行向量 ![\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D) ，就代表了这根直线，也就是所谓的余向量。

更严格来说，应该把余向量看作表示这根直线的线性函数：

![\mathcal{L}(\overrightarrow {v})=0\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathcal%7BL%7D%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29%3D0%5C%5C)

给行向量一个符号：

![\alpha =\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C)

那么，这样表示看着是不是更像函数：
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-039e0870c23677f6bf2b7210de101813_b.jpg)
值得注意的是，余向量是 ![\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D) 的话，它代表的线性函数并没有指定等号右边必须为0：
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-6e5ff4b671d085079dda0538170e30dc_b.gif)
实际上，它代表了所有平行的直线。

写成线性函数的样子就是这样的：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-812260316bd854f3fe02c09a59e41a81_b.gif)

![\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \end{pmatrix}=6\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+x+%5C%5C+y+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3D6%5C%5C)

**2.2 对偶空间**

向量空间定义是这样的：

> **设****![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)****为一向量组，如果****![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)****非空，且****![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)****对于向量的加法及数乘两种运算封闭，那么就称****![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)****为向量空间。 所谓封闭，是指在****![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)****中向量进行数乘和加减，其结果依然在****![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V)中。具体的说，就是:**
- **若****![\overrightarrow {a}\in V,\overrightarrow {b}\in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow+%7Ba%7D%5Cin+V%2C%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bb%7D%5Cin+V)****，则****![\overrightarrow {a}+\overrightarrow {b} \in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow+%7Ba%7D%2B%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bb%7D+%5Cin+V)**
- **若****![\overrightarrow {a}\in V,k\in \mathbb {R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow+%7Ba%7D%5Cin+V%2Ck%5Cin+%5Cmathbb+%7BR%7D)****，则****![k\overrightarrow {a} \in V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Coverrightarrow+%7Ba%7D+%5Cin+V)**


也就是说，向量空间 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 是对向量的数乘和加法封闭的空间。

同样的道理，有个数乘和加法之后就可以定义对偶空间了。

对于余向量的数乘和加法封闭的空间，称为对偶空间，一般记为 ![V^*](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%2A) （其实余向量也是向量，对偶空间也是向量空间）。

![\begin{cases} \epsilon ^1(\overrightarrow {e_1})=1\quad \epsilon ^1(\overrightarrow {e_2})=0 \\ \epsilon ^2(\overrightarrow {e_1})=0\quad \epsilon ^2(\overrightarrow {e_2})=1 \end{cases}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%3D1%5Cquad+%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%3D0+%5C%5C+%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%3D0%5Cquad+%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%3D1+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D%5C%5C)

**3.2 余向量的基作用在向量上**

先来算一下，余向量的基和向量之间的计算结果是什么？

![ \begin{align*} \epsilon ^1(\overrightarrow {v}) & =\epsilon ^1(v^1\overrightarrow {e_1}+v^2\overrightarrow {e_2})\\ & =\epsilon ^1(v^1\overrightarrow {e_1})+\epsilon ^1(v^2\overrightarrow {e_2})\\ & =v^1\underbrace{\epsilon ^1(\overrightarrow {e_1})}_{1}+v^2\underbrace{\epsilon ^1(\overrightarrow {e_2})}_{0}\\ & =v^1 \end{align*} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29+%26+%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28v%5E1%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%2Bv%5E2%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28v%5E1%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%2B%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28v%5E2%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%5C%5C+%26+%3Dv%5E1%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%7D_%7B1%7D%2Bv%5E2%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%7D_%7B0%7D%5C%5C+%26+%3Dv%5E1+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C)

![ \begin{align*} \epsilon ^2(\overrightarrow {v}) & =\epsilon ^2(v^1\overrightarrow {e_1}+v^2\overrightarrow {e_2})\\ & =\epsilon ^2(v^1\overrightarrow {e_1})+\epsilon ^1(v^2\overrightarrow {e_2})\\ & =v^1\underbrace{\epsilon ^2(\overrightarrow {e_1})}_{0}+v^2\underbrace{\epsilon ^2(\overrightarrow {e_2})}_{1}\\ & =v^2 \end{align*} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29+%26+%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28v%5E1%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%2Bv%5E2%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28v%5E1%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%2B%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28v%5E2%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%5C%5C+%26+%3Dv%5E1%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%7D_%7B0%7D%2Bv%5E2%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%7D_%7B1%7D%5C%5C+%26+%3Dv%5E2+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C)

看来，余向量的基有把坐标的分量分离出来的作用：

![\begin{cases} v^1=\epsilon ^1(\overrightarrow {v})\\ v^2=\epsilon ^2(\overrightarrow {v}) \end{cases} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+v%5E1%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29%5C%5C+v%5E2%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D+%5C%5C)

这点从几何上也可以看出，比如，这个向量与 ![\epsilon ^1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cepsilon+%5E1) 交于 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) ：
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-51e7abec15b70698d3be14a1157f6a8e_b.jpg)
与 ![\epsilon ^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cepsilon+%5E2) 交于 ![2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2) :
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-e573884143f01fb21c8a5d958fba5503_b.jpg)
上面这个向量确实就是：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-4dc41bcbdf9b44a30528d65cc2fe9c85_b.jpg)
**3.3 余向量通过基来表示**

把之前的余向量用符号来表示：

![\alpha =\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C)

看看它是怎么作用到向量上的：

![ \begin{align*} \alpha (\overrightarrow {v}) & =\alpha (v^1\overrightarrow {e_1}+v^2\overrightarrow {e_2})\\ & =\alpha (v^1\overrightarrow {e_1})+\alpha (v^2\overrightarrow {e_2})\\ & =v^1\alpha (\overrightarrow {e_1})+v^2\alpha (\overrightarrow {e_2}) \end{align*} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29+%26+%3D%5Calpha+%28v%5E1%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%2Bv%5E2%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Calpha+%28v%5E1%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%2B%5Calpha+%28v%5E2%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%5C%5C+%26+%3Dv%5E1%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%2Bv%5E2%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C)

令：

![\alpha (\overrightarrow {e_1})=a_1\quad \alpha (\overrightarrow {e_2})=a_2\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%3Da_1%5Cquad+%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%3Da_2%5C%5C)

其中， ![a_1,a_2\in \mathbb {R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_1%2Ca_2%5Cin+%5Cmathbb+%7BR%7D) （余向量是输入向量，输出实数的线性函数）

并且根据刚才的结论：

![\begin{cases} v^1=\epsilon ^1(\overrightarrow {v})\\ v^2=\epsilon ^2(\overrightarrow {v}) \end{cases}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+v%5E1%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29%5C%5C+v%5E2%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D%5C%5C)

所以上式可以变为：

![ \begin{align*} \alpha (\overrightarrow {v}) & =v^1\alpha (\overrightarrow {e_1})+v^2\alpha (\overrightarrow {e_2})\\ & =\epsilon ^1(\overrightarrow {v})a_1+\epsilon ^2(\overrightarrow {v})a_1\\ & =(a_1\epsilon ^1+a_2\epsilon ^2)(\overrightarrow {v}) \end{align*} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29+%26+%3Dv%5E1%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%2Bv%5E2%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E1%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29a_1%2B%5Cepsilon+%5E2%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29a_1%5C%5C+%26+%3D%28a_1%5Cepsilon+%5E1%2Ba_2%5Cepsilon+%5E2%29%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C)

可见余向量可以由余向量的基来线性表示：

![\alpha =a_1\epsilon ^1+a_2\epsilon ^2\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%3Da_1%5Cepsilon+%5E1%2Ba_2%5Cepsilon+%5E2%5C%5C)

其中：

![\begin{cases} a_1=\alpha (\overrightarrow {e_1})\\ a_2=\alpha (\overrightarrow {e_2}) \end{cases}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+a_1%3D%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D%29%5C%5C+a_2%3D%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D%29+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D%5C%5C)

![a_1,a_2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_1%2Ca_2) 可以称为此基下的坐标，我这里用的是下标，大家应该就明白是协变量了，后面还会对此进行说明。

**3.4 小结**

停顿下，至此，得到了下面这些结论，注意，虽然上面举例子画图用的是单位正交基，但是下面的结论对所有基都成立。

余向量的基的定义：

![ \epsilon ^ i(\overrightarrow {e_ j})=\delta _{ij}= \begin{cases} 1\quad i=j\\ 0\quad i\ne j \end{cases} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cepsilon+%5E+i%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_+j%7D%29%3D%5Cdelta+_%7Bij%7D%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+1%5Cquad+i%3Dj%5C%5C+0%5Cquad+i%5Cne+j+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D+%5C%5C)

余向量与向量之间的作用：

![v^ i=\epsilon ^ i(\overrightarrow {v})\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%5E+i%3D%5Cepsilon+%5E+i%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Bv%7D%29%5C%5C)

余向量用基来表示（用了爱因斯塔标记法，把 ![\sum ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum+) 符号去掉了）：

![\alpha =a_ i\epsilon ^ i\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%3Da_+i%5Cepsilon+%5E+i%5C%5C)

**4.2 余向量的转换**

根据之前我们得出的结论：

![\alpha =a_ i\epsilon ^ i\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%3Da_+i%5Cepsilon+%5E+i%5C%5C)

其中：

![a_ i=\alpha (\overrightarrow {e_ i})\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_+i%3D%5Calpha+%28%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_+i%7D%29%5C%5C)

可知，余向量的坐标可以由余向量与基来决定。

根据这个结论，其实可以通过几何看出余向量是什么。比如，之前 ![\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D) ：
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-d27b55119764d632235c7b297a49e51e_b.jpg)

![\overrightarrow {e_1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_1%7D) 与余向量交于 ![2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2) ， ![\overrightarrow {e_2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be_2%7D) 与余向量交于 ![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) ，所以余向量为 ![\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D) 。

在我们关心的另外一个基下：
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-e4c97372b71961a825f7cbfe479bc5d8_b.jpg)

![\overrightarrow {e'_1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be%27_1%7D) 与余向量交于 ![3](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3) ， ![\overrightarrow {e'_2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow+%7Be%27_2%7D) 与余向量交于 ![-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1) ，所以余向量为 ![\begin{pmatrix} 3 & -1 \end{pmatrix}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+3+%26+-1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D) 。

从代数上这么求：

![\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}F=\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix} 1 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix} 3 & -1 \end{pmatrix}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7DF%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+1+%26+-1+%5C%5C+1+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+3+%26+-1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C)

转回来可以这么算：

![\begin{pmatrix} 3 & -1 \end{pmatrix}B=\begin{pmatrix} 3 & -1 \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix} 0.5 & 0.5 \\ -0.5 & 0.5 \end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+3+%26+-1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7DB%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+3+%26+-1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+0.5+%26+0.5+%5C%5C+-0.5+%26+0.5+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+2+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C)

![ \begin{cases} \alpha '_ j=F^ i_ j\alpha _ i\\ \alpha _ j=B^ i_ j\alpha '_ i \end{cases} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%5Calpha+%27_+j%3DF%5E+i_+j%5Calpha+_+i%5C%5C+%5Calpha+_+j%3DB%5E+i_+j%5Calpha+%27_+i+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D+%5C%5C)

**4.3 余向量的基的转换**

向量的基和向量之间的转换是反的，可以这么想象，基变长了，那么要表示同一个点，坐标自然需要变小，来补偿这个变化。

同理，余向量的基和余向量之间的转换也是反的，余向量的基是逆变量：

![ \begin{cases} \epsilon '^ i=B^ i_ j\epsilon ^ j\\ \epsilon ^ i=F^ i_ j\epsilon '^ j\\ \end{cases} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%5Cepsilon+%27%5E+i%3DB%5E+i_+j%5Cepsilon+%5E+j%5C%5C+%5Cepsilon+%5E+i%3DF%5E+i_+j%5Cepsilon+%27%5E+j%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D+%5C%5C)
