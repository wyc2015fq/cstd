**1 抛物线下的曲边梯形**

**1.1 问题**

之前介绍过，要求![f(x)=x^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Dx%5E2) ，![x\in[0,a]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin%5B0%2Ca%5D) 之间的曲边梯形的面积![A](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A) ：
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ace70a8c34f7e130db616eda6995c760_b.jpg)
可以把![[0,a]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2Ca%5D) 均分为![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 份，以每一份线段为底，以这一份线段的右侧的函数值为高做矩形：
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-7669dfd8c3afa5e96bbeccd138211444_b.jpg)

把坐标组成两个集合：

![\{a_i\}=\left\{\frac{0a}{n}, \frac{a}{n}, \frac{2a}{n},\cdots, \frac{(n-1)a}{n}\right\}\quad \{b_i\}=\left\{\frac{a}{n}, \frac{2a}{n},\cdots, \frac{(n-1)a}{n},\frac{na}{n}\right\}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ba_i%5C%7D%3D%5Cleft%5C%7B%5Cfrac%7B0a%7D%7Bn%7D%2C+%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bn%7D%2C+%5Cfrac%7B2a%7D%7Bn%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C+%5Cfrac%7B%28n-1%29a%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%5C%7D%5Cquad+%5C%7Bb_i%5C%7D%3D%5Cleft%5C%7B%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bn%7D%2C+%5Cfrac%7B2a%7D%7Bn%7D%2C%5Ccdots%2C+%5Cfrac%7B%28n-1%29a%7D%7Bn%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7Bna%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%5C%7D%5C%5C)

因此，以左侧的函数值为高的矩形和可以如下计算：
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-d4d846e132a7d713dae12a50d28a4e7b_b.jpg)
同样的道理，可以得到以右侧的函数值为高的矩形和：

当![n\to\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cto%5Cinfty) 的时候，两者是相等的，它们都是曲边梯形的面积：

**2 狄利克雷函数的曲边梯形**

之前介绍连续的时候就介绍过狄利克雷函数：

![ D(x)=\begin{cases}     1&,x为有理数\\     0&,x为无理数 \end{cases} \\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+D%28x%29%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+++++1%26%2Cx%E4%B8%BA%E6%9C%89%E7%90%86%E6%95%B0%5C%5C+++++0%26%2Cx%E4%B8%BA%E6%97%A0%E7%90%86%E6%95%B0+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D+%5C%5C)

也见识过它的古怪性质。这里也要把它拉出来作一个反面典型。![D(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28x%29) 的图像是没有办法画的，非要画也就是这样的：
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-6c38032ff1a4c843d839afb946865674_b.jpg)
假设要求![[0,1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2C1%5D) 内的曲边梯形面积，尝试对![[0,1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2C1%5D) 进行![5](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=5) 等分，那么等分点必然为有理数点（下图为了演示方便，调整了下![xy](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=xy) 坐标的比例）：
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-30ea806e3e92774a4182ca6b4d6fcdba_b.jpg)
所以这些等分点的函数值必然为1。以1为高，以等分区间长度为底作矩形，可以得到：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-bb64f49f6dc4cbb4a5843977e212bfb1_b.jpg)
这些矩形的和必然为1，可以想象进行![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 等分也依然为1，所以有：

![A_{有理}=1\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%9C%89%E7%90%86%7D%3D1%5C%5C)

下面换一种划分方式，以邻近的两个无理数作为端点划分区间，这些区间的端点的函数值必然为0，以区间长度为底，0为高，得到的矩形和为：

![A_{无理}=0\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%97%A0%E7%90%86%7D%3D0%5C%5C)

可见，对于![D(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D%28x%29) 而言，不同的划分区间、不同的高的取法，会导致不同的矩形和：

![A_{有理}\ne A _{无理}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%9C%89%E7%90%86%7D%5Cne+A+_%7B%E6%97%A0%E7%90%86%7D%5C%5C)

**3 黎曼和**
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-ca8928e8c14e605bf8d94cb197e31d12_b.jpg)
格奥尔格·弗雷德里希·波恩哈德·黎曼（1826－1866）是德国数学家，黎曼几何学创始人，复变函数论创始人之一。在数学界搞风搞雨的黎曼猜想也是他的杰作。

基于对刚才两种情况：
- 抛物线下的曲边梯形
- 狄利克雷函数下的曲边梯形

的思考，看到不同划分带来的效果，黎曼先发明了黎曼和，进而定义了曲边梯形的面积，也就是定积分。

**3.1 任意划分**

![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 不一定需要均分为![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 份，可以任意分割：
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-6005c782f20c64d3ad0600e7cfc492f0_b.gif)
很显然用于分割区间的点符合：

![a < x_1 < x_2 < \cdots < x_{n-1} < b\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a+%3C+x_1+%3C+x_2+%3C+%5Ccdots+%3C+x_%7Bn-1%7D+%3C+b%5C%5C)

令![x_0=a,x_n=b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0%3Da%2Cx_n%3Db) ，那么集合：

![P=\{x_0, x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_{n}\}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%3D%5C%7Bx_0%2C+x_1%2C+x_2%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+x_%7Bn%7D%5C%7D%5C%5C)

称为![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的一个![\color{Salmon}{划分}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E5%88%92%E5%88%86%7D) 。划分![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) 定义了![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n) 个子区间：

![[x_0, x_1], [x_1, x_2], \cdots,[x_{k-1}, x_k],\cdots, [x_{n-1}, x_n]\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_0%2C+x_1%5D%2C+%5Bx_1%2C+x_2%5D%2C+%5Ccdots%2C%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2C+x_k%5D%2C%5Ccdots%2C+%5Bx_%7Bn-1%7D%2C+x_n%5D%5C%5C)

![[x_0, x_1]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_0%2C+x_1%5D) 称为第![1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1) 个子区间，更一般的![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D) 被称为第![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个子区间：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-984dcdf0f7b3794f155f7f4e96dc03d1_b.jpg)
第![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个子区间的长度为![\Delta x_k=x_k-x_{k-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+x_k%3Dx_k-x_%7Bk-1%7D) ：
![](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-96a4d28eac66e5b7cc1614c190ff6387_b.jpg)
**3.2 任意高度**

对于某一个划分![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P) ，在其第![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 个子区间内随便选一个数![\xi_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi_k) ：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-3272e410a6bb8c2387bfad8c1c6323d5_b.gif)
以![f(\xi_k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Cxi_k%29) 作为矩形的高：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-d8a7735e2a1fc10e980506c151d55d65_b.jpg)
那么矩形的高度也可以是任意的：
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-8ff4bac50ca60b9b7222e54df11a0096_b.gif)
**3.3 黎曼和**

根据刚才的讲解，可以得到如下定义：

> **设函数![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29)****在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D)****上有定义，在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D)****上任意插入若干个分点：![a = x_0 < x_1 < x_2\cdots < x_n = b\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a+%3D+x_0+%3C+x_1+%3C+x_2%5Ccdots+%3C+x_n+%3D+b%5C%5C)**
**这些分点的集合：**
**![P=\{x_0, x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_{n}\}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%3D%5C%7Bx_0%2C+x_1%2C+x_2%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+x_%7Bn%7D%5C%7D%5C%5C)**
**称为![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D)****的一个![\color{Salmon}{划分}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E5%88%92%E5%88%86%7D)****。划分![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P)****定义了![n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n)****个子区间：**
**![[x_0, x_1], [x_1, x_2], \cdots, [x_{n-1}, x_n]\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_0%2C+x_1%5D%2C+%5Bx_1%2C+x_2%5D%2C+%5Ccdots%2C+%5Bx_%7Bn-1%7D%2C+x_n%5D%5C%5C)**
**它们的长度依次为：**
**![\Delta x_1=x_1-x_0,\Delta x_2=x_2-x_1,\cdots,\Delta x_n=x_n-x_{n-1}\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+x_1%3Dx_1-x_0%2C%5CDelta+x_2%3Dx_2-x_1%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5CDelta+x_n%3Dx_n-x_%7Bn-1%7D%5C%5C)**
**在每个子区间![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D)****上任取选取一个数![\xi_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi_k)****，以![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D)****为底，![f(\xi_i)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Cxi_i%29)****为高构造矩形，这些矩形的和：**
**![A_n=\sum_{k=1}^{n}f(\xi_k)\Delta x_k\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_n%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df%28%5Cxi_k%29%5CDelta+x_k%5C%5C)**
**称为![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f)****在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D)****上的![\color{Salmon}{黎曼和}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E9%BB%8E%E6%9B%BC%E5%92%8C%7D)****。**

之前计算的![A_L](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_L) 、![A_R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_R) 是黎曼和：
![](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-772021bf7b1140c79ce89bf9d7d8c5ea_b.jpg)
狄利克雷函数中划分出来的矩形和![A_{有理}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%9C%89%E7%90%86%7D) 、![A_{无理}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%97%A0%E7%90%86%7D) 也是黎曼和。

**4 定积分**

随着![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D) 的划分不断变细，所有子区间的长度趋于0时，黎曼和不断地逼近曲边梯形的面积：
![](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-ffb6bd83ee0d17e23df7a226d8f706f5_b.gif)
这个过程的严格化如下：

> **设函数![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29)****在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D)****上有定义，对于![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D)****上的任意划分![P](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P)****，![\xi_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxi_k)****为子区间![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D)****上任意选取的数，子区间![[x_{k-1},x_k]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Bx_%7Bk-1%7D%2Cx_k%5D)****的长度为![\Delta x_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+x_k)****，记：![\lambda=max\{\Delta x_1,\Delta x_2,...,\Delta x_n\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%3Dmax%5C%7B%5CDelta+x_1%2C%5CDelta+x_2%2C...%2C%5CDelta+x_n%5C%7D)**
**如果下述极限存在：**
**![I=\lim_{\lambda\to0}\sum_{k=1}^{n}f(\xi_k)\Delta x_k\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%5Clim_%7B%5Clambda%5Cto0%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df%28%5Cxi_k%29%5CDelta+x_k%5C%5C)**
**则称![\color{Salmon}{被积函数}f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E8%A2%AB%E7%A7%AF%E5%87%BD%E6%95%B0%7Df%28x%29)****在![\color{Salmon}{积分区间}[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E5%8C%BA%E9%97%B4%7D%5Ba%2Cb%5D)****上![\color{Salmon}{可积}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E5%8F%AF%E7%A7%AF%7D)****，![a](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a)****为![\color{Salmon}{积分下限}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E4%B8%8B%E9%99%90%7D)****，![b](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b)****为![\color{Salmon}{积分上限}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E4%B8%8A%E9%99%90%7D)****，![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I)****为![f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29)****在![[a,b]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D)****上的![\color{Salmon}{定积分}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E5%AE%9A%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%7D)****，![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x)****为![\color{Salmon}{积分变量}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7B%E7%A7%AF%E5%88%86%E5%8F%98%E9%87%8F%7D)****，可以标记如下：**
**![I=\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I%3D%5Cint_%7Ba%7D%5E%7Bb%7Df%28x%29dx%5C%5C)**

回到之前讨论的问题：
- 抛物线下的曲边梯形：![A_{Ln}=A_{Rn}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7BLn%7D%3DA_%7BRn%7D) ，以及各种划分都相等，所以![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 存在，可积
- 狄利克雷函数下的曲边梯形：![A_{有理}\ne A_{无理}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B%E6%9C%89%E7%90%86%7D%5Cne+A_%7B%E6%97%A0%E7%90%86%7D) ，所以![I](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=I) 不存在，不可积

这里新引入的积分符号是莱布尼兹创造的：

![S\quad\xrightarrow{\quad拉长\quad}\quad \int\\](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%5Cquad%5Cxrightarrow%7B%5Cquad%E6%8B%89%E9%95%BF%5Cquad%7D%5Cquad+%5Cint%5C%5C)

其中，![S](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S) 代表英文中的求和（“sum”），拉长的![\int](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint) 则表明积分是和的极限（“limits of sums”）。这个符号相当精练，可以表达非常丰富的信息：
![](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-32e90f06f260661ab085cf8bb9faba60_b.jpg)
