## **一、一般化的迁移法：基础准备**

如果我们定义 ![S_u f=f(\cdot+u)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_u+f%3Df%28%5Ccdot%2Bu%29)![\,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%2C) ，这等于定义一个从群 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 到 ![L^p(\mathbb{T})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep%28%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%29) 的一个「群操作」，也就是说

![S: \mathbb{R}\to \mathcal{L}(L^p(\mathbb{T}))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%3A+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5Cto+%5Cmathcal%7BL%7D%28L%5Ep%28%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%29%29)

是一个群同态，并且 ![u\mapsto S_u f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%5Cmapsto+S_u+f) 是连续的。那么（1）可以写成 

![H_kf=\int_\mathbb{R}k(u)S_{-u} f du](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_kf%3D%5Cint_%5Cmathbb%7BR%7Dk%28u%29S_%7B-u%7D+f+du) .

这启发我们推广这个结果，设 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 是一个拓扑群， 而 ![R](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R) 是一个在 ![L^p(X,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep%28X%2C%5Cmu%29) 上的连续群表示，也就是

A: ![R: G\to \mathcal{L}(L^p(X,\mu))](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%3A+G%5Cto+%5Cmathcal%7BL%7D%28L%5Ep%28X%2C%5Cmu%29%29)  是一个群同态； 

B: 对于任意 ![f\in L^p(X,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L%5Ep%28X%2C%5Cmu%29) ,函数 ![G\ni u\mapsto R_u f\in L^p(X,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G%5Cni+u%5Cmapsto+R_u+f%5Cin+L%5Ep%28X%2C%5Cmu%29) 是连续的。 

群操作是普遍存在的一个概念，在分析里面，我们关注的是至少具备「连续性」的群操作，也就是所谓的连续群表示。 比如，我们设 ![G=\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G%3D%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) , 平移算子 ![(\tau_x f)(y)= f(y-x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Ctau_x+f%29%28y%29%3D+f%28y-x%29) 就是 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 在 ![L^p(\mathbb{R}) (1\leq p<+\infty)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%29+%281%5Cleq+p%3C%2B%5Cinfty%29) 的连续群表示。 

设 ![k\in L^1(G)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cin+L%5E1%28G%29) 并且带有紧支集，如果我们可以定义一个在 ![ L^p(X,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+L%5Ep%28X%2C%5Cmu%29) 上的线性算子：

![(H_k F)(x)=\int_G k(u)(R_{-u}F)(x) du](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H_k+F%29%28x%29%3D%5Cint_G+k%28u%29%28R_%7B-u%7DF%29%28x%29+du)

这个算子是适定的，我们设 ![c=\sup_{u\in G} \|R_u\|<+\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%3D%5Csup_%7Bu%5Cin+G%7D+%5C%7CR_u%5C%7C%3C%2B%5Cinfty) ,从而可以直接计算得到

![\|H_k F\|_{L^p}\leq \int_G |k(u)|\|R_{-u} F\|_{L^p} du\leq c\int_{G} |k(u)| du \|F\|_{L^p}=c\|k\|_{L^1(G)}\|F\|_{L^p}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_k+F%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%7D%5Cleq+%5Cint_G+%7Ck%28u%29%7C%5C%7CR_%7B-u%7D+F%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%7D+du%5Cleq+c%5Cint_%7BG%7D+%7Ck%28u%29%7C+du+%5C%7CF%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%7D%3Dc%5C%7Ck%5C%7C_%7BL%5E1%28G%29%7D%5C%7CF%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%7D)

另外一方面，根据Young式积分不等式，

![N_p(k)=\sup_{\|g\|_{L^p}=1}\|k\star g\|_{L^p}\leq \|k\|_{L^1(G)}<+\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_p%28k%29%3D%5Csup_%7B%5C%7Cg%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%7D%3D1%7D%5C%7Ck%5Cstar+g%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%7D%5Cleq+%5C%7Ck%5C%7C_%7BL%5E1%28G%29%7D%3C%2B%5Cinfty) .

我们希望得到一个更好的如下估计：

**猜想：** 算子 ![H_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_k) 在 ![L^p(X,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep%28X%2C%5Cmu%29) 上的范数可以用 ![N_p(k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_p%28k%29) 估计, 也就是

![\|H_k\|_{\mathcal{L}(L^p(X))}\lesssim N_p(k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_k%5C%7C_%7B%5Cmathcal%7BL%7D%28L%5Ep%28X%29%29%7D%5Clesssim+N_p%28k%29) .

**为什么我们能把它联系到一起呢？**

设我们首先用任意 ![R_v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R_v) 作用到 ![H_k F](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_k+F)

![R_vH_k F= \int_{G} k(u)(R_{v u^{-1}}F)(x) du ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R_vH_k+F%3D+%5Cint_%7BG%7D+k%28u%29%28R_%7Bv+u%5E%7B-1%7D%7DF%29%28x%29+du+)

如果我们设 ![g_x(w)=(R_w F)(x) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g_x%28w%29%3D%28R_w+F%29%28x%29+) ,  那么

![(R_vH_kF)(x)=(k\star g_x)(v)=\int_G k(u)g_x(v-u)du](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28R_vH_kF%29%28x%29%3D%28k%5Cstar+g_x%29%28v%29%3D%5Cint_G+k%28u%29g_x%28v-u%29du). 

如此我们就能把算子 ![H_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_k) 和算子 ![g\mapsto g\star k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%5Cmapsto+g%5Cstar+k) 联系在一起。

## **二、主要结果及其证明**

我们需要给 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 增加一个条件，这个条件的作用我们后续在证明后解释，本质上它是一个技术性假设，也就说这个假设是我们证明思路必须的条件。

设 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 满足： 对于任意一个紧集 ![C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C) 和 ![\epsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cepsilon%3E0) , 存在一个单位元的开领域 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 使得

![\frac{|V C^{-1}|}{|V|}\leq 1+\epsilon.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%7CV+C%5E%7B-1%7D%7C%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D%5Cleq+1%2B%5Cepsilon.)

这里 ![|V|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7CV%7C) 表示 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 的Haar测度。我们管这个性质叫可均性 (amenable).

如果 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 本身是紧的，那么我们直接选取 ![V=G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%3DG) 就好，也就是说有的紧拓扑群满足可均性。 

如果 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 是 ![LCA](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=LCA) 的，我们可以证明它也是可均的，**但是其证明并不简单，大家可以尝试去证明。 **

**定理：** 在上述假设下，算子 ![H_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_k) 在 ![L^p(X,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep%28X%2C%5Cmu%29) 上的范数可以用 ![N_p(k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_p%28k%29) 估计, 也就是

![\|H_k\|_{\mathcal{L}(L^p(X))}\leq c^2 N_p(k) ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_k%5C%7C_%7B%5Cmathcal%7BL%7D%28L%5Ep%28X%29%29%7D%5Cleq+c%5E2+N_p%28k%29+) .

**证明： **我们首先注意到

（1） ![\|H_k F\|_p=\| R_{-v}R_vH_kF\|\leq\|R_{-v}\| \|R_v H_k F\|_p \leq c\|R_v H_k F\|_p.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_k+F%5C%7C_p%3D%5C%7C+R_%7B-v%7DR_vH_kF%5C%7C%5Cleq%5C%7CR_%7B-v%7D%5C%7C+%5C%7CR_v+H_k+F%5C%7C_p+%5Cleq+c%5C%7CR_v+H_k+F%5C%7C_p.)

设 ![ C](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+C) 就是 ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 在 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 的紧支集，对于  ![\epsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cepsilon%3E0) , 存在一个单位元的开领域 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 使得

![\frac{|V C^{-1}|}{|V|}\leq 1+\epsilon.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%7CV+C%5E%7B-1%7D%7C%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D%5Cleq+1%2B%5Cepsilon.)

根据（1）我们在 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 在做个积分然后平均下来就得到了下面的结果，

![\int_{X}|(H_k F)(x)|^p d\mu(x) \leq c^p \frac{1}{|V|}\int_V\int_{X}|(R_v H_k F)(x)|^p d\mu(x) dv ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7BX%7D%7C%28H_k+F%29%28x%29%7C%5Ep+d%5Cmu%28x%29+%5Cleq+c%5Ep+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D%5Cint_V%5Cint_%7BX%7D%7C%28R_v+H_k+F%29%28x%29%7C%5Ep+d%5Cmu%28x%29+dv+)

设 ![\chi ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cchi+) 是 ![V C^{-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V+C%5E%7B-1%7D) 上的指示函数，这样的我们就可以把积分从 ![V](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V) 拓展到 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G)

![\begin{align*} &\int_{X}|(H_k F)(x)|^p d\mu(x)\\ \leq& c^p \frac{1}{|V|}\int_V\int_{X}|\int_{G}k(u)(R_{vu^{-1}} F)(x)du|^p d\mu(x) dv \\ \leq& c^p \frac{1}{|V|}\int_G\int_{X}|\int_{G}k(u)\chi_{vu^{-1}}(R_{vu^{-1}} F)(x)du |^p d\mu(x) dv\\ =&c^p \frac{1}{|V|}\int_{X}\left( \int_G|\int_{G}k(u)\chi_{vu^{-1}}(R_{vu^{-1}} F)(x)du |^p dv\right) d\mu(x) , \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Cint_%7BX%7D%7C%28H_k+F%29%28x%29%7C%5Ep+d%5Cmu%28x%29%5C%5C+%5Cleq%26+c%5Ep+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D%5Cint_V%5Cint_%7BX%7D%7C%5Cint_%7BG%7Dk%28u%29%28R_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7D+F%29%28x%29du%7C%5Ep+d%5Cmu%28x%29+dv+%5C%5C+%5Cleq%26+c%5Ep+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D%5Cint_G%5Cint_%7BX%7D%7C%5Cint_%7BG%7Dk%28u%29%5Cchi_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7D%28R_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7D+F%29%28x%29du+%7C%5Ep+d%5Cmu%28x%29+dv%5C%5C+%3D%26c%5Ep+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D%5Cint_%7BX%7D%5Cleft%28+%5Cint_G%7C%5Cint_%7BG%7Dk%28u%29%5Cchi_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7D%28R_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7D+F%29%28x%29du+%7C%5Ep+dv%5Cright%29+d%5Cmu%28x%29+%2C+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

这时候我们把 ![\chi_{vu^{-1}}(R_{vu^{-1}} F)(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cchi_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7D%28R_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7D+F%29%28x%29)看成一个整体，那么

![\int_{G}k(u)(\chi_{vu^{-1}}R_{vu^{-1}} F)(x)du=k\star \chi(R F)(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7BG%7Dk%28u%29%28%5Cchi_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7DR_%7Bvu%5E%7B-1%7D%7D+F%29%28x%29du%3Dk%5Cstar+%5Cchi%28R+F%29%28x%29)

因为根据 ![N_p(k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N_p%28k%29) 的定义，上面的结果可以估计为

![\begin{align*} &c^p \frac{1}{|V|}\int_{X} N_p(k)^p\int_G |\chi_w (R_w F)(x)|^p dw d\mu(x) \\ = & c^p \frac{ N_p(k)^p}{|V|} \int_G\chi_w  d w\int_X|(R_w F)(x)|^p d\mu(x) \\ \leq &     c^p \frac{ N_p(k)^p}{|V|} \int_G\chi_w  d w  \times c^p\|F\|_p^p \\ =&\frac{c^{2p N_p(k)^p\|F\|^p_p}}{|V|}|VC^{-1}|\\ \leq& c^{2p} N_p(k)^p\|F\|^p_p(1+\epsilon),  \end{align*}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26c%5Ep+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D%5Cint_%7BX%7D+N_p%28k%29%5Ep%5Cint_G+%7C%5Cchi_w+%28R_w+F%29%28x%29%7C%5Ep+dw+d%5Cmu%28x%29+%5C%5C+%3D+%26+c%5Ep+%5Cfrac%7B+N_p%28k%29%5Ep%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D+%5Cint_G%5Cchi_w++d+w%5Cint_X%7C%28R_w+F%29%28x%29%7C%5Ep+d%5Cmu%28x%29+%5C%5C+%5Cleq+%26+++++c%5Ep+%5Cfrac%7B+N_p%28k%29%5Ep%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D+%5Cint_G%5Cchi_w++d+w++%5Ctimes+c%5Ep%5C%7CF%5C%7C_p%5Ep+%5C%5C+%3D%26%5Cfrac%7Bc%5E%7B2p+N_p%28k%29%5Ep%5C%7CF%5C%7C%5Ep_p%7D%7D%7B%7CV%7C%7D%7CVC%5E%7B-1%7D%7C%5C%5C+%5Cleq%26+c%5E%7B2p%7D+N_p%28k%29%5Ep%5C%7CF%5C%7C%5Ep_p%281%2B%5Cepsilon%29%2C++%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D)

这里我们利用了Fubini定理，换句话说我们假设了 ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是 一个可加有限的测度空间。

我们最终得到了

![\int_{X}|(H_k F)(x)|^p d\mu(x)\leq c^{2p} N_p(k)^p\|F\|^p_p(1+\epsilon).](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7BX%7D%7C%28H_k+F%29%28x%29%7C%5Ep+d%5Cmu%28x%29%5Cleq+c%5E%7B2p%7D+N_p%28k%29%5Ep%5C%7CF%5C%7C%5Ep_p%281%2B%5Cepsilon%29.)

因为 ![\epsilon>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cepsilon%3E0) 是任意的，我们得到了最终结果。 

## **三、一些改进**

在上述证明中，我们需要几个条件：

第一， ![k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k) 有一个紧支集；第二， ![X](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X) 是 ![\sigma-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma-) finite；第三， ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 是可均的。

第一个条件本质上不是必须的，我们可以这样绕过去：对于任意 ![k\in L^1(G)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cin+L%5E1%28G%29) , 存在 ![\{k_n\}\subset L^1(G)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Bk_n%5C%7D%5Csubset+L%5E1%28G%29) 使得每个 ![k_n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_n) 都有紧支集并且 ![\|k_n-k\|_{L^1(G)}\to 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7Ck_n-k%5C%7C_%7BL%5E1%28G%29%7D%5Cto+0) , 由此可得

![\|H_k-H_{k_n}\|\lesssim \|k_n-k\|\to 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_k-H_%7Bk_n%7D%5C%7C%5Clesssim+%5C%7Ck_n-k%5C%7C%5Cto+0) ， ![|N_p(k)-N_p(k_n)|\leq N_p(k-k_n)\to 0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7CN_p%28k%29-N_p%28k_n%29%7C%5Cleq+N_p%28k-k_n%29%5Cto+0)

 在根据主要结果

![\|H_{k_n}\|\leq c^2 N_{p}(k_n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_%7Bk_n%7D%5C%7C%5Cleq+c%5E2+N_%7Bp%7D%28k_n%29) ，

我们得到了 ![\|H_{k}\|\leq c^2 N_{p}(k)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_%7Bk%7D%5C%7C%5Cleq+c%5E2+N_%7Bp%7D%28k%29) 。

第二个条件也不是本质的， 对于任意可测空间 ![(X,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28X%2C%5Cmu%29) 和 ![f\in L^p(X,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L%5Ep%28X%2C%5Cmu%29) , 我们可以找到一个 ![\sigma-](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma-) finite的子可测集合 ![X_0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_0) 使得 ![f\in L^p(X_0,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L%5Ep%28X_0%2C%5Cmu%29) , 并且 ![H_k f,R_vH_k f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_k+f%2CR_vH_k+f) 也是 ![ L^p(X_0,\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+L%5Ep%28X_0%2C%5Cmu%29) 中的元素。

第三个条件很可惜，这个条件才是真正的限制条件，不过，这个条件在实际的应用过程中是足够的了。实际上用到的群基本满足这个条件。
