# 调和分析中的迁移法 （1） - 知乎
# 

我打算用系列文章介绍一下调和分析中的一种方法：迁移法（Transference method）。缘起是在E.M. Stein去世那天我回头看了一本他写的书：

Topics in Harmonic analysis related to the Littlewood-Paley Theory

这本书介绍了Littlewood-Paley理论在李群上的推广，而且Stein在这本书中采用的方法和前人是不同的，使得大家可以窥见一般理论的核心，其中的一个技巧就是迁移法，虽然他本人没有这样称呼它。此后这个方法由Coifman和Weiss发展，这个方法在一般的LCA群（局部紧的交换群，比如 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) ），紧群（旋转群 ![SO(n)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=SO%28n%29) ）, 非紧半单李群（ ![SL(2,\mathbb{C})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=SL%282%2C%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D%29) ）都有些应用。

当然了，这次一系列的文章的主要目标是古典（LCA）群 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) （ ![\mathbb{R}^n,\mathbb{Z},\mathbb{T}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%2C%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D%2C%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%5En) ）上的卷积形算子和一般可测函数空间的算子之间联系。假设 ![T_{u}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_%7Bu%7D) 是群 ![G](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G) 在空间 ![L^p(\mu)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep%28%5Cmu%29) 上的（强）连续群表示（下次介绍具体定义），我们的目标是研究算子

![H_kf:=\int_{G}k(u)T_{-u}f du](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_kf%3A%3D%5Cint_%7BG%7Dk%28u%29T_%7B-u%7Df+du) ,

和卷积算子

![(Kf)(x)=\int_{G}k(x-u)f(u)du](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Kf%29%28x%29%3D%5Cint_%7BG%7Dk%28x-u%29f%28u%29du) ,

的联系。这里的 ![du](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=du) 表示群G上的Haar meausre，特别的，我们希望通过后者的范数去估计前者的范数。

我们首先介绍一下希尔伯特变换和如何通过「迁移」它去估计一般的「奇异积分」。 

## 一、 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的希尔伯特变换

我们谈论 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上的希尔伯特变换，对于任意 ![f\in L^1(\mathbb{R})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L%5E1%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%29) ,  我们可以找到一个定义在上半平面 ![\mathbb{R}^2_+=\{(x,y); y>0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E2_%2B%3D%5C%7B%28x%2Cy%29%3B+y%3E0%5C%7D) 的调和函数 ![u](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u) 使得它刚好满足边界条件 ![u(x,0)=f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%28x%2C0%29%3Df%28x%29) ，也就是说

![\Delta u=0 \quad\text{in}\quad \mathbb{R}^2_+ \\ u(x,0)=f(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+u%3D0+%5Cquad%5Ctext%7Bin%7D%5Cquad+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E2_%2B+%5C%5C+u%28x%2C0%29%3Df%28x%29)

 这个解本身可以通过一个卷积的方式得到，也就是假设

![P_y(x)=\frac{y}{\pi(x^2+y^2)}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_y%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7By%7D%7B%5Cpi%28x%5E2%2By%5E2%29%7D)     ( ![x\in\mathbb{R},\,\,y>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2C%5C%2C%5C%2Cy%3E0) )

那么上述方程的解就是

![u(x,y)=(P_y\star f)(x)=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{\mathbb{R}}\frac{y}{(x-t)^2+y^2}f(t)dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%28x%2Cy%29%3D%28P_y%5Cstar+f%29%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D%5Cfrac%7By%7D%7B%28x-t%29%5E2%2By%5E2%7Df%28t%29dt)

我们可以证明 ![\lim_{y\to 0}u(x,y)=f(x) \,\, a.e.](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7By%5Cto+0%7Du%28x%2Cy%29%3Df%28x%29+%5C%2C%5C%2C+a.e.) 。需要知道的是，对于一个(实)调和函数 ![u](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u) ，根据复分析的知识，我们知道你总可以找到另一个实调和函数 ![v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v) 使得 ![u+iv](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%2Biv) 刚好是一个复解析函数，这个函数 ![ v](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+v) 一般叫做 ![u](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u) 的共轭调和函数（有些书上叫conjugated function）。当然了，这不只是限定于实值调和函数，对于复的也成立。只是对于实的情况，这个问题特别好理解。

有意思的是，对于调和函数 ![u(x,y)=(P_y\star f)(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=u%28x%2Cy%29%3D%28P_y%5Cstar+f%29%28x%29) ，它的共轭（调和）函数的表达式也可以写出来，那就是设

![Q_y(x)=\frac{1}{\pi}\frac{x}{x^2+y^2}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Q_y%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cfrac%7Bx%7D%7Bx%5E2%2By%5E2%7D) ,

那么 ![v(x,y)=(Q_y\star f)(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=v%28x%2Cy%29%3D%28Q_y%5Cstar+f%29%28x%29)

如果我们设 ![z=x+iy](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=z%3Dx%2Biy) ,那么

![P_y(x)+iQ_y(x)=\frac{i}{\pi z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_y%28x%29%2BiQ_y%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7B%5Cpi+z%7D)

我们知道 ![P_y\star f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_y%5Cstar+f) 的极限刚好是 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ,那么 ![Q_y\star f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Q_y%5Cstar+f) 的极限是什么？我们把这个极限定义为希尔伯特变换，也就是说

![Hf=\lim_{y\to 0}(Q_y\star f)(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Hf%3D%5Clim_%7By%5Cto+0%7D%28Q_y%5Cstar+f%29%28x%29) ,

通过计算，我们可以形式地写成

![(Hf)(x)=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{\mathbb{R}}\frac{f(t)}{x-t}dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Hf%29%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28t%29%7D%7Bx-t%7Ddt) ,

之所以说是「形式」，是因为对于一般的函数 ![f\in L^1(\mathbb{R})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L%5E1%28%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%29) ,这个积分不一定存在。 这是奇异积分，对于奇异积分，我们实质在算的是「主部」 ，确切的说，就是下面这个极限

![(Hf)(x)=\lim_{\epsilon\to 0}\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{|x-t|> \epsilon}\frac{f(t)}{x-t}dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28Hf%29%28x%29%3D%5Clim_%7B%5Cepsilon%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B%7Cx-t%7C%3E+%5Cepsilon%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28t%29%7D%7Bx-t%7Ddt)

**有些书上直接拿这个表达式作为希尔伯特变换的定义**，但是这种定义你完全看不出它的「起源」。我这里特意讲一讲希尔伯特变换的起源，帮助大家理解它到底是怎么来的。

对于几乎处处的 ![x](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x) ,这个极限是存在的。 而且

(i) 对于任意 ![ f\in L^p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+f%5Cin+L%5Ep) ( ![1<p<+\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%3Cp%3C%2B%5Cinfty) ), ![ Hf\in L^p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+Hf%5Cin+L%5Ep) ,并且 ![\|Hf\|_p\leq A_p \|f\|_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CHf%5C%7C_p%5Cleq+A_p+%5C%7Cf%5C%7C_p)

(ii) 对于 ![f\in L^1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L%5E1) , 如果我们设 ![(H^*f)=\sup_{\epsilon>0}|\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{|x-t|> \epsilon}\frac{f(t)}{x-t}dt|](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H%5E%2Af%29%3D%5Csup_%7B%5Cepsilon%3E0%7D%7C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B%7Cx-t%7C%3E+%5Cepsilon%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28t%29%7D%7Bx-t%7Ddt%7C) , 那么 ![H^* f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%2A+f) 是 ![(1,1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%2C1%29)-函数，并且 ![\|H^* f\|_{1,1}\leq A_1\|f\|_1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH%5E%2A+f%5C%7C_%7B1%2C1%7D%5Cleq+A_1%5C%7Cf%5C%7C_1) .

(iii) 对于 ![f\in L^2,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cin+L%5E2%2C)![Hf\in L^2](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Hf%5Cin+L%5E2) 并且它的傅立叶变换刚好满足

![\widehat{Hf}(\xi)=-\text{sgan}(\xi)i\hat{f}(\xi)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidehat%7BHf%7D%28%5Cxi%29%3D-%5Ctext%7Bsgan%7D%28%5Cxi%29i%5Chat%7Bf%7D%28%5Cxi%29)

值得一提的是，在环上也可以定义希尔伯特变换 ![H_{\mathbb{T}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%7D) ，它的样子是

![(H_{\mathbb{T}}f)(\theta)=\lim_{\epsilon\to 0}\int_{|\theta-t|>\epsilon}\cot(\pi(\theta-t))f(t)dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%7Df%29%28%5Ctheta%29%3D%5Clim_%7B%5Cepsilon%5Cto+0%7D%5Cint_%7B%7C%5Ctheta-t%7C%3E%5Cepsilon%7D%5Ccot%28%5Cpi%28%5Ctheta-t%29%29f%28t%29dt) .

很多教材上还采用截断希尔伯特变换，利用它来研究希尔伯特变换，所谓截断希尔伯特变换就是

![(H_\epsilon f)(x)=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{\frac{1}{\epsilon}>|x-t|> \epsilon}\frac{f(t)}{x-t}dt](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H_%5Cepsilon+f%29%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cepsilon%7D%3E%7Cx-t%7C%3E+%5Cepsilon%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28t%29%7D%7Bx-t%7Ddt) ,

截断希尔伯特变换依然是 ![L^p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep) 到 ![L^p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep) 的有界算子，也就是

![\|H_\epsilon f\|_p\leq A_p\|f\|_p\quad (1<p<+\infty)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_%5Cepsilon+f%5C%7C_p%5Cleq+A_p%5C%7Cf%5C%7C_p%5Cquad+%281%3Cp%3C%2B%5Cinfty%29)

## 二、 ![\mathbb{T}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D) 上的希尔伯特变换.

我们在这一小节通过迁移实数轴上的希尔伯特变换来估计环上的希尔伯特变换（下面的推导过程是比较快的，严格性需要大家自己补）。我们这里把环看成 ![\mathbb{R}/\mathbb{Z}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2F%5Cmathbb%7BZ%7D) , 也就是模1的实数。那么一个 ![\mathbb{T}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D) 上的函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 等价于 ![\mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 上周期为1的函数。

我们知道一个著名的公式

![\pi\cot(\pi \theta)=\lim_{N\to+\infty}\sum_{n=-N}^{n=N} \frac{1}{\theta+n} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi%5Ccot%28%5Cpi+%5Ctheta%29%3D%5Clim_%7BN%5Cto%2B%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D-N%7D%5E%7Bn%3DN%7D+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Ctheta%2Bn%7D+) ,

对于周期函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) ,这里简变起见，不妨认 ![f\mid_{\mathbb{T}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5Cmid_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%7D) 在 ![(0,1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%280%2C1%29) 内有紧支集。然后我们发现

![(H_{1/N}f)(x)=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{1/N<|t|<N}\frac{f(x-t)}{t}dt=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{1/N}^{1-1/N} f(x-\theta)\sum_{n=-N}^N \frac{1}{\theta+n} d\theta+ \epsilon_N(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H_%7B1%2FN%7Df%29%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B1%2FN%3C%7Ct%7C%3CN%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x-t%29%7D%7Bt%7Ddt%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B1%2FN%7D%5E%7B1-1%2FN%7D+f%28x-%5Ctheta%29%5Csum_%7Bn%3D-N%7D%5EN+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Ctheta%2Bn%7D+d%5Ctheta%2B+%5Cepsilon_N%28x%29) ,

其中当 ![N\to\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N%5Cto%5Cinfty) 时， ![\epsilon_N(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cepsilon_N%28x%29) 一致收敛到0。 因此，我们有

![\lim_{N\to\infty}\{ (H_{1/N}f)(x)-(H_{\mathbb{T}}f)(x)\}=0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7BN%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5C%7B+%28H_%7B1%2FN%7Df%29%28x%29-%28H_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%7Df%29%28x%29%5C%7D%3D0) ，

所以，我们只需要考虑 ![(H_{1/N}f)(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H_%7B1%2FN%7Df%29%28x%29) 的 ![L^p(\mathbb{T})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5Ep%28%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%29) 上的估计， 对于这个问题，我们根据 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 的平移不变性可知我们可以随意移动任意的 ![s](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=s) ,

![\int_0^1|(H_{1/N}f)(x)|^pdx=\int_0^1\left|\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{1/N<|t|<N}\frac{f(x+s-t)}{t}dt\right|^p dx=\int_0^1\int_{-M}^M\frac{1}{2M}ds\left|\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{1/N<|t|<N}\frac{f(x+s-t)}{t}dt\right|^p dx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5E1%7C%28H_%7B1%2FN%7Df%29%28x%29%7C%5Epdx%3D%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B1%2FN%3C%7Ct%7C%3CN%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%2Bs-t%29%7D%7Bt%7Ddt%5Cright%7C%5Ep+dx%3D%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cint_%7B-M%7D%5EM%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2M%7Dds%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B1%2FN%3C%7Ct%7C%3CN%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%2Bs-t%29%7D%7Bt%7Ddt%5Cright%7C%5Ep+dx)

设 ![\chi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cchi) 是 ![[-M-N,M+N]](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B-M-N%2CM%2BN%5D) 上的示性函数，那么上述积分就变成了

![\int_0^1|(H_{1/N}f)(x)|^pdx=\frac{1}{2M}\int_0^1\int_{-M}^M\left|\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{1/N<|t|<N}\frac{\chi(s-t)f(x+s-t)}{t}dt\right|^p ds dx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5E1%7C%28H_%7B1%2FN%7Df%29%28x%29%7C%5Epdx%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2M%7D%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cint_%7B-M%7D%5EM%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D%5Cint_%7B1%2FN%3C%7Ct%7C%3CN%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cchi%28s-t%29f%28x%2Bs-t%29%7D%7Bt%7Ddt%5Cright%7C%5Ep+ds+dx) .

利用一下下面的结果

![\|H_{1/N}f\|_p\leq A_p\|f\|_p\quad (1<p<+\infty)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_%7B1%2FN%7Df%5C%7C_p%5Cleq+A_p%5C%7Cf%5C%7C_p%5Cquad+%281%3Cp%3C%2B%5Cinfty%29) ,

也就是取 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 为 ![f(x+\cdot)\chi(\cdot)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%2B%5Ccdot%29%5Cchi%28%5Ccdot%29) ,并且将 ![\int_{-M}^M](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B-M%7D%5EM) 放大到 ![\int_{\mathbb{R}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D) ，我们得到

![\int_0^1|(H_{1/N}f)(x)|^pdx\leq \frac{A_p^p}{2M}\int_0^1\int_{\mathbb{R}}\left| \chi(t)f(x+t)\right|^p  dtdx](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5E1%7C%28H_%7B1%2FN%7Df%29%28x%29%7C%5Epdx%5Cleq+%5Cfrac%7BA_p%5Ep%7D%7B2M%7D%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D%5Cleft%7C+%5Cchi%28t%29f%28x%2Bt%29%5Cright%7C%5Ep++dtdx)

![\int_0^1\int_{\mathbb{R}}\left| \chi(t)f(x+t)\right|^p  dtdx=\int_0^1|\chi(t)|^p\int_{\mathbb{R}} dt\left| f(x+t)\right|^p  dx=2(M+N)\|f\|_{L^p(\mathbb{T})}^p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D%5Cleft%7C+%5Cchi%28t%29f%28x%2Bt%29%5Cright%7C%5Ep++dtdx%3D%5Cint_0%5E1%7C%5Cchi%28t%29%7C%5Ep%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D+dt%5Cleft%7C+f%28x%2Bt%29%5Cright%7C%5Ep++dx%3D2%28M%2BN%29%5C%7Cf%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%28%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%29%7D%5Ep) ,

因此，

![\int_0^1|(H_{1/N}f)(x)|^pdx\leq A_p^p\frac{M+N}{M}\|f\|_{L^p(\mathbb{T})}^p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_0%5E1%7C%28H_%7B1%2FN%7Df%29%28x%29%7C%5Epdx%5Cleq+A_p%5Ep%5Cfrac%7BM%2BN%7D%7BM%7D%5C%7Cf%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%28%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%29%7D%5Ep)

让 ![M\to\infty](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%5Cto%5Cinfty) ,我们就得到了

![\|H_{1/N}f\|_{L^p(\mathbb{T})}\leq A_p\|f\|_{L^p(\mathbb{T})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_%7B1%2FN%7Df%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%28%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%29%7D%5Cleq+A_p%5C%7Cf%5C%7C_%7BL%5Ep%28%5Cmathbb%7BT%7D%29%7D)

而这个就是我们想要的结果。

## 三、奇对称的奇异积分

在上面的例子中，我们通过迁移实数轴上的希尔伯特变换来估计环上的希尔伯特变换。现在我们考虑跟一般的奇异积分算子：

![(H_kf)(x):=\int_{\mathbb{R}^n}k(x-y) f(y)dy](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H_kf%29%28x%29%3A%3D%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%7Dk%28x-y%29+f%28y%29dy) ,

其中

![k(u)=\frac{\Omega(y)}{|y|^n}\quad y\in \mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%28u%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5COmega%28y%29%7D%7B%7Cy%7C%5En%7D%5Cquad+y%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) ,

我们假设 ![\Omega(y):\mathbb{R}^n\backslash \{0\}\to \mathbb{R}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%28y%29%3A%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%5Cbackslash+%5C%7B0%5C%7D%5Cto+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D) 是奇对称的0阶齐次函数：也就是说，对于任意 ![t>0](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3E0) 和 ![y\in \mathbb{R}^n\backslash \{0\}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%5Cbackslash+%5C%7B0%5C%7D)

![\Omega(-y)=-\Omega(y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%28-y%29%3D-%5COmega%28y%29) 和 ![\Omega(ty)=\Omega(y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%28ty%29%3D%5COmega%28y%29) 成立。我们还假设 ![\Omega](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega) 限制到 ![\mathbb{S}^{n-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D) 上是可积的。

举个例子，给定 ![1\leq i\leq n,](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Cleq+i%5Cleq+n%2C) 我们可以定义

![\Omega(y)=\frac{y_i}{|y|}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5COmega%28y%29%3D%5Cfrac%7By_i%7D%7B%7Cy%7C%7D)

这个时候 ![H_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_k) 叫Riezs积分/变换(一般需要乘以一个常数 ![c_n=\frac{\Gamma(\frac{n+1}{2})}{\pi^{\frac{n+1}{2}}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_n%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5CGamma%28%5Cfrac%7Bn%2B1%7D%7B2%7D%29%7D%7B%5Cpi%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7Bn%2B1%7D%7B2%7D%7D%7D) )。这个奇异积分在偏微分方程的Lp估计中是特别有用的。奇异积分算子 ![H_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H_k) 和下面的截断奇异积分算子

![(H^\epsilon_kf)(x)=\int_{\epsilon<|y|<\frac{1}{\epsilon}}k(y) f(x-y)dy](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H%5E%5Cepsilon_kf%29%28x%29%3D%5Cint_%7B%5Cepsilon%3C%7Cy%7C%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cepsilon%7D%7Dk%28y%29+f%28x-y%29dy)

是息息相关的，

![(H_kf)(x)=\lim_{\epsilon\to 0}(H^\epsilon_k f)(x)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28H_kf%29%28x%29%3D%5Clim_%7B%5Cepsilon%5Cto+0%7D%28H%5E%5Cepsilon_k+f%29%28x%29) .

我们下面证明这个积分算子 ![H^\epsilon_k](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5E%5Cepsilon_k) 可以表示成截断希尔伯特变换的「平均」。我们如下定义单参数连续群 ![\{U_{y'}^t\}_{t\in\mathbb{R}}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7BU_%7By%27%7D%5Et%5C%7D_%7Bt%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D)

![U^t_{y'}x=x+y't \quad t\in \mathbb{R}, y'\in \mathbb{S}^{n-1}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%5Et_%7By%27%7Dx%3Dx%2By%27t+%5Cquad+t%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%2C+y%27%5Cin+%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D) ,

设函数 ![f](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f) 定义在 ![\mathbb{R}^n](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En) ,我们定义双参数的定义在实轴上的函数 ![g^x_{y'}(t)=f(U_{y'}^tx)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%5Ex_%7By%27%7D%28t%29%3Df%28U_%7By%27%7D%5Etx%29) ，如果我们发现

![2\int_{\epsilon<|y|<\frac{1}{\epsilon}}k(y) f(x-y)dy=2\int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}}\Omega(y')\{\int_{\epsilon}^{1/\epsilon}\frac{f(x-ry')}{r^n}r^{n-1}dr\} dy'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Cint_%7B%5Cepsilon%3C%7Cy%7C%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cepsilon%7D%7Dk%28y%29+f%28x-y%29dy%3D2%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D%5COmega%28y%27%29%5C%7B%5Cint_%7B%5Cepsilon%7D%5E%7B1%2F%5Cepsilon%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x-ry%27%29%7D%7Br%5En%7Dr%5E%7Bn-1%7Ddr%5C%7D+dy%27)

根据奇对称性，我们不能发现上面左边等于

![\pi\int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}}\Omega(y') (H_\epsilon g^x_{y'})(0) dy'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D%5COmega%28y%27%29+%28H_%5Cepsilon+g%5Ex_%7By%27%7D%29%280%29+dy%27) , 

所以我们只需要估计这个式子就可以了，暂时忽略 ![\pi](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cpi) ,我们利用Minkowski积分不等式可发现

![(\int_{\mathbb{R}^n}|\int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}}\Omega(y') (H_\epsilon g^x_{y'})(0) dy'|^p dx)^{1/p} \leq  \int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}} ( \int_{\mathbb{R}^n}|\Omega(y') (H_\epsilon g^x_{y'})(0) |^p  dx)^{1/p} dy'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%7D%7C%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D%5COmega%28y%27%29+%28H_%5Cepsilon+g%5Ex_%7By%27%7D%29%280%29+dy%27%7C%5Ep+dx%29%5E%7B1%2Fp%7D+%5Cleq++%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D+%28+%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%7D%7C%5COmega%28y%27%29+%28H_%5Cepsilon+g%5Ex_%7By%27%7D%29%280%29+%7C%5Ep++dx%29%5E%7B1%2Fp%7D+dy%27)

设 ![\mathbb{R}^{n-1}_{y'}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D_%7By%27%7D) 是和向量 ![y'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%27) 垂直的平面，我们利用一个技巧性的积分变换可得

![\int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}}  \int_{\mathbb{R}^n}|\Omega(y') (H_\epsilon g^x_{y'})(0) |^p  dx dy'\leq   \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}} |\Omega(y')| \left( \int_{\mathbb{R}_{y'}^{n-1}}\int_{\mathbb{R}}|\int_{\epsilon<|t|<\frac{1}{\epsilon}}\frac{f((s-t)y'+w)}{t} dt |^p  dsdw \right)^{1/p}dy'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D++%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%7D%7C%5COmega%28y%27%29+%28H_%5Cepsilon+g%5Ex_%7By%27%7D%29%280%29+%7C%5Ep++dx+dy%27%5Cleq+++%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cpi%7D+%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D+%7C%5COmega%28y%27%29%7C+%5Cleft%28+%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D_%7By%27%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D%7C%5Cint_%7B%5Cepsilon%3C%7Ct%7C%3C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cepsilon%7D%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28%28s-t%29y%27%2Bw%29%7D%7Bt%7D+dt+%7C%5Ep++dsdw+%5Cright%29%5E%7B1%2Fp%7Ddy%27)

然后利用一下截断希尔伯特变换的估计，我们得到

![(\int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}}  \int_{\mathbb{R}^n}|\Omega(y') (H_\epsilon g^x_{y'})(0) |^p  dx dy')^{1/p}\leq    \int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}} |\Omega(y')|  A_p\left(\int_{\mathbb{R}_{y'}^{n-1}}\int_{\mathbb{R}}|f(sy'+w)|^p  dsdw\right)^{1/p} dy'](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D++%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5En%7D%7C%5COmega%28y%27%29+%28H_%5Cepsilon+g%5Ex_%7By%27%7D%29%280%29+%7C%5Ep++dx+dy%27%29%5E%7B1%2Fp%7D%5Cleq++++%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D+%7C%5COmega%28y%27%29%7C++A_p%5Cleft%28%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D_%7By%27%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%7D%7Cf%28sy%27%2Bw%29%7C%5Ep++dsdw%5Cright%29%5E%7B1%2Fp%7D+dy%27)

总结上述的结果我们得到了

![2\|H_k^\epsilon f\|_p\leq \pi A_p\int_{\mathbb{S}^{n-1}} |\Omega(y')|  dy'( \int_{\mathbb{R}^{n}}|f(x)|^p dx)^{\frac{1}{p}}  ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5C%7CH_k%5E%5Cepsilon+f%5C%7C_p%5Cleq+%5Cpi+A_p%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D+%7C%5COmega%28y%27%29%7C++dy%27%28+%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%7Cf%28x%29%7C%5Ep+dx%29%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bp%7D%7D++) ,

这证明了

![\|H_k^\epsilon f\|_p\leq B_p \|f\|_p](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%7CH_k%5E%5Cepsilon+f%5C%7C_p%5Cleq+B_p+%5C%7Cf%5C%7C_p) ,

其中 ![B_p=\frac{A_p\pi}{2}\|\Omega\|_{L^1(\mathbb{S}^{n-1})}](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_p%3D%5Cfrac%7BA_p%5Cpi%7D%7B2%7D%5C%7C%5COmega%5C%7C_%7BL%5E1%28%5Cmathbb%7BS%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%29%7D) . 这里采用的技巧归功于Calderon和Zymund。
