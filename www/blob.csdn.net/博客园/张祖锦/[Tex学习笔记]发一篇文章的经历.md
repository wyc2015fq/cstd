# [[Tex学习笔记]发一篇文章的经历](https://www.cnblogs.com/zhangzujin/p/3750225.html)





打算在

[INTERNATIONAL JOURNAL OF](http://www.m-hikari.com/ijcms.html)
[CONTEMPORARY MATHEMATICAL SCIENCES](http://www.m-hikari.com/ijcms.html)

发一篇文章, 所以就直接在 [作者指引](http://www.m-hikari.com/guide2.html)中下载 tex 模版, 写好后发邮件到 [ijcms@m-hikari.com](mailto:ijcms@m-hikari.com), 内容如下:




主题: Submitting An Article to IJCMS+文章的英文题目



内容: 

Dear Edtior, 

    The authors would like to submit an article to IJCMS for possible publication. We are looking forward to your correspondence. Thank you very much. With best wishes.



附件: 你的文章



这样就等编辑回复了. 一般会给你一个稿件编号, 就等着审稿意见回来.
