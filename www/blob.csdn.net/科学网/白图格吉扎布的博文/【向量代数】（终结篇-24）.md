Combining the three ***trends***: up, even, or down, with three turns (***TT***): concave, straight, or convex, these nine states describes each of the 17 funds and market more clearly than using scalars before. Then the 17 funds can be put in order by their Trend Values and/or Second Degree Trend Values, to help investors make decisions, as we suppose that the market systems have some inertia in their neighborhood.
