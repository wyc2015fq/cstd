# 【向量代数】（终结篇14）

For system monitoring, we introduce time index: *k*,*k*-1,*k*-2

Then we define the quotients of present IV(k) over previous IV(k-1) as the System Successional Trends of the market, and each/every fund:

Trend(*i,k*)= IV(*i,k*)/IV(*i,k*-1) ,*i*=1,2,…17

The expectation of the trend values are 1:

Trend(*i,k*)=IV(*i,k*)/IV(*i,k*-1)  =1, where *i*=1,2,...17,means that IV  is no change. If 

Trend(*i,k*)=IV(*i,k*)/IV(*i,k*-1)  >1, where *i*=1,2,...17,means that the IV of given fund is increasing, but

Trend(*i,k*)=IV(*i,k*)/IV(*i,k*-1)  <1, where *i*=1,2,...17,means that the IV of given fund is decreasing.
