Working on COSINE PAGE

Create the page of cosine, name it COSINE

A1: "DATE", E1:GZ1=DATA!E1:GZ1

A2: "SGI", E2:GZ2 =DATA!E2:GZ2

GZ3=DATA!GZ3/GZ$2, Decimal 4

Copy and paste to COSINE!A3:GZ32




NEXT COSINE GRAPHICS

COSINE!$GA$3:$GZ$3,TITLE COSINE!$A$3




Then change $3 to $4, $5,… till $32,  and copy and paste to every single row, after extended the hight of the row.

Tedious, boring, but important.
