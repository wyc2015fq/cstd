WORKSHEET DESIGNING

TREND ANALYSIS PAGE

DAILY




(A3:A32): STOCK CODE 

(D3:D32): The closing prices of 30 stocks of the day before yesterday.

 (D2): SGI of the day before yesterday. 

 SGI=SQRT(SUMSQ(DAILY!D3:D32))




(E3:E32): The closing prices of 30 stocks of yesterday.

 (E2): SGI of yesterday. 




(F3:F32): Theclosing prices of 30 stocks of today.

 (F2): SGI of today.




(G2): SGI%, =E2/D2, increasing rate of SGI

(G3:G32): Trendvalues of the 30 stocks of yesterday. The expectation is one, greater than one,means increasing, less than one means decreasing.




(H2): SGI%, =F2/E2, the increasing rate of SGI.

(H3:H32): Trend values of the 30 stocks of today. Stock% adjusted by SGI%. The expectation is one, greater than one, means increasing, less than one means decreasing.




(I2): Trend%, increasing rate MIT2/MIT1

(I3:I32): SecondDegree Trend values of the 30 stocks. The expectation is one, greater than one, means concave, less than one means convex.




(J2): MIT2*MITproduction of Trends

(J3:J32): AcomulatedTrend values (longer term trends)of the 30 stocks. The expectation is one, greater than one, means increasing, less than one means decreasing﻿﻿.
