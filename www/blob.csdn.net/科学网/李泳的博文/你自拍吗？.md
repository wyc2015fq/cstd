已有 2211 次阅读2013-11-22 09:04|个人分类:[随想](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=279992&do=blog&classid=112648&view=me)|系统分类:[海外观察](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=279992&catid=2)







《牛津词典》日前发布了2013年最热单词是selfie (selfy)，自拍。（[http://blog.oxforddictionaries.com/2013/11/word-of-the-year-2013-winner/](http://blog.oxforddictionaries.com/2013/11/word-of-the-year-2013-winner/)）这个词儿去年六月就在牛津词典在线出现了。当然，热词儿不必是新的，但必须引领当年的潮流（However, it does need to** demonstrate some kind of prominence over the preceding year or so**）。这样看来，我已经远远out了，我每天都读点儿英文，却没见过这个词儿，也没玩儿过自拍。



其他几个候选词儿也很有意思（[http://blog.oxforddictionaries.com/2013/11/word-of-the-year-runners-up/](http://blog.oxforddictionaries.com/2013/11/word-of-the-year-runners-up/)），我们欣赏它们的漫画吧：
