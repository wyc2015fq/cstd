# 科学、宗教与哲学                           

已有 2325 次阅读2013-11-25 09:06|个人分类:[随想](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=279992&do=blog&classid=112648&view=me)|系统分类:[观点评述](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=279992&catid=3)







歌德在1813年1月6日给**Fritz Lacobi**的信中，有趣地比较了科学、艺术和道德与宗教（神学）的瓜葛：

**When we do natural science, we are pantheists; when we do poetry, we are polytheists; when we moralize, we are monotheists.**



这段话更完整的版本是

![](http://image.sciencenet.cn/album/201311/25/090400knxdhxm13x979wwd.jpg)



歌老的话令我想起爱老的话——他在Schilpp为他编的70岁纪念文集（***Albert Einstein: Philosopher-Scientist***, Tudor Publishing Company, 1949）的后面，写了篇“对批评的答复”（**Remarks to the Essays Appearing in this Collective Volume**），警告科学家不要过分依赖认识论——从系统的认识论来看，科学家不过是肆无忌惮的机会主义者（He [the scientist] must appear to the systematic epistemologist as a type of** unscrupulous opportunist**）**:**



lhe appears as ***realist ***in so far as he seeks to describe a world independent of the acts of perception; 

lan ***idealist ***in so far as he looks upon the concepts and theories as the free inventions of the human spirit (not logically derivable from what is empirically given); 

las ***positivist ***in so far as he considers his concepts and theories justified *only *to the extent to which they furnish a logical representation of relations among sensory experiences. 

lHe may even appear as a ***Platonist *or *Pythagorean ***in so far as he considers the viewpoint of logical simplicity as an indispensable and effective tool of his research.



两段话很好说明了科学和艺术与宗教与哲学的三元纠结，也因为这三原色，科学才那么绚烂。如果硬把三元分离，做一个“彻底的唯物主义的科学”，那科学就像雾霾的天空，沉闷而死寂了。
