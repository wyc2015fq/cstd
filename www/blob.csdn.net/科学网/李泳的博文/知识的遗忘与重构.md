# 知识的遗忘与重构                           

已有 2254 次阅读2015-11-5 09:02|个人分类:[物理](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=279992&do=blog&classid=113414&view=me)|系统分类:[科普集锦](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=279992&catid=7)








张三丰向无忌演示太极拳，然后问他记住多少，无忌说都忘了（小说里没有这句话，电视剧里加的）。这里的忘，是得意忘言得鱼忘筌的忘，学精神而不学形式。费曼老师讲物理，也常喜欢说忘，忘了怎么办呢？自己找回来：

you can re-create thethings that you’ve forgotten *perpetually*-- if you don't forget too much, and if you know enough. In other words, there comesa time -- which you haven't quite got to, yet -- where you’ll know so manythings that as you forget them,you can reconstruct themself from the pieces that you can still remember. It is therefore of first-rateimportance that you know how to "***triangulate***" -- that is, toknow how to figure something out from what you already know. *It is absolutely necessary*. 

当然，费老也说了，别忘太多（don't forget too much），要能从记得的碎片还原（或重构）忘记的东西（you canreconstruct them from the pieces that you can still remember）——这儿有一个“度”的问题。**Triangulate**是费老对重构的形象化说法（如图），通过逻辑寻找失去或遗忘的关系——重要的是，有时还能发现新的东西。




![](http://image.sciencenet.cn/album/201511/05/090207syo1x3k8fio18fof.jpg)

左图是原先完整的知识，右图虚线部分是遗忘的，通过三角形重构关联。

![](http://image.sciencenet.cn/album/201511/05/090217z7c373chez42j2ah.jpg)

发现原来没有的关系


那么，需要多少记忆碎片才好还原呢？显然没有好的答案。费老总是尽可能少地用别人做的东西来做自己的事情。他的口述历史说


I try always to work **with****the least knowledge possible**, with little knowledge of what other people are doing, because I feel** more happy being more individual**, if I am not following the line and getting confused by what they say. 

费老把自然的最小作用量原理用于自然律的创造，这是很有趣的，其实也是很“自然”的——欧几里得几何不就是那么建造起来的吗。
