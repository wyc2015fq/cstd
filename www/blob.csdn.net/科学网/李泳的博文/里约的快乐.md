# 里约的快乐                           

已有 1806 次阅读2016-8-7 01:30|个人分类:[随想](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=279992&do=blog&classid=112648&view=me)|系统分类:[海外观察](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=279992&catid=2)








@  北京奥运会开幕式流行的词儿是spectacular，罗格说spectacularand unforgettable and moving，布莱尔说“the spectacular to end all spectaculars and probably cannever be bettered.”里约开幕式也有个词儿，是导演Daniela Thomas说的，MacGyveringenuity，像戏里的MacGyver那样，一把瑞士军刀打天下，用寻常的小东西解决大问题（he solves complex problems by making things out of ordinary objects）。Maracanã的场景能令人感到真正的“众乐乐”。因为政治气候不大好，它正好远离政治；从北京到伦敦到索契到里约，OG终于回归Amazon的自然了。



![](http://image.sciencenet.cn/album/201608/07/013819mpcwcwml0po6jv7j.png)




@  美国旗手Michael Phelps飞鱼说，他从悉尼到雅典到北京到伦敦，境界不同了：去悉尼重在参与，去雅典想赢金牌；去北京想超越别人；去伦敦想创造历史。（ForSydney, I just wanted to make the team. For Athens, I wanted to win gold for mycountry. For Beijing, I wanted to do something nobody else had done. In London,I wanted to make history.）
