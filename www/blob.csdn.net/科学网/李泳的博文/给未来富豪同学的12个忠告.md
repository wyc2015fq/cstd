# 给未来富豪同学的12个忠告                           

已有 2734 次阅读2013-2-5 09:43|个人分类:[随想](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=279992&do=blog&classid=112648&view=me)|系统分类:[人文社科](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=279992&catid=10)|关键词:富豪,未来,style|[未来](misc.php?mod=tag&id=1953), [style](misc.php?mod=tag&id=2822), [富豪](misc.php?mod=tag&id=24489)






浏览了一篇短文“**12 pieces of advice to graduates billionaires**”，我读了没什么特别的感觉，因为那些话都不新鲜了。也许想成为bill的同学能与他们产生共鸣——



1）**If you live each day as if the last day of life **（Steve Jobs, Stanford University, 2005 ）

2) **your ability more, People expect of you more** (Bill Gate, Harvard University, 2007)

3) **All of us at one time or another have make-up **(Oprah Winfrey, Duke University, 2009)

4）**Don't ever be the smartest person in the room** (Michael Dale, the University of Texas, 2003 )

5) **Keep optimistic, don't be depressed for too long** (Michael Bloomberg, Tufts University, 2007)

6)** if you love what you do, things would be a lot easier** (Mark Zuckerberg, California -- Monroe Bella Hong Kong Park community school, 2011)

7) **Don't have passion, toughness** (Steve Ballmer, University of Southern California, 2011)

8) **Do not deliberately plan** (Eric Schmidt, Carnegie - Mellon University, 2009)

9)** Timid you** (Eli Blood, University of California at Los Angeles art and Architecture College, 2006)

10) **Bucking the trend, do the right thing** (Reid Hoffman, Information Technology College of University of California at Berkeley, 2011)

11) **independent thinking** (Karl Icahn, Searl University Business School Drake, 2008)

12) **people around you is very important** (Steve Keith)



我以为顶顶重要的是地11点：**独立的思想**，第10点与它相关，其余的可以认为是实现它的“技术路线”。因为第11点最重要，所以上面的那些话都可以不听——那第11点到底听还是不听呢？
