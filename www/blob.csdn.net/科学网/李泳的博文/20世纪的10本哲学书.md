# 20世纪的10本哲学书                           

已有 3346 次阅读2013-9-30 08:44|个人分类:[随想](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=279992&do=blog&classid=112648&view=me)|系统分类:[人文社科](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=279992&catid=10)|关键词:世纪,哲学|[哲学](misc.php?mod=tag&id=637), [世纪](misc.php?mod=tag&id=7003)





偶然看见南安普顿大学哲学教授**Ray Monk**列了20世纪10大哲学著作（也许称得上magnum opus），但作者也惭愧这个书单儿“失衡”（**unashamedly unbalanced**），因为其中4部是维特根斯坦的，2部是罗素的。Monk曾写过那两位的传记——大概偏爱作者，所以多选他们的书；也许因为先偏爱那些书，后来才为作者立传——所以他辩护说，**I make no apologies for this. In my view, Wittgenstein towers above all other 20th century philosophers to such an extent that it is surprising to find any books not written by him included in such a list.** ([http://www.theguardian.com/books/2001/nov/01/bestbooks.philosophy?guni=Article:in%20body%20link](http://www.theguardian.com/books/2001/nov/01/bestbooks.philosophy?guni=Article:in%20body%20link) )

尽管书目的“偏见”是肯定的，还是值得乜着眼来看看：



**1. *The Principles of Mathematics***** by Bertrand Russell**

**2. *Logic and Knowledge***** by Bertrand Russell**

**3.* Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus *****by Ludwig Wittgenstein**

**4. *Philosophical Investigations*****by Ludwig Wittgenstein**

**5.* Remarks on the Foundations of Mathematics *****by Ludwig Wittgenstein**

**6. *Culture and Value***** by Ludwig Wittgenstein**

**7.* The Crisis of European Sciences and Transcendental Phenomenology*****     by Edmund Husserl**

**8. *The Bounds of Sense *****by PF Strawson**

**9. *An Autobiography *****by RG Collingwood**

**10. *Metaphysics as a Guide to Morals *****by Iris Murdoch**
