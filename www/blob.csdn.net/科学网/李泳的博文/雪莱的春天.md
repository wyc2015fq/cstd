# 雪莱的春天                           

已有 2312 次阅读2015-3-27 08:43|个人分类:[诗歌](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=279992&do=blog&classid=114085&view=me)|系统分类:[人文社科](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=279992&catid=10)






**——好玩儿的字母（S）**



我们趁着春天来玩儿Spring所在的S。在***The Poetic Wreath***中，S引领的词儿有一大块，像一片绿色的田野：



Satan; Saturn; Science; Scott; Selfishness; Sensuality; Shakespeare;  

Sheep-washing; Shepherds; Sleep; Sloth; Slaughter; Slavery; Sobriety; Solitude; 

Sophistry; Sorrow;  Sounds; Spenser; Spring; Stars; 

Story-Telling; Streams; Style; Sufferings; Sun; Supper; Swan; Swimming; Sympathy



春天的诗句很多，我们只“点水”雪莱的长诗***The Revoltof Islam***——虽然题目如此，却并不是描写真实的Islam起义，更没“预言”当今时事。题目原先叫Laon and Cythna，是一篇沉重反抗暴君的史诗，4818行。

小雪说，这首诗是他做的“民心倾向实验”——对幸福的渴望能在摇撼时代的暴风雨中生存多久？（It is an experiment on the temper of the public mind, as to how fara thirst for a happier condition of moral and political society survives, amongthe enlightened and refined, the tempests which have shaken the age in which welive.）虽然题目是“起义”，诗中却没有报复、嫉妒和偏见，它处处赞美的是爱，它才应该是统治精神世界的唯一法则。（There is no quarter given to Revenge, orEnvy, or Prejudice. **Love is celebrated everywhere as the sole law whichshould govern the moral world.**）

鲁迅《摩罗力诗说》评论说，“凡修黎（即雪莱）怀抱，多抒于此。篇中英雄莱昂，以热诚雄辩，警其国民，鼓吹自由，抨击专制，顾正义终败，而压制于以凯还，莱昂遂为正义死。是诗所函，有无量希望信仰，暨无穷之爱，穷追不舍，终以殒亡。盖莱昂者，实诗人之先觉，亦即修黎之化身也。”

看几行描写春天的诗句，扑面而来的就是爱、希望、青春和欢乐：



**O Spring, of hope,and love, and youth, and gladness**

**Wind-wingedemblem! brightest, best and fairest!**

**Whence comestthou, when, with dark Winter's sadness**

**The tears thatfade in sunny smiles thou sharest?**

**Sister of joy,thou art the child who wearest**

**Thy mother's dyingsmile, tender and sweet;**

**Thy mother Autumn,for whose grave thou bearest**

**Fresh flowers, andbeams like flowers, with gentle feet, **

**Disturbing not theleaves which are her winding-sheet.**
