# 饿不死的希腊精神                           

已有 3036 次阅读2015-7-6 08:55|个人分类:[随想](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&uid=279992&do=blog&classid=112648&view=me)|系统分类:[海外观察](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=279992&catid=2)









希腊精神还留下什么？从昨天的全民公决看，至少还留下一点“修辞术”，也就是亚里士多德前辈定义的那种“在任何情况下都能找到说服办法的能力”：the faculty of observing in any given case the available means ofpersuasion（或译作，anability, in each [particular] case, to see the available means of persuasion）。

投票上印着这样一个文绉绉（jargon-saturated）的问题：



![](http://image.sciencenet.cn/album/201507/06/0853468id768ttc1v8ci67.jpg)

英译为

Should the proposal that was submitted by the European Commission, the European Central Bank, and the International Monetary Fund at the Eurogroup of 25 June 2015, which consists of two parts that together constitute their comprehensive proposal, be accepted? The first document is titled ‘Reforms for the completion of the Current Programme and beyond’ and the second ‘Preliminary Debt Sustainability Analysis.’ 



如此问题很妙，很“温柔敦厚”，不像其他地方的公投那么燃萁煮豆。不论yes或no都还有谈判的余地。正如一个希腊艺术家说的，事情还能坏到哪儿去呢，这正是谈判的好机会（It can’t get any worse than it is now. This will give us a betterchance for negotiations.）
