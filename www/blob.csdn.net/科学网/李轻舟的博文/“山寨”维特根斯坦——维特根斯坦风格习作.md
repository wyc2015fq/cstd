# “山寨”维特根斯坦——维特根斯坦风格习作

Exercises of Wittgenstein’s style

维特根斯坦风格习作





按：《逻辑哲学论》、《哲学研究》的言说风格我已经在博文《Wittgenstein以及哲学家们的“腔调”》（[http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-217073-798010.html](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-217073-798010.html)）中探讨过了——**原来逻辑学也可以“不事体系”！**。中国人（或者东方人）对这种“格言体”（或“语录体”）言说风格应该有天然的亲近感，于是乎壮着胆子“山寨”一把（山寨维特根斯坦，也顺便山寨尼采），望方家勿怪......



![](http://image.sciencenet.cn/album/201407/13/2110570oaj2zato1adbzwz.jpg)

Ludwig Josef Johann Wittgenstein

他就喜欢这么直勾勾盯着你，一直盯得你发毛......





I

1. It is criminal trick to raise a confessional threshold.

提高忏悔的门槛是罪犯的伎俩。



2. Life is eternal free fall. I call it “dynamics of existence”.

人生就是永恒的自由落体。我称之为“存在动力学”。



2. To Chinese, the problem on linguistics is hiding things themselves by hunting for background, motivation, qualification and so on.

中国人的语言学问题是对背景、动机、资格……的追溯掩盖了事物本身。



3. Time is long enough, and history is various full. Similar is in the loop, and progress is a rainbow.

时间足够久远，历史多样饱和。相似在循环，进步是梦幻。



4. Abstract in the cloud is reality under a table.

云端的抽象是桌面下的实在。



5. God finds himself by creating, but king does by depriving. Natural rights have been deprived so long that dictator could exchange whimmy charity for thanksgiving of slaves.

神发现自己通过创造，而君王通过掠夺。天赋权利沦丧已久以致主子可以用心血来潮的施舍兑换奴隶的感恩戴德。



6. We never hold anything, we just get desire for everything from people.

我们不曾占有任何事物，我们只是从人群中收获对所有事物的欲望。



7.Please give Tolstoya simple and noble death, howevera simple and noble death may not be a person's death.

请给予托尔斯泰一个朴素而高尚的死亡，然而一个朴素而高尚的死亡未必是一个人的死亡。



8. You have to make yourself noble, but you mustn’t ask others noble. Also, you have obligation to moral, but others have right to immorality. To others, immorality is a natural right, too.

你有高尚的义务，却没有要求别人高尚的权力。同样地，你有道德的义务，而别人有不道德的权力。对别人而言，不道德也是一种天赋权利。



9.The best sign of a society without moral is that everything is almost belong to moral overnight.

社会道德失范的最佳判据是一夜之间几乎所有事情都属于道德层面。



10. Great is on yesterday and tomorrow, not today.

伟大在过去与未来，不在现在。



11. Philosophy is philosopher’s explanation of his strange life.

哲学就是哲学家对自己特立独行生活的解释。



12. Eternal hungry, that is soul.

永恒的饥饿，就是灵魂。



13. On statistics, the expectation of sample may not be in the sample. Similarly, when I use a term such as people or human, it may not reach to a person or man.

在统计学中，样本期望不一定存在于样本之中。类似地，当我使用“人民”或“人类”这样的术语时，并不一定能指谓一个人。



14. Imaginary hope is the origin of baying, it’s morphine and opium.

预设的希望是挣扎之源，它是吗啡和鸦片。



15. Menopause is no problems on woman but on man. So the turn of life is always a problem to others not you.

更年期就是女人没症状，但男人不舒服。所以你人生的转轨总是别人的难题。



16. If condition on history and logic is full, everything seems to be natural.

如果历史的或逻辑的前提具足，所有事看起来都是自然而然的。



17. Atomic bomb is born with fear to people without fear to death.

原子弹诞生于对死亡无所畏惧之人的畏惧。



18. Knowledge changes fate, it becomes a horrible teleology.

知识改变命运，它变成了可憎的目的论。



19. Dialectics is rough to world, a disease of sense.

辩证法是对世界的粗暴，是一种理智的疾病。



20. All problems on metaphysics could be reduced as finding somewhere exact to cut off the chain of causality.

一切形而上难题都可以化约为寻求一个适当位置斩断因果链。



21. Only a man who must go and realize to hell would be afraid of doom someday.

只有注定且意识到注定要下地狱的人才会对未来某一天的世界末日耿耿于怀。



22. Letter is silent soul, it could abduct one who use it.

文字是沉默的灵魂，它可以绑架使用它的人。



23. Every psychosis is disease of society.

每一种精神病都是社会病。



24. To Chinese, the limit of car consumption is tank.

在中国，汽车消费的尽头是"坦克"。



25. Fine thought is clear to yourself at least.

好的思想至少对你自己是清晰的。



26. Does freedom need to be given? Does truth need to be cleared? If so, why?

自由需要被给予吗？真理需要被澄清吗？如果需要，为什么？



27. No doubts then trust, not no trusts then doubt.

无可置疑所以相信，而非不可相信所以怀疑。



28. Power of thought is represented in constructing nothingness. Human wisdom is represented in conscious lie.

思的力量体现在构造虚无。人的智慧体现在有意识的谎言。



29. An apple hit mind or head, some are curious but others are angry.

一颗苹果砸到头，被砸到心智的人好奇，而被砸到脑袋的人愤怒。



30.Logic is a great problem for human: Actually, you use it to comfort yourself not persuade others. However, without it, you give up the last right to comfort yourself.

逻辑是人类的一大难题：你使用它时实际是在安慰自己而非说服他人，但若你弃之不用，就连安慰自己这最后的权力都放弃了。



31. How bad it is doesn’t rest on robbing or stealing but what to be robbed or stolen.

情况的糟糕程度并不取决于抢劫还是偷窃，而在于被抢劫或偷窃的是什么。



32. Is sense a hypochondria?

理智是不是一种忧郁症？



33. The term “show-off” presents user’s self-knowledge to emptiness, indeed.

“卖弄”这个词事实上表现了使用者对自己无知的自觉。



II

34. Violence of consensus is that you can never clear up the steel seal of it.

舆论暴力就是你永远无法消除它为你打上的钢印。



35. When I use the term “being or existence”, do I suppose the existence of law as essence? How do we know the existence of law in the world? And how do you know that every series has one general term formula at least, whatever analytical or nonanalytical?

当我使用“存在”这个词时，是否预设了作为本质的规律的存在？我们凭什么知道世界是有规律的？我们又凭什么知道任何一个序列都至少存在一个通项公式，无论它是解析的还是非解析的？



36. The real dilemma of a detective novel is that every suspect has entered before. It changes a filling in fact to a choice in game. The perfect crime is not a perfect proof elsewhere, but that don’t lead to prove.

一部侦探小说的真正困境是每一个嫌疑人都已经出场了。它把现实中的填空题变成了游戏中的选择题。完美的犯罪不是一个完美的不在场证明，而是不要引起任何证明。



37. Lie is a trick to be, as a result of natural (maybe societal) selection or evolution.

谎言是生存的技巧，作为自然（或社会）选择或者说进化的产物。



38. A fine solution is just to one problem, and a fine problem brings many solutions, as Hilbert told, a hen with golden eggs.

一个好的解只能对应一个问题，而一个好的问题可以有很多解，就像希尔伯特说的那样，一只下金蛋的母鸡。



39. Why critique? The more love, the more doubts.

为什么批判？爱之深，责之切。



40. The man who was sucked by Dracula would become a Dracula. The man who was impinged would impinge others by “rights”.

被吸血鬼咬过的人会变成吸血鬼。被侵害过的人会“正当地”侵害别人。



41. Logic is toughness of thought, and philosophy is sense of reality to life.

逻辑是思维的韧性，而哲学是生活的质感。



42. Someone talks about something by historical experience continually, it’s not to analyze fact but fan the flame.

有些人频繁调用历史经验谈论某些事情，不是在分析事实而是在煽动情绪。



43. You can’t prove your sublimity by enemy’s contemptibility.

你不能用敌人的卑鄙来证明自己的崇高。



III

44. The great leader being makes people turn to nothingness.

伟大领袖的存在使人民变得渺小。



45. No truth that is not a truth.

没有真相本身不是一个真相。
