作者：蒋迅

我曾经介绍过[斯坦福大学2011年秋季的三个免费在线课程](home.php?mod=space&uid=420554&do=blog&id=496980)：人工智能、机器学习和数据库导引。现在下一个学期的免费课程又出来了，而且更多：
- [CS 101 by Nick Parlante](http://cs101-class.org/)
- [Natural Language Processing by Dan Jurafsky and Chris Manning](http://nlp-class.org/)
- [Software Engineering for SAAS by Armando Fox and David Patterson](http://saas-class.org/)
- [Human-Computer Interfaces by Scott Klemmer](http://hci-class.org/)
- [Game Theory by Matthew Jackson and Yoav Shoham](http://game-theory-class.org/)
- [Probabilistic Graphical Models by Daphne Koller](http://pgm-class.org/)
- [Machine Learning by Andrew Ng](http://jan2012.ml-class.org/)
- [Cryptography by Dan Boneh](http://www.crypto-class.org/)
- [Design and Analysis of Algorithms I by Tim Roughgarden](http://www.algo-class.org/)

- [The Lean Launchpad by Steve Blank](http://www.launchpad-class.org/)
- [Technology Entrepreneurship by Chuck Eesley](http://www.venture-class.org/)
- [Making Green Buildings by Martin Fischer](http://www.greenbuilding-class.org/)
- [Information Theory by Tsachy Weissman](http://www.infotheory-class.org/)
- [Anatomy by Dr Srivastava](http://www.anatomy-class.org/)
- [Model Thinking by Scott E Page](http://www.modelthinker-class.org/)
- [Computer Security by Dan Boneh, John Mitchell and Dawn Song](http://www.security-class.org/)

一个明显的趋势就是大学的免费在线课程越来越多，越做越好。从课堂教学走向在线教育日益成熟。请到[这里](http://lifehacker.com/201979/technophilia-get-a-free-college-education-online)和[这里](http://education-portal.com/articles/Un%E4%B8%8Dversities_with_the_Best_Free_Online_Courses.html)去找你感兴趣的课程。我介绍过的另一个在线大学“[可汉大学](home.php?mod=space&uid=420554&do=blog&id=428188)”又在着手走向课堂教学。我知道这个现象在中国也在发生着。科学网上也有人在呼吁“科学网大学”。我们希望能有成果。

废话不说了。对这些课有兴趣吗？赶紧报名吧。
