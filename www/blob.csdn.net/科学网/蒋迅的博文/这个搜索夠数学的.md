谷歌3D搜索
这是在[木遥](http://www.weibo.com/farmostwood)的微博上看到的：“用非低级浏览器在 google 里搜索 [(-sqrt(1-x^2-(y-abs(x))^2))*cos(30*((1-x^2-(y-abs(x))^2))), x is from -1 to 1, y is from -1 to 1.5, z is from -1 to 1](https://www.google.com/#hl=en&output=search&sclient=psy-ab&q=%28-sqrt%281-x%5E2-%28y-abs%28x%29%29%5E2%29%29*cos%2830*%28%281-x%5E2-%28y-abs%28x%29%29%5E2%29%29%29%2C+x+is+from+-1+to+1%2C+y+is+from+-1+to+1.5%2C+z+is+from+-1+to+1&oq=%28-sqrt%281-x%5E2-%28y-abs%28x%29%29%5E2%29%29*cos%2830*%28%281-x%5E2-%28y-abs%28x%29%29%5E2%29%29%29%2C+x+is+from+-1+to+1%2C+y+is+from+-1+to+1.5%2C+z+is+from+-1+to+1&aq=f&aqi=&aql=&gs_l=hp.12...878l878l0l4110l1l0l0l0l0l0l0l0ll0l0.frgbld.&pbx=1&bav=on.2,or.r_gc.r_pw.r_qf.,cf.osb&fp=9b0c83a130f3bca0&biw=1344&bih=740)”，竟然得到这个好玩的东西。低级浏览器是做不到的。

突然发现，[新浪微博关闭评论](http://www.weibo.com/z/notice20120331/)。木遥觉得是上头下达命令的大佬只是随口说了一句「关闭评论！」，却根本没想到微博还可以转发……
