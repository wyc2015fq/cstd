# 数学教学实践：大学讲师指南                           

已有 2800 次阅读2013-3-22 07:36|系统分类:[教学心得](http://blog.sciencenet.cn/home.php?mod=space&do=blog&view=all&uid=81613&catid=9)



数学“青椒”同仁们有福了，这里我向大家推荐 John H Mason教授专门为大学数学青年教师写的一本教学实践方面的书。

Mathematics Teaching Practice: Guide for University & College Lecturers By John H Mason

2002 | 200 Pages | ISBN: 1898563799 










This book offers hundreds of suggestions for making small and medium sized changes for lectures, tutorials, task design, or problem solving. Here is guidance and inspiration for effective mathematics teaching in a modern technological environment, directed to teachers who are unhappy with results or experience, or those now in teacher training or new to the profession. Commencing with a range of student behaviours and attitudes that have struck and amazed tutors and lecturers, Professor Mason offers a wealth of partial diagnoses, followed by specific advice and suggestions for remedial actions.
